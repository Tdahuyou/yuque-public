# 音标

- 英 `/'zɪgzæg/`
- 美 `/'zɪɡzæɡ/`

# 词义

adj. 曲折的；锯齿形的；之字形的


v. 使成之字形；使曲折行进
`to move forward in sharp angles, first to the left and then to the right etc`

n. 之字形；Z字形
`a pattern that looks like a line of z’s joined together`

adv. 曲折地；之字形地；Z字形地
`to move forward in sharp angles, first to the left and then to the right etc`



zig+zag(急转)→两个“Z”像中文的“之”字， 两个“Z”字急转→之字形

# 同义

adj. 曲折的；锯齿形的；之字形的
`flexural` `labyrinthic`

vi. 曲折行进；作之字形行进
`corkscrew`

n. 之字形；Z字形
`zz`

adv. 曲折地；之字形地；Z字形地
`circuitously`

# 短语

- `zigzag course` 曲折航线

# 例句

Some species weave designs into their webs, creating zigzag patterns that can either attract prey, like bees, in bright sunlight, or camouflage the net in the shade.
一些种类的蜘蛛能设计织网，设计出可以在明媚的阳光下吸引猎物，如蜜蜂，或在阴暗处能掩饰蜘蛛网的之字形图案。
