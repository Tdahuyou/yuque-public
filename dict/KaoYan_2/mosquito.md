# 音标

- 英 `/mɒ'skiːtəʊ/`
- 美 `/məˈskiːtoʊ/`

# 词义

n. 蚊子
`a small flying insect that sucks the blood of people and animals, sometimes spreading the disease  malaria `



“貌似黑头”→蚊子很像鼻子上的黑头→蚊子

# 同义

n. 蚊子
`skeeter`

# 短语

- `mosquito net` n. 蚊帐
- `mosquito killer` 蚊香器

# 例句

The male mosquito never stings.
雄蚊不叮。

Are your mosquito bites still itching?
你被蚊子咬的地方还痒吗?

The frog darted out its tongue to catch a mosquito.
这只青蛙突然伸出舌头捕捉蚊子。
