# 音标

- 英 `/tʃɒp/`
- 美 `/tʃɑp/`

# 词义

n. 砍；排骨；商标；削球
`a small piece of meat on a bone, usually cut from a sheep or pig`

v. 剁碎；砍
`to cut something into smaller pieces`
