# 音标

- 英 `/træmp/`
- 美 `/træmp/`

# 词义

v. 流浪；践踏，踩；脚步沉重地行走
`to walk somewhere slowly and with heavy steps`

n. 流浪者；沉重的脚步声；徒步旅行
`someone who has no home or job and moves from place to place, often asking for food or money`



流浪者(tramp)随处扎营(camp)

# 同义

vi. 流浪；践踏，踩；脚步沉重地行走
`step` `vagabondize`

n. 流浪者；沉重的脚步声；徒步旅行
`Vagabonds` `Nads`

vt. 践踏，踩；走过
`ride down` `tread on sth`

# 同根

- n. `trampling` 践踏；踩踏
- v. `trampling` 蹂躏（trample的ing形式）

# 短语

- `on the tramp` 走江湖，跑码头；漂泊着

# 例句

He tramped the streets looking for work.
他在街上四处奔走，寻找工作。
