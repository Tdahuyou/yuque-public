# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'kriːs/`
- 美 `/dɪ'kris/`

# 词义

n. 减少，减小；减少量
`the process of becoming less, or the amount by which something becomes less`

v. 减少，减小
`to become less or go down to a lower level, or to make something do this`



de(相反)+cre(增长， 产生)+ase→与增长相反的是减少→减少， 减小

# 同义

n. 减少，减小；减少量
`reduction` `loss`

vi. 减少，减小
`to cut down` `fall off`

vt. 减少，减小
`shorten` `weaken`

# 同根

- adj. `decreasing` 渐减的
- v. `decreasing` 减少；缩减（decrease的ing形式）

# 短语

- `on the decrease` 在减少中
- `decrease by` 减少了…

# 例句

In Spain and Portugal there has been a decrease in the number of young people out of work.
在西班牙和葡萄牙，失业青年人数已经有所下降。
