# 音标

- 英 `/beɪt/`
- 美 `/bet/`

# 词义

v. 引诱；在…中放诱饵；折磨
`to put bait on a hook to catch fish or in a trap to catch animals`

n. 饵；诱饵
`food used to attract fish, animals, or birds so that you can catch them`



等(wait)鱼吃饵(bait)

# 同义

vt. 引诱；在…中放诱饵；折磨
`harry` `worry` `trouble` `tear` `induce`

n. 饵；诱饵
`gentle` `lure`

# 同根

- n. `baiting` 诱饵
- v. `baiting` 诱惑（bait的ing形式）

# 短语

- `cut bait` [美国俚语]中止；停止
- `live bait` 活诱饵
- `rise to the bait` 入圈套；上当受骗

# 例句

He baited his hook with pie.
他把馅饼放在钓钩上作饵。
