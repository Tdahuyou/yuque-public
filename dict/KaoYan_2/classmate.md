# 音标

- 英 `/'klɑːsmeɪt/`
- 美 `/'klæsmet/`

# 词义

n. 同班同学
`a member of the same class in a school, college or – in the US – a university`

# 例句

He acquainted his classmate with my sister.
他把我妹妹介绍给他的同班同学。

He acquainted his classmate with my younger brother.
他把我的弟弟介绍给他的同班同学。

I, who am your classmate, can understand you.
我是你的同班同学,能了解你。
