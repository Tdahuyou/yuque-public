# 音标

- 英 `/prɪ'lɪmɪn(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/prɪ'lɪmɪnɛri/`

# 词义

n. 准备；预赛；初步措施
`something that is said or done first, to introduce or prepare for something else`

adj. 初步的；开始的；预备的
`happening before something that is more important, often in order to prepare for it`



pre(在…之前)＋limin(门槛)+ary→进入门槛之前→预备的

# 同义

n. 准备；预赛；初步措施
`preparation` `arrangement` `provision`

adj. 初步的；开始的；预备的
`opening` `rudimentary`
