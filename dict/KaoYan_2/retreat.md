# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'triːt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'trit/`

# 词义

n. 撤退；休息寓所；撤退
`a movement away from the enemy after a defeat in battle`

v. 撤退；退避；向后倾
`to move away from the enemy after being defeated in battle`



re(向后)+treat(=tract， 拉， 拽)→向后拉→撤退， 退却

# 同义

n. 撤退；休息寓所；撤退
`withdrawal` `pullback`

vi. 撤退；退避；向后倾
`withdraw` `bug out`

# 同根

- n. `retreatment` 再处理；再精制

# 短语

- `retreat into oneself` 退隐；离群索居
- `retreat from` 退出；放弃
- `in full retreat` 全线溃退
- `beat a retreat` [军]撤退；放弃

# 例句

Napoleon’s retreat from Moscow
拿破仑从莫斯科的撤退

The rebel forces are in full retreat (= retreating very fast ) .
叛军全线溃退。

The bugler sounded the retreat (= gave a loud signal for retreat ) .
司号兵吹响撤退号。
