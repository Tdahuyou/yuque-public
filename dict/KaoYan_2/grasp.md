# 音标

- 英 `/grɑːsp/`
- 美 `/ɡræsp/`

# 词义

n. 抓住；理解；控制
`the way you hold something or your ability to hold it`

v. 抓住；领会
`to completely understand a fact or an idea, especially a complicated one`



他见到她宛如抓住(grasp)一根救命稻草(grass)

# 同义

n. 抓住；理解；控制
`control` `understanding` `comprehension` `guiding` `hold`

vt. 抓住；领会
`see` `appreciate` `noble` `collar`

vi. 抓
`strike` `scratch`

# 同根

- adj. `grasping` 贪婪的；紧紧抓住的
- adj. `graspable` 可抓住的；能理解的
- v. `grasping` 理解，领会（grasp的现在分词形式）；抓紧，抓牢

# 短语

- `grasp at` v. 想抓住；攫取
- `grasp the overall situation` 把握大局

# 例句

Luke took her arm in a firm grasp and led her through the gate.
卢克紧紧抓着她的胳膊，领她进了门。

He had allowed the ball to slip from his grasp.
他让球从手里滑了出去。
