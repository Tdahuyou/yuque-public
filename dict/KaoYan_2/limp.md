# 音标

- 英 `/lɪmp/`
- 美 `/lɪmp/`

# 词义

adj. 柔软的，无力的；软弱的
`not firm or strong`

v. 跛行，一拐一拐地走；缓慢费力地前进
`to walk slowly and with difficulty because one leg is hurt or injured`

n. 跛行
`the way someone walks when they are limping`
