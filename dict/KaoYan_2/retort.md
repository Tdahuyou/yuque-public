# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'tɔːt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'tɔrt/`

# 词义

n. 反驳，顶嘴；曲颈瓶
`a short angry or humorous reply`

v. 反驳，反击
`to reply quickly, in an angry or humorous way`



re(反)+tort(扭)→反扭→反驳

# 同义

n. 反驳，顶嘴；曲颈瓶
`contradiction` `oppugnation`

vt. 反驳，反击
`refute` `disprove`

vi. 反驳，回嘴
`talk back` `answer sb. back`

# 短语

- `retort pouch` 蒸煮袋；杀菌袋

# 例句

He was about to make a sharp retort .
他正要尖刻地反驳。
