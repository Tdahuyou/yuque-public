# 音标

- 英 `/'set(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'sɛtl/`

# 词义

v. 解决；定居；沉淀；下陷
`to end an argument or solve a disagreement`

n. 有背长椅
`a seat, for two or more people, usually made of wood with a high back and arms, and sometimes having a storage space in the boxlike seat `

# 同义

vi. 解决；定居；沉淀；下陷
`deposit` `beach` `work out` `figure out`

vt. 解决；安排；使…定居
`decide` `schedule` `budget` `place in` `work out`

# 同根

- adj. `settled` 稳定的；固定的
- n. `settlement` 解决，处理；[会计] 结算；沉降；殖民
- n. `settler` 移居者；殖民者
- n. `settling` 安置；固定；沉淀物；移居
- v. `settled` 解决；定居（settle的过去分词）
- v. `settling` 解决；安居（settle的ing形式）

# 短语

- `settle down` 定居；安定下来；专心于
- `settle in` v. 迁入
- `settle for` 满足于
- `settle on` 选定；授与；停在
- `settle into` 习惯于...
- `settle with` 与…清算；和…和解
- `settle up` 付清；了结；结帐
- `settle an account` 结算
- `settle dispute` 解决争端；解决争议
- `settle account` 结帐

# 例句

We hope the factions will be able to settle their differences (= agree to stop arguing ) by peaceful means.
我们希望各派系能以和平方式解决分歧。

Forensic tests should settle the question of whether Bates was actually present at the scene of the crime.
法医检验应该能够解决贝茨是否在犯罪现场的问题。

They might be willing to settle out of court (= come to an agreement without going to a court of law ) .
他们或许会愿意庭外和解。
