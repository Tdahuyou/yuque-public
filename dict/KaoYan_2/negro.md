# 音标

- 英 `/'niːgrəʊ/`
- 美 `/'nigro/`

# 词义

n. 黑人
`A Negro is someone with dark skin who comes from Africa or whose ancestors came from Africa`

adj. 黑人的
`relating to or characteristic of Negroes `

# 短语

- `negro spiritual` n. 黑人灵歌

# 例句

Slavery was “an unqualified evil to the negro, the white man, and the State,” said Abraham Lincoln in the 1850s.
奴隶制，“对于黑人、白人与合众国来说都是一种彻底的邪恶，”1850年林肯如此说道。
