# 音标

- 英 `/skiː/`
- 美 `/ski/`

# 词义

n. 滑雪橇
`one of a pair of long thin narrow pieces of wood or plastic that you fasten to your boots and use for moving on snow or on water`

v. 滑雪
`to move on skis for sport or in order to travel on snow or water`

adj. 滑雪（用）的
`You use ski to refer to things that are concerned with skiing`



喜欢滑雪(ski)时滑过天际(sky)的感觉

# 同义

vi. 滑雪
`skee`

# 同根

- n. `skiing` 滑雪运动；滑雪术
- n. `skier` 滑雪的人
- v. `skiing` 滑雪（ski的ing形式）

# 短语

- `ski resort` 滑雪胜地
- `water ski` 滑水橇
- `ski jumping` 跳台滑雪（滑雪运动项目之一）
- `ski pole` 滑雪杖
- `ski jump` 跳台滑雪
- `jet ski` 喷气式滑雪板或滑橇
- `ski suit` 滑雪服

# 例句

ski slopes
滑雪坡

a ski resort (= where people can go skiing )
滑雪度假胜地

...the Swiss ski resort of Klosters.
