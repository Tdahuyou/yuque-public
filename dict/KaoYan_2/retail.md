# 音标

- 英 `/'riːteɪl/`
- 美 `/'ritel/`

# 词义

v. 零售；转述
`to be sold for a particular price in a shop`

n. 零售
`the sale of goods in shops to customers, for their own use and not for selling to anyone else`

adv. 以零售方式
`to be sold for a particular price in a shop`

adj. 零售的




re+tail(切割)→割成小块卖→零售

# 同义

n. 零售
`sell-through` `resale`

# 同根

- n. `retailer` 零售商；传播的人
- n. `retailing` 零售业
- v. `retailing` 零售（retail的ing形式）
