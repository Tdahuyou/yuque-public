# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒsɪkjuːt/`
- 美 `/'prɑsɪ'kjʊt/`

# 词义

v. 检举；贯彻；从事；依法进行
`if a lawyer prosecutes a case, he or she tries to prove that the person charged with a crime is guilty`



pro(前面)+secut(跟随)+e→跟随前面→继续从事

# 同义

vt. 检举；贯彻；从事；依法进行
`carry out` `undertake` `pursue` `address` `have`

vi. 起诉；告发；作检察官
`law` `take legal action`

# 同根

- n. `prosecution` 起诉，检举；进行；经营
- n. `prosecutor` 检察官；公诉人；[法] 起诉人；实行者

# 例句

Mrs Lynn Smith, prosecuting, said the offence took place on January 27.
控方律师林恩·史密斯夫人称案件发生于1月27日。
