# 音标

- 英 `/klɪŋ/`
- 美 `/klɪŋ/`

# 词义

v. 坚持，墨守；紧贴；附着
`If someone clings to a position or a possession they have, they do everything they can to keep it even though this may be very difficult`

# 同义

vi. 坚持，墨守；紧贴；附着
`stay` `stand on`

# 短语

- `cling film` 食品薄膜

# 例句

Instead, he appears determined to cling to power.
反而，他看来坚决要握紧权力不放。

Another congressman clung on with a majority of only 18.
另一位国会议员在获得仅仅18张多数票的情况下保住了位子。
