# 音标

- 英 `/ʌndə'niːθ/`
- 美 `/,ʌndɚ'niθ/`

# 词义

prep. 在…的下面；在…的形式下；在…的支配下
`directly under another object or covered by it`

adv. 在下面；在底下
`Underneath is also an adverb`

n. 下面；底部
`the bottom surface of something, or the part of something that is below or under something else`

adj. 下面的；底层的


# 同义

adv. 在下面；在底下
`hereunder` `down below`

n. 下面；底部
`basis` `bottom` `base`

adj. 下面的；底层的
`following` `nether`

# 例句

He got out of the car and looked underneath.
他下车往底下看了看。

It’s near where the railway goes underneath the road.
它靠近铁路从公路下方穿过的地方。

She was wearing a smart jacket with a T-shirt underneath.
她穿了件时髦的夹克，里面是一件 T 恤衫。

Her blonde hair was hidden underneath a baseball cap.
一顶棒球帽裹住了她的满头金发。

He has on his jeans and a long-sleeved blue denim shirt with a white T-shirt underneath.
他身穿牛仔裤和一件蓝色长袖粗斜纹棉布衬衫，里面还有件白色T恤衫。

The shooting-range is lit from underneath by rows of ruby-red light fixtures.
射击场被一排排宝石红照明装置从下面照亮。
