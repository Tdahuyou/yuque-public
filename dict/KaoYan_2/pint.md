# 音标

- 英 `/paɪnt/`
- 美 `/paɪnt/`

# 词义

n. 品脱；一品脱的量；一品脱牛奶或啤酒
`a unit for measuring an amount of liquid, especially beer or milk. In Britain a pint is equal to 0.568 litres, and in the US it is equal to 0.473 litres.`



发音记忆

# 短语

- `half pint` n. 半品脱；矮子

# 例句

He’s gone down the pub for a quick pint.
他去小酒馆匆匆喝上一品脱啤酒。
