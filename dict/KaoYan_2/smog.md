# 音标

- 英 `/smɒg/`
- 美 `/smɑːɡ/`

# 词义

n. 烟雾
`dirty air that looks like a mixture of smoke and  fog  , caused by smoke from cars and factories in cities`



smoke(烟)+fog(雾)→烟雾

# 同义

n. [环境]烟雾
`vapor` `smoke shell`

# 同根

- adj. `smoggy` 烟雾弥漫的

# 短语

- `photochemical smog` n. 光化雾

# 例句

Cars cause pollution, both smog and acid rain.
汽车引起污染，既有烟雾又有酸雨。
