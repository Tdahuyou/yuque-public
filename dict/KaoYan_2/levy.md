# 音标

- 英 `/'levɪ/`
- 美 `/'lɛvi/`

# 词义

n. 征收；征兵，征税
`A levy is a sum of money that you have to pay, for example, as a tax to the government`

v. 征收（税等）；征集（兵等）
`to officially say that people must pay a tax or charge`



lev(举； 升)+y→将钱物上交→征收； 征税

# 同义

n. [税收]征收；征兵，征税
`collection` `taxation` `expropriation`

vt. [税收]征收（税等）；征集（兵等）
`toll`

vi. 征税；征兵
`tax collection` `toll`

# 短语

- `tax levy` 纳税；征收税款
- `levy on` 征税；扣押
- `levy tax` 征税
- `transaction levy` 交易征费

# 例句

...an annual levy on all drivers.
…向所有司机征收的年税。
