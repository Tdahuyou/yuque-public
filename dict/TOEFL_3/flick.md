# 音标

- 英 `/flɪk/`
- 美 `/flɪk/`

# 词义

v. 轻拍；移动
`to move with a sudden quick movement, or to make something move in this way`

n. 轻拍；浏览
`to look at a book, magazine, set of pictures etc very quickly`



fli(看做fly， 飞) + ck → 轻拍翅膀， 展翅高飞 → 轻拍

# 同根

- adj. `flickering` 闪烁的，忽隐忽现的；摇曳的
- n. `flicker` 闪烁；闪光；电影
- v. `flickering` 闪烁（flicker的ing形式）
- vi. `flicker` 闪烁；摇曳；颤动；扑动翅膀；假装昏倒
- vt. `flicker` 使闪烁；使摇曳

# 例句

His tongue flicked across his lips.
他的舌头在双唇间快速移动。
