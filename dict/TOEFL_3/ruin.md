# 音标

- 英 `/'ruːɪn/`
- 美 `/'ruɪn/`

# 词义

vt. 破坏，毁灭；使破产
`to make someone lose all their money`

n. 毁灭，毁坏；
`Ruin is the state of being severely damaged or spoiled, or the process of reaching this state`



一场大雨(rain)毁了(ruin)庄稼

# 同义

n. 废墟；毁坏；灭亡
`demolition` `disruption`

vt. 毁灭；使破产
`devastate` `go to the dogs`

vi. 破产；堕落；被毁灭
`fail` `go bankrupt`

# 同根

- adj. `ruined` 毁灭的；荒废的
- adj. `ruinous` 破坏性的，毁灭性的；零落的
- adv. `ruinously` 毁灭地；败坏地
- n. `ruination` 毁灭；祸根
- v. `ruined` 毁灭（ruin的变形）

# 短语

- `in ruins` 成为废墟
- `financial ruin` 经济损失

# 例句

Jefferson was ruined by the lawsuit.
杰弗逊因为那场官司倾家荡产。

The vineyards were falling into ruin.
那些葡萄园当时正日渐没落。
