# 音标

- 英 `/'jougərt/`
- 美 `/'joɡət/`

# 词义

n. 酸奶
`Yogurt is a food in the form of a thick, slightly sour liquid that is made by adding bacteria to milk. A yogurt is a small container of yogurt. `

# 同义

n. 酸奶酪，[食品]酸乳（等于yoghurt）
`acid milk` `yoghourt`

如果你喜欢农家鲜干酪，那就加些水果和酸奶吧！
