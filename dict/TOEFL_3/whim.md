# 音标

- 英 `/wɪm/`
- 美 `/wɪm/`

# 词义

n. 一时的兴致，冲动；怪念头，奇想
`a sudden feeling that you would like to do or have something, especially when there is no important or good reason`

# 同义

n. 奇想；一时的兴致；怪念头；幻想
`vagary` `fancy`

# 同根

- n. `whimsy` 怪念头；反复无常
- n. `whimsey` 奇想；怪念头

# 例句

We decided, more or less on a whim, to sail to Morocco.
我们多少有些心血来潮地决定乘船去摩洛哥。
