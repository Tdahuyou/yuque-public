# 音标

- 英 `/ˌrestə'reɪʃn/`
- 美 `/'rɛstə'reʃən/`

# 词义

n. 恢复；整修，修复；复位，复原；归还
`when you repair something such as an old building or a piece of furniture, so that it looks the same as when it was first built or made`

# 同义

n. 恢复；复位；王政复辟；归还
`recovery` `replacement` `retrieval` `renewal` `resumption`

# 同根

- adj. `restorative` 滋补的，有助于复元的；恢复健康的
- n. `restorative` [药] 恢复药，滋补剂
- n. `restorer` 修补者；[计] 复位器；[计] 恢复设备；修建者
- vi. `restore` 恢复；还原
- vt. `restore` 恢复；修复；归还

# 短语

- `restoration ecology` 恢复生态学

# 例句

Major restoration work will begin in May.
大规模的修复工作将在5月开始。
