# 音标

- 英 `/'kɑːnfɪskeɪt/`
- 美 `/'kɑnfɪsket/`

# 词义

vt. 没收，充公，征用
`to officially take private property away from someone, usually as a punishment`



con(和) + fisc(箱； 金库) + ate → 使和金库分开 → 没收， 充公， 征用

# 同义

vt. 没收；充公；查抄
`sequestrate` `expropriate`

adj. 被没收的
`forfeit`

# 同根

- n. `confiscation` 没收；征用；充公

# 例句

Miss Williams confiscated all our sweets.
威廉斯老师没收了我们所有的糖果。

Many opposition supporters had their goods confiscated.
许多反对党支持者的财产被充公了。
