# 音标

- 英 `/tɔːr'neɪdou/`
- 美 `/tɔr'nedo/`

# 词义

n. 飓风，龙卷风
`an extremely violent storm consisting of air that spins very quickly and causes a lot of damage`



torn(转动) + ado → 能转动万物的风 → 飓风， 龙卷风

# 同义

n. [气象]龙卷风；旋风；暴风；大雷雨
`cyclone` `Tornado Jockey`
