# 音标

- 英 `/ʃraud/`
- 美 `/ʃraʊd/`

# 词义

vt. 隐藏，遮蔽
`to cover or hide something`

n. 裹尸布，寿衣；遮蔽物
`a cloth that is wrapped around a dead person’s body before it is buried`



下大雨(pour)了， 应该(should)找个地方避避(shroud)

# 同义

n. 寿衣；覆盖物；船的横桅索；[电]护罩
`covering` `cerement`

vt. 覆盖；包以尸衣
`invest` `house` `sheet` `roof` `cap`

# 例句

Joseph was shrouded under a dark blanket.
约瑟夫身上裹着一条深色的毯子。
