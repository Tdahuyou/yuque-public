# 音标

- 英 `/'spɑːnsər/`
- 美 `/'spɑnsɚ/`

# 词义

vt. 主办；赞助
`to give money to a sports event, theatre, institution etc, especially in exchange for the right to advertise`

n. 发起者；赞助者
`someone who officially introduces or supports a proposal for a new law`



spons(音似： 四帮四) + or → 以四帮四的形式资助别人 → 赞助

# 同义

n. 赞助者；主办者；保证人
`friend` `guarantee` `entrepreneur`

vt. 赞助；发起
`launch` `support of`

# 同根

- n. `sponsorship` 赞助；发起；保证人的地位；教父母身份

# 例句

The competition was sponsored by British Airways.
此项比赛由英国航空公司赞助。

government-sponsored projects
政府资助的项目
