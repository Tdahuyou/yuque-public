# 音标

- 英 `/heɪl/`
- 美 `/hel/`

# 词义

v. 招呼；高呼；赞扬，称颂；下冰雹
`to call to someone in order to greet them or try to attract their attention`

n. 冰雹；一阵
`frozen raindrops which fall as hard balls of ice`



冰雹(hail)让航行(sail)计划流产(fail)了

# 同义

n. [气象]冰雹；致敬；招呼；一阵
`precipitation` `blast`

int. 万岁；欢迎
`welcome` `rah`

# 短语

- `hail from` （车、船）来自；（人）出生于
- `hail storm` 冰雹风暴

# 例句

She leaned out of the window and hailed a passerby.
她探出窗户向一个路人大喊。
