# 音标

- 英 `/fou'ment/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

vt. 煽动，挑起，激起
`to cause trouble and make people start fighting each other or opposing the government`



fom(看做form， 形成) + ent → 帮助形成 → 煽动

# 同义

vt. 煽动；挑起；热敷
`fan` `egg`

# 同根

- n. `fomentation` 热敷；煽动
- n. `fomenter` 煽动者；挑唆者

# 例句

They were accused of fomenting rebellion.
他们被指控煽动叛乱。
