# 音标

- 英 `/stæʃ/`
- 美 `/stæʃ/`

# 词义

vt. 隐藏，藏匿
`to store something secretly or safely somewhere`

n. 贮藏物
`an amount of something that is kept in a secret place, especially money, weapons, or drugs`



st + ash(灰) → 躲在灰里 → 隐藏， 藏匿

# 同义

vt. 存放；贮藏
`store` `deposit`

vi. 存放；藏起来
`put out of sight` `trust with`

# 例句

We went for the bottle of whisky that we had stashed behind the bookcase.
我们去取那瓶我们藏在书柜后面的威士忌。
