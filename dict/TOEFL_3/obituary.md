# 音标

- 英 `/ou'bɪtʃueri/`
- 美 `/o'bɪtʃuɛri/`

# 词义

n. 讣闻，讣告
`an article in a newspaper about the life of someone who has just died`



ob(=to向， 往) + it(走， 离去) + uary → 到某地去再相见(是去世的委婉说法) → 讣闻， 讣告

# 同义

adj. 讣告的；死亡的
`deceased.`

n. 讣告
`necrology`

# 同根

- n. `obit` 讣告（等于obituary）；忌辰；葬礼

# 短语

- `obituary notice` n. 讣告

# 例句

His obituary was published in one edition of his own newspaper before it was discovered that he was alive.
他的讣文刊登在他自己的报纸版面上之后，才发现他还活着。
