# 音标

- 英 `/'weðərɪŋ/`
- 美 `/'wɛðərɪŋ/`

# 词义

n. 侵蚀，风化
`the mechanical and chemical breakdown of rocks by the action of rain, snow, cold, etc `

# 同义

n. [地质]风化作用；[建][水利]泻水斜度；挡风雨材料
`eolation`

v. 风干；使退色；经受住（weather的ing形式）
`airing`

# 短语

- `weathering crust` 风化壳
- `weathering steel` [材料学]耐候钢；耐候性钢材
- `chemical weathering` [地质]化学风化作用
- `weathering test` 耐候性试验；风化试验
- `weathering process` 风化过程
- `accelerated weathering` 加速风化

# 例句

Certain plants play an additional role in weathering .
某些植物起了风化作用的辅助作用。

A climate with high temperature and arid condition is highly favourable for salt weathering.
高温和干旱相结合的气候最有利于盐风化过程。
