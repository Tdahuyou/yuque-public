# 音标

- 英 `/'riːəltər/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 房地产经纪人
`A Realtor is a person whose job is to sell houses, buildings, and land, and who is a member of the National Association of Realtors`



real(不动产的) + tor → 房地产经纪人

# 同义

n. （美）房地产经纪人
`real-estate agent` `real estate broker`
