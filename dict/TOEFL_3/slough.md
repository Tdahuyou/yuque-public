# 音标

- 英 `/slʌf；slaʊ/`
- 美 `/slʌf; slaʊ; slu/`

# 词义

vt. 使脱落
`When a plant sloughs its leaves, or an animal such as a snake sloughs its skin, the leaves or skin come off naturally`

n. 泥坑，沼泽；绝境
`an area of land covered in deep dirty water or mud`



s (音似： 四) + lough(湖) → 湖四周有很多泥坑 → 泥坑

# 同义

n. 蜕下的皮（或壳）；绝境；[地理]泥沼；[外科]腐肉
`extremity` `serbonian bog`

vt. 使陷入泥沼；抛弃
`embog` `cast aside`

vi. 蜕皮；脱落；在泥浆中跋涉
`shed skin` `come away`

# 同根

- adj. `sloughy` 脱落的；泥泞的；腐肉的；腐痂覆盖的

# 短语

- `slough off` 丢弃；抛弃

# 例句

All reptiles have to slough their skin to grow.
所有爬行动物都必须自然脱皮才能成长
