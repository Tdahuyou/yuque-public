# 音标

- 英 `/graus/`
- 美 `/ɡraʊs/`

# 词义

v. 发牢骚，抱怨
`to complain about something`

n. 松鸡；怨言
`a small bird that is hunted and shot for food and sport, or the flesh of this bird`



松鸡(grouse)抱怨(grouse)说， 它总是在清早被唤醒(arouse)

# 同义

n. [鸟]松鸡；雷鸟科的猎鸟；怨言
`jay` `jaybird`

vi. 埋怨
`find fault`

# 例句

"How come we never know what's going on?" he groused.
“我们怎么会不知道发生了什么事？”他抱怨道。
