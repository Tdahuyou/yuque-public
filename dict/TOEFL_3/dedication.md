# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdedɪ'keɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,dɛdɪ'keʃən/`

# 词义

n. 献身，奉献；献词；奉献仪式
`the words used at the beginning of a book, film, or piece of music, thanking someone or saying that book etc has been written to show respect for them`

# 同义

n. 奉献；献身
`commitment` `devotion`

# 同根

- adj. `dedicated` 专用的；专注的；献身的
- v. `dedicated` 以…奉献；把…用于（dedicate的过去式和过去分词）
- vt. `dedicate` 致力；献身；题献

# 短语

- `professional dedication` 敬业精神

# 例句

To reach a high level of skill requires talent, dedication, and a lot of hard work.
要达到高级水平需要天分，还需要全身心的投入和很多的努力。
