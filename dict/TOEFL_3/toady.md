# 音标

- 英 `/'toudi/`
- 美 `/'todi/`

# 词义

vi. 谄媚，奉承
`to pretend to like an important person and do things for them, so that they will help you – used to show disapproval`

n. 谄媚者，马屁精
`someone who pretends to like an important person and does things for them, so that that person will help them – used to show disapproval`



今天(today)遇见一个马屁精(toady)
