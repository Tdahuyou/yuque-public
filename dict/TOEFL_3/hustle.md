# 音标

- 英 `/'hʌsl/`
- 美 `/'hʌsl/`

# 词义

v. 推搡，硬挤
`to make someone move quickly, especially by pushing them roughly`

n. 忙碌；喧闹
`busy and noisy activity`

# 同义

vt. 催促；猛推；强夺
`crowd` `urge`

vi. 赶紧；硬挤过去；拼命挣钱
`make haste` `jump to it`

n. 推；奔忙；拥挤喧嚷
`push` `jundy`

# 同根

- n. `hustler` 催促者；妓女；骗子

# 短语

- `hustle and bustle` 熙熙攘攘，忙碌

# 例句

the hustle and bustle of the marketplace
市场里的熙熙攘攘
