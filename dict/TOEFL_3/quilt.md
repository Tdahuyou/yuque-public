# 音标

- 英 `/kwɪlt/`
- 美 `/kwɪlt/`

# 词义

v. 缝被；缝制
`If you quilt, or if you quilt a piece of fabric, you make a quilt`

n. 被褥
`a warm thick cover for a bed, made by sewing two layers of cloth together, with feathers or a thick material in between them`



他辞职(quit)后就天天躺在被窝(quilt)里度日

# 同义

n. 被子；棉被
`bedquilt` `comforter`

# 同根

- n. `quilting` 被上缝花纹的缝法；缝被子

# 短语

- `quilt cover` 被套，被罩
- `patchwork quilt` 拼布床单；用小块布缝缀的被罩

# 例句

Maggie knows how to quilt.
玛吉知道怎样缝被子。

Quilting a bed cover can be laborious.
缝制一个床罩会很费力的。
