# 音标

- 英 `/'kuʃn/`
- 美 `/'kʊʃən/`

# 词义

vt. 起缓冲作用
`to make the effect of a fall or hit less painful, for example by having something soft in the way`

n. 垫子
`a cloth bag filled with soft material that you put on a chair or the floor to make it more comfortable`



“苦行” → 苦行僧盘腿坐在垫子上 → 垫子

# 同义

n. [轻]垫子；起缓解作用之物；（猪等的）臀肉；银行储蓄
`gaddi` `mat`

# 同根

- adj. `cushiony` 似垫子的；柔软的
- n. `cushioning` 缓冲器；软垫
- v. `cushioning` 缓和（cushion的ing形式）

# 短语

- `air cushion` 气垫
- `seat cushion` 弹性座垫
- `back cushion` 靠背垫
- `cushion layer` 垫层
- `cushion the blow` 给垫着点儿；说话绵软一点以免打击过重
- `rubber cushion` 橡皮缓冲器，橡皮垫
- `cushion cap` 承台
- `cushion cover` 垫子套
- `cushion rubber` 垫层橡胶；缓冲橡胶
- `cushion for leaning on` 靠垫

# 例句

His landing was cushioned by the fresh snow that had fallen.
他落地时刚下的雪起到了缓冲作用。
