# 音标

- 英 `/spraut/`
- 美 `/spraʊt/`

# 词义

v. 发芽，抽条，生长；迅速出现，涌现出
`if vegetables, seeds, or plants sprout, they start to grow, producing  shoots  ,  buds  , or leaves`

n. 新芽，嫩枝，苗
`a new growth on a plant`



spr(看做spring， 春天) + out(出) → 春天来了， 柳树发芽了 → 发芽； 新芽

# 同义

vi. 发芽；长芽
`put` `come into bud`

vt. 使发芽；使萌芽
`germinate` `bud`

n. [植]芽；萌芽；苗芽
`seed` `bud`

# 短语

- `bean sprout` 豆芽

# 例句

Move the pots outside when the seeds begin to sprout.
种子开始发芽时把盆搬到外面去。

Trees were starting to sprout new leaves.
树上开始抽出新叶。
