# 音标

- 英 `/skwɜːrt/`
- 美 `/skwɝt/`

# 词义

v. 喷射，喷；向…喷射
`if you squirt liquid or if it squirts somewhere, it is forced out in a thin fast stream`

n. 喷射；〈口，贬〉无名小辈
`Squirt is also a noun`



往裙子(skirt)上喷(squirt)香水

# 同义

n. 喷射；[医]注射
`injection` `emission` `jet`

vi. 喷出
`belch out` `spout out`

# 同根

- n. `squirter` 喷射者；喷射器

# 短语

- `squirt gun` 水枪

# 例句

Some kids squirted a water pistol in her face.
有几个小孩用水枪朝她脸上喷水。

It just needs a little squirt of oil.
它只需喷点油。
