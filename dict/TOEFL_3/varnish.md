# 音标

- 英 `/'vɑːrnɪʃ/`
- 美 `/'vɑrnɪʃ/`

# 词义

vt. 上清漆
`to cover something with varnish`

n. 清漆
`a clear liquid that is painted onto things, especially things made of wood, to protect them, or the hard shiny surface produced by this`



涂上清漆(varnish)， 这个图案就不容易消失(vanish)了

# 同义

n. 亮光漆，清漆；虚饰；光泽面
`vernis` `frills`

vt. 装饰，粉饰；在…上涂清漆；使…有光泽
`colour` `paint` `hang` `sugar`

# 短语

- `baking varnish` 烘干清漆，烤干清漆
- `nail varnish` 指甲油（等于nail polish）
- `gloss varnish` 上光漆；有光清漆

# 例句

Varnish the table with two or three coats of water-based varnish.
给桌子涂上2到3层水性清漆。
