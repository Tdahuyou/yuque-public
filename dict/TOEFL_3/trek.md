# 音标

- 英 `/trek/`
- 美 `/trɛk/`

# 词义

n&v. 艰苦跋涉
`a long and difficult journey, made especially on foot as an adventure`



tr(看做try， 努力) + ek(看做nek， 山峡) → 努力翻越山峡 → 艰苦跋涉

# 同义

n. 艰苦跋涉
`treck`

vt. （牛）拉（货车）；搬运
`pull` `draw` `carry`

vi. 艰苦跋涉
`treck`

# 例句

a lonely trek through the forest
穿越森林的孤独跋涉
