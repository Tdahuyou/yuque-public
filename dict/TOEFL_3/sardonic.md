# 音标

- 英 `/sɑːr'dɑːnɪk/`
- 美 `/sɑːrˈdɑːnɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 嘲笑的，讥讽的
`showing that you do not have a good opinion of someone or something, and feel that you are better than them`

# 同义

adj. 讽刺的；嘲笑的，冷笑的
`acid` `ironic`

# 同根

- adv. `sardonically` 冷嘲地；讽刺地

# 例句

He looked at her with sardonic amusement.
他带着嘲讽的愉悦神情看着她。
