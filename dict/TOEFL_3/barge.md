# 音标

- 英 `/bɑːrdʒ/`
- 美 `/bɑrdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 冲撞，乱闯
`to move somewhere in a rough careless way, often hitting against things`

n. 驳船
`a large low boat with a flat bottom, used for carrying goods on a  canal  or river`



“八只” → 八只驳船 → 驳船

# 同义

vi. 蹒跚；闯入
`titubate` `burst into`

n. [船]驳船；游艇
`lighter` `catamaran`

# 短语

- `barge in` v. 闯入；干涉
- `barge into` 闯入；与…相撞
- `barge pole` n. 驳船撑竿

# 例句

She ran outside, barging past bushes and shrubs.
她跑了出去，磕磕绊绊经过一些灌木丛。
