# 音标

- 英 `/hɪtʃ/`
- 美 `/hɪtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 搭便车；提起，拉起；拴住
`to get free rides from the drivers of passing cars by standing at the side of the road and putting a hand out with the thumb raised`

n. 故障，障碍
`a small problem that makes something difficult or delays it for a short time`



hit(撞) + ch → 车子撞坏后， 渴望能搭便车 → 搭便车

# 同义

n. 故障；钩；猛拉；急推；蹒跚
`fault` `trouble`

vt. 搭便车；钩住；套住；猛拉；使结婚
`ring` `thumb a ride`

vi. 被钩住；急动；蹒跚；搭便车旅行；结婚
`get married` `titubate`

# 同根

- n. `hitchhiker` 搭便车的旅行者；顺便插入的广告

# 短语

- `without a hitch` 顺利地，成功地，容易地；不停地

# 例句

There was no garage in sight, so I hitched a lift into town.
附近没有汽车修理厂，于是我搭便车进了城。
