# 音标

- 英 `/lʌʃ/`
- 美 `/lʌʃ/`

# 词义

adj. 茂盛的
`plants that are lush grow many leaves and look healthy and strong`

# 同义

adj. 丰富的，豪华的；苍翠繁茂的
`abundant` `grand` `full` `ample` `plentiful`

vi. 喝酒
`wine` `liquor` `knock over a drink`

n. 酒；酒鬼
`wine` `liquor` `drink`

vt. 饮
`drink` `inhaust`

# 例句

a lush green mountainous island
草木茂盛苍翠、山峦起伏的岛

The fields were lush with grass and flowers.
田野里花草繁茂。
