# 音标

- 英 `/ˌmesə'liθik/`
- 美 `/ˌmiːzə(ʊ)ˈlɪθɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 中石器时代的
`of or relating to the Mesolithic `

n. 中石器时代
`the period between the Palaeolithic and the Neolithic, in Europe from about 12 000 to 3000 bc, characterized by the appearance of microliths `
