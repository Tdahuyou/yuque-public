# 音标

- 英 `/'trɑːpɪk/`
- 美 `/'trɑpɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 热带的


n. 回归线；热带地区
`one of the two imaginary lines around the world, either the Tropic of Cancer which is 23.5˚ north of the  equator  , or the Tropic of Capricorn which is 23.5˚ south of the equator`



trop(转) + ic → 太阳每年在回归线来回转 → 回归线

# 同义

n. [地理]热带；[天]回归线
`torrid zone` `regression line`

adj. [地理]热带的
`intertropical` `subsolar`

# 同根

- adj. `tropical` 热带的；热情的；酷热的
- adv. `tropically` 关于热带方面
- n. `tropics` 热带地区

# 短语

- `tropic of cancer` 北回归线
- `tropic of capricorn` [天]南回归线

# 例句

The tropic sun glared down on us all day.
热带的太阳整日灼晒着我们。

There was a restaurant a little way off Broadway where one could be served almost al fresco in a tropic array of screening flora.
离百老汇不远有一家餐馆，在那里用餐几乎像是置身于户外一排热带遮护植物中一样。
