# 音标

- 英 `/ˌriːə'bɪlɪteɪt/`
- 美 `/,riə'bɪlə,tet/`

# 词义

vt. 使恢复原状，修复；改造，使恢复正常生活；恢复…的名誉
`to make people think that someone or something is good again after a period when people had a bad opinion of them`



re(再， 又) + hab(拥有) + ilit + ate → 重新拥有 → 使恢复原状， 修复

# 同义

vt. 使康复；使恢复名誉；使恢复原状
`repristinate` `unmake`

vi. 复兴；复权；恢复正常生活
`revive`

# 同根

- adj. `rehabilitative` 复职的，复原的
- n. `rehabilitation` 复原

# 例句

The Prime Minister seems to be trying to rehabilitate the former defence secretary.
首相似乎在努力为前国防大臣恢复名誉。
