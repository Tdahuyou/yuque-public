# 音标

- 英 `/sə'maɪz/`
- 美 `/sɝ'maɪz/`

# 词义

v. 推测，猜测
`to guess that something is true, using the information you know already`

n. 推测，猜测
`If you say that a particular conclusion is surmise, you mean that it is a guess based on the available evidence and you do not know for certain that it is true`



sur(在…之上) + mis(放) + e → 放在事情之上 → 推测， 猜测

# 同义

vt. 猜测；推测
`make a guess at` `guess at sth`

vi. 猜测；认为
`consider` `guess at sth`

n. 推测；猜度
`speculation` `theory` `guess` `projection`

# 同根

- adj. `surmisable` 可以推测得出的，可揣度的

# 例句

There's so little to go on, we can only surmise what happened.
几乎没有什么可继续进行的，我们只能猜测发生过的事。

It is mere surmise that Bosch had Brant's poem in mind when doing this painting.
博斯画这幅画时头脑里想着勃朗特的诗，这只是猜测而已。
