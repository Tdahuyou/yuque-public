# 音标

- 英 `/kwɑːʃ/`
- 美 `/kwɑʃ/`

# 词义

vt. 撤销，废止；镇压；平息
`to say or do something to stop something from continuing`



qu + ash(灰) → 使成灰 → 平息

# 同义

vt. 撤销；镇压；宣布无效；捣碎
`suppress` `rat on`

# 例句

A hospital chief executive has quashed rumours that people will lose their jobs.
医院院长已澄清了员工将失业的谣言。

The government immediately moved to quash the revolt.
政府立即采取行动平定叛乱。
