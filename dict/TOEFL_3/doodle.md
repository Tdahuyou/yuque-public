# 音标

- 英 `/'duːdl/`
- 美 `/'dudl/`

# 词义

vi. 乱涂，胡画
`to draw shapes, lines, or patterns without really thinking about what you are doing`



一边吃面条(noodle)一边随手乱涂乱画(doodle)

# 同义

vi. 涂鸦；闲混；随意弹奏
`lounge away` `drone away`

n. 涂鸦；蠢人
`ass` `conehead`

vt. 乱涂出；闲混；随意弹奏
`lounge away` `drone away`

# 例句

Brad was doodling on a sheet of paper.
布拉德在一张纸上乱涂着。
