# 音标

- 英 `/spɪr/`
- 美 `/spɪr/`

# 词义

v. 刺，戳
`to push or throw a spear into something, especially in order to kill it`

n. 矛，枪；嫩叶
`a pole with a sharp pointed blade at one end, used as a weapon in the past`



用s刺(spear)梨(pear)

# 同义

n. 矛，枪
`pike`

# 短语

- `spear head` 刨尖头

# 例句

Spear a piece of fish with a carving fork and dip it in the batter.
用切肉叉叉起一片鱼并在面糊里蘸一下。
