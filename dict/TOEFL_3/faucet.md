# 音标

- 英 `/'fɔːsɪt/`
- 美 `/'fɔsɪt/`

# 词义

n. 龙头，旋塞
`the thing that you turn on and off to control the flow of water from a pipe`

# 同义

n. [机]旋塞；插口
`soket`

# 短语

- `water faucet` n. 水龙头

# 例句

If God turns off one faucet, he can turn on another.
如果上帝关上你的一个水龙头，他能开启另一个。

Your security is not in your bank account; your security is in the Lord. If God turns off one faucet, he can turn on another.
“你的安全并不在你的银行账户里；而在于上帝。如果上帝关住一个你的水龙头，他能开启另一个”。
