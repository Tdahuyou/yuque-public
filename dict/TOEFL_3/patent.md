# 音标

- 英 `/'pæt(ə)nt; 'peɪt(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'pætnt/`

# 词义

adj. 有专利的；显而易见的
`You use patent to describe something, especially something bad, in order to indicate in an emphatic way that you think its nature or existence is clear and obvious`

vt. 取得专利权
`to obtain a special document giving you the right to make or sell a new  invention  or product`

n. 专利权
`a special document that gives you the right to make or sell a new  invention  or product that no one else is allowed to copy`

# 同义

adj. [专利]专利的；新奇的；显然的
`apparent` `novel` `transparent` `decided`

n. [专利]专利权；执照；专利品
`license` `permit`

# 同根

- adj. `patented` 专利的
- n. `patentee` 专利权所有人
