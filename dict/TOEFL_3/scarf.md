# 音标

- 英 `/skɑːrf/`
- 美 `/skɑrf/`

# 词义

n. 围巾，披巾，头巾
`a piece of cloth that you wear around your neck, head, or shoulders, especially to keep warm`



为了遮住脖子上的伤疤(scar)， 围了条围巾(scarf)

# 同义

n. [服装]围巾；嵌接，嵌接处；头巾领巾
`muffler` `neckcloth`

# 例句

He reached up to loosen the scarf around his neck.
他伸出手松开围在脖子上的围巾。
