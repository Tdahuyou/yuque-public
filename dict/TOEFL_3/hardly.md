# 音标

- 英 `/'hɑːrdli/`
- 美 `/'hɑrdli/`

# 词义

adv. 几乎不，简直不；刚刚，仅仅
`used to say that something has only just happened`

# 同义

adv. 几乎不，简直不；刚刚
`just` `ill` `fresh` `scarcely` `barely`

# 短语

- `hardly any` 几乎没有
- `hardly ever` 几乎不；几乎从来不
- `hardly more than` 不足；仅仅
- `hardly when` 几乎未来得及就…；刚一…就…

# 例句

The building work has hardly begun.
施工才刚刚开始。
