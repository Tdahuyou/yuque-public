# 音标

- 英 `/kræmp/`
- 美 `/kræmp/`

# 词义

n. 痉挛，抽筋；
`a severe pain that you get in part of your body when a muscle becomes too tight, making it difficult for you to move that part of your body`

vt. 阻碍，阻止




出去露营(camp)居然腿抽筋(cramp)

# 同义

n. 痉挛，绞痛；[五金][机]铁夹钳
`fit` `spasm`

vt. 束缚，限制；使…抽筋；以铁箍扣紧
`chain` `block`

adj. 狭窄的；难解的；受限制的
`narrow` `restricted` `mysterious` `confined` `strait`

# 同根

- adj. `cramped` 狭窄的；难懂的；难辨的
- v. `cramped` 用夹钳夹；约束（cramp的过去分词）

# 例句

Several players were suffering from cramp.
有几名球员抽筋了。

muscle cramps
肌肉痉挛
