# 音标

- 英 `/'haɪpərtekst/`
- 美 `/'haɪpɚ'tɛkst/`

# 词义

n. 超文本
`a way of writing computer documents that makes it possible to move from one document to another by  clicking  on words or pictures, especially on the Internet`



hyper(高于) + text(文本) → 超文本

# 短语

- `hypertext markup language` 超文本标记语言；超文本置标语言

# 例句

...information embroidered with colourful graphics and tied together by hypertext links.
…饰以彩色图表并用超文本链接组合在一起的信息。
