# 音标

- 英 `/sə'bɔːrn/`
- 美 `/sə'bɔrn/`

# 词义

vt. 收买，买通，唆使
`to persuade someone to tell lies in a court of law or to do something else that is illegal, especially for money`



被收买(suborn)了去杀人放火， 真是够笨的(stubborn)

# 同义

vt. 唆使；收买；使作伪证
`set on` `instigate`

# 同根

- n. `subornation` 收买
