# 音标

- 英 `/swuːp/`
- 美 `/swup/`

# 词义

v. 猛扑；突然袭击
`if the police, army etc swoop on a place, they go there without any warning in order to look for someone or something`

n. 突然行动
`a sudden surprise attack on a place in order to get something or take people away – used especially in news reports`

# 同义

vi. 猛扑；突然袭击；突然下降；飞扑
`bound on` `surprise attack`

n. 猛扑；俯冲；突然袭击
`surprise` `subduction`

vt. 攫取；抓去
`grab` `pounce at`

# 短语

- `at one fell swoop` 一举，一下子

# 例句

The eagle hovered, ready to swoop at any moment.
鹰在天空盘旋，随时准备俯冲下来。
