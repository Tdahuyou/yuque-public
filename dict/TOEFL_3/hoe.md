# 音标

- 英 `/hou/`
- 美 `/ho/`

# 词义

vt. 用锄头锄
`If you hoe a field or crop, you use a hoe on the weeds or soil there`

n. 锄头
`a garden tool with a long handle, used for removing  weeds (= unwanted plants )  from the surface of the soil`



用锄头(hoe)挖洞(hole)

# 同根

- n. `hoeing` [农学] 锄地
- v. `hoeing` [农学] 锄地；挖掘；锄草（hoe的现在分词）

# 例句

I have to feed the chickens and hoe the potatoes.
我必须喂鸡，还得给土豆除草松土。
