# 音标

- 英 `/'ɔːrkɪd/`
- 美 `/'ɔrkɪd/`

# 词义

n. 兰，兰花
`a plant that has flowers which are brightly coloured and usually shaped`



or + chid(看做child， 孩子) → 孩子天真纯洁像兰花 → 兰花

# 同义

n. [园艺]兰花；[植]兰科植物；淡紫色
`cymbidium` `Arethusa`

adj. 淡紫色的
`violescent` `lavender`
