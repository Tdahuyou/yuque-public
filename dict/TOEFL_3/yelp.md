# 音标

- 英 `/jelp/`
- 美 `/jɛlp/`

# 词义

v. 尖叫
`If a person or dog yelps, they give a sudden short cry, often because of fear or pain`

n. 短而尖的叫声
`a short sharp high cry which a person or an animal makes because they are excited, in pain, surprised etc`



发出尖叫(yelp)， 寻求帮助(help)

# 同义

vi. 叫喊；狗吠
`bawl out`

vt. （因痛而）叫喊，叫喊着说
`cry` `bawl out`

n. 尖叫，大叫；吠声
`outcry` `screeching`

# 例句

Her dog yelped and came to heel.
她的狗汪地叫了一声，紧跟了上来。
