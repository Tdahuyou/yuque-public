# 音标

- 英 `/kəm'plaɪ/`
- 美 `/kəm'plaɪ/`

# 词义

vi. 服从；遵守
`to do what you have to do or are asked to do`



com(表加强) + ply(装满) → 完全装满 → 服从

# 同义

vi. 遵守；顺从，遵从；答应
`engage` `abide by/obey/observe`

# 短语

- `comply with` 照做，遵守

# 例句

The newspaper was asked by federal agents for assistance and agreed to comply.
联邦探员要求这家报社给予协助，报社同意配合。
