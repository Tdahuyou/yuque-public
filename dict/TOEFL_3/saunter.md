# 音标

- 英 `/'sɔːntər/`
- 美 `/'sɔntɚ/`

# 词义

vi&n. 闲逛，漫步
`to walk in a slow relaxed way, especially so that you look confident or proud`



s(看做see， 看) + aunt(姑姑) + er → 看姑姑去 → 闲逛而去 → 闲逛

# 同义

n. 漫步；闲逛
`bummel` `stroll`

vi. 闲逛；漫步
`lig` `knock around`

# 同根

- n. `saunterer` 漫步者

# 例句

We watched our fellow students saunter into the building.
我们看着同学们漫步走进了那栋楼。
