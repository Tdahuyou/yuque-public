# 音标

- 英 `/'steɪdʒkoutʃ/`
- 美 `/'stedʒkotʃ/`

# 词义

n. 公共马车，驿站马车
`a vehicle pulled by horses that was used in past times for carrying passengers and letters`



stage(驿站) + coach(马车) → 驿站马车， 公共马车

# 同义

n. 公共马车；驿站马车
`dawk`

# 例句

You know, sometimes, Ward, I think we should just hop onto a stagecoach and go out west, Missouri territory or some place like that.
你知道，有时，沃德，我觉得我们应该跳上驿站马车去西部，密苏里地区或者和那里相似的某个地方。
