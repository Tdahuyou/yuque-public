# 音标

- 英 `/ʃred/`
- 美 `/ʃrɛd/`

# 词义

n. 碎片，细条；些许，少量
`a small thin piece that is torn or cut roughly from something`

vt. 切碎，撕碎
`to cut or tear something into small thin pieces`



精明的(shrewd)人连小碎纸片(shred)也不会随便丢

# 同义

n. 碎片；少量剩余；最少量；破布
`debris` `chip`

vi. 撕碎
`rip up` `tear to pieces`

# 同根

- n. `shredder` 碎纸机；撕碎者；切菜器

# 短语

- `tear to shreds` ◎撕碎，扯碎

# 例句

Cut the cabbage into fine long shreds.
把这棵卷心菜切成细长条。
