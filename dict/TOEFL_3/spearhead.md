# 音标

- 英 `/'spɪrhed/`
- 美 `/'spɪr'hɛd/`

# 词义

n. 先锋，前锋，先头部队
`a person or group of people who lead an attack or organized action`

vt. 为…作先锋；带头做
`to lead an attack or organized action`



spear(矛) + head(头) → 矛头 → 先锋

# 同义

n. 矛头；先锋；先锋部队
`vanguard` `pioneer`

vt. 带头；做先锋
`take a lead` `tape head`

# 例句

the troops who spearheaded the rescue mission
在救援任务中充当先锋的部队
