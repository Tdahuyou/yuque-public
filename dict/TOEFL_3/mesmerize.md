# 音标

- 英 `/'mezməraɪz/`
- 美 `/'mɛzməraɪz/`

# 词义

vt. 施催眠术；使入迷，迷住
`if you are mesmerized by someone or something, you cannot stop watching them or listening to them because they are so attractive or have such a powerful effect`

# 同义

vt. 施催眠术；迷住；以魅力迫使
`possess` `spell`

# 同根

- adj. `mesmerized` 着迷的
- adj. `mesmeric` 催眠术的
- n. `mesmerism` 催眠术；催眠状态；迷惑
- n. `mesmerist` 施催眠术者；催眠师
- v. `mesmerized` 施催眠术，迷住，迷惑（mesmerize的过去式）
- vt. `mesmerise` 施以催眠术；使…入迷；使…目瞪口呆

# 例句

The first time I saw Diana I was mesmerized by her beauty.
我第一次见到戴安娜就被她的美貌迷住了。
