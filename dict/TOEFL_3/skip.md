# 音标

- 英 `/skɪp/`
- 美 `/skɪp/`

# 词义

v. 跳；跳过，略过，漏过；不出席
`to not do something that you usually do or that you should do`

n. 跳；跳过
`a skipping movement`

# 同义

vi. 跳跃；跳绳；遗漏；跳读
`dance` `to leap`

vt. 跳过；遗漏
`jump` `clear`

n. 跳跃；跳读
`jump` `spring`

# 短语

- `skip over` 略过；遗漏；作短期旅行
- `skip rope` 跳绳

# 例句

She skipped lunch in order to go shopping.
为了去购物，她没有吃午饭。

Williams skipped the game to be with his wife in the hospital.
威廉斯为了在医院里陪他妻子而放弃了这场比赛。
