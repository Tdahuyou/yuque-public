# 音标

- 英 `/'poudiəm/`
- 美 `/'podɪəm/`

# 词义

n. 讲坛，讲台；指挥台
`a small raised area for a performer, speaker, or musical  conductor  to stand on`



pod(=ped 脚) + ium → 立足之处 → 讲坛

# 同义

n. [交]乐队指挥台；矮墙；[建]墩座墙
`sunk fence`

# 例句

Unsteadily he mounted the podium, adjusted the microphone, coughed, and went completely blank.
他摇摇晃晃地走上讲台，调好麦克风，咳嗽了几声，接着脑子就一片空白。
