# 音标

- 英 `/'rævɪdʒ/`
- 美 `/'rævɪdʒ/`

# 词义

vt. 毁坏；抢劫，掠夺
`A town, country, or economy that has been ravaged is one that has been damaged so much that it is almost completely destroyed`



rav(抓， 拿) + age → 抢夺 → 毁坏； 掠夺

# 同义

vt. 毁坏；破坏；掠夺
`destroy` `harry` `do for`

n. 蹂躏，破坏
`destruction` `breaking` `blasting`

vi. 毁坏；掠夺
`do for` `play havoc with`

# 同根

- adj. `raven` 乌黑的
- n. `raven` 掠夺，劫掠；大乌鸦
- vi. `raven` 掠夺；狼吞虎咽
- vt. `raven` 掠夺；狼吞虎咽

# 例句

The country has been ravaged by civil war.
这个国家被内战摧毁了。
