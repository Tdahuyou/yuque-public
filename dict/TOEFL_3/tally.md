# 音标

- 英 `/'tæli/`
- 美 `/'tæli/`

# 词义

v. 吻合
`if numbers or statements tally, they match exactly`

n. 记录；账
`a record of how much you have spent, won etc by a particular point in time`

# 同义

n. 计数器；标签；记账
`counter` `ticket` `label`

vt. 使符合；计算；记录
`cast` `register` `figure` `shape`

vi. 一致；记分
`cotton` `square` `identify` `score` `coincide`

# 同根

- adj. `tall` 高的；长的；过分的；夸大的
- adv. `tall` 夸大地
- n. `tallyman` 以分期方式售货的商人；装卸货计数人；带样品卖货的人

# 短语

- `tally with` 与…相符合
- `tally up` 总结；加一；结算
- `medal tally` 奖牌总数

# 例句

Some of the records held by the accounts departments did not tally.
会计部门的一些账目有出入。
