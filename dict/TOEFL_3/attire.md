# 音标

- 英 `/ə'taɪər/`
- 美 `/ə'taiə/`

# 词义

n. 穿着，服饰
`Your attire is the clothes you are wearing`

vt. 给…穿衣，打扮




at + tire(疲劳) → 穿得太多， 累 → 穿着， 服饰

# 同义

n. 服装；盛装
`clothing` `wear` `garment` `apparel`

vt. 打扮；使穿衣
`dress oneself` `habit` `array`

# 例句

...seven women dressed in their finest attire.
...七位着盛装的女士。
