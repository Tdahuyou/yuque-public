# 音标

- 英 `/ˌsemi'ɑːtɪks/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 符号学
`the way in which people communicate through signs and images, or the study of this`



semi(符号) + ot + ics(…学) → 符号学

# 同义

n. [语]记号语言学；[临床]症状学
`semeiology`

# 同根

- adj. `semiotic` 症状的；符号学的；记号语言的
- n. `semiology` 记号学；记号语言；[临床] 症状学

# 例句

This paper approaches English euphemisms from Peirce s semiotics.
英语委婉语作为一种语言符号，也具有这些性质。

With the aid of concepts in topology such as space, region, boundary and displacement, Lotman described the space structure of cultural semiotics.
洛特曼借助拓扑学中空间、域、界、移等概念，对文化符号域的空间结构进行了描述。
