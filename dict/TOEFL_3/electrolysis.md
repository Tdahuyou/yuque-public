# 音标

- 英 `/ɪˌlek'trɑːləsɪs/`
- 美 `/ɪ,lɛk'trɑləsɪs/`

# 词义

n. 电解作用；电解术
`the process of separating a liquid into its chemical parts by passing an electric current through it`



electro(电流) + lys(松开) + is → 用电来进行分解 → 电解作用

# 同义

n. [化工][化学]电解，电解作用；以电针除痣
`electrolytic action`

# 同根

- adj. `electrolytic` 电解的；电解质的；由电解产生的
- n. `electrolyte` 电解液，电解质；电解
- vt. `electrocute` （美）以电椅处死；使触电致死

# 短语

- `water electrolysis` 水电解

# 例句

Instead, he says, the solution to large-scale hydrogen production lies in using renewable electricity to extract hydrogen from water via electrolysis.
相反他认为大规模生产氢的解决方案在于使用可更新的电通过电解方式从水中提取氢。
