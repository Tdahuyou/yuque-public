# 音标

- 英 `/træns/`
- 美 `/træns/`

# 词义

n. 恍惚；昏睡状态
`a state in which you behave as if you were asleep but are still able to hear and understand what is said to you`

# 同义

n. 恍惚；出神；着迷，入迷
`possession` `absentmindedness`

vt. 使恍惚；使发呆
`bemuse`

# 例句

Like a man in a trance, Blake found his way back to his rooms.
像一个处于催眠状态的人，布莱克回到了自己的房间。
