# 音标

- 英 `/kwɑː'druːpl/`
- 美 `/kwɑ'drupl/`

# 词义

v. 成四倍
`to increase and become four times as big or as high, or to make something increase in this way`

adj. 四部分组成的；四倍的
`four times as big or as many`

n. 四倍




quadr(四) + u + ple(折叠) → 折叠四次 → 四倍的

# 同义

adj. [数]四倍的；四重的
`fourfold`

# 同根

- adj. `quadruplicate` 四倍的；四次方的
- n. `quadruplicate` 一式四份
- n. `quadrupling` 四倍
- v. `quadrupling` 使成四倍（quadruple的ing形式）
- vt. `quadruplicate` 使成四倍

# 例句

Food prices quadrupled during the war.
战争期间食品价格翻了两番。

The company has quadrupled its profits in just three years.
公司利润在短短三年里增长至原来的四倍。
