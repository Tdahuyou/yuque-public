# 音标

- 英 `/'lɔːsuːt/`
- 美 `/'lɔsut/`

# 词义

n. 诉讼
`a problem or complaint that a person or organization brings to a court of law to be settled`



law(法律) + suit(诉讼) → 诉讼

# 同义

n. 诉讼（尤指非刑事案件）；诉讼案件
`litigation` `suit` `law`

# 例句

The dispute culminated last week in a lawsuit against the government.
这场针对政府诉讼案的争论在上星期达到了高潮。
