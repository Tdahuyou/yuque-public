# 音标

- 英 `/gurd/`
- 美 `/ɡʊrd/`

# 词义

n. 葫芦；葫芦制成的容器
`a round fruit whose outer shell can be used as a container, or the container made from this fruit`

# 同义

n. 葫芦，葫芦属植物；脑瓜
`cucurbit` `calabash`

# 短语

- `bitter gourd` 苦瓜，苦味葫芦
- `wax gourd` 冬瓜
- `towel gourd` 丝瓜
- `white gourd` n. [植]冬瓜
- `bottle gourd` 葫芦；瓢葫芦；葫芦瓜
- `sponge gourd` n. 丝瓜
