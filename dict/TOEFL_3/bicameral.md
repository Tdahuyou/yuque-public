# 音标

- 英 `/ˌbaɪ'kæmərəl/`
- 美 `/,baɪ'kæmərəl/`

# 词义

adj. 两院制的，有两个议院的
`a bicameral  legislature (=  part of the government that makes laws  )  consists of two parts, such as the Senate and the House of Representatives in the US Congress`

# 同根

- adj. `bicipital` 二头肌的；有两头的
