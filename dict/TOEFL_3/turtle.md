# 音标

- 英 `/'tɜːrtl/`
- 美 `/'tɝtl/`

# 词义

n. 龟，海龟
`a  reptile  that lives mainly in water and has a soft body covered by a hard shell`



“特逗” → 龟(turtle)的样子特逗 → 龟

# 同义

n. [脊椎]龟，甲鱼；海龟
`tortoise` `chelonian`

# 短语

- `sea turtle` 海龟
- `turtle shell` 龟壳；鳖甲
- `a little turtle` 一只小海龟
- `snapping turtle` n. 啮龟；麝香鳖
- `green turtle` 甲鱼；绿海龟
- `teenage mutant ninja turtle` 忍者神龟（美国动画电影）

# 例句

...a pet turtle.
…一只宠物龟。
