# 音标

- 英 `/klʌmp/`
- 美 `/klʌmp/`

# 词义

v. 聚集，成群；以沉重的脚步行走
`to walk with slow noisy steps`

n. 丛，簇，束；块，团；群，组
`a group of trees, bushes, or other plants growing very close together`

# 同义

n. 丛；笨重的脚步声；土块
`cluster` `plexus`

vt. 使成一丛；使凝结成块
`clot`

# 例句

Hair and soap had clumped together in the drain.
头发和肥皂在排水管里结成了团。
