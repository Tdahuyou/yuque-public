# 音标

- 英 `/smɜːrk/`
- 美 `/smɝk/`

# 词义

vi. 假笑，得意地笑
`to smile in an unpleasant way that shows that you are pleased by someone else’s bad luck or think you are better than other people`



smi(le)(微笑) + rk(看做clerk， 店员) → 店员的微笑 → 假笑

# 同义

n. 傻笑；假笑；得意的笑
`horselaugh` `simper`

vi. 傻笑；假笑
`giggle`

# 例句

The boys tried not to smirk.
男孩们强忍住得意的笑。
