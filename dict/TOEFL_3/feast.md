# 音标

- 英 `/fiːst/`
- 美 `/fist/`

# 词义

v. 尽情享用
`to eat a lot of a particular food with great enjoyment`

n. 节日；盛宴，宴会
`a large meal where a lot of people celebrate a special occasion`



太阳刚从东方(east)跳出， 盛宴(feast)就开始了

# 同义

vt. 享受；款待，宴请
`enjoy` `guest`

n. 筵席，宴会；节日
`festival` `holiday`

vi. 享受；参加宴会
`banquet`

# 短语

- `feast on` 尽情欣赏；尽情享受；大吃大喝
- `feast day` n. 节日；斋日；宗教节日
- `wedding feast` n. 喜筵

# 例句

We feasted on chicken and roast potatoes.
我们饱餐了一顿鸡肉和烤土豆。
