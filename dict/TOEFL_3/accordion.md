# 音标

- 英 `/ə'kɔːrdiən/`
- 美 `/ə'kɔrdɪən/`

# 词义

n. 手风琴
`a musical instrument like a large box that you hold in both hands. You play it by pressing the sides together and pulling them out again, while you push buttons and  key s  .`



来自accord(n./vi. 一致， 调和)

# 同义

adj. 可折叠的
`folding` `collapsable`

n. 手风琴
`squeezebox`

# 同根

- n. `accordionist` 手风琴师
