# 音标

- 英 `/'peɪtriɑːrki/`
- 美 `/'petrɪɑrki/`

# 词义

n. 父权制，父系社会，家长统治
`a social system in which the oldest man rules his family and passes power and possessions on to his sons`

# 同根

- adj. `patriarchal` 家长的；族长的；由族长统治的
- adj. `patriarchic` 族长的；家长的
- adj. `patrilineal` 父系的
- n. `patriarch` 家长；族长；元老；创始人

# 例句

The main cause of women's and children's oppression is patriarchy.
妇女及儿童受欺压的主要根源在于父权制度。
