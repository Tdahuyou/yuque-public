# 音标

- 英 `/uːz/`
- 美 `/uz/`

# 词义

v. 渗出，慢慢流出；洋溢着，充满
`if a thick liquid oozes from something or if something oozes a thick liquid, that liquid flows from it very slowly`

n. 泥浆；缓慢渗出
`a slow flowing or leaking `



oo(形似泥浆冒的泡泡) + ze → 渗出； 泥浆

# 同义

vt. 渗出；泄漏
`leak out` `uncork`

vi. 渗出；泄漏
`leak out` `seep`

n. [地质]软泥
`slud`

# 同根

- adj. `oozy` 渗出的；软泥的

# 例句

A cut on his cheek was still oozing blood.
他脸上的伤口还在渗血。
