# 音标

- 英 `/fə'mɪliəraɪz/`
- 美 `/fə'mɪlɪəraɪz/`

# 词义

vt. 使熟悉
`to learn about something so that you understand it, or to teach someone else about something so that they understand it`



来自familiar(a. 熟悉的)

# 同义

vt. 使熟悉
`acquaint sb. with`

# 同根

- adj. `familiar` 熟悉的；常见的；亲近的
- n. `familiar` 常客；密友
- n. `familiarity` 熟悉，精通；亲密；随便
- n. `familiarization` 熟悉，精通；亲密
- vt. `familiarise` 使熟悉（等于familiarize）

# 短语

- `familiarize oneself with` 使自己熟悉某物

# 例句

Employees must familiarize themselves with the health and safety manual.
雇员必须熟悉健康和安全手册。
