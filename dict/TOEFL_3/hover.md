# 音标

- 英 `/'hʌvər/`
- 美 `/'hʌvɚ/`

# 词义

vi. 徘徊，彷徨；翱翔，盘旋
`To hover means to stay in the same position in the air without moving forward or backward. Many birds and insects can hover by moving their wings very quickly. `



在爱人(lover)身边徘徊(hover)

# 同义

vi. 盘旋，翱翔；徘徊
`plane` `orbit`

n. 徘徊；盘旋；犹豫
`vibration` `spinning around`

vt. 孵；徘徊在…近旁
`brood` `hatch`

# 短语

- `hover over` 在…盘旋

# 例句

Beautiful butterflies hovered above the wild flowers.
美丽的蝴蝶在野花上方盘旋。
