# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'rævl/`
- 美 `/ʌn'rævl/`

# 词义

v. 澄清；解体，瓦解；解开
`if a system, plan, organization etc unravels, it starts to fail`



un(解开) + ravel(纠缠) → 解开纠缠 → 澄清

# 同义

vt. 解开；阐明；解决；拆散
`decide` `settle` `illustrate`

vi. 解决；散开
`settle` `work out` `figure out`

# 例句

The company started to unravel when two of the directors were arrested.
两名董事被捕以后，公司开始分崩离析。
