# 音标

- 英 `/ə'genst/`
- 美 `/əˈɡɛnst;əˈɡenst/`

# 词义

prep. 逆，反；违反；对…不利；紧靠，倚在；与…对照，对比；用…抵付；用…交换；防备，预防
`used to say who someone is competing with or trying to defeat in a game, battle etc`



again(又， 再) + st → 再一次反对 → 反(对)

# 同义

prep. 反对，违反；靠；倚；防备
`in opposition to` `in contravention of`

adj. 不利的；对立的
`adverse` `contrary` `opposite` `disadvantageous`

# 短语

- `up against` 面临
- `go against` 不利于；违反；反对
- `over against` 在…对面；正对着；与…相反；与…形成对照
- `as against` 与…相对照；比…
- `back against` 背朝着

# 例句

Gambotti was injured in last Saturday’s game against the Lions.
甘博蒂在上周六与雄狮队的比赛中受了伤。

We’ll be competing against the best companies in Europe.
我们要和欧洲一些顶级的公司竞争。
