# 音标

- 英 `/in'sted/`
- 美 `/ɪn'stɛd/`

# 词义

adv. 代替，顶替，作为替代；反而，却
`used to say what is done, when you have just said that a particular thing is not done`

# 短语

- `instead of` 代替；不是…而是…

# 例句

Geoff didn’t study law. Instead, he decided to become an actor.
杰夫没学法律，相反，他决定当演员。

If Jo can’t attend the meeting, I could go instead.
如果乔不能去开会，我可以代他去。
