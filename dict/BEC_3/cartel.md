# 音标

- 英 `/kɑː'tel/`
- 美 `/kɑr'tɛl/`

# 词义

n. 卡特尔；联合企业
`a group of people or companies who agree to sell something at a particular price in order to prevent competition and increase profits`

# 同义

n. 卡特尔；企业联合；垄断联盟；俘虏交换条约
`kartell`

# 短语

- `drug cartel` 贩毒集团

# 例句

an illegal drug cartel
非法贩毒集团
