# 音标

- 英 `/lɔdʒ/`
- 美 `/lɑdʒ/`

# 词义

vi. 暂住，借宿
`to pay to live in a room in someone’s house`

vt. 供…以临时住宿
`If an object lodges somewhere, it becomes stuck there`

n. 乡间小屋，旅舍；寄宿者，投宿者
`a small house on the land of a large country house, usually at the main entrance gate`

# 同义

n. 旅馆；门房；集会处；山林小屋
`hotel` `fonda`

vt. 提出；寄存；借住；嵌入
`introduce` `present` `prefer` `file` `advance`

vi. 寄宿；临时住宿
`board` `guest`

# 同根

- n. `lodging` 寄宿；寄宿处；出租的房间、住房
- n. `lodgement` 住宿；寄存；沉积（等于lodgment）
- n. `lodger` 房客；寄宿人；投宿者

# 短语

- `lodge a claim` 提出索赔
- `lodge a complaint` 投诉；对…提出控告

# 例句

a building used to lodge prisoners of war
一座关押战俘的建筑物

The bullet lodged in the sergeant's leg, shattering his thigh bone.
子弹射入这个军士的腿部，击碎了他的大腿骨。
