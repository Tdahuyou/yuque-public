# 音标

- 英 `/'weistidʒ/`
- 美 `/'westɪdʒ/`

# 词义

n. 废物；消耗；商品损耗率；人员浪费
`when something is lost or destroyed, especially in a way that is not useful or reasonable, or the amount that is lost or destroyed`

# 同义

n. 损耗；消瘦；衰老
`wear` `dissipation`

# 例句

The system used to result in a great deal of food wastage.
这种做法过去造成了大量的食物浪费。
