# 音标

- 英 `/'skeipgəut/`
- 美 `/'skepɡot/`

# 词义

n. 替罪羊，代人受过的人
`someone who is blamed for something bad that happens, even if it is not their fault`

# 同义

n. 替罪羊，替人顶罪者；替身
`goat` `stand-in`

# 例句

I don't think I deserve to be made the scapegoat for a couple of bad results.
我认为我不该为出现的一些不良后果充当替罪羊。
