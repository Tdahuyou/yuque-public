# 音标

- 英 `/kən'spaiə/`
- 美 `/kən'spaɪɚ/`

# 词义

vi. 密谋；合力，协作
`If two or more people or groups conspire to do something illegal or harmful, they make a secret agreement to do it`



con(共同) + spir(呼吸) + e → 共同呼吸 → 密谋

# 同义

vi. [法]共谋；协力
`cooperate` `concert`

# 同根

- n. `conspiracy` 阴谋；共谋；阴谋集团

# 例句

They'd conspired to overthrow the government.
他们曾合谋推翻政府。

...a defendant accused of conspiring with his brother to commit robberies.
…被指控与其兄弟合谋抢劫的一名被告。
