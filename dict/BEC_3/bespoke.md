# 音标

- 英 `/bi'spəuk/`
- 美 `/bɪ'spok/`

# 词义

adj. 订货的；定做的；预约的
`a bespoke product, especially computer software or a piece of clothing, has been specially made for a particular customer`

# 同义

adj. 定做的；预定的
`custom` `scheduled`

vt. 预约，显示出
`reserve` `preengage`

# 例句

the cost of development of a bespoke system
定制系统的开发成本
