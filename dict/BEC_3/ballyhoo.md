# 音标

- 英 `/ˌbæli'huː; 'bæliˌhuː/`
- 美 `/'bælɪhu/`

# 词义

n. 招揽生意的广告；大吹大擂
`when there is a lot of excitement or anger about something – used to show disapproval`

# 同义

n. 大肆宣传；大吹大擂
`hype` `blurb`

vi. 大肆宣传；大吹大擂
`crow one's head off`

vt. 大肆宣传；大吹大擂
`crow one's head off` `hype`

# 例句

After all the ballyhoo, the film was a flop.
尽管炒作得厉害，这部电影还是不卖座。
