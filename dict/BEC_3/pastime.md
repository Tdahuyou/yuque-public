# 音标

- 英 `/'pɑːstaim/`
- 美 `/'pæstaɪm/`

# 词义

n. 消遣，娱乐
`something that you do because you think it is enjoyable or interesting`



pas(看作past， 过去的) + time(时光) → 让时光过去 → 消磨时光 → 消遣

# 同义

n. 娱乐，消遣
`entertainment` `recreation` `pleasure` `sport` `occupation`

# 例句

Reading was her favourite pastime.
阅读是她最喜爱的消遣方式。
