# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdisə'pɔintmənt/`
- 美 `/,dɪsə'pɔɪntmənt/`

# 词义

n. 失望
`a feeling of unhappiness because something is not as good as you expected, or has not happened in the way you hoped`



disappoint(使失望) + ment(名词后缀) → 失望

# 同义

n. 失望；沮丧
`depression` `sell`

# 同根

- adj. `disappointed` 失望的，沮丧的；受挫折的
- adj. `disappointing` 令人失望的；令人扫兴的
- adv. `disappointedly` 失望地
- adv. `disappointingly` 令人失望地
- v. `disappointing` 令人失望（disappoint的ing形式）；辜负…的期望
- vt. `disappoint` 使失望

# 例句

He could see the disappointment in her eyes.
他看到了她眼中的失望。

Several people expressed disappointment at the delay.
有好几个人对这次延误表示失望。

She hid her bitter disappointment .
她把深深的沮丧掩饰起来。
