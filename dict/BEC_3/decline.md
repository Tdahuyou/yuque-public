# 音标

- 英 `/di'klain/`
- 美 `/dɪ'klaɪn/`

# 词义

vi. 减少，下降；衰落
`to decrease in quantity or importance`

vt. 婉言拒绝
`to say no politely when someone invites you somewhere, offers you something, or wants you to do something`

n. 下降，减少；谢绝，拒绝；斜坡
`If there is a decline in something, it becomes less in quantity, importance, or quality`



de(向下) + cline(倾斜) → 向下倾斜 → 下降； 衰落

# 同义

n. 下降；衰退；斜面
`reduction` `recession` `fall`

vi. 下降；衰落；谢绝
`go down` `slump`

vt. 谢绝；婉拒
`excuse oneself from`

# 同根

- n. `declination` 倾斜；偏差；衰微

# 短语

- `on the decline` 在走下坡路；在衰退中
- `decline and fall` 衰亡，衰败
- `in decline` 在衰退中
- `economic decline` 经济衰退
- `rate of decline` 递减速度
- `decline stage` 衰退期；下降阶段

# 例句

Spending on information technology has declined.
用于信息技术的支出减少了。

Car sales have declined by a quarter.
汽车销量下滑了四分之一。

After the war, the city declined in importance.
战后这座城市的重要性下降了。

Official figures show a sharp decline in the number of foreign tourists.
官方数字显示外国游客数量骤降。
