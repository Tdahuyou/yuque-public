# 音标

- 英 `/lə'dʒistiks/`
- 美 `/lə'dʒɪstɪks/`

# 词义

n. 物流；后勤
`the practical arrangements that are needed in order to make a plan that involves a lot of people and equipment successful`

# 同义

n. [军]后勤；后勤学
`office support`

# 同根

- adj. `logistic` 后勤学的；[数] 符号逻辑的
- n. `logistician` 物流师；军需官；后勤人员
