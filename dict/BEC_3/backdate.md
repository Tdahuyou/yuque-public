# 音标

- 英 `/ˌbæk'deit/`
- 美 `/'bæk'det/`

# 词义

v. 倒填日期；回溯
`to write an earlier date on a document or cheque than when it was actually written`



back(早过去的) + date(日期) → 把发生的日期说成比实际的更早 → 回溯

# 同义

vt. 回溯，追溯；倒填日期
`remount to` `go back`

# 例句

The contract that was signed on Thursday morning was backdated to March 11.
星期四上午所签的合同从3月11日起生效。
