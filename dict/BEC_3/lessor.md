# 音标

- 英 `/le'sɔː/`
- 美 `/lɛ'sɔr/`

# 词义

n. 出租人
`someone who allows someone else to use their house, building, land etc for a period of time for payment`

# 同义

n. [租赁]出租人
`leaser`

# 同根

- n. `lessee` 承租人
