# 音标

- 英 `/ˌəuvə'pei/`
- 美 `/,əuvə'pei/`

# 词义

vi. 付得过多
`to pay someone more money than they deserve`

vt. 多付
`to pay someone more money than they deserve`



over(过度) + pay(付钱) → 付得过多

# 同根

- n. `overpayment` 多付的款项

# 例句

Most big companies continue to overpay their top executives.
多数大公司继续给高管支付过高的薪水。
