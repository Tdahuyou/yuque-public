# 音标

- 英 `/həːt/`
- 美 `/hɝ​t/`

# 词义

vi. 痛，受痛苦
`to feel pain in part of your body`

vt. 使受伤，弄痛；伤…的感情；危害；损害
`to injure yourself or someone else`

n. 伤害
`a feeling of great unhappiness because someone, especially someone you trust, has treated you badly or unfairly`

# 同义

vt. 使受伤；损害；使疼痛；使痛心
`wound` `blast`

vi. 感到疼痛；有坏处；带来痛苦
`pain`

n. 痛苦；危害；痛苦的原因
`smart` `painfulness`

adj. 受伤的；痛苦的；受损的
`injured` `painful` `wounded` `bitter`

# 同根

- adj. `hurting` 受伤害的；受损的
- adj. `hurtful` 造成损害的
- v. `hurting` 伤害；伤心；弄痛（hurt的ing形式）

# 短语

- `get hurt` 受伤
- `feel hurt` 感到不快
- `hurt badly` 头痛的厉害，严重受伤

# 例句

My back hurts.
我背痛。

Where does it hurt?
哪儿痛？

It hurts when I try to move my leg.
我的腿一动就痛。
