# 音标

- 英 `/'kjuːdɔs/`
- 美 `/'kudɑs/`

# 词义

n. 名望，荣誉，声誉
`the state of being admired and respected for being important or for doing something important`



因为做(do)善(kind)事赢得声誉(kudos)

# 同义

n. 荣誉；名望；称赞
`honor` `glory` `praise` `fame`

# 例句

He acquired kudos just by appearing on television.
他上电视后就出名了。
