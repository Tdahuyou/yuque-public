# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃəuruːm/`
- 美 `/'ʃorʊm/`

# 词义

n. 展厅
`A showroom is a shop in which goods are displayed for sale, especially goods such as cars or electrical or gas appliances`

# 同义

n. 陈列室；样品间
`exhibition room`

# 同根

- n. `showing` 放映；表演；陈列
- v. `showing` 展示（show的ing形式）

# 例句

...a car showroom.
…汽车展销店。
