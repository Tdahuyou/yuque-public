# 音标

- 英 `/ə'priːʃieit/`
- 美 `/ə'priʃɪet/`

# 词义

vi. 增值
`to gradually become more valuable over a period of time`

vt. 重视；为…表示感激；意识到
`used to thank someone in a polite way or to say that you are grateful for something they have done`



ap + preci(价值) + ate → 给以价值 → 重视

# 同义

vt. 欣赏；感激；领会；鉴别
`enjoy` `see` `grasp`

vi. 增值；涨价
`value added` `rise in price`

# 同根

- adj. `appreciative` 感激的；赏识的；有欣赏力的；承认有价值的
- adv. `appreciatively` 欣赏地，赞赏地；感激地
- n. `appreciation` 欣赏，鉴别；增值；感谢

# 例句

Most investments are expected to appreciate at a steady rate.
大多数投资都应该会平稳增值。
