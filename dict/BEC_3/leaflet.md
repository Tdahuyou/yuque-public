# 音标

- 英 `/'liːflit/`
- 美 `/'liflət/`

# 词义

v. 散发传单
`to give leaflets to people`

n. 传单，散页印刷品，小册子
`a small book or piece of paper advertising something or giving information on a particular subject`



leaf(树叶) + let → 像一片树叶大小的传单 → 传单

# 同义

n. [植]小叶；传单
`circular` `lobule`

# 例句

He’s leafleting the neighbourhood.
他在向街区居民散发传单。
