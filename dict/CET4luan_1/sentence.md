# 音标

- 英 `/'sentəns/`
- 美 `/'sɛntəns/`

# 词义

n. 判决
`a punishment that a judge gives to someone who is guilty of a crime`

v. 判决
`if a judge sentences someone who is guilty of a crime, they give them a punishment`

# 同义

n. [语][计]句子，命题；[法]宣判，判决
`award` `proposition`

vt. 判决，[法]宣判
`determine` `adjudge`

# 同根

- adj. `sentential` 句子的；判决的

# 例句

She received an eight-year prison sentence.
她被判八年徒刑。

He has just begun a life sentence for murder.
他犯了谋杀罪，刚开始服无期徒刑。

Sanchez was sentenced to three years in prison.
桑切斯被判了三年监禁。
