# 音标

- 英 `/'remədɪ/`
- 美 `/'rɛmədi/`

# 词义

n. 药物
`a medicine to cure an illness or pain that is not very serious`



re(表加强) ＋ med(治疗) ＋ y → 医治， 治疗

# 同义

vt. 补救；治疗；纠正
`cure` `help` `repair`

n. 补救；治疗；赔偿
`therapy` `satisfaction` `cure`

# 同根

- adj. `remedial` 治疗的；补救的；矫正的
- adj. `remediable` 可治疗的；可矫正的；可补救的
- n. `remediation` 补救；矫正；补习

# 短语

- `remedy for` 补救，解决；对…治疗法
- `sovereign remedy` ◎灵药；特效药
- `legal remedy` 法律补偿
- `folk remedy` 偏方

# 例句

a home remedy (= one that you make at home ) for sore throats
治疗喉咙疼痛的家庭疗法

The herb is used as a folk remedy (= a traditional medicine, rather than one a doctor gives you ) for a baby’s teething pains.
这种草药是治疗幼儿出牙疼痛的民间药方。
