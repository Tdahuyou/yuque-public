# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'sweɪd/`
- 美 `/dɪ'swed/`

# 词义

v. 劝阻，劝止
`to persuade someone not to do something`



dis(不) + suade(敦促) → 敦促某人不做 → 劝阻

# 同义

vt. 劝阻，劝止
`discourage from` `dehort`

# 同根

- adj. `dissuasive` 劝诫的，劝阻的
- n. `dissuasion` 劝阻；劝戒；谏诤

# 例句

Doctors had tried to dissuade patients from smoking.
医生曾试图说服病人不要抽烟。
