# 音标

- 英 `/'nɒkdaʊn/`
- 美 `/'nɑk,daʊn/`

# 词义

adj. 击倒的；可拆卸的；廉价的
`A knockdown price is much lower than it would be normally`

n. 把人打倒的一击；可拆卸的东西；降价
`when a boxer falls down when he is hit`

# 同义

adj. 击倒的；[机]可拆卸的；廉价的
`budget` `dismountable`

# 例句

...the chance to buy it now at a knockdown price.
...现在以低价购买它的机会。
