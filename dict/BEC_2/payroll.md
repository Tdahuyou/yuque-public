# 音标

- 英 `/'peɪrəʊl/`
- 美 `/'perol/`

# 词义

n. 工资单；在册职工人数；工资名单
`The people on the payroll of a company or an organization are the people who work for it and are paid by it`



pay(支付) + roll(名单) → 工资表

# 同义

n. [劳经]工资单
`payslip`

# 短语

- `payroll system` 工资管理系统
- `payroll tax` 工资税

# 例句

They had 87,000 employees on the payroll.
他们有87000人在在职人员工薪名册上。
