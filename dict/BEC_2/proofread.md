# 音标

- 英 `/'pruːfriːd/`
- 美 `/'prufrid/`

# 词义

v. 校对，校勘
`to read through something that is written or printed in order to correct any mistakes in it`



proof(验证) + read(阅读) → 一边读一边验证 → 校对

# 同义

vt. [印刷]校对，校勘
`check upon` `collate`

# 同根

- n. `proofreader` 校对者，校对员

# 例句

I didn't even have the chance to proofread my own report.
我甚至没有机会校对自己的报告。
