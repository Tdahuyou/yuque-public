# 音标

- 英 `/'weɪvə/`
- 美 `/'wevɚ/`

# 词义

n. 弃权，放弃；弃权证书
`an official written statement saying that a right, legal process etc can be waived`

# 同义

n. 弃权，[法]放弃；弃权证书
`abstention` `nonuser`

# 同根

- vt. `waive` 放弃；搁置

# 短语

- `tuition waiver` 学费减免

# 例句

...a waiver of constitutional rights.
…一位放弃宪法权利者。
