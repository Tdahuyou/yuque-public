# 音标

- 英 `/'begə/`
- 美 `/'bɛɡɚ/`

# 词义

n. 乞丐
`someone who lives by asking people for food and money`

vt. 使贫穷
`to make someone very poor`

# 同义

n. 乞丐；穷人；家伙
`apple` `customer` `egg` `fellow` `guy`

vt. 使贫穷；使沦为乞丐
`impoverish` `pauperize`

# 同根

- adj. `beggarly` 赤贫的；像乞丐的
- n. `beggary` 赤贫；卑劣；行乞

# 例句

the beggars on the streets
街头乞丐
