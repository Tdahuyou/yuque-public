# 音标

# 词义

v. 用爪子抓，用蹄扒；翻找
`if an animal paws a surface, it touches or rubs one place several times with its paw`

n. 爪子
`an animal’s foot that has nails or  claw s `



我看见(saw)雪地上的爪子(paw)印
