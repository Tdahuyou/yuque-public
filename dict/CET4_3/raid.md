# 音标

- 英 `/reɪd/`
- 美 `/red/`

# 词义

n&vt. 袭击；突入查抄，突入搜捕；劫掠
`a short attack on a place by soldiers, planes, or ships, intended to cause damage but not take control`



大雨(rain)突袭(raid)， 收衣服啦

# 同义

n. [军]袭击；突袭；搜捕；抢劫
`robbery` `assault`

vt. [军]袭击，突袭
`hit` `descend on`

# 同根

- n. `raider` 袭击者；侵入者

# 短语

- `air raid` 空袭
