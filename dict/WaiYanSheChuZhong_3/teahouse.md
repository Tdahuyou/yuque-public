# 音标

- 英 `/'tiːhaʊs/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. （尤指亚洲的）茶馆
`a special house in China or Japan where tea is served, often as part of a ceremony`

# 同义

n. 茶馆；茶室
`teashop` `tearoom`

# 例句

Another dust-up took place in the teahouse.
茶馆里又发生了一场打斗。

Do you know that this restaurant used to be the teahouse where we first met?
你知道吗，这个餐馆就是我们第一次见面的那个茶馆呢。
