# 音标

- 英 `/lʌn(d)ʒ/`
- 美 `/lʌndʒ/`

# 词义

n. 刺
`a thrust made by putting one foot forward and making the back leg straight`

v. 越近，前冲
`If you lunge in a particular direction, you move in that direction suddenly and clumsily`



向长沙发(lounge)直扑(lunge)过去

# 同义

vt. 刺；戳；使前冲
`stick` `pink`

vi. 刺；突进
`run through` `poke`
