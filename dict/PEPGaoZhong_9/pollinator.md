# 词义

n. 虫媒
`A pollinator is something that pollinates plants, especially a type of insect.`

# 同根

- n. `pollen` [植] 花粉
- n. `pollination` [农学] 授粉（作用）
- n. `pollinium` [植] 花粉块
- n. `pollinosis` 枯草热，花粉病
- vt. `pollen` 传授花粉给
- vt. `pollinate` 对...授粉
- vt. `pollenate` 给…授花粉

# 例句

“We’re the first to cultivate this bee and the first to provide as much evidence of its potential as a red raspberry and blackberry pollinator,” says Cane.
