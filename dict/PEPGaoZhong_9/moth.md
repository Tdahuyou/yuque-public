# 音标

- 英 `/mɒθ/`
- 美 `/mɔθ/`

# 词义

n. 蛾
`an insect related to the  butterfly  that flies mainly at night and is attracted to lights. Some moths eat holes in cloth.`



“莫死” → 不畏死 → 飞蛾(moth)扑火哪畏死

# 同义

n. [昆]蛾；蛀虫
`miller` `deathwatch beetle`

# 同根

- adj. `mothy` 多蛾的；虫蛀的

# 短语

- `diamondback moth` 小菜蛾
- `silkworm moth` 蚕蛾
- `gypsy moth` 舞毒蛾（等于Porthetria dispar）
- `silk moth` 蚕蛾

# 例句

My husband, an otherwise sensible man, has the regrettable habit of turning on the television each and every morning, like a moth to the flame.
我的丈夫，他在其他方面都很明智，可是有一个令人遗憾的习惯，每一天的早晨，他总是要打开电视机，就像飞蛾扑火那样。
