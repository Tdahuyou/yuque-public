# 音标

- 英 `/'glɑːsɪz/`
- 美 `/'glæsɪz/`

# 词义

`Glasses are two lenses in a frame that some people wear in front of their eyes in order to help them see better`

# 同义

n. [轻]眼镜；玻璃（glass的复数形式）；[光]双筒望远镜
`spectacles` `cheaters`

# 短语

- `wear glasses` 戴眼镜；戴着眼镜
- `a pair of glasses` 一副眼镜
- `dark glasses` 墨镜；深色眼镜
- `presbyopic glasses` 老花镜
- `field glasses` 望远镜；双筒望远镜

# 例句

...the 140 million Americans who wear glasses or contact lenses.
…1.4亿戴眼镜或者隐性眼镜的美国人。
