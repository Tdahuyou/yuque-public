# 音标

- 英 `/'haʊswaɪf/`
- 美 `/'haʊs'waɪf/`

# 词义

n. 家庭主妇
`a married woman who works at home doing the cooking, cleaning etc, but does not have a job outside the house`

# 同义

n. 家庭主妇
`materfamilias` `frow`

# 同根

- adj. `housewifely` 主妇们的；节俭的

# 例句

Married at nineteen, she was a traditional housewife and mother of four children.
她19岁就结婚了，是个传统的家庭主妇和4个孩子的母亲。
