# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'fiːt/`
- 美 `/dɪ'fit/`

# 词义

v. 打败；战胜；使受挫
`to win a victory over someone in a war, competition, game etc`

n. 失败
`failure to win or succeed`



击败(defeat)敌人， 战绩(feat)累累

# 同义

vt. 击败，战胜；挫败；使…失败
`down` `get the best of`

n. 失败；战胜
`failure` `loss` `losing` `reverse` `collapse`

# 同根

- adj. `defeatist` 失败主义（者）的
- n. `defeatist` 失败主义者
- n. `defeatism` 失败主义

# 短语

- `suffer defeat` 遭受失败

# 例句

They hoped to defeat the enemy at sea.
他们希望在海上击败敌人。

She was a woman who hated to admit defeat.
她是个不愿认输的女人。
