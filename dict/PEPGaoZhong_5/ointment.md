# 音标

- 英 `/'ɒɪntm(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'ɔɪntmənt/`

# 词义

n. 药膏；油膏
`a soft cream that you rub into your skin, especially as a medical treatment`



oint(=oil油) + ment → 油膏

# 同义

n. 药膏；[药]油膏
`unguent` `factice`

# 例句

A range of ointments and creams is available for the treatment of eczema.
有一系列的药膏和乳剂可用于湿疹的治疗。
