# 音标

- 英 `/'swəʊlən/`
- 美 `/'swolən/`

# 词义

adj. 肿胀的
`a part of your body that is swollen is bigger than usual, especially because you are ill or injured`

# 同义

adj. 肿胀的，浮肿的；浮夸的；激动兴奋的
`turgid` `tumid`

# 例句

swollen glands
肿胀的腺体

a badly swollen ankle
肿得厉害的脚踝

His eyes were swollen from crying.
他的眼睛哭肿了。
