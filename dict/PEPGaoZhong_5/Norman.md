# 音标

- 英 `/'nɔ:mən/`
- 美 `/'nɔrmən/`

# 词义

n. 诺曼人
`The Normans were the people who came from northern France and took control of England in 1066, or their descendants`

adj. 诺曼的
`built in the style that was popular during the 11th and 12th centuries in Europe`

# 短语

- `norman conquest` [史]诺曼底人对英格兰的军事征服
- `norman bethune` 诺尔曼白求恩
- `norman french` 诺曼法语（中世纪诺曼第及英国使用的法语）

# 例句

a Norman church
诺曼式教堂
