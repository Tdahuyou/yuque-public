# 音标

- 英 `/ˈsʌspekt; (for v.) səˈspekt/`
- 美 `/sʌspɛkt; (for v.) səˈspɛkt/`

# 词义

v. 怀疑
`to think that something is probably true, especially something bad`

n. 被怀疑者；嫌疑犯
`A suspect is a person who the police or authorities think may be guilty of a crime`



sus + pect ( = spect看) → 在下面看一看 → 怀疑

# 同义

n. [法]嫌疑犯
`sus`

adj. 可疑的；不可信的
`doubtful` `questionable`

vt. 怀疑；猜想
`dispute` `imagine` `wonder` `question`

vi. 怀疑；猜想
`wonder` `imagine` `question` `suppose`

# 同根

- adj. `suspected` 有嫌疑的
- adj. `suspensive` 暂停的；悬而不决的；可疑的；暧昧的
- v. `suspected` 怀疑（suspect的过去分词和过去式）

# 短语

- `prime suspect` 嫌疑重犯；主要嫌疑犯
- `suspect of` 怀疑

# 例句

She strongly suspected he was lying to her.
她非常怀疑他在对她撒谎。

She’s not going to be very happy about this, I suspect .
我想对于此事她不会太高兴。

Police have arrested a suspect in a series of killings and sexual assaults in the city.
警方逮捕了该城市一系列杀人强奸案件中的一名嫌疑犯。
