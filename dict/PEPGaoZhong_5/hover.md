# 音标

- 英 `/'hɒvə/`
- 美 `/'hʌvɚ/`

# 词义

v. 盘旋
`if a bird, insect, or  helicopter  hovers, it stays in one place in the air`



徘徊(hover)在情人(lover)之间

# 同义

vi. 盘旋，翱翔；徘徊
`plane` `orbit`

n. 徘徊；盘旋；犹豫
`vibration` `spinning around`

vt. 孵；徘徊在…近旁
`brood` `hatch`

# 短语

- `hover over` 在…盘旋

# 例句

flies hovering above the surface of the water
盘旋在水面上方的苍蝇
