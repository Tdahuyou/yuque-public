# 音标

- 英 `/ʌnfə'getəb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/,ʌnfɚ'ɡɛtəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 难忘的；永远记得的
`an unforgettable experience, sight etc affects you so strongly that you will never forget it, especially because it is particularly good or beautiful`

# 同义

adj. 难忘的
`memorable` `indelible`

# 例句

A visit to the museum is an unforgettable experience.
对该博物馆的参观是一次难忘的经历。
