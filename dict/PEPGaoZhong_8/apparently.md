# 音标

- 英 `/ə'pærəntlɪ/`
- 美 `/ə'pærəntli/`

# 词义

adv. 据说
`used to say that you have heard that something is true, although you are not completely sure about it`

# 同义

adv. 显然地；似乎，表面上
`transparently` `manifestly`

# 同根

- adj. `apparent` 显然的；表面上的

# 例句

Apparently the company is losing a lot of money.
据说该公司目前亏损很大。
