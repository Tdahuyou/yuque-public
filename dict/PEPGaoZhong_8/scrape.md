# 音标

- 英 `/skreɪp/`
- 美 `/skrep/`

# 词义

v. 擦净；削平；磨光
`If something scrapes against something else, it rubs against it, making a noise or causing slight damage`



scrap(碎屑) ＋ e → 碎屑是被刮下来的 → 刮

# 同义

n. 刮掉；擦痕；困境；刮擦声
`difficulty` `corner` `dilemma` `fix`

vt. 刮；擦伤；挖成
`raw` `brush burn`

vi. 刮掉；刮出刺耳声
`shave off`

# 同根

- adj. `scraping` 刮擦的；吝啬的
- n. `scraper` 刮刀；铲土机；守财奴
- n. `scraping` 刮削下的碎屑；刮擦声；抹去
- n. `scraped` 刮掉，刮痕；报废
- v. `scraping` 刮，擦（scrape的现在分词）
- v. `scraped` 刮掉，擦掉（scrape的过去分词）

# 短语

- `scrape together` 东拼西凑
- `scrape up` vt. 积攒；积蓄；凑集
- `scrape off` 刮掉；擦去
- `scrape along` 勉强糊口
- `scrape through` 勉强通过

# 例句

The only sound is that of knives and forks scraping against china.
惟一的声音是刀叉刮擦瓷器的声音。

The car hurtled past us, scraping the wall and screeching to a halt.
那辆汽车从我们身旁疾驰而过，刮擦着墙嘎然停下。
