# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'mɑːk/`
- 美 `/rɪ'mɑrk/`

# 词义

n. 谈论；评述；言论
`something that you say when you express an opinion or say what you have noticed`

v. 谈论；评论；说起
`to say something, especially about something you have just noticed`



re(一再) ＋ mark(做标记) → 一再做标记 → 评论

# 同义

n. 注意；言辞
`regard` `notice` `publicity`

vt. 评论；觉察
`observe on` `to comment on`

vi. 谈论
`talk of` `discourse`

# 同根

- adj. `remarkable` 卓越的；非凡的；值得注意的
- adv. `remarkably` 显著地；非常地；引人注目地

# 短语

- `remark on` 评论；谈论；议论

# 例句

Unfortunately, a local journalist overheard the remark.
不巧一名当地记者无意中听到了这些话。

the chairman’s introductory remarks
主席的开场白

‘This house must be very old,’ he remarked. “
“这幢房子一定很古老了。”他说道。
