# 音标

- 英 `/'ɔːrɒks; 'aʊ-/`
- 美 `/'ɔrɑks/`

# 词义

n. 原牛（古代欧洲野牛，已灭绝）
`a recently extinct member of the cattle tribe, Bos primigenius, that inhabited forests in N Africa, Europe, and SW Asia`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]欧洲野牛
`wisent` `bison`

# 例句

Distinctions between aurochs and American bisons are not substantial.
欧洲野牛和美洲野牛的区别不明显。
