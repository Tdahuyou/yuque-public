# 音标

- 英 `/'kɜːtsɪ/`
- 美 `/'kɝtsi/`

# 词义

v. 行屈膝礼
`if a woman curtsies, she bends her knees with one foot in front of the other as a sign of respect for an important person`

n. （女子行的）屈膝礼
`Curtsy is also a noun`



curt(简略的) + sy → 一种简略的礼节 → 屈膝礼

# 同根

- n. `curtsey` 女人行的鞠躬礼；屈膝礼
- vi. `curtsey` 行屈膝礼

# 例句

We were taught how to curtsy to the Queen.
有人教我们如何向女王行屈膝礼。

She gave a curtsy.
她行了个屈膝礼。
