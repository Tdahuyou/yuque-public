# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'faɪ/`
- 美 `/'difaɪ/`

# 词义

vt. 违抗，反抗；蔑视
`to refuse to obey a law or rule, or refuse to do what someone in authority tells you to do`



de(离开)+fy(信赖)→远离信赖→不服从， (公然)违抗

# 同义

vt. 藐视；公然反抗；挑衅；使落空
`spurn at` `sneeze at`

n. 挑战；对抗
`challenge` `dare` `war` `confrontation`

# 例句

people who openly defy the law
公然抗拒法律的人
