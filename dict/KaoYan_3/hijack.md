# 音标

- 英 `/'haɪdʒæk/`
- 美 `/'haɪdʒæk/`

# 词义

vt. 劫持
`to use violence or threats to take control of a plane, vehicle, or ship`

# 同义

vt. 抢劫；揩油
`rob of` `spoliate`

n. 劫持；威逼；敲诈
`chantage` `extortion`

# 同根

- n. `hijacker` 强盗；抢劫者

# 例句

The airliner was hijacked by a group of terrorists.
该航班被一帮恐怖分子劫持。
