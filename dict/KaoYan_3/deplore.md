# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'plɔː/`
- 美 `/dɪ'plɔr/`

# 词义

vt. 悲悼，哀叹；谴责
`to disapprove of something very strongly and criticize it severely, especially publicly`



de(变坏)+plore(流出)→流露出伤心的情绪→悲悼， 哀叹

# 同义

vt. 谴责；悲悼；哀叹；对…深感遗憾
`condemn` `denounce`

# 同根

- adj. `deplorable` 可叹的；凄惨的
- adv. `deplorably` 悲惨地；可叹地

# 例句

The UN deplored the invasion as a ‘violation of international law’.
联合国谴责这次入侵 “违反了国际法” 。
