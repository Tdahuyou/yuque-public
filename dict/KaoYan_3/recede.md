# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'siːd/`
- 美 `/rɪ'sid/`

# 词义

vi. 退，退去；渐渐远去；向后倾斜，缩A
`if something you can see or hear recedes, it gets further and further away until it disappears`



re(向后)+ced(走)+e→向后走→退

# 同义

vi. 后退；减弱
`lower` `rein back`

vt. 归还
`replace` `return` `restore` `give again`

# 短语

- `recede from` v. 收回；撤回

# 例句

The pain in his head gradually receded.
他的头痛逐渐缓解。
