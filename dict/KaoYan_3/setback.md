# 音标

- 英 `/'setbæk/`
- 美 `/'sɛtbæk/`

# 词义

n. 挫折；倒退；失败
`a problem that delays or prevents progress, or makes things worse than they were`



来自词组set back (阻碍； 使受挫折； 使退步)

# 同义

n. 挫折；退步；逆流
`frustration` `clawback`

# 例句

The team’s hopes of playing in Europe suffered a setback last night.
球队赴欧洲参加比赛的希望昨晚遇到了阻碍。
