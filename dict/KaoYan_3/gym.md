# 音标

- 英 `/dʒɪm'n3ɪziəm/`
- 美 `/dʒɪm/`

# 词义

n. 体育馆，健身房
`a special building or room that has equipment for doing physical exercise`

# 同义

n. 健身房；体育；体育馆
`gymnasium` `physical culture`

# 同根

- adj. `gymnastic` 体操的，体育的
- n. `gymnastics` 体操；体育；体操运动

# 例句

I go to the gym as often as I can.
我一有空就去健身房健身。
