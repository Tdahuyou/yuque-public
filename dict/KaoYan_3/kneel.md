# 音标

- 英 `/niːl/`
- 美 `/nil/`

# 词义

vi. 跪，下跪
`to be in or move into a position where your body is resting on your knees`

# 同义

vi. 跪下，跪
`drop to one's knees` `go down on one's knees`

# 同根

- n. `kneeling` 跪着，跪下
- n. `kneeler` 跪者；跪台；跪拜者；跪垫
- v. `kneeling` 跪着（kneel的ing形式）；跪下

# 短语

- `kneel down` 下跪；跪倒

# 例句

Tom knelt down and patted the dog.
汤姆跪下来轻轻拍了拍那只狗。
