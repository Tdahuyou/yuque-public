# 音标

- 英 `/snætʃ/`
- 美 `/snætʃ/`

# 词义

v. 攫取，抢夺
`to take something away from someone with a quick, often violent, movement`

n. 攫取，抢夺
`when someone quickly takes or steals something`



和catch(v. 抓)一起记

# 同义

n. 抢夺；抓举；小量
`reif` `despoilment`

vt. 夺得；抽空做；及时救助
`capture`

vi. 抢走；很快接受
`reave`

# 同根

- n. `snatcher` 绑架者；抢夺贼

# 短语

- `snatch at` 抓取

# 例句

The thief snatched her purse and ran.
小偷抢了她的钱包就跑。
