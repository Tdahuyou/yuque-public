# 音标

- 英 `/reɪp/`
- 美 `/rep/`

# 词义

vt. 强奸
`to force someone to have sex, especially by using violence`

n. 强奸
`the crime of forcing someone to have sex, especially by using violence`



r+ape(猿)→禽兽之举→强奸

# 同义

n. 强奸，掠夺；[园艺]油菜；葡萄渣
`violation` `cole`

vt. 强奸；掠夺，抢夺
`harry` `despoil`

# 同根

- adj. `rapacious` 贪婪的；掠夺的
- n. `rapist` 强奸犯
- n. `raper` 强奸犯
- n. `rapacity` 贪婪；掠夺
- n. `rapeseed` 油菜籽

# 短语

- `rape oil` n. 菜油
- `rape seed` 油菜籽
- `gang rape` 轮奸
- `statutory rape` 法定强奸罪

# 例句

She had been raped and stabbed.
她遭到强奸，还被刀捅了。
