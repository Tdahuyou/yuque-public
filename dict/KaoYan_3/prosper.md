# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒspə/`
- 美 `/'prɑspɚ/`

# 词义

v. 繁荣；使成功
`If people or businesses prosper, they are successful and do well`



pro(向前)+sper(希望)→充满发展的希望→繁荣

# 同义

vi. 繁荣，昌盛；成功
`arrive` `flower` `win` `thrive`

# 同根

- adj. `prosperous` 繁荣的；兴旺的
- adv. `prosperously` 繁荣地；幸运地
- n. `prosperity` 繁荣，成功

# 例句

His business continued to prosper.
他的生意持续兴隆。
