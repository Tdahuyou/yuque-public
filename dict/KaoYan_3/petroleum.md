# 音标

- 英 `/pə'trəuliəm/`
- 美 `/pə'trolɪəm/`

# 词义

n. 石油
`oil that is obtained from below the surface of the Earth and is used to make petrol,  paraffin  , and various chemical substances`



petr(石)+oleum→石油

# 同义

n. [油气]石油
`fossil oil` `geoline`

# 同根

- adj. `petrochemical` 石化的
- n. `petrochemical` 石油化学产品
