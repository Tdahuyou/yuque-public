# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒpə/`
- 美 `/'kɑpɚ/`

# 词义

n. 铜<；铜币，铜制器
`a soft reddish-brown metal that allows electricity and heat to pass through it easily, and is used to make electrical wires, water pipes etc. It is a chemical element: symbol Cu`

# 同义

n. [化学]铜；铜币；警察
`police` `pig`

adj. 铜的
`cupreous`

# 同根

- adj. `coppery` 含铜的；铜制的；铜一样的
- n. `cop` 巡警，警官
- vt. `cop` 抓住
