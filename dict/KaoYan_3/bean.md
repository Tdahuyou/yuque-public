# 音标

- 英 `/biːn/`
- 美 `/bin/`

# 词义

n. 豆，菜豆，蚕豆
`a seed or a  pod (=  case containing seeds  )  , that comes from a climbing plant and is cooked as food. There are very many types of beans.`



搞笑短剧《憨豆先生》的英文名就是Mr. Bean

# 同义

n. [植]豆；嘴峰；毫无价值的东西
`legumen` `nihil`
