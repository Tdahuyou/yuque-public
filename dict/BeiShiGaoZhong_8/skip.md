# 音标

- 英 `/skɪp/`
- 美 `/skɪp/`

# 词义

vt. 跳过
`to not read, mention, or deal with something that would normally come or happen next`



滑雪(ski)的时候跳过(skip)了好几个坑

# 同义

vi. 跳跃；跳绳；遗漏；跳读
`dance` `to leap`

vt. 跳过；遗漏
`jump` `clear`

n. 跳跃；跳读
`jump` `spring`

# 短语

- `skip over` 略过；遗漏；作短期旅行
- `skip rope` 跳绳

# 例句

I decided to skip the first chapter.
我决定跳过第一章。
