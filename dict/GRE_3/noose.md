# 音标

- 英 `/nuːs/`
- 美 `/nus/`

# 词义

n. 绳圈，套索
`a ring formed by the end of a piece of rope, which closes more tightly as it is pulled, used especially for killing someone by hanging them`



和loose(adj. 松的)一起记

# 同义

n. 套索；束缚；绞刑
`chain` `restriction` `hanging`

vt. 用套索捉；使落入圈套
`reata`

# 例句

...a horrifying videotape of a man swinging from a noose.
…一盘一名男子吊在绞索上的恐怖录像带。
