# 音标

- 英 `/'gɪgl/`
- 美 `/'ɡɪɡl/`

# 词义

v. 咯咯笑
`to laugh quickly, quietly, and in a high voice, because something is funny or because you are nervous or embarrassed`



“叽咯” → 发出叽咯咯的笑声 → 咯咯笑

# 同义

vi. 傻笑；咯咯地笑
`smirk`

# 例句

If you can’t stop giggling you’ll have to leave the room.
如果你再咯咯地笑个不停，就给我离开屋子。
