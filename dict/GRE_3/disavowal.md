# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdɪsə'vauəl/`
- 美 `/,dɪsə'vaʊəl/`

# 词义

n. 否认
`A disavowal of something is a statement that you are not connected with it or responsible for it, or that you no longer agree with or believe in it`



dis(不) + a(=ad 向) + vow(=voc 叫喊) + al → 向人们大喊说不是 → 否认

# 同义

n. 否定，否认；拒绝；不承认
`contradiction` `negative` `rejection`

# 同根

- vt. `disavow` 否认，否定；抵赖；拒绝对…的责任

# 例句

...a public disavowal of his beliefs.
...一个对他信仰的公开否认。
