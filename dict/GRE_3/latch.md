# 音标

- 英 `/lætʃ/`
- 美 `/lætʃ/`

# 词义

n. 〔门或窗的〕闩，插销
`a small metal or plastic object used to keep a door, gate, or window closed`

v. 用碰(簧)锁锁上；用插销插上
`to fasten a door, gate, or window with a latch`



和catch(v. 抓住)一起记

# 同义

vi. 占有，抓住；闭锁
`take possession of` `seize on`

n. 门闩
`door release` `bolt`

# 短语

- `latch on` 占有；抓住；理解
- `on the latch` 关着但不上锁

# 例句

Gwen lifted the latch and opened the gate.
格温拉起门闩，把门打开。
