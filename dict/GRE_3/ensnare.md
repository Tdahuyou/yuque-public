# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'sner/`
- 美 `/in'snεə/`

# 词义

v. 诱入陷阱，进入罗网
`to trap someone in an unpleasant or illegal situation, from which they cannot escape`



en(进入) + snare(罗网， 陷阱) → 诱入陷阱， 进入罗网

# 同义

vt. 诱捕；诱入陷阱；进入罗网
`trap`

# 例句

He was concerned the campaign could ensnare innocent people.
他担心这场运动可能会陷害无辜的人。
