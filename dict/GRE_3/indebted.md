# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'detɪd/`
- 美 `/ɪn'dɛtɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 感激的，蒙恩的
`If you say that you are indebted to someone for something, you mean that you are very grateful to them for something`



in(进入) + debt(债务) + ed → 欠人情债的 → 感激的， 蒙恩的

# 同义

adj. 负债的；感激的；受惠的
`appreciative` `obliged`

# 同根

- n. `indebtedness` 债务；受恩惠

# 例句

I am deeply indebted to him for his help.
我对他的帮助深深感激。
