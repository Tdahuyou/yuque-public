# 音标

- 英 `/'neglɪdʒənt/`
- 美 `/'nɛɡlɪdʒənt/`

# 词义

adj. 疏忽的，粗心大意的
`not taking enough care over something that you are responsible for, with the result that serious mistakes are made`

# 同义

adj. 疏忽的；粗心大意的
`inadvertent` `inattentive`

# 同根

- adj. `neglected` 被忽视的；未被好好照管的
- adj. `neglectful` 疏忽的；忽略的；不小心的
- adv. `negligently` 疏忽地；粗心大意地
- n. `neglect` 疏忽，忽视；怠慢
- n. `negligence` 疏忽；忽视；粗心大意
- v. `neglected` 忽视；疏忽（neglect的过去分词）
- vt. `neglect` 疏忽，忽视；忽略

# 例句

Mr Brown was found guilty of negligent driving.
布朗先生被判疏忽驾驶罪。
