# 音标

- 英 `/plæt/`
- 美 `/plet, plæt/`

# 词义

v. 编成辫
`to twist three long pieces of hair or rope over and under each other to make one long piece`

n. 发辫
`a length of something, usually hair, that has been plaited`

# 同义

n. 褶；辫状物
`pleat`

vt. 编织；把…编成辫（等于pleat）；给…打褶
`pleach` `complect`

# 例句

She plaited her hair hurriedly.
她匆匆地把头发编成辫子。

a plaited leather belt
编成辫状的皮带
