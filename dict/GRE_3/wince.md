# 音标

- 英 `/wɪns/`
- 美 `/wɪns/`

# 词义

v. 畏缩，退缩
`to suddenly feel very uncomfortable or embarrassed because of something that happens, something you remember etc`

# 同义

vi. 畏缩，退避
`recoil from` `shrink back from`

n. 畏缩；脸部肌肉的抽搐
`backwardness` `self-distrust`

# 例句

The insertion of the needle under the skin made him wince.
针刺入皮下使他畏缩起来。
