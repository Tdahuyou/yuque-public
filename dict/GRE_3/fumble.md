# 音标

- 英 `/'fʌmbl/`
- 美 `/'fʌmbl/`

# 词义

v. 摸索，笨拙地搜寻；弄乱，搞糟
`When you are trying to say something, if you fumble for the right words, you speak in a clumsy and unclear way`

# 同义

vt. 摸索；笨拙地做；漏接
`feel about` `fish for`

vi. 摸索；笨拙地行动；漏球
`feel about` `fish for`

n. 摸索；笨拙的处理；漏球
`floccillation`

# 同根

- adj. `fumbling` 笨拙的；支支吾吾的
- n. `fumbler` 愚笨的人；摸索者
- v. `fumbling` 摸索（fumble的ing形式）

# 例句

I fumbled for something to say.
我苯嘴拙舌找话讲。
