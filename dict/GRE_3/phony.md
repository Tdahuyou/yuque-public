# 音标

- 英 `/'fəʊnɪ/`
- 美 `/'foni/`

# 词义

adj. 假的，欺骗的
`If you describe something as phony, you disapprove of it because it is false rather than genuine`

# 同义

adj. 假的，欺骗的
`spurious` `unreal`

n. 假冒者；赝品
`personator` `stumour`

# 同根

- adj. `phoney` 假的；伪造的（等于phony）
- n. `phoney` 骗子；假货

# 例句

He'd telephoned with some phony excuse she didn't believe for a minute.
他用一些假的借口打电话，她根本就不信。
