# 音标

- 英 `/'vɑːli/`
- 美 `/'vɑli/`

# 词义

v. 齐发，群射；截击
`to hit or kick a ball before it touches the ground, especially in tennis or football`

n. 齐发，群射
`a large number of bullets, rocks etc shot or thrown through the air at the same time`

# 同义

n. [军]齐射；齐射出的子弹；凌空状态
`salvo`

vi. 截击；齐鸣；进行群射
`salvo`

# 例句

He volleyed the ball spectacularly into the far corner of the net.
他一个精彩的凌空抽射，将球踢进了球网的远角。
