# 音标

- 英 `/'tɔːtn/`
- 美 `/'tɔtn/`

# 词义

v. 拉紧，绷紧
`to make something stretch tight, or to become stretched tight`



来自taut(adj. 绷紧的)

# 同义

vi. 拉紧
`strain` `tense`

vt. 拉紧；使绷紧
`strain` `tighten up`

# 同根

- adj. `taut` 拉紧的；紧张的；整洁的
- adv. `tautly` 绷紧地；紧张地
- n. `tautness` 拉紧；紧固度
- vt. `taut` 使纠缠；使缠结

# 例句

I suddenly felt the fishing line tauten.
我突然感到鱼竿的绳子拉紧了。

Some of them squat with horse stance, with their teeth clenched, tauten the rope;
有的蹲住马步，咬紧牙关，拉紧绳子；
