# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdemə'lɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,dɛmə'lɪʃən; ,dimə'lɪʃən/`

# 词义

n. 破坏，毁坏
`The demolition of a structure, for example, a building, is the act of deliberately destroying it, often in order to build something else in its place`



来自demolish(v. 拆毁)

# 同义

n. 拆除（等于demolishment）；破坏；毁坏
`destruction` `breaking` `blasting`

# 同根

- vt. `demolish` 拆除；破坏；毁坏；推翻；驳倒

# 短语

- `demolition blasting` 拆除爆破
- `demolition work` 拆毁工作

# 例句

The project required the total demolition of the old bridge.
这项工程要求那座旧桥的彻底拆除。
