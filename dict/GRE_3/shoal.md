# 音标

- 英 `/ʃoul/`
- 美 `/ʃol/`

# 词义

adj. 浅的，薄的


n. 浅滩，浅水处；一群
`a large group of fish swimming together`



形似拼音shao， 水少的地方 → 浅滩， 浅水处

# 同义

n. [地理]浅滩；鱼群；潜在危险
`school` `bank` `shallow` `ford`

vi. 变浅
`shallow`

vt. 使变浅；驶入
`shallow` `to put in`

adj. 浅的
`low` `shallow` `flat`

# 同根

- adj. `shoaly` 多浅滩的；多险阻的

# 例句

A preliminary study of a shoal often flesh-and-blood sticklebacks shows that they do indeed mingle with therobot and that they follow its leadership cues as predicted.
对一个有十条真实刺鱼鱼群的初步研究显示，他们确实接纳了那条机器鱼，并且接受它的领导指示。
