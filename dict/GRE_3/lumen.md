# 音标

- 英 `/'luːmen/`
- 美 `/'lumɛn/`

# 词义

n. 流明
`the derived SI unit of luminous flux; the flux emitted in a solid angle of 1 steradian by a point source having a uniform intensity of 1 candela `



lum(光) + en → 流明

# 同义

n. 流明（光束的能量单位）；（管状器官内的）内腔
`entocoel` `lumina`

# 例句

An older unit, the lumen per square foot, or foot-candle, has become obsolete .
一种较老的单位是每平方米英尺的流明数，即英寸烛光，这种单位已经废除。
