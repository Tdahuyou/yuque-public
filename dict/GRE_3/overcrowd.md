# 音标

- 英 `/ˌouvər'kraud/`
- 美 `/,əuvə'kraud/`

# 词义

v. 过度拥挤
`to fill (a room, vehicle, city, etc) with more people or things than is desirable `

# 同根

- adj. `overcritical` 过度指摘的；吹毛求疵的
- n. `overdrive` 超速档；[机] 超速传动
- n. `overdress` [服装] 外衣
- n. `overcompensation` 过度补偿
- vi. `overdress` 穿得过分讲究；穿得过多
- vi. `overcompensate` 过度补偿
- vt. `overdrive` 驱使过度；虐待；工作过度
- vt. `overdress` 过度装饰；使穿著过分讲究
- vt. `overcompensate` 给与…过分的补偿

# 例句

The manager of that theatre created a dangerous situation by allowing people to overcrowd the balcony.
那家剧场的经理让过多的人拥挤在楼厅里，造成了一种危险的情形。
