# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌmpaɪər/`
- 美 `/'ʌmpaɪɚ/`

# 词义

v. 对…进行仲裁


n. 仲裁者
`An umpire is a person whose job is to make sure that a sports contest or game is played fairly and that the rules are not broken`

# 同义

vi. 当裁判，任裁判
`ump`

vt. 仲裁，裁判
`arbitrate`

n. 裁判员，仲裁人
`judge` `referee`

# 同根

- n. `umpirage` 仲裁；公断；仲裁人之地位

# 例句

He umpired baseball games.
他过去担任棒球比赛的裁判。

The umpire's decision is final.
裁判的判决是不可更改的。
