# 音标

- 英 `/'skruːpl/`
- 美 `/'skrupl/`

# 词义

v. 顾忌
`to be willing to do something even though it may be wrong or may upset people`

n. 顾忌，迟疑
`a belief about what is right and wrong that prevents you from doing bad things`

# 同义

n. 微量；顾虑；踌躇
`suggestion` `trace` `drop`

vi. 有顾忌；踌躇
`hesitate` `crane`

vt. 对…有顾虑
`shy of`

# 同根

- adv. `scrupulously` 严谨地；小心翼翼地；多顾虑地

# 短语

- `without scruple` 肆无忌惮地，毫无顾忌地

# 例句

They did not scruple to bomb innocent civilians.
他们毫无顾忌地轰炸无辜平民。
