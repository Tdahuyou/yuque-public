# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'reɪdieɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪ'redɪɪt/`

# 词义

v. 照射；照耀，照亮
`if someone or something is irradiated,  x-rays  or  radioactive  beams are passed through them`



ir(在里面) + rad(光线) + iate → 使在光线里面 → 照射； 照耀

# 同义

vt. 照耀；使灿烂；启发；放射
`suggest` `ray`

vi. 照射；使某事物生辉
`beam`

adj. 发光的
`luminous` `light-emitting`

# 同根

- n. `irradiation` 照射；发光，放射

# 例句

...the Chernobyl disaster, which irradiated large parts of Europe.
...辐射了欧洲大部的切尔诺贝利灾难。
