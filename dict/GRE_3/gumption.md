# 音标

- 英 `/'gʌmpʃn/`
- 美 `/'ɡʌmpʃən/`

# 词义

n. 进取心，魄力；精明强干
`the ability and determination to decide what needs to be done and to do it`

# 同义

n. 进取心；气概；精力
`enterprise` `energy` `steam` `push` `vigor`

# 同根

- adj. `gumptious` 机灵的；精明能干的；精力充沛的

# 例句

At least she had the gumption to phone me.
至少她有这个头脑，给我打了电话。
