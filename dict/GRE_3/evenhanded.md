# 音标

- 英 `/'iːvn'hændɪd/`
- 美 `/'i:vən'hændid/`

# 词义

adj. 公平的，不偏不倚的
`If someone is evenhanded, they are completely fair, especially when they are judging other people or dealing with two groups of people`



even(平的) + hand(手) + ed → 两手放得一样平 → 公平的

# 同义

adj. 公平的；公平无私的
`fair` `just` `impartial` `equitable`

# 例句

...an evenhanded approach to the war on drugs.
...反毒品战中公正无私的做法。
