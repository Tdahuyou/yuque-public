# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'pʌlʃn/`
- 美 `/rɪ'pʌlʃən/`

# 词义

n. 厌恶，反感；排斥力
`a feeling that you want to avoid something or move away from it, because it is extremely unpleasant`

# 同义

n. 排斥；反驳；反感；厌恶
`contradiction` `hate`

# 同根

- adj. `repulsive` 排斥的；令人厌恶的；击退的；冷淡的
- n. `repulse` 拒绝；击退
- n. `repulsiveness` 排斥；厌恶
- vt. `repulse` 拒绝；驱逐；憎恶

# 短语

- `coulomb repulsion` [电工]库仑排斥

# 例句

I felt a mixture of amazement and repulsion.
我感到既吃惊又反感。
