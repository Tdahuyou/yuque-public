# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪstrəl/`
- 美 `/'mɪstrəl/`

# 词义

n. 寒冷且干燥的强风
`a strong cold dry wind that blows through the Rhône valley and S France to the Mediterranean coast, mainly in the winter `



mist(雾) + ral → 风起雾散 → 寒冷且干燥的强风
