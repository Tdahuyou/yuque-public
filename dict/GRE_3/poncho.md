# 音标

- 英 `/'pɑːntʃou/`
- 美 `/'pɑntʃo/`

# 词义

n. 斗篷；雨披
`a type of coat consisting of one large piece of cloth, with a hole in the middle for your head`

# 同义

n. 雨披；南美披风
`raincape`
