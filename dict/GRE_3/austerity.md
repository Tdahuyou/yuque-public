# 音标

- 英 `/ɔː'sterəti/`
- 美 `/ɔ'stɛrəti/`

# 词义

n. 朴素，艰苦
`bad economic conditions in which people do not have much money to spend`

# 同义

n. 紧缩；朴素；苦行；严厉
`simplicity` `contraction`

# 同根

- adj. `austere` 严峻的；简朴的；苦行的；无装饰的
- adv. `austerely` 严格地；简朴地

# 例句

a time of great austerity after the war
战后极其艰苦的岁月

the austerities of present Eastern Europe
现时东欧的艰难困苦
