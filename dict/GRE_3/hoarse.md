# 音标

- 英 `/hɔːrs/`
- 美 `/hɔrs/`

# 词义

adj. 嘶哑的，粗哑的
`if you are hoarse, or if your voice is hoarse, you speak in a low rough voice, for example because your throat is sore`



horse(马)中间加一个a → 马的叫声很嘶哑 → 嘶哑的

# 同义

adj. [耳鼻喉]嘶哑的
`throaty`

# 同根

- adv. `hoarsely` 嘶哑地；刺耳地
- n. `hoarseness` 嘶哑；刺耳

# 例句

He was hoarse from laughing.
他笑得嗓子都沙哑了。
