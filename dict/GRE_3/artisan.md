# 音标

- 英 `/'ɑːrtəzn/`
- 美 `/'ɑrtəzn/`

# 词义

n. 技工
`someone who does skilled work, making things with their hands`



arti(技术) + san(人) → 技工

# 同义

n. [劳经]工匠，技工
`workman` `craftsman`

# 例句

The artisan can cut stones into various shapes.
这工匠能把石头雕成各种形状。

The authors are award-winning artisan chocolatiers who make their own chocolate right from beans in micro-batches.
作者是获过奖的巧克力技工，他们用一小批的豆子做巧克力。
