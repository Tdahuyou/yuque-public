# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪndɪ'skrɪmɪnət/`
- 美 `/,ɪndɪ'skrɪmɪnət/`

# 词义

adj. 不加选择的；随意的，任意的
`If you describe an action as indiscriminate, you are critical of it because it does not involve any careful thought or choice`

# 同义

adj. 任意的；无差别的；不分皂白的
`random` `arbitrary`

# 同根

- adj. `indiscriminating` 不区别的；无差别的
- adv. `indiscriminately` 不加选择地；任意地

# 例句

The indiscriminate use of fertilizers is damaging to the environment.
化肥的滥用化正在损害环境。
