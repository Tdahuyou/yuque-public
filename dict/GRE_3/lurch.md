# 音标

- 英 `/lɜːrtʃ/`
- 美 `/lɝtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 突然不稳地行走[移动]，蹒跚而行
`to walk or move suddenly in an uncontrolled or unsteady way`

n. 突然的移动
`a sudden movement`



和lunch(n. 午餐)一起记

# 同义

n. 突然倾斜；蹒跚；挫折
`frustration` `setback`

vi. 倾斜；蹒跚
`pitch` `slope` `lean`

vt. 击败
`defeat` `down`

# 例句

Virginia’s heart lurched painfully in her chest.
弗吉尼娅胸口一阵疼痛。
