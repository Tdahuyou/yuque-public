# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'tɜːrstɪs/`
- 美 `/ɪn'tɝstɪs/`

# 词义

n. 裂缝，空隙
`a small space or crack in something or between things`



inter(在…中间) + sti(站) + ice → 站在二者之间 → 空隙

# 同义

n. [建]裂缝；[物]空隙
`crack` `clearance` `split` `hiatus`

# 同根

- adj. `interstitial` 间质的；空隙的；填隙的
- n. `interstitial` 填隙原子；节间

# 例句

Any of the open spaces in a net or network; an interstice.
网孔，空隙任一种网或网状物的镂空部分；空隙。

A small space or interstice in a tissue or part, such as the area bounded by small veins in a leaf or the wing of an insect.
小区，细隙，叶脉网隙组织或部分的小空间或空隙，如叶子中的细脉或昆虫的翼周围的区域。
