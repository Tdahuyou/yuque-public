# 音标

- 英 `/rest/`
- 美 `/rɛst/`

# 词义

v. 扭，拧；夺取，费力取得
`to take power or influence away from someone, especially when this is difficult`

# 同义

vt. 用力拧；强夺；歪曲
`colour` `take by storm`

n. 扭，拧
`tweak` `twist`

# 例句

They are fighting to wrest control of the party from the old leaders.
他们正极力从老一辈领导人手中夺取对党的控制权。
