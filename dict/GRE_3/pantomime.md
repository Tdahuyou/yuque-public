# 音标

- 英 `/'pæntəmaɪm/`
- 美 `/'pæntəmaɪm/`

# 词义

n. 哑剧
`a type of play for children that is performed in Britain around Christmas, in which traditional stories are performed with jokes, music, and songs`



panto(=pan 全部) + mim(模仿) + e → 模仿他人进行的表演 → 哑剧

# 同义

n. 哑剧；舞剧；手势
`motion` `sign`

vi. 打手势；演哑剧
`motion` `mum`

# 同根

- n. `pantomimist` 哑剧演员；凭动作表演的喜剧演员

# 例句

They were made welcome with the usual pantomime of exaggerated smiles and gestures.
他们以那种具有夸张的微笑和动作的一般闹剧受到欢迎。
