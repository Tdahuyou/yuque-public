# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'plɔːr/`
- 美 `/dɪ'plɔr/`

# 词义

v. 悲悼，哀叹；谴责
`If you say that you deplore something, you think it is very wrong or immoral`



de(向下) + plor(喊) + e → 哀叹

# 同义

vt. 谴责；悲悼；哀叹；对…深感遗憾
`condemn` `denounce`

# 同根

- adj. `deplorable` 可叹的；凄惨的
- adv. `deplorably` 悲惨地；可叹地

# 例句

Muslim and Jewish leaders have issued statements deploring the violence and urging the United Nations to take action.
伊斯兰教和犹太教领袖已经发表声明谴责该暴力行为，并敦促联合国采取行动。
