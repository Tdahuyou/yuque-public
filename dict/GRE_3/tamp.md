# 音标

- 英 `/tæmp/`
- 美 `/tæmp/`

# 词义

v. 捣实，夯实
`to press or push something down by lightly hitting it several times`



“踏” → 用力踏 → 夯实

# 同义

vt. 夯实，砸实；填塞
`stuff up` `obturate`

# 同根

- n. `tamper` 填塞者；捣棒
- vi. `tamper` 篡改；干预；损害；削弱；玩弄；贿赂
- vt. `tamper` 篡改

# 例句

The old man tamped down the tobacco with his thumb.
老人用大拇指把烟叶压实。
