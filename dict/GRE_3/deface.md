# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'feɪs/`
- 美 `/dɪ'fes/`

# 词义

v. 损坏
`to spoil the surface or appearance of something, especially by writing on it or breaking it`



de(变坏) + face(脸面) → 把脸面弄坏 → 损坏

# 同义

vt. 损伤外观，丑化
`to smear`

# 同根

- n. `defacement` 损坏外表；乱涂；损毁物

# 例句

Most of the monuments had been broken or defaced.
大多数纪念碑都被破坏或遭污损。
