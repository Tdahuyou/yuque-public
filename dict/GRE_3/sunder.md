# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌndər/`
- 美 `/'sʌndɚ/`

# 词义

v. 分裂，分离
`If people or things are sundered, they are separated or split by something`



“散的” → 分离

# 同义

vt. 切开；切断
`excide` `slice from`

vi. 分离；断开
`keep apart` `draw apart`

# 短语

- `in sunder` 分开着，个别地

# 例句

The city is being sundered by racial tension.
该城市因民族关系紧张而正在分裂。

Police moved in to separate the two groups, already sundered by distrust.
警方介入将已分裂又互不信任的两组人隔离开。
