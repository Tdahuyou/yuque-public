# 音标

- 英 `/'relɪʃ/`
- 美 `/'rɛlɪʃ/`

# 词义

v. 喜好，享受
`to enjoy an experience or the thought of something that is going to happen`

n. 美味，风味；喜好，兴趣
`Relish is also a noun`



rel(看作real， 真正的) + ish(看作fish， 鱼) → 真正的鱼 → 美味

# 同义

n. 滋味；风味；食欲；开胃小菜；含义
`flavor` `meaning` `import` `appetite`

vt. 品味；喜爱；给…加佐料
`enjoy` `favour`

# 例句

I relish the challenge of doing jobs that others turn down.
我喜欢接受挑战做别人拒绝做的工作。

The three men ate with relish.
那3个人吃得津津有味。
