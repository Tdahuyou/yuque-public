# 音标

- 英 `/ˌouvər'riːtʃ/`
- 美 `/,ovɚ'ritʃ/`

# 词义

v. 做事过头
`to try to do more than you have the power, ability, or money to do`



over(过分) + reach(伸出) → 做过了 → 做事过头

# 同义

vt. 走过头；过度伸张；诈骗
`outstep` `nobble`

# 同根

- n. `overproduction` 生产过剩；过度产生
- n. `overreaction` 过度反应
- n. `overprotection` 过度保护

# 例句

The company overreached itself financially.
该公司的做法超出其经济实力。
