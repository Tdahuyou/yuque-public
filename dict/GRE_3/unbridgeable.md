# 音标

# 词义

adj. 不能架桥的，不能逾越的
`An unbridgeable gap or divide between two sides in an argument is so great that the two sides seem unlikely ever to agree`

# 例句

...the so-called unbridgeable gap between the world of humans and that of chimpanzees.
...人类社会和黑猩猩社会间所谓的不可逾越的鸿沟。
