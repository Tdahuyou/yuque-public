# 音标

- 英 `/'keɪpər/`
- 美 `/'kepɚ/`

# 词义

v. 雀跃
`to jump around and play in a happy excited way`

n. 雀跃，跳跃
`a short jumping or dancing movement`



cape(披风) + r → 第一次穿披风走路的人 → 雀跃

# 同义

vi. 雀跃
`gambol` `bound with`

n. 跳跃，雀跃；[园艺]刺山柑花蕾；不法活动
`jump` `spring`

# 例句

Children capered around in front of the Smithsonian Institution.
孩子们在史密森学会的门前雀跃嬉戏。
