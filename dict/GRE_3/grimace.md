# 音标

- 英 `/grɪ'meɪs/`
- 美 `/ɡrɪ'mes/`

# 词义

v. 扮鬼脸


n. 鬼脸，面部扭曲
`an expression you make by twisting your face because you do not like something or because you are feeling pain`



grim(可怕的) + ace(看作face) → 可怕的脸 → 鬼脸

# 同义

vi. 扮鬼脸；作怪相；作苦相
`make faces` `pull faces`

n. 鬼脸；怪相；痛苦的表情
`moue`

# 例句

She started to sit up, grimaced, and sank back weakly against the pillow.
她开始坐起来，痛苦地皱着脸，又虚弱地靠回到枕头上。
