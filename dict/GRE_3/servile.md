# 音标

- 英 `/'sɜːrvl/`
- 美 `/sɝ​vl/`

# 词义

adj. 奴性的，百依百顺的
`If you say that someone is servile, you disapprove of them because they are too eager to obey someone or do things for them`



serv(服务) + ile → 百依百顺的

# 同义

adj. 奴隶的；奴性的；卑屈的；卑屈的
`slavish`

# 同根

- n. `servitude` 劳役，奴役；地役权；奴隶状态
- n. `servility` 奴性；奴态；卑屈

# 例句

He was subservient and servile.
他恭敬顺从，卑躬屈膝。
