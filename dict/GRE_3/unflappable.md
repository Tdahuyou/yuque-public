# 音标

- 英 `/ˌʌn'flæpəbl/`
- 美 `/,ʌn'flæpəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 不惊慌的，镇定的
`having the ability to stay calm and not become upset, even in difficult situations`

# 同义

adj. 镇定的，不慌张的
`collected` `unperturbed`

# 例句

Only his physical gifts and unflappable determination could nullify the Magic's season sweep.
只有他那非凡的身体天赋和镇定的夺冠决心可以让魔术队本赛季对湖人的横扫失去意义。
