# 音标

- 英 `/skrɪmp/`
- 美 `/skrɪmp/`

# 词义

v. 节省，精打细算
`If you scrimp on things, you live cheaply and spend as little money as possible`

# 同义

vt. 节省；过度缩减；克扣
`economize on` `save on`

vi. 精打细算；吝啬
`pinch pennies` `scrape and screw`

adj. 吝啬的；不足的
`short` `tight` `insufficient` `deficient` `lacking`

# 同根

- adj. `scrimpy` 缩减的；吝啬的；不足的

# 短语

- `scrimp and save` 节俭

# 例句

Scrimping on safety measures can be a false economy.
在安全措施方面节省开支可能会得不偿失。
