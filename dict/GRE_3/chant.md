# 音标

- 英 `/tʃænt/`
- 美 `/tʃænt/`

# 词义

v. 歌唱，吟诵
`to sing or say a religious song or prayer in a way that involves using only one note or  tone `

n. 圣歌
`a regularly repeated tune, often with many words sung on one note, especially used for religious prayers`



“唱” → 歌唱

# 同义

n. 圣歌；赞美诗
`spiritual` `motet`

vt. 唱；诵扬
`sing`

vi. 唱歌；反覆地唱歌
`sing` `descant`

# 同根

- n. `chanter` 吟唱者；唱弥撒的神父；风笛的笛管；骗卖者

# 短语

- `gregorian chant` 格列高利圣咏（教皇格列高利一世采用，常无伴奏）

# 例句

a priest chanting the liturgy
念诵祈祷文的牧师
