# 音标

- 英 `/mi'ændər/`
- 美 `/mɪ'ændɚ/`

# 词义

v. 蜿蜒而流；漫步
`if a river, stream, road etc meanders, it has a lot of bends rather than going in a straight line`



来自the Meander， 一条以蜿蜒曲折而著名的河流

# 同义

n. 漫步；曲流（常用复数）
`bummel` `stroll`

vt. 漫步；蜿蜒缓慢流动
`wander off` `roam about`

vi. 漫步；漫谈
`wander off` `mosey`

# 例句

We took a gravel road that meandered through farmland.
我们走上了一条蜿蜒穿越农田的石子路。
