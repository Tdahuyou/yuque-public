# 音标

- 英 `/'fɔːreɪ/`
- 美 `/'fɔre/`

# 词义

v. 突袭，偷袭；劫掠，掠夺


n. 突袭
`a short sudden attack by a group of soldiers, especially in order to get food or supplies`



fo(看作for， 为了) + ray(光线) → 为了光明， 偷袭敌人 → 突袭， 偷袭

# 同义

n. 突袭；侵略；攻击
`attack` `attempt` `invasion` `push` `aggression`

vi. 袭击
`descend on` `set at`

vt. 劫掠
`reave`

# 例句

These base camps were used by the PKK guerrillas to make forays into Turkey.
这些基地营被库尔德工人党游击队用来对土耳其进行突袭。
