# 音标

- 英 `/'toudi/`
- 美 `/'todi/`

# 词义

n. 谄媚者，马屁精
`someone who pretends to like an important person and does things for them, so that that person will help them – used to show disapproval`



toad(癞蛤蟆) + y → 像蛤蟆一样趴在地上的人 → 马屁精

# 同义

vi. 谄媚
`gloze`

vt. 谄媚
`beslaver` `flatter`

n. 谄媚者
`kiss-ass` `sycophant`

# 例句

Hypocrite and toady and inebriate!
伪君子谄媚者和醉鬼。
