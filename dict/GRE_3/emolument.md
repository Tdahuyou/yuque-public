# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'mɑːljumənt/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 报酬，薪水
`Emoluments are money or other forms of payment that a person receives for doing work`



e + molu(碾碎) + ment → 磨坊主加工粮食后所得的钱 → 报酬

# 同义

n. 薪水，报酬
`compensation` `payment` `consideration` `salary` `reward`

# 例句

He could earn up to a million dollars a year in salary and emoluments from many directorships.
他每年可赚高达一百万美金的薪水另加担任多个董事职位的酬金。
