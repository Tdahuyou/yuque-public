# 音标

- 英 `/skɔːld/`
- 美 `/skɔld/`

# 词义

v. 烫伤，烫洗
`to burn your skin with hot liquid or steam`

n. 烫伤
`a burn on your skin caused by hot liquid or steam`

# 同义

vt. 烫伤；烫洗；加热；晒焦
`to get a burn` `heat up`

vi. 烫伤；烫洗；煮沸
`to get a burn` `boil out`

n. 烫伤；烫洗
`empyrosis`

# 同根

- adj. `scalding` 滚烫的；尖刻的
- n. `scalding` [外科] 烫伤
- v. `scalding` 烫（scald的ing形式）

# 例句

Don’t scald yourself with that kettle!
小心别让水壶烫着！
