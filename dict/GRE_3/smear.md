# 音标

- 英 `/smɪr/`
- 美 `/smɪr/`

# 词义

v. 弄脏，玷污
`to put dirty or oily marks on something`

n. 污迹，污点
`a dirty mark made by a small amount of something spread across a surface`

# 同义

vt. 诽谤；弄脏；涂上；把…擦模糊
`soil` `mud` `spot` `wrong` `dirty`

n. [生物]涂片；诽谤；污点
`scandal` `personalities`

vi. 被弄脏
`smudge`

# 短语

- `pap smear` 子宫颈抹片检查
- `blood smear` 血涂片；血液涂片

# 例句

smeared windows
脏了的窗户
