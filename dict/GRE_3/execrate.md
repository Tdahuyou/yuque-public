# 音标

- 英 `/'eksɪkreɪt/`
- 美 `/'ɛksɪˌkret/`

# 词义

v. 憎恶；咒骂
`to express strong disapproval or hatred for someone or something`



ex(出) + ecr(=secr神圣的) + ate → 走出了神圣 → 咒骂
