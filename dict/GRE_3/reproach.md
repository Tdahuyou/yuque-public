# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'proutʃ/`
- 美 `/rɪ'protʃ/`

# 词义

n. 谴责，责骂
`If you look at or speak to someone with reproach, you show or say that you are disappointed, upset, or angry because they have done something wrong`



re(反) + proach(靠近) → 以反对的方式靠近 → 谴责

# 同义

n. 责备；耻辱
`blame` `thumbs-down`

vt. 责备；申斥
`blame for` `talk to`

# 同根

- adj. `reproachful` 责备的；申斥的；非难的
- adj. `reproachless` 无可非议的；无可非难的
- adv. `reproachfully` 责备地
- n. `reproacher` 责难者

# 短语

- `beyond reproach` 无可指责；无瑕疵的
- `above reproach` 无可指责；完美

# 例句

He looked at her with reproach.
他用责备的目光看着她。
