# 音标

- 英 `/haɪ'pɜːrbəli/`
- 美 `/haɪ'pɝbəli/`

# 词义

n. 夸张法
`a way of describing something by saying it is much bigger, smaller, worse etc than it actually is`



hyper(过度) + bole(扔) → 扔得过度 → 夸张法

# 同根

- adj. `hyperbolic` 双曲线的；夸张的

# 例句

It was not hyperbole to call it the worst storm in twenty years.
称它是20年来最大的风暴并不为过。
