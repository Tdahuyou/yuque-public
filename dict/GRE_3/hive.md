# 音标

- 英 `/haɪv/`
- 美 `/haɪv/`

# 词义

n. 蜂房，蜂巢；热闹的场所
`A hive is a structure in which bees are kept, which is designed so that the beekeeper can collect the honey that they produce`

# 同义

n. 蜂房，蜂巢；热闹的场所；熙攘喧闹的人群
`apiary` `comb`

# 短语

- `hive off` 脱离编制；分出

# 例句

This marketplace was once a hive of activity.
这个市场曾经熙熙攘攘。
