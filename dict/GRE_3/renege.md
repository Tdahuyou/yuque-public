# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'niːg/`
- 美 `/rɪ'niɡ/`

# 词义

v. 食言，违约
`to not do something you have promised or agreed to do`



re(反) + neg(否认) + e → 反过来不承认 → 食言， 违约

# 同义

vi. 食言；违例出牌
`eat his words` `break one's promise`

vt. 否认
`deny` `disown`

# 例句

They reneged on a pledge to release the hostages.
他们违背承诺，没有释放人质。
