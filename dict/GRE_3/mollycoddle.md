# 音标

- 英 `/'mɑːlikɑːdl/`
- 美 `/'mɑlɪkɑdl/`

# 词义

v. 过分爱惜，娇惯
`to treat someone too kindly and to protect them too much from anything unpleasant`

n. 娇生惯养的人




moll(软的) + y + coddle(纵容) → 娇惯

# 同义

vt. 溺爱；纵容
`baby` `dote upon`

n. 女性化的男人；懦夫
`chicken` `nancy`

# 同根

- n. `molly` 懦夫；娘娘腔的男人

# 例句

He had been mollycoddled as a young boy.
他从小娇生惯养。
