# 音标

- 英 `/'ruːɪn/`
- 美 `/'ruɪn/`

# 词义

v. 毁坏；毁灭；使破产
`to spoil or destroy something completely`

n. 废墟；祸根；毁坏
`the part of a building that is left after the rest has been destroyed`



大雨(rain)毁坏了(ruin)庄稼

# 同义

n. 废墟；毁坏；灭亡
`demolition` `disruption`

vt. 毁灭；使破产
`devastate` `go to the dogs`

vi. 破产；堕落；被毁灭
`fail` `go bankrupt`

# 同根

- adj. `ruined` 毁灭的；荒废的
- adj. `ruinous` 破坏性的，毁灭性的；零落的
- adv. `ruinously` 毁灭地；败坏地
- n. `ruination` 毁灭；祸根
- v. `ruined` 毁灭（ruin的变形）

# 短语

- `in ruins` 成为废墟
- `financial ruin` 经济损失

# 例句

This illness has ruined my life.
这个病毁掉了我的生活。

His career would be ruined.
他的事业将被毁掉。

All this mud’s going to ruin my shoes.
这泥巴会把我的鞋弄坏。
