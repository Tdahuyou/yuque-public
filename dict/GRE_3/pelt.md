# 音标

- 英 `/pelt/`
- 美 `/pɛlt/`

# 词义

v. 扔
`If you pelt someone with things, you throw things at them`

n. 毛皮
`the skin of a dead animal, especially with the fur or hair still on it`

# 同义

vt. 攻击或投掷；剥皮
`skin` `shell`

n. [皮革]毛皮；打击
`blow` `stroke` `shock` `strike` `hit`

# 同根

- adj. `pelting` 盛怒的；微不足道的；没价值的
- n. `pelter` 雨大降；投掷者或器具；大怒
- v. `pelting` 投掷；攻击（pelt的ing形式）

# 例句

Some of the younger men began to pelt one another with snowballs.
一些年纪稍轻的人开始相互扔雪球。
