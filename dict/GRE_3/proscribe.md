# 音标

- 英 `/prou'skraɪb/`
- 美 `/pro'skraɪb/`

# 词义

v. 禁止
`to officially say that something is not allowed to exist or be done`



pro(前面) + scrib(写) + e → 写在前面 → 禁止

# 同义

vt. 剥夺……的公权；禁止
`bar` `inhibit`

# 同根

- n. `proscription` 禁止；放逐；剥夺人权

# 例句

The Act proscribes discrimination on the grounds of race.
这项法令禁止种族歧视。
