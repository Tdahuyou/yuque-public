# 音标

- 英 `/ˌmɑːn'suːn/`
- 美 `/,mɑn'sun/`

# 词义

n. 季风；雨季
`the season, from about April to October, when it rains a lot in India and other southern Asian countries`

# 同义

n. [气候]季风；（印度等地的）雨季；季候风
`rainy season` `raining period`

# 短语

- `monsoon climate` 季风气候
- `monsoon season` 季风季节
- `subtropical monsoon climate` 亚热带季风性气候
- `monsoon rainfall` 季风降雨；季风降水；季风雨量
- `monsoon rain` 季风雨

# 例句

...the end of the monsoon.
…雨季的结束。
