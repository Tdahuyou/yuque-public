# 音标

- 英 `/'prɑːstreɪt/`
- 美 `/'prɑstret/`

# 词义

adj. 俯卧的；沮丧的
`lying on your front with your face towards the ground`

v. 使下跪鞠躬，使拜服
`If you prostrate yourself, you lie down flat on the ground, on your front, usually to show respect for God or a person in authority`

# 同义

adj. 俯卧的；拜倒的；降伏的；沮丧的
`blue` `prone` `disappointed`

# 同根

- n. `prostration` 平伏；跪倒；虚脱

# 例句

They found him lying prostrate on the floor.
他们发现他趴在地板上。

They prostrated themselves before their king.
他们向他们的国王跪拜行礼。
