# 音标

- 英 `/sɪ'nɑːptɪk/`
- 美 `/sɪ'nɑptɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 摘要的
`of or relating to a synopsis `

# 同义

adj. [气象]天气的；概要的；对观福音书的
`capsule`

# 同根

- n. `synopsis` 概要，大纲

# 短语

- `synoptic meteorology` 天气学

# 例句

The information based on bibliographical sources has not been included in the synoptic table because they were too heterogeneous.
依靠文献来源而得到的信息未列入这个概要性的表格，因为它们太参差不齐了。
