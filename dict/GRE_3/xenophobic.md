# 音标

- 英 `/ˌzenə'foubik/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

adj. 恐外的，恐惧外国人的
`If you describe someone as xenophobic, you disapprove of them because they show strong dislike or fear of people from other countries`

# 同根

- n. `xenophobia` 仇外；对外国人的畏惧和憎恨

# 例句

Service in the armed forces gave many Americans a less xenophobic view of the world.
在武装部队的服役使得许多美国人有了较不排外的世界观。
