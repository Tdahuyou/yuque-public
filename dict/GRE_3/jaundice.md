# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒɔːndɪs/`
- 美 `/'dʒɔndɪs/`

# 词义

n. 偏见；黄疸
`a medical condition in which your skin and the white part of your eyes become yellow`

# 同义

n. [临床]黄疸；偏见；乖僻
`icterus` `bias`

# 同根

- adj. `jaundiced` 有偏见的；患黄疸病的
- v. `jaundiced` 使有偏见（jaundice的过去式）；使患黄疸病

# 短语

- `obstructive jaundice` 梗阻性黄疸；阻塞性黄疸
- `neonatal jaundice` 新生儿黄疸

# 例句

Leptospirosis causes severe jaundice, kidney failure and bleeding in the lungs.
钩端螺旋体导致严重的黄疸、肾衰竭和肺出血。

The "yellow" in the name is explained by the jaundice that affects some patients, causing yellow eyes and yellow skin.
名称中的“黄”字由影响一些患者的黄疸使眼睛和皮肤颜色发黄而得到解释。
