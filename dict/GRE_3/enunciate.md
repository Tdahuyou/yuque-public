# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'nʌnsieɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪ'nʌnsɪet/`

# 词义

v. 发音；表达
`When you enunciate a word or part of a word, you pronounce it clearly`



e(出) + nunci(=nounce报告， 说) + ate → 说出来 → 发音， 表达

# 同义

vt. 发音；阐明；宣布
`bill` `illustrate`

vi. 阐明；清晰地发音
`shed light on` `explain clearly`

# 同根

- n. `enunciation` 阐明，表明；清晰的发音

# 例句

His voice was harsh as he enunciated each word carefully.
他用刺耳的声音小心地一字一句地念着。

She enunciates very slowly and carefully.
她慢慢地、仔细地读着。
