# 音标

- 英 `/ou'biːsns/`
- 美 `/o'bisns/`

# 词义

n. 鞠躬，敬礼
`respect and obedience to someone or something, often shown by bending your head or the upper part of your body`



ob(加强) + eis(=aud 听话) + ance → 表示听话 → 鞠躬

# 同义

n. 敬礼；尊敬
`respect` `worship`

# 例句

While he was still young and strong all paid obeisance to him.
他年轻力壮时所有人都对他毕恭毕敬。
