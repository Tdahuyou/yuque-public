# 音标

- 英 `/'pentʃənt/`
- 美 `/'pentʃənt/`

# 词义

n. 爱好，嗜好
`if you have a penchant for something, you like that thing very much and try to do it or have it often`



pench(=pend挂) + ant → 对…挂着一颗心 → 爱好

# 同义

n. 嗜好；倾向
`tendency` `liability` `weakness` `habit` `trend`

# 例句

a penchant for fast cars
对跑车的酷爱
