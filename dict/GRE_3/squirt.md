# 音标

- 英 `/skwɜːrt/`
- 美 `/skwɝt/`

# 词义

v. 喷出，溅进
`if you squirt liquid or if it squirts somewhere, it is forced out in a thin fast stream`

# 同义

n. 喷射；[医]注射
`injection` `emission` `jet`

vi. 喷出
`belch out` `spout out`

# 同根

- n. `squirter` 喷射者；喷射器

# 短语

- `squirt gun` 水枪

# 例句

Norman cut open his pie and squirted tomato sauce into it.
诺曼切开他的馅饼，往里面挤了番茄酱。
