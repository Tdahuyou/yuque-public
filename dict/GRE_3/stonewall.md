# 音标

- 英 `/'stoun'wɔːl/`
- 美 `/'ston'wɔl/`

# 词义

v. 拖延议事，设置障碍
`to delay a discussion, decision etc by talking a lot and refusing to answer questions`

# 同义

vt. 妨碍；阻碍；小心翼翼地打球
`let` `slow`

vi. 妨碍；阻碍；小心翼翼地打球
`stand in the way of` `hinder from`

# 同根

- n. `stonewalling` 石墙；阻碍议程
- v. `stonewalling` 妨碍（stonewall的ing形式）

# 例句

The administration is just stonewalling in an attempt to hide their political embarrassment.
政府只是在故意拖延，企图掩盖其政治窘态。
