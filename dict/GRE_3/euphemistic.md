# 音标

- 英 `/ˌjuːfə'mɪstɪk/`
- 美 `/,jʊfə'mɪstɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 委婉的
`euphemistic language uses polite words and expressions to avoid shocking or upsetting people`

# 同义

adj. 委婉的；婉言的
`periphrastic`

# 同根

- adv. `euphemistically` 委婉地；婉言地
- n. `euphemism` 委婉语；委婉说法
- vi. `euphemize` 用婉转的话说
- vt. `euphemize` 委婉地说

# 例句

...a euphemistic way of saying that someone has been lying.
…说某人一直在撒谎的委婉说法。
