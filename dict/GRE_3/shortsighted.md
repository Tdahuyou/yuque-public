# 音标

- 英 `/ˌʃɔːrt'saɪtɪd/`
- 美 `/'ʃɔ:'saitid/`

# 词义

adj. 缺乏远见的；近视的
`If you are shortsighted, you cannot see things properly when they are far away, because there is something wrong with your eyes`

# 同义

adj. 目光短浅的；近视的
`myopic` `bleary-eyed`

# 同根

- n. `shortsightedness` 近视

# 例句

As you get older, you may become long-sighted or shortsighted.
随着年龄的增长，你可能变得远视或近视。
