# 音标

- 英 `/'glɑːsəri/`
- 美 `/'ɡlɑsəri/`

# 词义

n. 词汇表；难词表
`a list of special words and explanations of their meanings, often at the end of a book`



gloss(舌头， 语言) + ary → 词汇表

# 同根

- n. `glossarist` 注解者；评注者；词表编辑者

# 例句

A glossary of terms is included for the reader's convenience.
为了方便读者，还收录了术语汇编。
