# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'testeɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'tɛstet/`

# 词义

adj. 未留遗嘱的
`(of a person) not having made a will `



in(无) + test(看到) + ate → 没有留下可以看的东西 → 未留遗嘱的

# 同根

- n. `intestacy` 无遗嘱死亡

# 例句

The thesis analyses the tendency in correlative aspects of testamental succession and intestate succession.
从遗嘱继承制度和无遗嘱继承制度的相关方面可了解这一变迁。

When someone dies intestate, the property automatically go to the survive partner unless there are children.
当某人无遗嘱死亡， 财产自动归幸存的伴侣，除非他还有孩子。

A person appointed by a probate court to administer the estate of a person who died intestate (without a will).
法院任命一个管理遗产的人而该已死的人没有订立遗嘱（无遗嘱）。
