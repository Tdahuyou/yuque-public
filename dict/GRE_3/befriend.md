# 音标

- 英 `/bɪ'frend/`
- 美 `/bi'frend/`

# 词义

v. 以朋友态度对待
`to behave in a friendly way towards someone, especially someone who is younger or needs help`



be + riend(朋友) → 当作朋友 → 以朋友态度对待

# 同义

vt. 帮助；待人如友；扶助
`facilitate` `lend a hand`

# 例句

They befriended me when I first arrived in London as a student.
我初到伦敦求学时，他们待我如同朋友。
