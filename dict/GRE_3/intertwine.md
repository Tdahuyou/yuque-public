# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪntər'twaɪn/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɚ'twaɪn/`

# 词义

v. 纠缠，缠绕
`if two things intertwine, or if they are intertwined, they are twisted together（`



inter + twine(细绳) → 多股绳交织在一起 → 纠缠

# 同义

vi. 纠缠；编结
`ply with` `keep at`

vt. 缠绕；纠缠
`wind` `pursue`

# 同根

- adj. `intertwined` 缠绕的；错综复杂的
- v. `intertwined` 使缠结，缠绕（intertwine的过去式）

# 例句

Trees, undergrowth and creepers intertwined, blocking our way.
树木、灌木和攀藤植物相互缠绕，挡了我们的路。
