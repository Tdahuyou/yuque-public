# 音标

- 英 `/ˌpredl'ekʃn/`
- 美 `/,prɛdl'ɛkʃən/`

# 词义

n. 偏爱，嗜好
`if you have a predilection for something, especially something unusual, you like it very much`



pre + dilection(看作direction， 趋向) → 兴趣的趋向 → 偏爱， 嗜好

# 同义

n. 偏爱，嗜好
`weakness` `preference` `habit` `favour` `appetite`

# 例句

...his predilection for fast cars and fast horses.
...他对跑车和骏马的偏爱。
