# 音标

- 英 `/snɪr/`
- 美 `/snɪr/`

# 词义

v. 嘲笑，鄙视
`to smile or speak in a very unkind way that shows you have no respect for someone or something`

# 同义

vi. 嘲笑，冷笑
`laugh at` `make a mockery of`

vt. 嘲笑，冷笑
`laugh at` `deride`

n. 嘲笑，冷笑
`derision` `sarcasm`

# 同根

- adv. `sneeringly` 嘲笑地；轻蔑地

# 短语

- `sneer at` 嘲笑；蔑视

# 例句

‘Is that your best outfit?’ he sneered.
“那就是你最好的衣服了？”他讥笑道。
