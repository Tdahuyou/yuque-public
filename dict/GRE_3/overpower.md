# 音标

- 英 `/ˌouvər'pauər/`
- 美 `/'ovɚ'paʊɚ/`

# 词义

v. 压倒
`If something such as a colour or flavour overpowers another colour or flavour, it is so strong that it makes the second one less noticeable`

# 同义

vt. 压倒；克服；使无法忍受
`snow under` `outbrave`

# 同根

- adj. `overpowering` 压倒性的；无法抵抗的

# 例句

A delicate wine will be overpowered by strong food.
一种精美的葡萄酒会被味道浓烈的食物压过。
