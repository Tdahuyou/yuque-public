# 音标

- 英 `/tɔːnt/`
- 美 `/tɔnt/`

# 词义

v. 嘲弄，嘲讽
`to try to make someone angry or upset by saying unkind things to them`

n. 嘲笑，讥讽
`a remark or joke intended to make someone angry or upset`

# 同义

n. 嘲弄；讥讽
`mockery` `derisiveness`

vt. 奚落；逗弄
`tantalise`

# 例句

‘And he’ll believe you, will he?’ Maria taunted.
“他会相信你的，对吧？”玛丽亚嘲讽道。
