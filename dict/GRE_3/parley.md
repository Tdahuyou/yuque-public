# 音标

- 英 `/'pɑːrli/`
- 美 `/'pɑrli/`

# 词义

v. 和谈，会谈
`When two opposing people or groups parley, they meet to discuss something in order to come to an agreement`

n. 和谈；会谈
`a discussion in which enemies try to achieve peace`



parl(讲话) ＋ ey → 会谈

# 同义

n. 谈判；会谈
`negotiation` `treaty`

vt. 谈判；会谈
`negotiate about`

# 例句

...a place where we meet and parley.
...我们见面协商的地方。

I don't think you've ever tried parleying with Jorge, have you?
我想你没有尝试与乔治和谈过，是吗？
