# 音标

- 英 `/bʌldʒ/`
- 美 `/bʌldʒ/`

# 词义

v. 膨胀，鼓起curveoutward)
`to stick out in a rounded shape, especially because something is very full or too tight`

n. 凸起，膨胀
`a curved mass on the surface of something, usually caused by something under or inside it`

# 同义

n. 胀；膨胀；凸出部分
`expansion` `inflation`

vt. 使膨胀；使凸起
`expand` `plim`

vi. 膨胀；凸出
`fill` `swell with`

# 同根

- adj. `bulbed` 有球茎的；球状的
- adj. `bulgy` 凸出的；膨胀的；肿胀的
- n. `bulging` 膨胀，凸出；打气；折皱
- v. `bulbed` 生球茎；膨胀成球状（bulb的过去分词形式）

# 例句

Jiro waddled closer, his belly bulging and distended.
吉罗大腹便便地蹒跚而来。
