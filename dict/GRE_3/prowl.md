# 音标

- 英 `/praul/`
- 美 `/praʊl/`

# 词义

v. 潜行，悄悄踱步
`if an animal prowls, it moves around an area quietly, especially because it is hunting another animal`

n. 四处觅食，徘徊
`if someone is on the prowl, they are moving around different places, looking for an opportunity to do something`

# 同义

vi. 徘徊，潜行
`hang about` `wander about`

n. 徘徊，潜行；悄悄踱步
`spinning around`

# 同根

- n. `prowler` 徘徊者；小偷

# 短语

- `on the prowl` 徘徊

# 例句

gangs of teenagers prowling the streets
在大街上鬼鬼祟祟四处游荡的少年团伙
