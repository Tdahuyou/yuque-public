# 音标

- 英 `/'gɑːblət/`
- 美 `/'ɡɑblət/`

# 词义

n. 高脚酒杯
`a cup made of glass or metal, with a base and a stem but no handle`

# 同义

n. 酒杯；高脚杯
`drinking cup` `Tassie`

# 短语

- `goblet cell` 杯状细胞

# 例句

The goblet was mantling with foam.
这个高脚杯盖满了泡沫。

Suddenly, his friend saw the reflection of a snack swimming in the goblet.
忽然，朋友看见酒杯里有条蛇影在游动。

"Yes . . . yes, this'll make it stop, " lied Harry. He tipped the contents of the goblet into Dumbledore's open mouth.
“好的……好的，这就让它停止。”哈利哄骗他说。又把酒杯里的液体灌进了邓布利多张开的嘴巴里。
