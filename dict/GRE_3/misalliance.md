# 音标

- 英 `/ˌmɪsə'laɪəns/`
- 美 `/'mɪsə'laɪəns/`

# 词义

n. 不适当的结合
`a situation in which two people or organizations have agreed to work together, marry each other etc, but are not suitable for each other`



mis(坏) + alliance(结盟， 联盟) → 坏的联盟 → 不适当的结合

# 例句

It was a hopeless misalliance.
这是极其错误的结合。
