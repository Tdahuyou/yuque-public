# 音标

- 英 `/'priːsept/`
- 美 `/'prisɛpt/`

# 词义

n. 箴言，格言；规则
`A precept is a general rule that helps you to decide how you should behave in particular circumstances`



pre(预先) + cept(拿住) → 预先接受的话 → 格言

# 同义

n. 规则；格言；训诫；命令
`instruction` `regulation` `order` `bidding` `word`

# 同根

- n. `preceptor` 校长；教师；指导医师

# 例句

...an electoral process based on the central precept that all people are born equal.
…以所有人生而平等这一核心准则为基础的一种选举程序。
