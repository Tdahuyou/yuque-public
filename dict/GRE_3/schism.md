# 音标

- 英 `/'skɪzəm/`
- 美 `/'skɪzəm/`

# 词义

n. 教会分裂；分裂
`the separation of a group into two groups, caused by a disagreement about its aims and beliefs, especially in the Christian church`

# 同义

n. 分裂；分裂教会罪
`disrupture` `disjuncture`

# 同根

- adj. `schismatic` 分裂的；派别的
- n. `schismatic` 分裂者；主张分裂者

# 例句

The church seems to be on the brink of schism.
该教会看来处于分裂的边缘。
