# 音标

- 英 `/houn/`
- 美 `/hon/`

# 词义

v. 磨刀
`to improve your skill at doing something, especially when you are already very good at it`

n. 磨刀石
`a fine whetstone, esp for sharpening razors `



注意不要和horn(n. 号角)相混

# 同义

n. [机]磨刀石；想念；抱怨
`complaint` `sharpening stone`

# 例句

He set about honing his skills as a draughtsman.
他着手提高自己的绘画技艺。

(= extremely well-developed ) intuition
极度发达的直觉
