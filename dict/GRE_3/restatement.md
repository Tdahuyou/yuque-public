# 音标

- 英 `/ˌriː'steɪtmənt/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 再声明，重述
`A restatement of something that has been said or written is another statement that repeats it, usually in a slightly different form`

# 同根

- vt. `restate` 重申；重新叙述；重讲

# 例句

I hope this book is not yet another restatement of the prevailing wisdom.
我希望这本书不会又是重复一些流行的至理名言。
