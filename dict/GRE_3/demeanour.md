# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'miːnər/`
- 美 `/di'mi:nə/`

# 词义

n. 举止，行为
`the way someone behaves, dresses, speaks etc that shows what their character is like`



来自demean， 古义等于conduct(n. 行为)

# 同义

n. 行为
`behavior` `dealing` `going` `act` `work`

# 同根

- n. `demeanor` 风度；举止；行为

# 短语

- `elegant demeanour` 风采

# 例句

his quiet, reserved demeanour
他少言寡语、含蓄的举止
