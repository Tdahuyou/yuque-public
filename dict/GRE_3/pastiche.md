# 音标

- 英 `/pæ'stiːʃ/`
- 美 `/pæs'ti:ʃ, pɑ:/`

# 词义

n. 混合拼凑的作品
`a piece of writing, music, film etc that is deliberately made in the style of someone or something else`

# 同义

vt. 东拼西凑；混杂
`scrape together` `interweave`

# 例句

His bathroom is a brilliant pastiche of expensive interior design.
他的浴室是高档室内设计的集大成之作。
