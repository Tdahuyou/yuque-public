# 音标

- 英 `/skʌf/`
- 美 `/skʌf/`

# 词义

v. 拖着脚走
`to walk in a slow lazy way, not lifting your feet up very high`

# 同义

vt. 使磨损；践踏；拖着脚走
`trample down` `ride down`

vi. 磨损；拖着脚走
`tear and wear` `wear off`

n. 拖着脚走；磨损之处
`shuffle`

# 例句

Polly, bewildered and embarrassed, dropped her head and scuffed her feet.
波莉既困惑又尴尬，低下头拖着脚走开了。
