# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪgnəmɪni/`
- 美 `/'ɪɡnəmɪni/`

# 词义

n. 羞耻，耻辱
`an event or situation that makes you feel ashamed or embarrassed, especially in public`



ig(不) + nomin(名声) + y → 名声不好 → 耻辱

# 同义

n. 耻辱；不体面；丑行
`opprobrium` `stigma`

# 同根

- adj. `ignominious` 可耻的；下流的
- adv. `ignominiously` 可耻地

# 例句

...the ignominy of being fired.
...被开除的羞辱。
