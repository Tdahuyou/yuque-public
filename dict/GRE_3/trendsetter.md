# 音标

- 英 `/'trendsetər/`
- 美 `/'trɛndsɛtɚ/`

# 词义

n. 引领新潮的人
`someone who starts a new fashion or makes it popular`

# 例句

With the unique combination, we underscore our position as a trendsetter in automation systems and bring this business into a new dimension, " said Klaus Kleinfeld, President and CEO of Siemens AG."
通过这一非凡的联合，我们加强了我们作为自动化系统领域潮流引领者的地位，并且将这项事业带领到一个新的规模。
