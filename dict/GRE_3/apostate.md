# 音标

- 英 `/ə'pɑːsteɪt/`
- 美 `//əˈpɑːsteɪt//`

# 词义

n. 背教者；变节者
`someone who has stopped believing in a religion or supporting a political party`

# 同义

n. 脱党者；变节者；叛教者
`turncoat` `flopper`

# 同根

- n. `apostasy` 变节；脱党；背教
- vi. `apostatize` 变节，脱党；放弃信仰

# 例句

Extremes meet. the most fur ious anarchist become the most barefaced apostate.
两极相通。最激烈的无政府主义者，居然成了最露骨的变节者。

This religion gives its followers just two options be a terrorist or be an apostate either ways you are dead.
宗教给了追随者两个选择，要么当恐怖分子，要么是叛教者。
