# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪrɪ'proutʃəbl/`
- 美 `/,ɪrɪ'protʃəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 无可指责的，无瑕疵的
`something, such as someone’s behaviour, that is irreproachable is so good that you cannot criticize it`

# 同义

adj. 无可指责的；[法]无过失的
`unimpeachable` `unerring`

# 同根

- adv. `irreproachably` 无过失地；不可非难地

# 例句

She welcomed her unexpected visitor with irreproachable politeness.
她以无可指摘的礼仪接待了不速之客。
