# 音标

- 英 `/dʒæb/`
- 美 `/dʒæb/`

# 词义

v. 猛刺
`to push something into or towards something else with short quick movements`



和job(n. 工作)一起记

# 同义

vt. 戳；猛击；用拨火棒等捅
`stick` `pink`

vi. 戳；刺；用拳猛击
`run through` `poke`

n. 戳；猛击；注射
`injection` `dig`

# 短语

- `jab at` 猛击；猛戳

# 例句

He saw her jab her thumb on a red button – a panic button.
他看见她用拇指猛按了一个红色按钮–应急按钮。
