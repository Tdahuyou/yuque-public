# 音标

- 英 `/ˌvælɪ'dɪktəri/`
- 美 `/,vælɪ'dɪktəri/`

# 词义

adj. 告别的，离别的
`A valedictory speech, letter, or performance is one that is intended as a way of saying goodbye when someone leaves another person, a place, or a job`



val(值得的) + e + dict(说) + ory → 告别的

# 同义

adj. 告别的
`farewell`

# 同根

- n. `valedictorian` 告别演说者；致告别辞者
- n. `valediction` 告别演说；告别词

# 例句

Mr. Walker made his valedictory address after two years as chairman.
在担任主席职务两年后，沃克先生发表了告别演说。
