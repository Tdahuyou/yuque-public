# 音标

- 英 `/tʃɑːrt/`
- 美 `/tʃɑrt/`

# 词义

v. 绘制地图，制订计划
`to make a plan of what should be done to achieve a particular result`

n. 图表
`information that is clearly arranged in the form of a simple picture, set of figures,  graph  etc, or a piece of paper with this information on it`

# 同义

n. 图表；海图；图纸
`graph` `diagram`

# 同根

- adj. `chartless` 图籍未载的；尚未绘入地图或海图的
- n. `chartist` 宪章派；图解专家；宪章运动者

# 例句

Each team was responsible for making its own decisions and charting its own course.
每个小组都是自己负责作出决定、制订计划。
