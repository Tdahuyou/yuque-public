# 音标

- 英 `/mɪns/`
- 美 `/mɪns/`

# 词义

v. 切碎；装腔作势地小步走
`If you mince food such as meat or vegetables, you cut or grind it up into very small pieces, usually in a machine`



minute(adj. 微小的)； minutia(n. 细节)

# 同义

vt. 切碎；矫揉做作地说
`to chop` `to cut up`

vi. 装腔作势；碎步走
`assume` `attitudinize`

# 同根

- adj. `mincing` 装腔作势的；矫饰的
- n. `mincer` 碎肉器（等于mincing machine）；说话吞吐的人
- v. `mincing` 切碎（mince的现在分词）

# 短语

- `mince pie` 肉馅饼

# 例句

Perhaps I'll buy lean meat and mince it myself.
也许我会买来瘦肉然后自己把它铰碎。
