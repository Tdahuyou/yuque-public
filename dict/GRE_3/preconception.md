# 音标

- 英 `/ˌpriːkən'sepʃn/`
- 美 `/,prikən'sɛpʃən/`

# 词义

n. 先入之见；偏见
`a belief or opinion that you have already formed before you know the actual facts, and that may be wrong`



pre(在前面) + conception(观念) → 先入之见

# 同义

n. 偏见；先入之见；预想
`forecast` `bias`

# 同根

- adj. `preconceived` 预想的；（思想或观点）事先形成的
- v. `preconceived` 预想（preconceive的过去式和过去分词）
- vt. `preconceive` 预想，使成先入之见；对…事先加以考虑

# 例句

Did you have any preconceptions about the sort of people who did computing?
你对那些使用计算机的人有什么先入之见吗？
