# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'vɪkt/`
- 美 `/ɪ'vɪkt/`

# 词义

v. 驱逐
`to tell someone legally that they must leave the house they are living in`



e + vict(征服) → 把…征服出去 → 驱逐

# 同义

vt. 驱逐；逐出
`cast out` `expel`

# 同根

- n. `eviction` 逐出；赶出；收回

# 例句

They were evicted from their apartment after their mother became addicted to drugs.
他们的母亲染上毒瘾后，他们就被逐出了他们的公寓。
