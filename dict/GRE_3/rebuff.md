# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'bʌf/`
- 美 `/rɪ'bʌf/`

# 词义

v. 断然拒绝
`If you rebuff someone or rebuff a suggestion that they make, you refuse to do what they suggest`



re(反) ＋ buff(=puff喷， 吹) → 反过喷气 → 断然拒绝

# 同义

n. 断然拒绝；回绝；漠不关心
`indifference` `apathy`

vt. 断然拒绝
`slap on`

# 例句

His proposals have already been rebuffed by the governor.
他的建议已被州长拒绝了。
