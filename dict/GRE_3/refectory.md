# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'fektri/`
- 美 `/rɪ'fɛktri/`

# 词义

n. 餐厅，食堂
`a large room in a school, college etc where meals are served and eaten`



来自refection(n. 食品， 小吃)

# 同义

n. 食堂；（修道院，学院等处的）餐厅
`canteen` `soupery`
