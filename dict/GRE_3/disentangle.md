# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdɪsɪn'tæŋgl/`
- 美 `/,dɪsɪn'tæŋɡl/`

# 词义

v. 解决；解脱，解开
`to escape from a difficult situation that you are involved in`



dis(不) + entangle(纠缠) → 摆脱纠缠 → 解脱

# 同义

vi. 解决；松开；解开纠结；解决（纠纷）
`settle` `work out` `figure out`

vt. 解开；松开；使解脱
`unlash` `unbind`

# 同根

- n. `disentanglement` 解开纠结

# 例句

She had just disentangled herself from a long relationship.
她刚从一段长久的感情中解脱出来。
