# 音标

- 英 `/sælv/`
- 美 `/sæv/`

# 词义

v. 减轻，缓和
`if you do something to salve your conscience, you do it to make yourself feel less guilty`

n. 药膏
`a substance that you put on sore skin to make it less painful`



salv(救) + e → 解救的东西 → 药膏

# 同义

vt. 涂油膏；缓和
`relax` `tone down`

n. 药膏；安慰
`relief` `comfort` `ointment`

# 例句

lip salve
润唇膏
