# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'kɔːrəm/`
- 美 `/dɪ'kɔrəm/`

# 词义

n. 礼节，礼貌
`Decorum is behaviour that people consider to be correct, polite, and respectable`



decor(美， 装饰) + um → 美的行为 → 礼节

# 同义

n. 礼仪；礼貌；端正；恪守礼仪
`ceremony` `protocol`

# 同根

- adj. `decorous` 有礼貌的，高雅的；端正的
- adv. `decorously` 有礼貌地；高雅地
- n. `decorousness` 端正；有礼

# 例句

I was treated with decorum and respect throughout the investigation.
整个调查当中我受到了礼遇和尊重。
