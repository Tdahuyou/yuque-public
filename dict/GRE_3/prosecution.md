# 音标

- 英 `/ˌprɑːsɪ'kjuːʃn/`
- 美 `/,prɑsɪ'kjuʃən/`

# 词义

n. 起诉，检举；进行，经营
`when a charge is made against someone for a crime, or when someone is judged for a crime in a court of law`



来自prosecute(v. 起诉， 检举)

# 同义

n. 起诉，检举；进行；经营
`going on` `operation` `litigation` `process` `conduct`

# 同根

- n. `prosecutor` 检察官；公诉人；[法] 起诉人；实行者
- vi. `prosecute` 起诉；告发；作检察官
- vt. `prosecute` 检举；贯彻；从事；依法进行

# 例句

a criminal prosecution
刑事诉讼

The evidence is not sufficient to bring a prosecution against him.
现有证据不足以对他提出起诉。
