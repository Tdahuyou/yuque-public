# 音标

- 英 `/'zelətri/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 狂热
`extreme or excessive zeal or devotion `

# 同义

n. 狂热的行为；狂言；狂热
`abandon` `mania`

# 同根

- n. `zealot` 狂热者；犹太教狂热信徒

# 例句

Even so, there is some fear of zealotry.
即便如此，还是担心狂热分子。

But at a time when the western world is battling to defend free speech against religious zealotry, they look particularly indefensible.
但每次当西方社会奋起身来为他们的言论自由辩护以反抗宗教狂热时，他们看起来却是那么的无言以辩。

Tory distrust of Europe has injected zealotry into what was once a pro-business party famed for its pragmatism.
保守派不相信欧洲已经注入了狂热到曾经因它们的实用主义而闻名于世的亲企党中。
