# 音标

- 英 `/səb'suːm/`
- 美 `/səb'sʊm/`

# 词义

v. 包含，包括
`If something is subsumed within a larger group or class, it is included within it, rather than being considered as something separate`



sub(下面)+sume(拿) → 拿在下面 → 包含

# 例句

After that the two alliances might be subsumed into a new European security system.
之后两个联盟可能会被归入新的欧洲保安系统中。

With unification, East Germany was subsumed by capitalist West Germany.
统一后，东德被归入资本主义的西德。
