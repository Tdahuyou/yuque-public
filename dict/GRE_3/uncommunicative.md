# 音标

- 英 `/ˌʌnkə'mjuːnɪkətɪv/`
- 美 `/'ʌnkə'mjʊnəkətɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 不爱说话的，拘谨的
`If you describe someone as uncommunicative, you are critical of them because they do not talk to other people very much and are unwilling to express opinions or give information`



与incommunicative(adj. 不爱交际的， 沉默寡言的)一起记

# 同义

adj. 沉默寡言的，拘礼的
`incommunicative` `taciturn`

# 例句

My daughter is very difficult, uncommunicative and moody.
我女儿很难对付，她不爱说话而且喜怒无常。
