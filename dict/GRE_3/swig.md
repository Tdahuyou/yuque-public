# 音标

- 英 `/swɪg/`
- 美 `/swɪɡ/`

# 词义

v. 痛饮


# 同义

n. 痛饮，大喝；牛饮
`hoptoad`

vt. 牛饮；大喝特喝，豪饮
`binge drinking` `drink like a fish`

# 例句

But while thirst is a good indicator that you are suffering from dehydration, your body can give you earlier warning signs that it's time to take a swig.
但是尽管口渴是脱水了的暗示，身体会更早发出警告信号，该是痛饮的时候了。
