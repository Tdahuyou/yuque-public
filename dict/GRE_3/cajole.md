# 音标

- 英 `/kə'dʒoul/`
- 美 `/kə'dʒol/`

# 词义

v. 哄骗
`to gradually persuade someone to do something by being nice to them, or making promises to them`



caj(=cage笼子) + ole → 把(鸟)诱入笼子 → 哄骗

# 同义

vt. 以甜言蜜语哄骗；勾引
`accost`

# 同根

- n. `cajolery` 甜言蜜语；诱骗；谄媚

# 例句

Do you think you can cajole some money out of your father?
你认为你能从你父亲那里骗到一些钱吗?。

If a person often have lies flow and mouth, thus to cajole others, over time, he would lose the trust of the people.
一个人如果经常有谎言流与口中，从而去哄骗他人，久而久之，他便会失去人们的信任。
