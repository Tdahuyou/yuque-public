# 音标

- 英 `/ˌelɪ'dʒaɪək/`
- 美 `/,eli'dʒaiək/`

# 词义

adj. 哀歌的，挽歌的
`showing that you feel sad about someone who has died or something that no longer exists`

n. 哀歌，挽歌


# 同义

adj. 挽歌的；哀悼的，哀伤的
`monodic` `condolent`

n. 挽歌，哀歌
`keen` `threnode`

# 同根

- n. `elegy` 挽歌，哀歌
- vi. `elegize` 作哀歌
- vt. `elegize` 以挽歌哀悼，作挽歌祭吊

# 例句

He spoke of her in elegiac tones.
他以悲伤的口吻谈到了她。
