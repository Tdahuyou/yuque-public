# 音标

- 英 `/'lautɪʃ/`
- 美 `/'lautiʃ/`

# 词义

adj. 粗鲁的
`If you describe a man or a boy as loutish, you are critical of them because their behaviour is impolite and aggressive`



来自lout(n. 蠢人， 笨蛋)

# 同义

adj. 粗野的；笨拙的；无礼的
`robust` `gross` `rough` `country` `bush`

# 同根

- n. `lout` 笨拙的人
- vi. `lout` 鞠躬；屈服
- vt. `lout` 愚弄

# 例句

I was appalled by the loutish behaviour.
这种粗野行为令我大为震惊。
