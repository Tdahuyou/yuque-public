# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌndərstʌdi/`
- 美 `/'ʌndə,stʌdi/`

# 词义

v. 充当…的替角
`to be an understudy for a particular actor in a play`

n. 预备演员，替角
`an actor who learns a part in a play so that they can act the part if the usual actor is ill`

# 例句

When the play's star broke her leg her understudy wept crocodile tears.
当这部剧的主演摔坏了腿时，她的替角流下了鳄鱼的眼泪。

While the star is recuperating from her illness, her role will be played by her understudy.
当明星正在休养恢复健康时，她的角色由修补演员代替。
