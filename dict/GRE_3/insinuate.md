# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'sɪnjueɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'sɪnjuet/`

# 词义

v. 暗指，暗示
`to say something which seems to mean something unpleasant without saying it openly, especially suggesting that someone is being dishonest`



in(进入) + sinu(弯曲) + ate → 绕弯说出来 → 暗指

# 同义

vt. 暗示；使逐渐而巧妙地取得；使迂回地潜入
`imply` `hint at`

vi. 暗讽；说含沙射影的话
`speak at`

# 同根

- adj. `insinuating` 暗示的；曲意巴结的
- n. `insinuation` 暗示；暗讽；间接的讽刺
- v. `insinuating` 暗示；使…潜入；讨好（insinuate的ing形式）

# 例句

What are you trying to insinuate?
你想暗示什么？
