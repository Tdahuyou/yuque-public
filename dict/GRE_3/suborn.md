# 音标

- 英 `/sə'bɔːrn/`
- 美 `/sə'bɔrn/`

# 词义

v. 收买，贿赂
`to persuade someone to tell lies in a court of law or to do something else that is illegal, especially for money`



sub(下面)+orn(装饰) → 在下面给人好处 → 贿赂

# 同义

vt. 唆使；收买；使作伪证
`set on` `instigate`

# 同根

- n. `subornation` 收买

# 例句

an attempt to suborn a witness
收买证人的企图
