# 音标

- 英 `/'fiːæt/`
- 美 `/ˈfiət, -ˌæt, -ˌɑt, ˈfaɪˌæt, -ət/`

# 词义

n. 法令，政令
`an official order given by someone in a position of authority, without considering what other people want`



fi(看作fire) + at → 对…开火 → 政令

# 同义

n. 命令；许可；政法
`instruction` `order` `bidding` `telling` `permission`

vt. 批准；颁布
`confirm` `authorize`

# 短语

- `fiat money` （美）不能兑换的纸币

# 例句

He has tried to impose solutions to the country's problems by fiat.
他已经试图采取强制性法令来解决该国的问题。
