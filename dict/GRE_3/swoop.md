# 音标

- 英 `/swuːp/`
- 美 `/swup/`

# 词义

v. 猛扑；攫取
`if a bird or aircraft swoops, it moves suddenly down through the air, especially in order to attack something`

# 同义

vi. 猛扑；突然袭击；突然下降；飞扑
`bound on` `surprise attack`

n. 猛扑；俯冲；突然袭击
`surprise` `subduction`

vt. 攫取；抓去
`grab` `pounce at`

# 短语

- `at one fell swoop` 一举，一下子

# 例句

The eagle hovered, ready to swoop at any moment.
鹰在天空盘旋，随时准备俯冲下来。
