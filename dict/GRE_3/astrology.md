# 音标

- 英 `/ə'strɑːlədʒi/`
- 美 `/ə'strɑlədʒi/`

# 词义

n. 占星术；占星学
`the study of the positions and movements of the stars and how they might influence people and events`



astro(星) + (o)logy(学) → 占星学

# 同义

n. [天]占星术；占星学；星座
`horoscope` `constellation`

# 同根

- adj. `astrological` 占星的；占星学的，占星术的
- n. `astrologer` 占星家
- n. `astrologist` 占星家

# 例句

Why do people believe in astrology?
人们为什么相信占星学呢？

She's busy writing a book on astrology.
她正忙着写一本关于占星术的书。

Many people have misconceptions about astrology.
许多人对占星学有误解。
