# 音标

- 英 `/'ekspərgeɪt/`
- 美 `/'ɛkspɚɡet/`

# 词义

v. 删除，删节
`If someone expurgates a piece of writing, they remove parts of it before it is published because they think those parts will offend or shock people`



ex + purg(清洗) + ate → 清洗掉不好的东西 → 删除

# 同义

vt. 删除，删去
`cancel` `delete from`

# 同根

- adj. `expurgated` 经过删除的
- n. `expurgation` 消去；廓清；删去
- n. `expurgator` 修订者；削除者
- v. `expurgated` 删除（expurgate的过去式和过去分词）

# 例句

He heavily expurgated the work in its second edition.
在第二版中，他大幅删减了该作品。
