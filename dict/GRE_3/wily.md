# 音标

- 英 `/'waɪli/`
- 美 `/'waɪli/`

# 词义

adj. 诡计多端的，狡猾的
`clever at getting what you want, especially by tricking people`



来自wile(n. 诡计)

# 同义

adj. 狡猾的；诡计多端的
`designing` `knowing` `subtle` `tricky` `guileful`

# 同根

- n. `wile` 诡计，阴谋
- n. `wiliness` 狡猾；诡计多端
- vt. `wile` 欺骗，诱骗；消遣

# 例句

a wily politician
狡猾的政客
