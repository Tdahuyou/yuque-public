# 音标

- 英 `/'hɪpəkrɪt/`
- 美 `/'hɪpə'krɪt/`

# 词义

n. 伪善者，伪君子
`someone who pretends to have certain beliefs or opinions that they do not really have – used to show disapproval`



hypo(在下面) + crit(判断) + e → 在背后下判断 → 伪君子

# 同义

n. 伪君子；伪善者
`pseudo` `dissembler`

# 同根

- adv. `hypocritically` 伪善地
- n. `hypocrisy` 虚伪；伪善

# 例句

The magazine wrongly suggested he was a liar and a hypocrite.
该杂志错误地暗示他是个骗子和伪君子。
