# 音标

- 英 `/pɔːn/`
- 美 `/pɔn/`

# 词义

v. 典当，抵押
`to leave something valuable with a pawnbroker in order to borrow money from them`

n. 典当，抵押；被利用的小人物
`an article deposited as security `



和pawnbroker(n. 典当商， 当铺老板)一起记

# 同义

n. [贸易]典当；[经]抵押物；兵，卒；人质
`hostage`

# 同根

- n. `pawnbroker` 当铺老板；典当商

# 短语

- `pawn shop` 当铺

# 例句

He is contemplating pawning his watch.
他在考虑把手表当了。
