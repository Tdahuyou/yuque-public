# 音标

- 英 `/lɑːrk/`
- 美 `/lɑrk/`

# 词义

v. 玩乐，嬉耍
`to have a good time by frolicking `

n. 玩乐
`something that you do to amuse yourself or as a joke`



在公园(park)玩乐(lark)

# 同义

n. 云雀；百灵鸟；欢乐
`joy` `festival`

vt. 愚弄
`befool` `assify`

# 短语

- `for a lark` 当作玩笑；为了取乐
- `rise with the lark` 早起

# 例句

Paint the whole room? Sod that for a lark!
油漆整个房间？ 开什么玩笑！
