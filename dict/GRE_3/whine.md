# 音标

- 英 `/waɪn/`
- 美 `/waɪn/`

# 词义

v. 哀号，号哭
`If something or someone whines, they make a long, high-pitched noise, especially one that sounds sad or unpleasant`

# 同义

vi. 发牢骚；哭诉；嘎嘎响；发呜呜声
`complain` `pinge`

n. 抱怨；牢骚；哀鸣
`complaint` `beef`

# 同根

- adj. `whiny` 烦躁的；爱抱怨的；常发牢骚的
- adj. `whiney` 嘀咕不停的；烦躁的；好发牢骚的
- adv. `whiningly` 哀鸣似地
- n. `whiner` 哀诉者；悲嗥者；啜泣者

# 短语

- `whine about` 发牢骚

# 例句

He could hear her dog barking and whining in the background.
他能听到她的狗在远处吠叫哀鸣。
