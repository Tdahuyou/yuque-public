# 音标

- 英 `/ˌriːə'bɪlɪteɪt/`
- 美 `/,riə'bɪlə,tet/`

# 词义

v. 使恢复
`to help someone to live a healthy, useful, or active life again after they have been seriously ill or in prison`



re + hab(拥有) + ilit + ate → 使重新拥有 → 使恢复

# 同义

vt. 使康复；使恢复名誉；使恢复原状
`repristinate` `unmake`

vi. 复兴；复权；恢复正常生活
`revive`

# 同根

- adj. `rehabilitative` 复职的，复原的
- n. `rehabilitation` 复原

# 例句

a special unit for rehabilitating stroke patients
协助中风患者康复的特殊病房
