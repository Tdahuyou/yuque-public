# 音标

- 英 `/flaut/`
- 美 `/flaʊt/`

# 词义

v. 蔑视；违抗
`to deliberately disobey a law, rule etc, without trying to hide what you are doing`



fl(=fly， 飞) + out(出去) → 飞出去 → 不再服从命令 → 违抗

# 同义

n. 嘲笑；轻视；愚弄
`despite` `derision`

vt. 嘲笑；藐视；愚弄
`scoff at` `laugh at`

vi. 嘲笑；表示轻蔑
`laugh at` `make a mockery of`

# 例句

Some companies flout the rules and employ children as young as seven.
有些公司无视规定，雇用年仅七岁的儿童。
