# 音标

- 英 `/'emɪseri/`
- 美 `/'ɛmɪsɛri/`

# 词义

n. 密使，特使
`someone who is sent with an official message or to do official work`



e(出去) + miss(送) + ary(人) → 送出去的人 → 特使

# 同义

n. 使者；间谍；密使
`illegal` `envoy`

# 例句

Japan is sending two emissaries to Washington to discuss trade issues.
日本将派两位特使去华盛顿商讨贸易问题。
