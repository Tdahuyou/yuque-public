# 音标

- 英 `/'niːouneɪt/`
- 美 `/'nionet/`

# 词义

n. 新生儿
`a newborn child, esp in the first week of life and up to four weeks old `



neo(新的) + nat(出生) + e → 新生儿

# 同义

n. 婴儿
`baby` `infant` `babe`

# 同根

- adj. `neonatal` 新生的；初生的

# 例句

Often parents will bring their neonate to the ED with concerns about the umbilical cord and it is just a simple granuloma or normal detachment. But is it omphalitis???
家长往往会带新生儿因担忧脐带到ED，它通常只是一个简单的肉芽肿或正常脱落。但它会是脐带炎吗？？？
