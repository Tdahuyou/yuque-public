# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spærɪdʒ/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spærɪdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 贬低，轻蔑
`to criticize someone or something in a way that shows you do not think they are very good or important`



dis(除去) + par(平等) + age → 剥夺平等 → 贬低

# 同义

vt. 蔑视；毁谤
`hold in scorn` `contemn`

# 同根

- adj. `disparaging` 毁谤的；轻蔑的
- adv. `disparagingly` 以轻视的态度，蔑视地；以贬抑的口吻
- n. `disparagement` 轻蔑；轻视
- n. `disparager` 毁谤者
- v. `disparaging` 蔑视（disparage的ing形式）

# 例句

Matcham’s theatres were widely disparaged by architects.
马彻姆设计的剧院遭到了建筑师的普遍抨击。
