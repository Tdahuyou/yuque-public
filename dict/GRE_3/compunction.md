# 音标

- 英 `/kəm'pʌŋkʃn/`
- 美 `/kəm'pʌŋkʃən/`

# 词义

n. 懊悔；良心不安
`If you say that someone has no compunction about doing something, you mean that they do it without feeling ashamed or guilty`



com＋punct(刺， 点)＋ion → (心)不断被刺 → 良心不安

# 同义

n. 悔恨，后悔；内疚
`the worm of conscience` `repentance`

# 例句

He has no compunction about relating how he killed his father.
他毫无悔意地讲述了他如何杀死自己的父亲。
