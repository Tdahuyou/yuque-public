# 音标

- 英 `/'neməsɪs/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 报应
`an opponent or enemy that is likely to be impossible for you to defeat, or a situation that is likely to be impossible for you to deal with`



来自希腊神话中的复仇女神Nemesis

# 同义

n. 报应；给与惩罚的人；天罚
`payment` `retribution`

# 例句

Let not nemesis catch me by the swift ships.
不要让报应女神娜美西斯用快捷的船舰捉拿我。

We worry that if the companion is not found, this paper will be our nemesis.
我们担心，如果没有找到伴星，这篇论文就会成为我们的报应。

We considered startlingly that all the things before our eyes were out of nemesis , which were merely man's life .
我们吃惊地以为眼前一切是报应，这只不过是人生。
