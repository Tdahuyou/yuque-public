# 音标

- 英 `/ˌʌndə'reɪt/`
- 美 `/,ʌndə'ret/`

# 词义

v. 低估，轻视
`If you underrate someone or something, you do not recognize how intelligent, important, or significant they are`



under(不足， 少于) + rate(估价) → 低估

# 同义

vt. 低估；看轻
`disappreciate` `sell short`

# 例句

We women have a lot of good business skills, although we tend to underrate ourselves.
我们女性有许多好的商业技能，只是我们往往低估自己。
