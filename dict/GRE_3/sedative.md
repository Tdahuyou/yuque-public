# 音标

- 英 `/'sedətɪv/`
- 美 `/'sɛdətɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 镇静的


n. 镇静剂
`a drug used to make someone calm or go to sleep`

# 同义

n. [药][医]镇静剂；能使安静的东西；止痛药
`killer` `tranquillizer`

adj. [医]使镇静的；使安静的
`calmative`

# 同根

- adj. `sedate` 安静的；沉着的
- adv. `sedately` 安详地；镇静地
- n. `sedation` 镇静；镇静作用；镇静状态
- n. `sedateness` 安详；镇静
- vt. `sedate` 给…服镇静剂

# 例句

Valerian is a safe and effective sedative with reliable results for most kids.
缬草是安全有效的镇静剂，对大多数儿童很有效。

And as a doctor who swore to do no harm, I guess I just still don't understand how you could give this clearly troubled person this powerful sedative in a non-hospital setting?
而作为一位曾发誓救人的医生，我想我还是不了解你能出乎医德考虑之外给这位显然陷入困境的人这种强大的镇静剂?。

After taking a sedative she was able to get to sleep.
服用了镇静剂后，她能够入睡了。
