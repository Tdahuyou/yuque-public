# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'lɪnieɪt/`
- 美 `/dɪ'lɪnɪet/`

# 词义

v. 勾画，描述
`If you delineate something such as an idea or situation, you describe it or define it, often in a lot of detail`



de(加强) + line(线条) + ate → 加强线条 → 勾画

# 同义

vt. 描绘；描写；画…的轮廓
`represent` `trace` `picture` `figure`

# 同根

- adj. `delineative` 描绘的；叙述的
- n. `delineation` 描述；画轮廓

# 例句

Biography must to some extent delineate characters.
在某种程度上，传记一定要描述人物。
