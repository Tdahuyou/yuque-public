# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'kænt/`
- 美 `/ri'kænt/`

# 词义

v. 撤回，放弃)
`If you recant, you say publicly that you no longer hold a set of beliefs that you had in the past`



re(反) ＋ cant(唱) → 唱反调 → 放弃(信仰)

# 同义

vt. 放弃；撤回
`desert` `yield` `quit`

# 同根

- n. `recantation` 改变论调；取消前言

# 例句

White House officials ordered Williams to recant.
白宫官员命令威廉斯公开宣布放弃自己的观点。

...a man who had refused after torture to recant his heresy.
...遭受酷刑后仍拒绝放弃其异教信仰的一名男子。
