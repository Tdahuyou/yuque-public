# 音标

- 英 `/'poulstər/`
- 美 `/'polstɚ/`

# 词义

n. 民意调查员
`someone who works for a company that prepares and asks questions to find out what people think about a particular subject`



poll(民意调查) + st + er(人) → 民意调查员
