# 音标

- 英 `/'bɜːrθraɪt/`
- 美 `/'bɝθraɪt/`

# 词义

n. 与生俱来的权利
`something such as a right, property, money etc that you believe you should have because of the family or country you belong to`

# 同义

n. 与生俱来的权利；长子继承权
`primogeniture`

# 例句

Freedom of speech is every American’s birthright.
言论自由是每一个美国人与生俱来的权利。
