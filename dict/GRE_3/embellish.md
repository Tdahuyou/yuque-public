# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'belɪʃ/`
- 美 `/im'beliʃ/`

# 词义

v. 装饰，美化
`to make something more beautiful by adding decorations to it`



em + bell(美的) + ish → 使…美丽 → 装饰， 美化

# 同义

vt. 修饰；装饰；润色
`paint` `hang` `modify`

# 同根

- n. `embellishment` 装饰，修饰；润色

# 例句

The boat was embellished with carvings in red and blue.
这艘船饰有红、蓝雕刻。
