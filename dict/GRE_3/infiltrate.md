# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪnfɪltreɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'fɪltret/`

# 词义

v. 渗透，渗入
`to secretly join an organization or enter a place in order to find out information about it or harm it`



in(进入) + filtr(过滤) + ate → 过滤进去 → 渗透， 渗入

# 同义

vt. 使潜入；使渗入，使浸润
`insinuate into`

vi. 渗入
`filter` `sink in`

# 同根

- n. `infiltration` 渗透；渗透物
- n. `infiltrator` 渗入者，渗透者

# 例句

Police attempts to infiltrate neo-Nazi groups were largely unsuccessful.
警方想打入新纳粹团体的行动基本上不成功。
