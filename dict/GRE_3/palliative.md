# 音标

- 英 `/'pæliətɪv/`
- 美 `/'pælɪ'etɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 减轻的，缓和的


n. 缓释剂
`A palliative is a drug or medical treatment that relieves suffering without treating the cause of the suffering`

# 同义

n. 辩解；缓和剂；暂时姑息的手段
`explanation` `exculpation`

adj. 缓和的；掩饰的；可斟酌的
`relaxative` `anetic`

# 同根

- n. `palliation` （痛苦的）减轻；缓和；辩解
- vt. `palliate` 减轻；掩饰；辩解

# 短语

- `palliative treatment` 姑息疗法，舒减疗法

# 例句

Good palliative care does not rely on expensive technology, but it does need staff to clean, feed and wash people.
良好的缓痛护理并不需要昂贵的科技，而需要大量为病人擦洗饲喂的烦琐劳动。
