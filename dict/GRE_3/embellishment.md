# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'belɪʃmənt/`
- 美 `/ɪm'bɛlɪʃmənt/`

# 词义

n. 装饰；装饰品
`An embellishment is a decoration added to something to make it seem more attractive or interesting`

# 同义

n. [服装]装饰，[建]修饰；润色
`decoration` `adornment`

# 同根

- vi. `embellish` 装饰起来；加以润色
- vt. `embellish` 修饰；装饰；润色

# 例句

...Renaissance embellishments.
...文艺复兴时期的装饰。

...public buildings with little bits of decoration and embellishment.
...鲜有装饰的公共建筑物。
