# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'luːʒn/`
- 美 `/dɪ'luʒn/`

# 词义

n. 欺骗；幻想
`Delusion is the state of believing things that are not true`



来自delude(v. 欺骗)

# 同义

n. 迷惑，欺骗；错觉；幻想
`fraud` `befuddlement`

# 同根

- adj. `delusional` 妄想的
- adj. `delusive` 欺瞒的；使迷惑的
- adj. `delusory` 迷惑的，困惑的（等于delusive）
- adv. `delusively` 欺瞒地；困惑地
- vt. `delude` 迷惑；逃避；使失望

# 例句

Insinuations about her mental state, about her capacity for delusion, were being made.
当时有对她的精神状态的影射——说她有妄想症的影射。
