# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'suːsiənt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'sʊsiənt/`

# 词义

adj. 漫不经心的
`An insouciant action or quality shows someone's lack of concern about something that they are expected to take more seriously`

# 同义

adj. 漫不经心的，漠不关心的；无忧无虑的
`indifferent` `careless`

# 同根

- n. `insouciance` 无忧无虑；漫不经心；满不在乎

# 例句

Television producers seem irresponsibly insouciant about churning out violence.
电视制片人似乎对大量制作暴力节目抱着不负责任的无所谓的态度。
