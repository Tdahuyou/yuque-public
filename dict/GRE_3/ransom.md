# 音标

- 英 `/'rænsəm/`
- 美 `/'rænsəm/`

# 词义

v. 赎回
`to pay an amount of money so that someone who is being held as a prisoner is set free`

n. 赎金；赎身
`an amount of money that is paid to free someone who is held as a prisoner`

# 同义

vt. 赎回，赎；勒索赎金；救赎；得赎金后释放
`redeem` `atone for`

n. 赎金；赎身，赎回
`redemption`

# 短语

- `a king's ransom` ◎作为被俘国王赎金的巨款

# 例句

They were all ransomed and returned unharmed.
付了赎金之后，他们都毫发无损地回来了。
