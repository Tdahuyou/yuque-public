# 音标

- 英 `/'flaɪti/`
- 美 `/'flaiti/`

# 词义

adj. 轻浮的；反复无常的
`a woman who is flighty changes her ideas and opinions often, and only remains interested in people or things for a short time`



f + light(轻的) + y → 因为轻而飘浮着 → 轻浮的

# 同义

adj. 轻浮的；轻狂的；心情浮动的
`flirty` `light-minded`

# 同根

- n. `flightiness` 疯狂；轻浮

# 例句

Isabelle was a frivolous little fool, vain and flighty.
伊莎贝尔曾是一个轻率的小傻瓜，自负而轻浮。
