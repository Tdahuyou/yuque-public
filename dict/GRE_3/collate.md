# 音标

- 英 `/kə'leɪt/`
- 美 `/kə'let/`

# 词义

v. 对照，核对
`to gather information together, examine it carefully, and compare it with other information to find any differences`



col(共同) + late(放) → 放到一起 → 核对

# 同义

vt. 核对，校对；校勘
`check against` `check through`

# 同根

- n. `collation` 校对；斋日的点心；牧师职务

# 例句

Shall we collate the accounts today?
今天我们要核对帐目吗？
