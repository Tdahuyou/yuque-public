# 音标

- 英 `/'tʌsl/`
- 美 `/'tʌsl/`

# 词义

v. 扭打，争斗
`to fight or struggle without using any weapons, by pulling or pushing someone rather than hitting them`

n. 扭打，争斗；争辩
`a fight using a lot of energy, in which two people get hold of each other and struggle`



tuss(看作fuss， 忙乱) + le → 为什么忙乱， 因为有人扭打搏斗 → 扭打， 争斗

# 同义

vi. 打斗；扭打；剧烈的斗争
`scuffle`

n. 争斗，扭打
`handicuff`

# 例句

They ended up ripping down perimeter fencing and tussling with the security staff.
他们最终扯掉了周边的栅栏，并和保安人员扭打起来。
