# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪntər'wiːv/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɚ'wiv/`

# 词义

v. 交织
`if two things are interwoven, they are closely related or combined in a complicated way`



inter(在…中间) + weave(编织) → 交织

# 同义

vt. （使）交织；织进；（使）混杂
`raddle`

# 例句

For these people, land is inextricably interwoven with life itself.
对那些人来说，土地和生活本身紧密交织在一起。
