# 音标

- 英 `/'buːi/`
- 美 `/'bʊi/`

# 词义

v. 支持，鼓励


n. 浮标；救生圈
`an object that floats on the sea, a lake etc to mark a safe or dangerous area`

# 同义

n. [水运]浮标；浮筒；救生圈；航标
`dan` `drogue`

vt. 使浮起；支撑；鼓励
`encourage` `support` `heart` `uphold` `sustain`

# 短语

- `mooring buoy` 系泊浮筒；系船浮筒
- `life buoy` 救生圈；救生衣
