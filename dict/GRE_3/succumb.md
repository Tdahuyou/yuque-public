# 音标

- 英 `/sə'kʌm/`
- 美 `/sə'kʌm/`

# 词义

v. 屈从，屈服；因…死亡
`to stop opposing someone or something that is stronger than you, and allow them to take control`



suc(下面) + cumb(躺) → 躺下去 → 因…死亡

# 同义

vi. 屈服；死；被压垮
`to surrender` `genuflect`

# 例句

Don't succumb to the temptation to have just one cigarette.
不要屈服于只抽一支烟的诱惑。
