# 音标

- 英 `/ˌoupə'lesns/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 乳白光


# 同根

- adj. `opalescent` 乳白色的；发乳白光的
- adj. `opaline` 蛋白石的，蛋白石状的；发乳光的；乳白色的
- n. `opal` 猫眼石，蛋白石；乳白玻璃
- n. `opaline` 乳白色；乳白玻璃
- vi. `opalesce` 发出乳白色光，发乳白光
- vt. `opalize` 使成乳色；使发乳白光

In view of above-mentioned argument, the fractal mechanism of critical system and the critical opalescence phenomenon are interpreted.
在此基础上，对临界系统的分形机制和临界乳光现象进行了解释。

It was found by the ceramic archaeologists of the Fujian Provincial Museum that some vessels discovered in the Huaian kiln have light blue opalescence.
