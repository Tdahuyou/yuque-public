# 音标

- 英 `/'pedntri/`
- 美 `/'pɛdntri/`

# 词义

n. 迂腐
`If you accuse someone of pedantry, you mean that you disapprove of them because they pay too much attention to unimportant details or traditional rules, especially in connection with academic subjects`

# 同根

- adj. `pedantic` 迂腐的；学究式的；卖弄学问的；假装学者的
- adv. `pedantically` 卖弄学问地；学究式地，迂腐地
- n. `pedant` 学究；书呆子；卖弄学问的人；空谈家
- n. `pedagogue` 教师，教员；卖弄学问者
- n. `pedagog` （小学）教师；喜欢卖弄学问的教师（等于pedagogue）

# 例句

He had all the illumination of wisdom and none of its pedantry .
他具有智慧的一切光辉，却没有丝毫学究的迂腐气息。

A fool's brain digests philosophy into folly, science into superstition, and art into pedantry.
愚蠢者将哲学曲解成愚昧，将科学领会成迷信，将艺术转变成迂腐。
