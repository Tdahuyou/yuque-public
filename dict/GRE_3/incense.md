# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪnsens/`
- 美 `/'ɪnsɛns/`

# 词义

v. 激怒
`to make someone very angry`

n. 香味
`a substance that has a pleasant smell when you burn it`



in(进入) + cens(=cand发光) + e → 焚烧 → 点燃怒气 → 激怒

# 同义

n. 香；奉承
`court` `joss stick`

# 同根

- adj. `incensed` 愤怒的，激怒了的
- v. `incensed` 激怒（incense的过去分词）

# 短语

- `incense burner` 香炉

# 例句

Spectators were incensed by the referee’s decision.
观众对裁判的判罚极为愤怒。
