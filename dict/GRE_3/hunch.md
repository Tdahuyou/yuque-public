# 音标

- 英 `/hʌntʃ/`
- 美 `/hʌntʃ/`

# 词义

n. 直觉，预感
`if you have a hunch that something is true or will happen, you feel that it is true or will happen`

# 同义

n. 预感；大块；肉峰
`bulk` `anticipation`

vt. 耸肩；预感到；弯腰驼背
`shrug`

vi. 隆起；向前移动
`bunch` `move forward`

# 同根

- adj. `hunchbacked` 驼背的

# 短语

- `have a hunch that` [口语]总感到，总怀疑

# 例句

I had a hunch that Susan and I would work well together.
我凭直觉感到我和苏珊共事会很融洽。
