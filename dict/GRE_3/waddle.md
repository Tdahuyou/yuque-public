# 音标

- 英 `/'wɑːdl/`
- 美 `/'wɑdl/`

# 词义

v. 摇摇摆摆地走
`to walk with short steps, with your body moving from one side to another – used especially about people or birds with fat bodies and short legs`



“歪倒” → 走路走得歪歪斜斜， 像要倒下去 → 摇摇摆摆地走

# 同义

n. 蹒跚；摇摆的步子
`titubation` `hitchiness`

vi. 摇摇摆摆地走；蹒跚而行
`wag`
