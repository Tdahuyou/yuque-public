# 音标

- 英 `/'eɪprən/`
- 美 `/'eprən/`

# 词义

n. 围裙
`a piece of clothing that covers the front part of your clothes and is tied around your waist, worn to keep your clothes clean, especially while cooking`



在四月(April)穿上围裙(apron)去干活

# 同义

n. [轻]围裙；[航]停机坪；舞台口
`sarong` `pinafore`

vt. 着围裙于；围绕
`enclose` `enring`

# 短语

- `parking apron` 停机坪
- `wharf apron` 码头岸肩；自码头货棚至水边间的空间

# 例句

She wiped her eyes with her apron.
她用她的围裙擦干了眼泪。

She had put her louis in the pocket of her new apron.
她已把她的那枚路易放在她新围裙的口袋里了。
