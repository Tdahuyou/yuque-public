# 音标

- 英 `/əb'sest/`
- 美 `/əb'sest/`

# 词义

adj. 着迷的，沉迷的
`If someone is obsessed with a person or thing, they keep thinking about them and find it difficult to think about anything else`



来自obsess(v. 迷住； 困扰)

# 同义

adj. 着迷的；无法摆脱的
`crazy` `mad`

v. 着迷（obsess的过去式）；纠缠
`implicated`

# 例句

He was obsessed with gangster movies.
他那时迷上了警匪片。
