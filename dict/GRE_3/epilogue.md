# 音标

- 英 `/'epɪlɔːg/`
- 美 `/'ɛpɪlɔɡ/`

# 词义

n. 收场白；尾声
`a speech or piece of writing that is added to the end of a book, film, or play and discusses or explains the ending`



epi(在…后) + logue(说话) → 在后面说话 → 尾声

# 同义

n. 结语，收场白；尾声，后记
`finale` `endgame`

# 例句

a disastrous epilogue to his career
他事业的灾难性终结
