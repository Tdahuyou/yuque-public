# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪntər'leɪs/`
- 美 `/'ɪntɚ'les/`

# 词义

v. 编织；交错
`to join together (patterns, fingers, etc) by crossing, as if woven; intertwine `



inter(在…中间) + lac(线) + e → 使线在中间交叉 → 编织

# 同义

vt. [计]使交错；使交织
`thread` `complect`

vi. 交织；[计]交错
`twine`

# 同根

- adj. `interlaced` 交错的；交织的
- n. `interlacing` 交错，隔行；隔行扫描
- v. `interlaced` 交错（interlace的过去分词）
- v. `interlacing` 交错，交织（interlace现在分词）

# 例句

Twist or braid together interlace .
搓或纺织在一起，使交织。

Riparian zone was an important interlace or eco-tone between aquatic ecosystems and upland ecosystems.
湖滨带是水生和陆地生态系统间的过渡带或生态交错区。

Dynamic geometries interlace the many spaces with 3 dimensional curves ensuring that the project cannot be comprehended from one single vantage point.
动态几何图形和许多有三维曲线的空间交错，确保项目无法从单个有利位置来了解。
