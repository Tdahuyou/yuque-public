# 音标

- 英 `/ˌtæmbə'riːn/`
- 美 `/,tæmbə'rin/`

# 词义

n. 铃鼓，小手鼓
`a circular musical instrument consisting of a frame covered with skin or plastic and small pieces of metal that hang around the edge. You shake it or hit it with your hand.`



来自tambour(n. 鼓)

# 同义

n. 小手鼓；非洲野鸽
`timbrel` `bongo drum`

# 例句

The maenad on the right is playing a tambourine.
神像右边的女祭司正演奏著小手鼓。
