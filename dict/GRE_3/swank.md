# 音标

- 英 `/swæŋk/`
- 美 `/swæŋk/`

# 词义

v. 夸耀，炫耀
`to speak or behave in a way that shows you think you are better than other people`



swan(天鹅)+k → 像天鹅一样骄傲 → 炫耀

# 同义

n. 出风头；虚张声势
`bravado` `lifemanship`

vi. 炫耀；出风头
`show off` `brag about`

adj. 爱打扮的，华丽的
`magnificent` `brave` `gorgeous`

# 同根

- adj. `swanky` 炫耀的；时髦的；爱出风头的
- n. `swanky` 活跃的帅小伙子

# 例句

I wish you’d stop swanking!
我希望你别再吹了！
