# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃɑːpwɔːrn/`
- 美 `/'ʃɑpwɔrn/`

# 词义

adj. 在商店中陈列久了的；陈旧的，磨损的
`a product that is shopworn is slightly damaged or dirty because it has been in a shop for a long time`

# 同义

adj. 陈旧的；陈腐的
`old` `timeworn`

# 同根

- adj. `shopsoiled` 陈列久了的；陈旧的（等于shopworn）

# 例句

shopworn management techniques
老一套的管理手法
