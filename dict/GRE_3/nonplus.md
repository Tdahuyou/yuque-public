# 音标

- 英 `/ˌnɑːn'plʌs/`
- 美 `/,nɔn'plʌs, 'nɔnplʌs/`

# 词义

v. 使困惑
`to put at a loss; confound `

n. 迷惑，困惑
`a state of utter perplexity prohibiting action or speech `



non + plus(有利的因素) → 不利 → 迷惑

# 同义

n. 迷惑；困惑
`confusion` `delusion`

vt. 使困惑
`bepuzzle` `perplex`

# 例句

he was nonplussed by the sudden announcement
