# 音标

- 英 `/'bʌtrəs/`
- 美 `/'bʌtrəs/`

# 词义

v. 支持
`to support a system, idea, argument etc, especially by providing money`

n. 拱墙，拱壁
`a brick or stone structure built to support a wall`

# 同义

n. [建]扶壁；拱壁；支撑物
`support` `upholder`

vt. 支持；以扶壁支撑
`encourage` `second` `back` `carry` `hand`

# 例句

The evidence seemed to buttress their argument.
证据似乎支持他们的论点。
