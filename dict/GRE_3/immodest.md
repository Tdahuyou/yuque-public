# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'mɑːdɪst/`
- 美 `/ɪ'mɑdɪst/`

# 词义

adj. 不谦虚的；不正派的
`having a very high opinion of yourself and your abilities, and not embarrassed about telling people how clever you are`

# 同义

adj. 不谦虚的；不庄重的
`undignified`

# 同根

- adv. `immodestly` 不客气地；不谦虚地；放肆地
- n. `immodesty` 无礼；不谦虚；粗鲁

# 例句

Webb was an immodest publicist of his own achievements.
韦布宣扬起自己的成绩来毫不谦虚。
