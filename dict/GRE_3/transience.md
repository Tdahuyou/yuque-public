# 音标

- 英 `/'trænʃəns/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 短暂，稍纵即逝
`If you talk about the transience of a situation, you mean that it lasts only a short time or is constantly changing`

# 同义

n. 短暂；无常；顷刻（等于transiency）
`anicca` `impermanence`

# 同根

- adj. `transient` 短暂的；路过的
- n. `transient` 瞬变现象；过往旅客；候鸟

# 例句

...the superficiality and transience of the club scene.
...这个俱乐部现场的肤浅和稍纵即逝。
