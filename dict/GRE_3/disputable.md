# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spjuːtəbl/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spjutəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 有争议的
`something that is disputable is not definitely true or right, and therefore is something that you can argue about`

# 同根

- adj. `disputed` 有争议的
- adj. `disputant` 争论的
- adj. `disputative` 好争论的；爱争辩的；有关争论的
- n. `dispute` 辩论；争吵
- n. `disputation` 争论；辩论；驳斥
- n. `disputant` 争论者，辩论者
- v. `disputed` 争论；激烈争辩（dispute的过去分词）
- vi. `dispute` 争论
- vt. `dispute` 辩论；怀疑；阻止；抗拒

# 例句

The referee's decision was disputable.
裁判员的裁决有争议。
