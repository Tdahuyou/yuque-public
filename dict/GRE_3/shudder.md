# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃʌdər/`
- 美 `/'ʃʌdɚ/`

# 词义

v. 战栗，发抖
`to shake for a short time because you are afraid or cold, or because you think something is very unpleasant`

n. 战栗，发抖
`a shaking movement`



“吓得” → 吓得肩膀(shoulder)直发抖(shudder)

# 同义

n. 发抖；战栗；震动
`shock` `shaking`

vi. 发抖；战栗
`shake` `tremble`

# 短语

- `shudder at` 不寒而栗；由于恐惧而发抖

# 例句

Maria shuddered as she stepped outside.
玛丽亚一走到外面就打了个冷战。
