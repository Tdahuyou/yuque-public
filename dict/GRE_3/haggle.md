# 音标

- 英 `/'hægl/`
- 美 `/'hæɡl/`

# 词义

v. 讨价还价
`to argue when you are trying to agree about the price of something`

# 同义

n. [贸易]讨价还价；争论
`controversy` `combat`

vt. 乱劈；乱砍
`cut and carve`

vi. 争论；乱劈
`debate` `argue with`

# 同根

- n. `haggling` 讨价还价；争论
- v. `haggling` 讨价还价；争辩（haggle的ing形式）

# 例句

Ella showed her the best places to go for a good buy, and taught her how to haggle with used furniture dealers.
埃拉告诉她买便宜货的最佳去处，还教她怎样与二手家具商讨价还价。
