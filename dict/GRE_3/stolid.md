# 音标

- 英 `/'stɑːlɪd/`
- 美 `/'stɑlɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 无动于衷的
`someone who is stolid does not react to situations or seem excited by them when most people would react – used to show disapproval`



solid(adj. 结实的)中间加个t

# 同义

adj. 迟钝的；缺乏热情的；冷漠的
`distant` `slow`

# 同根

- adv. `stolidly` 神经麻木地；冷淡地
- n. `stolidity` 感觉麻木；迟钝

# 例句

He conceals his feelings behind a rather stolid manner.
他装作无动于衷的样子以掩盖自己的感情。
