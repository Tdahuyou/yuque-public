# 音标

- 英 `/'skaɪrɑːkɪt/`
- 美 `/'skaɪ'rɑkɪt/`

# 词义

v. 突升，猛涨
`if a price or an amount skyrockets, it greatly increases very quickly`



sky(天空) ＋ rocket(火箭) → 火箭冲向天空， 突然升高 → 突升

# 同义

vi. 飞涨，突然高升
`sky` `sharp increase`

# 例句

The trade deficit has skyrocketed.
贸易逆差已经急剧增长。

skyrocketing inflation
飙升的通货膨胀率
