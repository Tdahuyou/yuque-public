# 音标

- 英 `/'piːsmiːl/`
- 美 `/'pismil/`

# 词义

adj. 一件一件的，零碎的
`a process that is piecemeal happens slowly and in stages that are not regular or planned properly`

# 同义

adj. 零碎的；逐渐的
`gradual` `bitty`

n. 片；块
`block` `mass` `slip`

vt. 粉碎
`comminute` `pulverize`

# 例句

The buildings have been adapted in a piecemeal fashion .
那些大楼的改造是零星进行的。

a piecemeal approach to the problem
一种头痛医头、脚痛医脚的解决问题方式
