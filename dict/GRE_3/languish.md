# 音标

- 英 `/'læŋgwɪʃ/`
- 美 `/'læŋɡwɪʃ/`

# 词义

v. 衰弱，憔悴
`ifsomething languishes, it fails to improve and develop or become successful`



lang(松弛) + uish → 衰弱

# 同义

vi. 憔悴；凋萎；失去活力；苦思
`consume away` `pine`

# 同根

- adj. `languishing` 渴望的；含情脉脉的；渐渐衰弱的

# 例句

The housing market continues to languish.
住宅市场继续疲软。

The shares are languishing at just 46p after yesterday’s fall.
股价经历了昨天的下跌后，就停滞在46便士上了。

West Ham United are currently languishing at the bottom of the league.
西汉姆联队目前在联赛中一直排名垫底。
