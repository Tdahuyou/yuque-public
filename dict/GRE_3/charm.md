# 音标

- 英 `/tʃɑːrm/`
- 美 `/tʃɑrm/`

# 词义

v. 吸引，迷住
`to attract someone and make them like you, sometimes in order to make them do something for you`

n. 魅力；咒语，咒符
`a special quality someone or something has that makes people like them, feel attracted to them, or be easily influenced by them – used to show approval`



char(音似： 茶) + m(看作man) → 被男士约出去喝茶， 因为很有魅力 → 魅力

# 同义

n. 魅力，吸引力；魔力
`grace` `appeal` `fascination`

vt. 使陶醉；行魔法
`intoxicate`

# 同根

- adj. `charming` 迷人的；可爱的
- adj. `charismatic` 超凡魅力的；神赐能力的
- adj. `charmed` 着迷的；喜悦的
- n. `charmer` 魔术师；可爱的人
- v. `charming` 使陶醉（charm的现在分词）
- v. `charmed` 迷住；对…行魔法；哄诱（charm的过去分词）

# 短语

- `artistic charm` 艺术魅力
- `like a charm` 效验如神地；异常成功地
- `lucky charm` 幸运符；护身符

# 例句

We were charmed by the friendliness of the local people.
我们被当地人的友好吸引住了。
