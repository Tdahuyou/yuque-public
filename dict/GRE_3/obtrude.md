# 音标

- 英 `/əb'truːd/`
- 美 `/əb'tru:d, ɔb-/`

# 词义

v. 突出；强加
`if something obtrudes, or you obtrude something, it becomes noticeable where it is not wanted`



ob(向外) + trud(伸出) + e → 向外伸 → 突出

# 同义

vt. 逼使；强迫；冲出
`force` `enforce`

vi. 打扰；闯入；强加于人
`interfere` `burst into`

# 例句

There are facts which I would not obtrude upon a child.
有些事实不应该强加于儿童。
