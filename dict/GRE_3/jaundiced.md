# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒɔːndɪst/`
- 美 `/'dʒɔndɪst/`

# 词义

adj. 有偏见的
`If someone has a jaundiced view of something, they can see only the bad aspects of it`



来自jaundice(n. 偏见)

# 同义

adj. 有偏见的；患黄疸病的
`biased` `tendentious`

# 同根

- n. `jaundice` 黄疸；偏见；乖僻
- vt. `jaundice` 使怀偏见；使患黄疸

# 短语

- `obstructive jaundice` 梗阻性黄疸；阻塞性黄疸
- `neonatal jaundice` 新生儿黄疸

# 例句

The financial markets are taking a jaundiced view of the Government's motives.
金融市场正用一个偏颇的角度来看政府的动机。
