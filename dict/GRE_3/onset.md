# 音标

- 英 `/'ɑːnset/`
- 美 `/'ɑnsɛt/`

# 词义

n. 开始，发作；攻击，袭击
`Theonsetof something is the beginning of it, used especially to refer to something unpleasant`

# 同义

n. 开始，着手；发作；攻击，进攻
`beginning` `threshold` `opening` `conception` `fresh`

# 短语

- `onset time` 初动时间

# 例句

Most of the passes have been closed with the onset of winter.
随着冬天的来临，大多数的关卡都已经关闭。
