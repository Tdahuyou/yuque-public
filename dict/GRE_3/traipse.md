# 音标

- 英 `/treɪps/`
- 美 `/treps/`

# 词义

v. 漫步，闲荡
`to walk somewhere in a slow or unwilling way because you are tired or bored`

# 同义

vi. 疲惫地走；闲荡；拖曳
`moon` `drag`

n. 闲荡；漫步；无精打采者
`mopery` `fooling around`

vt. 在…游荡；走过
`step by` `walk through`

# 例句

A better tip still is to get off your bottom and strut ( or even traipse) around the office.
还有个更好的建议，就是站起来，饶著办公室大踏步，甚至可以到处闲逛。
