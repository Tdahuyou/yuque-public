# 音标

- 英 `/aust/`
- 美 `/aʊst/`

# 词义

v. 驱逐，把…赶走
`to force someone out of a position of power, especially so that you can take their place`



out(出去)中加上s → 死也要让他出去 → 驱逐

# 同义

vt. 驱逐；剥夺；取代
`shear` `expel`

# 同根

- n. `ouster` 驱逐；剥夺；罢黜

# 例句

The leaders have been ousted from power by nationalists.
那些领导人已被民族主义者赶下了台。
