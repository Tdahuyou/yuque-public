# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'sɪprəkeɪt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'sɪprəket/`

# 词义

v. 回报，答谢
`to do or give something, because something similar has been done or given to you`



re + cip(收下) + rocate → 再次收下 → 答谢

# 同义

vt. 报答；互换；互给
`return` `requite`

vi. [机]往复运动；互换；酬答；互给
`to and fro motion` `alternate motion`

# 同根

- adj. `reciprocating` 往复的；交互的；摆动的
- n. `reciprocating` 往复
- n. `reciprocation` 交换；往复运动；报答
- v. `reciprocating` 往复运动（reciprocate的ing形式）；互给；报答

# 例句

When he spoke I was expected to reciprocate with some remark of my own.
他在说话时，我也应该说点什么回应他。
