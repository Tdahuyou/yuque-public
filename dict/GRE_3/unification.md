# 音标

- 英 `/ˌjuːnɪfɪ'keɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,jʊnəfə'keʃən/`

# 词义

n. 统一，一致
`the act of combining two or more groups, countries etc to make a single group or country`



来自unify(v. 统一)

# 同义

n. 统一；一致；联合
`accordance` `alliance` `agreement` `identity` `combination` `union` `association`

# 例句

...the process of European unification.
…欧洲统一进程。
