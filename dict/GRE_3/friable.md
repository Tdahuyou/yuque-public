# 音标

- 英 `/'fraɪəbl/`
- 美 `/'fraɪəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 脆的，易碎的
`friable rocks or soil are easily broken into very small pieces or into powder`

# 同义

adj. 易碎的；脆弱的
`delicate` `tender` `fragile` `brittle`

# 同根

- n. `friability` 易碎性；脆弱

# 例句

The softer, friable part of land; soil, especially productive soil.
泥软且易碎的陆地部分；泥土，尤指肥沃的泥土。

This patient had severe ulcerative, friable atheromatous plaques and had undergone angiography, which increases the risk for such emboli.
该病人有严重的溃疡形成，易碎的粥样斑块，同时也做了会增加血栓形成危险的血管造影术。
