# 音标

- 英 `/'derəgeɪt/`
- 美 `/'dɛrəɡet/`

# 词义

v. 贬低，诽谤
`to cause to seem inferior or be in disrepute; detract `



de(坏) + rog(问， 说) + ate → 说坏话 → 贬低

# 同义

vi. 减损；贬损
`preach down` `take away from`

vt. 毁损；贬低；诽谤
`lower` `wrong`

# 同根

- adj. `derogatory` 贬损的
- adj. `derogative` 减损的；毁损的
- n. `derogation` 毁损，减损；堕落

# 短语

- `derogate from` 毁损；违背

# 例句

People often derogate others in social comparison and the past selves in temporal comparison so that they can achieve self-enhancement and ultimately get good self-feelings.
在社会比较中人们会贬低他人，在时间比较中人们会贬低过去的自我，从而来达到提高自我，最终使现在的自我感觉良好。
