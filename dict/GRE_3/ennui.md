# 音标

- 英 `/ɑːn'wiː/`
- 美 `/ɑn'wi/`

# 词义

n. 倦怠；
`a feeling of being tired, bored, and unsatisfied with your life`

# 同义

n. （法）厌倦，无聊；倦怠
`boredom` `taedium`

# 例句

The patient's cheerful smile was just a facade ; actually she was suffering from ennui.
那病人愉快的微笑只是表面上的；事实上她倦怠的很。

To overcome ennui, Orbuch said, both parties should focus on adding spice to the relationship, which can be as simple as a new restaurant or vacation spot.
为了克服倦怠，欧布斯说，双方应集中于增加关系的情趣，这些情趣可以是简单的如一间新开的餐厅或是度假地。
