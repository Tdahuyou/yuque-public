# 音标

- 英 `/'plækɑːrd/`
- 美 `/'plækɑrd/`

# 词义

v. 张贴布告


n. 招贴，布告
`a large notice or advertisement on a piece of card, which is put up or carried in a public place`

# 同义

n. 海报，标语牌；招贴
`poster` `handbill`

vt. 张贴，悬挂；公布；张贴公告于
`post` `hang` `paste` `publish`

# 例句

A long placard bore the names of all the new recruits.
长长一张布告上写着全部新兵的名字。
