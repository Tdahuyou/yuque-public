# 音标

- 英 `/ə'fɪks/`
- 美 `/ə'fɪks/`

# 词义

v. 黏上，贴上；添上
`to fasten or stick something to something else`

n. 词缀
`a group of letters added to the beginning or end of a word to change its meaning or use, such as ‘un-’, ‘mis-’, ‘-ness’, or ‘-ly’`



af + fix(固定) → 固定上去 → 黏上， 贴上

# 同义

vt. 粘上；署名；将罪责加之于
`paste on`

n. [语]词缀；附加物
`appendix` `annexment`

# 同根

- n. `affixation` 附加，附加法
