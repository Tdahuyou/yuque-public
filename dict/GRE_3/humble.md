# 音标

- 英 `/'hʌmbl/`
- 美 `/'hʌmbl/`

# 词义

adj. 卑微的；谦虚的
`not considering yourself or your ideas to be as important as other people’s`

v. 使谦卑
`if you are humbled, you realize that you are not as important, good, kind etc as you thought you were`



hum(地面) + ble → 接近地面的 → 低下的 → 卑微的

# 例句

a modest and humble man
一个谦卑的男人
