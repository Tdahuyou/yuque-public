# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'duːsəbl/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

adj. 可诱导的


# 同义

adj. 可诱导的；可导致的
`derivable`

# 例句

This diauxie curve is typical of inducible enzymes.
这种双峰曲线是诱导酶的典型增长曲线。

Until now, crop breeders have paid little attention to inducible resistance.
直到现在为止， 作物培育者还很少注意诱导抗性。

Recently it was reported that an intravenously inducible medicine has now come on the market.
最近，据报道说一种在静脉内可诱导的药现在已经上市了。
