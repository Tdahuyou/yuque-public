# 音标

- 英 `/ɔːr'deɪn/`
- 美 `/ɔr'den/`

# 词义

v. 任命；颁发命令
`If some authority or power ordains something, they decide that it should happen or be in existence`

# 同义

vt. 任命某人为牧师；授某人以圣职；（上帝、法律等）命令；注定
`require` `order` `command` `fate`

# 例句

Nehru ordained that socialism should rule.
尼赫鲁下命实行社会主义制度。

His rule was ordained by heaven.
他的统治是天命的。
