# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdiː'briːf/`
- 美 `/di'brif/`

# 词义

v. 盘问，听取报告
`to ask someone questions about a job they have just done or an experience they have just had, in order to gather information`



de + brief(简述) → 听取报告

# 同义

vt. 盘问；听取报告
`cross examination` `catechize`

# 同根

- n. `debriefing` 任务报告；任务报告中提出的情报

# 例句

The returning bomber crews were debriefed.
询问了返回的轰炸机机组人员以了解情况。
