# 音标

- 英 `/'pæŋkriəs/`
- 美 `/'pæŋkrɪəs/`

# 词义

n. 胰腺
`a  gland  inside your body, near your stomach, that produces  insulin  and a liquid that helps your body to use the food that you eat`



pan(全部) + cre(肉) + as → 给身体生长提供养分的器官 → 胰腺

# 同根

- adj. `pancreatic` 胰的；胰腺的

# 短语

- `pancreas islet` 胰岛
