# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'pedɪmənt/`
- 美 `/ɪm'pɛdɪmənt/`

# 词义

n. 妨碍，阻碍物
`a situation or event that makes it difficult or impossible for someone or something to succeed or make progress`

# 同义

n. 口吃；妨碍；阻止
`prevention` `intervention` `violation` `check`

# 同根

- vt. `impede` 阻碍；妨碍；阻止

# 短语

- `speech impediment` 言语障碍者

# 例句

a speech impediment
言语障碍
