# 音标

- 英 `/'reprɪmænd/`
- 美 `/'rɛprɪmænd/`

# 词义

v. 训诫，谴责
`to tell someone officially that something they have done is very wrong`

n. 训诫，谴责
`Reprimand is also a noun`



re(重新) + prim(首要) + (m)and(命令) → 再次给以严厉的命令 → 谴责

# 同义

n. 谴责；训斥；申诉
`hell` `condemnation`

vt. 谴责；训斥；责难
`condemn` `denounce`

# 例句

He was reprimanded by a teacher for talking in the corridor.
他因在走廊里说话而被一位老师训斥。

He has been fined five thousand dollars and given a severe reprimand.
他被罚款5000美元，并且受到严厉的训斥。
