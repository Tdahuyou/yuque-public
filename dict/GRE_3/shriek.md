# 音标

- 英 `/ʃriːk/`
- 美 `/ʃrik/`

# 词义

v. 尖叫
`to make a very high loud sound, especially because you are afraid, angry, excited, or in pain`

# 同义

vi. 尖叫；促人注意
`pipe` `scream`

n. 尖声；尖锐的响声
`piping`

# 例句

They were dragged from their homes, shrieking and weeping.
他们哭喊着被人从家里拖出去。

He shrieked in agony.
他痛苦地尖叫起来。
