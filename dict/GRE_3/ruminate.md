# 音标

- 英 `/'ruːmɪneɪt/`
- 美 `/'rumɪnet/`

# 词义

v. 反刍；深思
`if animals such as cows ruminate, they bring food back into their mouths from their stomachs and  chew  it again`

# 同义

vt. 反刍；沉思；反复思考
`chew the cud` `remasticate`

vi. 沉思，反刍
`meditate on` `be deep in thought`

# 同根

- adj. `ruminant` 反刍的；沉思的
- adj. `ruminative` 默想的，沉思的；爱反复思考的
- n. `ruminant` 反刍动物
- n. `rumination` 沉思；反刍
- n. `ruminator` 沉思默想的人；好思考的人

# 例句

The cow began to ruminate after eating up grass.
牛吃完草后开始反刍。
