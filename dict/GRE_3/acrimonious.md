# 音标

- 英 `/ˌækrɪ'mouniəs/`
- 美 `/,ækri'məunjəs/`

# 词义

adj. 尖酸刻薄的，激烈的
`an acrimonious meeting or discussion is one in which people argue a lot and get very angry`

# 同义

adj. 严厉的；辛辣的；刻薄的
`severe` `tight` `hard` `harsh` `acid`

# 同根

- n. `acrimony` 辛辣；尖刻；严厉

# 例句

The meeting ended in an acrimonious dispute.
会议在激烈的辩论中结束。
