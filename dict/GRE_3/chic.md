# 音标

- 英 `/ʃiːk/`
- 美 `/ʃɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 漂亮的，时髦的
`very fashionable and expensive, and showing good judgement of what is attractive and good style`

# 同义

n. 时髦；别致的款式
`stylishness` `trendiness`

adj. 别致的
`picturesque`

# 同根

- adj. `chichi` 精致的；装模作样的
- n. `chichi` 矫揉；时髦的人；过度精巧的东西

# 例句

Margaret was looking very chic in blue.
玛格丽特穿着蓝色的衣服看上去很优雅。

a chic restaurant
一家雅致的餐厅
