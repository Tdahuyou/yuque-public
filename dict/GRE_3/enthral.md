# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'θrɔːl/`
- 美 `/ɪn'θrɔl/`

# 词义

v. 迷惑
`to make someone very interested and excited, so that they listen or watch something very carefully`



en(使) + thral(奴隶) → 使成为奴隶 → 迷惑

# 同义

vt. 奴役；使…做奴隶；迷惑（等于enthrall）
`inthrall` `beslave`

# 同根

- adj. `enthralling` 迷人的
- adj. `enthralled` 被迷住的
- n. `enthrallment` 奴役；沉迷
- v. `enthralling` 迷惑；奴役（enthral的现在分词）
- v. `enthralled` 迷住；奴役；令人愉快（enthral的过去分词）
- vt. `enthrall` 迷住，使着迷

# 例句

The passengers were enthralled by the scenery.
乘客们被这景色迷住了。
