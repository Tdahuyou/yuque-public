# 音标

- 英 `/ə'plɑːm/`
- 美 `/ə'plɑm/`

# 词义

n. 沉着，镇静
`If you do something with aplomb, you do it with confidence in a relaxed way`



apl(看作apple) + omb(看作tomb) → 坟墓中的苹果， 很静 → 镇静

# 同义

n. 沉着；垂直；泰然自若
`self-possession` `composedness`

# 例句

He delivered the speech with his usual aplomb.
他以惯常的沉着语气作了演讲。
