# 音标

- 英 `/'taɪmwɔːrn/`
- 美 `/'taɪm,worn/`

# 词义

adj. 陈旧的，陈腐的
`Something that is timeworn is old or has been used a lot over a long period of time`



time(时间)+worn(用旧的) → 陈旧的

# 同义

adj. 陈旧的；老朽的
`old` `shopworn`

# 例句

Even in the dim light the equipment looked old and timeworn.
即便在昏暗的光线下，这台设备看起来还是陈旧的。
