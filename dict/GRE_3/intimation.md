# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪntɪ'meɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɪ'meʃən/`

# 词义

n. 暗示
`An intimation is an indirect suggestion or sign that something is likely to happen or be true`

# 同义

n. [心理]暗示；告知；讽示
`connotation` `reminder` `implication` `allusion`

# 同根

- adj. `intimate` 亲密的；私人的；精通的；有性关系的
- n. `intimate` 知己；至交
- vt. `intimate` 暗示；通知；宣布

# 例句

I did not have any intimation that he was going to resign.
我没发现任何他要辞职的暗示。
