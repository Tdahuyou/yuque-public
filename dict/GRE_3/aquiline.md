# 音标

- 英 `/'ækwɪlaɪn/`
- 美 `/'ækwɪlaɪn/`

# 词义

adj. 鹰的，似鹰的
`If someone has an aquiline nose or profile, their nose is large, thin, and usually curved`



aquil(鹰) + ine → 鹰的

# 同义

adj. 鹰的；似鹰的，像鹰的；钩状的
`accipitral` `falconine`

# 例句

He had a thin aquiline nose and deep-set brown eyes.
他长着细长的鹰钩鼻和一双深陷的褐色眼睛。
