# 音标

- 英 `/ˌmɪnju'et/`
- 美 `/,mɪnju'ɛt/`

# 词义

n. 小步舞
`a slow dance of the 17th and 18th centuries, or a piece of music for this dance`



min(小的) + uet → 小步舞

# 例句

E—Egret in a minuet.
白鹭在跳小步舞。

The pupils laughed at the sight of their teacher dancing a minuet in the yard.
小学生们一看到他们的老师在院子里跳小舞步都笑起来了。
