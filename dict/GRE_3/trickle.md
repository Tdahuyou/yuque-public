# 音标

- 英 `/'trɪkl/`
- 美 `/'trɪkl/`

# 词义

v. 细细地流
`if liquid trickles somewhere, it flows slowly in drops or in a thin stream`

n. 细流
`a thin slow flow of liquid`

# 同义

vi. 滴；细细地流；慢慢地移动
`creep`

n. 滴，淌；细流
`drop` `gutta`

# 短语

- `trickle down` 向下滴流
- `trickle irrigation` 滴灌；滴灌系统

# 例句

A tear trickled down the old man's cheek.
一滴泪水顺着老人的面颊流了下来。
