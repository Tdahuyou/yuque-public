# 音标

- 英 `/ˌstræŋgju'leɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,stræŋɡju'leʃən/`

# 词义

n. 扼杀，勒死
`the act of killing someone by pressing on their throat, or the fact of being killed in this way`



来自strangle(v. 扼杀， 抑制)

# 同义

n. [医]绞窄；勒杀；钳制；压缩
`compression` `hitting`

# 同根

- vi. `strangulate` 窒息
- vt. `strangulate` 勒杀；使窒息；绞死

# 例句

He is charged with the strangulation of two students.
他被指控勒死了两名学生。
