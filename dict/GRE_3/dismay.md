# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'meɪ/`
- 美 `/dɪs'me/`

# 词义

v. 使气馁


n. 沮丧，气馁
`the worry, disappoint-ment, or unhappiness you feel when something unpleasant happens`



dis(不) + may(可能) → 不可能做 → 沮丧

# 同义

n. 沮丧，灰心；惊慌
`depression` `alarm` `disappointment`

vt. 使沮丧；使惊慌
`depress` `dispirit`

# 同根

- adj. `dismayed` 吃惊，惊愕；心慌；心中不快
- v. `dismayed` 使…灰心；使…害怕（dismay的过去式及过去分词）

# 例句

The committee was dismayed by what it had been told.
该委员会被所告知的情况搞得焦虑不安。
