# 音标

- 英 `/slɑːg/`
- 美 `/slɑɡ/`

# 词义

v. 猛击；苦干
`to work hard at something without stopping, especially when the work is difficult, tiring, or boring`

# 同义

vt. 猛击；步履艰难地行
`jab at` `lash out`

vi. 猛击；顽强行进
`drive` `belt`

n. 苦干；跋涉
`tug`

# 同根

- n. `slogger` 猛击者；乱打者；勤劳的工作者

# 例句

Mother slogged all her life for us.
妈妈为我们操劳了一辈子。
