# 音标

- 英 `/gʌlp/`
- 美 `/ɡʌlp/`

# 词义

v. 吞食，咽下；抵制，忍住
`If you gulp something, you eat or drink it very quickly by swallowing large quantities of it at once`

# 同义

vt. 狼吞虎咽地吃；大口地吸
`wolf`

vi. 哽住；喘不过气
`lose one's breath`

n. 一大口（尤指液体）；吞咽
`deglutition` `swallowing`

# 同根

- n. `gulper` 囊咽鱼

# 短语

- `gulp down` 狼吞虎咽地吃；忍住

# 例句

She quickly gulped her soda.
她很快地把汽水一饮而尽。
