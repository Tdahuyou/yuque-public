# 音标

- 英 `/kə'nɑːrd/`
- 美 `/kə'nɑrd/`

# 词义

n. 谣言，假新闻
`a piece of news that is false and is told to people deliberately in order to harm someone`



金丝雀(canary)在造谣(canard)

# 同义

n. 误传，谬传；谣言
`misrepresentation` `rumor`

# 例句

The charge that Harding was a political stooge may be a canard.
关于哈丁是政治密探的指控可能是个谣传。
