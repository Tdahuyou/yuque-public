# 音标

- 英 `/ə'bleɪz/`
- 美 `/ə'blez/`

# 词义

adj. 着火的，燃烧的；闪耀的
`burning strongly with a lot of flames`

# 同义

adj. 着火的；闪耀的；激昂的
`brilliant` `heated`

adv. 着火；闪耀
`on fire`

# 例句

Within minutes the whole house was ablaze.
几分钟内，整座房子就成了一片火海。

The factory had been set ablaze (=  made to burn  ) .
那家工厂被纵火焚烧。
