# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒesteɪt/`
- 美 `/'dʒɛstet/`

# 词义

v. 怀孕，孕育；构思
`to carry (developing young) in the uterus during pregnancy `



gest(=carry带有) + ate → 有了 → 怀孕

# 同义

vt. 使怀孕
`impregnate`

# 同根

- adj. `gestational` 妊娠期的；受孕的
- n. `gestation` 酝酿；怀孕；妊娠期

# 例句

It was in ancient times that the thought of pedagogical pragmatics began to gestate and form.
古代是教学语用学思想的孕育和形成时期。
