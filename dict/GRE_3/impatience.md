# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'peɪʃns/`
- 美 `/ɪm'peʃəns/`

# 词义

n. 不耐烦，焦躁
`annoyance at having to accept delays, other people’s weaknesses etc`



来自patient (adj. 有耐心的)

# 同义

n. 急躁；无耐心
`temperament` `precipitance`

# 同根

- adj. `impatient` 焦躁的；不耐心的
- adv. `impatiently` 无耐性地

# 例句

A note of impatience had entered his voice.
他的语气开始透出一丝不耐烦。
