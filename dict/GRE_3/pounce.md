# 音标

- 英 `/pauns/`
- 美 `/paʊns/`

# 词义

v. 猛扑；突然袭击
`to suddenly move forward and attack someone or something, after waiting to attack them`

n. 爪；猛扑
`the act of pouncing; a spring or swoop `

# 同义

vi. 突袭，猛扑
`sweep down` `bound on`

n. 猛扑，爪
`talon` `unguis`

# 短语

- `pounce on` 猛扑向；突然袭击

# 例句

The cat was hiding in the bushes, ready to pounce.
猫躲在灌木丛中，准备扑上去。
