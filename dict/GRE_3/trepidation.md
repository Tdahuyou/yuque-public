# 音标

- 英 `/ˌtrepɪ'deɪʃn/`
- 美 `/ˌtrɛpəˈdeʃən/`

# 词义

n. 恐惧，惶恐
`a feeling of anxiety or fear about something that is going to happen`



trep(害怕) + id + ation → 恐惧， 惶恐

# 同义

n. 恐惧；惊恐；忧虑；颤抖
`fear` `care` `disturbance` `worries`

# 同根

- adj. `trepid` 惊恐的；胆小的；不安的

# 例句

With some trepidation, I opened the door.
我有些惴惴不安地打开了门。
