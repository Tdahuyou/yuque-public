# 音标

- 英 `/fɔːr'ber/`
- 美 `/fɔr'bɛr/`

# 词义

v. 克制；忍耐
`to not do something you could or would like to do because you think it is wiser not to`

# 同义

vt. 忍耐；克制
`resist` `tough`

vi. 忍耐；克制
`resist` `to endure`

n. 祖先
`father` `root`

# 同根

- adj. `forbearing` 宽容的；忍耐的
- n. `forbearance` 自制，忍耐；宽容
- v. `forbearing` 克制（forbear的现在分词）

# 短语

- `bear and forbear` [古语]一忍再忍

# 例句

I forbore to comment on this.
我不欲评论它
