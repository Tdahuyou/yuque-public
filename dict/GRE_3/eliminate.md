# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'lɪmɪneɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪ'lɪmɪnet/`

# 词义

v. 除去，淘汰
`to defeat a team or person in a competition, so that they no longer take part in it`



e(出) + limin(看作limit， 界限) + ate → 划到界限之外 → 除去， 淘汰

# 同义

vt. 消除；排除
`avoid` `exclude`

# 同根

- n. `elimination` 消除；淘汰；除去
- n. `eliminator` 消除器；消除者

# 短语

- `eliminate poverty` 消除贫困
- `eliminate noise` 消除噪声
- `eliminate illiteracy` 扫盲，扫除文盲

# 例句

Our team was eliminated in the first round.
我们队第一轮就被淘汰了。
