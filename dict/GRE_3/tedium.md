# 音标

- 英 `/'tiːdiəm/`
- 美 `/'tidɪəm/`

# 词义

n. 冗长乏味
`the feeling of being bored because the things you are doing are not interesting and continue for a long time without changing`



媒体(medium)的节目都很乏味(tedium)

# 同义

n. 沉闷；单调乏味；厌烦
`heavy-footedness` `oppression`

# 同根

- adj. `tedious` 沉闷的；冗长乏味的
- adv. `tediously` 沉闷地；冗长而乏味地
- n. `tediousness` 沉闷；乏味

# 例句

We sang while we worked, to relieve the tedium .
我们边唱歌边干活来排遣无聊。
