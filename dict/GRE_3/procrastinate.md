# 音标

- 英 `/prou'kræstɪneɪt/`
- 美 `/pro'kræstɪnet/`

# 词义

v. 耽搁，拖延
`to delay doing something that you ought to do, usually because you do not want to do it`



pro(向前) + crastin(明天) + ate → 直到明天再干 → 拖延

# 同义

vt. 耽搁，延迟
`time delay` `detain`

vi. 耽搁，延迟
`wait` `time delay`

# 同根

- n. `procrastination` 耽搁，拖延
- n. `procrastinator` 拖延者，拖拉者；因循者

# 例句

People often procrastinate when it comes to paperwork.
遇到文书工作，人们常常会拖拖拉拉。
