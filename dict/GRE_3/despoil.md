# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spɔɪl/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spɔɪl/`

# 词义

v. 夺取，抢夺
`To despoil a place means to make it less attractive, valuable, or important by taking things away from it or by destroying it`



de + spoil(夺取， 宠坏) → 夺取， 抢夺

# 同义

vt. 掠夺，剥夺；夺取
`harry` `shear` `seize`

# 同根

- n. `despoilment` 剥夺；抢夺

# 例句

...people who despoil the countryside.
...掠夺乡村的人。
