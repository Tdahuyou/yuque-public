# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'trʌst/`
- 美 `/ɪn'trʌst/`

# 词义

v. 委托；托付
`to make someone responsible for doing something important, or for taking care of someone`



en(使) + trust(相信) → 给予信任 → 委托

# 同义

vt. 委托，[贸易]信托
`refer` `leave in trust`

# 短语

- `entrust with` 委托；信托

# 例句

He entrusted his cash to a business partner for investment in a series of projects.
他把现款委托给一个生意合伙人投资一系列项目。
