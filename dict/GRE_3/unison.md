# 音标

- 英 `/'juːnɪsn/`
- 美 `/'jʊnəsn/`

# 词义

n. 齐奏，齐唱；一致，协调
`if people speak or do something in unison, they say the same words at the same time or do the same thing at the same time`

# 同义

n. 和谐；齐唱；同度；[声]同音
`accordance` `concert`

# 短语

- `in unison` 一致地

# 例句

‘Good morning!’ the kids replied in unison.
“早上好！”孩子们齐声应答。
