# 音标

- 英 `/'traɪfl/`
- 美 `/'traɪfl/`

# 词义

n. 微不足道的事物，琐事
`A trifle is something that is considered to have little importance, value, or significance`

# 同义

n. 琐事；蛋糕；少量
`cake` `bit`

vi. 开玩笑；闲混；嘲弄
`fool` `kid around`

vt. 浪费；虚度
`lose` `spend` `moon` `fool away`

# 同根

- adj. `trifling` 微不足道的；轻浮的
- v. `trifling` 浪费，闲聊（trifle的现在分词）

# 短语

- `trifle with` 玩弄；视同儿戏；心不在焉地做
- `mere trifle` 小意思

# 例句

He had no money to spare on trifles.
他没钱花在琐碎的东西上。
