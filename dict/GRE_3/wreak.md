# 音标

- 英 `/riːk/`
- 美 `/rik/`

# 词义

v. 发泄，报复；发泄怒火
`to do something unpleasant to someone to punish them for something they have done to you`

# 同义

vt. 发泄；报仇
`give vent to` `take revenge`

# 短语

- `wreak havoc` 肆虐；造成严重破坏

# 例句

He promised to wreak vengeance on those who had betrayed him.
他发誓要报复那些背叛他的人。
