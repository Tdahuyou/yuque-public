# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'mɪtns/`
- 美 `/rɪ'mɪtns/`

# 词义

n. 汇款
`an amount of money that you send to pay for something`

# 同根

- n. `remit` 移交的事物
- vi. `remit` 汇款；缓和
- vt. `remit` 宽恕；免除；减轻；传送；使恢复原状

# 短语

- `remittance slip` [经]汇款凭单
- `postal remittance` [经]邮政划拨
- `outward remittance` [经]汇出汇款
- `bank remittance` 银行汇款底单
- `inward remittance` 汇入汇款

# 例句

Please enclose your remittance, making checks payable to Valley Technology Services.
请随信附上您的汇款，支票收款方为瓦利科技服务公司。
