# 音标

- 英 `/snaɪp/`
- 美 `/snaɪp/`

# 词义

v. 狙击
`to shoot from a hidden position at people who are not protected`

# 同义

n. [军]狙击；[鸟]鹬；香烟屁股；被开玩笑的人物
`sandpiper`

vi. 狙击；[军]伏击；抨击；猎鸟；诽谤
`game bird` `fling at`

# 同根

- n. `sniper` 狙击兵

# 例句

The media were still sniping at the president's adviser yesterday.
昨天媒体依旧在抨击总统的顾问。
