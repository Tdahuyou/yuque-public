# 音标

- 英 `/'tremər/`
- 美 `/'trɛmɚ/`

# 词义

n. 颤动；颤抖，战栗
`a slight shaking movement in your body that you cannot control, especially because you are ill, weak, or upset`



trem(抖动)+or → 颤动； 颤抖

# 同义

n. [医]震颤；[物]颤动
`fremitus` `zitterbewegung`

# 同根

- adj. `tremulous` 胆小的；战栗的；震颤的
- adv. `tremulously` 发抖地；震颤地；歪斜地；过分敏感地
- n. `trembler` 震颤的东西；发抖的人；摇尾鸟

# 例句

News of 160 lay-offs had sent tremors through the community.
裁员160人的消息在社区中引起了波动。
