# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪnsɪn'sɪr/`
- 美 `/,ɪnsɪn'sɪr/`

# 词义

adj. 不诚恳的，虚伪的
`pretending to be pleased, sympathetic etc, especially by saying nice things, but not really meaning what you say`

# 同义

adj. 不诚实的；虚假的
`dishonest` `unfaithful`

# 同根

- adv. `insincerely` 无诚意地；不诚实地
- n. `insincerity` 伪善；无诚意；不诚实

# 例句

an insincere smile
虚伪的微笑

an offer which she knew to be impractical and insincere
一个她认为不实际、不真诚的建议
