# 音标

- 英 `/'staɪfl/`
- 美 `/'staɪfl/`

# 词义

v. 感到窒息；抑止
`if you are stifled by something, it stops you breathing comfortably`

# 同义

vt. 扼杀；使窒息；藏匿
`kill` `snuff out`

vi. 窒息；被扼杀
`choke`

# 同根

- adj. `stifling` 令人窒息的；沉闷的
- v. `stifling` 使窒息；粉碎（stifle的现在分词）

# 例句

He was almost stifled by the fumes.
他差点被烟熏得窒息了。
