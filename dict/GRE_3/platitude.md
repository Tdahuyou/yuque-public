# 音标

- 英 `/'plætɪtuːd/`
- 美 `/'plætɪtud/`

# 词义

n. 陈词滥调
`a statement that has been made many times before and is not interesting or clever – used to show disapproval`



plat(平的) + itude → 平庸之词 → 陈词滥调

# 同义

n. 陈词滥调；平凡；陈腐
`lousy cliches` `banality`

# 同根

- adj. `platitudinous` 平凡的，陈腐的；陈词滥调的
- vi. `platitudinize` 说平凡的话；用陈词滥调
- vt. `platitudinize` 说平凡的话；说陈腔滥调

# 例句

His excuse was the platitude ‘boys will be boys.’
他还是那一套理由:“男孩总归是男孩。”
