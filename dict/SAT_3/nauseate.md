# 音标

- 英 `/'nɔːzieit/`
- 美 `/'nɔzɪet/`

# 词义

v. 产生恶感，厌恶；作呕
`If something nauseates you, it makes you feel as if you are going to vomit`



nau (船) + se + ate (使…) → 晕船使人作呕 → 作呕

# 同义

vi. 作呕；厌恶；产生恶感
`turn away from` `keck`

vt. 使厌恶；使恶心；使作呕
`indispose` `repel`

# 同根

- adj. `nauseous` 令人作呕的；厌恶的
- adj. `nauseated` 厌恶的；作呕的
- adj. `nauseating` 令人恶心的；厌恶的
- n. `nausea` 恶心，晕船；极端的憎恶
- v. `nauseated` 作呕；厌恶（nauseate的过去分词）
- v. `nauseating` 使恶心（nauseate的ing形式）

# 例句

The smell of frying nauseated her.
油炸味道让她恶心。

She could not eat anything without feeling nauseated.
她吃什么都想吐。
