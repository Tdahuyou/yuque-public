# 音标

- 英 `/'vɔːdəvil/`
- 美 `/'vɔdəvɪl/`

# 词义

n. 歌舞杂耍；综合演出
`a type of theatre entertainment, popular from the 1880s to the 1950s, in which there were many short performances of different kinds, including singing, dancing, jokes etc`

# 同义

n. 杂耍；轻歌舞剧；歌舞杂耍表演
`variety` `jugglery`

# 同根

- n. `vaudevillian` 杂耍演员；轻歌舞剧编剧者

# 例句

A case might be made for the blackface minstrel show vaudeville or for the entertainments of some medicine shows camivals and riverboats as american folk drama.
滑稽说唱团演出、杂耍演出，或一些娱乐性的药品巡回演出、表演会与内河游艺船等，均可作为美国民间戏剧的例证。
