# 音标

- 英 `/'pəːkəˌleit/`
- 美 `/'pɝkəlet/`

# 词义

v. 过滤
`if liquid, light, or air percolates somewhere, it passes slowly through a material that has very small holes in it`



per (贯穿) + col (l) ate (过滤) → 过滤出 → 过滤

# 同义

vi. 过滤；渗出；浸透
`filtrate` `water logging`

vt. 使渗出；使过滤
`exude`

n. 滤过液；渗出液
`transduate` `diffusate`

# 同根

- n. `percolation` 过滤；浸透
- n. `percolator` 过滤器；渗滤器；咖啡渗滤壶
