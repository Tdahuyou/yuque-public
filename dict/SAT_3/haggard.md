# 音标

- 英 `/'hægəd/`
- 美 `/'hæɡɚd/`

# 词义

adj. 憔悴的，形容枯槁的；野性的
`someone who looks haggard has lines on their face and dark marks around their eyes, especially because they are ill, worried, or tired`



“黑色的” → 他一脸黑色， 很憔悴 → 憔悴的

# 同义

adj. 憔悴的；野性的
`gaunt` `toilworn`

# 例句

Sam looked tired and haggard.
萨姆看起来疲惫而憔悴。

a haggard face
憔悴的面容
