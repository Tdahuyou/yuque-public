# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒipsi/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 吉普赛人
`a member of a group of people originally from India, who traditionally live and travel around in  caravans  , and who now live all over the world. Most gypsies prefer to be called  romanies  .`

# 同义

n. 吉卜赛人；吉卜赛语；像吉布赛的人；歌舞剧中的歌舞队员
`Gippy` `diddy`

vi. 流浪
`vagabondize` `stray`

# 短语

- `gypsy moth` 舞毒蛾（等于Porthetria dispar）

# 例句

I informed myself from the gypsy .
我是听吉卜赛人说的。

She has pure gypsy blood in her veins.
她血管里流的是纯吉卜赛人的血液。
