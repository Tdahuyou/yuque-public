# 音标

- 英 `/'væsəleit/`
- 美 `/'væsileit/`

# 词义

v. 摆动，震荡；摇摆不定，犹豫不决
`If you vacillate between two alternatives or choices, you keep changing your mind`



vacill (摇摆) + ate → 摇摆不定

# 同义

vi. 犹豫；踌躇；摇摆
`hesitate` `daiker`

# 同根

- adj. `vacillant` 犹豫不决的；游移的；摇摆不定的
- adj. `vacillating` 优柔寡断的；犹豫的
- n. `vacillation` 踌躇；不果断；游移不定
- n. `vacillator` 犹豫的人
- v. `vacillating` 动摇（vacillate的ing形式）；犹豫

# 例句

She vacillates between men twice her age and men younger than she.
她在两个男人之间摇摆不定，一个年龄比她大一倍，一个比她小。

We cannot vacillate on the question of the party's leadership.
在党的领导权问题上我们不能动摇。
