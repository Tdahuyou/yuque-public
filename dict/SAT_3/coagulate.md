# 音标

- 英 `/kəu'ægjuleit/`
- 美 `/ko'æɡjulet/`

# 词义

v. 凝结
`if a liquid coagulates, or something coagulates it, it becomes thick and almost solid`



co (一起) + ag (做) + ulate → 做到一起 → 使凝结

# 同义

vt. 使…凝结
`congeal`

vi. 凝结
`concrete` `to freeze`

# 同根

- adj. `coagulable` 可凝结的
- n. `coagulation` 凝固，凝结；凝结物
- n. `coagulant` [建][化工] 促凝剂；凝血剂；凝结剂
- n. `coagulator` [助剂] 凝结剂；[化工] 凝结器
- n. `coagulum` 凝结物；凝固物；凝块

# 例句

The blood had not coagulated.
血未凝结。
