# 音标

- 英 `/əb'siːkwiəs/`
- 美 `/əb'sikwɪəs/`

# 词义

adj. 谄媚的，奉承的，拍马屁的
`very eager to please or agree with people who are powerful – used in order to show disapproval`



ob (接近) + sequ (跟随) + i + ous (…的) → 紧跟着 → 谄媚的

# 同义

adj. 谄媚的；奉承的；顺从的
`flattering` `well-oiled`

# 同根

- adv. `obsequiously` 谄媚地；奉承地
- n. `obsequiousness` 谄媚；奉承

# 例句

an obsequious smile
谄媚的微笑
