# 音标

- 英 `/'prɔdigəl/`
- 美 `/'prɑdɪɡl/`

# 词义

adj. 浪费的
`someone who leaves their family and home without the approval of their family, but who is sorry later and returns`

n. 挥霍者
`someone who spends money carelessly and wastes their time – used humorously`



prodig (巨大， 浪费) + al → 浪费的

# 同义

adj. 挥霍的；十分慷慨的
`flush`

n. 浪子；挥霍者
`dissipator` `spendthrifty`

# 同根

- adv. `prodigally` 浪费地；丰饶地
- n. `prodigality` 浪费；慷慨；丰富

# 短语

- `prodigal son` n. 悔改的罪人；回头的浪子

# 例句

a prodigal lifestyle
奢侈的生活方式
