# 音标

- 英 `/'vaisrɔi/`
- 美 `/'vaɪsrɔɪ/`

# 词义

n. 总督
`a man who was sent by a king or queen in the past to rule another country`

# 同义

n. 总督；（北美）一种黑色蝴蝶
`dey` `doge`

# 同根

- adj. `viceregal` 总督的；代表王权的
- n. `vicereine` 总督夫人；女总督；副王夫人
- n. `viceroyalty` 总督的职位（职权或辖区）
- n. `viceroyship` 任期；职权；辖区（总督的）

# 例句

And the viceroy was there with his lady.
总督也带着夫人去啦。

How do you think this trade viceroy will deal with the chancellor's demands?
贸易总督会怎样回应首相的要求呢?。
