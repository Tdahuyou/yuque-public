# 音标

- 英 `/ˌaut'duː/`
- 美 `/,aʊt'du/`

# 词义

v. 胜过
`to be better or more successful than someone else at doing something`



out (超过) + do → 胜过

# 同义

vt. 超过；胜过
`better than` `overcome` `top` `cap`

# 短语

- `outdo oneself` 尽自己最大努力；超过自己原来水平

# 例句

When it comes to speed of response, a small firm can outdo a big company.
说到反应速度，小公司会胜过大公司。
