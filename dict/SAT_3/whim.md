# 音标

- 英 `/hwim/`
- 美 `/wɪm/`

# 词义

n. 一时的兴致；幻想
`a sudden feeling that you would like to do or have something, especially when there is no important or good reason`



不要在意him (他) whim (一时的兴致) → 一时的兴致

# 同义

n. 奇想；一时的兴致；怪念头；幻想
`vagary` `fancy`

# 同根

- n. `whimsy` 怪念头；反复无常
- n. `whimsey` 奇想；怪念头

# 例句

Using a treeview to represent arbitrary objects organized in an arbitrary fashion at the whim of a programmer is asking for big trouble when it comes to usability.
程序员一时兴起以一种武断的方式来采用树形视图表现随意的对象，在可用性方面会遇到很大的问题。
