# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdispen'seiʃən/`
- 美 `/,dispen'seiʃən/`

# 词义

n. 分配；豁免
`A dispensation is special permission to do something that is normally not allowed`



来自dispense (v. 分配， 分发)

# 同义

n. 分配；免除；豁免；天命
`allocation` `distribution` `assignment` `destiny` `immunity`

# 同根

- n. `dispenser` 药剂师；施与者；分配者；自动售货机
- n. `dispensability` 可分配；可特别恕免；可宽恕
- vi. `dispense` 免除，豁免
- vt. `dispense` 分配，分发；免除；执行

# 例句

They were promised dispensation from military service.
他们被许诺将被特免不用服兵役。
