# 音标

- 英 `/ˌresti'tjuːʃən/`
- 美 `/'rɛstə'tʊʃən/`

# 词义

n. 归还
`the act of giving back something that was lost or stolen to its owner, or of paying for damage`



re (重新) + stitut (站立) + ion → 重新站过去 → 归还

# 同义

n. 恢复；赔偿；归还
`recovery` `satisfaction` `retrieval` `renewal` `return`

# 同根

- vt. `restitute` 复原；归还；补偿

# 短语

- `coefficient of restitution` 恢复系数；回弹系数

# 例句

The offender must make restitution for the hurt that he or she has caused.
违法者必须为其所造成的伤害作出赔偿。
