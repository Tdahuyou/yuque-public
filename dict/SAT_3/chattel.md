# 音标

- 英 `/'tʃætəl/`
- 美 `/'tʃætl/`

# 词义

n. 动产；私人财产
`a piece of personal property that you can move from one place to another`



在战争 (battle) 中很多人沦为奴隶 (chattel)

# 同义

n. 动产；奴隶（常用复数）
`goods` `slave`

# 短语

- `chattel mortgage` n. 动产抵押

# 例句

a society in which women are considered to be chattels
视女人为私人财产的社会
