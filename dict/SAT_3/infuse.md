# 音标

- 英 `/in'fjuːz/`
- 美 `/ɪn'fjuz/`

# 词义

v. 输入，注入；浸泡
`if you infuse tea or herbs or if they infuse, you leave them in very hot water while their taste passes into the water`



in (进入) + fuse (倾注) → 注入

# 同义

vt. 灌输；使充满；浸渍
`freight` `indoctrinate`

# 同根

- n. `infusion` 灌输；浸泡；注入物；激励

# 例句

Many of the girls seemed to be infused with excitement on seeing the snow.
许多女孩子似乎一看到雪心里就充满了兴奋。
