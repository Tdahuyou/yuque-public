# 音标

- 英 `/kʌl/`
- 美 `/kʌl/`

# 词义

v. 精选
`to kill animals so that there are not too many of them, or so that a disease does not spread`



斗牛用的公牛 (bull) 要精选 (cull)

# 同义

vt. 精选；采集（鲜花等）；剔除
`pick` `handpick`

# 例句

They are then taken back to the slaughterhouse, where workers cull them and excise their livers.
然后它们会被送回屠宰场，那里的工人会进行精选，并切除它们的肝脏。
