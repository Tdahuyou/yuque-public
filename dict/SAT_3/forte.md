# 音标

- 英 `/fɔːt/`
- 美 `/'fɔrte/`

# 词义

n. 特长；专长
`to be something that you do well or are skilled at`



fort (强的) + e → 强的地方 → 专长

# 同义

n. 长处；特长
`strength` `excellence`

adv. 响亮地
`loudly` `ringingly`

# 例句

He found that running long distances was not his forte .
他发现长跑并不是他的强项。

As a writer, her forte is comedy.
作为作家，她的专长是写喜剧。
