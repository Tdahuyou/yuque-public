# 音标

- 英 `/bi'gail/`
- 美 `/bɪ'ɡaɪl/`

# 词义

vt. 诱骗；诱惑
`If someone beguiles you into doing something, they trick you into doing it`



be + guile (欺诈) → 欺骗 → 诱骗

# 同义

vt. 欺骗；使着迷；轻松地消磨
`sell` `fox` `kid`

# 同根

- adj. `beguiling` 欺骗的；消遣的；令人陶醉的
- n. `beguilement` 欺骗；欺瞒；消遣
- n. `beguiler` 消遣者；欺骗者
- v. `beguiling` 欺骗；使愉快地度过；迷住（beguile的ing形式）

# 例句

He used his newspapers to beguile the readers into buying shares in his company.
他利用他的报纸诱导读者购买他公司的股票。
