# 音标

- 英 `/'pinəkl/`
- 美 `/'pɪnəkl/`

# 词义

n. 山顶；顶点；尖顶
`If someone reaches the pinnacle of their career or the pinnacle of a particular area of life, they are at the highest point of it`



pin (针) + nacle → 山顶上长满树叶像针一样尖的pine (松树) → 山顶

# 同义

n. 高峰；小尖塔；尖峰；极点
`pole` `climax`

# 例句

She was still at the pinnacle of her career.
她依然处在事业的巅峰时期。
