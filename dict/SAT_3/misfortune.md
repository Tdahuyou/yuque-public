# 音标

- 英 `/mis'fɔːtʃən/`
- 美 `/'mɪs'fɔrtʃən/`

# 词义

n. 不幸；灾祸，灾难
`very bad luck, or something that happens to you as a result of bad luck`



mis (坏) + fortune (运气) → 坏运气 → 不幸

# 同义

n. 不幸；灾祸，灾难
`disaster` `evil` `tragedy` `grief`

# 例句

It seems the banks always profit from farmers’ misfortunes.
银行似乎总是从农民的不幸中获益。
