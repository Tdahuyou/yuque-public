# 音标

- 英 `/'kiːpseik/`
- 美 `/'kipsek/`

# 词义

n. 纪念品
`a small object that you keep to remind you of someone`

# 同义

n. 纪念品
`momento` `memento`

# 例句

My friend gave me his picture as a keepsake before going away .
我的朋友在离别前给我一张照片留作纪念。

I thought you'd lIke to have him, perhaps, as a sort of keepsake.
我想您也许会希望把他作为一种纪念品吧。
