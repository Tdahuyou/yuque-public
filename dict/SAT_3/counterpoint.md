# 音标

- 英 `/'kauntəpɔint/`
- 美 `/'kaʊntɚpɔɪnt/`

# 词义

n. 对音位；对应物
`the combination of two or more tunes played together so that they sound like one tune`



counter (对应的) + point (点) → 对应物

# 同义

n. 复调；对位法；旋律配合；对应物
`polyphony` `opposite number`

# 例句

It related to the harmonious of interval, harmony and counterpoint.
这种审美形态包括音程的“和谐”、和声的“和谐”、复调对位中的“和谐”。

So, there's the melody up above, this bassoon playing the counterpoint, kind of in the middle, and then the bass playing these notes quietly underneath.
然后，旋律在上面，这支巴松管来演奏对位，基本上在中间，而低音提琴在底下悄悄地演奏这些音符。
