# 音标

- 英 `/di'faiənt/`
- 美 `/dɪ'faɪənt/`

# 词义

adj. 挑战的，挑衅的，目中无人的
`If you say that someone is defiant, you mean they show aggression or independence by refusing to obey someone`



de (去掉) + fi ( = fid， 信心) + ant → 去掉别人的信心 → 蔑视的

# 同义

adj. 挑衅的；目中无人的，蔑视的；挑战的
`challenging` `in-your-face`

# 同根

- adv. `defiantly` 挑战地；对抗地
- n. `defiance` 蔑视；挑战；反抗

# 例句

The players are in a defiant mood as they prepare for tomorrow's game.
在准备明天的比赛时，球员们态度挑衅。
