# 音标

- 英 `/ri'kænt/`
- 美 `/ri'kænt/`

# 词义

v. 放弃；撤回
`to say publicly that you no longer have a political or religious belief that you had before`



re (反) + cant (唱) → 唱反调 → 放弃

# 同义

vt. 放弃；撤回
`desert` `yield` `quit`

# 同根

- n. `recantation` 改变论调；取消前言

# 例句

White House officials ordered Williams to recant.
白宫官员命令威廉斯公开宣布放弃自己的观点。
