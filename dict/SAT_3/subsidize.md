# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌbsidaiz/`
- 美 `/'sʌbsə'daɪz/`

# 词义

v. 给补助金/津贴/奖金
`if a government or organization subsidizes a company, activity etc, it pays part of its costs`



来自subsidy (n. 补助金， 津贴)

# 同义

vt. 资助；给与奖助金；向…行贿
`fund` `stake`

# 同根

- adj. `subsidized` 补贴的，补助的
- n. `subsidy` 补贴；津贴；补助金
- n. `subsidization` 补助；提供津贴；奖金
- v. `subsidized` 资助或补助（subsidize的过去时和过去分词）
- vi. `subsidise` 消退；下沉；平息
- vt. `subsidise` 资助（等于subsidize）；给...补助金

# 例句

Farming is heavily subsidized (= subsidized a lot ) by the government.
农业得到政府的大量补贴。
