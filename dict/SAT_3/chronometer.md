# 音标

- 英 `/krə'nɔmitə/`
- 美 `/krə'nɑmɪtɚ/`

# 词义

n. 记时计
`a very exact clock, used for scientific purposes`



chron (时间) + o + meter (测量) → 测量时间 → 计时计

# 同根

- n. `chronograph` 记时计；记秒表

# 例句

Murchison followed with his eye the hand of his chronometer.
莫奇生的眼睛追随着他的时计的秒针。

'Where's Walter, I wonder! ' said Solomon Gills, after he had carefully put up the chronometer again. 'Here's dinner been ready, half an hour, and no Walter! '
“不知道沃尔特在哪里！”吉尔斯把精密计时表重新小心地藏好以后，说道，“晚饭已经准备好半个小时了，可是却不见沃尔特！”
