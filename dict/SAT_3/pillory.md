# 音标

- 英 `/'piləri/`
- 美 `/'pɪləri/`

# 词义

n. 颈手枷
`a wooden frame with holes for someone’s head and hands to be locked into, used in the past as a way of publicly punishing someone`



著名戏曲《苏三起解》中苏三戴着的刑具就是pillory (颈手枷)

# 例句
