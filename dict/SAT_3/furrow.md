# 音标

- 英 `/'fʌrəu/`
- 美 `/'fɝo/`

# 词义

n. 分裂沟
`A furrow is a long, thin line in the earth which a farmer makes in order to plant seeds or to allow water to flow along`

# 同义

n. 皱纹；[农学]犁沟；车辙
`rugosity` `wrinkle`

vt. 犁；耕；弄绉
`till` `plough`

vi. 犁田；开沟；犁出浪迹
`break ground`

# 短语

- `furrow irrigation` 沟灌，畦灌；畦沟灌溉

# 例句

...furrows of roses and corn.
…一垄一垄的玫瑰和玉米。
