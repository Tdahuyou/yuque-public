# 音标

- 英 `/'senʃə/`
- 美 `/'sɛnʃɚ/`

# 词义

n. 严厉批评，谴责
`the act of expressing strong disapproval and criticism`

v. 严厉批评，正式谴责
`to officially criticize someone for something they have done wrong`



检查员 (censor) 趁机责难 (censure)

# 同义

vt. 责难，责备
`impugn` `decry`

n. 责难
`decrial` `dyslogy`

vi. 谴责，责备
`accuse of` `blame for`

# 同根

- adj. `censurable` 该责备的；可非难的

# 例句

a vote of censure
进行谴责的表决
