# 音标

- 英 `/biː'ætiˌtjuːd/`
- 美 `/bɪ'ætɪtud/`

# 词义

n. 至福；祝福
`supreme blessedness or happiness `



我的态度 (attitude) 是祝福 (beatitude)

# 同义

n. 祝福；至福
`wish` `blessing`

# 同根

- adj. `beatific` 幸福的；祝福的；快乐的

# 例句

The word "beatitude" comes from the Latin word that means "blessed, " the first word in each statement.
