# 音标

- 英 `/'witiŋli/`
- 美 `/'wɪtɪŋli/`

# 词义

adv. 有意地，故意地
`If you do something wittingly, you are fully aware of what you are doing and what its consequences will be`



来自witting (a. 有意的)

# 同义

adv. 有意地，存心地
`of purpose` `intentionally`

# 同根

- adj. `witting` 知晓的；有意的
- n. `witting` 消息；情报
- v. `witting` 知道（wit的ing形式）

# 例句

When she had an affair with her friend's husband, she wittingly set off a chain of crises.
她和她朋友的丈夫私通，故意激起了一连串危机。
