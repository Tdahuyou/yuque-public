# 音标

- 英 `/'presidʒ/`
- 美 `/'prɛsɪdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 预示；预知
`to be a sign that something is going to happen, especially something bad`



pre (预先) + sage (智者， 智慧) → 智者有预示事态发展的能力 → 预示

# 同义

n. 预感；前兆
`anticipation` `symption`

vt. 预感；预言
`predict` `prognosticate`

vi. 预示；预言
`soothsay` `divine`

# 例句

The large number of moderate earthquakes that have occurred recently could presage a larger quake soon.
最近多次发生中等强度的地震，这预示着可能很快就会发生更强烈的地震。
