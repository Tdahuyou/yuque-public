# 音标

- 英 `/vi'væsəti/`
- 美 `/vi'væsəti/`

# 词义

n. 活泼
`If you say that someone has vivacity, you mean that they are lively, exciting, and attractive`



viv (生命) + acity → 活泼

# 同义

n. 活泼；快活；精神充沛
`animation` `sunshine` `brightness` `volatility`

# 同根

- adj. `vivacious` 活泼的；快活的；有生气的
- adj. `vivace` 活泼的
- adv. `vivace` 活泼地
- adv. `vivaciously` 愉快地；活泼地；快活地
- n. `vividness` 生动；活泼；明亮

# 例句

He was charmed by her vivacity and high spirits.
她的活泼与兴高采烈的情绪把他迷住了。

We were all affected by the vivacity of this lovely little boy.
这个可爱的小男孩的快活使我们都受到感染。
