# 音标

- 英 `/əd'vəːsəti/`
- 美 `/əd'vɝsəti/`

# 词义

n. 不幸，灾祸
`a situation in which you have a lot of problems that seem to be caused by bad luck`



advers (看作adverse， 不利的) + ity (状况) → 不利的状况 → 不幸， 灾祸

# 同义

n. 逆境；不幸；灾难；灾祸
`disaster` `tragedy` `evil` `grief`

# 例句

his courage in the face of adversity
他面对逆境的勇气
