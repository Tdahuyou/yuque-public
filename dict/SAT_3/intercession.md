# 音标

- 英 `/ˌintə'seʃən/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɚ'sɛʃən/`

# 词义

n. 代为求情
`when someone talks to a person in authority in order to prevent something bad happening to someone else`

# 同义

n. 调解；说情；仲裁
`arbitration` `mediation`

# 同根

- n. `intercessor` 调解人；仲裁者
- vi. `intercede` 调解，调停；求情，说项

# 例句

As we go on in intercession we may find that our obedience to God is going to cost other people more than we thought.
我们代求的时候，会发觉对神的顺服，与别人的得失有极大的关系，这是我们想不到的。
