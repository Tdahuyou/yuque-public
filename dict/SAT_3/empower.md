# 音标

- 英 `/im'pauə/`
- 美 `/ɪm'paʊɚ/`

# 词义

v. 授权，准许
`to give a person or organization the legal right to do something`



em (使成为) + power (权力) → 使成为有权力的 → 授权

# 同义

vt. 授权，允许；使能够
`enable` `let` `have` `grant`

# 同根

- n. `empowerment` 许可，授权

# 例句

The army is now empowered to operate on a shoot-to-kill basis.
军队现在被授权依据“杀无赦”原则行动。
