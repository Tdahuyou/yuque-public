# 音标

- 英 `/ˌnɑːsi'sistik/`
- 美 `/,nɑrsɪ'sɪstɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 自恋的，自我陶醉的
`If you describe someone as narcissistic, you disapprove of them because they think about themselves a lot and admire themselves too much`

# 同根

- n. `narcissism` [心理] 自恋，自我陶醉
- n. `narcissist` 自我陶醉者
- n. `narcist` 自我陶醉者

# 例句

He was insufferable at times – self-centred and narcissistic.
他有时候真让人难以忍受，自我中心又自恋。
