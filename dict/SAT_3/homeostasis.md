# 音标

- 英 `/ˌhəumiəu'steisis/`
- 美 `/,homɪə'stesɪs/`

# 词义

n. 体内平衡
`the maintenance of metabolic equilibrium within an animal by a tendency to compensate for disrupting changes `

# 同义

n. [生理]体内平衡；[生化][自]内稳态
`homoeostasis`

# 同根

- adj. `homeostatic` 自我平衡的；原状稳定的
- adv. `homeostatically` 原状稳定地；自我平衡地

# 例句

Any interference with homeostasis is likely to be damaging to the organism.
对体内平衡的任何干扰都有可能损害人体的有机体。

The return of the disequilibrated organ systems of the body to steady states or dynamic stability is known as homeostasis.
人们身体里失调的器官系统恢复到稳定的或动态稳定状态的过程被称为体内平衡。
