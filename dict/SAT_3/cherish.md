# 音标

- 英 `/'tʃeriʃ/`
- 美 `/'tʃɛrɪʃ/`

# 词义

v. 珍爱；怀抱
`to love someone or something very much and take care of them well`



好好珍爱 (cherish)， 不让它受到毁坏 (perish)

# 同义

vt. 珍爱；怀抱
`treasure` `imbosom`

# 短语

- `cherish time` 珍惜时间

# 例句

In marriage, a man promises to cherish his wife.
在婚姻中，男人承诺要爱护妻子。

his most cherished possession
他最珍爱的物品
