# 音标

- 英 `/'brɔŋkəs/`
- 美 `/'brɑŋkəs/`

# 词义

n. 【解】支气管
`either of the two main branches of the trachea, which contain cartilage within their walls `

# 同义

n. 支气管
`bronchial tube` `branchus`

# 同根

- adj. `bronchial` 支气管的
- adj. `bronchitic` 支气管炎的
- adj. `bronchoscopic` 支气管镜的
- n. `bronchodilator` [内科][药] 支气管扩张剂；支气管扩张器

# 例句

Small branch of the bronchus that carries air to and from the alveoli.
细支气管-- 支气管的细小分支，输送空气进出肺泡。

With contaminated hand, turn the child's head to right side to suction left bronchus.
用已污染的手将小儿的头部转向右侧以吸引左侧支气管。
