# 音标

- 英 `/'hʌmbʌg/`
- 美 `/'hʌmbʌɡ/`

# 词义

n. 欺骗
`someone who behaves in a dishonest or insincere way, for example by pretending to be someone they are not`



hum (嗡嗡声) + bug (小虫) → 小虫嗡嗡叫， 欺骗少不了 → 欺骗

# 同义

n. 骗子；欺骗；谎话
`fraud` `sharp`

vt. 欺骗；哄骗
`sell` `fox` `kid` `trick`

vi. 行骗
`mountebank`

# 例句

There was all the usual humbug and obligatory compliments from ministers.
全都是些部长们常说的谎言和奉承恭维。
