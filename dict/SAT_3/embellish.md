# 音标

- 英 `/im'beliʃ/`
- 美 `/im'beliʃ/`

# 词义

v. 装饰，美化；添加细节
`to make something more beautiful by adding decorations to it`



em + bell (美) + ish → 使…美 → 装饰

# 同义

vt. 修饰；装饰；润色
`paint` `hang` `modify`

# 同根

- n. `embellishment` 装饰，修饰；润色

# 例句

She gave an embellished account of what had happened.
她添油加醋地把发生的事描述了一番。
