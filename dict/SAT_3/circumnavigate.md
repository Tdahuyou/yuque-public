# 音标

- 英 `/ˌsəːkəm'nævigeit/`
- 美 `/,sɝkəm'nævɪɡet/`

# 词义

v. 环航
`to sail, fly, or travel completely around the Earth, an island etc`



circum (绕) + navigate (航海) → 绕着航海 → 环航

# 同根

- n. `circumnavigation` 世界一周旅行；周游世界
- n. `circumnavigator` 世界一周旅行者；周游世界者

# 例句

For this year at least, our race to circumnavigate the globe in less than 80 days is over.
至少在今年，我们在八十天内完成环球航行结束了。
