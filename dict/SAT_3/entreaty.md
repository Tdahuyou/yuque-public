# 音标

- 英 `/in'triːti/`
- 美 `/ɪn'triti/`

# 词义

n. 恳求；乞求
`a serious request in which you ask someone to do something for you`



来自entreat (v. 恳求)

# 同义

n. 恳求；乞求
`prayer` `suit` `imploration`

# 同根

- adv. `entreatingly` 乞求地；哀求地
- vi. `entreat` 恳求；乞求
- vt. `entreat` 恳求；请求

# 例句

She declined the Republican Party's entreaties to run for his seat.
她拒绝了共和党让她竞选他的席位的数次恳求。
