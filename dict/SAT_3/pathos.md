# 音标

- 英 `/'peθɔs/`
- 美 `/'peθɑs/`

# 词义

n. 痛苦，感伤；悲悯，同情
`the quality that a person, situation, film, or play has that makes you feel pity and sadness`



path (感情) + os → 痛苦， 感伤

# 同义

n. 悲怅；痛苦；同情；哀婉动人的词句
`smart` `feeling` `hurt` `compassion` `sympathy`

# 例句

the pathos of the woman trying to keep her lover
那个女人试图留住爱侣的哀婉之情
