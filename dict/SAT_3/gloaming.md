# 音标

- 英 `/'gləumiŋ/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 黄昏；薄暮
`the time in the early evening when it is becoming dark`

# 同义

n. 黄昏；夕幕
`dark` `nightfall`

# 同根

- n. `gloam` 黄昏；薄暮
- vi. `gloam` 变朦胧

# 例句

In the gloaming, the crows flew in to gather for the night in a stand of bare oaks.
薄暮时分，鸦群飞向光秃秃的橡树枝条上过夜。
