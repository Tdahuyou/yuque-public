# 音标

- 英 `/'tʌndrə/`
- 美 `/'tʌndrə/`

# 词义

n. 苔原；冻土地带
`the large flat areas of land in the north of Russia, Canada etc, where it is very cold and there are no trees`

# 同义

n. [生态]苔原；[地理]冻原；冻土地带
`cold desert` `frozen ground`

# 短语

- `arctic tundra` 北极冻原；北极苔原；藓苔湿原
- `alpine tundra` 高山冻原；高山苔原

# 例句

Gley soils are typical of tundra , meadows, and boggy areas.
该种土壤是苔原、 草地以及沼地所特有的。

The park contains 150 lakes and 450 miles of streams, plus ecosystems ranging from wetlands to pine forests to montane areas to alpine tundra.
这个国家公园包括150个湖泊和450英里河流以及从湿地到松林、从山丘到高山冻原等各种生态系统。
