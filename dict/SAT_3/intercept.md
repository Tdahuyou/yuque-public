# 音标

- 英 `/ˌintə'sept/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɚ'sɛpt/`

# 词义

vt. 中途拦截，截击，截取
`to stop something or someone that is going from one place to another before they get there`



inter (在…中间) + cept (拿) → 中途拿下 → 中途拦截

# 同义

vt. 拦截；截断；窃听
`block tackle` `hold up`

# 同根

- n. `interception` 拦截，截住；截断，截取；侦听，窃听
- n. `interceptor` [航][军] 拦截机；妨碍者

# 短语

- `intercept method` 截距法；顶距差法

# 例句

an attempt to intercept drugs being smuggled over the border
阻截毒品走私入境的努力

His phone calls were intercepted.
他的电话被截听了。
