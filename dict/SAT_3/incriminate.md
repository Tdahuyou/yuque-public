# 音标

- 英 `/in'krimineit/`
- 美 `/ɪn'krɪmɪnet/`

# 词义

v. 控告…有罪；使负罪；控告
`If something incriminates you, it suggests that you are responsible for something bad, especially a crime`



in (进入) + crimin (罪行) + ate → 控告…有罪； 使负罪； 控告

# 同义

vt. 控告；暗示...有罪
`law` `sue for`

# 同根

- adj. `incriminating` 归罪的
- adj. `incriminatory` 成为有罪的；负罪的
- n. `incrimination` 控告；连累
- v. `incriminating` 牵连（incriminate的现在分词）

# 例句

He claimed that the drugs had been planted to incriminate him.
他声称那些毒品是有人为了栽赃陷害他而放在那里的。
