# 音标

- 英 `/'kɔːniə/`
- 美 `/'kɔrnɪə/`

# 词义

n. 角膜
`the transparent protective covering on the outer surface of your eye`

# 同根

- adj. `corneal` [解剖] 角膜的

# 例句

They accommodate by greatly changing the curvature of the cornea.
它们由大大地改变角膜曲率的方法来调节。

Squamous cell carcinoma of the cornea or conjunctiva: a rare tumour of the surface of the eye.
角膜或结膜鳞状细胞癌：眼睛表面的一种罕见的肿瘤。

Pterygium is a triangular sheet of fibrovascular tissue that invades the clear cornea from the bulbar conjunctiva, and has a high recurrence rate after simple excision.
翼状胬肉是一三角形纤维血管组织，从球结膜侵入透明角膜，在单纯切除术后具有很高的复发率。
