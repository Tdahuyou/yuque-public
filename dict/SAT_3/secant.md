# 音标

- 英 `/'siːkənt/`
- 美 `/'sikænt/`

# 词义

n. 割线
`(of an angle) a trigonometric function that in a right-angled triangle is the ratio of the length of the hypotenuse to that of the adjacent side; the reciprocal of cosine `

# 同义

adj. 割的；切的；交叉的
`cross` `incisal`

# 短语

- `secant method` [化]割线法；正割法
- `hyperbolic secant` 双曲正割

# 例句

Then the numerical results show that new algorithm effectively overcomes the shortcomings of secant method, and evidently improves the convergent speed, convergent range and algorithm stability.
数值实验结果表明，这种算法不仅有效克服了割线法的缺陷，而且收敛速度、收敛范围和算法的稳定性也得到明显提高。
