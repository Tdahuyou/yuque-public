# 音标

- 英 `/ˌrepə'reiʃən/`
- 美 `/ˌrepəˈreɪʃn/`

# 词义

n. 赔偿，弥补，修理
`money paid by a defeated country after a war, for all the deaths, damage etc it has caused`



repara (看作repair，  修理) + tion (表示名词) → 修理

# 同义

n. 赔偿；修理；赔款
`satisfaction` `renovation`

# 例句

The government agreed to pay reparations to victims.
政府同意向受害者支付赔款。
