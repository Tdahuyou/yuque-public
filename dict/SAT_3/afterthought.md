# 音标

- 英 `/'ɑːftəθɔːt/`
- 美 `/'æftəθɔt/`

# 词义

n. 事后的想法，追悔
`something that you mention or add later because you did not think of it or plan it before`



after (后来) + thought (想法) → 事后的想法

# 同根

- adv. `afterwards` 后来；然后

# 例句

He added as an afterthought, ‘Bring Melanie too.'
他想了想又说道: “把梅拉妮也带来。”
