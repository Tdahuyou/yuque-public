# 音标

- 英 `/i'stiːm/`
- 美 `/ɪ'stim/`

# 词义

n. 尊敬，敬重
`a feeling of respect for someone, or a good opinion of someone`

v. 尊敬，敬重
`to respect and admire someone or something`



我们应该尊重 (esteem) 队 (team) 里的每位成员

# 同义

vt. 尊敬；认为；考虑；估价
`assess` `honor` `expect` `evaluate` `guess`

n. 尊重；尊敬
`estimation` `respect` `regard` `worship`

# 短语

- `self esteem` 自尊

# 例句

He is held in high esteem by colleagues in the construction industry.
他深受建筑业的同事们的敬重。
