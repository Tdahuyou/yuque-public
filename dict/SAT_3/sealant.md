# 音标

- 英 `/'siːlənt/`
- 美 `/'silənt/`

# 词义

n. 密封剂
`a substance that is put on the surface of something to protect it from air, water etc`

# 同义

n. [机]密封剂
`encapsulant` `aquaseal`

# 同根

- adj. `sealed` 密封的；未知的
- n. `seal` 密封；印章；海豹；封条；标志
- n. `sealing` 封闭，[机] 密封；补铸件的漏洞；猎捕海豹业
- n. `sealer` 盖印人；检验员；密封层；捕海豹的船或猎人
- v. `sealed` 封闭；盖印（seal的过去分词）
- vi. `seal` 猎海豹
- vt. `seal` 密封；盖章

# 短语

- `silicone sealant` 硅酮密封剂，硅酮嵌缝膏；硅密封胶
- `joint sealant` 接口密封带；填缝料；夹口胶
- `fissure sealant` 窝沟封闭剂；齿面裂沟封剂

# 例句

Apply a 6-mm (1/4-in) diameter bead of sealant to the window opening flanges.
在窗户开口凸缘，使用一个直径为6-公厘（1/4-英寸）密封剂压条。

Apply a non-hardening sealant to the threads of the terminal box connection port flexible metal conduit and seal fitting for waterproofing.
在接线盒连接口、挠性金属导管及密封件螺纹处涂上非硬化密封剂，用于防水。

The preparation methods of deketoxime type one-part RTV silicone rubber sealant on spread ability and for formed in place gasket were introduced respectively.
介绍了可涂饰性及就地成形密封垫圈用脱酮肟型单组分RTV硅橡胶密封剂的配制方法。
