# 音标

- 英 `/'episəud/`
- 美 `/'ɛpɪsod/`

# 词义

n. 一段情节，片断，一集
`a television or radio programme that is one of a series of programmes in which the same story is continued each week`

# 同义

n. 插曲；一段情节；插话；有趣的事件
`incident` `intermezzo`

# 同根

- adj. `episodic` 插话式的

# 例句

Watch next week’s thrilling episode!
请收看下周精彩的一集！
