# 音标

- 英 `/'piːsˌmiːl/`
- 美 `/'pismil/`

# 词义

adj. 逐渐的
`If you describe a change or process as piecemeal, you disapprove of it because it happens gradually, usually at irregular intervals, and is probably not satisfactory`



piece (片) + meal (进食) → 面包要一片片地吃 → 逐渐的

# 同义

adj. 零碎的；逐渐的
`gradual` `bitty`

n. 片；块
`block` `mass` `slip`

vt. 粉碎
`comminute` `pulverize`

# 例句

These piecemeal solutions won't work.
这些零敲碎打的解决办法不会有效。
