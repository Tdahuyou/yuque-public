# 音标

- 英 `/de'spɔtik/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spɑtɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 专制的；专横的；暴虐的
`If you say that someone is despotic, you are emphasizing that they use their power over other people in a very unfair or cruel way`

# 同义

adj. 暴虐的，暴君的；专横的
`tyrannous` `Neronian`

# 同根

- n. `despot` 专制君主，暴君；独裁者

# 例句

The country was ruled by a despotic tyrant.
该国家被一位专横的暴君统治着。
