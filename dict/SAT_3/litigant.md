# 音标

- 英 `/'litigənt/`
- 美 `/'lɪtɪɡənt/`

# 词义

n. 诉讼人
`someone who is making a claim against someone or defending themselves against a claim in a court of law`



litig (打官司) + ant → 打官司的人 → 诉讼人

# 同义

adj. 诉讼的
`contentious`

# 同根

- adj. `litigious` 好诉讼的；好争论的
- n. `litigation` 诉讼；起诉
- n. `litigator` 诉讼律师；诉讼人
- vi. `litigate` 对簿法庭
- vt. `litigate` 在法庭相争；提出诉讼

# 例句

Speech or reply by the third party and his litigant representative.
第三人及其诉讼代理人发言或者答辩。

The self-admission in civil proceeding can confine adjudicative power and free the litigant of burden of proof.
民事诉讼中的自认，具有拘束审判权力和免除一方当事人举证责任的功能。
