# 音标

- 英 `/ri'sesiv/`
- 美 `/rɪ'sɛsɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 隐性的
`a recessive  gene  is passed to children from their parents only if both parents have the gene`

# 同义

adj. 隐性的；逆行的；后退的
`regressive` `antidromic`

# 同根

- adj. `recessional` 后退的；（英）议会休会的
- n. `recession` 衰退；不景气；后退；凹处
- n. `recess` 休息；休会；凹处
- n. `recessional` （礼拜仪式后牧师及唱诗班退场时所唱的）退场赞美诗
- vi. `recess` 休息；休假
- vt. `recess` 使凹进；把…放在隐蔽处

# 短语

- `recessive gene` 隐性基因
- `recessive unemployment` 隐性失业
- `recessive trait` 隐性特质，隐性品质

# 例句

In normal seedlings the deleterious recessive genes are not expressed.
有害的隐性基因在正常菌木中不表现出来。

An example of negative eugenics would be the attempt to eradicate a harmful recessive gene.
消极优生学的范例是试图破坏一种对人体有害的隐性基因。

Most plants carry a large genetic load for deleterious recessive genes of which a few are homozygous and depress growth.
多数植物带有大量有害的隐性基因的“遗传负荷”，其中少数是纯合的，因而降低了生长。
