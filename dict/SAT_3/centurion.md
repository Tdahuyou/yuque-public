# 音标

- 英 `/sen'tjuəriən/`
- 美 `/sɛn'tʃʊrɪən/`

# 词义

n. 百人队长，百夫长
`an army officer of ancient Rome, who was in charge of about 100 soldiers`

When Jesus had entered Capernaum, a centurion came to him, asking for help.
