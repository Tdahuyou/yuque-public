# 音标

- 英 `/'siləbəs/`
- 美 `/'sɪləbəs/`

# 词义

n. 课程大纲
`a plan that states exactly what students at a school or college should learn in a particular subject`



syl (综合) + labus ( = label， 标签) → 综合一下贴上标签 → 课程大纲

# 同义

n. 教学大纲，摘要；课程表
`abstract` `brief` `summary` `resume`

# 例句

The course syllabus consisted mainly of novels by African-American authors, male and female.
该课程的教学大纲主要包括了非裔美国男女作家的小说。
