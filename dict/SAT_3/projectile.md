# 音标

- 英 `/prə'dʒektail/`
- 美 `/prə'dʒɛktl/`

# 词义

n. 抛体
`an object that is thrown at someone or is fired from a gun or other weapon, such as a bullet, stone, or  shell `

# 同根

- adj. `projecting` 突出的；伸出的
- v. `projecting` 使突出（project的现在分词）；计划；发射

# 短语

- `rocket projectile` 火箭弹；喷气火箭
- `projectile motion` 抛物运动；抛射体运动

# 例句

The vertical and horizontal motions of a projectile can be treated independently.
抛射体的竖直方向和水平方向的运动能够分开来处理。

Single OR multiple gripper OR projectile looms, rapier looms, water jet looms and air jet looms.
单片梭或多片梭织机，或抛射（片梭）织机；剑杆织机；喷水织机和喷气织机。
