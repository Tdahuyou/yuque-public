# 音标

- 英 `/ˌriː'tʌtʃ/`
- 美 `/,ri'tʌtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 润饰
`to improve a picture or photograph by painting over marks or making other small changes`



re + touch (用画笔轻画) → 一再用画笔轻画 → 润饰

# 同义

vt. 润饰；修整
`trim up` `give shape to`

vi. 润饰；修整
`trim up` `give shape to`

# 例句

postcards that have been retouched to cover the grey skies
经过修饰把灰色天空掩盖起来的明信片
