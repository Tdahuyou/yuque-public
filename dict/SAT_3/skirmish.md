# 音标

- 英 `/'skəːmiʃ/`
- 美 `/'skɝmɪʃ/`

# 词义

n. 小冲突
`a fight between small groups of soldiers, ships etc, especially one that happens away from the main part of a battle – used in news reports`



skir (看作skirt， 裙子) + mish (看作famish， 饥饿) → 女人会为了裙子而争吵， 为了穿漂亮的裙子宁可饿肚子 → 小冲突

# 同根

- n. `skirmisher` 散兵；进行小争斗者

# 例句

Border skirmishes between India and Pakistan were common.
印巴边境的小冲突曾经是常有的事。
