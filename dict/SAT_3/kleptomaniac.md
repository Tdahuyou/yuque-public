# 音标

- 英 `/ˌkleptə'meiniæk/`
- 美 `/,klɛptə'menɪæk/`

# 词义

n. 有盗窃癖的人
`someone suffering from kleptomania`

# 例句

A kleptomaniac is a compulsive thief who steals because he or she cannot help it.
盗窃狂身不由己的小偷，偷东西是因为无法自制。

The vicious kleptomaniac was eventually overthrown after losing his cold war sponsors in the west.
这个恶毒的盗窃狂最终在失去他冷战时期的西方赞助者之后被推翻。
