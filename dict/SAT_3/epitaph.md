# 音标

- 英 `/'epitɑːf/`
- 美 `/'ɛpɪtæf/`

# 词义

n. 墓志铭，碑文
`a short piece of writing on the stone over someone’s grave(= place in the ground where someone is buried )`



epi (在…上) + taph ( = tomb， 墓) → 在墓碑上刻的字 → 墓志铭

# 同义

n. 碑文，墓志铭
`epigraph`

# 例句

He wanted these lines as his epitaph.
他想把这些文字作为他的墓志铭。

His epitaph should be: He did us good.
他的墓志铭应当是：他为我们做了好事。

His epitaph for himself would have well suited man as he wanted him to be.
他为自己写的墓志铭非常适合像他所期望成为的那种人。
