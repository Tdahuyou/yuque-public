# 音标

- 英 `/'səunɑː/`
- 美 `/'sonɑr/`

# 词义

n. 声纳
`equipment on a ship or  submarine  that uses  sound waves  to find out the position of objects under the water`

# 短语

- `passive sonar` 被动声纳；无源声纳
- `dipping sonar` 吊放声纳；投吊式声纳
