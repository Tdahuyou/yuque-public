# 音标

- 英 `/kə'luːʒn/`
- 美 `/kə'lʊʒən/`

# 词义

n. 共谋，勾结
`a secret agreement that two or more people make in order to do something dishonest`



col (共同) + lus (玩耍) + ion (名词后缀) → 共谋， 勾结

# 同义

n. 勾结；[法]共谋
`conspiracy` `complicity`

# 同根

- adj. `collusive` 共谋的
- vi. `collude` 勾结；串通；共谋

# 短语

- `in collusion with` 串通；与…勾结

# 例句

He found no evidence of collusion between record companies and retailers.
他找不出唱片公司与零售商互相勾结的证据。
