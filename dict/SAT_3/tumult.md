# 音标

- 英 `/'tjuːmʌlt/`
- 美 `/'tumʌlt/`

# 词义

n. 骚动；烦乱
`a confused, noisy, and excited situation, often caused by a large crowd`



tumu (看作tumour， 癌症) + lt → 谈癌色变， 让人烦乱 → 烦乱

# 同义

n. 骚动；骚乱；吵闹；激动
`combustion` `row` `disturbance` `disorder` `sound`

# 同根

- adj. `tumultuous` 吵闹的；骚乱的；狂暴的
- adv. `tumultuously` 喧闹地；骚乱地

# 例句

I could simply not be heard in the tumult.
一片嘈杂声中别人根本听不到我说话。
