# 音标

- 英 `/əˌbɔmi'neiʃən/`
- 美 `/ə,bɑmɪ'neʃən/`

# 词义

n. 憎恨，厌恶；可憎的事物
`If you say that something is an abomination, you think that it is completely unacceptable`

# 同义

n. 厌恶；憎恨；令人厌恶的事物
`despite` `hate` `hatred` `aversion`

# 同根

- n. `abominator` 嫌恶者；憎恶者
- vt. `abominate` 痛恨；憎恶

# 例句

Slavery was an abomination.
奴隶制令人深恶痛绝。
