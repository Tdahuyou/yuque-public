# 音标

- 英 `/'prɔktə/`
- 美 `/'prɑktɚ/`

# 词义

n. 学监
`someone who watches students in an examination to make sure that they do not cheat`



学监 (proctor) 是学校纪律的保护者 (protector)

# 同义

n. 代理人；学监
`deputy` `agent` `factor` `secondary` `proxy`

vt. 监督
`police` `oversee` `to monitor`

# 同根

- n. `procurator` 检察官；代理人；行政长官
- n. `proctorship` 代理人之职；学监之职位或任期

# 例句

One student who arrived four minutes late in 2007 was turned away, even though she and her mother knelt before the exam proctor, begging for leniency.
2007年高考中，一位考生迟到了四分钟就被拒绝入场。 虽然她和妈妈在监考老师面前下跪哀求，最终也未能进入考场。
