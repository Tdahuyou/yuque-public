# 音标

- 英 `/'ekskʌlpeit/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

v. 开脱；申明无罪
`to prove that someone is not guilty of something`



ex + culp (罪行) + ate → 开脱

# 同根

- adj. `exculpatory` 申明无罪的；辩解的
- n. `exculpation` 辩解；使无罪

# 例句

These causes, however, never served to exculpate "ordinary Germans" who shared in the guilt either for participating in or not resisting Nazi rule in Germany.
但这些原因，都不足以证明所谓“德国平民”是无罪的，因为他们有些也参与了这场罪行，至少并没有反抗纳粹在德国的统治。
