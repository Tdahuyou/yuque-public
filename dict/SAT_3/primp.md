# 音标

- 英 `/primp/`
- 美 `/prɪmp/`

# 词义

v. 细心打扮，过分装饰
`to make yourself look attractive by arranging your hair, putting on  make-up  etc`



prim (最早， 最好) + p → 向最好处打扮 → 细心打扮

# 同义

vt. 精心打扮；装饰
`paint` `hang`

vi. 装饰；打扮
`dress oneself` `sharp` `trick out`

# 例句

She spends hours primping in front of the mirror.
她总是花很长时间在镜子前梳妆打扮。
