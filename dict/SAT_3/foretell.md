# 音标

- 英 `/fɔː'tel/`
- 美 `/fɔr'tɛl/`

# 词义

v. 预言，预示，预测
`to say what will happen in the future, especially by using special magical powers`



fore (在之前) + tell (告诉) → 在之前告诉结果 → 预言

# 同义

vt. 预言；预示；预告
`indicate` `predict` `shadow` `announce`

vi. 预言；预示；预告
`soothsay` `divine`

# 同根

- n. `foretoken` 预兆；预示
- vbl. `foretelling` 预言
- vt. `foretoken` 成为…之预兆

# 例句

the birth of Christ, foretold by prophets
先知们所预言的基督的诞生
