# 音标

- 英 `/fɔː'beərəns/`
- 美 `/fɔr'bɛrəns/`

# 词义

n. 自制，忍耐
`the quality of being patient, able to control your emotions, and willing to forgive someone who has upset you`



for (禁止) + bear (忍受) + ance → 自己要忍受诱惑， 禁止做错误的事情 → 自制

# 同义

n. 自制，忍耐；宽容
`tolerance` `possession` `charity` `mercy`

# 同根

- adj. `forbearing` 宽容的；忍耐的
- n. `forbear` 祖先
- v. `forbearing` 克制（forbear的现在分词）
- vi. `forbear` 忍耐；克制
- vt. `forbear` 忍耐；克制

# 例句

There is a limit at which forbearance ceases to be a virtue.
忍耐超过了一定的限度便不再是美德了。

But the forbearance shown by a valiant and brave person is true forgiveness.
但是及其勇敢者所表现出的忍耐是真正的宽恕。

If it continues at this space, both DPRK's strategic strike capability and America's "strategic forbearance" may reach the critical point.
照此速度发展下去，不论是朝鲜的战略打击能力，还是美国的“战略忍耐”限度，都有可能突破临界点。
