# 音标

- 英 `/træns'mjuːt/`
- 美 `/trænz'mjut/`

# 词义

v. 改变
`to change one substance or type of thing into another`



trans (改变) + mute (变化) → 变化

# 同义

vt. 使变形；使变质
`deform` `warp`

vi. 变形；变质
`become deformed` `to morph`

# 同根

- adj. `transmutable` 可变形的
- n. `transmutation` 变形；变化；演变
- n. `transmutability` 可变化；可变性
- n. `transmuter` 炼金师

# 例句

A radioactive atom could transmute itself into an entirely different kind of atom.
放射性原子本身能嬗变为性质完全不同的另一种原子。

At that time, the CSO was heavily involved in trying to transmute dangerous chemicals via nuclear reactions.
当时，中央安全机构正积极参与通过核反应嬗变危险的化学物质。
