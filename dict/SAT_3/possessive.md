# 音标

- 英 `/pə'zesiv/`
- 美 `/pə'zɛsɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 所有的，占有的
`wanting someone to have feelings of love or friendship for you and no one else`

n. 所有格
`an adjective,  pronoun  , or form of a word that shows that something belongs to someone or something`



来自possess (v. 占有， 拥有)

# 同义

adj. 占有的；所有的；所有格的；占有欲强的
`any` `having`

# 同根

- n. `possession` 拥有；财产；领地；自制；着迷
- n. `possessor` 持有人；所有人
- n. `possessiveness` 占有；自制力
- vt. `possess` 控制；使掌握；持有；迷住

# 例句

Danny could be very jealous and possessive about me.
丹尼会很嫉妒，对我独占欲极强。
