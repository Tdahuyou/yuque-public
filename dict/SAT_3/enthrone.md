# 音标

- 英 `/in'θrəun/`
- 美 `/ɪn'θron/`

# 词义

v. 使登基；崇拜，尊崇
`When kings, queens, emperors, or bishops are enthroned, they officially take on their role during a special ceremony`



en (使…) + throne (宝座) → 使登上宝座 → 登基

# 同义

vt. 使登基；立…为王；任为主教；崇拜
`king` `worship`

# 同根

- n. `enthronement` 即位；登基典礼，就任主教的仪式
- n. `enthronization` 授予主教职位；登基典礼（等于enthronement）

# 例句

Emperor Akihito of Japan has been enthroned in Tokyo.
日皇明仁已在东京登基。

He is expected to be enthroned early next year as the spiritual leader.
人们预计他将于明年初作为精神领袖登位。
