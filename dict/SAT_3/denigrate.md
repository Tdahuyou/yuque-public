# 音标

- 英 `/'denigreit/`
- 美 `/'dɛnɪɡret/`

# 词义

v. 贬低，诋毁
`to say things to make someone or something seem less important or good`



de (否定) + nigr (黑的) + ate → 否定的， 抹黑的 → 贬低， 诋毁

# 同义

vt. 诋毁；使变黑；玷污
`black` `cloud`

# 同根

- n. `denigration` 诋毁

# 例句

people who denigrate their own country
贬损自己国家的人
