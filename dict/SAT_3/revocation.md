# 音标

- 英 `/ˌrevə'keiʃən/`
- 美 `/,rɛvə'keʃən/`

# 词义

n. 撤回
`the act of revoking a law, decision, or agreement`

# 同义

n. 取消；撤回；废除
`cancellation` `withdrawal`

# 同根

- adj. `revocable` 可撤销的；可废除的

# 例句

The assignment, delegation or revocation shall be in writing and shall not take effect until copies have been received by both Parties.
此类委托、授权或撤回应是书面的并且在合同双方接到副本之前不能生效。
