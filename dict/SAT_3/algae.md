# 音标

- 英 `/'ældʒiː/`
- 美 `/'ældʒi/`

# 词义

n. 海藻；藻类
`a very simple plant without stems or leaves that grows in or near water`

# 同义

n. 藻类；[植]海藻
`seaweed` `varech`

# 短语

- `green algae` 绿藻类
- `blue-green algae` 蓝绿藻
- `brown algae` [植]褐藻
- `algae removal` 灭藻
- `red algae` [植]红藻
- `marine algae` 海藻类，海洋藻类
- `blue algae` 蓝藻类，蓝藻
- `algae control` 藻类控制
- `blue green algae` 蓝绿藻，蓝藻

# 例句

...an effort to control toxic algae in Green Lake.
…控制绿湖里有毒水藻的努力。
