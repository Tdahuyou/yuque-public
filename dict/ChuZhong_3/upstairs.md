# 音标

- 英 `/ʌp'steəz/`
- 美 `/,ʌp'stɛrz/`

# 词义

adv. 在楼上，往楼上
`towards or on an upper floor in a building`

adj. 楼上的
`An upstairs room or object is situated on a floor of a building that is higher than the ground floor`

n. 楼上
`one or all of the upper floors in a building`



up(上面)+stairs(楼梯)→在楼上

# 同义

adv. 在楼上，向楼上；上楼；往楼上
`abovestairs`

# 短语

- `go upstairs` 上楼；爬楼梯

# 例句

I went upstairs and had a shower.
我上楼冲了个澡。

She’s upstairs in bed feeling ill.
她身体不舒服，在楼上躺着。

Marsani moved into the upstairs apartment.
玛萨妮搬进了楼上的公寓。
