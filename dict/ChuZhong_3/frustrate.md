# 音标

- 英 `/'frʌstreɪt/`
- 美 `/'frʌstret/`

# 词义

v. 使沮丧，使灰心；失败
`if something frustrates you, it makes you feel annoyed or angry because you are unable to do what you want`

# 同义

vt. 挫败；阻挠；使感到灰心
`defeat` `discomfit`

vi. 失败；受挫
`founder` `come to naught`

adj. 挫败的；无益的
`thwarted` `unprofitable`

# 同根

- adj. `frustrated` 失意的，挫败的；泄气的
- adj. `frustrating` 令人沮丧的
- n. `frustration` 挫折
- v. `frustrated` 挫败；阻挠（frustrate的过去式和过去分词形式）
- v. `frustrating` 使沮丧（frustrate的ing形式）

# 例句

The fact that he’s working with amateurs really frustrates him.
与生手共事着实让他沮丧。
