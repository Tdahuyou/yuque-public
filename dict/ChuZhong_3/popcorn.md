# 音标

- 英 `/'pɒpkɔːn/`
- 美 `/'pɑpkɔrn/`

# 词义

n. 爆米花
`a kind of corn that swells and bursts open when heated, and is usually eaten warm with salt or sugar as a  snack `



pop(流行的)+corn(玉米)→流行的玉米， 大家都爱吃→爆米花

# 同义

n. 爆米花，爆玉米花
`puffed rice`
