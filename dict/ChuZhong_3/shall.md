# 音标

- 英 `/ʃæl/`
- 美 `/ʃəl/`

# 词义

modal verb. 〔某事〕必定，一定〔发生〕
`used to emphasize that something will definitely happen, or that you are determined that something should happen`

# 同义

aux. 应；会；将；必须
`would` `must`

# 短语

- `shall we` 我们…好吗
- `what shall i do` 我该怎么办
- `shall we dance` 我们跳舞吧

# 例句

The truth shall make you free.
真理必令你们得自由。

I said you could go, and so you shall.
我说了你可以去，你就一定可以去。
