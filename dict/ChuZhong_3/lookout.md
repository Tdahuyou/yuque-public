# 音标

- 英 `/'lukaut/`
- 美 `/'lʊk'aʊt/`

# 词义

n. 警戒，监视；■望台，监视哨；■望员，监视者；与个人有关的事情，任务
`someone whose duty is to watch carefully for something, especially for danger`

# 同义

n. [军]监视；监视哨；警戒；守望者；担心的事
`observation` `monitoring` `surveillance` `guard` `watch`

# 短语

- `on the lookout for` 寻找；注意；警戒
- `be on the lookout` 注意...
- `lookout mountain` 观景峰

# 例句

We’re always on the lookout for new business opportunities.
我们一直在留意，等待新的商机。
