# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪndʒə(r) /`
- 美 `/'ɪndʒɚ/`

# 词义

v. 伤害，损害，损伤
`If you injure a person or animal, you damage some part of their body`

# 同义

vt. 伤害，损害
`hurt` `knife`

# 同根

- adj. `injured` 受伤的；受损害的
- adj. `injurious` 有害的；诽谤的
- adv. `injuriously` 有害地；伤害地
- n. `injury` 伤害，损害；受伤处

# 例句

A number of bombs have exploded, seriously injuring at least five people.
一些炸弹爆炸了，至少5人严重受伤。
