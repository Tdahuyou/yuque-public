# 音标

- 英 `/ðə/`
- 美 `/ðə; ði/`

# 词义

art. 这；那


adv. 更加（用于比较级，最高级前）
`used before two  comparative  adjectives or adverbs to show that the degree of one event or situation is related to the degree of another one`

# 例句

What should I do with the token?
我该怎么用这个辅币呢？

What do they do with the coin?
他们是怎样处理这枚硬币的?。
