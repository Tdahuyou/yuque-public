# 音标

- 英 `/'pɪknɪk/`
- 美 `/'pɪknɪk/`

# 词义

v. [picnicked-picnicked,picnicking]去野餐
`to have a picnic`

n. 野餐
`if you have a picnic, you take food and eat it outdoors, especially in the country`

# 同义

n. 野餐
`junket`

# 短语

- `have a picnic` 去野餐；举行野餐
- `no picnic` 不容易
- `go on a picnic` 去野餐，去野炊
- `picnic basket` 野餐篮
- `picnic lunch` 便当，野餐
- `picnic area` n. 野餐区

# 例句

We picnicked on the beach.
我们在海滩上野餐。
