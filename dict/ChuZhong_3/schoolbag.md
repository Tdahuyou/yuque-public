# 音标

- 英 `/'skuːlbæg/`
- 美 `/ˈskulˌbæɡ/`

# 词义

n. 书包
`A schoolbag is a bag that children use to carry books and other things to and from school`



school(学校)+bag(包)→书包

# 同义

n. 书包
`satchel` `mochila`

# 例句

What do you have in your schoolbag?
你书包里有什么东西呢？

I have a new schoolbag.
我有一个新的书包。

This is his textbook. Mine is in my schoolbag.
这是他的课本。 我的课本在我书包里。
