# 音标

- 英 `/suːp/`
- 美 `/sup/`

# 词义

n. 汤
`cooked liquid food, often containing small pieces of meat, fish, or vegetables`



这汤(soup)里可不能加肥皂(soap)

# 同义

n. 汤，羹；马力
`horsepower` `gippo`

vt. 加速；增加马力
`increase speed` `quicken up`

# 同根

- adj. `soupy` 汤汁一样的；浓云密布的；多愁善感的

# 短语

- `in the soup` adv. [口]在困境中
- `chicken soup` 鸡汤；心灵鸡汤
- `noodle soup` 面汤，清汤面条
- `thick soup` 浓汤
- `tomato soup` 番茄汤
- `vegetable soup` 蔬菜汤
- `mushroom soup` 蘑菇汤
- `duck soup` n. 易事；好欺侮的人
- `from soup to nuts` 从头到尾；一应俱全
- `clear soup` 清汤
- `onion soup` 洋葱汤；葱头汤
- `soup stock` 老汤；肉汁清汤
- `soup spoon` 汤匙
- `soup of the day` 例汤
- `corn soup` 玉米浓汤
- `soup kitchen` 流动厨房；施粥场
- `seaweed soup` 紫菜汤
- `soup plate` 汤盘；汤盆
- `pumpkin soup` 南瓜汤
- `wonton soup` 馄饨汤

# 例句

homemade tomato soup
自制的番茄汤
