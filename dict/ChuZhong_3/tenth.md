# 音标

- 英 `/tenθ/`
- 美 `/tɛnθ/`

# 词义

num. 第十
`one of ten equal parts of something`

n. 十分之一
`one of ten equal parts of something`

# 同义

adj. 十分之一的；第十个的
`decimus`

# 同根

- adv. `tenthly` 第十

# 短语

- `nine tenths` 几乎全部

# 例句

He has just published his tenth novel.
他刚刚发表了他的第十本小说。

Not because she lost, in fact she just won her last election to her tenth term by a landslide.
不只因为她的失败，事实上她只是由于占绝对优势的选举赢得第十期最后的选举。

Many already go for the second option: one-tenth of remittances to Africa come from within the continent.
很多人已经选择第二个选择了：十分之一汇向非洲国家的汇款来自欧洲国家。
