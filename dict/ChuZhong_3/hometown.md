# 音标

- 英 `/'həum'taun/`
- 美 `/'homtaʊn/`

# 词义

adj. 家乡的


n. 家乡
`Someone's hometown is the town where they live or the town that they come from`



home(家)+town(乡镇)→家乡

# 同义

n. 家乡；故乡
`country` `native land`

# 例句

Do you like your hometown?
你喜欢你的家乡吗？

What do you think of your hometown?
你觉得你的家乡怎么样？
