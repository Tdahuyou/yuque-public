# 音标

- 英 `/'bɑːθruːm/`
- 美 `/'bæθrum/`

# 词义

n. 浴室；盥洗室
`A bathroom is a room in a house that contains a bath or shower, a sink, and sometimes a toilet`



bath (洗澡)+room(房间)→洗澡的房间→浴室

# 同义

n. 浴室；厕所；盥洗室
`john` `convenience` `toilet`

# 同根

- n. `bath` 沐浴；浴室；浴盆
- vi. `bath` 洗澡
- vt. `bath` 洗澡

- `bathroom accessories` 浴室配件
- `go to the bathroom` 上厕所
- `bathroom cabinet` 浴室柜
- `bathroom equipment` 浴室设备
- `bedroom and bathroom` 卧室和浴室
- `bathroom scale` 浴室磅秤；体重秤
