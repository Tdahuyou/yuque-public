# 音标

- 英 `/sɪns/`
- 美 `/sɪns/`

# 词义

adv. 后来，
`When you are talking about an event or situation in the past, you use since to indicate that another event happened at some point later in time`

conj. 自…以来，从…以后；因为，既然


prep. 自…以来，从…以后
`from a particular time or event in the past until the present, or in that period of time`

# 同义

conj. 因为；由于；既然；自…以来；自…以后
`because` `for` `seeing` `when` `now`

adv. 后来
`after` `subsequently` `later on`

# 短语

- `ever since` adv. 从那时到现在
- `long since` 很久以前；从前
- `since when` 自什么时候起，打什么时候开始

# 例句

About six thousand people were arrested, several hundred of whom have since been released.
约六千人被逮捕，不过他们中有几百人后来被释放了。
