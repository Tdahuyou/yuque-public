# 音标

- 英 `/ˌpaɪə'nɪə(r)/`
- 美 `/,paɪə'nɪr/`

# 词义

v. 开拓，开创
`to be the first person to do, invent or use something`

n. 先驱者，倡导者
`Someone who is referred to as a pioneer in a particular area of activity is one of the first people to be involved in it and develop it`

# 同义

n. 先锋；拓荒者
`vanguard` `avant-courier`

vt. 开辟；倡导；提倡
`advocate` `inaugurate`

# 短语

- `young pioneer` 少先队员
- `pioneer park` 创业园
- `pioneer plant` 先锋植物

# 例句

The new cancer treatment was pioneered in the early eighties by Dr Sylvia Bannerjee.
这种新的癌症治疗方法是由西尔维娅·班纳基医生在80年代初期开创的。

...one of the leading pioneers of photojournalism.
…摄影新闻界的主要先驱之一。
