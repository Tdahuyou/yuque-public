# 音标

- 英 `/'zɪgzæg/`
- 美 `/'zɪɡzæɡ/`

# 词义

adj. “之”字形的


v. 弯弯曲曲行进
`to move forward in sharp angles, first to the left and then to the right etc`

n. “之”字形；蜿蜒曲折
`A zigzag is a line that has a series of angles in it like a continuous series of Ws`



zig(急弯)+zag(急弯)→蜿蜒曲折

# 同义

adj. 曲折的；锯齿形的；之字形的
`flexural` `labyrinthic`

vi. 曲折行进；作之字形行进
`corkscrew`

n. 之字形；Z字形
`zz`

adv. 曲折地；之字形地；Z字形地
`circuitously`

# 短语

- `zigzag course` 曲折航线

# 例句

The path zigzagged down the hillside.
小路沿着山坡蜿蜒而下。

They staggered in a zigzag across the road.
他们东倒西歪地踉踉跄跄穿过了公路。
