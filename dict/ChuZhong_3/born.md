# 音标

- 英 `/bɔːn/`
- 美 `/bɔrn/`

# 词义

adj. 出生的，产生的；天生的
`You use born to describe someone who has a natural ability to do a particular activity or job. For example, if you are a born cook, you have a natural ability to cook well. `

# 同义

adj. 天生的
`natural` `inborn`

# 同根

- n. `bear` 熊
- n. `birth` 出生；血统，出身；起源
- vi. `bear` 承受；结果实
- vt. `bear` 忍受；具有；支撑

# 短语

- `was born` 出生
- `born on` 出生于（时间）
- `born of` 源于，出生于
- `born into` ..家庭
- `born again` 再生
- `be born with` 天生具有
- `born yesterday` 乳臭未干
- `first born` adj. 头生的；最长的
- `born approximation` 玻恩近似；玻饵似
- `born to win` 天生大赢家；强者的诞生；生而为赢
- `born days` [口语]有生之日
- `born in the purple` 出身显贵

# 例句

Jack was a born teacher.
杰克是个天生的教师。
