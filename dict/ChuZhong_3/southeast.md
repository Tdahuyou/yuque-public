# 音标

- 英 `/ˌsau'θiːst/`
- 美 `/,saʊθ'ist/`

# 词义

adv. 向东南
`If you go southeast, you travel toward the southeast`

adj. 东南方的，东南部的，来自东南的
`a southeast wind comes from the southeast`

n. 东南，东南
`the direction that is exactly between south and east`



south(南方)+east(东方)→东南方

# 同根

- adj. `southeasterly` 往东南的；来自东南的
- adj. `southerly` 来自南方的；向南的
- adv. `southeasterly` 往东南；来自东南
- adv. `southerly` 来自南方；向南
- n. `southeaster` 东南的强风；东南风
- n. `southerly` 南风

# 短语

- `southeast asia` 东南亚

# 例句

I know we have to go southeast, more or less.
我知道我们大概得往东南方向走。
