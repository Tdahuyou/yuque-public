# 音标

- 英 `/ˈpresiənt/`
- 美 `/'prɛsɪənt/`

# 词义

adj. 有先见之明的
`If you say that someone or something was prescient, you mean that they were able to know or predict what was going to happen in the future`

# 同义

adj. 预知的；有先见之明的
`provident` `previsional`

# 同根

- n. `prescience` 先见；预知

# 例句

...an eerily prescient comedy about a populist multimillionaire political candidate.
...围绕一位身价数百万的民粹主义政治候选人的一部喜剧，其预见性近乎诡异。
