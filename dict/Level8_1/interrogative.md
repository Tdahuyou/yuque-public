# 音标

- 英 `/,ɪntə'rɒgətɪv/`
- 美 `/,ɪntə'rɑɡətɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 提问；质问的
`an interrogative sentence, pronoun etc asks a question or has the form of a question. For example, ‘who’ and ‘what’ are interrogative pronouns.`

# 同根

- adj. `interrogatory` 质问的；疑问的
- n. `interrogation` 讯问；审问；[语] 疑问句
- n. `interrogator` 质问者；询问机
- n. `interrogatory` 质问；疑问
- vi. `interrogate` 审问；质问
- vt. `interrogate` 审问；质问；[计] 询问

# 短语

- `interrogative sentence` 疑问句
- `interrogative pronoun` 疑问代词

# 例句

He made a further interrogative noise.
他发出了进一步疑问的声音。
