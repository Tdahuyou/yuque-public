# 音标

- 英 `/'ruːmɪ/`
- 美 `/'rumi/`

# 词义

adj. 宽敞的
`a house, car etc that is roomy is large and has a lot of space inside it`

# 同义

adj. 宽敞的；广阔的；宽大的
`extensive` `vast` `wider` `spacious` `capacious`

# 同根

- adv. `roomily` 宽敞地；广阔地
- n. `roominess` 广阔；宽敞

# 例句

The car is roomy and a good choice for anyone who needs to carry equipment.
这车内部宽敞，对于任何需要携带设备的人来说是个不错的选择。
