# 音标

- 英 `/dɪsə'biːdɪənt/`
- 美 `/'dɪsə'bidɪənt/`

# 词义

adj. 不服从的，违背的
`deliberately not doing what you are told to do by your parents, teacher, etc`

# 同义

adj. 不服从的；违背的；不孝的
`nonconforming` `unfilial`

# 同根

- n. `disobedience` 不服从；违反，违抗

# 例句

Her tone was that of a parent to a disobedient child.
她的声音听上去好像家长在教训不听话的小孩。
