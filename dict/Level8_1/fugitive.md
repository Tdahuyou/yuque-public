# 音标

- 英 `/ˈfjudʒɪtɪv/`
- 美 `/'fjudʒətɪv/`

# 词义

n. 逃亡者
`someone who is trying to avoid being caught by the police`

# 同义

adj. 逃亡的；无常的；易变的
`uncertain` `mobile` `variable` `liquid` `unstable`

n. 逃亡者；难捕捉之物
`runagate` `levanter`

# 同根

- adj. `fugacious` 无常的；短暂的；易逃逸的
- n. `fugacity` 无常；易逃逸；不安定

# 例句

The rebel leader was a fugitive from justice.
叛军首领是一名逃避司法的人。
