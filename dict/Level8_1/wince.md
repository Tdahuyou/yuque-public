# 音标

- 英 `/wɪns/`
- 美 `/wɪns/`

# 词义

v. 畏缩，皱眉
`to suddenly change the expression on your face as a reaction to something painful or upsetting`

# 同义

vi. 畏缩，退避
`recoil from` `shrink back from`

n. 畏缩；脸部肌肉的抽搐
`backwardness` `self-distrust`

# 例句

Sandra winced as the dentist started to drill.
牙医开始钻牙了，桑德拉敛眉蹙额。
