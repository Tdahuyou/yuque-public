# 音标

- 英 `/seɪdʒ/`
- 美 `/sedʒ/`

# 词义

n. 胜任，贤人
`someone, especially an old man, who is very wise`

adj. 精明的，贤明的
`very wise, especially as a result of a lot of experience`

# 同义

n. 圣人；贤人；哲人
`saint` `sadu`

adj. 明智的；贤明的；审慎的
`wise` `calculating` `advisable` `sensible`

# 同根

- adv. `sagely` 贤能地；贤明地

# 例句

Not everybody is a sage. Who can be entirely free from error?
人非圣贤，孰能无过?
