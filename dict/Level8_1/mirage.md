# 音标

- 英 `/'mirɑ:ʒ, mi'rɑ:ʒ/`
- 美 `/mə'rɑʒ/`

# 词义

n. 海市蜃楼，幻觉=miracle
`an effect caused by hot air in a desert, which makes you think that you can see objects when they are not actually there`

# 同义

n. [气象]海市蜃楼；幻想，妄想
`Dessert Mirage` `Fata Morgana`

# 例句

It hovered before his eyes like the mirage of an oasis.
它在他眼前晃动，好像海市蜃楼里的绿洲。
