# 音标

- 英 `/əˈfɪlieɪt/`
- 美 `/ə'fɪlɪet/`

# 词义

v. 附属于
`if a group or organization affiliates to or with another larger one, it forms a close connection with it`

# 同义

n. 联号；隶属的机构等
`associated company`

vt. 使附属；接纳；使紧密联系
`receive` `take on sb`

vi. 参加，加入；发生联系
`participate in` `take part in` `go through`

# 同根

- adj. `affiliated` 附属的；有关连的
- n. `affiliation` 友好关系；加入；联盟；从属关系
- v. `affiliated` 使隶属；加入（affiliate的过去分词）

# 短语

- `affiliate marketing` 联盟营销；从属营销
- `affiliate program` 联盟计划；伙伴计划
- `affiliate with` v. 交往
- `affiliate company` 联营公司

# 例句

What of the affiliate groups or the "network of networks"?
那些附属组织或者称“网络的网络”又将怎样？

Afterwards, data can be shared between and among the root and affiliate registries with the assurance that each given key partition is unique.
这样，在确保每一个给定的键分区是唯一的情况下，根注册中心和附属注册中心之间就可以共享数据了。
