# 音标

- 英 `/,i:kwə'niməti, ,e-/`
- 美 `/,ɛkwə'nɪməti/`

# 词义

n. 平静，镇定
`calmness in the way that you react to things, which means that you do not become upset or annoyed`

# 同义

n. 平静；镇定
`peace` `equilibrium`

# 同根

- adj. `equanimous` 安静的；镇定的

# 例句

He received the news with surprising equanimity.
他得到消息时显得异常镇静。
