# 音标

- 英 `/,ɒnə(ʊ)mætə'piːə/`
- 美 `/,ɑnə,mætə'piə/`

# 词义

n. 拟声
`the use of words that sound like the thing that they are describing, for example ‘hiss’ or ‘boom’`

# 同义

n. 拟声；声喻法
`whump` `echoism`

# 例句

Who cares about onomatopoeia?
谁在意拟声呢？

In fact, the word Okobo is an onomatopoeia, that is it represents the sound of walking in them.
实际上，欧扣波这个词是个拟声词，象征穿着它走路时发出的声音。
