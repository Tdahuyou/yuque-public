# 音标

- 英 `/'spæŋɡl/`
- 美 `/'spæŋgl/`

# 词义

n. 闪光的饰品；
`a small piece of shiny metal or plastic sewn onto clothes to give them a shining effect`

v. 使发光
`to cover something with shiny points of light`

# 同根

- adj. `spangled` 装饰着…的；闪烁的
- v. `spangled` 用闪亮金属片装饰；使…闪烁（spangle的过去分词）

# 例句

...robes that glittered with spangles.
...缀着闪光饰片的袍子。
