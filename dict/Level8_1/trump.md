# 音标

- 英 `/trʌmp/`
- 美 `/trʌmp/`

# 词义

v. 胜过
`to do better than someone else in a situation when people are competing with each other`

# 同义

n. 王牌；法宝；喇叭
`horn` `matador`

vt. 胜过；打出王牌赢
`overcome` `exceed` `cap` `transcend`

vi. 出王牌；吹喇叭
`blow job`

# 短语

- `donald trump` 唐纳德·特朗普（美国地产大亨）

# 例句

By wearing a simple but stunning dress, she had trumped them all.
她穿了一条简单却惊艳的裙子，力压群芳。
