# 音标

- 英 `/ˈfɜ:rtɪv/`
- 美 `/'fə:tiv/`

# 词义

adj. 偷偷摸摸的，鬼鬼祟祟的
`behaving as if you want to keep something secret`

# 同义

adj. 鬼鬼祟祟的，秘密的
`secret` `underground` `chamber` `inside` `cabinet`

# 同根

- adv. `furtively` 偷偷地；暗中地
- n. `furtiveness` 偷偷摸摸；鬼鬼祟祟

# 例句

There was something furtive about his actions.
他的行为有点鬼鬼祟祟。
