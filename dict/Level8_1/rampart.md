# 音标

- 英 `/'ræmpɑ:t/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. （城堡等周围宽口的）防御土墙；防御，保护
`a wide pile of earth or a stone wall built to protect a castle or city in the past`

# 同义

n. 壁垒；垒；防御土墙
`base` `vallum`

vt. 用壁垒围绕；防卫
`protect` `defend against`

# 例句

...a walk along the ramparts of the Old City.
...一次沿着老城的土城墙的漫步。
