# 音标

- 英 `/'prelju:d/`
- 美 `/'prɛljud/`

# 词义

n. 前奏，序曲
`if an event is a prelude to a more important event, it happens just before it and makes people expect it`

# 同义

n. 前奏；序幕；前奏曲
`intrada` `prolusion`

vi. 作为序曲；奏序曲
`sound off`

# 例句

Living together as a prelude to marriage is now considered acceptable in many countries.
婚前同居如今在许多国家都已被接受。
