# 音标

- 英 `/,sɜ:kəm’spekt/`
- 美 `/'sɝkəm'spɛkt/`

# 词义

adj. 谨慎的，小心的
`thinking carefully about something before doing it, in order to avoid risk`

# 同义

adj. 细心的，周到的；慎重的
`measured` `scrupulous`

# 同根

- adv. `circumspectly` 慎重地；留心地
- n. `circumspection` 慎重；细心

# 例句

The governor was usually circumspect when dealing with the media.
这位州长和媒体打交道时通常都很谨慎小心。
