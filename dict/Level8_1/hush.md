# 音标

- 英 `/hʌʃ/`
- 美 `/hʌʃ/`

# 词义

vt&vi. （使）安静下来
`to make someone stop shouting, talking, crying etc`

n. 安静，寂静
`a period of silence, especially when people are expecting something to happen`

# 同义

vt. 安静；肃静；缄默
`keep silent`

n. 安静；肃静；沉默
`silence` `tranquilness`

vi. 沉默；安静下来
`pipe down` `fall silent`

int. 嘘；别作声
`sh` `hist`

# 同根

- adj. `hushed` 寂静的；安静的
- v. `hushed` 安静；缄默；肃静（hush的过去分词）

# 短语

- `hush money` 堵嘴钱；封口钱
- `hush puppies` （美）油炸玉米饼
- `hush up` 肃静；掩盖，防止…张扬出去

# 例句

Ella asked them to hush their voices.
埃拉叫他们别作声。
