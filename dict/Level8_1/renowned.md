# 音标

- 英 `/ri'naund/`
- 美 `/ri'naund/`

# 词义

adj. 有名的
`known and admired by a lot of people, especially for a special skill, achievement, or quality`

# 同义

adj. 著名的；有声望的
`famous` `noted` `distinguished` `celebrated` `famed`

# 同根

- n. `renown` 声誉；名望
- vt. `renown` 使有声望

# 例句

The area is renowned for its Romanesque churches.
这个地区以其罗马式教堂闻名。
