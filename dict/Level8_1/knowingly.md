# 音标

- 英 `/ˈnəʊɪŋli/`
- 美 `/'noɪŋli/`

# 词义

adv. 特意地；故意地
`in a way that shows you know about something secret or embarrass­ing`

# 同义

adv. 故意地；机警地，狡黠地
`deliberately` `intentionally`

# 同根

- adj. `knowing` 博学的；狡猾的；心照不宣的
- n. `knowing` 认知
- v. `knowing` 知道；认识；确信（know的ing形式）

# 例句

The two women smiled knowingly at each other.
两个女人会意地相视一笑。
