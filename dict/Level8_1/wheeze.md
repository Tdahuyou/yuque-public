# 音标

- 英 `/wiːz/`
- 美 `/wiz/`

# 词义

v. 喘息，呼哧呼哧的作响
`If someone wheezes, they breathe with difficulty and make a whistling sound`

# 同义

vi. 喘息；呼哧呼哧地响
`catch one's breath` `pant`

n. 喘息；喘气声
`hyperpnea`

# 同根

- n. `wheezing` 哮喘，气喘
- v. `wheezing` 喘息着说；喘息地发出（wheeze的ing形式）

# 例句

He had serious problems with his chest and wheezed and coughed all the time.
他胸部有严重问题，总是不停地气喘和咳嗽。
