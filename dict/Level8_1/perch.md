# 音标

- 英 `/pə:tʃ/`
- 美 `/pɝtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 栖息+on，位于高处
`When a bird perches on something such as a branch or a wall, it lands on it and stands there`

# 同义

n. 高位；栖木；杆
`pole` `high-order`

vt. 栖息；就位；位于；使坐落于
`inhabit` `take post`

vi. 栖息；就位；位于
`sit` `take post`

# 同根

- n. `percher` 栖于树上的鸟；高坐者；织物检查工

# 例句

A blackbird flew down and perched on the parapet outside his window.
一只乌鸫飞下来，栖息在他家窗外的矮墙上。
