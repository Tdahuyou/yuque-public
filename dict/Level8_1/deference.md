# 音标

- 英 `/'defərəns/`
- 美 `/'dɛfərəns/`

# 词义

n. 顺从，服从
`polite behaviour that shows that you respect someone and are therefore willing to accept their opinions or judgment`

# 同义

n. 顺从
`compliance` `Friend VS Lover`

# 同根

- adj. `deferential` 恭敬的；惯于顺从的
- vi. `defer` 推迟；延期；服从
- vt. `defer` 使推迟；使延期

# 短语

- `in deference to` 鉴于；听从
