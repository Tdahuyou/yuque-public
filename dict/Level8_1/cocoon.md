# 音标

- 英 `/kəˈkun/`
- 美 `/kə'kʊn/`

# 词义

n. 茧，蚕茧
`a silk cover that young  moths  and other insects make to protect themselves while they are growing`

# 同义

n. [昆]茧；卵囊
`oocyst`

# 同根

- n. `cocooning` 茧式生活（闲暇时闭门不出的生活方式）；茧式涂封
- v. `cocooning` 作茧；过茧式生活；将…密封起来（cocoon的ing形式）

# 例句

...like a butterfly emerging from a cocoon.
…好似蝴蝶破茧而出。
