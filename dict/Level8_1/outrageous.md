# 音标

- 英 `/aʊt'reɪdʒəs/`
- 美 `/aʊt'redʒəs/`

# 词义

adj. 暴怒的，令人吃惊的
`If you describe something as outrageous, you are emphasizing that it is unacceptable or very shocking`

# 同义

adj. 粗暴的；可恶的；令人吃惊的
`bearish` `abhorrent`

# 同根

- adv. `outrageously` 凶残地，残暴地
- n. `outrage` 愤怒，愤慨；暴行；侮辱
- vt. `outrage` 凌辱，强奸；对…施暴行；激起愤怒

# 例句

By diplomatic standards, this was outrageous behaviour.
按外交标准，这是不可接受的行为。
