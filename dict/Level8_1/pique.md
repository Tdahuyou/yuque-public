# 音标

- 英 `/pi:k/`
- 美 `/pik/`

# 词义

v. 激起，引起
`to make you feel interested in something or someone`

# 同义

vt. 刺激；伤害…自尊心；激怒
`stimulate` `fan` `anger` `motivate` `needle`

n. 生气；愠怒；呕气
`vitality` `animation` `pet`

# 同根

- adj. `piquant` 辛辣的；活泼的；痛快的；顽皮的；刺激的

# 短语

- `out of pique` 由于反感(或忿怒)

# 例句

She was hostile to him, which piqued his curiosity.
她对他有敌意，这激起了他的好奇心。
