# 音标

- 英 `/mɔθ/`
- 美 `/mɔθ/`

# 词义

n. 蛾
`an insect related to the  butterfly  that flies mainly at night and is attracted to lights. Some moths eat holes in cloth.`

# 同义

n. [昆]蛾；蛀虫
`miller` `deathwatch beetle`

# 同根

- adj. `mothy` 多蛾的；虫蛀的

# 短语

- `diamondback moth` 小菜蛾
- `silkworm moth` 蚕蛾
- `gypsy moth` 舞毒蛾（等于Porthetria dispar）
- `silk moth` 蚕蛾

# 例句

What do a hummingbird, a moth, and a maple tree have in common?
蜂鸟、蛾子和枫树之间有什么共同之处呢？

Around streetlights in leafy suburbs what often seems like a moth at first glance may in fact be a small bat.
在绿树成荫的郊区，街灯周围经常有乍一看象蛾子的飞行物，而事实上可能是一只微蝠。

Authorities say the mob of 'roos is endangering a species of local lizard and the threatened gold sun moth through overgrazing.
有关部门表示，由于过度放牧，这些袋鼠已经对一种当地蜥蜴和金日蛾的生存构成了威胁。
