# 音标

- 英 `/kəʊ'ɜːs/`
- 美 `/ko'ɝs/`

# 词义

v. 强制，迫使
`to force someone to do something they do not want to do by threatening them`



co (看作com， 一起) + erce (限制) → 限制在一起 → 强制

# 同义

vt. 强制，迫使
`pressure` `enforce`

# 同根

- adj. `coercive` 强制的；胁迫的；高压的
- n. `coercion` 强制；强迫；高压政治；威压

# 例句

Potter had argued that the government coerced him into pleading guilty.
波特曾争辩说是政府胁迫他认罪。
