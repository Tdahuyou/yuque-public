# 音标

- 英 `/'venəreɪt/`
- 美 `/'vɛnəret/`

# 词义

v. 崇敬，尊敬
`to honour or respect someone or something because they are old, holy, or connected with the past`



vener (尊敬) + ate → 崇敬

# 同义

vt. 崇敬，尊敬
`honor` `regard` `worship`

# 同根

- adj. `venerable` 庄严的，值得尊敬的；珍贵的
- n. `veneration` 尊敬；崇拜
- n. `venerability` 尊敬；庄严；历史悠久
- n. `venerator` 崇拜者；尊敬者

# 例句

a symbol of Arab courage, to be venerated for generations
受到世代尊崇的阿拉伯人勇气的象征
