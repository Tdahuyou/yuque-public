# 音标

- 英 `/jɪp/`
- 美 `/jɪp/`

# 词义

n. 叫喊声
`Yip is also a noun`

v. 犬吠；尖叫
`If a dog or other animal yips, it gives a sudden short cry, often because of fear or pain`

# 同义

vi. 犬吠；尖叫
`pipe` `bellen`

# 例句

...a yip of pain.
...一声痛苦的吠叫。

Far up the west rim of the canyon, a coyote yipped twice.
远处峡谷的西岸，一只丛林狼嚎叫了两声。
