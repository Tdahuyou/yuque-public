# 音标

- 英 `/'aɪpiːs/`
- 美 `/'aɪ'pis/`

# 词义

n. [光]目镜，[光]接目镜
`the glass piece that you look through in a microscope or telescope`

# 同义

n. [光]目镜，接目镜
`ocular lens`

# 例句

Power, or magnification, of a telescope is actually the ratio between two independent optical systems – (1) the telescope itself, and (2) the eyepiece (ocular) you are using.
 望远镜的放大倍数其实就是两个独立的光学系统的比值-(1)望远镜本身(2)以及所使用的目镜。
