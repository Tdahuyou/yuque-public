# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'bʌf/`
- 美 `/rɪ'bʌf/`

# 词义

n. 断然拒绝；回绝；漠不关心
`an unkind or unfriendly answer to a friendly suggestion or offer of help`

v. 断然拒绝
`If you rebuff someone or rebuff a suggestion that they make, you refuse to do what they suggest`



re (反) + buff ( = puff， 喷； 吹) → 反过来喷气 → 回绝

# 同义

n. 断然拒绝；回绝；漠不关心
`indifference` `apathy`

vt. 断然拒绝
`slap on`

# 例句

He received a humiliating rebuff from his manager.
他遭到经理的断然拒绝，十分丢脸。

His proposals have already been rebuffed by the governor.
他的建议已被州长拒绝了。
