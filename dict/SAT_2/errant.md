# 音标

- 英 `/'er(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'ɛrənt/`

# 词义

adj. 不定的；周游的；错误的；偏离正路的
`Errant is used to describe someone whose actions are considered unacceptable or wrong by other people. For example, an errant husband is unfaithful to his wife. `



err (错误) + ant → 错误的

# 同义

adj. 不定的；周游的；错误的；偏离正路的
`false` `wrong` `improper`

# 同根

- n. `errancy` 错误；错误状态；犯错误的倾向

# 短语

- `knight errant` n. 唐·吉诃德式的人物；游侠骑士

# 例句

Usually his cases involved errant husbands and wandering wives.
通常他接手的案子都涉及出轨的丈夫和离家出走的妻子。
