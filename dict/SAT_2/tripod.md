# 音标

- 英 `/'traɪpɒd/`
- 美 `/'traɪpɑd/`

# 词义

n. [摄]三脚架；三脚桌
`a support with three legs, used for a piece of equipment, camera etc`



tri (三) + pod (脚) → 三脚架

# 同义

n. [摄]三脚架；三脚桌
`spider` `trivet`

# 例句

I left the camera and tripod in the rain and jumped in my vehicle until I had calmed down.
我只好将摄像机和三脚架留在雨中，跳进车内。 后来我才平静下来。

It is very important that you do not move your computer or tripod during the whole process.
非常重要的是在整个过程中你千万不可移动电脑或三脚架。
