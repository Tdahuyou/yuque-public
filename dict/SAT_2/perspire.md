# 音标

- 英 `/pə'spaɪə/`
- 美 `/pɚ'spaɪɚ/`

# 词义

v. 流汗；分泌；渗出
`if you perspire, parts of your body become wet, especially because you are hot or have been doing hard work`



per + spire (呼吸) → 皮肤的呼吸 → 出汗

# 同义

vi. 流汗；分泌；渗出
`seep`

# 同根

- n. `perspiration` 汗水；流汗；努力

# 例句

Willie was perspiring heavily .
威利大汗淋漓。
