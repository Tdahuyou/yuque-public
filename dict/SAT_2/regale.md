# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'geɪl/`
- 美 `/rɪ'ɡel/`

# 词义

v. 取悦；盛情款待
`If someone regales you with stories or jokes, they tell you a lot of them, whether you want to hear them or not`

n. 款待




re (使) + gale (高兴) → 使高兴

# 同义

vt. 盛情款待
`do proud`

vi. 享用；享受
`feast`

n. 款待
`entertainment` `treat`

# 例句

He was constantly regaled with tales of woe.
别人老是给他讲些倒霉事儿来逗他开心。
