# 音标

- 英 `/wiːn/`
- 美 `/win/`

# 词义

v. 使断奶；使断念；使放弃
`to gradually stop feeding a baby or young animal on its mother’s milk and start giving it ordinary food`

n. （苏格兰）幼儿




那孩子会自己穿衣 (wear)， 早该断奶(wean)了 → 使断奶

# 同义

vt. 使断奶；使断念；使放弃
`ablactate`

n. （苏格兰）幼儿
`infant` `papoose`

# 同根

- adj. `weaning` 断奶的
- n. `weaning` 断奶
- v. `weaning` 断奶（wean的ing形式）；使断绝

# 例句

When would be the best time to start weaning my baby?
