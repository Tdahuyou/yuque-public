# 音标

- 英 `/æl'vɪələ; ,ælvɪ'əʊlə/`
- 美 `/æl'viəlɚ/`

# 词义

adj. 肺泡的；齿槽的
`of, relating to, or resembling an alveolus `

n. 齿槽音
`a  consonant  sound such as /  t  / or /  d  / that you make by putting the end of your tongue behind your upper front teeth`

# 同根

- adj. `alveolate` [生物] 蜂窝状的；有肺泡的

# 短语

- `alveolar ridge` [医]牙槽嵴
- `alveolar macrophage` [医]肺泡巨噬细胞

# 例句

Their principle function is production of type III collagen, elastin, and proteoglycans of the extracellular matrix of the alveolar septa.
其主要的功能是产生III型胶原，弹性蛋白和肺泡隔细胞外基质中的蛋白多糖。

The effects of quartz on membranous fluidity labelled with DPH and ANS and the production of oxygen free radicals of alveolar macrophages were studied in vitro.
采用体外细胞实验方法，观察了石英尘对DPH和ANS标记的肺胞巨噬细胞膜流动性和荧光性质的影响，同时测定了肺泡巨噬细胞活性氧的产生。
