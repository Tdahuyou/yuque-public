# 音标

- 英 `/'pærəfreɪz/`
- 美 `/ˈpærəfreɪz/`

# 词义

n. 释义；解释；意译
`a statement that expresses in a shorter, clearer, or different way what someone has said or written`

v. 释义
`to express in a shorter, clearer, or different way what someone has said or written`



para (辅助) + phrase (短语) → 起辅助解释的短语 → 解释

# 同义

vt. 改述
`rephrase`

vi. 释义；意译
`free translation`

# 同根

- n. `paraphrasis` 释义；意译；改变措辞

# 例句

The last two clauses were an exact quote rather than a paraphrase of Mr. Forth's remarks.
最后两个分句是对福思先生言论的准确引用而不是改述。
