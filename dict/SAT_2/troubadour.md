# 音标

- 英 `/'truːbədɔː/`
- 美 `/'trubədɔr/`

# 词义

n. 行吟诗人；民谣歌手
`a type of singer and poet who travelled around the  palaces  and castles of Southern Europe in the 12th and 13th cen-turies`

# 同义

n. 行吟诗人；民谣歌手
`balladeer`

# 例句

I needed to be with people who admired my long hair and troubadour credentials, you see.
因为我想要与赞赏我的长头发的人在一起，然后让他们看到我真的是一位有实力的行呤歌手。
