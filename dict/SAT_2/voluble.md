# 音标

- 英 `/'vɒljʊb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'vɑljəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 健谈的；缠绕的；易旋转的
`If you say that someone is voluble, you mean that they talk a lot with great energy and enthusiasm`



volu (看作volume， 音量) + ble → 谈话中音量一直没变 → 健谈的

# 同义

adj. 健谈的；缠绕的；易旋转的
`talkable` `communicative`

# 同根

- n. `volubility` 流利；健谈

# 例句

She was voluble with excitement.
她因为激动而变得很健谈。

Bert is a voluble, gregarious man.
伯特是个健谈且善于交际的人。
