# 音标

- 英 `/'mɔːd(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'mɔrdnt/`

# 词义

n. 涟音；波音
`a melodic ornament consisting of the rapid alternation of a note with a note one degree lower than it `

# 同义

n. 涟音；波音
`Boeing`

# 例句

Players often play this trill as a mordent and in most cases even this is not done properly.
演奏者们常常把颤音演奏成波音，在绝大多数情况下波音也不能完整的演奏好。
