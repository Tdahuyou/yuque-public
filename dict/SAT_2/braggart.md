# 音标

- 英 `/'brægət; -ɑːt/`
- 美 `/'bræɡɚt/`

# 词义

n. 吹嘘；好自夸者；大言者
`someone who is always talking too proudly about what they own or have done`

adj. 吹牛的；自夸的
`boastful `



brag (吹牛) + gart → 吹牛者

# 同义

n. 吹嘘；好自夸者；大言者
`gassing` `boasting`

adj. 吹牛的；自夸的
`boastful` `windy`

# 同根

- adj. `bragger` 自夸的（brag的比较级形式）
- n. `bragging` 吹牛
- n. `bragger` 吹牛者
- v. `bragging` 夸耀（brag的ing形式）
- vi. `bragging` 夸口

# 例句

Her father was an old unmarried professor of mathematics, a brutal man and a braggart, who went out to give lessons in spite of his age.
她的父亲是个性情粗暴、爱吹牛的老数学教师，从没正式结过婚，虽然上了年纪，却还靠替人补课度日。
