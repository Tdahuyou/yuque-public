# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌŋ(k)tjʊəs/`
- 美 `/'ʌŋktʃuəs/`

# 词义

adj. 油质的；虚情假意的；油腔滑调的
`If you describe someone as unctuous, you are critical of them because they seem to be full of praise, kindness, or interest, but are obviously insincere`



unct (油) + uous → 油脂的； 油腔滑调的

# 同义

adj. [食品]油质的；虚情假意的；油腔滑调的
`oily` `oleaginous`

# 同根

- adv. `unctuously` 油腔滑调地；油腻地
- n. `unctuousness` 润滑性；滑腻性；油性

# 例句

...the kind of unctuous tone that I've heard often at diplomatic parties.
...我在外交聚会上经常听到的那种虚情假意的腔调。
