# 音标

- 英 `/,ɪnkə'məʊdɪəs/`
- 美 `/,inkə'məudjəs/`

# 词义

adj. 不便的；狭窄的
`insufficiently spacious; cramped `

# 同义

adj. 不便的；狭窄的
`narrow` `confined`

# 同根

- vt. `incommode` 添麻烦；使感不便；妨碍

# 例句

I awoke with stiff and cramped muscles from having slept long on the incommodious seat of a day coach.
由于在座席客车不舒服的座位上睡了太久，我肌肉僵硬麻痹的醒来。

Indeed, while the internal structure of the code that comprises the application is incommodious to change and difficult to trace, externally the system may fulfill its functional requirements well.
必须承认，当组成程序的代码的内部结构不适应更改，或者很难追踪时，在外部系统可能还会很好的履行它的职责。
