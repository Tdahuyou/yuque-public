# 音标

- 英 `/'vəʊt(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/'votəri/`

# 词义

n. 崇拜者；出家人
`someone who regularly practises a particular religion`

adj. 立誓任圣职的；誓约的
`ardently devoted to the services or worship of God, a deity, or a saint `



vot (宣誓) + ary → 宣誓忠诚信仰的人 → 信徒

# 同义

n. 崇拜者；出家人
`worshiper` `adorer`

# 例句

The organismic, "what you can do for your country" implies that government is the master or the deity, the citizen, the servant or the votary.
而后半句" 你能为你的国家做些什么"则意味着政府是主人，或者是神祗，公民只是仆人或者信徒。
