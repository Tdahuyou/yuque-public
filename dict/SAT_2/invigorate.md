# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'vɪgəreɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'vɪɡəret/`

# 词义

v. 鼓舞；使精力充沛
`to make the people in an organization or group feel excited again, so that they want to make something successful`



in (进入) + vig (活力) + or + ate (使…) → 使活力进入 → 鼓舞

# 同义

vt. 鼓舞；使精力充沛
`stimulate` `lift` `spirit` `inspire`

# 同根

- adj. `invigorating` 爽快的；精力充沛的
- adj. `invigorated` 精力充沛的；生气勃勃的
- n. `invigoration` 鼓舞；身心愉快；精力充沛；滋补
- n. `invigorator` 强壮剂，补药
- v. `invigorated` 鼓舞；增添活力（invigorate的过去式）
- vt. `invigorating` 振兴（invigorate的ing形式）

# 例句

Carey’s hope was that the church would be renewed and invigorated.
凯里希望教会能复兴。
