# 音标

- 英 `/'bjuːteɪn/`
- 美 `/'bjʊten/`

# 词义

n. [有化]丁烷
`a gas stored in liquid form, used for cooking and heating`

# 例句

Portable stove such as butane or charcoal.
便携炉，比如丁烷或木炭
