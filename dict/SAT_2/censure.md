# 音标

- 英 `/'senʃə/`
- 美 `/'sɛnʃɚ/`

# 词义

v. 责难，责备
`If you censure someone for something that they have done, you tell them that you strongly disapprove of it`

n. 责难
`the act of expressing strong disapproval and criticism`



检查员 (censor) 趁机责难 (censure)

# 同义

vt. 责难，责备
`impugn` `decry`

n. 责难
`decrial` `dyslogy`

vi. 谴责，责备
`accuse of` `blame for`

# 同根

- adj. `censurable` 该责备的；可非难的

# 例句

The ethics committee may take a decision to admonish him or to censure him.
道德规范委员会可能决定对他进行警告或批评。
