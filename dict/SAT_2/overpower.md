# 音标

- 英 `/əʊvə'paʊə/`
- 美 `/'ovɚ'paʊɚ/`

# 词义

v. 压倒；克服；使无法忍受
`If you overpower someone, you manage to take hold of and keep hold of them, although they struggle a lot`



over (在…之上) + power (力量) → 压制力量 → 压倒

# 同义

vt. 压倒；克服；使无法忍受
`snow under` `outbrave`

# 同根

- adj. `overpowering` 压倒性的；无法抵抗的

# 例句

It took ten guardsmen to overpower him.
动用了10个守卫才将他制服。
