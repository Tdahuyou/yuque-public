# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'kʌmbə; en-/`
- 美 `/ɪn'kʌmbɚ/`

# 词义

v. 阻塞；妨害；拖累
`to make it difficult for you to do something or for something to happen`



en (使) + cumber (阻碍) → 阻碍

# 同义

vt. 阻塞；妨害；拖累
`drag` `plug up`

# 同根

- n. `encumbrance` 累赘；妨害物

# 例句

He died in 1874, heavily encumbered by debt.
他于1874年去世，负债累累。
