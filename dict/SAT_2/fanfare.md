# 音标

- 英 `/'fænfeə/`
- 美 `/'fæn'fɛr/`

# 词义

n. 吹牛，炫耀；喇叭或号角嘹亮的吹奏声
`A fanfare is a short, loud tune played on trumpets or other similar instruments to announce a special event`

vt. 热热闹闹地宣布




歌迷 (fan) 们用号角吹奏 (fanfare) 告别 (farewell) 偶像

# 同义

The ceremony opened with a fanfare of trumpets.
