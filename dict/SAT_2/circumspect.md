# 音标

- 英 `/'sɜːkəmspekt/`
- 美 `/'sɝkəm'spɛkt/`

# 词义

adj. 细心的，周到的；慎重的
`If you are circumspect, you are cautious in what you do and say and do not take risks`



circum (周围) + spect (看) → 向周围看 → 慎重的

# 同义

adj. 细心的，周到的；慎重的
`measured` `scrupulous`

# 同根

- adv. `circumspectly` 慎重地；留心地
- n. `circumspection` 慎重；细心

# 例句

The banks should have been more circumspect in their dealings.
银行在交易时应更加谨慎。
