# 音标

- 英 `/,dʒʌkstə'pəʊz/`
- 美 `/'dʒʌkstə'poz/`

# 词义

v. 并列；并置
`to put things together, especially things that are not normally together, in order to compare them or to make something new`



juxta (接近) + pose (放) → 紧挨着放 → 并置， 并列

# 同义

vt. 并列；并置
`counterpose`

# 同根

- n. `juxtaposition` 并置，并列；毗邻

# 例句

The technique Mr. Wilson uses most often is to juxtapose things for dramatic effect.
威尔逊先生最常用的技巧是把事物并列起来以获得戏剧性效果。
