# 音标

- 英 `/'pɪktəgrɑːf/`
- 美 `/'pɪktoɡræf/`

# 词义

n. [语]象形文字；古代石壁画；统计图表
`A pictogram is a simple drawing that represents something. Pictograms were used as the earliest form of writing. `

# 同义

n. [语]象形文字；古代石壁画；[统计]统计图表
`hieroglyphic writing` `ideograph`

# 同根

- adj. `pictographic` 象形文字的，绘画文字的

# 例句

...a pictogram of a pine tree.
…一幅代表一棵松树的象形图画。
