# 音标

- 英 `/əb'stiːmɪəs/`
- 美 `/əb'stimɪəs/`

# 词义

adj. 节约的，节省的；有节制的
`careful not to have too much food, drink etc`



abs (不) + tem (酒) + ious → 不沾酒 → 有节制的

# 同义

adj. 节约的，节省的；有节制的
`economical` `managing` `saving` `moderate`

# 同根

- adv. `abstemiously` 适度地；有节制地

# 例句

The Norwegian adolescents who said they regularly use alcohol and cigarettes were no more likely to report acne than those who were abstemious.
说自己长了青春痘的那些经常抽烟喝酒的挪威青少年并不比说自己长了青春痘的有节制的孩子们多。
