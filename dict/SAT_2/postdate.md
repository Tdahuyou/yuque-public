# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊst'deɪt/`
- 美 `/'post'det/`

# 词义

v. 填迟……的日期；把……的日期填迟；继……之后
`if you postdate a cheque, you write it with a date that is later than the actual date, so that it will not become effective until that time`

n. 事后日期；推迟日期




post (在后面) + date (日期) → 推迟…的日期

# 例句

Some of the mosaics postdate this period.
这些镶嵌画中有些要晚于这段时期。
