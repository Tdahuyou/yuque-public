# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'tætʃ/`
- 美 `/dɪ'tætʃ/`

# 词义

v. 分离；派遣；使超然
`If you detach one thing from another that it is attached to, you remove it. If one thing detaches from another, it becomes separated from it. `



de (分离) + tach (看作touch， 接触) → 不接触 → 分开

# 同义

vt. 分离；派遣；使超然
`mission` `keep apart`

# 同根

- adj. `detached` 分离的，分开的；超然的
- adj. `detachable` 可分开的；可拆开的；可分遣的
- n. `detachment` 分离，拆开；超然；分遣；分遣队
- v. `detached` 分离

# 短语

- `detach from` 从…分离；拆卸

# 例句

Detach the white part of the application form and keep it for reference only.
取下申请表的白色部分，仅备参考。

They clambered back under the falls to detach the raft from a jagged rock.
他们爬回到瀑布下面以便将卡在锯齿状岩石间的小艇拉出来。
