# 音标

- 英 `/'ædʒʊt(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'ædʒʊtənt/`

# 词义

n. 副官，助手
`an army officer responsible for office work`

adj. 辅助的


# 同义

n. 副官，助手
`friend` `assistant` `aid`

adj. 辅助的
`assistant` `helping` `subsidiary` `auxiliary` `aided`

# 例句

As adjutant I was responsible for his day-to-day programme.
作为副官，我有责任安排他的日常事务。
