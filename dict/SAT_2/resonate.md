# 音标

- 英 `/'rez(ə)neɪt/`
- 美 `/'rɛzə'net/`

# 词义

v. 共鸣；共振
`if something such as an event or a message resonates, it seems important or good to people, or continues to do this`



re (回) + son (声音) + ate (使…) → 使产生回声 → 使共鸣

# 同义

vi. 共鸣；共振
`sympathise`

# 同根

- adj. `resonant` 洪亮的，共振的；共鸣的
- adj. `resonating` 产生共鸣的
- n. `resonance` [力] 共振；共鸣；反响
- n. `resonator` [声] 共鸣器，共鸣体；共振器
- n. `resonating` 感通
- v. `resonating` 产生共鸣；回响（resonate 的ing形式）

# 例句

The bass guitar began to thump so loudly that it resonated in my head.
低音吉他开始砰然作响，震得我的脑袋嗡嗡的。
