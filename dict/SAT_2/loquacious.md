# 音标

- 英 `/lə'kweɪʃəs/`
- 美 `/lə'kweʃəs/`

# 词义

adj. 饶舌的，多话的
`a loquacious person likes to talk a lot`



loqu (说话) + aci + ous (…的) → 老说话的 → 多话的

# 同义

adj. 饶舌的，多话的
`talkative` `loose-tongued`

# 同根

- n. `loquacity` 多话，饶舌；喧噪

# 例句

He said that despite older adults being less “loquacious” or talkative than their younger adult counterparts, they elicit much more vocal responses when calling.
他说，不管老人不太“饶舌”还是比年轻同伴更多话，他们呼叫时都能激发更多的语音回应。
