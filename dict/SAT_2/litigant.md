# 音标

- 英 `/'lɪtɪg(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'lɪtɪɡənt/`

# 词义

n. 诉讼当事人
`someone who is making a claim against someone or defending themselves against a claim in a court of law`

adj. 诉讼的




litig (打官司) + ant → 打官司的人 → 诉讼人

# 同义

adj. 诉讼的
`contentious`

# 同根

- adj. `litigious` 好诉讼的；好争论的
- n. `litigation` 诉讼；起诉
- n. `litigator` 诉讼律师；诉讼人
- vi. `litigate` 对簿法庭
- vt. `litigate` 在法庭相争；提出诉讼

# 例句

Touching the case, I suggest that we should go to ask the litigant.
关于这个案子，我建议我们去问问当事人。

How can I as a private litigant seek redress for defamation that ruins my business?
做为私人诉讼当事人，我怎么对毁了我的事业的诽谤寻求补救呢？
