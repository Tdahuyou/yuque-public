# 音标

- 英 `/'sɪtəd(ə)l; -del/`
- 美 `/ˈsɪtədəl/`

# 词义

n. 城堡；大本营；避难处
`a strong  fort (= small castle )  built in the past as a place where people could go for safety if their city was attacked`

# 同义

n. 城堡；大本营；避难处
`castle` `alcazar`

# 例句

The citadel at Besançon towered above the river.
贝桑松城堡高过河面。
