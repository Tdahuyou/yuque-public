# 音标

- 英 `/ə'θɜːst/`
- 美 `/ə'θə:st/`

# 词义

adj. 渴望的
`having an eager desire; longing `

# 同义

adj. 渴望的
`eager` `desired` `hungry` `anxious` `sick`

# 例句

I am restless. I am athirst for faraway things.
我心绪不宁。我渴望着遥远的事物。

I will give unto him that is athirst of the fountain of the water of life freely.
我要将生命泉的水白白赐给那口渴的人喝。
