# 音标

- 英 `/'pɒlɪglɒt/`
- 美 `/'pɑlɪɡlɑt/`

# 词义

n. 通晓数种语言的人；用通晓数种语言记载的书
`A polyglot is a person who speaks or understands many languages`

adj. 通数国语言，数国语言的
`Polyglot is used to describe something such as a book or society in which several different languages are used`



poly (多) + glot (声门； 语言) → 多种语言的

# 例句

...Chicago's polyglot population.
...芝加哥的多语种人口。
