# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spærɪdʒ/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spærɪdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 蔑视；毁谤
`If you disparage someone or something, you speak about them in a way which shows that you do not have a good opinion of them`



他居然贬损 (disparage) 这位著名作家的文章 (paragraph)

# 同义

vt. 蔑视；毁谤
`hold in scorn` `contemn`

# 同根

- adj. `disparaging` 毁谤的；轻蔑的
- adv. `disparagingly` 以轻视的态度，蔑视地；以贬抑的口吻
- n. `disparagement` 轻蔑；轻视
- n. `disparager` 毁谤者
- v. `disparaging` 蔑视（disparage的ing形式）

# 例句

...Larkin's tendency to disparage literature.
…拉金诋毁文学的倾向。
