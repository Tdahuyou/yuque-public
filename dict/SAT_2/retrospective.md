# 音标

- 英 `/retrə(ʊ)'spektɪv/`
- 美 `/,rɛtrə'spɛktɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 回顾的；怀旧的；可追溯的
`related to or thinking about the past`

n. 回顾展
`a show of the work of an  artist  , actor,  film-maker  etc that includes examples of all the kinds of work they have done`



retro (后退) + spect (看) + ive → 回顾的

# 同义

adj. 回顾的；怀旧的；可追溯的
`nostalgic` `reminiscent`

# 同根

- adv. `retrospectively` 回顾地
- n. `retrospect` 回顾，追溯
- n. `retrospection` 回顾；反省；追忆
- vi. `retrospect` 回顾，追溯；回想
- vt. `retrospect` 回顾；追忆

# 短语

- `retrospective cohort study` 回顾性队列研究；回顾性队列调查

# 例句

a retrospective study of 110 patients
对110名病人进行的回溯研究
