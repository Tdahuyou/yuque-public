# 音标

- 英 `/'swɪnd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/swɪndl/`

# 词义

n. 欺诈；骗局
`a situation in which someone gets money by deceiving someone else`

v. 诈骗；骗取
`to get money from someone by deceiving them`



s + wind (风) + le → 四处吹风， 搞诈骗

# 同义

n. 欺诈；骗局
`fraudulence` `confidence trick`

vt. 诈骗；骗取
`nobble` `linken`

vi. 诈骗
`shark` `con game`

# 同根

- n. `swindler` 骗子
