# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'sɜːdʒ(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'sɝdʒənt/`

# 词义

adj. 叛乱的；起义的


n. 叛乱者；起义者
`one of a group of people fighting against the government of their own country, or against authority`



in (内部) + surge (浪涛； 升起) + nt → 内部起浪潮 → 起义的

# 同义

adj. 叛乱的；起义的
`revolting`

n. 叛乱者；起义者
`rebeldom` `riser`

# 同根

- adj. `insurrectional` 叛乱的；起义的；暴动的
- adj. `insurrectionary` 暴动的；起义的，造反的
- adv. `insurgently` 汹涌地；造反地
- n. `insurgency` 叛乱；暴动；叛乱状态
- n. `insurrection` 暴动；叛乱
- n. `insurgence` 暴动；起义；造反；叛乱
- n. `insurrectionary` 暴动者，起义者
- n. `insurrectionist` 起义者；造反者；暴动者

# 例句

When reporting their own activities US Units are inclined to classify civilian kills as insurgent kills, downplay the number of people killed or otherwise make excuses for themselves.
当报道他们自己活动，美军倾向于将平民的伤亡推给叛乱者，对人们被杀的数目一笔带过从而来未自己制造出借口。
