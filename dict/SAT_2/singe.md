# 音标

- 英 `/sɪn(d)ʒ/`
- 美 `/sɪndʒ/`

# 词义

v. 烧焦；烤焦
`to burn the surface of something slightly, or to be burned slightly`

n. 烧焦；烤焦
`a mark on the surface of something where it has been burnt slightly`



sing (唱； 作响) + e → 烧焦的木材噼啪作响 → 烧焦

# 同义

vt. 烧焦；烤焦
`air burn` `scorch`

vi. 烧焦；烤焦
`air burn` `char`

n. 烧焦；烤焦
`scorch`

# 例句

The flames had singed her hair.
火焰燎了她的头发。
