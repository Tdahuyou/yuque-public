# 音标

- 英 `/ə'frʌnt/`
- 美 `/ə'frʌnt/`

# 词义

v. 冒犯，有意冒犯；面对；公开侮辱
`to offend or insult someone, especially by not showing respect`

n. 轻蔑；公开侮辱
`a remark or action that offends or insults someone`



af (看作ad， 向…) + front (面部) → 冲着某人面部叫骂 → 公开侮辱

# 同义

vt. 冒犯，有意冒犯；面对；公开侮辱
`give offense` `offend`

n. 轻蔑；公开侮辱
`floccinaucinihilipilification` `scornfulness`

# 例句

He stepped back, affronted by the question.
他觉得这个问题语带冒犯，退后了几步。
