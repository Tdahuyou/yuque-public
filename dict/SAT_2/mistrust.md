# 音标

- 英 `/mɪs'trʌst/`
- 美 `/,mɪs'trʌst/`

# 词义

v. 不信任；怀疑
`to not trust someone, especially because you think they may treat you unfairly or dishonestly`

n. 不信任；怀疑
`the feeling that you cannot trust someone, especially because you think they may treat you unfairly or dishonestly`



mis (错误， 坏) + trust (相信) → 不相信， 猜疑

# 同义

vt. 不信任；怀疑
`dispute` `question` `wonder`

vi. 不信任；怀疑
`wonder` `question`

n. 不信任；怀疑
`doubt` `challenge`

# 同根

- adj. `mistrustful` 不信任的；深疑的，多疑的

# 例句

As a very small child she had learned to mistrust adults.
她很小的时候就学会了不相信大人。
