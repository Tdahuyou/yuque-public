# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'mɒlɪənt/`
- 美 `/ɪ'mɑlɪənt/`

# 词义

n. [药]润肤剂；软化剂
`An emollient is a liquid or cream that you put on your skin to make it softer or to reduce pain`

adj. 使柔软的
`making someone feel calmer when they have been angry`



e + moll ( = soft， 软) + ient → 使肌肤变柔软的东西 → 润肤剂

# 同义

n. [药]润肤剂；软化剂
`softener` `softening agent`

# 例句

The choice of emollient should be made taking into account budgetary limitations and ease of supply.
选择润肤剂应考虑到预算的多少，以及是否容易供应。
