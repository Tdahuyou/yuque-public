# 音标

- 英 `/sɪə/`
- 美 `/sɪr/`

# 词义

v. 烤焦；使…枯萎
`To sear something means to burn its surface with a sudden intense heat`

adj. 枯萎的；烤焦的


n. 烙印；烧焦痕迹
`a mark caused by searing `



灼烧 (sear) 使人无法忍受 (bear)

# 同义
