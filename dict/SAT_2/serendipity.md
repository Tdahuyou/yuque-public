# 音标

- 英 `/,ser(ə)n'dɪpɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/,sɛrən'dɪpəti/`

# 词义

n. 意外发现珍奇事物的本领；有意外发现珍宝的运气
`Serendipity is the luck some people have in finding or creating interesting or valuable things by chance`

# 同根

- adj. `serendipitous` 偶然发现的

# 例句

Some of the best effects in my garden have been the result of serendipity.
我花园里最珍贵的几件物品是机缘巧合之下意外所得。
