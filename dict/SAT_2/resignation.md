# 音标

- 英 `/rezɪg'neɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,rɛzɪɡ'neʃən/`

# 词义

n. 辞职；放弃；辞职书；顺从
`an occasion when you officially announce that you have decided to leave your job or an organization, or a written statement that says you will be leaving`



来自resign (辞职) + ation → 辞职

# 同义

n. 辞职；放弃；辞职书；顺从
`compliance` `demission`

# 同根

- adj. `resigned` 顺从的；已辞职的；己放弃的
- adv. `resignedly` 听从地；服从地
- n. `resign` 辞去职务
- v. `resigned` 辞职；顺从（resign的过去分词）
- vi. `resign` 辞职
- vt. `resign` 辞职；放弃；委托；使听从

# 例句

Illness forced his resignation.
他不得不因病辞职。

Further resignations are expected later this week.
本周后半周会有更多人辞职。
