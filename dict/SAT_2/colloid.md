# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒlɒɪd/`
- 美 `/'kɑlɔɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 胶质的
`of or relating to the gluelike translucent material found in certain degenerating tissues `

n. [物化]胶质
`a mixture having particles of one component, with diameters between 10–7 and 10–9 metres, suspended in a continuous phase of another component`

# 同义

adj. [物化]胶质的
`gluey`

n. [物化]胶质
`gelatine` `pectin`

# 同根

- adj. `colloidal` 胶体的；胶质的；胶状的

# 短语

- `colloid mill` n. 胶体磨
- `protective colloid` [化]保护胶体
- `colloid chemistry` 胶体化学
