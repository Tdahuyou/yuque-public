# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'pjuːt/`
- 美 `/im'pju:t/`

# 词义

v. 归罪于，归咎于；嫁祸于
`If you impute something such as blame or a crime to someone, you say that they are responsible for it or are the cause of it`



im (在…) + pute (思考) → 思考后认为错误在… → 归咎于

# 同义

vt. 归罪于，归咎于；嫁祸于
`blame` `charge upon`

# 同根

- adj. `imputable` 可归因的；可嫁祸的；使负责的
- n. `imputation` 归罪；非难；归咎；污名

# 例句

It is grossly unfair to impute blame to the United Nations.
把责任归咎于联合国是极不公平的。
