# 音标

- 英 `/kɒndɪ'sendɪŋ/`
- 美 `/,kɑndɪ'sɛndɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 表现出优越感的；居高临下的
`behaving as though you think you are better, more intelligent, or more important than other people – used to show disapproval`

v. 屈尊（condescend的ing形式）




来自condescend (v. 谦逊， 屈尊)

# 同义

adj. 谦逊的；故意屈尊的；有优越感的
`modest` `unassuming`

# 同根

- adv. `condescendingly` 谦逊地；俯就地；屈尊地
- n. `condescension` 谦虚；屈尊；傲慢态度
- vi. `condescend` 屈尊；谦逊

# 例句

Professor Hutter’s manner is extremely conde-scending.
赫特教授的态度极为傲慢。
