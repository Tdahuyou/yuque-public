# 音标

- 英 `/bɪ'grʌdʒ/`
- 美 `/bɪ'ɡrʌdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 羡慕，嫉妒；吝惜，舍不得给
`to feel angry or upset with someone because they have something that you think they do not deserve`



be + grudge (不给予) → 舍不得给

# 同义

vt. 羡慕，嫉妒；吝惜，舍不得给
`spare` `envy`

# 例句

I certainly don't begrudge him the Nobel Prize.
我肯定不会妒忌他得了获诺贝尔奖。
