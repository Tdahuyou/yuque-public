# 音标

- 英 `/spɔː/`
- 美 `/spɔr/`

# 词义

n. 孢子
`a cell like a seed that is produced by some plants such as  mushrooms  and can develop into a new plant`

vi. 长孢子


# 同根

- adj. `sporogenous` 产孢子的
- n. `sporotrichosis` 侧孢病霉；[医] 孢子丝菌病；分支孢菌病
