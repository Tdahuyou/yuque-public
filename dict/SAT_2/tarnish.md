# 音标

- 英 `/'tɑːnɪʃ/`
- 美 `/'tɑrnɪʃ/`

# 词义

n. 晦暗，无光泽；污点
`dullness of colour, or loss of brightness`

v. 玷污；使……失去光泽；使……变灰暗
`if an event or fact tarnishes someone’s  reputation  , record, image etc, it makes it worse`



tarn (隐藏) + ish → 隐藏光泽 → 失去光泽

# 同义

n. 晦暗，无光泽；污点
`dulness` `discoloration`

vt. 玷污；使……失去光泽；使……变灰暗
`cloud` `pollute`

# 例句

His regime was tarnished by human rights abuses.
他的政权因践踏人权而形象受损。
