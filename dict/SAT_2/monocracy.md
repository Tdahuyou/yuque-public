# 同义

n. 独裁政治

The citizen politics is a kind of monocracy politics, and the law is the body of people's will.
国民政治是法治政治，法律是人民意志的体现；国民政治的实现、维持和运用。

Party rights are the outcome and symbol of modern democracy and monocracy, which possess the nature of legitimacy and democracy.
