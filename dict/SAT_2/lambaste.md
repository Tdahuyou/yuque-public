# 音标

- 英 `/læm'beɪst/`
- 美 `/læm'best/`

# 词义

v. 痛打；严责；鞭打
`If you lambaste someone, you criticize them severely, usually in public`



lam (鞭打) + baste (狠揍) → 用鞭子狠揍 → 痛打

# 同义

vt. 痛打；严责；鞭打
`hide` `trounce`

# 例句

And you can be sure that when the referee cheats our boys, I'll be ready to lambaste him.
而且你可以放心，如果裁判对孩子们不公平，我会公开斥责他。
