# 音标

- 英 `/'piːsmiːl/`
- 美 `/'pismil/`

# 词义

adv. 零碎地；逐个地
`Piecemeal is also an adverb`

adj. 零碎的；逐渐的
`a process that is piecemeal happens slowly and in stages that are not regular or planned properly`

n. 片；块


vt. 粉碎




piece (片) + meal (进食) → 面包要一片片地吃 → 逐渐的

# 同义

adj. 零碎的；逐渐的
`gradual` `bitty`

n. 片；块
`block` `mass` `slip`

vt. 粉碎
`comminute` `pulverize`

# 例句

It was built piecemeal over some 130 years.
它是在大约一百三十年间一点一点建造起来的。
