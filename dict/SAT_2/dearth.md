# 音标

- 英 `/dɜːθ/`
- 美 `/də:θ/`

# 词义

n. 缺乏；饥馑；粮食不足
`a situation in which there are very few of something that people want or need`



物以稀 (dearth) 为贵 (dear)

# 同义

n. 缺乏；饥馑；粮食不足
`shortage` `deficiency` `short of` `need` `drought`

# 例句

Construction had slowed because of a dearth of labourers.
建筑进程缓慢是因为劳动力不足。
