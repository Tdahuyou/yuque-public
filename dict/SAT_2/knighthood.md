# 音标

- 英 `/'naɪthʊd/`
- 美 `/'naɪthʊd/`

# 词义

n. 骑士；骑士身份
`A knighthood is a title that is given to a man by a British king or queen for his achievements or his service to his country`



knight (骑士) + hood (身份) → 骑士身份

# 同义

n. 骑士；骑士身份
`Cavs` `ritter`

# 同根

- adj. `knightly` 骑士的，侠义的；英勇高洁的
- adv. `knightly` 骑士般地
- n. `knight` 骑士，武士；爵士
- n. `knightliness` 骑士似的；骑士的；勋爵士的
- vt. `knight` 授以爵位

# 例句

When he finally received his knighthood in 1975 Chaplin was 85.
当他最终在1975年得到爵士封号时，卓别林已经85岁了。
