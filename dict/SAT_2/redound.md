# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'daʊnd/`
- 美 `/rɪ'daʊnd/`

# 词义

v. 有助于；报偿，报应；被转移
`If an action or situation redounds to your benefit or advantage, it gives people a good impression of you or brings you something that can improve your situation`

# 同义

vi. 有助于；报偿，报应；被转移
`play to` `conduce to`

vt. 带来
`bring along` `to bring about`

# 短语

- `redound upon` v. 回报

# 例句

The success in the Middle East redounds to his benefit.
中东地区的该项成就对他有益。
