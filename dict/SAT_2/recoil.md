# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'kɒɪl/`
- 美 `/'rikɔɪl/`

# 词义

v. 畏缩；弹回；报应
`to move back suddenly and quickly from something you do not like or are afraid of`

n. 畏缩；弹回；反作用
`Recoil is also a noun`

# 同义

vi. 畏缩；弹回；报应
`rebound` `shrink back from`

n. 畏缩；弹回；反作用
`reaction` `backwardness`

# 短语

- `recoil from` 畏缩，后退
- `recoil force` n. 反冲力；坐后力

# 例句

We recoil in horror from the thought of subjecting someone to extreme pain.
想到要让一个人经受极度的痛苦，我们就会恐惧地退缩。

...his small body jerking in recoil from the volume of his shouting.
…他小小身体在他大声的叫喊中畏缩地抽搐着。
