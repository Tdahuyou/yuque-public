# 音标

- 英 `/kweɪl/`
- 美 `/kwel/`

# 词义

v. 畏缩，胆怯；感到恐惧
`to be afraid and show it by shaking a little bit or moving back slightly`

n. 鹌鹑
`a small fat bird with a short tail that is hunted for food or sport, or the meat from this bird`



“愧” → 有愧于人 → 畏惧， 颤抖

# 同义

vi. 畏缩，胆怯；感到恐惧
`recoil from` `shrink back from`

# 短语

- `quail egg` 鹌鹑蛋

# 例句

The very words make many of us quail.
这几个字眼让我们中的很多人都感到畏缩。
