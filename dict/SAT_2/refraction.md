# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'frækʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/rɪ'frækʃən/`

# 词义

n. 折射；折光


# 同根

- adj. `refractive` 折射的
- adj. `refractile` 折射的；有折射力的；能折射的
- n. `refractometer` [物] 折射计
- n. `refractivity` [光] 折射率；折射性；[物] 折射本领
- vt. `refract` 使折射

# 短语

- `refraction index` 折射指数，折光指数
- `index of refraction` [物]折射率
- `negative refraction` 负折射
- `double refraction` 双折射
- `refraction coefficient` 折射系数
- `seismic refraction` 震波折射

# 例句

If water had a negative index of refraction, then a straw placed in a glass would appear to bend back under itself.
如果水具有负折射率，那么放在玻璃杯中的吸管似乎可能弯曲到自身下方。

With all that reflection and refraction, modernism creates the illusion that there is an illusion when in fact it is a straightforward statement of money and power.
通过反射和折射，现代主义创造了一个幻觉存在的假象，而实际上它是对金钱和权力不加掩饰的声明。

Rapidly changing refraction due to turbulence along the line of sight affects different colors of light by different amounts and generally produces a twinkling effect for stars.
由于湍流而快速变化的折射在视线中会影响到光的不同颜色，这种影响也各不相同，一般会给恒星产生一种闪烁的效果。
