# 音标

- 英 `/əbˈstrɛpərəs/`
- 美 `/əb'strɛpərəs/`

# 词义

adj. 吵闹的，喧嚣的；难驾驭的
`If you say that someone is obstreperous, you think that they are noisy and difficult to control`



ob + streper (喧闹) + ous → 喧嚣的

# 同义

adj. 吵闹的，喧嚣的；难驾驭的
`rip-roaring` `tumultuous`

# 例句

You know I have no intention of being awkward and obstreperous.
你知道我无意存心作对。
