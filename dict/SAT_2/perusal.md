# 音标

- 英 `/pə'ruːz(ə)l/`
- 美 `/pə'ru:zəl/`

# 词义

n. 熟读；精读
`Perusal of something such as a letter, article, or document is the action of reading it`

# 同义

n. 熟读；精读
`intensive reading`

# 同根

- vt. `peruse` 详细考察；精读

# 例句

They undertook to send each of us a sample contract for perusal.
他们答应给我们每人寄送一份合同样本以供阅读。
