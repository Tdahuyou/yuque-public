# 词义

n. 跨栏赛跑
`Hurdles is a race in which people have to jump over a number of obstacles that are also called hurdles. You can use hurdles to refer to one or more races. `

# 同义

n. 障碍；跨栏；跨栏跑；障碍赛跑（hurdle的复数）

# 短语
