# 音标

- 英 `/ðəʊ/`
- 美 `/ðo/`

# 词义

conj. 虽然；但是
`used to introduce a statement that makes the main statement coming after it seem surprising, unlikely, or unexpected`

# 同义

adv. 可是，虽然；不过；然而
`nevertheless` `not but that`

conj. 虽然；尽管
`although` `while` `as` `albeit`

# 短语

- `even though` 虽然，即使
- `as though` 好像；仿佛
- `go though` 走过；排练；经历；从头到尾地阅读

# 例句

Though she’s almost 40, she still plans to compete.
尽管已经快40岁了，她仍然打算参加比赛。

Pascal went ahead with the experiment even though he knew it was dangerous.
虽然帕斯卡知道这个试验很危险，但他仍然进行下去。
