# 音标

- 英 `/,kæŋgə'ruː/`
- 美 `/,kæŋɡə'ru/`

# 词义

n. 袋鼠
`an Australian animal that moves by jumping and carries its babies in a pouch (= a special pocket of skin ) on its stomach`



“看加入” → 看着袋鼠宝宝进入妈妈的口袋 → 袋鼠

# 同义

n. [脊椎]袋鼠
`roo`

# 短语

- `kangaroo island` n. 袋鼠岛（澳大利亚岛，等于坎加鲁岛）

# 例句

The kangaroo bounded across the road.
那只袋鼠跃过道路。

The echidna lays eggs like a duck but suckles its young in a pouch like a kangaroo.
针鼹鼠像鸡鸭一样下蛋，但却像袋鼠一样在袋里哺乳幼崽。

Legend has it that the kangaroo gets its name from an early meeting between local aborigines and white settlers.
关于袋鼠名字的由来有这样一个传说：早期殖民者初到澳洲，见到了当地的土著人。
