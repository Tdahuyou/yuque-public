# 音标

- 英 `/'reɪnkəʊt/`
- 美 `/ˈreɪnkoʊt/`

# 词义

n. （美）雨衣
`a coat that you wear to protect yourself from rain`



rain(下雨)+coat(外套)→下雨时穿的外套→雨衣

# 同义

n. （美）雨衣
`slicker` `cravenette`

# 例句

He dipped in his raincoat pocket.
他把手深深地伸进雨衣口袋里。

The dark clouds remembered me to take my raincoat along.
乌云提醒我带上雨衣。

If I had taken my raincoat with me this morning, I would not be wet now.
(从句指过去,主句指现在)。如果我今天早上带了雨衣，现在就不会被淋湿了。
