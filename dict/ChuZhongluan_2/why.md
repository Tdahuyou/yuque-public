# 音标

- 英 `/waɪ/`
- 美 `/hwaɪ/`

# 词义

int. 哎呀！什么？
`used when you are surprised or have suddenly realized something`

adv. 为什么
`used to ask or talk about the reason for something`

# 同义

adv. 为什么
`whereto` `forwhy`

# 短语

- `no why` 不为什么

# 例句

Why, look who’s here!
哎呀，看看这是谁！

And I thought to myself, why, I can do that.
我就对自己说，嗨，我能行的。
