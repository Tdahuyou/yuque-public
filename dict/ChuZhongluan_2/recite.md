# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'saɪt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'saɪt/`

# 词义

v. 背诵；叙述；列举
`to say a poem, piece of literature etc that you have learned, for people to listen to`

# 同义

vt. 背诵；叙述；列举
`repeat` `specify` `relate`

vi. 背诵；叙述
`say over` `learn sth by memory`

# 同根

- adj. `recitative` 吟诵的；背诵的；叙述的
- n. `recital` 朗诵，吟诵；独奏会；背诵；独唱会
- n. `recitation` 背诵；朗诵；详述；背诵的诗
- n. `recitative` 朗诵调；宣叙调
- n. `reciter` 朗诵者；背诵者

# 例句

She recited a poem that she had learnt at school.
她背诵了一首学校里学的诗。
