# 音标

- 英 `/tiː/`
- 美 `/ti/`

# 词义

n. 茶叶；茶树；茶点
`a hot brown drink made by pouring boiling water onto the dried leaves from a particular Asian bush, or a cup of this drink`

vt. 给…沏茶


vi. 喝茶；进茶点


# 同义

n. 茶叶；茶树；茶点
`tealeaf` `refreshments`
