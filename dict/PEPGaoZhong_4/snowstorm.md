# 音标

- 英 `/'snəʊstɔːm/`
- 美 `/'snostɔrm/`

# 词义

n. 暴风雪
`a storm with strong winds and a lot of snow`

# 同义

n. 暴风雪；[气象]雪暴
`blizzard` `stormy weather`

# 例句

Just as it’s important not to miss the forest for the trees, neither should we miss the climate for the snowstorm.
对于树木，我们必然会追根到森林；对于暴风雪，我们也要溯源到气候变化。
