# 音标

- 英 `/rɪd/`
- 美 `/rɪd/`

# 词义

v. 摆脱；除去
`to take action so that you no longer have something unpleasant that you do not want`

# 同义

vt. 使摆脱；使去掉
`free from` `debarrass`

# 同根

- n. `riddance` 摆脱；驱逐；解除

# 短语

- `get rid of` 摆脱，除去
- `rid of` 除去，摆脱
- `get rid` 摆脱；排除；处理掉
- `got rid of` v. 免除，除去，摆脱
- `get rid off` 摆脱，去掉

# 例句

The proposals are an attempt to rid the country of political corruption.
这些提议是使该国摆脱政治腐败的一种尝试。
