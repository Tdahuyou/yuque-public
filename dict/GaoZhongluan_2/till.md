# 音标

- 英 `/tɪl/`
- 美 `/tɪl/`

# 词义

n. [地理][水文]冰碛；放钱的抽屉；备用现金
`money in the till is money that a company or organization has`

prep. 直到
`until`

conj. 直到为止


v. 耕种；犁
`to prepare land for growing crops`
