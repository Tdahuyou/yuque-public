# 音标

- 英 `/niː/`
- 美 `/ni/`

# 词义

n. 膝盖，膝
`the joint that bends in the middle of your leg`

v. 用膝盖碰
`to hit someone with your knee`

# 同义

n. 膝盖，[解剖]膝
`lap` `genu`

# 短语

- `knee joint` 膝关节；弯头结合
- `on one's knee` ◎(恳求、祈祷、屈服时)跪着
- `knee pad` n. 护膝
- `knee jerk` [医]膝反射

# 例句

Lucy had a bandage round her knee.
露西的膝盖上缠着绷带。

a knee injury
膝伤
