# 词义

n. 佛教徒
`A Buddhist is a person whose religion is Buddhism`

adj. 佛教的
`Buddhist means relating or referring to Buddhism`

# 短语

- `buddhist temple` 佛寺；佛堂；佛庙
- `buddhist nun` 尼姑
- `buddhist monk` 头陀，和尚；比丘
- `buddhist scriptures` 佛经
