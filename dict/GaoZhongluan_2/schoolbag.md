# 音标

- 英 `/'skuːlbæɡ/`
- 美 `/ˈskulˌbæɡ/`

# 词义

n. 书包
`A schoolbag is a bag that children use to carry books and other things to and from school`

# 同义

n. 书包
`satchel` `mochila`

# 例句

She ran back to the classroom to get her schoolbag.
她跑回教室去取书包。

I was straightening my schoolbag when you phoned me.
你给我打电话时，我正在整理书包。

Mom, could you buy me a new green leather schoolbag?
妈妈，你能为我买一个新的绿色皮书包吗？
