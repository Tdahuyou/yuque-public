# 音标

- 英 `/'vɪdɪə(ʊ)fəʊn/`
- 美 `/'vɪdɪofon/`

# 词义

n. 电视电话
`a type of telephone that allows you to see the person you are talking to on a screen`



video (视频) +phone (电话) →可视电话

# 同义

n. 电视电话
`picturephone`

# 例句

Perhaps as soon as 2007, these assistants will hover over an astronaut's shoulder, serving as an all-in-one PDA, videophone, and air monitor.
最快到2007年，这类助理型机器人就会出现在宇航员的箭头，它能用做PDA、可视电话并监测空气。
