# 音标

- 英 `/saɪ/`
- 美 `/saɪ/`

# 词义

v. 叹息，叹气
`to breathe in and out making a long sound, especially because you are bored, disappointed, tired etc`

n. 叹息，叹气
`an act or sound of sighing`

# 同义

vi. 叹息，叹气
`suspire`

n. 叹息，叹气
`suspiration`

vt. 叹息，叹气
`suspire`

# 短语

- `with a sigh` 长叹一声
- `deep sigh` 深深的叹息
- `sigh for` 渴望；为……叹息
- `heave a sigh` 叹了口气

# 例句

‘Well, there’s nothing we can do about it now,’ she sighed.
“唉，我们现在对此无能为力。”她叹口气说。
