# 音标

- 英 `/ˌʌn'dɒkjʊmentɪd/`
- 美 `/,ʌn'dɑkjumɛntɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 无证明的
`without the required official papers, permits, etc.`

The place is rife with smugglers, dealers, undocumented immigrants, and slaves.
