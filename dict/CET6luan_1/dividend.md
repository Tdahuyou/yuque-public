# 音标

- 英 `/'dɪvɪdend/`
- 美 `/'dɪvɪdɛnd/`

# 词义

n. 分红，红利
`a part of a company’s profit that is divided among the people with shares in the company`

# 同义

n. 股息；[数]被除数；奖金
`bonus` `stake`

# 短语

- `dividend policy` 股息分配方针
- `cash dividend` 现金股利
- `dividend distribution` 股息分配
- `stock dividend` 股票的股息
- `dividend yield` 股息生息率（每股股利与目前价格的比率）
- `dividend income` 股利收益
- `interim dividend` 期中股利；暂时股息
- `final dividend` 期末股息
- `dividend rate` 股利率，股息率
- `dividend tax` [经]纯益税

# 例句

The first quarter dividend has been increased by nearly 4 percent.
第一季度的股息增加了近4％。
