# 音标

- 英 `/məʊ'tel/`
- 美 `/mo'tɛl/`

# 词义

n. 汽车旅馆
`a hotel for people who are travelling by car, where you can park your car outside your room`



motor(汽车) ＋ hotel(旅馆) → 汽车旅馆

# 例句

The gas station just neighbours to the motel.
加油站就在汽车旅馆的附近。

I also have to get back to the motel. The manager took my passport last night, and now he and the passport are nowhere to be found.
我也必须回到汽车旅馆去，因为旅馆经理昨天晚上拿走了我的护照，到现在为止他本人和我的护照都不知所踪。
