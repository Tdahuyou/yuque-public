# 音标

- 英 `/'traʊzəz/`
- 美 `/'traʊzɚz/`

# 词义

n. [用复数]裤子
`a piece of clothing that covers the lower half of your body, with a separate part fitting over each leg`

# 同义

n. [服装]裤子，长裤
`pants` `britches`

# 短语

- `a pair of trousers` 一条裤子

# 例句

His trousers were slightly too short.
他的裤子有点短。

I need a new pair of trousers for work.
我需要一条上班穿的新裤子。
