# 音标

- 英 `/'spaɪdə/`
- 美 `/'spaɪdɚ/`

# 词义

n. 蜘蛛
`a small creature with eight legs, which catches insects using a fine network of sticky threads`



好莱坞电影Spiderman (《蜘蛛侠》)

# 同义

n. [无脊椎]蜘蛛；设圈套者；三脚架
`araneid` `tripod`

# 同根

- adj. `spidery` 细长足的；蜘蛛一般的；蜘蛛网一般的

# 短语

- `spider silk` 蜘蛛丝
- `spider web` n. 蜘蛛网
- `red spider` 红蜘蛛；山楂叶螨

# 例句

Do you think that the spider can web anything?
你觉得这蜘蛛能用网捕住什么吗?

Why not hold spider of the week contests in your home throughout this season?
在这整个蜘蛛出没的季节里，为什么不在你家里举行蜘蛛周竞赛？

Likewise, by these investigators’ logic, male arachnophobes should get a mild tickle down there whenever they spy a spider scurrying across their desk.
同样的，按这些研究者的逻辑，男性蜘蛛恐惧者每次看到蜘蛛爬过桌子都应该下身瘙痒咯？
