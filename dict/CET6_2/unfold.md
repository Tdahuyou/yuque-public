# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'fəʊld/`
- 美 `/ʌn'fold/`

# 词义

v. 展开；展现，呈现
`to spread open or flat sth that has previously been folded; to become open and flat`



un(不) ＋ fold(折叠) → 展开， 打开

# 同义

vt. 打开；呈现
`crack` `assume` `open up`

vi. 展开；显露
`expand` `develop`

# 例句

She unfolded her arms.
她张开双臂。

As the story unfolds, we learn more about Max’s childhood.
随着故事的展开，我们对马克斯的童年有了更多的了解。
