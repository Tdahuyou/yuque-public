# 音标

- 英 `/'trɪbjuːt/`
- 美 `/'trɪbjut/`

# 词义

n. 献礼；贡物
`something that you say, do, or give in order to express your respect or admiration for someone`



tribut(给予) ＋ e → 贡品

# 同义

n. 礼物；[税收]贡物；颂词
`gift` `giving` `present`

# 短语

- `pay tribute` 致敬；朝贡

# 例句

This book is a fitting tribute to the bravery of the pioneers.
本书是对先驱们大无畏精神恰如其分的献礼。
