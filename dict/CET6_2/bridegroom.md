# 音标

- 英 `/'braɪdgruːm/`
- 美 `/'braɪdɡrum/`

# 词义

n. 新郎
`a man at the time he gets married, or just after he is married`

# 同义

n. 新郎
`groom`

# 例句

The bridegroom asked the guests to stand and then toasted the bridesmaids.
新郎请客人起立为女傧相干杯.

The bride and bridegroom received their guests in the great hall.
新娘和新郎在大厅迎接客人。
