# 音标

- 英 `/trɒt/`
- 美 `/trɑt/`

# 词义

v. （马）小跑
`if a horse trots, it moves fairly quickly with each front leg moving at the same time as the opposite back leg`

n. （马）小跑；慢跑
`the movement of a horse at trotting speed`

# 同义

n. （人）慢跑；马小跑的步态；小跑的马蹄声；刚学步的小孩；老太婆；腹泻；（学生作弊用的）译文对照本
`dogging` `diarrhea`

vi. （马）小跑；（人）慢跑；快步走
`double` `go jogging`

# 同根

- n. `trotter` 猪脚；小跑步的马；快步走的人；当跑腿的人

# 短语

- `on the trot` 一个接一个
- `fox trot` 狐步舞曲；狐步舞

# 例句

Alan took the reins and the small horse started trotting.
艾伦抓住缰绳，小马开始慢跑起来。

Our horses slowed to a trot.
我们的马减速慢跑。

She broke into a trot (= started running slowly ) and hurried on ahead of us.
她突然小跑起来，赶到我们前头。
