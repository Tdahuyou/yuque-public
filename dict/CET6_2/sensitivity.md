# 词义

n. 敏感（性）；灵敏性
`when a situation or subject needs to be dealt with carefully because it is secret or may offend people`

# 例句

It’s a matter of great political sensitivity.
这件事在政治上非常敏感。

The sensitivity of the detector can be increased.
这种探测器的灵敏度还可以提高。
