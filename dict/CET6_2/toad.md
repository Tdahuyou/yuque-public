# 音标

- 英 `/təʊd/`
- 美 `/tod/`

# 词义

n. 蟾蜍，癞蛤蟆
`a small animal that looks like a large frog and lives mostly on land`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]蟾蜍；癞蛤蟆；讨厌的家伙
`hoptoad` `sad apple`

# 短语

- `tree toad` n. 雨蛙

# 例句

Toads have drier, lumpier skins than frogs and spend less time in the water.
蟾蜍的皮比青蛙干燥，疙瘩也更多，呆在水中的时间比青蛙少。
