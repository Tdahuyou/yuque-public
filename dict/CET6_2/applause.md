# 音标

- 英 `/ə'plɔːz/`
- 美 `/ə'plɔz/`

# 词义

n. 喝彩；夸奖，称赞
`the sound of many people hitting their hands together and shouting, to show that they have enjoyed something`

# 同义

n. 欢呼，喝采；鼓掌欢迎
`acclamation` `cheer`

# 例句

She got a round of applause (= a short period of applause) when she finished.
她结束的时候获得一阵掌声。
