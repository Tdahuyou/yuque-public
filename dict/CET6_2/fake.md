# 音标

- 英 `/feɪk/`
- 美 `/feɪk/`

# 词义

n. 假货，膺品
`a copy of a valuable object, painting etc that is intended to deceive people`

adj. 假的
`not real and seeming to be something it is not, in order to deceive people`



打击造(make)假(fake)

# 同根

- n. `fakery` 伪造；假货
- n. `faker` 摊贩；骗子；伪造者，伪装者

# 短语

- `fake commodity` 假货
- `head fake` 以头部做出假动作
- `fake fur` 人造革；假裘皮；仿造皮毛

# 例句

The painting was judged a fake.
这幅画被鉴定为赝品。

I gave a fake name.
我报了个假名。
