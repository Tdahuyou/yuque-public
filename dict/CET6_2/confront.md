# 音标

- 英 `/kən'frʌnt/`
- 美 `/kən'frʌnt/`

# 词义

v. 使面对；使对证
`(of problems or a difficult situation) to appear and need to be dealt with by sb`



con ＋ front(前面) → 在前面出现 → 迎面遇到

# 同义

vt. 面对；遭遇；比较
`front` `compare`

# 同根

- adj. `confrontational` 对抗的；对抗性的
- n. `confrontation` 对抗；面对；对质

# 短语

- `confront with` 使面临

# 例句

The problems confronting the new government were enormous.
新政府面临的难题是巨大的。

I confronted him with my suspicions, and he admitted everything.
我当面向他提出我的怀疑，他什么都承认了。
