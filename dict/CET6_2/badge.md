# 音标

- 英 `/bædʒ/`
- 美 `/bædʒ/`

# 词义

n. 徽章，像章；标志
`a small piece of metal or plastic, with a design or words on it, that a person wears to show that they belong to an organization, support sth, have achieved sth, have a particular rank, etc.`

# 同义

n. [轻]徽章；证章；标记
`marks` `bedge`

# 例句

We were each handed a badge with our name on it.
我们每个人都拿到一个徽章，上面有我们的名字。

He now sees his wartime injuries as a badge of honor.
现在，他把自己在战争中受的伤看成是一种光荣标记。
