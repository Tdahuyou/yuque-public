# 音标

- 英 `/'bæŋkrʌpt/`
- 美 `/'bæŋkrʌpt/`

# 词义

adj. 破产的
`without enough money to pay what you owe`

v. 使破产
`to make a person, business, or country bankrupt or very poor`

# 同义

adj. [经]破产的
`broke` `belly-up`

vt. [经]使破产
`put out of business` `bust`

n. [经]破产者
`insolvent`

# 同根

- n. `bankruptcy` 破产

# 短语

- `go bankrupt` 破产
- `bankrupt of` 完全缺乏

# 例句

The firm went bankrupt before the building work was completed.
建筑工程尚未竣工，公司就破产了。

Johns had been nearly bankrupted through a failed business venture.
约翰斯因为商业投资失败几近破产。
