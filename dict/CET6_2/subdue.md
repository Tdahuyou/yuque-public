# 音标

- 英 `/səb'djuː/`
- 美 `/səb'du/`

# 词义

v. 使屈服，征服
`to bring sb/sth under control, especially by using force`

# 同义

vt. 征服；抑制；减轻
`master` `stay` `restrain` `moderate`

# 同根

- adj. `subdued` 减弱的；被制服的；被抑制的
- v. `subdued` 使服从，压制，减弱（subdue 的过去式和过去分词）

# 例句

Senior government officials admit they have not been able to subdue the rebels.
高级政府官员们承认他们还没能制服反叛者们。
