# 音标

- 英 `/rɪns/`
- 美 `/rɪns/`

# 词义

v. 冲洗；嗽（口）
`to wash clothes, dishes, vegetables etc quickly with water, especially running water, and without soap`

# 同义

vt. 漱；冲洗掉；漂净
`gargle`

n. 冲洗；漂洗；[轻]染发剂；染发
`irrigation` `hair dye`

# 同根

- n. `rinsing` 清水；残渣
- v. `rinsing` （用清水）冲洗；涮，漱口；（用水）送下（rinse的现在分词）

# 短语

- `rinse with` 用…漂掉；用…冲洗掉
- `rinse thoroughly` 彻底清洗
- `rinse water` 冲洗水
- `rinse out` 冲洗掉

# 例句

Let me just rinse my hands.
让我冲个手。

After her meal she invariably rinsed out her mouth.
饭后她总漱口。
