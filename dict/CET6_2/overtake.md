# 音标

- 英 `/əʊvə'teɪk/`
- 美 `/,ovɚ'tek/`

# 词义

v. 突然发生；压倒
`If an event overtakes you, it happens unexpectedly or suddenly.`

# 同义

vt. 赶上；压倒；突然来袭
`catch up with` `to catch up`

vi. 超车
`cut in`

# 同根

- n. `overtaking` 超车；追越；赶上
- v. `overtaking` 超过；压倒（overtake的ing形式）

# 例句

Tragedy was shortly to overtake him, however.
然而，不幸将很快降临到他的头上。

Something like panic overtook me...
一种恐慌的感觉袭裹了我。
