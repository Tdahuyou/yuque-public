# 音标

- 英 `/'pʊlɪ/`
- 美 `/'pʊli/`

# 词义

n. 滑轮，滑车，皮带轮
`a piece of equipment consisting of a wheel over which a rope or chain is pulled to lift heavy things`

# 同义

n. [机]滑轮；皮带轮；滑车
`tackle-block` `trochlea`

# 短语

- `belt pulley` 皮带轮；皮带盘
- `guide pulley` 导向轮；压带轮
- `pulley block` 滑轮组
- `motor pulley` 电动机皮带轮
- `crank pulley` 曲轴皮带轮

# 例句

a system of ropes and pulleys
绳索滑轮系统
