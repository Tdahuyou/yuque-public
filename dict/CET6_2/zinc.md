# 音标

- 英 `/zɪŋk/`
- 美 `/zɪŋk/`

# 词义

n. 锌
`a blue-white metal that is used to make brass and to cover and protect objects made of iron. It is a chemical element: symbol Zn`

v. 在…上镀锌




锌的化学符号为Zn

# 短语

- `zinc oxide` n. 氧化锌
- `zinc alloy` 锌合金
- `zinc sulfate` n. [化]硫酸锌
- `zinc powder` 锌粉，锌蓝粉
- `zinc plating` 镀锌
- `zinc coating` 镀锌；镀锌层；镀锌制品
- `zinc concentrate` 锌精矿；精炼锌
- `zinc chloride` 氯化锌
- `zinc phosphate` 磷酸锌
- `zinc ore` 锌矿；锌矿砂；锌矿石
- `zinc salt` 锌盐
- `zinc acetate` n. [化]醋酸锌；乙酸锌
- `zinc carbonate` 碳酸锌
- `zinc borate` [化]硼酸锌
- `zinc coat` 镀锌；镀锌层
- `zinc stearate` [化]硬脂酸锌
- `zinc silicate` [化]硅酸锌
- `zinc sulphate` 硫酸锌
- `zinc dust` 锌粉
- `zinc sulphide` 硫化锌，闪锌矿；梳化锌
