# 音标

- 英 `/'dæz(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'dæzl/`

# 词义

v. 使目眩；使倾倒
`if a very bright light dazzles you, it stops you from seeing properly for a short time`



爵士乐(jazz)使人倾倒(dazzle)

# 同义

n. 耀眼的光；灿烂
`effulgence` `flashiness`

vi. 眼花缭乱；炫耀
`show off` `brag about`

# 同根

- adj. `dazzling` 耀眼的；眼花缭乱的
- adj. `dazzled` 目眩的；眼花撩乱的
- adv. `dazzlingly` 耀眼地；灿烂地
- adv. `dazedly` 头昏眼花地；恍惚地；眼花缭乱地
- v. `dazzling` 使…眼花（dazzle的ing形式）
- v. `dazzled` （使）眼花（dazzle的过去式）

# 例句

a deer dazzled by the headlights
被车头灯光照得目眩的鹿

As children, we were dazzled by my uncle’s good looks and charm.
小时候，我叔叔的英俊外表和魅力令我们倾倒。
