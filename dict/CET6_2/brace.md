# 词义

n. 支柱
`something that is used to strengthen or support something, or to make it stiff`

v. 拉紧，撑牢
`to make something stronger by supporting it`

# 例句

She had to wear a brace after the accident.
出事以后她只得使用支架。

Wait until we’ve braced the ladder.
等我们把梯子支撑住再说。
