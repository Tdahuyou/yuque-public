# 音标

- 英 `/ʃraɪn/`
- 美 `/ʃraɪn/`

# 词义

n. 神殿，神龛，圣祠
`a place that is connected with a holy event or holy person, and that people visit to pray`

# 同义

n. 圣地；神殿；神龛；圣祠
`temple` `Holy Land`

vt. 将…置于神龛内；把…奉为神圣
`enshrine`

# 短语

- `yasukuni shrine` 靖国神社

# 例句

his pilgrimage to the shrine of St John
他去敬奉圣约翰的圣地朝圣
