# 音标

- 英 `/'epɪsəʊd/`
- 美 `/'ɛpɪsod/`

# 词义

n. （人生的）一段经历；（小说的）片段，插曲
`an event, a situation, or a period of time in sb's life, a novel, etc. that is important or interesting in some way`

# 同义

n. 插曲；一段情节；插话；有趣的事件
`incident` `intermezzo`

# 同根

- adj. `episodic` 插话式的

# 例句

I'd like to try and forget the whole episode.
我倒想尽量把那段经历全部忘掉。

One of the funniest episodes in the book occurs in Chapter 6.
书中最有趣的片段之一在第6章。
