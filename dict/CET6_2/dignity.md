# 音标

- 英 `/'dɪgnɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/'dɪɡnəti/`

# 词义

n. 庄重；尊严；体面
`the ability to behave in a calm controlled way even in a difficult situation`



dign(有价值的) ＋ ity → 高贵

# 同义

n. 尊严；高贵
`sanctity` `nobility`

# 同根

- adj. `dignified` 庄严的；有尊严的；高贵的
- v. `dignified` 使高贵（dignify的过去式）
- vt. `dignify` 使高贵；增威严；授以荣誉

# 例句

We do this because we believe in the dignity and value of every person.
我们这样做是因为我们相信每个人的尊严和价值。

They have to be treated well, with dignity.
必须善待申请人，让人有尊严。

This award must be shared with everyone who strives for justice and dignity.
我一定要与所有为正义和尊严奋斗的人分享此奖。
