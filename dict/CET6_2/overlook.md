# 音标

- 英 `/əʊvə'lʊk/`
- 美 `/,ovɚ'lʊk/`

# 词义

v. 眺望；看漏；宽容
`if a house, room etc overlooks something, it has a view of it, usually from above`

# 同义

vt. 忽略；俯瞰；远眺；检查；高耸于…之上
`forget` `examine` `survey` `review` `inspect`

n. 忽视；眺望
`inobservancy` `negligence`

# 例句

Our room overlooks the ocean.
我们的房间可以俯视大海。

It is easy to overlook a small detail like that.
那样的小细节很容易被忽略。

She found him entertaining enough to overlook his faults.
她觉得他很有趣，足以让她包容他的缺点。
