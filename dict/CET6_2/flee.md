# 音标

- 英 `/fliː/`
- 美 `/fli/`

# 词义

v. 逃走，逃避
`to leave somewhere very quickly, in order to escape from danger`

# 同义

vi. 逃走；消失，消散
`lift` `disappear` `blank`

vt. 逃跑，逃走；逃避
`escape` `desert`

# 短语

- `flee from` vt. 从……逃出来

# 例句

His attackers turned and fled.
袭击他的人转身逃走了。

a camp for refugees fleeing from the war
收留战争难民的难民营
