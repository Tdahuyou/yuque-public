# 音标

- 英 `/kwest/`
- 美 `/kwɛst/`

# 词义

v. 寻找，追求
`to search for sth that is difficult to find`



问题(question)丢了ion需要寻找(quest)

# 同义

n. 追求；寻找
`pursuit` `looking for`

vi. 追求；寻找
`pursue / pursuit` `go after`

vt. 探索
`explore` `search after`

# 同根

- n. `quester` 探求者；追求者

# 短语

- `quest for` 追求；探索，设法找到
- `in quest of` 探寻，寻求；为了追求……

# 例句

He had been questing for religious belief from an early age.
他从早年开始就一直在寻求宗教信仰。

...his questing mind and boundless enthusiasm.
…他的探索型头脑和无限的热情
