# 音标

- 英 `/pæn(t)s/`
- 美 `/pænts/`

# 词义

n. 裤子；男用短衬裤
`a piece of clothing that covers you from your waist to your feet and has a separate part for each leg`

# 同义

n. 裤子；短裤
`britches` `short trousers`

# 短语

- `casual pants` 休闲裤
- `hot pants` 紧身短裤；热裤
- `short pants` 短裤
- `wear the pants` 掌权当家（尤指妇女）

# 例句

She was wearing dark blue pants and a white sweater.
她穿着深蓝色长裤和白色毛衣。

a pair of pants
一件内裤
