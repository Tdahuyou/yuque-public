# 音标

- 英 `/'væksɪneɪt/`
- 美 `/'væksə'net/`

# 词义

v. 给…接种疫苗
`to protect a person or animal from a disease by giving them a vaccine`

# 同根

- adj. `vaccine` 疫苗的；牛痘的
- n. `vaccine` 疫苗；牛痘苗
- n. `vaccination` 接种疫苗；种痘
- n. `vaccinator` 牛痘接种员；种痘刀
- n. `vaccinia` [兽医] 牛痘

# 例句

All children should be vaccinated against measles.
所有儿童都应该接种麻疹疫苗。
