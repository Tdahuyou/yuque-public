# 音标

- 英 `/'plʌndə/`
- 美 `/'plʌndɚ/`

# 词义

v. 掠夺，劫掠
`to steal large amounts of money or property from somewhere, especially while fighting in a war`

n. 掠夺，抢劫
`the act of plundering`

# 同义

n. 抢夺；战利品；掠夺品
`prize` `capture`

vt. 掠夺；抢劫；侵吞
`harry` `despoil`

vi. 掠夺；盗窃
`prey upon` `pillage`

# 同根

- n. `plunderage` 掠夺；盗用船货

# 例句

The rich provinces of Asia Minor were plundered by the invaders.
小亚细亚的富饶地区遭到侵略者的劫掠。

fear of invasion and plunder
对侵略和掠夺的恐惧
