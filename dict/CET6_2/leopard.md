# 音标

- 英 `/'lepəd/`
- 美 `/'lɛpɚd/`

# 词义

n. 豹
`a large animal of the cat family, with yellow fur and black spots, which lives in Africa and South Asia`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]豹；美洲豹
`panther, puma` `pard`

# 短语

- `snow leopard` 雪豹
- `clouded leopard` 云豹
- `leopard cat` 豹猫

# 例句

The young leopard separated from its mother would be eaten by other beast.
与母亲离散的幼豹很可能被其他的野兽吃掉。
