# 音标

- 英 `/'diːlə/`
- 美 `/'dilɚ/`

# 词义

n. 商人；毒品贩；发牌者
`someone who buys and sells a particular product, especially an expensive one`

# 同义

n. 经销商；商人
`distributor` `trader`

# 同根

- n. `dealership` 代理权；代理商；经销权

# 短语

- `drug dealer` 贩毒者；毒品走私犯
- `authorized dealer` 授权经销商；认可交易商
- `securities dealer` 证券交易商
- `franchised dealer` 特许零售商；特约经销商
- `retail dealer` 零售商

# 例句

...an antique dealer.
…一位古董商。

They will stay on the job for as long as it takes to clear every dealer from the street.
他们将坚持工作，直到街道上所有的毒品贩子都被清除。
