# 音标

- 英 `/'bjuːg(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'bjuɡl/`

# 词义

n. 军号，喇叭
`a musical instrument like a trumpet, that is used in the army to call soldiers`

# 同义

n. 喇叭；军号；筋骨草属植物
`horn` `loudspeaker`

vi. 吹号；吹喇叭
`play the trumpet` `blow job`

# 同根

- n. `bugler` 喇叭手

# 例句

The bugle sounded as the troops advanced.
军号吹响前进的号令。
