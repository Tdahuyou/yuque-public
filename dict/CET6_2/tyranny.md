# 音标

- 英 `/'tɪr(ə)nɪ/`
- 美 `/'tɪrəni/`

# 词义

n. 暴政，专制；残暴
`cruel and unfair government`

# 同义

n. 暴政；专横；严酷；残暴的行为（需用复数）
`peremptoriness` `misrule`

# 例句

organizations which have criticized the tyrannies of the government
批判政府暴政的组织

Gorky was often the victim of his grandfather’s tyranny.
高尔基经常是他祖父专横管制的受害者。
