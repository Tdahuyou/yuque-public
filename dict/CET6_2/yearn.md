# 音标

- 英 `/jɜːn/`
- 美 `/jɝn/`

# 词义

v. 渴望，向往
`to have a strong desire for something, especially something that is difficult or impossible to get`

# 同义

vi. 渴望，向往；思念，想念；怀念；同情，怜悯
`rare` `aspire`

# 同根

- adv. `yearningly` 思念地；渴望地；同情地
- n. `yearning` 渴望；怀念；同情
- n. `yearner` 向往型；思念者
- v. `yearning` 渴望；向往；想念（yearn的ing形式）

# 短语

- `yearn for` 渴望
- `yearn towards` 向往…；想念

# 例句

Hannah yearned for a child.
汉娜渴望有个孩子。
