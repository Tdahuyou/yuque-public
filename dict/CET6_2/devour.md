# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'vaʊə/`
- 美 `/dɪ'vaʊɚ/`

# 词义

v. 吞食；吞灭，毁灭
`to eat something quickly because you are very hungry`

# 同义

vt. 吞食；毁灭；凝视
`devastate` `wrack and ruin`

# 同根

- n. `devourer` 吞噬者；排出管

# 例句

The boys devoured their pancakes.
男孩子们狼吞虎咽地把薄煎饼吃了。

Her body had been almost entirely devoured by the disease.
她的身体几乎完全被疾病摧毁。
