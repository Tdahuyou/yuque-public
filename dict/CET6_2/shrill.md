# 音标

- 英 `/ʃrɪl/`
- 美 `/ʃrɪl/`

# 词义

adj. 尖声的
`(of sounds or voices) very high and loud, in an unpleasant way`

v. 尖声地叫
`to produce a very high and unpleasant sound`

# 同义

adj. [声]尖锐的；刺耳的
`grinding` `sharp` `harsh`

adv. [声]尖锐地
`acutely` `pointedly`

vi. 尖声喊叫
`scream out`

n. 尖叫声
`scream`

# 同根

- adj. `shrilly` 尖声的；耀眼的
- adv. `shrilly` 尖声地；耀眼地
- n. `shrillness` 尖锐刺耳

# 例句

‘That’s not true,’ she protested in a shrill voice.
“不是这样的。”她尖声抗议道。

The telephone shrilled twice.
刺耳的电话铃响了两下。
