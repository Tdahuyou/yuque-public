# 音标

- 英 `/ək'ses(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/ək'sɛsəri/`

# 词义

n. 同谋，帮凶
`someone who helps a criminal, especially by helping them hide from the police`

adj. 附属的
`not the most important when compared to others`



access(接近) ＋ ory → 接近(主件) → 附件， 配件

# 同义

n. 配件；附件；[法]从犯
`attachment` `mating parts`

adj. 副的；同谋的；[昆]附属的
`deputy` `vice` `subsidiary` `auxiliary`

# 同根

- adj. `accessary` 附属的；附带的；从犯的
- adj. `accessorial` 附属的；增加的；补充的
- n. `accessary` 附件；装饰品；从犯

# 短语

- `accessory equipment` 辅助设备
- `accessory nerve` 副神经
- `accessory ingredient` 助剂；配合剂

# 例句

an accessory to murder
谋杀案的帮凶

the accessory muscles of respiration
副呼吸肌
