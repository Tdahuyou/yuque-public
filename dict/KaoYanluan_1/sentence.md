# 音标

- 英 `/'sent(ə)ns/`
- 美 `/'sɛntəns/`

# 词义

n. 句子；判决,宣判
`a group of words that usually contains a subject and a verb, and expresses a complete idea. Sentences written in English begin with a capital letter and usually end with a  full stop  or a  question mark`

v. 宣判,判决
`if a judge sentences someone who is guilty of a crime, they give them a punishment`

# 同义

n. [语][计]句子，命题；[法]宣判，判决
`award` `proposition`

vt. 判决，[法]宣判
`determine` `adjudge`

# 同根

- adj. `sentential` 句子的；判决的

# 例句

His voice dropped at the end of the sentence.
到句子结尾处，他压低了嗓音。

She received an eight-year prison sentence.
她被判八年徒刑。

Sanchez was sentenced to three years in prison.
桑切斯被判了三年监禁。
