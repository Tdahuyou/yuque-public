# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'kwɪp/`
- 美 `/ɪ'kwɪp/`

# 词义

v. (with)装备,配备；训练
`to provide a person or place with the things that are needed for a particular kind of activity or work`

# 同义

vt. 装备，配备
`prepare` `provide`

# 同根

- n. `equipment` 设备，装备；器材
- n. `equipage` 装备，用具；马车及侍从

# 短语

- `equip with` 装备，配备；备有…，以…装备

# 例句

They spent a lot of money equipping the school with new computers.
他们花了很多钱给学校配备新计算机。

Relative poverty, however, did not prevent Martin from equipping himself with an excellent education.
然而相对的贫穷并没有妨碍马丁接受良好教育。
