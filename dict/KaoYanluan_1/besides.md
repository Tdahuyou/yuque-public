# 音标

- 英 `/bɪ'saɪdz/`
- 美 `/bɪ'saɪdz/`

# 词义

adv. 此外；并且
`used when adding another reason`

prep. 于…之外；除…以外
`in addition to someone or something else that you are mentioning`

# 同义

adv. 此外；而且
`moreover` `again` `also` `additionally` `then`

prep. 除…之外
`unless` `except as` `in addition to`

# 例句

I need the money. And besides, when I agree to do something, I do it.
我需要这笔钱，而且，我一旦答应做什么事情就会做到。

I think she has many good qualities besides being very beautiful.
我觉得她除了非常漂亮之外，还有许多好的品质。
