# 音标

- 英 `/ɪk'spekt; ek-/`
- 美 `/ɪk'spɛkt/`

# 词义

v. 预期；期望,指望
`to think that something will happen because it seems likely or has been planned`

# 同义

vt. 期望；指望；认为；预料
`promise oneself` `guess` `find` `feel` `make`

vi. 期待；预期
`look foward to` `to look forward to`

# 同根

- adj. `expectant` 期待的；怀孕的；预期中的
- adj. `expectable` 能预期的；意料中的
- adv. `expectantly` 期望地，期待地
- n. `expectation` 期待；预期；指望
- n. `expectancy` 期望，期待
- n. `expectant` 期待者；候选人

# 短语

- `expect too much of` 对(某人)期望过高
- `expect of` 对…期望（要求）
- `expect to do` 期待去做；期望做某事

# 例句

He will be hard to beat. I fully expect (= am completely sure about ) that and I’m ready.
他很难击败。我确信这一点，不过我准备好了。

‘Who are you?’ he murmured, only half expecting (= thinking it was possible, but not likely ) her to answer.
“你是谁？”他低声问道，预计她可能不会回答。

He didn’t get his expected pay rise.
他没有得到他预想的加薪。

The officer expects complete obedience from his troops.
这位军官要求部下绝对服从。
