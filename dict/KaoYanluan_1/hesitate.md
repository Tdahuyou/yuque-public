# 音标

- 英 `/'hezɪteɪt/`
- 美 `/'hɛzɪtet/`

# 词义

v. 犹豫,踌躇
`If you hesitate, you do not speak or act for a short time, usually because you are uncertain, embarrassed, or worried about what you are going to say or do.`



hes(黏附)+it+ate(做)→脚像被粘住了一样→踌躇

# 同义

vi. 踌躇，犹豫；不愿
`vacillate` `stand at`

vt. 踌躇，犹豫；有疑虑，不愿意
`stand at` `hang back`

# 同根

- adj. `hesitant` 迟疑的；踌躇的；犹豫不定的
- adj. `hesitating` 犹豫的
- adj. `hesitative` 支吾其辞的；踌躇的
- adv. `hesitantly` 迟疑地；踌躇地
- adv. `hesitatingly` 踌躇地；支吾其词地
- n. `hesitation` 犹豫
- n. `hesitance` 踌躇；犹豫
- n. `hesitancy` 踌躇，犹豫
- v. `hesitating` 犹豫（hesitate的ing形式）

# 短语

- `don't hesitate` 别再犹豫了
- `hesitate in` 吞吞吐吐

# 例句

The telephone rang. Catherine hesitated, debating whether to answer it.
电话响了。凯瑟琳迟疑着，考虑是否接听。
