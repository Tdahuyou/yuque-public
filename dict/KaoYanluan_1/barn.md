# 音标

- 英 `/bɑːn/`
- 美 `/bɑrn/`

# 词义

n. 谷仓,仓库
`a large farm building for storing crops, or for keeping animals in`



酒吧(bar)加一个门(n)就成了谷仓(barn)

# 同义

n. 谷仓；畜棚；车库；靶（核反应截面单位）
`granary` `garage`

# 短语

- `barn door` 挡光板；谷仓门

# 例句

The barn was heaped with grain.
谷仓里堆满了粮食。

The dilapidated barn detracts charm from the landscape.
圮废失修的谷仓有损景色的美。

The grain is liable to mildew when the humidity in the barn is too high.
仓库里潮气太大， 粮食就容易发霉。
