# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'kɜː/`
- 美 `/ɪn'kɝ/`

# 词义

v. 招致,惹起,遭受
`if you incur something unpleasant, it happens to you because of something you have done`



in(使)+cur(运行)→使运行→招致， 惹起

# 同义

vt. 招致，引发；蒙受
`invite` `court` `secure` `spell` `trigger`

# 同根

- n. `incurring` 招致（遭受）
- n. `incurrence` 招致；遭受

# 例句

She wondered what she’d done to incur his displeasure this time.
她奇怪她这次做了什么引起他的不悦。
