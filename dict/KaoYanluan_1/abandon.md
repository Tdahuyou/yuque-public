# 音标

- 英 `/ə'bænd(ə)n/`
- 美 `/ə'bændən/`

# 词义

v. 离弃，丢弃；遗弃，抛弃；放弃
`to go away from a place, vehicle etc permanently, especially because the situation makes it impossible for you to stay`



a(不)+band(乐队)+on(上)→乐队不再上台→放弃

# 同义

n. 狂热；放任
`loose` `mania`

vt. 遗弃；放弃
`desert` `yield` `quit`

# 同根

- adj. `abandoned` 被抛弃的；无约束的；恣意放荡的；寡廉鲜耻的
- n. `abandonment` 抛弃；放纵
- v. `abandoned` 抛弃（abandon的过去式和过去分词）

# 短语

- `with abandon` 恣意地，放纵地
- `abandon ship` 弃船

# 例句

We had to abandon the car and walk the rest of the way.
我们只好弃车，步行走完剩下的路。

How could she abandon her own child?
她怎么能抛弃自己的孩子？

They abandoned their attempt to recapture the castle.
他们放弃了夺回城堡的努力。
