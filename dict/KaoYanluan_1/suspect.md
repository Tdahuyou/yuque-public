# 音标

- 英 `/ˈsʌspekt; (for v.) səˈspekt/`
- 美 `/sʌspɛkt; (for v.) səˈspɛkt/`

# 词义

v. 猜想；怀疑
`to think that something is probably true, especially something bad`

adj. 可疑的
`not likely to be completely true`

n. 嫌疑犯
`someone who is thought to be guilty of a crime`



sus(下， 后)+(s)pect(看)→在下面偷偷地看→怀疑

# 同义

n. [法]嫌疑犯
`sus`

adj. 可疑的；不可信的
`doubtful` `questionable`

vt. 怀疑；猜想
`dispute` `imagine` `wonder` `question`

vi. 怀疑；猜想
`wonder` `imagine` `question` `suppose`

# 同根

- adj. `suspected` 有嫌疑的
- adj. `suspensive` 暂停的；悬而不决的；可疑的；暧昧的
- v. `suspected` 怀疑（suspect的过去分词和过去式）

# 短语

- `prime suspect` 嫌疑重犯；主要嫌疑犯
- `suspect of` 怀疑

# 例句

She strongly suspected he was lying to her.
她非常怀疑他在对她撒谎。

The two men were convicted on the basis of some highly suspect evidence.
两名男子被定了罪，而定罪证据尚有很大疑点。

Two suspects were arrested today in connection with the robbery.
有两名与劫案有关的嫌疑人今天被逮捕。
