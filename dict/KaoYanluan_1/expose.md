# 音标

- 英 `/ɪk'spəʊz; ek-/`
- 美 `/ɪk'spoz；ek-/`

# 词义

v. (to)使暴露,受到；揭露，揭发
`to put someone in a situation where they are not protected from something dangerous or unpleasant`



ex(出)+pos(放)+e→放出来→使暴露

# 同义

vt. 揭露，揭发；使曝光；显示
`reveal` `prove` `make clear` `disclose`

# 同根

- adj. `exposed` 暴露的，无掩蔽的
- n. `exposure` 暴露；曝光；揭露；陈列
- v. `exposed` 暴露，揭露（expose的过去分词）

# 例句

The report revealed that workers had been exposed to high levels of radiation.
报告披露工人们受到了高强度的辐射。

The film exposes the utter horror of war.
这部电影揭示了战争的极端恐怖。
