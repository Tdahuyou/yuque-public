# 音标

- 英 `/'pedɪkæb/`
- 美 `/'pɛdɪ,kæb/`

# 词义

n. （人力）三轮车
`a pedal-operated tricycle, available for hire, with an attached seat for one or two passengers`

# 同义

n. [车辆]三轮车
`tricycle` `trike` `trishaw`

# 例句

Even pedicab drivers were carrying tea samples and looking for clients on the street.
他说，“甚至连三轮车夫都带着茶样，满街寻找顾客。”
