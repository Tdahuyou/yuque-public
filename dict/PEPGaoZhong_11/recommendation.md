# 音标

- 英 `/,rekəmen'deɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,rɛkəmɛn'deʃən/`

# 词义

n. 推荐信，介绍，介绍信
`a formal letter or statement saying that someone would be a suitable person to do a job, take a course of study etc`



来自recommend(v. 推荐； 介绍)

# 同义

n. 推荐；建议；推荐信
`suggestion` `advice` `proposal` `instance`

# 同根

- vi. `recommend` 推荐；建议
- vt. `recommend` 推荐，介绍；劝告；使受欢迎；托付

# 短语

- `letter of recommendation` 保荐信，介绍信
- `recommendation letter` 推荐信
- `on the recommendation of` 通过…的推荐

# 例句

Try to get letters of recommendation from bosses and colleagues.
设法得到老板和同事的推荐信。
