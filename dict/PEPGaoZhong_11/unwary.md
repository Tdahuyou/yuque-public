# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'weərɪ/`
- 美 `/ʌn'wɛri/`

# 词义

adj. 无警惕的，易受欺骗的
`If you describe someone as unwary, you mean that they are not cautious or experienced and are therefore likely to be harmed or deceived`

# 同义

adj. 粗心的；不警惕的；不谨慎的
`careless` `sloppy`

# 同根

- adv. `unwarily` 不谨慎地；无防备地；不留神地；草率地
- n. `unwariness` 不注意；疏忽

# 例句

With its quicksands the river usually drowns a few unwary visitors every season.
这条有流沙的河每季都要淹死一些不警觉的游客。
