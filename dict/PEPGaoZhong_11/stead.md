# 音标

- 英 `/sted/`
- 美 `/stɛd/`

# 词义

n. 代替；用处，好处
`If you do something in someone's stead, you replace them and do it instead of them.`

# 同义

n. 代替；用处
`substitution` `lieu`

# 短语

- `in stead of` 代替

# 例句

My grandmother and aunt will be there in my parents' stead.
我的祖母和姑妈将代替我的父母出席。

These two games here will stand them in good stead for the future.
这里的两场比赛对他们的将来很有用。
