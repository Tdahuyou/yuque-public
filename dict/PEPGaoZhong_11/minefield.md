# 音标

- 英 `/'maɪnfiːld/`
- 美 `/'maɪn'fild/`

# 词义

n. 布雷区；充满隐伏危险的事物或场合
`an area where a lot of bombs have been hidden just below the ground or under water`

# 同义

n. 布雷区；充满隐伏危险的事物
`mining area`

# 同根

- n. `minelayer` 布雷舰（或飞机）

# 例句

They realized they had wandered into a minefield.
他们意识到自己闯入雷区了。

Choosing the right school can be a bit of a minefield.
要选出合适的学校有时有点困难。
