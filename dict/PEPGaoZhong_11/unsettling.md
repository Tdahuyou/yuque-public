# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'setliŋ/`
- 美 `/ʌn'sɛtlɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 使人不安的，（消息）混乱的
`If you describe something as unsettling, you mean that it makes you feel worried or uncertain`



un (不) + settle (安宁) → 使不安

# 同义

adj. 使人不安的；（消息）扰乱的
`disturbed`

# 例句

Phil had several unsettling dreams every night.
菲尔每晚都做好几个令他不安的梦。
