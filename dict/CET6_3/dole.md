# 音标

- 英 `/dəul/`
- 美 `/dol/`

# 词义

n. 救济，救济金
`money given by the government in Britain to people who are unemployed`

vt. 发放，发给


# 同义

n. [劳经]失业救济金；赈济物；施舍品
`unemployment benefit` `unemployment pension`

# 短语

- `dole out` 少量发放
- `on the dole` 领取救济金

# 例句

The number claiming dole went up by 3,500.
申请领取救济金者增加了3,500人。
