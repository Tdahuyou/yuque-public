# 音标

- 英 `/'hʌmbl/`
- 美 `/'hʌmbl/`

# 词义

adj. 谦逊的；地位低下的；简陋的
`not considering yourself or your ideas to be as important as other people’s`

vt. 使谦恭；使卑下
`to show that you are not too proud to ask for something, admit you are wrong etc`



hum(地) ＋ ble → 接近地的 → 谦逊的

# 例句

a modest and humble man
一个谦卑的男人
