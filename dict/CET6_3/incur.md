# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'kɜː/`
- 美 `/ɪn'kɝ/`

# 词义

vt. 招致，引起；遭受
`if you incur a cost, debt, or a fine, you have to pay money because of something you have done`



in(进入) ＋ cur(发生) → 使发生 → 招致； 遭受

# 同义

vt. 招致，引发；蒙受
`invite` `court` `secure` `spell` `trigger`

# 同根

- n. `incurring` 招致（遭受）
- n. `incurrence` 招致；遭受

# 例句

The government had also incurred huge debts.
政府也已承受了大笔债务。
