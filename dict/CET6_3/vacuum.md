# 音标

- 英 `/'vækjuəm/`
- 美 `/'vækjʊəm/`

# 词义

v. 收集；用真空吸尘器打扫
`to clean using a vacuum cleaner`

n. 真空；真空吸尘器
`a space that is completely empty of all gas, especially one from which all the air has been taken away`



vacu(=vac， 空)+ um → 真空

# 同义

n. [真空]真空；空间；真空吸尘器
`room` `gamma space`
