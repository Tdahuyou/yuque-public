# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'nauns/`
- 美 `/dɪ'naʊns/`

# 词义

vt. 谴责，指责；告发
`to express strong disapproval of someone or something, especially in public`



de(变坏) ＋ nounce(报告) → 打坏报告 → 指责； 告发

# 同义

vt. 谴责；告发；公然抨击；通告废除
`condemn` `denunciate`

# 同根

- n. `denouncement` 告发；痛骂；公开抨击

# 例句

The group denounced the failure by the authorities to take action.
该团体谴责当局没有采取行动。
