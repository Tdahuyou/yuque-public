# 音标

- 英 `/ˌæmju'nɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,æmju'nɪʃən/`

# 词义

n. 弹药，军火
`bullets, shells (  shell  ) etc that are fired from guns`



am(表加强) ＋ mun(服务) ＋ ition → 为战争服务 → 弹药， 军火

# 同义

n. [军]弹药；军火
`ammo` `cartridge`

# 短语

- `live ammunition` [军]实弹

# 例句

He had only seven rounds of ammunition for the revolver.
他只有7发左轮手枪的弹药。
