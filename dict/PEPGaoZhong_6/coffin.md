# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒfɪn/`
- 美 `/'kɔfɪn/`

# 词义

n. 棺材
`a long box in which a dead person is buried or burnt`

# 同义

n. 棺材
`bier` `casket`

# 短语

- `coffin chamber` 墓室

# 例句

When I die, I just need an ordinary coffin.
如果我死了，我只需要一个普通棺材。

What coffin?
什么棺材？

That that was the first problem. And then, that there was another: the empty coffin.
这是首先遇到的困难，还有一层困难，便是那口空棺材。
