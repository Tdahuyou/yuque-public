# 音标

- 英 `/'bek(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'bɛkən/`

# 词义

v. 召唤；吸引
`to make a signal to someone with your hand, to show that you want them to come towards you or to follow you`

n. 表召唤的点头；手势


# 同义

vt. 召唤；吸引
`attract` `absorb` `engage` `draw` `summon`

vi. 吸引；（招手或点头）示意
`attract` `turn one on`

n. 表召唤的点头；手势
`motion` `sign`

# 例句

I could see my husband beckoning me.
我看到我丈夫在向我招手。
