# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌbset/`
- 美 `/'sʌbsɛt/`

# 词义

n. [数]子集；子设备；小团体
`a group of people or things that is part of a larger group of people or things`

# 同义

n. [数]子集；子设备；小团体
`sub-class` `small group`

# 短语

- `data subset` 资料子集，数据子集

# 例句

...subsets of the population such as men, women, ethnic groups, etc.
...人口子集包括男人、女人、不同民族等。
