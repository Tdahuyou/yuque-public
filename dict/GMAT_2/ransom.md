# 音标

- 英 `/'ræns(ə)m/`
- 美 `/'rænsəm/`

# 词义

v. 赎回，赎；勒索赎金；救赎；得赎金后释放
`If you ransom someone who has been kidnapped, you pay the money to set them free`

n. 赎金；赎身，赎回
`an amount of money that is paid to free someone who is held as a prisoner`



ran(跑) + som(看作sum一笔钱) → 用一笔钱跑 → 赎金

# 同义

vt. 赎回，赎；勒索赎金；救赎；得赎金后释放
`redeem` `atone for`

n. 赎金；赎身，赎回
`redemption`

# 短语

- `a king's ransom` ◎作为被俘国王赎金的巨款

# 例句

The same system was used for ransoming or exchanging captives.
同一系统也被用来赎回或交换俘虏。
