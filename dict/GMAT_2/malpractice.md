# 音标

- 英 `/mæl'præktɪs/`
- 美 `/,mæl'præktɪs/`

# 词义

n. 玩忽职守；不法行为；治疗不当
`when a professional person makes a mistake or does not do their job properly and can be punished by a court`



mal(坏) + practice(实行) → 实行得不好 → 治疗失当

# 同义

n. 玩忽职守；不法行为；[临床]治疗不当
`dereliction` `fall asleep at the switch`

# 同根

- n. `malversation` 盗用；贪污；渎职；腐败行为

# 短语

- `malpractice insurance` 医疗事故保险

# 例句

Her doctor was found guilty of malpractice.
她的医生被裁定犯有渎职罪。
