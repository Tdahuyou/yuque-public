# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪnjʊend/`
- 美 `/'mɪnjʊ,ɛnd/`

# 词义

n. [数]被减数
`the number from which another number, the subtrahend, is to be subtracted `

# 例句

If the minuend is larger than the subtrahend, the difference will be positive;
如果减数大于减数，差异将是积极的；

In a subtraction operation, the number or quantity subtracted from the minuend.
当你作两个数的减法时，你用一个被减数去减另一个减数。
