# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'lɪŋkwɪʃ/`
- 美 `/rɪ'lɪŋkwɪʃ/`

# 词义

v. 放弃；放手；让渡
`to let someone else have your position, power, or rights, especially unwillingly`



re(重新) + linqu(=leave离开) + ish → 再次离开 → 放弃

# 同义

vt. 放弃；放手；让渡
`desert` `yield` `quit`

# 同根

- n. `relinquishment` 作罢；让渡

# 例句

No one wants to relinquish power once they have it.
人一旦有了权就不想放弃。
