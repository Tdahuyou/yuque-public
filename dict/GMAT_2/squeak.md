# 音标

- 英 `/skwiːk/`
- 美 `/skwik/`

# 词义

v. 告密；吱吱叫；侥幸成功
`to succeed, win, or pass a test by a very small amount so that you only just avoid failure`

n. 吱吱声；机会
`a very short high noise or cry`

# 同义

vi. 告密；吱吱叫；侥幸成功
`inform` `rat`

n. 吱吱声；机会
`opportunity` `chance` `opening` `room` `occasion`

# 同根

- adj. `squeaky` 发轧声的；吱吱响的
- n. `squeaker` 雏鸽；小猪；咬臼者；吱吱叫的东西；发出短促刺耳声的人（或物）

# 例句

A rat squeaked and ran into the bushes.
一只老鼠吱吱叫着跑进了灌木丛。
