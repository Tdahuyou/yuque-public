# 音标

- 英 `/'ræmdʒet/`
- 美 `/'ræmdʒɛt/`

# 词义

n. [航]冲压式喷气发动机
`a type of jet engine in which fuel is burned in a duct using air compressed by the forward speed of the aircraft `
