# 音标

- 英 `/ˈjuːnɪts/`
- 美 `/'juːnɪts/`

# 词义

n. [计量]单位
`If you consider something as a unit, you consider it as a single, complete thing`

# 同义

n. 单位
`entity unit`

# 短语

- `units concerned` 有关单位
- `international system of units` 国际单位制

# 例句

Agriculture was based in the past on the family as a unit.
农业过去以一个家庭单元为基础。
