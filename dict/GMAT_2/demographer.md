# 音标

- 英 `/diː'mɒɡrəfə/`
- 美 `/dɪ'mɑgrəfɚ/`

# 词义

n. [统计]人口统计学家，人口学家




demo(人民) + graph(写) + er → 人口统计学家

# 同根

- adj. `demographic` 人口统计学的；人口学的
- n. `demography` 人口统计学

# 例句

Male preference in South Korea “is over,” says Monica Das Gupta, a demographer and Asia expert at the World Bank.
莫妮卡，是人口统计学家，也是世界银行的亚洲专家，她说南韩偏爱男性的时代“结束了”。

The first to use it in its modern sense was Alfred Sauvy, a French demographer who drew a parallel with the “third estate” (the people) during the French revolution.
