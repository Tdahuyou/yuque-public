# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒdɪdʒɪ/`
- 美 `/'prɑdədʒi/`

# 词义

n. 奇迹，奇事；奇才；奇观；预兆
`A prodigy is someone young who has a great natural ability for something such as music, mathematics, or sports`

# 同义

n. 奇迹，奇事；奇才；奇观；预兆
`phenomenon` `wonder` `miracle` `warning`

# 同根

- adj. `prodigious` 惊人的，异常的，奇妙的；巨大的

# 短语

- `child prodigy` 神童

# 例句

The Russian tennis prodigy is well on the way to becoming the youngest world champion of all time.
这位俄罗斯网球奇才已很有望成为历史上最年轻的世界冠军。
