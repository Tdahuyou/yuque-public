# 音标

- 英 `/'njuːlɪwed/`
- 美 `/'njʊlɪ,wɛd/`

# 词义

adj. 新婚的


n. 新婚夫妇
`Newlyweds are a man and woman who have very recently got married to each other`



newly(新近地) + wed(结婚) → 新婚的人

# 例句

My newlywed wife and I are deeply in love.
我和我的新婚妻子彼此深爱着对方。

Lavalais raised his glass to propose a toast to the newlyweds.
拉瓦莱斯举起了他的酒杯，向这对新婚夫妇敬酒。
