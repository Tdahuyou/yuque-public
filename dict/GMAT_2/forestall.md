# 音标

- 英 `/fɔː'stɔːl/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

v. 先发制人，预先阻止；垄断，屯积；领先；占先一步
`to prevent something from happening or prevent someone from doing something by doing something first`



fore(预先) + stall(停止) → 预先停止 → 预防

# 同义

vt. 垄断；预先阻止；占先一步
`corner` `monopolize`

# 例句

a measure intended to forestall further attacks
意在预先阻止进一步攻击的措施
