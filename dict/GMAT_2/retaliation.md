# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ,tælɪ'eɪʃn/`
- 美 `/ri,tæli'eiʃən/`

# 词义

n. 报复；反击；回敬
`action against someone who has done something bad to you`



re(反) + tali(邪恶) + ation → 把邪恶还回去 → 报复

# 同义

n. 报复；反击；回敬
`revenge` `reprisal`

# 同根

- adj. `retaliatory` 报复的
- vi. `retaliate` 报复；回敬
- vt. `retaliate` 报复

# 短语

- `massive retaliation` 大规模报复

# 例句

There were no injuries or damage, but the attack raised the prospect of further Israeli retaliation.
这次袭击没有造成伤亡或者破坏，但是却使以色列有可能进一步报复。

The residents and witnesses all spoke on condition of anonymity for fear of retaliation.
传播短信的居民和目击者出于害怕报复的考虑均采用匿名。
