# 音标

- 英 `/'nəʊmæd/`
- 美 `/'nomæd/`

# 词义

n. 游牧民；流浪者
`a member of a tribe that travels from place to place instead of living in one place all the time, usually in order to find grass for their animals`

adj. 游牧的；流浪的


# 同义

n. 游牧民；流浪者
`Vagabonds` `Nads`

adj. 游牧的；流浪的
`ragamuffinly` `migratory`

# 同根

- adj. `nomadic` 游牧的；流浪的；游动的

# 例句

...a country of nomads who raise cattle and camels.
...一个饲养牛和骆驼的游牧国家。
