# 音标

- 英 `/'rɪdɪkjuːl/`
- 美 `/'rɪdɪ'kjʊl/`

# 词义

v. 嘲笑；嘲弄；愚弄
`to laugh at a person, idea etc and say that they are stupid`

n. 嘲笑；笑柄；愚弄
`unkind laughter or remarks that are intended to make someone or something seem stupid`



rid(笑) + icule → 嘲笑

# 同义

vt. 嘲笑；嘲弄；愚弄
`guy` `deride`

n. 嘲笑；笑柄；愚弄
`joke` `derision`

# 例句

At the time, his ideas were ridiculed.
当时他的观点被人讥笑。
