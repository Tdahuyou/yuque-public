# 音标

- 英 `/diːhaɪ'dreɪt; diː'haɪdreɪt/`
- 美 `/di'haɪdret/`

# 词义

v. 使…脱水；使极其口渴；使丧失力量和兴趣等
`to remove the liquid from a substance such as food or a chemical`



de(去掉) + hydr(水) + ated → 去水的 → 脱水

# 同根

- adj. `dehydrated` 脱水的
- n. `dehydration` 脱水

# 例句

The substance is dehydrated and stored as powder.
该物质经脱水处理后以粉末形式储存。
