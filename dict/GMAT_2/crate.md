# 音标

- 英 `/kreɪt/`
- 美 `/kret/`

# 词义

n. 板条箱；篓
`a large box made of wood or plastic that is used for carrying fruit, bottles etc`

v. 将某物装入大木箱或板条箱中
`to pack things into a crate`



编篮子(crate)也是一种创造(create)

# 短语

- `wooden crate` 木箱

# 例句

Equipment and office supplies were crated and shipped.
设备及办公用品被装箱运走了。
