# 音标

- 英 `/daɪ'vest; dɪ-/`
- 美 `/daɪ'vɛst/`

# 词义

v. 剥夺；使脱去，迫使放弃
`If you divest yourself of something that you own or are responsible for, you get rid of it or stop being responsible for it`



di(去掉) + vest(穿衣) → 脱去衣服， 摆脱束缚 → 剥夺

# 同义

vt. 剥夺；使脱去，迫使放弃
`shear` `strip of`

# 同根

- n. `divestiture` 脱衣；剥夺

# 例句

The company divested itself of its oil interests.
这家公司从石油生意中抽身。
