# 音标

- 英 `/,kaʊntə'veɪl/`
- 美 `/'kaʊntɚ,vel/`

# 词义

v. 抵销；补偿；对抗
`to act or act against with equal power or force `

# 同义

vt. 抵销；补偿；对抗
`pay` `make good`

vi. 抵销；对抗
`compete` `war` `balance`

# 例句

In civil litigation, the defendant can offer to countervail creditor's rights in order to ban down creditor's litigant appeal.
民事诉讼中被告可以减少或消灭对方诉讼请求为目的提出债权抵销的主张。
