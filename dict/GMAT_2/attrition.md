# 音标

- 英 `/ə'trɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/ə'trɪʃən/`

# 词义

n. 摩擦；磨损；消耗
`the process of gradually destroying your enemy or making them weak by attacking them continuously`



at + trit(磨擦) + ion → 磨损

# 同义

n. 摩擦；[机]磨损；消耗
`friction` `consumption`

# 同根

- adj. `attrited` 磨损的
- v. `attrited` 磨损；消耗（attrite的过去式和过去分词）

# 短语

- `attrition rate` 损耗率；退学率

# 例句

a war of attrition
消耗战
