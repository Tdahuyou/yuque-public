# 音标

- 英 `/kə'rɒtɪnɒɪd/`
- 美 `/kə'rɑtn,ɔɪd/`

# 词义

n. 类胡萝卜素
`any of a group of red or yellow pigments, including carotenes, found in plants and certain animal tissues `
