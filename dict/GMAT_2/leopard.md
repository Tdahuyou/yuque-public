# 音标

- 英 `/'lepəd/`
- 美 `/'lɛpɚd/`

# 词义

n. 豹；美洲豹
`a large animal of the cat family, with yellow fur and black spots, which lives in Africa and South Asia`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]豹；美洲豹
`panther, puma` `pard`

# 短语

- `snow leopard` 雪豹
- `clouded leopard` 云豹
- `leopard cat` 豹猫

# 例句

Her talent was not in gathering information but in analyzing and assessing—a meticulous organizer who plotted and planned with the cunning of a leopard.
她的才华不在信息收集，而在于分析和评估，一个细微谨慎的组织者，策划着，盘算着有如美洲豹般的狡猾。
