# 音标

- 英 `/wɔʃt/`
- 美 `/wɔʃt/`

# 词义

`If you wash something, you clean it using water and usually a substance such as soap or detergent`

# 短语

- `washed out` 褪色的，洗旧了的；筋疲力尽的
- `washed up` 洗净的；失败的
- `washed coal` 洗煤

# 例句

We did odd jobs like farm work and washing dishes.
我们做了一些诸如干农活儿和洗盘子之类的零活儿。

It took a long time to wash the mud out of his hair.
花了很长时间才把泥从他头发上洗掉。
