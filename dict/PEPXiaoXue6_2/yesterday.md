# 音标

- 英 `/ˈjestədɪ;-deɪ/`
- 美 `/ˈjɛstɚdɪ;-de/`

# 词义

`on or during the day before today`

# 同义

n. 昨天；往昔
`ayer`

# 同根

- n. `yesterdayness` 近来

# 短语

- `yesterday morning` 昨天早上
- `yesterday evening` 昨天晚上，前晚
- `day before yesterday` 前天
- `since yesterday` 从昨天
- `born yesterday` 乳臭未干

# 例句

What did you do yesterday?
昨天你做了什么？
