# 音标

- 英 `/wɒtʃ/`
- 美 `/wɑtʃ/`

# 词义

`If you watch someone or something, you look at them, usually for a period of time, and pay attention to what is happening`

# 短语

- `watch oneself` [美国英语]小心谨慎
- `watch tv` 看电视
- `watch out` 小心；提防
- `watch for` v. 等待；当心；守候
- `watch television` 看电视
- `watch out for` 注意寻找；戒备；小心提防
- `watch over` 看守；监视；照管
- `watch movies` 看电影
- `keep watch` 看守；值班；放哨；提防
- `close watch` 紧盯

# 例句

The man was standing in his doorway watching him.
那个男人正站在自家门道里看着他。

He seems to enjoy watching me work.
他似乎喜欢看我干活。

Here, now watch how I cut this, OK?
嘿，现在看我怎样切，好吗？

He watched as the Yankees rallied for a second comeback victory.
他看着北方佬为了又一次东山再起而集结。
