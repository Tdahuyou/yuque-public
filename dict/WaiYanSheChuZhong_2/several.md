# 音标

- 英 `/ˈsevrəl/`
- 美 `/ˈsɛvrəl/`

# 词义

adj. 几个；一些


# 同义

adj. 几个的；各自的
`respective` `each`

# 同根

- adv. `severally` 各自地；分别地；分开地

# 短语

- `in several` adv. 分别地；各个地
- `several times` 几次
- `several people` 几个人
- `several liability` 连带责任；个别责任
- `joint and several liability` 连带责任

# 例句

The teacher lunched several students at his home.
老师请几个学生在他家吃午饭。

We export tractors from the East Coast to several African countries.
我们从东海岸向好几个非洲国家出口拖拉机。

The integrated country will rend into several independent regions sooner or later.
那个完整的国家早晚会分裂成几个独立的地区。
