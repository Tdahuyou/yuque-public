# 音标

- 英 `/'tenɪs/`
- 美 `/ˈtɛnɪs/`

# 词义

n. 网球
`a game for two people or two pairs of people who use  rackets  to hit a small soft ball backwards and forwards over a net`

# 同根

- n. `tennist` 网球运动员

# 短语

- `table tennis` 乒乓球；桌球
- `play tennis` v. 打网球
- `tennis player` n. 网球选手
- `tennis court` 网球场
- `tennis ball` 网球
- `tennis racket` 网球拍；网坛骗局
- `tennis shoes` 网球鞋
- `lawn tennis` 草地网球
- `tennis elbow` 肘部发炎；网球运动员常见的肘伤
- `table tennis ball` n. 乒乓球

# 例句

She excels all of us at tennis.
她的网球比我们打得都好。

They often tennis in the afternoon.
他们常在下午打网球。

We rank him among the best tennis players.
我们把他列入最优秀网球运动员之列。
