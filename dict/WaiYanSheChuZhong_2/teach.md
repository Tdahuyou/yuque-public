# 音标

- 英 `/tiːtʃ/`
- 美 `/titʃ/`

# 词义

v. (taught)教；讲授
`to give lessons in a school, college, or university, or to help someone learn about something by giving them information`

# 同义

vt. 教；教授；教导
`wise` `enlighten`

# 同根

- adj. `teachable` 可教的；驯良的
- n. `teacher` 教师；导师
- n. `teaching` 教学；教义
- v. `teaching` 教学；教导（teach的ing形式）

# 短语

- `teach oneself` 自学
- `teach english` 教英语
- `teach school` 教书；当教师

# 例句

She taught me fractions and counting.
他教我分数和计算。
