# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃəʊkeɪs/`
- 美 `/'ʃokes/`

# 词义

n. 陈列橱，[家具]陈列柜；显示优点的东西
`an event or situation that is designed to show the good qualities of a person, organization, product etc`

vt. 使展现；在玻璃橱窗陈列
`If something is showcased, it is displayed or presented to its best advantage`

# 例句

Restored films are being showcased this month at a festival in Paris.
本月的巴黎电影节将展映一些修复版的电影。
