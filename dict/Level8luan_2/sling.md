# 音标

- 英 `/slɪŋ/`
- 美 `/slɪŋ/`

# 词义

n. [机]吊索；投石器；抛掷
`a set of ropes or strong pieces of cloth that are used to lift and carry heavy objects`

v. 用投石器投掷；吊起
`If you sling something over your shoulder or over something such as a chair, you hang it there loosely`

# 同义

n. [机]吊索；投石器；抛掷
`riser cable` `headwear`

vt. 用投石器投掷；吊起
`trice`

# 同根

- n. `slinger` 投石者；吊物工人；摔砂造模机；吊环
- n. `slinging` 吊起；抛砂

# 短语

- `sling someone out` 赶走某人，驱逐某人
- `rope sling` 吊索；吊货索环；索套

# 例句

They used slings of rope to lower us from one set of arms to another.
他们用几条绳索把我们从一组吊臂降到另外一组吊臂。

She slung her coat over her desk chair.
她把外衣搭在了桌椅背上。

He had a small green backpack slung over one shoulder.
他把一只绿色小背包搭在一边肩上。
