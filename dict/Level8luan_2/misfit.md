# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪsfɪt/`
- 美 `/'mɪsfɪt/`

# 词义

n. 不适合；不适应环境的人
`someone who does not seem to belong in a particular group of people, and who is not accepted by that group, because they are very different from the other group members`

vt. 对…不适合


# 同义

n. 不适合；不适应环境的人
`inconformity` `inappropriateness`

# 例句

I was very conscious of being a misfit at school.
我深深感到自己在学校里格格不入。

a social misfit
与社会格格不入的人
