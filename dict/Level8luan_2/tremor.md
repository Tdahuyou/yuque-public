# 音标

- 英 `/'tremə/`
- 美 `/'trɛmɚ/`

# 词义

n. [医]震颤；颤动
`If an event causes a tremor in a group or organization, it threatens to make the group or organization less strong or stable`

# 同义

n. [医]震颤；[物]颤动
`fremitus` `zitterbewegung`

# 同根

- adj. `tremulous` 胆小的；战栗的；震颤的
- adv. `tremulously` 发抖地；震颤地；歪斜地；过分敏感地
- n. `trembler` 震颤的东西；发抖的人；摇尾鸟

# 例句

News of 160 lay-offs had sent tremors through the community.
裁员160人的消息在社区中引起了波动。
