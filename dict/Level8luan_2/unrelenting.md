# 音标

- 英 `/ʌnrɪ'lentɪŋ/`
- 美 `/,ʌnrɪ'lɛntɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 无情的；不屈不挠的；不松懈的
`an unpleasant situation that is unrelenting continues for a long time without stopping`

# 同义

adj. 无情的；不屈不挠的；不松懈的
`tough` `relentless` `inexorable` `ruthless`

# 同根

- adv. `unrelentingly` 无情地

# 例句

the unrelenting pressures of the job
持续的工作压力
