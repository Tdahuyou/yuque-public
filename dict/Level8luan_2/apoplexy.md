# 音标

- 英 `/'æpəpleksɪ/`
- 美 `/'æpəplɛksi/`

# 词义

n. [医]中风
`an illness in your brain which causes you to suddenly lose your ability to move or think`

# 同义

n. [医]中风
`stroke`

# 同根

- adj. `apoplectic` 中风的；易怒的
- n. `apoplectic` 中风患者

# 短语

- `cerebral apoplexy` 脑溢血
