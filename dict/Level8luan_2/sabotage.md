# 音标

- 英 `/'sæbətɑːʒ/`
- 美 `/,sæbə'tɑʒ/`

# 词义

v. 妨害；对…采取破坏行动
`to secretly damage or destroy equipment, vehicles etc that belong to an enemy or opponent, so that they cannot be used`

n. 破坏；破坏活动；怠工
`deliberate damage that is done to equipment, vehicles etc in order to prevent an enemy or opponent from using them`

# 同义

vt. 妨害；对…采取破坏行动
`encumber` `incumber`

n. 破坏；破坏活动；怠工
`destruction` `breaking` `blasting` `disruption` `demolition`

# 例句

Every single plane had been sabotaged.
每架飞机都遭到了破坏。
