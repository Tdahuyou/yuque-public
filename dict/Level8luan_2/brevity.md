# 音标

- 英 `/'brevɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/'brɛvəti/`

# 词义

n. 简洁，简短；短暂，短促
`the quality of expressing something in very few words`

# 同义

n. 简洁，简短；短暂，短促
`compactness` `sententiousness`

# 同根

- n. `breve` （罗马教皇的）谕令；二全音符，短弱音符

# 例句

Letters published in the newspaper are edited for brevity and clarity.
报上刊登的来信都要编辑得简洁明了。
