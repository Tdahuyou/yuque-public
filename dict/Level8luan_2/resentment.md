# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'zentm(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'zɛntmənt/`

# 词义

n. 愤恨，怨恨
`a feeling of anger because something has happened that you think is unfair`

# 同义

n. 愤恨，怨恨
`spite` `hatred`

# 同根

- adj. `resentful` 充满忿恨的；厌恶的
- adv. `resentfully` 充满愤恨地
- vt. `resent` 怨恨；愤恨；厌恶

# 例句

She expressed resentment at being interviewed by a social worker.
她对于被一个社会工作者面试表示了怨恨。
