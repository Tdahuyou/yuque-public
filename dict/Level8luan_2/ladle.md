# 音标

- 英 `/'leɪd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'ledl/`

# 词义

v. 不分对象予以赠送；以杓舀取
`to put soup or other liquid food onto plates or into bowls, especially using a ladle`

n. 钢水包；杓子；长柄杓
`a large deep spoon with a long handle, used for lifting liquid food, especially soup, out of a container`

# 同义

n. 钢水包；杓子；长柄杓
`piggin`

# 短语

- `ladle furnace` 钢包炉
- `hot metal ladle` 铁水包，铁水罐
- `ladle out` 舀出；提供
- `ladle cover` 包罩；包盖

# 例句

Barry held the bowls while Liz ladled soup into them.
巴里端着碗，由利兹向碗里盛汤。
