# 音标

- 英 `/'klæmbə/`
- 美 `/'klæmbɚ/`

# 词义

v. 攀登，爬上
`to climb or move slowly somewhere, using your hands and feet because it is difficult or steep`

n. 攀登，爬上


# 同义

vi. 攀登，爬上
`scale` `climb`

vt. 爬，攀登
`climb` `scale`

n. 攀登，爬上
`climb` `escalade`

# 例句

We all clambered aboard and the boat pulled out.
我们全都爬上船后，船就开了。
