# 音标

- 英 `/njuː'bɔːn; 'njuːbɔːn/`
- 美 `/'nubɔrn/`

# 词义

adj. 新生的；再生的
`A newborn baby or animal is one that has just been born`

n. 婴儿
`The newborn are babies or animals who are newborn`

# 同义

adj. [儿科]新生的；再生的
`reproductive` `neonatal`

n. 婴儿
`baby` `infant` `neonate` `babe`

# 例句

The electronic sensor has been adapted to fit on a newborn baby.
电子传感器已被改装以适应新生儿。

Mild jaundice in the newborn is common and often clears without treatment.
新生儿有轻微的黄疸较为常见，通常会不治自愈。
