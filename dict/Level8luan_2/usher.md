# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌʃə/`
- 美 `/'ʌʃɚ/`

# 词义

n. 引座员，带位员；接待员；门房
`someone who shows people to their seats at a theatre, cinema, wedding etc`

v. 引导，招待；迎接；开辟
`to help someone to get from one place to another, especially by showing them the way`

# 同义

n. 引座员，带位员；接待员；门房
`porter` `receptionist`

vt. 引导，招待；迎接；开辟
`aim` `serve` `invite` `attend` `guest`

vi. 作招待员；当引座员
`ush`

# 同根

- n. `usherette` 女服务员，女引座员

# 短语

- `usher in` 领进，引进

# 例句

He did part-time work as an usher in a theatre.
他曾在一家剧院做兼职引座员。
