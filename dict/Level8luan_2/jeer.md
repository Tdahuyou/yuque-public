# 音标

- 英 `/dʒɪə/`
- 美 `/dʒɪr/`

# 词义

n. 嘲笑；戏弄；揶揄的言语


v. 嘲笑；戏弄
`to laugh at someone or shout unkind things at them in a way that shows you do not respect them`

# 同义

n. 嘲笑；戏弄；揶揄的言语
`derision` `razz`

vi. 嘲笑；戏弄
`laugh at` `make a mockery of`

vt. 嘲笑；戏弄
`laugh at` `deride`

# 同根

- adj. `jeering` 嘲弄的
- adv. `jeeringly` 嘲弄地
- n. `jeerer` 嘲笑者；戏弄者
- v. `jeering` 嘲弄（jeer的ing形式）

# 短语

- `jeer at` 嘲笑；嘲弄；戏弄

# 例句

‘You know I’m right!’ she jeered.
“你知道我是对的！”她嘲笑道。

The President was booed and jeered by a crowd of protesters.
总统被一群抗议者报以嘘声和嘲笑。
