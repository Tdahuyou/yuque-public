# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'mɒnstrətɪv/`
- 美 `/dɪ'mɑnstrətɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 说明的；证明的；公开表露感情的
`willing to show that you care about someone`

n. 指示词
`In grammar, the words "this," "that," "these," and "those" are sometimes called demonstratives`

# 同义

adj. 说明的；证明的；公开表露感情的
`illustrative` `expository`

n. 指示词
`deictics`

# 同根

- adv. `demonstratively` 论证地
- n. `demonstration` 示范；证明；示威游行
- n. `demonstrator` 示威者；论证者；指示者；证明者
- vi. `demonstrate` 示威
- vt. `demonstrate` 证明；展示；论证

# 例句

My mother wasn’t demonstrative; she never hugged me.
母亲感情内敛，从未拥抱过我。
