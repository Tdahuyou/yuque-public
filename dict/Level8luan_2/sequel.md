# 音标

- 英 `/'siːkw(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'sikwəl/`

# 词义

n. 续集；结局；继续；后果
`a book, film, play etc that continues the story of an earlier one, usually written or made by the same person`

# 同义

n. 续集；结局；继续；后果
`conclusion` `outcome` `ending` `continuation`

# 同根

- adv. `sequentially` 从而；继续地；循序地

# 短语

- `in the sequel` 结果；后来

# 例句

‘Star Wars’ and its sequels
《星球大战》及其续集
