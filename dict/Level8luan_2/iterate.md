# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪtəreɪt/`
- 美 `/'ɪtərət/`

# 词义

v. 迭代；重复；反复说；重做
`if a computer iterates, it goes through a set of instructions before going through them for a second time`

# 同义

vt. 迭代；重复；反复说；重做
`repeat` `echo`

# 同根

- adj. `iterative` [数] 迭代的；重复的，反复的
- n. `iteration` [数] 迭代；反复；重复
- n. `iterative` 反复体

# 短语

- `iterate through` 循环访问
