# 音标

- 英 `/'fiːtəs/`
- 美 `/'fitəs/`

# 词义

n. 胎儿，胎
`A fetus is an animal or human being in its later stages of development before it is born`

# 同义

n. 胎儿，胎
`foetus` `homunculi`

# 同根

- n. `fetology` 胎儿学

# 例句

Pregnant women who are heavy drinkers risk damaging the unborn fetus.
酗酒的孕妇冒有伤害胎儿的危险。
