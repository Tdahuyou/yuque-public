# 音标

- 英 `/'sɪfɪlɪs/`
- 美 `/'sɪfɪlɪs/`

# 词义

n. [性病]梅毒
`a very serious disease that is passed from one person to another during sexual activity`

# 同义

n. [性病]梅毒
`siff` `lues`

# 同根

- adj. `syphilitic` 梅毒的；感染梅毒的
- n. `syphilitic` 梅毒病人

# 短语

- `secondary syphilis` [医]二期梅毒
