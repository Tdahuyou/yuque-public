# 音标

- 英 `/mɪŋk/`
- 美 `/mɪŋk/`

# 词义

n. 貂；貂皮衣
`a small animal with soft brown fur, or the very valuable fur of this animal which is used to make coats, hats etc`

adj. 豪华的；富裕的


# 同义

n. 貂；[皮革]貂皮衣
`marten` `ermine`

adj. 豪华的；富裕的
`grand` `ritzy`

# 短语

- `mink coat` 貂皮大衣

# 例句

a mink coat
一件貂皮大衣
