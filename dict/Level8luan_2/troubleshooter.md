# 词义

n. 故障检修工；解决纠纷者；解决麻烦问题的能手
`A troubleshooter is a person whose job is to solve major problems or difficulties that occur in a company or government`

# 同根

- adj. `troublesome` 麻烦的；讨厌的；使人苦恼的
- adj. `troublesomeness` 易出故障的
- n. `trouble` 麻烦；烦恼；故障；动乱
- n. `troublemaker` 捣乱者，闹事者；惹麻烦的人
- vi. `trouble` 费心，烦恼
- vt. `trouble` 麻烦；使烦恼；折磨

# 例句

The United Nations dispatched a team of troubleshooters to Somalia today.
联合国今天派遣了一队纠纷人员到索马里。
