# 音标

- 英 `/sɪ'nɒnɪməs/`
- 美 `/sɪ'nɑnɪməs/`

# 词义

adj. 同义的；同义词的；同义突变的
`something that is synonymous with something else is considered to be very closely connected with it`

# 同根

- n. `synonym` 同义词；同义字
- n. `synonymy` 同义，同义词研究
- n. `synonymity` 同义性

# 短语

- `synonymous with` 与...同义

# 例句

"Getting fit" became synonymous not just with healthier living, but with a leaner, meaner body, and the ground was laid for a burgeoning gym industry.
“获得适合”成为同义词的不只是健康的生活，但一个更精简，寡情的身体，与地面是一个新兴的体育产业奠定。
