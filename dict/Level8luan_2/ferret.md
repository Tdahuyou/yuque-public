# 音标

- 英 `/'ferɪt/`
- 美 `/'fɛrɪt/`

# 词义

v. 搜出，查获；驱出；用雪貂猎取
`to hunt rats and rabbits using a ferret`

n. 雪貂；白鼬；侦探
`a small animal with a pointed nose, used to hunt rats and rabbits`

# 同义

vt. 搜出，查获；驱出；用雪貂猎取
`rake out` `seek out`

vi. 搜索，侦破
`seek` `beat for`

n. 雪貂；白鼬；侦探
`dick` `stoat`

# 短语

- `ferret out` 搜出，搜获
