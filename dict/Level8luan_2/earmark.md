# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪəmɑːk/`
- 美 `/'ɪrmɑrk/`

# 词义

v. 在耳朵上做记号；标记


n. 特征；耳上记号
`The earmark of something or someone is their most typical quality or feature`

# 同义

vt. 在耳朵上做记号；标记
`flag` `emblemize`

n. 特征；耳上记号
`characteristic` `specificity`

# 例句

schools earmarked for closure
被指定关闭的学校

Davis's solo work exhibits all the earmarks of his style: it is hesitant, tentative, spare.
戴维斯的独奏曲尽展其风格特征：犹豫、迟疑、俭朴。
