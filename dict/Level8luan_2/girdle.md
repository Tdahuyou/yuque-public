# 音标

- 英 `/'gɜːd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'gɝdl/`

# 词义

n. 腰带；围绕物；妇女紧身褡
`a piece of women’s underwear which fits tightly around her stomach, bottom, and  hips  and makes her look thinner`

v. 围绕；绕…而行；用带子捆扎
`to surround something`

# 同义

n. [古生]腰带；围绕物；妇女紧身褡
`waist belt` `waistband`

vt. 围绕；绕…而行；用带子捆扎
`tape` `enclose`

# 同根

- n. `girth` 周长；围长；肚带
- vi. `girth` 围长为
- vt. `girth` 围绕；包围

# 短语

- `shoulder girdle` 肩带；肩胛带
- `pelvic girdle` 骨盆带（等于pelvic arch）

# 例句

the formal garden that girdled the house
环绕屋子布置井然的花园
