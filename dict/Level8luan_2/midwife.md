# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪdwaɪf/`
- 美 `/'mɪd'waɪf/`

# 词义

n. 助产士；促成因素
`a specially trained nurse whose job is to help women when they are having a baby`

vt. 助胎儿出生；促成


# 同义

vt. 助胎儿出生；促成
`catalyse`

# 同根

- n. `midwifery` 产科学，助产学；助产术；促成

# 例句

You don't have to call the midwife as soon as labour starts.
你不必刚开始分娩就叫助产士。
