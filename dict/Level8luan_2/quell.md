# 音标

- 英 `/kwel/`
- 美 `/kwɛl/`

# 词义

v. 平息；镇压；减轻；消除
`to end a situation in which people are behaving violently or protesting, especially by using force`

n. (Quell)人名；(捷)奎尔；(西)克利


# 同义

vt. 平息；镇压；减轻；消除
`eliminate` `avoid` `moderate` `suppress`

# 例句

‘Jerry?’ she called, trying to quell the panic inside her.
