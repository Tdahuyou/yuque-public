# 音标

- 美 `/'rɔstrəm/`

# 词义

n. 讲坛；演讲者；嘴；喙
`a small  platform  that you stand on when you are making a speech or  conduct ing  musicians`

# 同义

n. 讲坛；演讲者；嘴；[动][植]喙
`mouth` `estrade`
