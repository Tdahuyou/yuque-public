# 音标

- 英 `/skwɪnt/`
- 美 `/skwɪnt/`

# 词义

v. 眯眼看；斜视；窥视；偏移
`to look at something with your eyes partly closed in order to see better`

n. 斜视；斜孔小窗；一瞥
`a condition of your eye muscles that makes each eye look in a slightly different direction`

adj. 斜视的；斜的


# 同义

vi. [眼科][电讯]斜视；患斜视；倾向
`tend` `affect`

n. 看
`look`

adj. [眼科][电讯]斜视的
`strabismic` `swivel-eyed`

# 同根

- adj. `squinty` 有斜视眼的；斜视的

# 短语

- `squint at` 斜眼看

# 例句

Anna squinted in the sudden bright sunlight.
太阳光突然变强，安娜眯起了眼睛。
