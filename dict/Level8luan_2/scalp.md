# 音标

- 英 `/skælp/`
- 美 `/skælp/`

# 词义

n. 头皮；战利品
`the skin on the top of your head`

v. 剥头皮
`to cut the hair and skin off the head of a dead enemy as a sign of victory`

# 同义

n. [解剖]头皮；战利品
`prize` `capture`

# 同根

- n. `scalper` 黄牛（专售戏票等牟利）；剥头皮的人；雕刻刀，圆凿子

# 例句

Massage the shampoo gently into your hair and scalp.
把洗发液轻轻揉进头发和头皮。
