# 音标

- 英 `/grɑːft/`
- 美 `/ɡræft/`

# 词义

v. 移植；嫁接；贪污
`to remove a piece of skin, bone etc from part of someone’s body and put it onto or into a part of their body that has been damaged`

n. 移植；嫁接；渎职
`a piece of healthy skin or bone taken from someone’s body and put in or on another part of their body that has been damaged`

# 同义

vi. [外科][生物]移植；[农学]嫁接；[法]贪污
`to transplant` `transfer of culture`

vt. [外科][生物]移植；[农学]嫁接；[法]贪污
`to transplant` `transfer of culture`

n. [外科][生物]移植；[农学]嫁接；赎职
`transplantation` `implantation`

# 同根

- n. `grafting` 嫁接法；移植法
- v. `grafting` [农学] 嫁接；接合；接受移植（graft的ing形式）

# 短语

- `graft copolymerization` 接枝共聚
- `graft copolymer` [化]接枝共聚物
- `bone graft` n. 移植骨；骨移植物
- `graft polymerization` 接合聚合
- `skin graft` 植皮手术，皮肤移植术；移植的皮肤
- `graft in` 嫁接
- `graft polymer` [化]接枝聚合物
- `graft rejection` 移植排斥
- `graft on` 嫁接（等于graft in）

# 例句

The top layer of skin has to be grafted onto the burns.
表层皮肤必须移植到烧伤处。
