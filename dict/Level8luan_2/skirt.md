# 音标

- 英 `/skɜːt/`
- 美 `/skɝt/`

# 词义

n. 裙子
`a piece of outer clothing worn by women and girls, which hangs down from the waist like the bottom part of a dress`

v. 绕过，回避；位于…边缘
`to avoid talking about an important subject, especially because it is difficult or embarrassing – used to show disapproval`

# 同义

n. 裙子
`petticoat`

vt. 绕过，回避；位于…边缘
`move round` `shun`

vi. 沿边走，绕开；环绕
`steer clear of` `circle round`

# 同根

- n. `skirting` 壁脚板；裙料
- v. `skirting` 避开；绕过…的边缘；沿边缘走（skirt的现在分词）

# 短语

- `piston skirt` 活塞裙
- `mini skirt` n. 迷你裙；超短裙
- `skirt length` 裙长

# 例句

She wore a white blouse and a plain black skirt.
她穿着白衬衣和纯黑的裙子。
