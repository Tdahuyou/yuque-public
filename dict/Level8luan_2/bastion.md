# 音标

- 英 `/'bæstɪən/`
- 美 `/'bæstɪən/`

# 词义

n. 堡垒；棱堡
`something that protects a way of life, principle etc that seems likely to change or end completely`

# 同义

n. 堡垒；棱堡
`tower` `fort`

# 同根

- adj. `bastioned` 具备棱堡的

# 例句

Pearl Harbor was the principal American bastion in the Pacific.
珍珠港曾是美国在太平洋上的重要军事据点。
