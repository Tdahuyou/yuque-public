# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪnset/`
- 美 `/'ɪn,sɛt; ɪn'sɛt/`

# 词义

n. 插图，插页；插入物


v. 嵌入；插入
`if something is inset with decorations or jewels, they are fixed into or on its surface`

# 同义

n. 插图，[印刷]插页；插入物
`inlet` `illustration`

vt. 嵌入；插入
`bed` `tumble in`

# 例句

The venture earned Mr Taylor (inset) millions of dollars.
这项风险投资使泰勒先生(见小图)赚了数百万美元。
