# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'pres/`
- 美 `/rɪ'prɛs/`

# 词义

v. 抑制；镇压（叛乱等）；约束
`if someone represses upsetting feelings, memories etc, they do not allow themselves to express or think about them`

# 同义

vt. 抑制；镇压（叛乱等）；约束
`restrict` `control` `stay`

vi. 抑制；镇压
`choke back` `to suppress`

# 同根

- adj. `repressive` 镇压的；压抑的；抑制的
- adj. `repressed` 被压抑的；被抑制的
- n. `repression` 抑制，[心理] 压抑；镇压
- n. `repressor` 镇压者
- n. `repressing` 重压；补加压力；[机] 再压
- v. `repressed` 抑制；镇压；约束（repress的过去分词）
- v. `repressing` 抑制；镇压；约束（repress的ing形式）

# 例句

He had long ago repressed the painful memories of his childhood.
他长久压抑着儿时的痛苦记忆。
