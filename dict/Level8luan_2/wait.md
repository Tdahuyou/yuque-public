# 音标

- 英 `/weɪt/`
- 美 `/wet/`

# 词义

v. 等候；推迟；延缓
`to stay somewhere or not do something until something else happens, someone arrives etc`

n. 等待；等候
`a period of time in which you wait for something to happen, someone to arrive etc`

# 同义

vt. 等候；推迟；延缓
`await` `phase back`

vi. [计]等待；耽搁；伺候用餐
`delay`

n. [计]等待；等候
`noop`

# 同根

- adj. `waiting` 等待的；服侍的
- n. `waiting` 等待；服侍
- v. `waiting` 等待；伺候（wait的ing形式）；延缓
