# 音标

- 英 `/θɜːməʊ'njuːklɪə/`
- 美 `/,θɝmo'nuklɪɚ/`

# 词义

adj. [核]热核的；高热原子核反应的
`thermonuclear weapons use a  nuclear  reaction, involving the splitting of atoms, to produce very high temperatures and a very powerful explosion`

# 短语

- `thermonuclear fusion` 热核融合；热核熔合；热核反应；热核聚变
- `thermonuclear reaction` 热核反应

# 例句

a thermonuclear device
热核装置
