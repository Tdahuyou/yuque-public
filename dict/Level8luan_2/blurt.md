# 音标

- 英 `/blɜːt/`
- 美 `/blɝt/`

# 词义

v. 未加思索地冲口说出；突然说出
`If someone blurts something, they say it suddenly, after trying hard to keep quiet or to keep it secret`

# 同义

vt. 未加思索地冲口说出；突然说出
`ejaculate`

# 短语

- `blurt out` 开始说话，脱口而出

# 例句

"I was looking for Sally," he blurted, and his eyes filled with tears.
他眼含热泪，突然脱口说道：“我在找莎丽。”
