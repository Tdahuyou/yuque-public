# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'teɪnə/`
- 美 `/rɪ'tenɚ/`

# 词义

n. 保持器；家臣；保持者；[机]护圈；预付费用
`a reduced amount of rent that you pay for a room, flat etc when you are not there, so that it will still be available when you return`

# 同义

n. [机]保持器；家臣；保持者；护圈；预付费用
`maintainer` `protective ring`

# 同根

- vt. `retain` 保持；雇；记住

# 短语

- `bearing retainer` 轴承承托

# 例句

I'll need a five-hundred-dollar retainer.
我将需要一笔500美元的预付聘金。
