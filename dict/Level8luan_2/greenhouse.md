# 音标

- 英 `/'griːnhaʊs/`
- 美 `/'ɡrinhaʊs/`

# 词义

n. 温室
`a glass building used for growing plants that need warmth, light, and protection`

# 同义

n. [园艺]温室
`heated housing` `glasshouse`

# 同根

- n. `greenery` 绿色植物；温室

# 短语

- `greenhouse gas` n. 二氧化碳、甲烷等导致温室效应的气体
- `greenhouse effect` [化]温室效应

# 例句

But these two countries are responsible for almost half of all greenhouse gas emissions.
但是这两个国家的温室气体排放量几乎占了全世界的一半。

A fiery steed is not trained in a courtyard, nor does a pine grow sturdy in a greenhouse.
院子里练不出千里马， 温室里长不出万年松。

If this is true, it means that the UK will not cut its greenhouse gases by 80% by 2050, as the government promised. It means it will cut them by 40%.
如果这是真的，这意味着英国将不会象政府承诺的那样，到2050年削减其80%的温室气体排放量，这意味着英国将只削减40%。
