# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'dʒʌstɪs/`
- 美 `/ɪn'dʒʌstɪs/`

# 词义

n. 不公正；不讲道义
`a situation in which people are treated very unfairly and not given their rights`

# 同义

n. [法]不公正；不讲道义
`wrong` `iniquity`

# 例句

The movie deals with injustices suffered by Native Americans.
这部电影讲述印第安人受到的不公正待遇。

He had developed a deep sense of social injustice.
他深深感到社会的不公。
