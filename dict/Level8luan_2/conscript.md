# 音标

- 英 `/kən'skrɪpt/`
- 美 `/kən'skrɪpt/`

# 词义

n. 应征士兵


v. 征召
`to make someone join the army, navy etc`

adj. 被征召的


# 同义

vt. 征召
`call sb up`

# 同根

- n. `conscription` 征兵；征兵制度；征用

# 例句

How can private companies compete fairly when their military rivals enjoy access to cheap land, credit and conscript labour—and have guns?
军方享有廉价的土地、信贷和士兵做劳工，而且手中有枪，私营公司怎么和他们公平竞争？
