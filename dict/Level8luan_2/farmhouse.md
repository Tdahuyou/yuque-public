# 音标

- 英 `/'fɑːmhaʊs/`
- 美 `/'fɑrm'haʊs/`

# 词义

n. 农家，[农工]农舍
`the main house on a farm, where the farmer lives`

# 例句

The wolf littered in a deserted farmhouse.
狼在一个没人居住的农家房子产仔。

After breaking out of jail, he hid out in a deserted farmhouse.
他越狱后躲藏在一个无人居住的农舍里。

After breaking out of the jail， he hid out in a deserted farmhouse.
他越狱后，暗藏在一个无人居住的农舍里。
