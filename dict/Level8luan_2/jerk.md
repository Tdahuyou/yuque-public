# 音标

- 英 `/dʒɜːk/`
- 美 `/dʒɝk/`

# 词义

n. 肌肉抽搐；性情古怪的人；蠢人；急拉
`If you call someone a jerk, you are insulting them because you think they are stupid or you do not like them`

v. 痉挛；急拉；颠簸地行进
`to pull something suddenly and roughly`

# 同义
