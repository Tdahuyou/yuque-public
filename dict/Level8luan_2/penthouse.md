# 音标

- 英 `/'penthaʊs/`
- 美 `/'pɛnthaʊs/`

# 词义

n. 阁楼；顶层公寓，屋顶房间
`a very expensive and comfortable apartment or set of rooms on the top floor of a building`

# 同义

n. 阁楼；顶层公寓，[建]屋顶房间
`mezzanine floor` `cockloft`

# 短语

- `penthouse suite` 顶楼套房

# 例句

a £7 million London penthouse
一套价值700万英镑的伦敦顶层高级公寓
