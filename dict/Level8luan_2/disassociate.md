# 音标

- 英 `/dɪsə'səʊʃɪeɪt; -sɪ-/`
- 美 `/,disə'səuʃieit/`

# 词义

v. 使分离（过去式disassociated，过去分词disassociated，现在分词disassociating，第三人称单数disassociates，名词disassociation）
`If you disassociate one group or thing from another, you separate them`

# 例句

...an attempt by the president to disassociate the military from politics.
...总统对分离军政的一次尝试。
