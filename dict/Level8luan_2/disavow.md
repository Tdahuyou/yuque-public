# 音标

- 英 `/dɪsə'vaʊ/`
- 美 `/,dɪsə'vaʊ/`

# 词义

v. 否认，否定；抵赖；拒绝对…的责任
`to say that you are not responsible for something, that you do not know about it, or that you are not involved with it`

# 同义

vt. 否认，否定；抵赖；拒绝对…的责任
`negative` `deny`

# 同根

- n. `disavowal` 否定，否认；拒绝；不承认

# 例句

Dr. Samuels immediately disavowed the newspaper story.
塞缪尔斯医生马上否认了报纸上的这条新闻。
