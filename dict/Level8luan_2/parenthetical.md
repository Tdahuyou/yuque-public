# 音标

- 英 `/,pær(ə)n'θetɪk(ə)l/`
- 美 `/,pærən'θɛtɪkl/`

# 词义

adj. 插句的；附加说明的；放在括号里的
`said or written while you are talking or writing about something else in order to explain something or add information`

# 同根

- adv. `parenthetically` 附带说明地；作为插入成分地；顺便说一句
- n. `parenthesis` 插入语；圆括号；附带

# 例句

parenthetical references to his childhood
关于他童年时期的补充说明
