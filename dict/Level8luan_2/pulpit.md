# 音标

- 英 `/'pʊlpɪt/`
- 美 `/'pʊlpɪt/`

# 词义

n. 讲道坛；高架操纵台；神职人员
`a raised structure inside a church that a priest or minister stands on when they speak to the people`

# 同义

n. 讲道坛；高架操纵台；神职人员
`clergy` `priestess`

# 例句

The time came for the sermon and he ascended the pulpit steps.
布道的时间到了，他迈上了讲坛的台阶。
