# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'swɜːvɪŋ/`
- 美 `/ʌn'swɝvɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 坚定的；始终不渝的；不歪的
`an unswerving belief or attitude is one that is very strong and never changes`

# 同义

adj. 坚定的；始终不渝的；不歪的
`committed` `firm` `confirmed` `stable` `decisive`

# 例句

...her unswerving belief in her father's innocence.
...她坚定地相信她父亲是清白的。
