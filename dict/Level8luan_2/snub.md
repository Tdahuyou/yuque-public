# 音标

- 英 `/snʌb/`
- 美 `/snʌb/`

# 词义

v. 冷落；严厉斥责；掐灭
`to treat someone rudely, especially by ignoring them when you meet`

n. 冷落；斥责；突然的停止
`an act of snubbing someone`

adj. 制动用的；短扁上翘的
