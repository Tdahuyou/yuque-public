# 音标

- 英 `/pɪ'ænəʊ/`
- 美 `/pɪ'æno/`

# 词义

n. 钢琴
`a large musical instrument that has a long row of black and white  key s  . You play the piano by sitting in front of it and pressing the keys.`

# 同根

- adj. `pianistic` 钢琴的；钢琴家的；擅长弹奏钢琴的
- n. `pianism` 钢琴演技；钢琴演奏技巧

# 短语

- `play the piano` 弹钢琴
- `piano music` 钢琴曲
- `play piano` 弹钢琴
- `piano concerto` 钢琴协奏曲
- `piano lessons` 钢琴课程，钢琴课
- `grand piano` n. 大钢琴；三角钢琴
- `piano keyboard` 钢琴键盘；琴键
- `electronic piano` 电子钢琴

# 例句

Jean accompanied her on the piano .
琼为她钢琴伴奏。
