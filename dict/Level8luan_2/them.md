# 音标

- 英 `/ðem; ðəm/`
- 美 `/ðɛm; ðəm/`

# 词义

pron. 他们；它们；她们
`used to refer to two or more people or things that have already been mentioned or are already known about`

n. (Them)人名；(老)探
`used when talking about someone who may be male or female, to avoid saying ‘him or her’`

# 短语

- `one of them` 他们中的一个
- `all of them` 他们全部；他们大家

# 例句

Has anyone seen my keys? I can’t find them anywhere.
有人见到我的钥匙吗？我哪儿都找不到。

The police were very helpful when I spoke to them.
我和警察说了，他们很帮忙。

I lent him several books, but he hasn’t read any of them.
我借给他几本书，但他一本还没看过。
