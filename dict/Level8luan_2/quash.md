# 音标

- 英 `/kwɒʃ/`
- 美 `/kwɑʃ/`

# 词义

v. 撤销；镇压；宣布无效；捣碎
`to officially say that a legal judgment or decision is no longer acceptable or correct`

# 同义

vt. 撤销；镇压；宣布无效；捣碎
`suppress` `rat on`

# 例句

The High Court later quashed his conviction for murder.
高等法院后来撤销了他犯有谋杀罪的判决。

The decision was quashed by the House of Lords.
这个决议被上议院推翻了。
