# 音标

- 英 `/plaɪt/`
- 美 `/plaɪt/`

# 词义

n. 困境；境况；誓约
`a very bad situation that someone is in`

v. 保证；约定
`to give or pledge (one's word) `

# 同义

n. 困境；境况；誓约
`difficulty` `corner` `dilemma` `fix` `predicament`

vt. 保证；约定
`undertake` `assure`

# 例句

the country’s economic plight
该国的经济困境

he plighted his word to attempt it
