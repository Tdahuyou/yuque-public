# 音标

- 英 `/'hedruːm; -rʊm/`
- 美 `/'hɛdrum/`

# 词义

n. 净空；净空高度；头上空间
`the amount of space above your head, especially when you are in a car`

# 同义

n. 净空；净空高度；头上空间
`clearance height` `overhead clearance`

# 例句

The forecabin, with 6ft headroom, also has plenty of room to stand and get dressed.
有6英尺净空的前舱也有足够大的空间供人站立和穿衣。
