# 音标

- 英 `/'fɔːrɑːm/`
- 美 `/'fɔ:rɑ:m/`

# 词义

n. 前臂
`the lower part of the arm, between the hand and the elbow`

v. 预先武装；准备
`to prepare or arm (someone, esp oneself) in advance `

# 同义

n. [解剖]前臂
`antebrachium` `cubitus`

vt. 预先武装；准备
`provide` `fix`

# 例句

...the tattoo on his forearm.
…他前臂上的刺青。
