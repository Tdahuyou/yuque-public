# 音标

- 英 `/tʃaɪm/`
- 美 `/tʃaɪm/`

# 词义

v. 鸣响；和谐
`if a bell or clock chimes, it makes a ringing sound, especially to tell you what time it is`

n. 钟声；一套发谐音的钟；和谐
`a ringing sound made by a bell or clock`

# 同义

vi. 鸣响；和谐
`cotton`

n. 钟声；一套发谐音的钟；和谐
`accordance` `ring` `bell` `concert`

# 短语

- `chime in` 插话
- `chime in with` 与...协调

# 例句

The clock in the hall chimed six.
大厅里的钟敲了六下。
