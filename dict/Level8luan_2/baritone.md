# 音标

- 英 `/'bærɪtəʊn/`
- 美 `/'bærə'ton/`

# 词义

n. 男中音；男中音歌手
`a male singing voice that is lower than a  tenor  but higher than a  bass  , or a man with a voice like this`

adj. 男中音的
`a baritone voice or instrument is lower than a  tenor  but higher than a  bass `

# 同义
