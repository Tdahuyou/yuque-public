# 音标

- 英 `/'ləʊkəst/`
- 美 `/'lokəst/`

# 词义

n. [植保]蝗虫，[昆]蚱蜢
`an insect that lives mainly in Asia and Africa and flies in a very large group, eating and destroying crops`

# 同义

n. [植保]蝗虫，[昆]蚱蜢
`grasshopper`

# 短语

- `black locust` 刺槐；洋槐
- `locust bean` 槐豆
- `locust tree` 刺槐，槐树
- `locust bean gum` 豆胶，槐树豆胶
- `honey locust` 皂荚树（北美产的）

# 例句

a swarm of locusts
一大群蝗虫
