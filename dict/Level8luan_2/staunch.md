# 音标

- 英 `/stɔːn(t)ʃ/`
- 美 `/stɔntʃ/`

# 词义

adj. 坚定的；忠诚的；坚固的
`giving strong loyal support to another person, organization, belief etc`

v. 止住；止血
`to stop the flow of liquid, especially of blood from a wound`

n. (Staunch)人名；(英)斯汤奇


# 同义

adj. 坚定的；忠诚的；坚固的
`committed` `loving` `firm` `confirmed` `devoted`

vt. 止住；止血
`rein in` `rein up`

# 同根

- adv. `staunchly` 坚定地；忠实地

# 例句

a staunch conservative
忠实的保守党党员
