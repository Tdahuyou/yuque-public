# 词义

n. 束缚；手铐
`an iron ring on a chain that is put around the wrist or  ankle  of a prisoner`

v. 束缚；给…上手铐
`If a prisoner is manacled, their wrists or legs are put in manacles in order to prevent them from moving or escaping`

# 同义

n. 束缚；手铐

vt. 束缚；给…上手铐
`chain` `bound`
