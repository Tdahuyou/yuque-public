# 音标

- 英 `/'æʃtreɪ/`
- 美 `/'æʃtre/`

# 词义

n. 烟灰缸
`a small dish where you put used cigarettes`

# 同义

n. 烟灰缸
`tobacco jar`

# 例句

I ground my cigarette into the ashtray.
我把香烟掐灭后扔入烟灰缸。

Drop your cigarette ashes into the ashtray.
请把香烟灰弹在烟灰缸里。

He stubbed out his cigarette in the ashtray.
他在烟灰缸内捻灭了他的香烟。
