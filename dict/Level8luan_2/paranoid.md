# 音标

- 英 `/'pærənɒɪd/`
- 美 `/'pærənɔɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 类似妄想狂的；属于偏执狂的
`suffering from a mental illness that makes you believe that other people are trying to harm you`

n. 患妄想狂的人；偏执狂患者
`A paranoid is someone who is paranoid`

# 例句

a patient suffering from paranoid schizophrenia
一个偏执型精神分裂症病人

...these sad, deluded paranoids.
…这些可悲的、被蒙蔽的妄想狂们。
