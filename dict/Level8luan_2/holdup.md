# 音标

- 英 `/'həʊldʌp/`
- 美 `/'hold,ʌp/`

# 词义

n. 持枪抢劫；交通阻塞
`A holdup is a situation in which someone is threatened with a weapon in order to make them hand over money or valuables`

# 同义

n. 持枪抢劫；交通阻塞
`stick-up` `traffic-jam`

# 短语

- `liquid holdup` [物]持液量

# 例句

What could have happened? A hold-up? There'd been no gunshot or scream.
