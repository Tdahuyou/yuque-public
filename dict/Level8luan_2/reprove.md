# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'pruːv/`
- 美 `/rɪ'pruv/`

# 词义

v. 责骂；谴责；非难
`to criticize someone for something that they have done`

# 同义

vt. 责骂；谴责；非难
`rate` `hop on`

vi. 责备；指责；非难
`blame for` `talk to`

# 同根

- adj. `reproving` 责备的；指摘的
- adv. `reprovingly` 挑剔地；申斥地；责备地
- n. `reproval` 谴责；非难
- v. `reproving` 责备；非难（reprove的现在分词）

# 例句

"There's no call for talk like that," Mrs. Evans reproved him.
“没有必要说那种话，”埃文斯太太责备他道。
