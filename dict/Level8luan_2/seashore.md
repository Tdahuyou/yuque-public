# 音标

- 英 `/'siːʃɔː(r)/`
- 美 `/'siʃɔr/`

# 词义

n. 海滨；海岸
`the land at the edge of the sea, consisting of sand and rocks`

adj. 海滨的；在海滨的


# 同义

n. [海洋]海滨；海岸
`shore` `oceanfront`

adj. [海洋]海滨的；在海滨的
`littoral`

# 例句

She takes her inspiration from shells and stones she finds on the seashore.
她从她在海岸边发现的贝壳和石头里获取灵感。
