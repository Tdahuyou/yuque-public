# 音标

- 英 `/piːk/`
- 美 `/pik/`

# 词义

n. 偷看；一瞥，看一眼
`Peek is also a noun`

v. 窥视，偷看
`to look quickly at something, or to look at something from behind something else, especially something that you are not supposed to see`

# 同义

n. 偷看；一瞥，看一眼
`glance` `dekko`

vi. 窥视，偷看
`peer` `look into`

# 同根

- n. `peer` 贵族；同等的人
- vi. `peer` 凝视，盯着看；窥视
- vt. `peer` 封为贵族；与…同等

# 短语

- `sneak peek` 先睹为快；快速前瞻

# 例句

Companies have been paying outrageous fees for a peek at the technical data.
各公司一直在为该技术资料的一窥支付惊人的费用。
