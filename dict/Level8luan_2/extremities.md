# 词义

n. 四肢，骨端；末端，极限（extremity复数形式）；手足
`The extremity of something is its farthest end or edge`

# 同义

n. 四肢，骨端；末端，极限（extremity复数形式）；手足
`limitation` `threshold` `terminal` `bottom`

# 短语

- `lower extremity` 下肢
- `upper extremity` 上肢
- `the last extremity` 极大的危险(或不幸消息)

# 例句

...a small port on the north-western extremity of the Iberian peninsula.
...一个位于伊比利亚半岛最西北端的小港口。

...the extremities of the aeroplane.
...飞机的两端。
