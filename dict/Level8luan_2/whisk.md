# 音标

- 英 `/wɪsk/`
- 美 `/wɪsk/`

# 词义

v. 拂，掸；搅拌；挥动
`to mix liquid, eggs etc very quickly so that air is mixed in, using a fork or a whisk`

n. 搅拌器；扫帚；毛掸子
`a small kitchen tool made of curved pieces of wire, used for mixing air into eggs, cream etc`

# 同义

vt. 拂，掸；搅拌；挥动
`mill` `mix round`

vi. 飞奔，疾过
`tear` `belt down`

n. 搅拌器；扫帚；毛掸子
`mixer` `agitator`

# 例句

Using a whisk, mix the yolks and sugar to a smooth paste.
用搅拌器将蛋黄和糖搅打成均匀的糊状。
