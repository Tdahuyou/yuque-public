# 音标

- 英 `/spɔːn/`
- 美 `/spɔn/`

# 词义

n. 卵；菌丝；产物
`the eggs of a fish or  frog  laid together in a soft mass`

v. 产卵；酿成，造成；大量生产
`to make a series of things happen or start to exist`
