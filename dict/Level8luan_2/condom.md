# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒndəm/`
- 美 `/'kɑndəm/`

# 词义

n. 避孕套；阴茎套
`a thin rubber bag that a man wears over his  penis (= sex organ )  during sex, to prevent a woman having a baby or to protect against disease`

# 同义

n. [医]避孕套；阴茎套
`French letter` `cundum`

# 例句

Once again, you need to be careful not to tear or puncture the condom in this process.
再说一遍，你要小心，在这个过程中不要撕裂或戳穿避孕套。

Make sure you wash your hands after you handled a used condom and before you have any further contact with your partner.
在处理了使用过的避孕套后，以及在和你的性伴侣有进一步的性接触之前，你一定要洗手。
