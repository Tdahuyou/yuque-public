# 音标

- 英 `/'gɒg(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'ɡɑɡl/`

# 词义

v. 眼珠转动；瞪眼看
`to look at something with your eyes wide open in surprise or shock`

n. 护目镜，风镜；眼睛睁视
`Goggles are large glasses that fit closely to your face around your eyes to protect them from such things as water, wind, or dust`

adj. 瞪眼的，睁眼的


# 同义

n. 护目镜，风镜；眼睛睁视
`protective glasses` `safety glasses`

# 例句

She goggled at me.
她瞪着我看。
