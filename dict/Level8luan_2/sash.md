# 音标

- 英 `/sæʃ/`
- 美 `/sæʃ/`

# 词义

n. 腰带；肩带；饰带；框格
`a long piece of cloth that you wear around your waist like a belt`

vt. 系上腰带；装以窗框
`to furnish with a sash, sashes, or sash windows `

# 同义

n. 腰带；肩带；[服装]饰带；框格
`hip girdle` `waist belt`

# 短语

- `window sash` n. 窗扇

# 例句

She wore a white dress with a thin blue sash.
她穿着一条白连衣裙，系着一根蓝色的细腰带。
