# 音标

- 英 `/glɪnt/`
- 美 `/ɡlɪnt/`

# 词义

n. 闪烁；闪光
`a look in someone’s eyes which shows a particular feeling`

v. 闪烁；（光线）反射；闪闪发光
`if a shiny surface glints, it gives out small flashes of light`

# 同义

n. 闪烁；闪光
`scintillation` `lambency`

vi. 闪烁；（光线）反射；闪闪发光
`reflect` `spark`

vt. 使闪光；使发光
`flash` `fire`

# 例句

a humorous glint in her eyes
她眼中闪烁的幽默
