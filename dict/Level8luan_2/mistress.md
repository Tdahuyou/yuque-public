# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪstrɪs/`
- 美 `/'mɪstrəs/`

# 词义

n. 情妇；女主人；主妇；女教师；女能人
`a woman that a man has a sexual relationship with, even though he is married to someone else`

# 同义

n. 情妇；女主人；主妇；女教师；女能人
`other woman` `moll`

# 例句

The Prince had shocked society by living openly with his mistress.
