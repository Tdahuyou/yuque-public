# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌndəteɪkə/`
- 美 `/'ʌndɚtekɚ/`

# 词义

n. 承担者；承办人；殡仪业者
`An undertaker is a person whose job is to deal with the bodies of people who have died and to arrange funerals`

# 同根

- n. `undertaking` 事业；企业；保证；殡仪业
- v. `undertaking` 同意；担任；许诺（undertake的ing形式）
- vt. `undertake` 承担，保证；从事；同意；试图

# 例句

An undertaker had already taken the body to be embalmed.
一位殡仪员已把尸体运去进行防腐处理。
