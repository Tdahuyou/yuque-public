# 音标

- 英 `/trɑːns/`
- 美 `/træns/`

# 词义

n. 恍惚；出神；着迷，入迷
`a state in which you behave as if you were asleep but are still able to hear and understand what is said to you`

v. 使恍惚；使发呆


# 同义

n. 恍惚；出神；着迷，入迷
`possession` `absentmindedness`

vt. 使恍惚；使发呆
`bemuse`

# 例句

The old man was in a trance, thinking of his past life.
这位老人回想着自己过去的生活， 神情恍惚。
