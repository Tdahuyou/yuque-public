# 音标

- 英 `/'ɡrɔvəl/`
- 美 `/'ɡrɑvl/`

# 词义

v. 匍匐；卑躬屈膝；趴
`to praise someone a lot or behave with a lot of respect towards them because you think that they are important and will be able to help you in some way – used to show disapproval`

# 同义

vi. 匍匐；卑躬屈膝；趴
`kowtow`

# 同根

- adj. `groveling` 匍匐的；卑躬屈节的
- adj. `grovelling` 奴颜婢膝的；卑下的；卑躬屈节的
- n. `groveler` 卑恭屈节的人
- v. `groveling` （在卑屈、恐惧等时）匍匐；卑躬屈膝（grovel的ing形式）
- v. `grovelling` 趴在地上；匍匐（grovel的ing形式）

# 例句

I saw him grovelling in the road for his hat.
我看到他趴在路上找帽子。
