# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'feɪθfʊl; -f(ə)l/`
- 美 `/ʌn'feθfl/`

# 词义

adj. 不忠实的；不诚实的；不准确的
`someone who is unfaithful has sex with someone who is not their wife, husband, or usual partner`

# 同义

adj. 不忠实的；不诚实的；不准确的
`faithless` `dishonest`

# 同根

- adv. `unfaithfully` 不忠实地
- n. `unfaithfulness` 不忠实；不信守婚誓

# 例句

James had been unfaithful to Christine for the entire four years they'd been together.
詹姆斯在与克里斯蒂娜一起的整整4年里一直是不忠的。
