# 音标

- 英 `/læps/`
- 美 `/læps/`

# 词义

n. (一时的)走神，判断错误
`A lapse of something such as concentration or judgment is a temporary lack of that thing, which can often cause you to make a mistake`

# 同义

n. 失效；流逝；过失
`error` `sin` `mistake` `blame` `passing`

vi. 失效；流逝；背离，失检；陷入
`cease to be in force` `out of service`

# 同根

- adj. `lapsed` 失效的；堕落的；转归他人的
- v. `lapsed` 陷入（lapse的过去分词）；流逝；结束

# 短语

- `lapse of time` 一段时间；时光的流逝
- `lapse of memory` 记错；记忆力衰退
- `time lapse` 时间流逝；时间推移
- `lapse into` 陷入
- `lapse from virtue` 堕落，道德败坏

# 例句

I had a little lapse of concentration in the middle of the race.
我在比赛中走了一下神。

He was a genius and because of it you could accept lapses of taste.
他是一个天才，因此他偶失得体，人们也可以接受。
