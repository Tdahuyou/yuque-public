# 音标

- 英 `/pɔːn/`
- 美 `/pɔn/`

# 词义

n. 典当；抵押物；兵，卒；人质
`one of the eight smallest and least valuable pieces which each player has in the game of  chess `

v. 当掉；以……担保
`If you pawn something that you own, you leave it with a pawnbroker, who gives you money for it and who can sell it if you do not pay back the money before a certain time`

# 同义

n. [贸易]典当；[经]抵押物；兵，卒；人质
`hostage`

# 同根

- n. `pawnbroker` 当铺老板；典当商

# 短语

- `pawn shop` 当铺

# 例句

He is contemplating pawning his watch.
他在考虑把手表当了。
