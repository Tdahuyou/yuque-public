# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'tælɪk/`
- 美 `/ɪ'tælɪk/`

# 词义

n. 斜体字（或字母、数码等）
`Italics are letters that slope to the right. Italics are often used to emphasize a particular word or sentence. The examples in this dictionary are printed in italics. `

adj. [印刷]斜体的
`Italic letters slope to the right`

# 同根

- vi. `italicize` 用斜体字
- vt. `italicize` 在字下划横线；用斜体字排字；强调
- vt. `italicise` 用斜体字印刷（等于italicize）

# 短语

- `italic type` 斜体活字；斜体

# 例句

The title is printed in italics.
标题被印成斜体。

She addressed them by hand in her beautiful italic script.
她用她漂亮的斜体字亲笔给他们写信。
