# 音标

- 英 `/riːə'semb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/,riə'sɛmbl/`

# 词义

v. 重新集合
`to bring together the different parts of something to make a whole again, after they have been separated`

# 同义

vt. 重新装配；重新召集
`repackage`

# 同根

- adv. `reassembly` 重组的；[电][机] 重新装配的

# 例句

The equipment had to be dismantled and reassembled at each new location.
得把这些设备拆开来，到各个新的地方重新组装。
