# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪnsens/`
- 美 `/'ɪnsɛns/`

# 词义

v. 向…焚香；使…发怒
`If you say that something incenses you, you mean that it makes you extremely angry`

n. 香；奉承
`a substance that has a pleasant smell when you burn it`

# 同义

n. 香；奉承
`court` `joss stick`

# 同根

- adj. `incensed` 愤怒的，激怒了的
- v. `incensed` 激怒（incense的过去分词）

# 短语

- `incense burner` 香炉

# 例句

This proposal will incense conservation campaigners.
这项提议将激怒自然保护的倡导者。
