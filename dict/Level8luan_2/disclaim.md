# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'kleɪm/`
- 美 `/dɪs'klem/`

# 词义

v. 否认，拒绝；放弃，弃权；拒绝承认
`to state, especially officially, that you are not responsible for something, that you do not know about it, or that you are not involved with it`

# 同义

vt. 否认，拒绝；放弃，弃权；拒绝承认
`yield` `desert` `negative` `refuse` `deny`

vi. 否认；放弃；弃权
`deny` `fall out of`

# 同根

- n. `disclaimer` 不承诺，免责声明；放弃，拒绝

# 例句

She disclaims any knowledge of her husband's business.
她声称自己对她丈夫的生意一无所知。
