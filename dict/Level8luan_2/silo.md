# 音标

- 英 `/'saɪləʊ/`
- 美 `/'saɪlo/`

# 词义

n. 筒仓；青贮窖；飞弹发射井
`a tall structure like a tower that is used for storing grain, winter food for farm animals etc`

vt. 把…存入青贮窖；把…储存在筒仓内


# 例句

Before silos were invented, cows gave less milk during winter because they had no green grass to eat.
筒仓发明之前，因为没有青草可以吃，奶牛在冬天产奶量减少。
