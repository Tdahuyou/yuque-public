# 音标

- 英 `/'kaʊədlɪ/`
- 美 `/'kauədli/`

# 词义

adj. 怯懦的，懦弱的；胆小的
`If you describe someone as cowardly, you disapprove of them because they are easily frightened and avoid doing dangerous and difficult things`

adv. 胆怯地


# 同义

adj. 怯懦的，懦弱的；胆小的
`faint-hearted` `unmanly`

adv. 胆怯地
`cravenly` `pusillanimously`

# 同根

- adj. `coward` 胆小的，懦怯的
- n. `coward` 懦夫，懦弱的人
- n. `cowardice` 怯懦；胆小
- n. `cowardliness` 胆怯；懦弱

# 例句

I was too cowardly to complain.
我胆小得都不敢抱怨。
