# 音标

- 英 `/kwɪp/`
- 美 `/kwɪp/`

# 词义

n. 妙语；嘲弄；讽刺语
`A quip is a remark that is intended be amusing or clever`

v. 嘲弄；讥讽


# 同义

n. 妙语；嘲弄；讽刺语
`punch line` `bon mot`

vi. 嘲弄；讥讽
`make game of` `poke fun at`

vt. 嘲弄
`guy` `monkey`

# 例句

The commentators make endless quips about the players' appearance.
解说员们对球员们的外貌没完没了地说着俏皮话。
