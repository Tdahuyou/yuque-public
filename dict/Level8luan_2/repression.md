# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'preʃən/`
- 美 `/rɪ'prɛʃən/`

# 词义

n. 抑制，[心理]压抑；镇压
`when someone does not allow themselves to express feelings or desires which they are ashamed of, especially sexual ones – used when you think someone should express these feelings`

# 同义

n. 抑制，[心理]压抑；镇压
`checking` `control` `restraint` `suppression` `inhibition`

# 同根

- adj. `repressive` 镇压的；压抑的；抑制的
- adj. `repressed` 被压抑的；被抑制的
- n. `repressor` 镇压者
- n. `repressing` 重压；补加压力；[机] 再压
- v. `repressed` 抑制；镇压；约束（repress的过去分词）
- v. `repressing` 抑制；镇压；约束（repress的ing形式）
- vi. `repress` 抑制；镇压
- vt. `repress` 抑制；镇压（叛乱等）；约束
