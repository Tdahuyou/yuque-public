# 音标

- 英 `/'kweɪvə/`
- 美 `/'kwevɚ/`

# 词义

v. 颤抖
`if your voice quavers, it shakes as you speak, especially because you are nervous or upset`

n. 颤抖；八分音符；颤音
`a musical note which continues for an eighth of the length of a semibreve`

# 同义

vt. 颤抖
`shiver`

vi. 颤抖；发颤音
`quake` `judder`

n. 颤抖；八分音符；颤音
`flesh-creep` `frisson`

# 同根

- adv. `quaveringly` 颤音地；震颤地

# 例句

‘It’s not true,’ she said, in a quavering voice.
“这不是真的。”她颤声说道。

‘No,’ he quavered.
“不。”他声音都颤抖了。
