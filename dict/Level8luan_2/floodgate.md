# 音标

- 英 `/'flʌdgeɪt/`
- 美 `/'flʌdɡet/`

# 词义

n. 水闸；水门；防潮水闸；制约（怒气）
`a gate that is used to control the flow of water from a large lake or river`

# 同义

n. 水闸；水门；防潮水闸；制约（怒气）
`sluice gate` `milldam`

# 例句

The government has just begun a multi-billion euro floodgate project aimed at stopping rising sea levels destroying the town.
当地政府已拨款数十亿欧元用于投资防潮水闸工程，目的是要停止海平面上涨对水城的破坏。
