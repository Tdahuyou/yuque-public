# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'mjuːnəreɪt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'mjunəret/`

# 词义

v. 酬劳；给与报酬；赔偿
`If you are remunerated for work that you do, you are paid for it`

# 同义

vt. 酬劳；给与报酬；赔偿
`make good` `indemnify`

# 同根

- adj. `remunerative` 有利可图的；有报酬的；合算的
- adj. `remunerated` 获得酬金的
- n. `remuneration` 报酬；酬劳，赔偿
- n. `remunerator` 酬劳者
- v. `remunerated` 付给…报酬（remunerate的过去式）

# 例句

You will be remunerated and so will your staff.
你将会得到报酬，你的员工也会。
