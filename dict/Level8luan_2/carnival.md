# 音标

- 英 `/'kɑːnɪv(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'kɑrnɪvl/`

# 词义

n. 狂欢节，嘉年华会；饮宴狂欢
`a public event at which people play music, wear special clothes, and dance in the streets`

# 同义

n. 狂欢节，嘉年华会；饮宴狂欢
`Dionysia` `Fasching`

# 短语

- `carnival parade` 狂欢节队伍

# 例句

preparations for this year’s carnival
今年狂欢节的准备工作

when it’s Carnival in Rio
里约热内卢举行狂欢节的时候

a carnival atmosphere in the town
城里的狂欢气氛
