# 音标

- 英 `/klɪn(t)ʃ/`
- 美 `/klɪntʃ/`

# 词义

v. 扭住；敲弯；最终确定或解决
`to finally agree on something or get something after trying very hard`

n. 拥吻；钉牢
`a situation in which two people who love each other hold each other tightly`

# 同根

- n. `clincher` [机] 紧钳；敲弯钉头的工具

# 例句

a young salesman eager to clinch the deal
一名急于想做成这笔生意的年轻推销员
