# 音标

- 英 `/'skaʊndr(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'skaʊndrəl/`

# 词义

adj. 卑鄙的


n. 恶棍；无赖；流氓
`a bad or dishonest man, especially someone who cheats or deceives other people`

# 同义

adj. 卑鄙的
`poor` `base` `mean` `dirty`

n. 恶棍；无赖；流氓
`tough` `cad`

# 同根

- adj. `scoundrelly` 恶棍的，流氓的
- n. `scoundrelly` 恶棍；流氓；无赖

# 例句

He is a lying scoundrel!
他是个无耻的骗子！
