# 音标

- 英 `/'saɪdwɔːk/`
- 美 `/'saɪdwɔk/`

# 词义

n. 人行道
`a hard surface or path at the side of a street for people to walk on`

# 同义

n. [交]人行道
`pavement` `footpath`

# 同根

- adj. `sideway` 旁边的；向侧面的（等于sideways）
- adv. `sideway` 斜地里
- n. `sideway` 人行道；小巷

# 例句

A child was toddling along the sidewalk.
一个小孩正沿着人行道蹒跚行走。

The blind man tapped his cane on the sidewalk.
盲人用手杖敲击人行道。

Then I noticed he was on the sidewalk behind me.
然后在人行道上我注意到他在我后面。
