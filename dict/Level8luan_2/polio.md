# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊlɪəʊ/`
- 美 `/'polɪo/`

# 词义

n. 小儿麻痹症（等于poliomyelitis）；脊髓灰质炎
`a serious infectious disease of the nerves in the  spine  , that often results in someone being permanently unable to move particular muscles`

# 同义

n. 小儿麻痹症（等于poliomyelitis）；脊髓灰质炎
`infantile paralysis` `tephromyelitis`

# 例句

Gladys was crippled by polio at the age of 3.
格拉迪丝3岁时因小儿麻痹症而瘸了。
