# 音标

- 英 `/dreɪp/`
- 美 `/drep/`

# 词义

v. 用布帘覆盖；使呈褶裥状
`If someone or something is draped in a piece of cloth, they are loosely covered by it`

n. 窗帘；褶裥
`Drapes are long heavy curtains`

# 同义

n. 窗帘；褶裥
`window curtain` `window hangings`

# 同根

- n. `drapery` 布料；帏帐；打褶的帐幔
- n. `draper` 布商；服装商

# 例句

...a casket draped in the Virginia flag.
…一口覆盖着弗吉尼亚州旗的棺材。

He pulled the drapes shut, locked the door behind him.
他拉上长帘,锁上了身后的门。
