# 音标

- 英 `/'rɒndɪvuː; -deɪvuː/`
- 美 `/'rɑndevʊ/`

# 词义

n. 约会；约会地点；集结地
`an arrangement to meet someone at a particular time and place, often secretly`

v. 会合；约会
`to meet someone at a time or place that was arranged earlier`

# 同义

n. 约会；约会地点；集结地
`date` `engagement`

vi. [军][航]会合；约会
`link up` `make an appointment`

# 例句

plans for a secret rendezvous
秘密约会的计划
