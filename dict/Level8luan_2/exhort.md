# 音标

- 英 `/ɪg'zɔːt; eg-/`
- 美 `/ɪɡ'zɔrt/`

# 词义

v. 忠告；劝诫
`If you exhort someone to do something, you try hard to persuade or encourage them to do it`

# 同义

vt. 忠告；劝诫
`sound advice` `advise`

vi. 劝告
`give advice` `advise to`

# 同根

- adj. `exhortative` 劝告的；劝勉的
- n. `exhortation` 讲道词，训词；劝告

# 例句

Kennedy exhorted his listeners to turn away from violence.
肯尼迪劝告他的听众要远离暴力。

He exhorted his companions, "Try to accomplish your aim with diligence."
他勉励他的同志，“尝试以勤奋来实现目标。”
