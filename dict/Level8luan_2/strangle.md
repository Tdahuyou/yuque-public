# 音标

- 英 `/'stræŋg(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'stræŋɡl/`

# 词义

v. 把…勒死；使…窒息
`To strangle someone means to kill them by squeezing their throat tightly so that they cannot breathe`

# 同义

vt. 把…勒死；使…窒息
`asphyxiate`

# 同根

- adj. `strangled` 卡住的
- v. `strangled` 掐死（strangle的过去分词）；抑制
- vi. `strangulate` 窒息
- vt. `strangulate` 勒杀；使窒息；绞死

# 例句

He tried to strangle a border policeman and steal his gun.
他试图扼死一位边防警察并偷走他的枪。
