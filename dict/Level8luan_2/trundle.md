# 音标

- 英 `/'trʌnd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'trʌndl/`

# 词义

n. 滚动；小脚轮


v. 使滚动；运送
`If you trundle something somewhere, especially a small, heavy object with wheels, you move or roll it along slowly`

# 同义

n. 滚动；小脚轮
`rolling motion` `breaking in`

vt. 使滚动；运送
`freight` `ship` `forward` `transit`

vi. 滚动；移动
`roll` `to move`

# 例句

The old man lifted the wheelbarrow and trundled it away.
老人抬起手推车，慢慢地推走了。
