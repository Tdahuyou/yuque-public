# 音标

- 英 `/'snɪkə/`
- 美 `/'snɪkɚ/`

# 词义

v. 偷笑，窃笑
`to laugh quietly and in a way that is not nice at something which is not supposed to be funny`

n. 窃笑
`Snicker is also a noun`

# 同义

vi. 偷笑，窃笑
`laugh in one's beard`

n. 窃笑
`snigger` `titter`

# 例句

We all snickered at Mrs. Swenson.
我们都暗暗嘲笑斯温森太太。

...a chorus of jeers and snickers.
...一阵嗤笑。
