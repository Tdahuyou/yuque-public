# 音标

- 英 `/'snɪf(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'snɪfl/`

# 词义

v. 抽鼻涕；抽噎（等于snuffle）
`If you sniffle, you keep sniffing, usually because you are crying or have a cold`

n. 鼻塞声；不断的吸鼻子（等于snuffle）
`if you have the sniffles, you keep sniffing, especially because you have a cold`

# 例句

"Please don't yell at me." She began to sniffle.
“请不要对我大嚷大叫。”她开始啜泣起来。
