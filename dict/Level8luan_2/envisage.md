# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'vɪzɪdʒ; en-/`
- 美 `/ɪn'vɪzɪdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 正视，面对；想像
`If you envisage something, you imagine that it is true, real, or likely to happen`

# 同义

vt. 正视，面对；想像
`imagine` `picture` `think of`

# 例句

He envisages the possibility of establishing direct diplomatic relations in the future.
他设想将来建立直接外交关系的可能性。
