# 音标

- 英 `/'sespɪt/`
- 美 `/'sespit/`

# 词义

n. 垃圾坑，污水坑；粪坑
`a large hole or container under the ground in which waste from a building, especially from the toilets, is collected`

# 同义

n. 垃圾坑，[环境]污水坑；粪坑
`sinkhole`

# 例句

For weeks the affair threatened to be a cesspit of scandal.
几个星期来，这一事件眼看就要成为丑闻的滋生源。
