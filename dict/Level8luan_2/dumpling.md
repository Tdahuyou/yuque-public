# 音标

- 英 `/'dʌmplɪŋ/`
- 美 `/'dʌmplɪŋ/`

# 词义

n. 饺子，汤团；面团布丁
`a round lump of flour and fat mixed with water, cooked in boiling liquid and served with meat`

# 同义

n. 饺子，汤团；面团布丁
`jiaozi` `tortellini`

# 短语

- `rice dumpling` 粽子
- `boiled dumpling` 水饺
- `chinese dumpling` 中国饺子

# 例句

chicken and dumplings
鸡肉和汤团
