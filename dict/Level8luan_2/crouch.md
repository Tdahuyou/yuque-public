# 音标

- 英 `/kraʊtʃ/`
- 美 `/kraʊtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 蹲伏，蜷伏；卑躬屈膝
`to lower your body close to the ground by bending your knees completely`

n. 蹲伏
`Crouch is also a noun`

# 同义

vi. 蹲伏，蜷伏；卑躬屈膝
`stay down` `grovel`

vt. 低头；屈膝
`head drop` `drop on one's knees`

n. 蹲伏
`couching`

# 例句

He crouched in the shadows near the doorway.
他蹲在门道近旁的阴暗处。

Paula crouched down and held her hands out to the fire.
葆拉蹲下来，把手伸向火堆。

They walked in a crouch, each bent over close to the ground.
他们蹲伏着行走，一个个弯着腰快要贴着地面了。
