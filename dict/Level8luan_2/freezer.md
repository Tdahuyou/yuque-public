# 音标

- 英 `/'friːzə/`
- 美 `/'frizɚ/`

# 词义

n. 冰箱；冷冻库；制冷工
`a large piece of electrical kitchen equipment in which food can be stored at very low temperatures for a long time`

# 同义

n. 冰箱；[制冷]冷冻库；制冷工
`refrigerator` `icebox`

# 同根

- adj. `freezing` 冰冻的；严寒的；冷冻用的
- n. `freeze` 冻结；凝固
- vi. `freeze` 冻结；冷冻；僵硬
- vt. `freeze` 使…冻住；使…结冰
