# 音标

- 英 `/kiː/`
- 美 `/ki/`

# 词义

n. 码头
`a place in a town or village where boats can be tied up or can stop to load and unload goods`

# 同义

n. [水运]码头
`landing` `pier` `shipside`

# 同根

- n. `quayage` 码头使用费；码头税；系船费

# 短语

- `quay crane` 港岸起重机
- `quay wall` 岸壁；码头岸壁

# 例句

Jack and Stephen were waiting for them on the quay.
杰克和斯蒂芬正在码头等他们。
