# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'pedɪm(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/ɪm'pɛdɪmənt/`

# 词义

n. 口吃；妨碍；阻止
`Something that is an impediment to a person or thing makes their movement, development, or progress difficult`

# 同义

n. 口吃；妨碍；阻止
`prevention` `intervention` `violation` `check`

# 同根

- vt. `impede` 阻碍；妨碍；阻止

# 短语

- `speech impediment` 言语障碍者

# 例句

He was satisfied that there was no legal impediment to the marriage.
他对于这场婚姻没有法律上的障碍感到很满意。
