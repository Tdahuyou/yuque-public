# 音标

- 英 `/'tæmpə/`
- 美 `/'tæmpɚ/`

# 词义

v. 篡改；干预；损害；削弱；玩弄；贿赂
`If someone tampers with something, they interfere with it or try to change it when they have no right to do so`

n. 填塞者；捣棒


# 同义

vi. 篡改；干预；损害；削弱；玩弄；贿赂
`intervene in` `take a hand in`

vt. 篡改
`manipulate` `interpolate`

# 同根

- n. `tamp` 捣棒；打夯的工具
- n. `tamponade` 填塞
- n. `tamponage` 压塞；填塞（等于tamponade）
- vt. `tamp` 夯实，砸实；填塞

# 短语

- `tamper with` 篡改；贿赂；胡乱摆弄
- `tamper proof` 干预防护

# 例句

I don't want to be accused of tampering with the evidence.
我不想被指控篡改证据。
