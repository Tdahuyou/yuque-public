# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'lektrəkjuːt/`
- 美 `/ɪ'lɛktrəkjut/`

# 词义

v. （美）以电椅处死；使触电致死
`if someone is electrocuted, they are injured or killed by electricity passing through their body`

# 同根

- n. `electrolysis` [化学] 电解，[化学] 电解作用；以电针除痣
- n. `electrocution` 电刑；电死
- vt. `electrify` 使电气化；使充电；使触电；使激动

# 例句

Last week a housewife was electrocuted by her washing machine.
上周有个家庭主妇被洗衣机电死了。
