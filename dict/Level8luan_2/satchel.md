# 音标

- 英 `/'sætʃ(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'sætʃəl/`

# 词义

n. 书包；小背包
`a leather bag that you carry over your shoulder, used especially in the past by children for carrying books to school`

# 同义

n. 书包；小背包
`schoolbag` `mochila`

# 例句

He rested his left handlightly but firmly on the half-opened flap of  his  satchel.
他将左手放松，但却紧紧抓住了半开口的小背包。
