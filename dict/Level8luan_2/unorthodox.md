# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'ɔːθədɒks/`
- 美 `/ʌn'ɔrθə'dɑks/`

# 词义

adj. 非正统的；异端的；异教的
`unorthodox opinions or methods are different from what is usual or accepted by most people`

# 同义

adj. 非正统的；异端的；异教的
`heterodox` `heretical`

# 例句

The reality-based show followed the unorthodox lives of Ozzy, his wife Sharon, daughter Kelly, and son, Jack.
这个写实剧取材于奥兹、其妻莎伦、女儿凯莉和儿子杰克的另类生活。
