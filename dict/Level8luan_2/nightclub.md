# 音标

- 英 `/'naɪtklʌb/`
- 美 `/'naitklʌb/`

# 词义

n. 夜总会
`a place where people go to dance and drink, which is open late at night`

vi. 去夜总会


# 同义

n. 夜总会
`club` `night spot` `boite`

# 例句

Getting into the brain is like getting into an exclusive nightclub where only the glamorous few are selected.
进入大脑，就像是获准进入独家夜总会，其中只有少数迷人的人物能被选中。
