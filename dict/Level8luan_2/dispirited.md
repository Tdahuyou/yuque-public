# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spɪrɪtɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 沮丧的；没有精神的；意气消沉的
`someone who is dispirited does not feel as hopeful, eager, or interested in something as they were in the past`

v. 使…沮丧；使…气馁（dispirit的过去分词）


# 同义

adj. 沮丧的；没有精神的；意气消沉的
`blue` `disappointed` `depressed`

# 同根

- adj. `dispiriting` 令人沮丧的；使人气馁的
- adv. `dispiritedly` 没精神地；气馁地
- v. `dispiriting` 使沮丧；使气馁（dispirit的ing形式）
- vt. `dispirit` 使气馁，使沮丧

# 例句

At last, dispirited and weary, they gave up the search.
他们又灰心又疲惫，最后放弃了搜寻。
