# 音标

- 英 `/'hɒlɪ/`
- 美 `/'hɑli/`

# 词义

n. 冬青树（等于holmoak）
`a small tree with sharp dark green leaves and red berries (  berry  ), or the leaves and berries of this tree, used as a decoration at Christmas`

adj. 冬青属植物的


# 同义

n. [植]冬青树（等于holm oak）
`evergreen tree` `wintergreen`

# 例句

The hall was decked with holly for the Christmas.
大厅装饰着冬青树枝迎接圣诞节。

The church was decked with holly for the holiday season.
教堂以冬青树枝装饰以迎接圣诞节。
