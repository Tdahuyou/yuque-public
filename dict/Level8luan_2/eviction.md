# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'vɪkʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/ɪ'vɪkʃən/`

# 词义

n. 逐出；赶出；收回
`Eviction is the act or process of officially forcing someone to leave a house or piece of land`

# 同义

n. 逐出；赶出；收回
`extrusion` `withdrawal`

# 同根

- vt. `evict` 驱逐；逐出

# 例句

He was facing eviction, along with his wife and family.
他及其妻儿正面临着被驱逐。
