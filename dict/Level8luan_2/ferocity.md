# 音标

- 英 `/fə'rɒsɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/fə'rɑsəti/`

# 词义

n. 凶猛；残忍；暴行
`the state of being extremely violent and severe`

# 同义

n. 凶猛；残忍；暴行
`fierceness` `cruelty`

# 同根

- [n. `ferociousness` 凶恶；厉害；残忍
- adj. `ferocious` 残忍的；惊人的
- adv. `ferociously` 残忍地；野蛮地；凶猛地

# 例句

The armed forces seem to have been taken by surprise by the ferocity of the attack.
武装部队好像被猛烈的进攻给镇住了。
