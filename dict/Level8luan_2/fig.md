# 音标

- 英 `/fɪg/`
- 美 `/fɪɡ/`

# 词义

n. 无花果；无花果树；少许，一些；无价值的东西；服装
`a soft sweet fruit with a lot of small seeds, often eaten dried, or the tree on which this fruit grows`

vt. 打扮；使马跑快
