# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'demnɪfaɪ/`
- 美 `/ɪn'dɛmnɪfaɪ/`

# 词义

v. 赔偿；保护；使免于受罚
`to promise to pay someone if something they own is damaged or lost`

# 同义

vt. 赔偿；保护；使免于受罚
`secure` `preserve` `make good`

# 同根

- n. `indemnification` 赔偿；保护；赦免；补偿金

# 例句

They agreed to indemnify the taxpayers against any loss.
他们同意赔偿纳税人的任何损失。
