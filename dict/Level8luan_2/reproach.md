# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'prəʊtʃ/`
- 美 `/rɪ'protʃ/`

# 词义

n. 责备；耻辱
`a remark that expresses criticism, blame, or disapproval`

v. 责备；申斥
`to feel guilty about something that you think you are responsible for`

# 同义

n. 责备；耻辱
`blame` `thumbs-down`

vt. 责备；申斥
`blame for` `talk to`

# 同根

- adj. `reproachful` 责备的；申斥的；非难的
- adj. `reproachless` 无可非议的；无可非难的
- adv. `reproachfully` 责备地
- n. `reproacher` 责难者

# 短语

- `beyond reproach` 无可指责；无瑕疵的
- `above reproach` 无可指责；完美

# 例句

He argued that the reproaches were unfair.
他争辩说这些批评不公正。
