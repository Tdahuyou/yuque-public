# 音标

- 英 `/'rʌb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'rʌbl/`

# 词义

n. 碎石，碎砖；粗石堆
`broken stones or bricks from a building or wall that has been destroyed`

# 同义

n. 碎石，碎砖；[建]粗石堆
`crushed stone` `rockfill`

# 短语

- `rubble mound` 毛石堆；毛石基；堆石斜坡堤

# 例句

Brick rubble is useful as the base for paths and patios.
碎砖可用作道路和天井的基石。
