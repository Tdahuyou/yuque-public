# 音标

- 英 `/'krɒsbəʊ/`
- 美 `/'krɔs'bo/`

# 词义

n. 弩；石弓
`a weapon like a small  bow  attached to a longer piece of wood, used for shooting  arrows  with a lot of force`

# 例句

The tag, which he deploys with a crossbow, is a hockey puck-sized transmitter that embeds into the thick walrus skin.
菲施巴赫用石弓将曲棍球大小的标签插进海象厚厚的皮肤里。
