# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌmwʌn/`
- 美 `/'sʌmwʌn/`

# 词义

pron. 有人，某人
`used to mean a person, when you do not know or do not say who the person is`

# 同根

- n. `somebody` 大人物；重要人物
- pron. `somebody` 有人；某人

# 例句

What would you do if someone tried to rob you in the street?
要是有人在街上抢劫你，你会怎么办？

Will someone please explain what’s going on?
谁能说说是怎么回事？

Have Brooks or someone fax this to New York right away.
叫布鲁克斯或谁立刻把这个发传真到纽约。
