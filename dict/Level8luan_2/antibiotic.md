# 音标

- 英 `/,æntɪbaɪ'ɒtɪk/`
- 美 `/,æntɪbaɪ'ɑtɪk; ˌæntaɪ-/`

# 词义

adj. 抗生的；抗菌的


n. 抗生素，抗菌素
`a drug that is used to kill  bacteria  and cure infections`

# 同义

adj. [药][微]抗生的；抗菌的
`germproof`

n. [药]抗生素，[微]抗菌素
`microbiotic` `bacteriophage`

# 短语

- `antibiotic resistance` 抗生素抗性；抗菌素耐药性
- `broad spectrum antibiotic` 广谱抗菌素

# 例句

"If you can stop the enzymes involved in that process with a small molecule or chemical," says Dunman, "that chemical could be an antibiotic.
“如果能用一小分子或化学物质阻止这一酶的活性过程，”德明说，“这种化学物质就能够用来做抗生素。”

If we all recognize that antibiotic resistance is one of the greatest threats to public health that we face today, we have to do something about this.
如果我们大家都能认识到耐抗生素（药性）已经成为我们今天面对的最大的公众健康威胁之一，我们就真的需要做点什么了。
