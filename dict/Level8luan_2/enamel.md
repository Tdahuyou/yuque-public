# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'næm(ə)l/`
- 美 `/ɪ'næml/`

# 词义

n. 搪瓷；珐琅；瓷釉；指甲油
`a hard shiny substance that is put onto metal, clay etc for decoration or protection`

v. 彩饰；涂以瓷釉


# 短语

- `enamel coating` 搪瓷护膜；搪瓷表层
- `porcelain enamel` 搪瓷；瓷釉
- `enamel paint` 瓷漆
- `coal tar enamel` 煤焦油磁漆

# 例句

...a white enamel saucepan.
…一个白色搪瓷煮锅。
