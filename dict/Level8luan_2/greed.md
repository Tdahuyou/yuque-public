# 音标

- 英 `/griːd/`
- 美 `/ɡrid/`

# 词义

n. 贪婪，贪心
`a strong desire for more food, money, power, possessions etc than you need`

# 同义

n. 贪婪，贪心
`covetousness` `avarice`

# 同根

- adj. `greedy` 贪婪的；贪吃的；渴望的
- adv. `greedily` 贪婪地；贪食地
- n. `greediness` 贪吃；贪欲；嘴馋

# 例句

people motivated by jealousy and greed
受妒忌和贪婪驱使的人们
