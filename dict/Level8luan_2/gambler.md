# 音标

- 英 `/'gæmblə(r)/`
- 美 `/'gæmblɚ/`

# 词义

n. 赌徒；投机商人
`A gambler is someone who gambles regularly, for example in card games or on horse racing`

# 同义

n. 赌徒；投机商人
`bettor` `gamester`

# 同根

- n. `gamble` 赌博；冒险；打赌
- n. `gambling` 赌博；投机
- v. `gambling` 赌博；打赌（gamble的ing形式）
- vi. `gamble` 赌博；孤注一掷；投机；打赌
- vt. `gamble` 赌博；孤注一掷；冒险假设

# 例句

There was a fellow in that casino tonight who's a very heavy gambler.
今晚那家赌场有个家伙是个嗜赌成性的赌棍。
