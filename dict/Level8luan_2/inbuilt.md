# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪnbɪlt/`
- 美 `/'ɪnbɪlt/`

# 词义

adj. 内置的；内藏的；嵌入的
`an inbuilt quality, feature etc is part of the nature of someone or something`

# 同义

adj. 内置的；内藏的；嵌入的
`included` `inserted`

# 例句

plants with inbuilt resistance to disease
天生能抵抗病害的植物

History has an inbuilt tendency to repeat itself.
历史本身往往会重演。
