# 音标

- 英 `/pɪə/`
- 美 `/pɪr/`

# 词义

n. 码头，直码头；桥墩；窗间壁
`a structure that is built over and into the water so that boats can stop next to it or people can walk along it`

# 同义

n. 码头，直码头；桥墩；窗间壁
`landing` `quay`

# 短语

- `bridge pier` 桥墩
- `pier foundation` 墩式地基，墩基础

# 例句

a yacht moored at a pier
停在码头的一艘游艇

strolling along Brighton Pier
在布赖顿码头散步
