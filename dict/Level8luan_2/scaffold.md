# 音标

- 英 `/'skæfəʊld; -f(ə)ld/`
- 美 `/'skæfold/`

# 词义

n. 脚手架；鹰架；绞刑台
`a structure built next to a wall, for workers to stand on while they build, repair, or paint a building`

v. 给…搭脚手架；用支架支撑


# 同义

n. [建]脚手架；鹰架；绞刑台
`false work` `lding`

# 同根

- n. `scaffolding` 脚手架；搭脚手架的材料

# 例句

Ascending the shaky ladder to the scaffold, More addressed the executioner.
摩尔沿着摇摇晃晃的梯子登上绞刑台，跟行刑人说了话。
