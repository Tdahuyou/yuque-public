# 音标

- 英 `/'ɜːθwɜːk/`
- 美 `/'ɝθwɝk/`

# 词义

n. [建]土方工程；土木工事
`a large long pile of earth, used in the past to stop attacks`

# 例句

Stonehenge is believed to be a large earthwork constructed about 5000 years ago.
巨石阵无疑是5000年前的一项巨大的土木工程。

Our company is an earthwork to do business!
我公司是一家以做土方工程的公司！
