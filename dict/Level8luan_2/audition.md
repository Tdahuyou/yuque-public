# 音标

- 英 `/ɔː'dɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/ɔ'dɪʃən/`

# 词义

n. 听力，听觉；试听
`An audition is a short performance given by an actor, dancer, or musician so that a director or conductor can decide if they are good enough to be in a play, film, or orchestra`

v. 试听；试音
`to take part in an audition`

# 同义

n. 听力，[生理]听觉；试听
`hearing` `ear`

# 同根

- adj. `auditory` 听觉的；耳朵的
- adj. `auditive` 耳朵的；听觉的
- n. `audit` 审计；[审计] 查帐
- n. `auditor` 审计员；听者；旁听生
- n. `auditory` 听众；礼堂
- vi. `audit` 审计；[审计] 查帐
- vt. `audit` （美）旁听

# 短语

- `audition for` 向…试唱，试演；参加…面试；面试

# 例句

...an audition for a Broadway musical.
…一部百老汇歌舞剧的试演。
