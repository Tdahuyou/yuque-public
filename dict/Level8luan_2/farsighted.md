# 音标

- 英 `/'fɑː'saɪtɪd/`
- 美 `/'fɑr'saɪtɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 有远见的；能看到远处的
`If you describe someone as farsighted, you admire them because they understand what is likely to happen in the future, and therefore make wise decisions and plans`

# 同义

adj. 有远见的；能看到远处的
`forward-looking` `sagacious`

# 同根

- adj. `farseeing` 目光远大的；有远见的
- n. `farsightedness` [眼科] 远视

# 例句

Haven't farsighted economists been telling us that in the future we will work less, not more?
有远见的经济学家们不是一直在告诉我们，将来我们会工作得更少而不是更多吗？
