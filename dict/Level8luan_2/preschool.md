# 音标

- 英 `/'priːskuːl/`
- 美 `/'pri'skʊl/`

# 词义

adj. 未满学龄的；就学前的，入学前的
`relating to the time in a child’s life before they are old enough to go to school`

n. 幼儿园，育幼院
`a school for children between two and five years of age`

# 同义

n. 幼儿园，育幼院
`kindergarten` `nursery school`

# 同根

- n. `preschooler` （美）学龄前儿童

# 短语

- `preschool education` 学前教育，幼儿教育

# 例句

preschool children
学龄前儿童
