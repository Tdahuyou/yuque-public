# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'mɒgrəfɪ/`
- 美 `/dɪ'mɑɡrəfi/`

# 词义

n. 人口统计学
`the study of human populations and the ways in which they change, for example the study of how many births, marriages and deaths happen in a particular place at a particular time`

# 同义

n. [统计]人口统计学
`larithmics`

# 同根

- adj. `demographic` 人口统计学的；人口学的
- n. `demographer` [统计] 人口统计学家，人口学家

# 例句

This slow-motion disaster, demography insists, cannot be avoided without a major shift in resources.
这是一场缓慢进行的灾难，人口统计学仍坚持不能避免资源的主要转移。
