# 音标

- 英 `/'steɪlmeɪt/`
- 美 `/'stelmet/`

# 词义

n. 僵局；陷于困境
`a situation in which it seems impossible to settle an argument or disagreement, and neither side can get an advantage`

v. 使僵持；使陷入困境


# 同义

n. 僵局；陷于困境
`impasse` `logjam`

vt. 使僵持；使陷入困境
`embog`

# 同根

- n. `stall` 货摊；畜栏；托辞
- vi. `stall` 停止，停转；拖延
- vt. `stall` 拖延；使停转；使陷于泥中

# 例句

an attempt to break the stalemate
打破僵局的努力

The discussions with the miners’ union ended in stalemate .
同矿工工会的谈判以陷入僵局而告终。
