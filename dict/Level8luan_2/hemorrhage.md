# 音标

- 英 `/'hemərɪdʒ/`
- 美 `/'hɛmərɪdʒ/`

# 词义

n. [病理]出血（等于haemorrhage）；番茄汁


v. [病理]出血
`If someone is haemorrhaging, there is serious bleeding inside their body`

# 同义

n. [病理]出血（等于haemorrhage）；番茄汁
`bleeding` `haemorrhage`

# 同根

- adj. `hemorrhagic` 出血的

# 短语

- `cerebral hemorrhage` 脑出血；大脑出血
- `intracerebral hemorrhage` 脑内出血
- `subarachnoid hemorrhage` [医]蛛网膜下出血
- `postpartum hemorrhage` 产后出血
- `upper gastrointestinal hemorrhage` 上消化道出血；上消化道血
- `intraventricular hemorrhage` 脑室内出血
- `ventricular hemorrhage` 脑室出血
- `internal hemorrhage` 内出血

# 例句

Shortly after his admission into the hospital he had a massive brain haemorrhage and died.
他住进医院不久，因脑部大量内出血而去世了。

I haemorrhaged badly after the birth of all three of my sons.
我把3个儿子都生下来之后，严重内出血。
