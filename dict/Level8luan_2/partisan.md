# 音标

- 英 `/'pɑːtɪzæn; ,pɑːtɪ'zæn/`
- 美 `/'pɑrtəzn/`

# 词义

adj. 党派的；效忠的；偏袒的；盲目推崇的
`strongly supporting a particular political party, plan or leader, usually without considering the other choices carefully`

n. 游击队；虔诚信徒；党羽
`a member of an armed group that fights against an enemy that has taken control of its country`

# 同义
