# 音标

- 英 `/prɒp/`
- 美 `/prɑp/`

# 词义

n. 支柱，支撑物；支持者；道具；（橄榄球中的）支柱前锋
`an object placed under or against something to hold it in a particular position`

v. 支撑，支持，维持；使倚靠在某物上
`to support something by leaning it against something, or by putting something else under, next to, or behind it`

# 同义

vt. 支撑；维持
`maintain` `support` `uphold` `sustain`

n. [矿业]支柱；支持者
`leg` `backbone` `stay` `advocate` `mainstay`

# 短语

- `prop up` v. 支撑；支持

# 例句

Anna looks after costumes and props.
安娜管理服装和道具。
