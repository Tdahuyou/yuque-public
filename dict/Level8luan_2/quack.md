# 音标

- 英 `/kwæk/`
- 美 `/kwæk/`

# 词义

n. 庸医；鸭叫声
`a doctor`

v. （鸭子）嘎嘎叫；吹嘘；大声闲聊
`When a duck quacks, it makes the noise that ducks typically make`

adj. 骗人的；冒牌医生的
`relating to the activities or medicines of someone who pretends to be a doctor`

# 同义

n. 庸医；鸭叫声
`barber-surgeon` `empiricist`

vi. （鸭子）嘎嘎叫；吹嘘；大声闲聊
`crow about` `brag about`

adj. 骗人的；冒牌医生的
`fallacious` `deceptious`

# 同根

- n. `quackery` 庸医的医术；骗子的行为

# 例句

You’d better go and see the quack with that burn.
你最好去找医生看看那处烧伤。

There were ducks quacking on the lawn.
有鸭子在草坪上发出嘎嘎的叫着。
