# 词义

adj. 巴洛克式的；结构复杂的，形式怪样的
`relating to the very decorated style of art, music, buildings etc, that was common in Europe in the 17th and early 18th centuries`

n. 巴洛克风格；巴洛克艺术
`used to describe baroque art, music, buildings etc`
