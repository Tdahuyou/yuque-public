# 音标

- 英 `/'kætʃwɜːd/`
- 美 `/'kætʃ,wɝd/`

# 词义

n. 标语，口号；流行语；口头禅
`a word or phrase that refers to a feature of a situation, product etc that is considered important`

# 同义

n. 标语，口号；流行语；口头禅
`slogan` `cry` `banner`

# 例句

Variety is the catchword at our latest venue, the Beehive Club.
形式多样化是我们新的场所——蜂房俱乐部的口号。
