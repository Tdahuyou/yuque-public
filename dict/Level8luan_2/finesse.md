# 音标

- 英 `/fɪ'nes/`
- 美 `/fɪ'nɛs/`

# 词义

n. 策略；纤细；灵巧
`if you do something with finesse, you do it with a lot of skill and style`

v. 巧妙处理；以偷牌方式先出
`to handle a situation well, but in a way that is slightly deceitful`

# 同义

n. 策略；纤细；灵巧
`strategy` `device` `facility`

# 例句

Dario played the sonata with great finesse.
达里奥以高超的技艺弹奏了这首奏鸣曲。
