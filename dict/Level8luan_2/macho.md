# 音标

- 英 `/'mætʃəʊ/`
- 美 `/'mɑtʃo/`

# 词义

adj. 大男子气概的
`behaving in a way that is traditionally typical of men, for example being strong or brave, or not showing your feelings – used humorously or in order to show disapproval`

n. 强壮男子；大丈夫


# 同义

n. 强壮男子；大丈夫
`Real Man`
