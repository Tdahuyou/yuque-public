# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒstɪtjuːt/`
- 美 `/'prɑstətut/`

# 词义

v. 使沦为妓女


adj. 卖淫的；堕落的


n. 妓女
`someone, especially a woman, who earns money by having sex with people`

# 同义

adj. 卖淫的；堕落的
`fallen` `vicious`

n. 妓女
`sporting woman` `hetaerae` `poule` `sporting lady`

# 同根

- n. `prostitution` 卖淫；滥用；出卖灵魂

# 短语

- `prostitute oneself` 卖淫

# 例句

He admitted last week he paid for sex with a prostitute.
他承认上星期曾和一名妓女有过性交易。
