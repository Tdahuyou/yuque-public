# 音标

- 英 `/mɪs'kærɪdʒ; 'mɪskærɪdʒ/`
- 美 `/'mɪskærɪdʒ/`

# 词义

n. [妇产]流产；失败；误送
`if a woman who is going to have a baby has a miscarriage, she gives birth before the baby is properly formed and it dies`

# 同义

n. [妇产]流产；失败；误送
`failure` `loss` `losing` `defeat` `reverse`

# 同根

- vi. `miscarry` 流产；失败；被误送

# 短语

- `miscarriage of justice` 误判；审判不公

# 例句

She had two miscarriages before she had her first child.
她生第一个孩子之前流产了两次。

One in five pregnancies ends in miscarriage .
有五分之一的妊娠以流产告终。
