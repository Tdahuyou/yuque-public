# 音标

- 英 `/ʃæm/`
- 美 `/ʃæm/`

# 词义

n. 假装；骗子；赝品
`someone or something that is not what they are claimed to be – used to show disapproval`

v. 假装；冒充
`to pretend to be upset, ill etc to gain sympathy or an advantage`

adj. 假的；虚假的；假装的
`made to appear real in order to deceive people`
