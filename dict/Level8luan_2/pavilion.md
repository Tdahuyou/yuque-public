# 音标

- 英 `/pə'vɪljən/`
- 美 `/pə'vɪlɪən/`

# 词义

n. 阁；亭子；大帐篷；展示馆
`a temporary building or tent which is used for public entertainment or  exhibition s  and is often large with a lot of space and light`

vt. 搭帐篷；置…于亭中；笼罩


# 同义

n. [建]阁；亭子；大帐篷；展示馆
`summerhouse` `gloriette`

vt. 搭帐篷；置…于亭中；笼罩
`fold` `hang over`

# 例句

the German pavilion at the World Trade Fair
世界贸易博览会上的德国展馆
