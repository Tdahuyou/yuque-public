# 音标

- 英 `/dʒæb/`
- 美 `/dʒæb/`

# 词义

v. 戳；猛击；用拨火棒等捅
`to push something into or towards something else with short quick movements`

n. 戳；猛击；注射
`a sudden hard hit, especially with a pointed object or your fist(= closed hand )`

# 同义

vt. 戳；猛击；用拨火棒等捅
`stick` `pink`

vi. 戳；刺；用拳猛击
`run through` `poke`

n. 戳；猛击；注射
`injection` `dig`

# 短语

- `jab at` 猛击；猛戳

# 例句

a boxer with a good left jab
能打出漂亮左刺拳的拳击手
