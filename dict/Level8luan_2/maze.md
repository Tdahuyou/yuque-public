# 音标

- 英 `/meɪz/`
- 美 `/mez/`

# 词义

n. 迷宫；迷惑；糊涂
`a specially designed system of paths, often in a park or public garden, which is difficult to find your way through`

vt. 迷失；使混乱；使困惑


# 同义

n. 迷宫；迷惑；糊涂
`labyrinth` `delusion`

vt. 迷失；使混乱；使困惑
`cloud` `confuse`

# 同根

- adj. `mazy` 迷宫般的；迷阵似的；错综的

# 例句

We got completely lost in the maze.
我们在迷宫中彻底迷了路。

the famous Hampton Court maze
著名的汉普顿宫迷宫
