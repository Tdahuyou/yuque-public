# 音标

- 英 `/kɒndɪ'send/`
- 美 `/,kɑndɪ'sɛnd/`

# 词义

v. 屈尊；俯就；（对某人）表现出优越感
`to behave as if you think you are better, more intelligent, or more important than other people – used to show disapproval`

# 同义

vi. 屈尊；谦逊
`deign`

# 同根

- adj. `condescending` 谦逊的；故意屈尊的；有优越感的
- adv. `condescendingly` 谦逊地；俯就地；屈尊地
- n. `condescension` 谦虚；屈尊；傲慢态度
- v. `condescending` 屈尊（condescend的ing形式）

# 例句

When he condescended to speak, he contradicted himself three or four times in the space of half an hour.
当他屈尊讲话时，半小时内有三四次前后矛盾。
