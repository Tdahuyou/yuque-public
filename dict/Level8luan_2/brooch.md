# 音标

- 英 `/brəʊtʃ/`
- 美 `/brotʃ/`

# 词义

n. （女用的）胸针，领针
`a piece of jewellery that you fasten to your clothes, usually worn by women`

# 同义

n. （女用的）胸针，领针
`breastpin`
