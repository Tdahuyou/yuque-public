# 音标

- 英 `/'dekəd(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'dɛkədənt/`

# 词义

adj. 颓废的；衰微的
`having low moral standards and being more concerned with pleasure than serious matters`

n. 颓废者


# 同义

adj. 颓废的；衰微的
`deteriorative` `overripe`

# 同根

- n. `decadence` 堕落，颓废；衰落
- n. `decadency` 衰微，堕落；颓废，衰落

# 例句

Pop music was condemned as decadent and crude.
流行音乐被指责为堕落而又粗俗。
