# 音标

- 英 `/stəuv/`
- 美 `/stov/`

# 词义

n. 炉子
`a piece of kitchen equipment on which you cook food in pots and pans, and that contains an  oven `

# 同义

n. 火炉；[炉窑]窑；温室
`furnace` `greenhouse`

# 短语

- `gas stove` n. 煤气炉
- `electric stove` 电炉
- `hot blast stove` 热风炉；热鼓风炉
- `cooking stove` 厨灶
- `kitchen stove` 厨灶；煤气灶
- `coal stove` 煤炉

# 例句

a wood-burning stove
烧木柴的火炉
