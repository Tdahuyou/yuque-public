# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌbsɪkwəntlɪ/`
- 美 `/'sʌbsɪkwəntli/`

# 词义

adv. 随后
`after an event in the past`

# 同义

adv. 随后，其后；后来
`after` `since`

# 同根

- adj. `subsequent` 后来的，随后的

# 例句

The book was subsequently translated into 15 languages.
该书后来被译成 15 种语言。

Subsequently, the company filed for bankruptcy.
随后，该公司申请了破产。
