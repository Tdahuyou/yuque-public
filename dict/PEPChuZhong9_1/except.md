# 音标

- 英 `/ɪkˈsept/`
- 美 `/ɪk'sɛpt/`

# 词义

prep. 除...之外
`used to introduce the only person, thing, action, fact, or situation about which a statement is not true`

conj. 除了；只是
`used to mention a fact that makes what you have just said seem less true`



ex(出) + cept(拿， 抓) → 不拿 → 将…除外

# 同义

vi. 反对
`object` `sit down on` `to oppose`

prep. 除…之外
`unless` `besides` `in addition to`

conj. 除了；要不是
`but for`

# 同根

- adj. `exceptional` 异常的，例外的
- adj. `exceptionable` 可反对的；可抗议的；引起反感的
- adv. `exceptionally` 异常地；特殊地；例外地
- n. `exception` 例外；异议
- n. `exceptional` 超常的学生

# 短语

- `except for` 除了…以外；要不是由于
- `except as` 除…之外

# 例句

They were all there except me.
除了我以外他们都在那里。

They all howled with laughter except me.
除了我以外，他们全都哈哈大笑起来。

I have earrings just like those, except they’re blue. ＃
我有一对耳环跟那一对很像，只是我的是蓝色的。
