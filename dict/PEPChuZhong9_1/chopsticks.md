# 音标

- 英 `/'tʃɒpstɪks/`
- 美 `/'tʃɑp,stɪks/`

# 词义

n. 筷子
`a pair of narrow sticks that are used for eating East Asian food`



chop(砍) +sticks(棍) →砍成两半的棍子→筷子

# 短语

- `disposable chopsticks` 一次性筷子

# 例句

We still lack a pair of chopsticks.
还少一双筷子。

Before lunch he taught the foreigner how to use chopsticks.
午饭前他教那个外国人怎样使筷子。
