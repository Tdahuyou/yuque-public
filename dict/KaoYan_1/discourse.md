# 音标

- 英 `/'dɪskɔːs; -'kɔːs/`
- 美 `/'dɪskɔrs/`

# 词义

n. 论文;演说;谈话;话语
`serious conversation or discussion between people`

v. 讲述,著述




dis+course(课程)→讲课→演讲

# 同义

n. 论述；谈话；演讲
`speech` `talk` `address` `lecture` `treatise`

vi. 演说；谈论；讲述
`orate` `speechify`

vt. 说出；演奏出
`come out with` `give voice to`

# 短语

- `discourse analysis` 话语分析
- `discourse upon` 论述，演讲
- `universe of discourse` n. 论域；讨论或辩论的范围

# 例句

Candidates should engage in serious political discourse.
候选人应该参加严肃的政治对话。
