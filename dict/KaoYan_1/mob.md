# 音标

- 英 `/mɒb/`
- 美 `/mɑb/`

# 词义

n. 乌合之众(尤指暴力者)
`an insulting expression meaning all the poorest and least educated people in society`

v. 围攻，聚众闹事
`if a group of birds or animals mob another bird or animal, they all attack it`



那群暴民(mob)到处抢劫(rob)

# 同义

n. 暴民，暴徒；民众；乌合之众
`mass` `clamjamfry`

vt. 大举包围，围攻；蜂拥进入
`beleaguer`

# 同根

- adj. `mobbish` 暴徒似的；滋扰的；无纪律的
- n. `mobster` 匪徒；暴徒
- n. `mobocracy` 暴民统治；暴民
