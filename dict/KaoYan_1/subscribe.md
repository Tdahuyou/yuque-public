# 音标

- 英 `/səb'skraɪb/`
- 美 `/səb'skraɪb/`

# 词义

v. (to)订阅，订购；同意vt.捐助，赞助
`to pay money, usually once a year, to have copies of a newspaper or magazine sent to you, or to have some other service`



sub(下面)+scrib(写)+e→写下订单→订购， 订阅

# 同义

vi. 订阅；捐款；[经]认购；赞成；签署
`sign` `donate money`

vt. 签署；赞成；捐助
`ink` `agree` `sign` `uphold`

# 同根

- n. `subscription` 捐献；订阅；订金；签署
- n. `subscriber` 订户；签署者；捐献者

# 例句

I've personally never subscribed to the view that either sex is superior to the other.
我个人从未持有过一种性别比另一种性别优越的观点。
