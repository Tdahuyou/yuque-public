# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'siːd/`
- 美 `/rɪ'sid/`

# 词义

v. 退回,后退;收回,撤回;跌落,缩减,贬值
`if water recedes, it moves back from an area that it was covering`



re(向后)+ced(走)+e→向后走→退

# 同义

vi. 后退；减弱
`lower` `rein back`

vt. 归还
`replace` `return` `restore` `give again`

# 短语

- `recede from` v. 收回；撤回

# 例句

The flood waters finally began to recede in November.
11 月，洪水终于开始退去。
