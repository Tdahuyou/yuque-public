# 音标

- 英 `/kən'siːd/`
- 美 `/kən'sid/`

# 词义

v. 承认；容许；(比赛结束前)认输；退让
`to admit that something is true or correct, although you wish it were not true`



con(=with， 带着)+ced(走)+e→带着走→让步

# 同义

vt. 承认；退让；给予，容许
`afford` `accept` `agree` `recognize` `extend`

vi. 让步
`meet halfway` `give ground`
