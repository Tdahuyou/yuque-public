# 音标

- 英 `/'snəʊmæn/`
- 美 `/'snomæn/`

# 词义

`a simple figure of a person made of snow, made especially by children`

# 同义

n. 雪人
`yeti` `Abominable Snowman`

# 短语

- `make a snowman` 堆雪人
- `abominable snowman` 喜马拉雅雪人；雪人

# 例句

The children were balling up snow to make a snowman.
孩子们正在把雪堆成球状，做一个雪人。

"He gave me a hug and a kiss and asked me where my snowman was," she said.
黛比说：“他给了我一个拥抱和亲吻。 还问我的雪人在哪里。”

If you are wrong,the snowman with the correct answer will chill your heart.
如果你是错误的，有正确答案的雪人将会使你的心脏变冷。
