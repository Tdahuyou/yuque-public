# 音标

- 英 `/ˈfebrʊərɪ/`
- 美 `/ˈfebrʊerɪ;'fɛbrʊɛrɪ/`

# 词义

`the second month of the year, between January and March`

# 例句

These trees come into leaf in February.
这些树二月里长叶子。

It should have been all over with and forgotten by the first of last February.
它早该在去年二月的第一天就结束了，然后被人忘得无影无踪。

So let us pray together on this February morning, but let us also work together in all the days and months ahead.
因此，在今天这个二月的早晨，让我们一起祈祷，而且也让我们在今后的岁月里一起工作。
