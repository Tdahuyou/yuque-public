# 音标

- 英 `/gə'rɪlə/`
- 美 `/ɡə'rɪlə/`

# 词义

n. 大猩猩
`a very large African monkey that is the largest of the  apes `

In fact, he found that there was scarcely any information on gorilla cognition at all.
事实上，他发现任何有关大猩猩认知的信息都非常稀少。
