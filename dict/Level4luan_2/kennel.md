# 音标

- 英 `/'ken(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'kɛnl/`

# 词义

n. 狗屋；养狗场；[建]阴沟；一群狗
`a place where dogs are kept for breeding or are cared for while their owners are away`

v. 把…关进狗舍；宿于狗舍
`to put or go into a kennel; keep or stay in a kennel `
