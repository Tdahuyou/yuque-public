# 音标

- 英 `/ˈvaɪələntli/`
- 美 `/'vaɪələntli/`

# 词义

adv. 猛烈地，激烈地；极端地
`with a lot of force in a way that is very difficult to control`

# 同义

adv. 猛烈地，激烈地；极端地
`strongly` `extremely` `heavily` `hard` `fiercely`

# 同根

- adj. `violent` 暴力的；猛烈的
- n. `violence` 暴力；侵犯；激烈；歪曲

# 例句

Several people have been violently attacked in the subway.
有几个人在地铁里受到了暴力袭击。
