# 音标

- 英 `/pruːn/`
- 美 `/prun/`

# 词义

v. 删除；减少
`to make something smaller by removing parts that you do not need or want`

n. 深紫红色；傻瓜；李子干
`a dried  plum  , often cooked before it is eaten`

# 同义

vi. 删除；减少
`delete from` `to cut down`

vt. 修剪；删除；剪去
`shear` `crop`

n. 深紫红色；傻瓜；李子干
`stupid` `fool`

# 同根

- adj. `pruning` 修剪用的
- n. `pruning` 修剪；剪枝
- v. `pruning` 修剪（prune的ing形式）

# 例句

The company is pruning staff in order to reduce costs.
公司正在裁员以减少开支。

The original version of the text has been pruned quite a bit.
原稿被大幅删节。
