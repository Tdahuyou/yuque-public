# 音标

- 英 `/eb/`
- 美 `/ɛb/`

# 词义

n. 衰退；退潮；衰落
`the flow of the sea away from the shore, when the tide goes out`

v. 衰退；减少；衰落；潮退
`if the tide ebbs, it flows away from the shore`

# 同义

n. 衰退；退潮；衰落
`recession` `decline`

vi. 衰退；减少；衰落；潮退
`fail` `flag` `decline`

# 短语

- `ebb and flow` 兴衰，起伏；潮的涨落
- `ebb tide` 退潮，落潮
- `at a low ebb` 处于低潮；衰败
- `flood and ebb` 潮汐
- `ebb away` 渐渐衰退；消逝

# 例句

His confidence is at a low ebb.
他的信心很低落。
