# 音标

- 英 `/fliː/`
- 美 `/fli/`

# 词义

n. 跳蚤；低廉的旅馆；生蚤的动物
`a very small insect without wings that jumps and bites animals and people to eat their blood`

# 同义

n. [昆]跳蚤；低廉的旅馆；生蚤的动物
`Siphonaptera`

# 短语

- `flea market` 跳蚤市场；廉价市场
- `water flea` [昆]水蚤（等于Cladocera）

# 例句

Are you sure the dog has fleas ?
你确定那只狗身上有跳蚤吗？
