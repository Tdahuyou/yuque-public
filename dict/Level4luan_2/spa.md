# 音标

- 英 `/spɑː/`
- 美 `/spɑ/`

# 词义

n. 矿泉；温泉浴场；矿泉治疗地
`a place where the water has special minerals in it, and where people go to improve their health by drinking the water or swimming in it`

# 同义

n. 矿泉；温泉浴场；矿泉治疗地
`mineral spring` `thermae`

# 短语

- `spa treatment` 矿泉疗法
- `health spa` 减肥温泉疗养地

# 例句

a historic spa town
一座历史上有名的矿泉城镇
