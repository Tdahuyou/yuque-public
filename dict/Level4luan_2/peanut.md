# 音标

- 英 `/'piːnʌt/`
- 美 `/'pinʌt/`

# 词义

n. 花生
`a pale brown nut in a thin shell which grows under the ground`

# 同义

n. [食品][作物]花生
`arachi` `earthpea`

# 短语

- `peanut butter` 花生酱
- `peanut oil` 花生油
- `peanut cake` 花生饼，花生糕
- `peanut meal` 花生粕；花生饼粉
- `roasted peanut` 烤花生，烘干花生

# 例句

a packet of roasted peanuts
一袋烤花生
