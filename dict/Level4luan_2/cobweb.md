# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒbweb/`
- 美 `/'kɑb'wɛb/`

# 词义

n. 蜘蛛网；蛛丝；圈套
`a net of sticky threads made by a  spider  to catch insects, that is inside a building and has not been removed`

vt. 使布满蛛网；使混乱


# 同义

n. 蜘蛛网；蛛丝；圈套
`mesh` `trap`

vt. 使布满蛛网；使混乱
`cloud` `confuse`

# 同根

- adj. `cobwebby` 蛛网密布的；蛛网似的；布满蜘蛛网的

# 例句

The windows are cracked and covered in cobwebs.
窗户裂开了，上面布满蜘蛛网。
