# 音标

- 英 `/'teɪkəʊvə/`
- 美 `/'tekovɚ/`

# 词义

n. 接管；验收
`when one company takes control of another by buying more than half its  shares `

# 同义

n. 接管；验收
`assumption of control` `acceptance check`

# 短语

- `hostile takeover` 恶性接收；敌意接管
- `takeover bid` 收购行动；收购竞争；购并出价
- `takeover offer` 收购要约；收购建议

# 例句

Thornbury has announced a takeover bid of a regional TV company.
索恩伯里已经宣布将出价收购一家地方电视公司。

He prevented a hostile takeover (= when the takeover is not wanted by the company being bought ) of the company.
他阻止了对公司的恶意收购。
