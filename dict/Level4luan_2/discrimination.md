# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ,skrɪmɪ'neɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/dɪ,skrɪmɪ'neʃən/`

# 词义

n. 歧视；区别，辨别；识别力
`the practice of treating one person or group differently from another in an unfair way`

# 同义

n. 歧视；区别，辨别；识别力
`distinction` `differentiation`

# 同根

- adj. `discriminatory` 有辨识力的；差别对待的
- adj. `discriminating` 识别的；有差别的；有识别力的
- adj. `discriminative` 区别的，歧视的；有识别力的
- adj. `discriminable` 可辨别的；可分别的
- n. `discriminator` [电子] 鉴别器；辨别者
- v. `discriminating` 识别（discriminate的ing形式）；区别对待
- vi. `discriminate` 歧视；区别；辨别
- vt. `discriminate` 歧视；区别；辨别

# 短语

- `racial discrimination` 种族歧视
- `sex discrimination` 性别歧视；雌雄鉴别
- `price discrimination` 价格歧视，差别取价
- `gender discrimination` 性别歧视
- `race discrimination` 种族歧视

# 例句

laws to prevent discrimination
防止歧视的法律
