# 音标

- 英 `/'mɑːtə/`
- 美 `/'mɑrtɚ/`

# 词义

v. 牺牲；折磨；杀害
`If someone is martyred, they are killed or made to suffer greatly because of their religious or political beliefs`

n. 烈士；殉道者
`someone who dies for their religious or political beliefs and is admired by people for this`

# 同义

vt. 牺牲；折磨；杀害
`harry` `worry` `trouble` `tear`

# 同根

- n. `martyrdom` 殉难，牺牲；受苦，受难

# 例句

St. Pancras was martyred in 304 A.D.
圣·潘克瑞斯在公元304年蒙难。
