# 音标

- 英 `/krʌtʃ/`
- 美 `/krʌtʃ/`

# 词义

n. 拐杖；支柱；依靠；胯部
`one of a pair of long sticks that you put under your arms to help you walk when you have hurt your leg`

vt. 用拐杖支撑；支持


# 同义

n. 拐杖；支柱；依靠；胯部
`leg` `backbone` `stay`

vt. 用拐杖支撑；支持
`encourage` `carry` `second` `back` `hand`

# 例句

As things got worse at work, he began to use alcohol as a crutch.
工作每况愈下，他就开始借酒消愁。
