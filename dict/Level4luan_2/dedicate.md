# 音标

- 英 `/'dedɪkeɪt/`
- 美 `/'dɛdɪket/`

# 词义

v. 致力；献身；题献
`to give all your attention and effort to one particular thing`

# 同义

vt. 致力；献身；题献
`give oneself` `devote  to`

# 同根

- adj. `dedicated` 专用的；专注的；献身的
- n. `dedication` 奉献；献身
- v. `dedicated` 以…奉献；把…用于（dedicate的过去式和过去分词）

# 例句

For the next few years, she dedicated herself to her work.
随后的几年里，她全身心地投入工作。
