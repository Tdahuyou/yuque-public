# 音标

- 英 `/gʌlp/`
- 美 `/ɡʌlp/`

# 词义

v. 狼吞虎咽地吃；大口地吸
`to swallow large quantities of food or drink quickly`

n. 一大口（尤指液体）；吞咽
`a large amount of something that you swallow quickly, or the action of swallowing`

# 同义

vt. 狼吞虎咽地吃；大口地吸
`wolf`

vi. 哽住；喘不过气
`lose one's breath`

n. 一大口（尤指液体）；吞咽
`deglutition` `swallowing`

# 同根

- n. `gulper` 囊咽鱼

# 短语

- `gulp down` 狼吞虎咽地吃；忍住

# 例句

She gulped down her breakfast and ran for the bus.
她狼吞虎咽地吃完早饭，跑去赶公交车了。
