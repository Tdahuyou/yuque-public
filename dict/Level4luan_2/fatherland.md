# 音标

- 英 `/'fɑːðəlænd/`
- 美 `/'fɑðɚlænd/`

# 词义

n. 祖国
`the place where someone or their family was born`

# 同义

n. 祖国
`motherland` `homeland`

# 例句

You must love everything that contributes to the glory and honour of the fatherland.
你一定要热爱那些,能为祖国增添光荣和辉煌的事物

Then will all the earth be your fatherland, and all your work and effort spread forth blessings.
那么普天之下到处都是你们的祖国，你们所有的工作和努力都会遍地开花，造福天下。
