# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌndəklɑːs/`
- 美 `/'ʌndəklæs/`

# 词义

n. 下层阶级，下层社会；一、二年级学生
`the lowest social class, consisting of people who are very poor and who are not likely to be able to improve their situation`

# 同义

n. 下层阶级，下层社会；一、二年级学生
`rag-tag` `sansculotte`

# 例句

an urban underclass, who have limited access to health care
不能充分享受医疗服务的城市贫困阶层
