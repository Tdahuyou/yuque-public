# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒŋkjʊbaɪn/`
- 美 `/'kɑŋkjubaɪn/`

# 词义

n. 妾；情妇；姘妇
`a woman in the past who lived with and had sex with a man who already had a wife or wives, but who was socially less important than the wives`

# 同义

n. 妾；情妇；姘妇
`mistress` `kept woman`

# 同根

- n. `concubinage` 纳妾；非法同居；妾制

# 短语

- `imperial concubine` 皇妃
