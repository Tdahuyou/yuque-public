# 音标

- 英 `/'graʊn(d)wɜːk/`
- 美 `/'graʊnd'wɝk/`

# 词义

n. 基础；地基，根基
`something that has to happen before an activity or plan can be successful`

# 同义

n. 基础；地基，根基
`elements` `basis` `bed` `radical` `sole`

# 同根

- n. `grounding` [电] 接地；基础；搁浅；（染色的）底色
- v. `grounding` [电] 接地（ground的ing形式）；搁浅

# 例句

His speech laid the groundwork for independence.
他的演说为独立打下了基础。

Much of the groundwork has already been done .
准备工作已经完成了很多。
