# 音标

- 英 `/mʌŋk/`
- 美 `/mʌŋk/`

# 词义

n. 僧侣，修道士；和尚
`a member of an all-male religious group that lives apart from other people in a  monastery `

# 同义

n. 僧侣，修道士；和尚
`religious` `monastery`

# 同根

- adj. `monkish` 僧侣的；苦行僧般的（常带贬义）

# 短语

- `buddhist monk` 头陀，和尚；比丘

# 例句

...saffron-robed Buddhist monks.
…身穿黄袍的和尚们。
