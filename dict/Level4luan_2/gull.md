# 音标

- 英 `/gʌl/`
- 美 `/ɡʌl/`

# 词义

n. [鸟]鸥；笨人；易受骗之人
`a large common black and white sea bird that lives near the sea`

v. 骗；欺诈
`to fool, cheat, or hoax `

# 同义

n. [鸟]鸥；笨人；易受骗之人
`simp` `schmuck`

vt. 骗；欺诈
`hornswoggle` `rip off`

# 同根

- adj. `gullible` 易受骗的；轻信的
- n. `gullibility` 轻信；易受骗

# 短语

- `sea gull` 海鸥
