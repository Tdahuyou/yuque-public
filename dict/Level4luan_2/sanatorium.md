# 词义

n. 疗养院；休养地
`a type of hospital for sick people who are getting better after a long illness but still need rest and a lot of care`

# 同义

n. 疗养院；休养地
`sanitarium` `rest home`

The sanatorium stood on a large plot of land by the coast.
