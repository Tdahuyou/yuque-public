# 音标

- 英 `/'drʌŋkəd/`
- 美 `/'drʌŋkɚd/`

# 词义

n. 酒鬼，醉汉
`A drunkard is someone who frequently gets drunk`

# 同义

n. 酒鬼，醉汉
`lusher` `bibber`
