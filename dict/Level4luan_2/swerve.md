# 音标

- 英 `/swɜːv/`
- 美 `/swɝv/`

# 词义

v. 转弯；突然转向；背离
`to make a sudden sideways movement while moving forwards, usually in order to avoid hitting something`

n. 转向；偏离的程度
`Swerve is also a noun`

# 同义

vi. 转弯；突然转向；背离
`make a turn` `bend`

n. 转向；偏离的程度
`turning` `obversion`

# 例句

Drivers coming in the opposite direction swerved to avoid the bodies.
从反方向过来的司机们突然转向以避开这些尸体。

He swung the car to the left and that swerve saved Malone's life.
他把车猛转向左边，那一转救了马隆的命。
