# 音标

- 英 `/'təʊneɪl/`
- 美 `/'tonel/`

# 词义

n. 脚趾甲；[木]斜钉
`the hard part that covers the top of each of your toes`

# 例句

This kind of fungal infection on feet and toenail can be prevented if you practice barefoot walking and give the scope to the feet to breath normally.
如果你练习光着脚走路，并且使你的脚能得到正常呼吸的机会，那么这种脚部及脚趾甲的真菌感染就可以得到抑制。

Heels contribute to hammertoes, neuromas (pinched nerves near the ball of the foot), bunions and “pump bump” (a painful bump on the back of the heel), as well as toenail problems.
高跟会导致槌状趾、神经瘤（靠近脚掌的捏神经）、囊炎肿和“Haglund畸形”（脚后跟痛苦的磕碰），以及脚趾甲问题。

You should cut your toenail.
你应该剪一剪你的脚趾甲了。
