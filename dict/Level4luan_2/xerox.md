# 词义

n. 施乐（商标名，美国办公设备制造公司）；影印，静电印刷品
`A Xerox is a machine that can make copies of pieces of paper which have writing or other marks on them`

v. 用静电复印法复印
`If you Xerox a document, you make a copy of it using a Xerox machine`
