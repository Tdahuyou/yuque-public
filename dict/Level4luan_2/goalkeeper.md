# 音标

- 英 `/'gəʊlkiːpə/`
- 美 `/'ɡolkipɚ/`

# 词义

n. 守门员
`the player in a sports team whose job is to try to stop the ball going into the goal`

# 同义

n. 守门员
`netkeeper` `wicket-keeper`

# 同根

- n. `goalie` 守门员（等于goalkeeper）
- n. `goaltender` （足球等运动的）守门员（等于goalkeeper）

# 例句

...the league's two best goalkeepers.
...这届联赛的两名最佳守门员。
