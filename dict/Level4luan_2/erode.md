# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'rəʊd/`
- 美 `/ɪ'rod/`

# 词义

v. 腐蚀，侵蚀
`if the weather erodes rock or soil, or if rock or soil erodes, its surface is gradually destroyed`

# 同义

vt. 腐蚀，侵蚀
`weather` `eat into`

vi. 侵蚀；受腐蚀
`eat into` `burn into`

# 同根

- adj. `eroded` 被侵蚀的，损坏了
- adj. `eroding` 侵蚀的
- adj. `erose` 啮蚀状的；凹凸不平的；不规则形状的
- adj. `erosive` 腐蚀的；冲蚀的；侵蚀性的
- n. `erosion` 侵蚀，腐蚀
- v. `eroded` 侵蚀；损坏（erode的过去分词形式）
- v. `eroding` 侵蚀；磨损（erode的ing形式）

# 例句

The cliffs are being constantly eroded by heavy seas.
悬崖正不断受到汹涌海浪的侵蚀。

The rocks have gradually eroded away.
岩石逐渐风化。
