# 音标

- 英 `/'dɪgnɪfaɪd/`
- 美 `/'dɪɡnɪfaɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 庄严的；有尊严的；高贵的
`behaving in a calm and serious way, even in a difficult situation, which makes people respect you`

v. 使高贵（dignify的过去式）


# 同义

adj. 庄严的；有尊严的；高贵的
`royal` `solemn` `sacred` `majestic` `distinguished`

# 同根

- n. `dignity` 尊严；高贵
- vt. `dignify` 使高贵；增威严；授以荣誉

# 例句

a dignified old lady
庄重的老太太

She made a dignified departure.
她不失尊严地离去。
