# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌnglɑːsɪz/`
- 美 `/'sʌnɡlæsɪz/`

# 词义

`dark glasses that you wear to protect your eyes when the sun is very bright`

# 例句

What do you think of my new sunglasses?
你觉得我的新太阳镜怎么样？

What color should I choose for my sunglasses?
我该挑一副什么颜色的太阳镜呢？

I can’t see her eyes, but I can see her eyebrows above the sunglasses.
我看不到她的眼睛，但是能看到她露在太阳镜上面的眉毛。
