# 音标

- 英 `/skɑːf/`
- 美 `/skɑrf/`

# 词义

`a piece of cloth that you wear around your neck, head, or shoulders, especially to keep warm`



这条围巾(scarf)的手艺(craft)真棒

# 同义

n. [服装]围巾；嵌接，嵌接处；头巾领巾
`muffler` `neckcloth`

# 例句

Hey, your scarf is slipping off.
喂， 你的围巾快掉了。

The scarf flapped her face.
围巾轻拂着她的脸。

She muffled her throat with a scarf.
她用围巾围住脖子。
