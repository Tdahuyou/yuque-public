# 音标

- 英 `/ðiːz/`
- 美 `/ðiz/`

# 词义

`You use these at the beginning of noun groups to refer to someone or something that you have already mentioned or identified`

# 短语

- `of these` 这些
- `these days` 这些天；目前

# 例句

A committee has been formed. These people can make decisions in ten minutes which would take us months.
委员会已经成立。这些人能在10分钟内做出决定，换作我们则要花几个月。
