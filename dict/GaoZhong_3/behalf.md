# 音标

- 英 `/ bɪ'hɑːf /`
- 美 `/bɪ'hæf/`

# 词义

n. 利益；代表
`instead of someone, or as their representative`

# 同义

n. 代表；利益
`representation` `deputy` `benefit` `sake` `profit`

# 短语

- `behalf of` 代表
- `on one's behalf` 为…的利益；代表
- `on behalf of` 代表；为了
- `on our behalf` 代表我方
- `on the behalf of` 代表
- `in behalf of` 为…之利益；代表…
- `on somebody's behalf` 为了某人；代表...一方；为了某人的利益

# 例句

She asked the doctor to speak to her parents on her behalf.
她请医生代她同她父母谈谈。

On behalf of everyone here, may I wish you a very happy retirement.
请允许我代表在座的各位祝你退休后生活愉快。
