# 音标

- 英 `/ weə /`
- 美 `/hweə(r); hwɛr/`

# 词义

adv&conj. 那儿；何处
`used to say that one person, thing, opinion etc is different from another`

# 同义

n. 地点
`location` `site` `spot`

# 短语

- `where it is` [口]问题的关键所在；实际情况

# 例句

Where others might have been satisfied, Dawson had higher ambitions.
别人或许这样就满足了，可道森却有更大的雄心。
