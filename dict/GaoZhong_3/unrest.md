# 音标

- 英 `/ ʌn'rest /`
- 美 `/ʌn'rɛst/`

# 词义

n. 不安；骚动
`a political situation in which people protest or behave violently`



un (不) +rest (休息， 安静) →不安

# 同义

n. 不安；动荡的局面；不安的状态
`tension` `unease`

# 短语

- `social unrest` 社会动荡；社会不安

# 例句

There is growing unrest throughout the country.
全国各地日益动荡不安。
