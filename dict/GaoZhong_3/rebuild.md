# 音标

- 英 `/ ˌriː'bɪld /`
- 美 `/,ri'bɪld/`

# 词义

vt. 重建
`to build something again, after it has been damaged or destroyed`

# 同义

vt. 重建；改造，重新组装；复原
`reconstruct` `reintegrate`

# 同根

- n. `rebuilding` 重建
- v. `rebuilding` 重建；使复原（rebuild的ing形式）

# 例句

The church was completely rebuilt in the last century.
这座教堂在上个世纪全面重建。
