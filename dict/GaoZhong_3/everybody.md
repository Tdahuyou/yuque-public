# 音标

- 英 `/ 'evribɒdi /`
- 美 `/'ɛvrɪbɑdi/`

# 词义

pron. 每人，人人
`everyone`

# 例句

We like everybody to say what they think.
我们喜欢让人人都说出他们的看法。

However it is impossible for us to make friends with everybody.
然而对我们来说是不可能和每个人交朋友。

And yet everybody has certain ideals which determine the direction of his endeavors and his judgments.
可是每个人都有一些理想，这些理想决定着他的努力和判断的方向。
