# 音标

- 英 `/ ˌdaun'steəz /`
- 美 `/'daʊn'stɛrz/`

# 词义

adv. 在楼下，到楼下
`towards or on a lower floor of a building, especially a house`

# 同义

adv. 在楼下
`belowstairs`

# 短语

- `go downstairs` 下楼

# 例句

Rosie ran downstairs to answer the door.
罗茜跑下楼去开门。

Charles was downstairs in the kitchen.
查尔斯在楼下厨房里。
