# 音标

- 英 `/ speɪd /`
- 美 `/sped/`

# 词义

n. 铲子；纸牌中的黑桃
`a  playing card  belonging to the set of cards that have one or more black shapes that look like pointed leaves printed on them`

# 同义

n. 铁锹，铲子
`rabbler`

vt. [建]铲；把……弄实抹平
`shool`

vi. [建]铲
`shovel`

# 同根

- n. `spadeful` 满满一铲子；一满铲

# 短语

- `in spades` [美俚]坦率地；肯定；非常

# 例句

the queen of spades
黑桃王后
