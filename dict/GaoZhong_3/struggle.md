# 音标

- 英 `/ 'strʌgl /`
- 美 `/'strʌɡl/`

# 词义

n&v. 努力，斗争，挣扎
`to be likely to fail, even though you are trying very hard`

# 同义

vi. 奋斗，努力；挣扎
`apply oneself` `labor`

n. 努力，奋斗；竞争
`effort` `competition` `pain` `trial` `war`

# 同根

- adj. `struggling` 奋斗的；努力的；苦斗的
- n. `struggler` 奋斗者；斗争者
- v. `struggling` 努力；斗争；竞争（struggle的ing形式）

# 短语

- `struggle for` 为…奋斗；为…争斗
- `struggle against` 与…作斗争，和…斗争
- `struggle with` 与…斗争
- `class struggle` 阶级斗争
- `power struggle` 权力斗争
- `struggle on` 挣扎下去

# 例句

The team has been struggling all season.
整个赛季球队都打得很艰难。
