# 音标

- 英 `/ 'butʃə /`
- 美 `/ˈbʊtʃər/`

# 词义

vt. 屠宰，残杀
`to kill animals and prepare them to be used as meat`

n. 肉店；屠夫
`a shop where you can buy meat`

# 同义

vt. 屠杀
`put to the sword` `shed blood`

n. 屠夫
`slaughterer` `flesher`

# 同根

- adj. `butcherly` 残忍的；笨手笨脚的
- adv. `butcherly` 如屠夫地；残忍地
- n. `butchery` 屠场；肉贩；肉食店

# 例句

Pigs were butchered, hams were hung to dry from the ceiling.
猪被宰杀了，火腿吊在屋顶下风干。
