# 音标

- 英 `/ 'ʃɔːtkʌmɪŋ /`
- 美 `/'ʃɔrtkʌmɪŋ/`

# 词义

n. [C]缺点，短处
`a fault or weakness that makes someone or something less successful or effective than they should be`

# 同义

n. 缺点；短处
`defect` `deficiency` `disadvantage` `vice` `fault`

# 例句

Peter was painfully aware of his own shortcomings.
彼得痛苦地认识到了自己的短处。
