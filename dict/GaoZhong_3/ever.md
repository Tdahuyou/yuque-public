# 音标

- 英 `/ 'evə /`
- 美 `/'ɛvɚ/`

# 词义

adv. 曾经；无论何时
`You use ever in expressions such as "did you ever" and "have you ever" to express surprise or shock at something you have just seen, heard, or experienced, especially when you expect people to agree with you`

# 同义

adv. 永远；曾经；究竟
`forever` `always` `once`

# 同根

- adv. `evermore` 永远，始终；今后

# 短语

- `for ever` 永远；总是
- `ever since` adv. 从那时到现在
- `as ever` 一直，依旧
- `ever more` 永远；始终
- `never ever` 绝不，从不
- `for ever and ever` 永远
- `ever so` 及其，非常
- `ever a` 任何一个
- `ever so much` 非常；万分

# 例句

Have you ever seen anything like it?
你可曾见过像这样的东西？
