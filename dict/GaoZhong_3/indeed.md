# 音标

- 英 `/ ɪn'diːd /`
- 美 `/ɪn'did/`

# 词义

adv. 确实，实在
`used to emphasize a statement or answer`

# 同义

adv. 的确；实在；真正地；甚至
`just` `sure` `even`

# 例句

The blood tests prove that Vince is indeed the father.
验血证明文斯确实是父亲。

‘Would it help if you had an assistant?’ ‘It would indeed.’
“你要是有个助手，会有帮助吗？”“那是肯定的。
