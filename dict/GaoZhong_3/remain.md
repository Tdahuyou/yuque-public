# 音标

- 英 `/ rɪ'meɪn /`
- 美 `/rɪ'men/`

# 词义

vi. 余下，留下
`to stay in the same place without moving away`

v. 保持，仍是
`If someone or something remains in a particular state or condition, they stay in that state or condition and do not change`



re(一再)+main(留)→余下， 留下

# 同义

vi. 保持；依然；留下；剩余；逗留；残存
`stop` `keep up`

n. 遗迹；剩余物，残骸
`debris` `relic`

# 同根

- adj. `remainder` 剩余的；吃剩的
- n. `remainder` [数] 余数，残余；剩余物；其余的人
- vi. `remainder` 廉价出售；削价出售
- vt. `remainder` 廉价出售；削价出售

# 短语

- `remain invincible` 立于不败之地
- `remain silent` 保持沉默
- `remain with` v. 属于
- `remain calm` 保持冷静，保持镇静；面不改色
- `remain of` 剩下；从...留下
- `to remain as` 一直是...；继续是

# 例句

The refugees were allowed to remain in the UK.
难民获准留在英国。

The three men remained silent.
这3个人保持沉默。

The government remained in control.
政府继续控制着局势。
