# 音标

- 英 `/ 'eniweɪ /`
- 美 `/'ɛnɪ'we/`

# 词义

adv. 不管怎样
`You use anyway or anyhow to suggest that a statement is true or relevant in spite of other things that have been said`

# 同义

adv. 无论如何，不管怎样；总之
`however` `anyhow`

# 同根

- adv. `anyhow` 总之；无论如何；不管怎样
- adv. `anywhere` 在任何地方；无论何处
- n. `anywhere` 任何地方

# 例句

I don't know why I settled on Miami, but anyway I did.
我不知道为什么我落户到了迈阿密，但不管怎样我做了。
