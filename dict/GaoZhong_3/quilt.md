# 音标

- 英 `/ kwɪlt /`
- 美 `/kwɪlt/`

# 词义

n. 加衬芯床罩
`
A

quilt

is a thin cover filled with feathers or some other warm, soft material, which you put over your blankets when you are in bed.
 `



撤去床罩(quilt)，准备睡觉，不许说话，保持安静(quiet)

# 同义

n. 被子；棉被
`bedquilt` `comforter`

# 同根

- n. `quilting` 被上缝花纹的缝法；缝被子

# 短语

- `quilt cover` 被套，被罩
- `patchwork quilt` 拼布床单；用小块布缝缀的被罩
