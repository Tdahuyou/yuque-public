# 音标

- 英 `/ ləuf /`
- 美 `/lof/`

# 词义

n. 一条
`bread that is shaped and baked in one piece and can be cut into slices`

# 同义

n. 条，一条面包；块；游荡
`bar` `piece` `block` `mass`

vt. 游荡；游手好闲；虚度光阴
`louse around` `potter about`

vi. 游荡；游手好闲；虚度光阴
`louse around` `potter about`

# 同根

- n. `loafer` 游手好闲的人；拖鞋

# 短语

- `loaf of bread` 长面包，面包片
- `meat loaf` 肉糜卷；肉馅糕

# 例句

a loaf of bread 
一条面包
