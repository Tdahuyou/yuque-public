# 音标

- 英 `/ ə'pendɪks /`
- 美 `/ə'pɛndɪks/`

# 词义

n. 附录，附属品；阑尾
`a small organ near your  bowel  , which has little or no use`



ap(表加强)+pend(悬挂)+ix→挂在书后面的东西→附录

# 同义

n. [图情]附录；阑尾；附加物
`supplement` `Appendix Appx`

# 同根

- adj. `appendant` 附属的；附加的
- n. `appendectomy` [外科] 阑尾切除术
- n. `appendant` 附属物；附属品
- n. `appendicectomy` 阑尾切除术

# 短语

- `vermiform appendix` n. 阑尾；蚓突

# 例句

Christine had to go into hospital to have her appendix out (=  have it removed  ) .
克里丝蒂娜不得不去医院切除阑尾。
