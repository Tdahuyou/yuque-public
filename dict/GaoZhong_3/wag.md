# 音标

- 英 `/ wæg /`
- 美 `/wæɡ/`

# 词义

v. 摇动，摆动
`if a dog wags its tail, or if its tail wags, the dog moves its tail many times from one side to the other`

# 同义

vt. 摇摆；摇动；饶舌
`rock` `shake`

vi. 摆动；喋喋不休；蹒跚而行
`to oscillate` `to swing`

n. 摇摆；爱说笑打趣的人
`swing` `ricketiness`

# 短语

- `wag its tail` （狗）摇尾巴

# 例句

‘You naughty girl!’ Mom said, wagging her finger at me.
“你这个调皮的丫头！”妈妈对我摇了摇手指说道。
