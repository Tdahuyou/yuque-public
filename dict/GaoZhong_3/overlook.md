# 音标

- 英 `/ ˌəuvə'luk /`
- 美 `/,ovɚ'lʊk/`

# 词义

vt. 俯瞰；远眺
`if a house, room etc overlooks something, it has a view of it, usually from above`



来自词组look over (从…上面看过去)

# 同义

vt. 忽略；俯瞰；远眺；检查；高耸于…之上
`forget` `examine` `survey` `review` `inspect`

n. 忽视；眺望
`inobservancy` `negligence`

# 例句

Our room overlooks the ocean.
我们的房间可以俯视大海。
