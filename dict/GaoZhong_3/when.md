# 音标

- 英 `/ wen /`
- 美 `/hwen; hwɛn/`

# 词义

adv. 在那时
`at or during the time that something happens`

conj. 什么时候；当…的时候


# 同义

conj. 考虑到；既然；当…时
`considering` `since` `as` `now`

n. 时间
`time` `zeit` `termin`

# 例句

Leonard was nine when his father died.
父亲去世时伦纳德九岁。

When the family came here from Russia, they were penniless.
那家人从俄罗斯迁来这里的时候身无分文。

When he was in the air force he flew Tornado jets.
他在空军服役时驾驶旋风式战斗机。
