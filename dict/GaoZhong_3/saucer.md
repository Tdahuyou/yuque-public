# 音标

- 英 `/ 'sɔːsə /`
- 美 `/'sɔsɚ/`

# 词义

n. 茶碟；小圆盘
`A saucer is a small curved plate on which you stand a cup`

# 同义

n. 茶托，浅碟；浅碟形物；眼睛
`eye` `optic`

# 短语

- `flying saucer` 飞碟
- `cup and saucer` 杯子碟子

# 例句

Rae's coffee cup clattered against the saucer as she picked it up.
蕾把咖啡杯拿起来的时候杯子碰响了茶碟。
