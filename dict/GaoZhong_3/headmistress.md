# 音标

- 英 `/ ˌhed'mɪstrəs /`
- 美 `/ˌhɛdˈmistrəs/`

# 词义

n. 女校长
`a female teacher who is in charge of a school`

# 同义

n. 女校长
`schoolmistress` `presidentess`

# 同根

- n. `headmaster` 校长

# 例句

She is the deputy headmistress of the school.
她是那所学校的代理校长。

I was called to account for my conduct by the headmistress.
我因为行为不检点而受到女校长的斥责。
