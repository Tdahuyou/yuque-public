# 音标

- 英 `/ ˌɔːltə'geðə /`
- 美 `/,ɔltə'ɡɛðɚ/`

# 词义

adv. 总共
`used to show that you are referring to the total amount`

# 同义

adv. 完全地；总共；总而言之
`totally` `entirely` `completely` `absolutely` `thoroughly` `strictly` `fully`

n. 整个；裸体
`birthday suit` `entireness`

# 例句

There were five people altogether.
总共有五个人。

How much do I owe you altogether?
我总共欠你多少钱？
