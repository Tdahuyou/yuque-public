# 音标

- 英 `/də(ʊ)'neɪt/`
- 美 `/'donet/`

# 词义

vt. 捐赠;赠送
`to give something, especially money, to a person or an organization in order to help them`



don(给予) ＋ ate(做) → 赠送

# 同义

vt. 捐赠；[经]捐献
`make a contribution to` `endow`

vi. 捐赠；[经]捐献
`give` `make a contribution to`

n. 捐赠；[经]捐献
`contribution` `endowment` `subscription`

# 同根

- adj. `donated` 捐赠的
- n. `donation` 捐款，捐赠物；捐赠
- v. `donated` 捐赠（donate的过去分词形式）

# 短语

- `donate money` 捐款

# 例句

He frequently donates large sums to charity.
他经常向慈善机构大笔捐款。
