# 音标

- 英 `/eə'rəʊbɪks/`
- 美 `/ɛ'robɪks/`

# 词义

n. 有氧运动法，增氧健身法
`a very active type of physical exercise done to music, usually in a class`



aero(空气) + b + ics(…活动) → 有氧运动

# 同根

- adj. `aerobic` 需氧的；增氧健身法的

# 例句

Water aerobics can be performed just about anywhere; from health and fitness clubs to public or private swimming pools to even shallow lakes. 
水中有氧运动没有地方限制，从健康和健身俱乐部，到公共或私人泳池，甚至浅水湖泊，都可以进行；

They not only do it with great workout equipment and well-designed aerobics classes, but with answers to every detail of the working professional's personal needs.
他们不仅有优质的健身器材，精心设计的有氧运动课程，而且对个人专业健身问题和需求也作出详尽的解答。
