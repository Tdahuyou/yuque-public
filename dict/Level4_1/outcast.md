# 音标

- 英 `/ˈaʊtkɑ:st/`
- 美 `/'aʊtkæst/`

# 词义

n. 被逐出者
`someone who is not accepted by the people they live among, or who has been forced out of their home`

# 同义

n. 流浪的人；被驱逐的人
`transportee`

adj. 被遗弃的；无家可归的；被逐出的
`homeless` `forlorn`

# 例句

Smokers often feel as though they are being treated as social outcasts.
吸烟者常常觉得自己遭到社会排斥。
