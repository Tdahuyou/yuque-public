# 音标

- 英 `/blɔ'keid/`
- 美 `/blɑ'ked/`

# 词义

n. 封锁
`the surrounding of an area by soldiers or ships to stop people or supplies entering or leaving`

# 同义

vt. 封锁
`blank off` `close off`

n. 阻塞
`jam-up` `snarl-up`

# 同根

- adj. `blocked` 堵塞的；被封锁的
- n. `blocking` 阻塞；封闭；大块
- v. `blocked` 阻塞（block的过去分词）
- v. `blocking` 阻塞（block的ing形式）

# 短语

- `run the blockade` [美国英语]偷越封锁线

# 例句

a naval blockade
海上封锁

They’ve imposed an economic blockade on the country.
他们对该国实行了经济封锁。

an agreement to lift the blockade
解除封锁的协议
