# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃn/`
- 美 `/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃn/`

# 词义

n. 抑制
`a feeling of shyness or embarrassment that stops you doing or saying what you really want`

# 同义

n. [心理][电子][通信]抑制；压抑；禁止
`checking` `control` `restraint` `suppression` `curb`

# 同根

- adj. `inhibitory` 禁止的，抑制的
- n. `inhibitor` [助剂] 抑制剂，抗化剂；抑制者
- vt. `inhibit` 抑制；禁止

# 短语

- `inhibition zone` 抑菌圈；抑制带
- `competitive inhibition` 竞争性抑制；竞争性抑制作用

# 例句

She had no inhibitions about saying what she felt.
她毫不顾忌地说出自己的感觉。

People tend to lose their inhibitions when they’ve drunk a lot of alcohol.
人们在喝多了酒之后往往会无所顾忌。
