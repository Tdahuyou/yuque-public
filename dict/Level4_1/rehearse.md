# 音标

- 英 `/rɪˈhɜ:s/`
- 美 `/rɪ'hɝs/`

# 词义

v. 彩排，预演
`to practise or make people practise something such as a play or concert in order to prepare for a public performance`

# 同义

vt. 排练；预演
`dry run` `go though`

vi. 排练；演习
`dry run` `go though`

# 同根

- n. `rehearsal` 排演；预演；练习；训练；叙述

# 例句

I think we need to rehearse the first scene again.
我想我们需要把第一场再排练一次。
