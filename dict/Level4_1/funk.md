# 音标

- 英 `/fʌŋk/`
- 美 `/fʌŋk/`

# 词义

n. 乡土音乐
`a style of music with a strong  rhythm  that is based on  jazz  and African music`

# 同义

n. 恐惧；怯懦；恐怖；臭味
`fear` `terror` `smell`

vt. 害怕；畏缩；使闻到臭味
`be nervous of` `be afraid to`

vi. 惊恐；畏缩；发出臭味
`recoil from` `shrink back from`

# 同根

- adj. `funky` 时髦的；畏缩的；恶臭的

# 短语

- `blue funk` n. 恐惧；精神紧张
- `in a blue funk` adv. 惊恐万状

# 例句

...a mixture of experimental jazz, soul and funk.
...实验性爵士乐、灵魂乐和疯克乐的混合。
