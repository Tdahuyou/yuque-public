# 音标

- 英 `/ˈəʊvl/`
- 美 `/'ovl/`

# 词义

adj. 椭圆形的
`Oval things have a shape that is like a circle but is wider in one direction than the other`

# 同义

adj. 椭圆的；[数]卵形的
`elliptical` `ovate`

n. 椭圆形；[数]卵形
`ellipse` `elliptical shape`

# 短语

- `oval office` 美国总统办公室
- `oval window` 卵圆窗

# 例句

...the small oval framed picture of a little boy.
…一个小男孩的镶在椭圆形框架里的小幅照片。
