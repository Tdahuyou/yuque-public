# 音标

- 英 `/kəmˈpæniənʃɪp/`
- 美 `/kəm'pænjən'ʃɪp/`

# 词义

n. 陪伴
`Companionship is having someone you know and like with you, instead of being on your own`

# 同义

n. 友谊；陪伴；交谊
`friendship` `fellowship`

# 同根

- adj. `companionable` 好交往的；友善的；适于做朋友的
- n. `company` 公司；陪伴，同伴；连队
- n. `companion` 同伴；朋友；指南；手册
- vi. `company` 交往
- vt. `company` 陪伴
- vt. `companion` 陪伴

# 例句

I depended on his companionship and on his judgment.
我依赖他的陪伴和他的判断。
