# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'trʌstfl/`
- 美 `/dis'trʌstful/`

# 词义

adj. 怀疑的；不信任的；可疑的
`If you are distrustful of someone or something, you think that they are not honest, reliable, or safe`

# 同义

adj. 怀疑的；不信任的；可疑的
`suspicious` `skeptical`

# 同根

- n. `distrust` 不信任
- vt. `distrust` 不信任

# 短语

- `distrustful of` 怀疑…，对…不相信，认为…靠不住

# 例句

Voters are deeply distrustful of all politicians.
选民们对所有政客都极端不信任。
