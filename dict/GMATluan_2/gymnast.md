# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒɪmnæst/`
- 美 `/'dʒɪmnæst/`

# 词义

n. 体操运动员
`someone who is good at gymnastics and competes against other people in gymnastics competitions`



gym(健身) + nast → 体操运动员

# 同根

- adj. `gymnastic` 体操的，体育的
- n. `gymnastics` 体操；体育；体操运动

# 例句

Nora was an excellent gymnast in this country.
诺拉是这个国家卓越的体操运动员。

I was 26 by then -- which is ancient for a male gymnast.
我那时26岁，作为一名男子体操运动员已经太老了。

Ah, the perspective of the proud gymnast.
嘿，自以为是的体操运动员的观点。
