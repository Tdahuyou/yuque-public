# 词义

n. 彩虹色




irid(＝iris虹光) ＋ escence → 彩虹色

# 同根

- adj. `iridescent` 彩虹色的；闪光的

# 例句

Tiny layers in the wing scales of blue morpho butterflies reflect light at multiple angles, leading to the interference patterns we see as iridescence.
蓝色大闪蝶翅膀上的细微层状结构会以不同角度反射光线，从而呈现出彩虹一般的干涉图样来。

The huge pool of blood in front of her was already assuming the iridescence of coagulation; and when the sun rose a hundred prismatic hues were reflected from it.
她面前的一摊血水已经凝固了，宛如彩虹的色彩；当太阳升起来时，上面就反射出一百种光谱的颜色。
