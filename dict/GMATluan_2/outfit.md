# 音标

- 英 `/'aʊtfɪt/`
- 美 `/'aʊtfɪt/`

# 词义

n. 机构；用具；全套装备
`a set of clothes worn together, especially for a special occasion`

v. 得到装备
`to provide someone or something with a set of clothes or equipment, especially ones that are needed for a particular purpose`

# 同义

n. 机构；用具；全套装备
`organization` `machinery` `tool` `house`

vt. 配备；供应
`serve` `accommodate`

# 同根

- n. `outfitting` 码头舾装；舾装设备
- n. `outfitter` [贸易] 旅行用品商；运动用具商；[贸易] 装饰用品商店；机器安装工
- v. `outfitting` 配备；供应（outfit的ing形式）

# 例句

She bought a new outfit for the party.
她为了这次派对买了套新衣服。

a cowboy outfit
一套牛仔服装
