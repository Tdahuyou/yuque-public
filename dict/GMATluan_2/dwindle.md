# 音标

- 英 `/'dwɪnd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'dwɪndl/`

# 词义

v. 减少；变小
`to gradually become less and less or smaller and smaller`



d + wind(风) + le → 随风而去越来越小 → 变小

# 同义

vi. 减少；变小
`to cut down` `fall off`

vt. 使缩小，使减少
`shrink` `astrict`

# 例句

The elephant population is dwindling.
大象的数量在减少。

His money had dwindled away.
他的钱在逐渐变少。
