# 音标

- 英 `/pə'rəʊl/`
- 美 `/pə'rol/`

# 词义

n. 语言；誓言，诺言；释放宣言
`If a prisoner is given parole, he or she is released before the official end of their prison sentence and has to promise to behave well`

v. 有条件释放，假释；使假释出狱；宣誓后释放
`to allow someone to leave prison on the condition that they promise to behave well`

# 同义

n. 语言；誓言，诺言；释放宣言
`language` `word` `tongue` `engagement`

# 短语

- `on parole` 获得假释
- `out on parole` [美国俚语](女子)离了婚的

# 例句

Although sentenced to life, he will become eligible for parole after serving 10 years.
尽管被判无期，服刑10年后他将有资格获得假释。

...a parole violation.
…一次假释违纪。
