# 音标

- 英 `/,spekjʊ'leɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,spɛkju'leʃən/`

# 词义

n. 投机；推测；思索；投机买卖
`when you guess about the possible causes or effects of something without knowing all the facts, or the guesses that you make`



来自speculate(v. 思索； 推测)

# 同义

n. 投机；推测；思索；投机买卖
`theory` `guess` `projection`

# 同根

- adj. `speculative` 投机的；推测的；思索性的
- n. `speculator` 投机者；思索者
- n. `speculativeness` 思辩
- vi. `speculate` 推测；投机；思索
- vt. `speculate` 推断

# 短语

- `speculation about` n. 关于…猜测

# 例句

The witness’s statement was pure speculation (= not based on any facts ) .
这名证人的陈述纯粹是猜测。
