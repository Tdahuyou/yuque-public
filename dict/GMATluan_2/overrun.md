# 音标

- 英 `/əʊvə'rʌn/`
- 美 `/'ovərʌn/`

# 词义

n. 泛滥成灾；超出限度
`Overrun is also a noun`

v. 泛滥；超过；蹂躏
`if unwanted things or people overrun a place, they spread over it in great numbers`



来自词组run over(泛滥)

# 同义

vt. 泛滥；超过；蹂躏
`better than` `top`

vi. 泛滥；蔓延
`creep` `to spread`

# 短语

- `overrun oneself` 奔跑过度而伤害身体

# 例句

cost overruns of £2 billion
20亿英镑的超额费用
