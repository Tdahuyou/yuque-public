# 音标

- 英 `/ɪnˈvɪʒn/`
- 美 `/ɪn'vɪʒn/`

# 词义

v. 想象；预想
`to imagine something that you think might happen in the future, especially something that you think will be good`



en + vis(看) + ion → 用心去看 → 想像

# 同义

vt. 想象；预想
`vision` `image`

# 例句

I envisioned a future of educational excellence.
我展望了一个有着优质教育的未来。
