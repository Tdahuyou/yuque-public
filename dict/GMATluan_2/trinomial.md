# 音标

- 英 `/traɪ'nəʊmɪəl/`
- 美 `/traɪ'nomɪəl/`

# 词义

n. [数]三项式；[生物]三名法
`a polynomial consisting of three terms, such as ax² + bx + c `

adj. 三项式的
`consisting of or relating to three terms `
