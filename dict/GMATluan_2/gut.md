# 音标

- 英 `/gʌt/`
- 美 `/ɡʌt/`

# 词义

n. 内脏；肠子；剧情；胆量；海峡；勇气；直觉；肠
`a reaction or feeling that you are sure is right, although you cannot give a reason for it`

v. 取出内脏；摧毁（建筑物等）的内部
`to remove the organs from inside a fish or animal in order to prepare it for cooking`

adj. 简单的；本质的，根本的；本能的，直觉的
`A gut feeling is based on instinct or emotion rather than reason`

# 同义

n. 内脏；肠子；剧情；胆量；海峡
`courage` `inside` `neck` `channel` `narrow`

vt. 取出内脏；摧毁内部装置
`eviscerate`

adj. 简单的；本质的，根本的
`simple` `organic` `plain` `fundamental` `essential`

# 同根

- adj. `gutless` 无胆量的；没勇气的；没有生气的

# 短语

- `gut feeling` 直觉

# 例句

He had a gut feeling that Sarah was lying.
他凭直觉感到萨拉在撒谎。

Let's have your gut reaction to the facts as we know them.
请告诉我们你对这些我们了解的事实的直觉反应。
