# 音标

- 英 `/præŋk/`
- 美 `/præŋk/`

# 词义

n. 恶作剧，开玩笑；戏谑
`a trick, especially one which is played on someone to make them look silly`

v. 装饰；打扮
`to dress or decorate showily or gaudily `



不要和plank(n. 厚木板)相混

# 同义

n. 恶作剧，开玩笑；戏谑
`mischief` `trick`

vt. 装饰；打扮
`paint` `hang` `dress oneself` `array` `trick out`

vi. 炫耀自己；胡闹
`play the peacock` `monkey business`

# 同根

- adj. `prankish` 恶作剧的
- n. `prankster` 爱开玩笑的人；顽皮的人；恶作剧的人

# 短语

- `play pranks` ◎戏弄，开玩笑

# 例句

a childish prank
孩子气的恶作剧
