# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'pɪkʃn/`
- 美 `/dɪ'pɪkʃən/`

# 词义

n. 描写，叙述
`A depiction of something is a picture or a written description of it`



来自depict(v. 描述)

# 同义

n. 描写，叙述
`description` `relation` `statement` `telling` `narrative`

# 同根

- adj. `depictive` 描写的；描述的
- vt. `depict` 描述；描画

# 例句

You would think that such western imports would champion any depiction of homosexuality on television, right?
你会想，像这些西方进口会拥护电视媒体对同性恋的描写，对吧？
