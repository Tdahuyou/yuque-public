There, scientists have been manipulating the levels of bone-morphogenetic protein or BMP in the brains of laboratory mice.

AIM: To explore the expression of bone morphogenetic protein (BMP) in transplantation healing with massive bone allograft and the significance by animal experiment.
目的：通过动物实验来探讨骨形态发生蛋白在大段同种异体骨移植愈合中的表达及其意义。
