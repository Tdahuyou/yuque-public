# 音标

- 英 `/,mɒnə(ʊ)njuːklɪ'əʊsɪs/`
- 美 `/,mɑno,nuklɪ'osɪs/`

# 词义

n. 单核细胞增多症；单核白血球增多症
`Mononucleosis is a disease which causes swollen glands, fever, and a sore throat`

# 同根

- adj. `mononuclear` [细胞] 单核的
- adj. `mononucleate` 细胞等单核的
- n. `mononuclear` 单核细胞

# 短语

- `infectious mononucleosis` 传染性单核细胞增多

# 例句

When the infection is systemic in nature, the lymphadenopathy is also usually generalized, as in tuberculosis, brucellosis, infectious mononucleosis, hepatitis, fungal infections, and toxoplasmosis.
当感染的性质是全市性的，淋巴结病常也是全身的，如在结核病、布鲁菌病、传染性单核细胞增多症、肝炎、霉菌感染和弓形虫病。
