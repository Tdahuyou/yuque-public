# 音标

- 英 `/riː'væmp/`
- 美 `/,ri'væmp/`

# 词义

v. 修补；翻新；修改
`to change something in order to improve it and make it seem more modern`

n. 改进；换新鞋面
`Revamp is also a noun`



re(重新) + vamp(修补) → 修订

# 同义

vt. 修补；翻新；修改
`modify` `piece up`

n. 改进；换新鞋面
`improvement` `reforming` `enrichment`

# 例句

Many older companies are revamping their image.
很多老牌公司都在更新公司形象。

The revamp includes replacing the old navy uniform with a crisp blue and white cotton outfit.
改进的内容包括将旧海军制服换成清新的蓝白相间的棉套装。
