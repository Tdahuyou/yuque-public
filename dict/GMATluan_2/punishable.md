# 音标

- 英 `/'pʌnɪʃəbl/`
- 美 `/'pʌnɪʃəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 可罚的；该罚的
`If a crime is punishable in a particular way, anyone who commits it is punished in that way`



来自punish(v. 惩罚)

# 同义

adj. 可罚的；该罚的
`condemnable`

# 同根

- adj. `punishing` 严厉的；打击沉重的；精疲力尽的
- n. `punishment` 惩罚；严厉对待，虐待
- v. `punishing` 惩罚；折磨（punish的ing形式）
- vi. `punish` 惩罚
- vt. `punish` 惩罚；严厉对待；贪婪地吃喝

# 例句

Treason in this country is still punishable by death.
在该国，叛国仍是死罪。
