# 词义

v. 再投资于；再授给
`to use money you have earned from  investment s  to buy additional investments`



re(再) + invest(投资) → 再投资

# 同义

vi. 再投资
`plough back`

# 例句

The central bank said interest rates would remain on hold at 0 to 0.25 per cent for an “extended period” and it would continue to reinvest in its securities portfolio.
美联储表示，利率将在“相当长一段时间”内继续维持在零到0.25%的水平，而且它将继续将投资所得再投资于它的证券组合。
