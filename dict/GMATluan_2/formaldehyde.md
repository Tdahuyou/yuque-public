# 音标

- 英 `/fɔː'mældɪhaɪd/`
- 美 `/fɔr'mældɪhaɪd/`

# 词义

n. 蚁醛，[有化]甲醛
`a strong-smelling gas that can be mixed with water and used for preserving things such as dead animals to be used in science etc`

# 同义

n. 蚁醛，[有化]甲醛
`methanal`

# 短语

- `urea formaldehyde` 脲醛；尿素甲醛
- `urea formaldehyde resin` 脲醛树脂；尿素树脂；脲醛尸
- `formaldehyde solution` 甲醛溶液；福尔马林
- `melamine formaldehyde resin` 三聚氰胺甲醛树脂
- `sodium formaldehyde sulfoxylate` 甲醛次硫酸氢钠

# 例句

frogs preserved in formaldehyde
保存在甲醛中的青蛙
