# 音标

- 英 `/'ræʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'ræʃən/`

# 词义

v. 配给；定量供应
`to control the supply of something because there is not enough`

n. 定量；口粮；配给量
`a fixed amount of something that people are allowed to have when there is not enough, for example during a war`



rat(计算) + ion → 计算着给 → 配给

# 同义

vt. 配给；[经管]定量供应
`admeasure` `allowance`

n. 定量；口粮；配给量
`quantification` `definite quantity`

# 同根

- adj. `rational` 合理的；理性的
- n. `rational` 有理数
- n. `rationing` [计划] 定量配给
- vi. `rationalize` 实行合理化；作辩解；合理地思考
- vt. `rationalize` 使……合理化；使……有理化；为……找借口

# 短语

- `grain ration` 口粮

# 例句

Fuel was rationed during the war.
战争期间，燃油定量供应。
