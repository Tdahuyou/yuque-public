# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'kluːs/`
- 美 `/'rɛklus/`

# 词义

n. 隐士；隐居者
`someone who chooses to live alone, and does not like seeing or talking to other people`

adj. 隐居的




re + cluse(关闭) → 重新把门关上 → 隐居的

# 同义

n. 隐士；隐居者
`hermit` `anchorite`

adj. 隐居的
`cloistered`

# 同根

- adj. `reclusive` 隐居的；隐遁的

# 例句

She became a recluse after her two sons were murdered.
两个儿子被杀后，她就一直离群索居。
