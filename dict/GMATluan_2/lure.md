# 音标

- 英 `/l(j)ʊə/`
- 美 `/lʊr/`

# 词义

n. 诱惑；饵；诱惑物
`something that attracts people, or the quality of being able to do this`

v. 诱惑；引诱
`to persuade someone to do something, especially something wrong or dangerous, by making it seem attractive or exciting`



纯(pure)属诱惑(lure)

# 同义

n. 诱惑；饵；诱惑物
`temptation` `gentle`

vt. 诱惑；引诱
`induce` `tempt`

# 例句

The excitement of hunting big game in Africa has been a lure to Europeans for 200 years.
在非洲捕捉大型猎物所带来的刺激，200年来一直吸引着欧洲人。
