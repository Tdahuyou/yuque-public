# 音标

- 英 `/liːtʃ/`
- 美 `/litʃ/`

# 词义

n. 过滤；过滤器
`the act or process of leaching `

v. 过滤；萃取
`if a substance leaches or is leached from a larger mass such as the soil, it is removed from it by water passing through the larger mass`



和beach(n. 海滩)一起记

# 同义

n. 过滤；过滤器
`filtration` `percolation`

vt. 过滤；萃取
`filter` `lauter`

# 同根

- n. `leaching` [矿业] 沥滤
- v. `leaching` [矿业][冶] 浸出（leach的ing形式）；[土壤] 淋洗；滤取；滤去

# 短语

- `leach liquor` 沥滤液，浸出液；浸提液

# 例句

Spreading wastewater over fields, and allowing it to leach back through the soil into local waterways, turns out to be a reasonable way to purify it.
将废水浇灌到田里，这些水经过土壤的过滤后又重新流回当地的水系，这已经被证明是非常合理的污水净化方法。
