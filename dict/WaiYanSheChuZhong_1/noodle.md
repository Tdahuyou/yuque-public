# 音标

- 英 `/'nuːd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'nudl/`

# 词义

n. 面条
`a long thin piece of food made from a mixture of flour, water, and eggs, usually cooked in soup or boiling water`

# 同义

n. 面条；笨蛋
`simple` `turkey` `put`

# 短语

- `instant noodle` 方便面；速食面；泡面
- `rice noodle` 米线
- `noodle soup` 面汤，清汤面条
- `fried noodle` 炒面；乌东面

# 例句

Serve the meat with rice or noodles.
肉配米饭或面条一起上。
