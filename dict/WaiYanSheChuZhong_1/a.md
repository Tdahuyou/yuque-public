# 音标

- 英 `/ə; eɪ/`
- 美 `/ə; e/`

# 词义

art. 一（个，件⋯⋯）
`used to show that you are talking about someone or something that has not been mentioned before, or that your listener does not know about`

# 例句

We have a problem.
我们遇到了一个麻烦。

There was a hole in the fence.
篱笆上有一个洞。

Suddenly they heard a loud bang.
他们突然听到砰的一声巨响。
