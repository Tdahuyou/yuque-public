# 音标

- 英 `/jes/`
- 美 `/jɛs/`

# 词义

adv. 是，是的
`used as an answer to say that you want something or want to do something`

# 同义

adv. 是
`yeah` `yep` `yeh`

n. 同意
`agreement` `Ok` `leave` `Okay` `consent`

# 短语

- `oh yes` 哦是的
- `if yes` 如果是
- `yes or no` 或否；是或不是
- `yes and no` 既肯定又否定
- `yes i do` 是的我做到了

# 例句

‘Would you like a sandwich?’ ‘Yes, please.’
“你想来个三明治吗？”“好的，谢谢。”

‘Would you like to come with us?’ ‘Yes, I’d love to.’
“你想和我们一起去吗？”“好的，我很乐意。”
