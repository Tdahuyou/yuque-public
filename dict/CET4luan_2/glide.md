# 音标

- 英 `/glaɪd/`
- 美 `/ɡlaɪd/`

# 词义

v. 滑动
`to move smoothly and quietly, as if without effort`

n. 滑行
`a smooth quiet movement that seems to take no effort`

# 同义

n. 滑翔；滑行；滑移；滑音
`slippage` `portamento`

vt. 滑翔；滑行；悄悄地走；消逝
`ebb away`

# 同根

- adj. `gliding` 滑行的；流畅的；滑顺的
- n. `glider` [航] 滑翔机；滑翔员；滑翔导弹
- v. `gliding` 滑翔；消逝；溜走（glide的ing形式）

# 例句

Swans went gliding past.
天鹅滑行而过。

the graceful glide of a skater
滑冰者优美的滑行动作
