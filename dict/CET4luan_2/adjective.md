# 音标

- 英 `/'ædʒɪktɪv/`
- 美 `/'ædʒɪktɪv/`

# 词义

n. 形容词
`a word that describes a noun or  pronoun. In the phrase ‘black hat’, ‘black’ is an adjective and in the sentence ‘It makes her happy’, ‘happy’ is an adjective.`

# 同义

adj. [语]形容词的；从属的
`dependent` `under`

# 同根

- adj. `adjectival` 形容词的
- adv. `adjectivally` 作形容词用地；形容词般地

# 例句

an adjectival phrase
形容词短语
