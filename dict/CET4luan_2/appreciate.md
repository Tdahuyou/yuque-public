# 音标

- 英 `/ə'priːʃɪeɪt; -sɪ-/`
- 美 `/ə'priʃɪet/`

# 词义

v. 领会；感谢；欣赏
`to understand how serious or important a situation or problem is or what someone’s feelings are`



ap(表加强) ＋ preci(价值) ＋ ate → 肯定价值 → 赏识

# 同义

vt. 欣赏；感激；领会；鉴别
`enjoy` `see` `grasp`

vi. 增值；涨价
`value added` `rise in price`

# 同根

- adj. `appreciative` 感激的；赏识的；有欣赏力的；承认有价值的
- adv. `appreciatively` 欣赏地，赞赏地；感激地
- n. `appreciation` 欣赏，鉴别；增值；感谢

# 例句

We appreciate that caring for children is an important job.
我们理解，照顾小孩子是个重要的工作。

Thanks ever so much for your help, I really appreciate it.
多谢你的帮助，我真的很感激。

Her abilities are not fully appreciated by her employer.
她的才干没有得到雇主的充分赏识。
