# 音标

- 英 `/gest/`
- 美 `/ɡɛst/`

# 词义

n. 客人，宾客；旅客
`a person that you have invited to your house or to a particular event that you are paying for`

# 同义

n. 客人，[经]宾客；顾客
`customer` `client` `consumer`

vt. 款待，招待
`serve` `invite` `attend`

vi. 作客，寄宿
`board` `stay over`

# 短语

- `guest room` 客房
- `guest house` 宾馆
- `honored guest` 贵宾
- `distinguished guest` 嘉宾；上宾；尊贵的客人
- `special guest` 特别来宾；贵宾
- `guest list` 客户名单
- `guest speaker` 特邀发言人，客席讲者；嘉宾演讲人
- `guest book` 贺客名簿；访客留名簿
- `guest professor` 客座教授
- `be my guest` 别客气，请用；请便
- `guest star` 客串
- `state guest` 国宾
- `guest of honor` 贵宾
- `be our guest` 当我们的贵宾；请随便
- `guest lecturer` 客座教授；特约讲师

# 例句

We have guests staying this weekend.
这周末我家有客人来住。

more than 100 wedding guests
100多位出席婚礼的宾客

We have accommodation for 500 guests.
我们有可供500位客人住宿的房间。

Guests should vacate their rooms by 10.30 a.m.
旅客应在上午10:30以前腾出房间。
