# 音标

- 英 `/'hɪlsaɪd/`
- 美 `/'hɪl'saɪd/`

# 词义

n. （小山）山腰，山坡
`the sloping side of a hill`

# 同义

n. 山坡，山腹；山腰
`mountainside` `brae`

# 例句

The airplane crashed on a hillside.
飞机在山坡上坠毁。

They are quarrying the hillside for marble.
他们正从山坡上采大理石。

They sat and had their lunch on a grassy hillside.
他们坐在长满草的山坡上吃午饭。
