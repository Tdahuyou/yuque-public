# 音标

- 英 `/ˈhæmbɜ:gə/`
- 美 `/hæmbɝ​ɡɚ/`

# 词义

n. 汉堡包，牛肉饼
`a flat round piece of finely cut beef which is cooked and eaten in a bread bun`

# 同义

n. 汉堡包，汉堡；牛肉饼，肉饼；碎牛肉
`burger` `beefburger`

# 例句

The whole stadium stink of the hamburger.
整个体育场内散发着汉堡包的香味。

Avoid unhealthy foods such as hamburger and chips.
不要吃不健康食品，如汉堡和薯条。
