# 音标

- 英 `/ɔːt/`
- 美 `/ɔt/`

# 词义

modal. 应当，应该
`it is morally right to do a particular thing or that it is morally right for a particular situation to exist, especially when giving or asking for advice or opinions.`

# 同义

aux. 应该，应当；大概
`should` `must`

vi. 应该，应当；大概
`would have` `be supposed to`

# 短语

- `ought to do sth` 应该；应该做某事
- `ought to know better` 应该懂得多一些

# 例句

Basically the system ought to have worked.
其实这套系统本应发挥作用的。
