# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃɪvə/`
- 美 `/'ʃɪvɚ/`

# 词义

v. 颤抖，哆嗦
`to shake slightly because you are cold or frightened`

n. 冷颤
`a slight shaking movement of your body caused by cold or fear`



shi(音似she， 她) ＋ ver → 她冻得一直颤抖 → 战栗， 颤抖

# 同义

n. 颤抖，战栗；碎片
`chip` `debris`

vi. 颤抖；哆嗦；打碎
`quake` `judder`

vt. 颤抖；打碎
`quaver` `to break up`

# 同根

- adj. `shivering` 颤抖的
- adj. `shivery` 颤抖的；寒冷的；令人毛骨悚然的
- n. `shivering` 颤抖；小片
- v. `shivering` 颤抖（shiver的ing形式）

# 例句

Jake stood shivering in the cold air.
大冷天里，杰克站着冻得瑟瑟发抖。

A shiver ran through (= went through ) me.
我浑身一阵颤抖。
