# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'gaɪz/`
- 美 `/dɪs'ɡaɪz/`

# 词义

v. 隐瞒，掩埋
`to hide sth or change it, so that it cannot be recognized`

n. 假装
`the art of changing your appearance so that people do not recognize you`



dis(表加强) ＋ guise(伪装) → 伪装； 掩饰

# 同义

vt. 掩饰；假装；隐瞒
`simulate` `come`

n. 伪装；假装；用作伪装的东西
`simulation` `acting`

# 短语

- `in disguise` 伪装；乔装
- `a blessing in disguise` 塞翁失马
- `in the disguise of` 伪装，假扮成；托辞；以……为借口

# 例句

He made no attempt to disguise his agitation.
他无意掩饰他的不安。

He is a master of disguise.
他是伪装高手。
