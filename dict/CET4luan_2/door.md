# 音标

- 英 `/dɔː/`
- 美 `/dɔr/`

# 词义

n. 门；通道；一家
`a piece of wood, glass, etc. that is opened and closed so that people can get in and out of a room, building, car, etc.; a similar thing in a cupboard/closet`

# 同义

n. [建]门；家，户；门口；通道
`home` `exit` `gateway` `channel` `route`

# 短语

- `at the door` 在门边
- `open the door` 开门
- `next door` n. 隔壁邻居
- `door to door` 挨家挨户；门到门
- `front door` n. 前门
- `back door` 后门；非法途径
- `close the door` 关门
- `open door` 门户开放；进入权
- `shut the door` 关门
- `glass door` n. 玻璃门
- `from door to door` 挨家挨户地；自始至终地
- `door lock` 门锁；车门锁
- `garage door` 汽车库门，车库门
- `side door` n. 侧门；旁门
- `wooden door` 木门
- `automatic door` 自动门
- `sliding door` n. 拉门；滑动门
- `knock on the door` 敲门
- `door handle` 门把手
- `slam the door` v. 摒弃；关门；拒绝
