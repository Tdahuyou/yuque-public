# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊɪm/`
- 美 `/'poəm/`

# 词义

n. 诗，韵文，诗体文
`a piece of writing that expresses emotions, experiences, and ideas, especially in short lines using words that  rhyme`

# 同义

n. 诗
`poetry` `verse`

# 短语

- `love poem` 爱情诗
- `narrative poem` 叙事诗；叙述诗
- `prose poem` n. 散文诗
- `symphonic poem` 交响诗

# 例句

I decided to write a poem about how I felt.
我决定把我的感受写成一首诗。
