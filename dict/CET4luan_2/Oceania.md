# 音标

- 英 `/,əʊsɪ'ɑːnɪə; -ʃɪ-/`
- 美 `/,oʃɪ'ænɪə/`

# 词义

n. 大洋洲
`a large region of the world consisting of the Pacific islands and the seas around them`
