# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'mɑːk/`
- 美 `/rɪ'mɑrk/`

# 词义

n. 评论，谈论
`something that you say when you express an opinion or say what you have noticed`

v. 评论，谈论
`to say or write a comment about sth/sb`



re(一再) ＋ mark(做标记) → 一再做标记 → 评论

# 同义

n. 注意；言辞
`regard` `notice` `publicity`

vt. 评论；觉察
`observe on` `to comment on`

vi. 谈论
`talk of` `discourse`

# 同根

- adj. `remarkable` 卓越的；非凡的；值得注意的
- adv. `remarkably` 显著地；非常地；引人注目地

# 短语

- `remark on` 评论；谈论；议论

# 例句

to make a remark
发表评论

Critics remarked that the play was not original.
评论家们指出这部戏剧缺乏创意
