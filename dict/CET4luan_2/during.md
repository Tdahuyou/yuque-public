# 音标

- 英 `/'djʊərɪŋ/`
- 美 `/'dʊrɪŋ/`

# 词义

prep. 在…期间
`from the beginning to the end of a period of time`

# 同义

prep. 在…的时候，在…的期间
`in time of`

# 例句

During the summer she worked as a lifeguard.
夏天她当过救生员。

He slept calmly during the early part of the night.
他上半夜睡得很安稳。

Foxes remain hidden during the day.
狐狸白天一直躲藏着。
