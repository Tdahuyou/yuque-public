# 词义

v. 流出；散发；脱落
`if you shed tears, you cry`

# 例句

She shed no tears when she heard he was dead.
她听到他的死讯时没流一滴眼泪。

The candles shed a soft glow on her face.
蜡烛在她的脸上映着一层柔光。

He strode across the bathroom, shedding wet clothes as he went.
他大步走过浴室，边走边把湿衣服脱下来。
