# 词义

n. 骚乱；混乱，杂乱
`a situation in which a lot of people behave in an uncontrolled, noisy, or violent way in public`

# 例句

A number of stadiums were closed because of crowd disorder.
因发生群众骚乱，一些体育场被关闭了。

His whole system was thrown into disorder.
他的全套系统被搞得乱七八糟。
