# 音标

- 英 `/bəʊθ/`
- 美 `/boθ/`

# 词义

pron. 两者（都）
`used to talk about two people, things etc together, and emphasize that each is included`

# 同义

adj. 两个的；两者的
`two`

adv. 并；又；两者皆
`again` `additionally`

# 短语

- `both of them` 他们俩都…
- `both and` 两个都；既...又...
- `both the two` 两者；并

# 例句

Both Helen’s parents are doctors.
海伦的父母都是医生。
