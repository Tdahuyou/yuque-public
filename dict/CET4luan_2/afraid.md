# 音标

- 英 `/ə'freɪd/`
- 美 `/ə'fred/`

# 词义

adj. 害怕的；担心的
`frightened because you think that you may get hurt or that something bad may happen`

# 同义

adj. 害怕的；恐怕；担心的
`worried` `scared`

# 短语

- `afraid of` 害怕
- `i'm afraid` 我害怕；恐怕

# 例句

There’s no need to be afraid.
没有必要害怕。

He was afraid that the other kids would laugh at him.
他担心其他孩子会嘲笑他。
