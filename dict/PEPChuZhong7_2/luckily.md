# 音标

- 英 `/'lʌkɪlɪ/`
- 美 `/'lʌkɪli/`

# 词义

adv. 幸运地；好运地
`used to say that it is good that something happened or was done because if it had not, the situation would be unpleasant or difficult`

# 同义

adv. 幸好，侥幸；幸运地
`happily` `fortunately`

# 同根

- adj. `lucky` 幸运的；侥幸的
- adj. `luckless` 不幸的，运气不好的
- n. `luck` 运气；幸运；带来好运的东西
- n. `luckiness` 幸运
- vi. `luck` 靠运气，走运；凑巧碰上

# 例句

Luckily the museum was not damaged by the earthquake.
幸好，博物馆没有在地震中遭到破坏。
