# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'kleɪm/`
- 美 `/dɪ'klem/`

# 词义

v. 慷慨陈词；演讲；朗读
`to speak loudly, sometimes with actions, so that people notice you`

# 同义

vi. 慷慨陈词；演讲；朗读
`speak` `give a lecture`

vt. 演讲；慷慨激昂地发表
`give a lecture` `discourse upon`

# 例句

He raised his right fist and declaimed: "Liar and cheat!"
他举起右拳慷慨陈词:"骗子！"
