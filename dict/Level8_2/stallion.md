# 音标

- 英 `/'stæljən/`
- 美 `/'stæljən/`

# 词义

n. 种马；成年公马
`a male horse that is fully grown, especially one that is used for breeding`

# 同义

n. [畜牧]种马；成年公马
`studhorse` `stone-horse`
