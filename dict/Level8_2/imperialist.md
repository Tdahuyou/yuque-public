# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'pɪərɪəlɪst/`
- 美 `/ɪm'pɪrɪəlɪst/`

# 词义

n. 帝国主义者
`An imperialist is someone who has imperialist views`

adj. 帝国主义的
`Imperialist means relating to or based on imperialism`

# 同根

- adj. `imperial` 帝国的；皇帝的；至高无上的；威严的
- adj. `imperialistic` 帝国主义的；帝国主义者的；拥护帝国主义的
- adv. `imperially` 帝王般地；威严地；帝国地
- n. `imperial` 纸张尺寸；特等品
- n. `imperialism` 帝国主义

# 例句

He claims that imperialists are trying to re-establish colonial rule in the country.
他声称帝国主义者正试图在该国重建殖民统治。

The developed nations have all benefited from their imperialist exploitation.
发达国家都从他们帝国主义的剥削中获益。
