# 音标

- 英 `/delv/`
- 美 `/dɛlv/`

# 词义

v. 钻研；探究；挖
`to try to find more information about someone or something`

n. 穴；洞


# 同义

vi. 钻研；探究；挖
`pick` `probe into`

vt. 钻研；探究；挖
`tunnel` `probe into`

n. 穴；洞
`cavity` `hole` `aperture`

# 例句

Tormented by her ignorance, Jenny delves into her mother's past.
珍妮为自己不了解真相而痛苦，她开始深入调查母亲的过去。
