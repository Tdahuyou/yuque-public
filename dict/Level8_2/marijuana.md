# 音标

- 英 `/,mærɪ'hwɑːnə/`
- 美 `/,mærə'wɑnə/`

# 词义

n. 大麻；大麻毒品
`an illegal drug smoked like a cigarette, made from the dried leaves of the  hemp  plant`

# 同义

n. [植][毒物]大麻；大麻毒品
`beng` `ganja`

# 例句

Unfortunately, the US does not discriminate between hemp and marijuana, so growing it for food and fiber in this country is illegal.
不幸的是，美国不区分毒品大麻和能量大麻，因此在该国把它添加它到粮食和用作提取纤维是非法的。
