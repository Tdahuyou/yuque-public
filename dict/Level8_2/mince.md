# 音标

- 英 `/mɪns/`
- 美 `/mɪns/`

# 词义

v. 切碎；矫揉做作地说
`to cut food, especially meat, into very small pieces, usually using a machine`

n. 切碎物，肉馅
`meat, especially  beef  , that has been cut into very small pieces using a special machine`

# 同义

vt. 切碎；矫揉做作地说
`to chop` `to cut up`

vi. 装腔作势；碎步走
`assume` `attitudinize`

# 同根

- adj. `mincing` 装腔作势的；矫饰的
- n. `mincer` 碎肉器（等于mincing machine）；说话吞吐的人
- v. `mincing` 切碎（mince的现在分词）

# 短语

- `mince pie` 肉馅饼

# 例句

minced lamb
碎羔羊肉

Mince the meat up with some onion and garlic.
把肉和些许洋葱、大蒜混合在一起剁碎。
