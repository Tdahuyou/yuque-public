# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'zentfʊl; -f(ə)l/`
- 美 `/rɪ'zɛntfl/`

# 词义

adj. 充满忿恨的；厌恶的
`feeling angry and upset about something that you think is unfair`

# 同义

adj. 充满忿恨的；厌恶的
`sick` `laith`

# 同根

- adv. `resentfully` 充满愤恨地
- n. `resentment` 愤恨，怨恨
- vt. `resent` 怨恨；愤恨；厌恶

# 例句

At first I felt very resentful and angry about losing my job.
起初,我对丢掉工作感到非常怨恨和恼怒。
