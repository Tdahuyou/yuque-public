# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'ʃraɪn; en-/`
- 美 `/ɪn'ʃraɪn/`

# 词义

v. 铭记，珍藏；把…置于神龛内；把…奉为神圣
`if something such as a tradition or right is enshrined in something, it is preserved and protected so that people will remember and respect it`

# 同义

vt. 铭记，珍藏；把…置于神龛内；把…奉为神圣
`brand` `heart`

# 例句

This system is enshrined in our Constitution.
我们的宪法使该制度神圣而不可侵犯。
