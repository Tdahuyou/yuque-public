- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/'ʃɑplɪft/`

# 词义

v. 从商店中偷商品
`If someone shoplifts, they steal goods from a shop by hiding them in a bag or in their clothes`

# 同根

- n. `shoplifting` 入店行窃
- n. `shoplifter` 商店扒手
- v. `shoplifting` 装作顾客在商店偷窃（shoplift的ing形式）

# 例句

He openly shoplifted from a supermarket.
他在一家超市公然行窃。
