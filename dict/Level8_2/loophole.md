# 音标

- 英 `/'luːphəʊl/`
- 美 `/'luphol/`

# 词义

n. 漏洞；枪眼；换气孔；射弹孔
`a small mistake in a law that makes it possible to avoid doing something that the law is supposed to make you do`

# 同义

n. 漏洞；枪眼；换气孔；射弹孔
`leak` `barbican`

# 例句

It is estimated that 60,000 businesses are exploiting a loophole in the law to avoid prosecution.
据估计6万家公司在钻法律的空子来逃避起诉。
