# 音标

- 英 `/kɒmen'deɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,kɑmɛn'deʃən/`

# 词义

n. 推荐；赞扬；奖状
`an official statement praising someone, especially someone who has been brave or very successful`

# 同义

n. 推荐；赞扬；奖状
`praise` `recommendation`

# 同根

- adj. `commendable` 值得赞美的；很好的；可推荐的
- vi. `commend` 称赞；表扬
- vt. `commend` 推荐；称赞；把…委托

# 例句

Go through your files and look for letters of commendation, awards, etc. you earned in your industry.
检查你所有的个人文件，寻找在你的行业中所获得的推荐信，奖励等等。
