# 音标

- 英 `/daʊn'hɑːtɪd/`
- 美 `/,daʊn'hɑrtɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 无精打采的；垂头丧气的
`feeling sad and disappointed, especially because you have tried to achieve something but have failed`

# 同义

adj. 无精打采的；垂头丧气的
`Mondayish` `unanimated`

# 例句

When no replies came, I began to feel downhearted.
一点回应都没有，我开始感到灰心了。
