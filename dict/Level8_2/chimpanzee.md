# 音标

- 英 `/tʃɪmpæn'ziː/`
- 美 `/,tʃɪmpæn'zi/`

# 词义

n. [脊椎]黑猩猩
`an intelligent African animal that is like a large monkey without a tail`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]黑猩猩
`jocko` `pongo`
