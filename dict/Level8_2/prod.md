# 音标

- 英 `/prɒd/`
- 美 `/prɑd/`

# 词义

n. 刺针；刺棒；签子
`a pointed instrument used for pushing animals, to make them move`

v. 刺，戳；刺激
`to quickly push something or someone with your finger or a pointed object`

# 同义

n. 刺针；刺棒；签子
`cnidocil` `bodkin`

vt. 刺，戳；刺激
`stick` `stimulate` `fan` `pink`

vi. 刺；捅
`poke` `run through`

# 同根

- n. `prodding` 促进；刺激
- vt. `prodding` 刺（prod的现在分词）

# 短语

- `on the prod` [美国方言]大发脾气

# 例句

a cattle prod
赶牛刺棒
