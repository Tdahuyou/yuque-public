# 音标

- 英 `/'wɜːlwɪnd/`
- 美 `/'wɝlwɪnd/`

# 词义

n. 旋风；旋风般的快速行动
`an extremely strong wind that moves quickly with a circular movement, causing a lot of damage`

adj. 旋风般的
`a whirlwind situation or event happens very quickly`

# 同义

n. [气象]旋风；旋风般的快速行动
`cyclone` `windspout`

# 例句

a whirlwind romance
旋风式恋爱
