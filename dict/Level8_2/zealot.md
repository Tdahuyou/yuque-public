# 音标

- 英 `/'zelət/`
- 美 `/'zɛlət/`

# 词义

n. 狂热者；犹太教狂热信徒
`someone who has extremely strong beliefs, especially religious or political beliefs, and is too eager to make other people share them`

# 同义

n. 狂热者；犹太教狂热信徒
