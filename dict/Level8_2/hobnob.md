# 音标

- 英 `/'hɒbnɒb/`
- 美 `/'hɑbnɑb/`

# 词义

v. 交谈；共饮；过从甚密；亲切交谈
`to spend time talking to people who are in a higher social position than you`

n. 交谈；共饮


# 同义
