# 音标

- 英 `/wɪtʃ/`
- 美 `/wɪtʃ/`

# 词义

n. 巫婆，女巫
`a woman who is supposed to have magic powers, especially to do bad things`

v. 迷惑；施巫术


# 同义

n. 巫婆，女巫
`carlin` `sorceress`

vt. 迷惑；施巫术
`ensorcel` `bamboozled`

# 同根

- adj. `witching` 有魅力的
- n. `witchery` 巫术；魅力
- n. `witching` 魅力；施行巫术
- v. `witching` 施巫术（witch的ing形式）

# 短语

- `witch doctor` 巫医
- `witch hazel` [植]金缕梅

# 例句

She was swindled out of ￡ 1,000 by a witch.
她被一个女巫骗了一千英镑。

The old witch said she could raise the dead.
老巫婆说她能起死回生。

A witch was supposed to take the shape of a cat.
据说女巫会化为猫的形状。
