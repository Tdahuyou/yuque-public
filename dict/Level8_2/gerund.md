# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒerʌnd/`
- 美 `/'dʒɛrənd/`

# 词义

n. 动名词
`a noun in the form of the  present participle  of a verb, for example ‘shopping’ in the sentence ‘I like shopping’`

# 同义

n. 动名词
`verbal noun` `v-ing`

# 同根

- adj. `gerundial` 动名词的
