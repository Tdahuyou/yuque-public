# 音标

- 英 `/'pælɪt/`
- 美 `/'pælət/`

# 词义

n. 调色板；颜料
`a thin curved board that an artist uses to mix paints, holding it by putting his or her thumb through a hole at the edge`

# 同义

n. [计]调色板；颜料
`color` `paint`

# 短语

- `color palette` 调色板
- `palette knife` 调色刀

# 例句

The painter's right hand holds the brush, the left the palette.
那位画家的右手握着画笔，左手拿着调色盘。
