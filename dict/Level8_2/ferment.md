# 音标

- 英 `/fə'ment/`
- 美 `/fɚ'mɛnt/`

# 词义

n. 发酵；酵素；动乱
`a situation of great excitement or trouble in a country, especially caused by political change`

v. 发酵；动乱
`if fruit, beer, wine etc ferments, or if it is fermented, the sugar in it changes to alcohol`

# 同义

n. [轻]发酵；[生化]酵素；动乱
`trouble` `zymolysis`

vi. [轻]发酵；动乱
`sour`

vt. [轻]使发酵；酝酿；使动乱
`leaven`

# 同根

- adj. `fermentable` 可发酵的；沸腾的
- n. `fermentation` 发酵

# 例句

The whole country has been in a state of political ferment for some months.
整个国家几个月来一直处在政治骚动的状态中。
