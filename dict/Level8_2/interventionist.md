# 音标

- 英 `/ɪntə'venʃ(ə)nɪst/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɚ'vɛnʃənɪst/`

# 词义

adj. 干涉主义的
`based on the belief of a government or organization that it should take action or spend money to influence the economy(= financial system ) or what happens in other countries`

n. 干涉主义者
`An interventionist is someone who supports interventionist policies`

# 同根

- n. `intervention` 介入；调停；妨碍
- n. `interventionism` 干涉主义（尤指主张干预国际事务）

# 例句

...Roosevelt's interventionist policy.
...罗斯福的干涉主义政策。
