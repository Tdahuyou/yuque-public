# 音标

- 英 `/riːə'preɪz/`
- 美 `/,riə'prez/`

# 词义

v. 重新评估；重新评价
`to examine something again in order to consider whether you should change it or your opinion of it`

# 同义

vt. 重新评估；重新评价
`transvalue` `revaluate`

# 同根

- n. `reappraisal` 重新评价；重新估计

# 例句

People began to reappraise their values.
人们开始重新审视他们的价值观。
