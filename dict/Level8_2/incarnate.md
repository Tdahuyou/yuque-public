# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'kɑːnət/`
- 美 `/ɪn'kɑrnət/`

# 词义

adj. 化身的；人体化的；肉色的
`having taken human form`

v. 体现；使…具体化；使实体化
`If you say that a quality is incarnated in a person, you mean that they represent that quality or are typical of it in an extreme form`

# 同义

adj. 化身的；人体化的；肉色的
`flesh-coloured` `carneous`

vt. 体现；使…具体化；使实体化
`embody` `concretise`

# 同根

- adj. `incarnadine` 肉色的；粉红色的
- n. `incarnation` 化身；道成肉身；典型
- n. `incarnadine` 肉色；深红色
- vt. `incarnadine` 染红

# 例句

Jesus, the incarnate Son of God
耶稣，上帝之子的化身

The media cast him as the devil incarnate (= someone very evil ).
媒体把他描述成魔鬼的化身。

The iniquities of the regime are incarnated in one man.
该体制的种种劣迹集中体现于一人。

...a writer who incarnates the changing consciousness of the Americas.
...典型地体现出美洲观念变化的一位作家。
