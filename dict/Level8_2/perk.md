# 音标

- 英 `/pɜːk/`
- 美 `/pɝk/`

# 词义

v. 振作；昂首


n. 小费；额外收入
`something that you get legally from your work in addition to your wages, such as goods, meals, or a car`

# 同义

vi. 振作；昂首
`stir oneself` `nerve oneself`

vt. 使振作；打扮；竖起
`dress oneself` `array`

n. 小费；额外收入
`fee` `tip`

# 短语

- `perk up` 昂首翘尾；活跃起来；振作精神

# 例句

A cup of coffee will soon perk you up.
你喝一杯咖啡马上精神会振作起来。
