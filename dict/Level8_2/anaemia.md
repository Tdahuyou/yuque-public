# 音标

- 英 `/ə'niːmɪə/`
- 美 `/ə'ni:mjə/`

# 词义

n. 贫血（症）；无活力；脸色苍白
`a medical condition in which there are too few red cells in your blood`

# 同义

n. 贫血（症）；无活力；脸色苍白
`anemia` `sickle cell disease`

# 同根

- adj. `anaemic` 贫血的，患贫血症的；无活力的

# 例句

She suffered from anaemia and even required blood transfusions.
她患有贫血症而且甚至需要输血。
