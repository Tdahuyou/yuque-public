# 音标

- 英 `/haɪpər'æktɪv/`
- 美 `/,haɪpɚ'æktɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 极度活跃的；活动过度的
`someone, especially a child, who is hyperactive is too active, and is not able to keep still or be quiet for very long`

# 同根

- n. `hyperactivity` 极度活跃；活动过度

# 例句

His research was used in planning treatments for hyperactive children.
他的研究被用来为患有多动症的儿童设计治疗方案。
