# 音标

- 英 `/'gʌt(ə)r(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'gʌtərəl/`

# 词义

adj. 咽喉的；喉咙的；喉音的
`Guttural sounds are harsh sounds that are produced at the back of a person's throat`

n. 喉音；喉音字


# 同义

adj. 咽喉的；喉咙的；喉音的
`laryngopharyngeal` `faucial`

n. 喉音；喉音字
`throatiness` `laryngal`

# 例句

Joe had a low, guttural voice with a Midwestern accent.
乔的嗓音低沉，有喉音，并带着中西部的口音。
