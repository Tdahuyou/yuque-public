# 音标

- 英 `/'greɪvjɑːd/`
- 美 `/'ɡrevjɑrd/`

# 词义

n. 墓地
`an area of ground where people are buried, often next to a church`

# 同义

n. 墓地
`cemetery` `boneyard`

# 短语

- `graveyard shift` 夜班；全体夜班的工人

# 例句

They made their way to a graveyard to pay their traditional respects to the dead.
他们一路前行来到墓地，以传统的方式祭奠死者。
