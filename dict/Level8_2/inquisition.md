# 音标

- 英 `/ɪŋkwɪ'zɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,ɪnkwɪ'zɪʃən/`

# 词义

n. 调查；宗教法庭；审讯
`a Roman Catholic organization in the past whose aim was to find and punish people who had unacceptable religious beliefs`

# 同义

n. 调查；宗教法庭；[法]审讯
`investigation` `probe` `research` `hearing` `trial`

# 同根

- adj. `inquisitive` 好奇的；好问的，爱打听的
- vt. `inquisite` 调查，审讯

# 例句

She is right to deplore the pogroms against Jews and the beginning of the inquisition.
她正确地谴责对犹太人的大屠杀和开始执行宗教法庭。
