# 音标

- 英 `/'pædlɒk/`
- 美 `/'pæd'lɑk/`

# 词义

n. 挂锁；关闭；禁止进入
`a lock that you can put on a gate, door, bicycle etc`

v. 用挂锁锁上；关闭
`If you padlock something, you lock it or fasten it to something else using a padlock`

# 同义

n. [五金]挂锁；关闭；禁止进入
`closure` `shutting down`

vt. 用挂锁锁上；关闭
`close down` `close up`

# 例句

He undid the padlock and eased back the lid.
他打开挂锁，轻轻推开箱盖。

Eddie parked his bicycle against a lamppost and padlocked it.
艾迪把他的自行车停靠在一根路灯柱上，然后用挂锁锁上了。
