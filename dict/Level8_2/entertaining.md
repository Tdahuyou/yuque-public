# 音标

- 英 `/entə'teɪnɪŋ/`
- 美 `/,ɛntɚ'tenɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 令人愉快的
`amusing and interesting`

v. 款待（entertain的ing形式）
`If you entertain, or entertain people, you provide food and drink for them, for example, when you have invited them to your house`

# 同义

adj. 令人愉快的
`pleasant` `lovely` `grateful` `enjoyable` `agreeable`

# 同根

- adv. `entertainingly` 有趣地；使人愉快地
- n. `entertainment` 娱乐；消遣；款待
- vi. `entertain` 款待
- vt. `entertain` 娱乐；招待；怀抱；容纳

# 短语

- `entertaining friends` 招待朋友

# 例句

Children’s TV nowadays is much more entertaining.
如今的儿童电视节目要比过去有趣多了。

an entertaining evening
令人愉快的夜晚

I don't like to entertain guests anymore.
我不再喜欢招待客人。

He loves to entertain.
他喜欢宴客。
