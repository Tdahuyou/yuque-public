# 音标

- 英 `/gə'zet/`
- 美 `/ɡə'zet/`

# 词义

n. （英）公报；报纸
`an official newspaper, especially one from the government giving important lists of people who have been employed by them etc`

v. 在报上刊载


# 同义

n. （英）公报；报纸
`newspaper` `paper`

# 同根

- n. `gazetteer` 地名辞典；公报作者

# 例句

the ‘Phoenix Gazette’
《凤凰城报》
