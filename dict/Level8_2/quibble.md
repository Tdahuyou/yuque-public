# 音标

- 英 `/'kwɪb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'kwɪbl/`

# 词义

n. 谬论；双关语；遁辞


v. 诡辩；挑剔；说模棱两可的话
`When people quibble over a small matter, they argue about it even though it is not important`

# 同义

n. 谬论；双关语；遁辞
`fallacy` `idola`

vi. 诡辩；挑剔；说模棱两可的话
`prove that black is white and white black` `carp at`

# 同根

- adj. `quibbling` 吹毛求疵的
- n. `quibbling` 诡辩；找碴子；吹毛求疵
- v. `quibbling` 推托；说模棱两可的话（quibble的ing形式）

# 例句

Lawmakers spent the day quibbling over the final wording of the resolution.
立法者们花了一天争辩决议的最后措词。
