# 音标

- 英 `/ɪk'spaɪrɪ; ek-/`
- 美 `/ɪk'spaɪəri/`

# 词义

n. 满期，逾期；呼气；终结
`The expiry of something such as a contract, deadline, or visa is the time that it comes to an end or stops being valid`

# 同义

n. 满期，逾期；呼气；终结
`aspiration` `expired air`

# 同根

- adj. `expired` 过期的；失效的
- adj. `expiratory` [生理] 呼气的；吐气的
- n. `expiration` 呼气；终结；届期
- v. `expired` 期满（expire的过去分词）
- vi. `expire` 期满；终止；死亡；呼气
- vt. `expire` 呼出（空气）

# 短语

- `expiry date` 到期日；有效期限
- `date of expiry` 终止日期

# 例句

The United Mine Workers are not on strike today, despite the expiry of their contract at midnight.
联合矿业工会今天没有罢工，尽管他们的合同于午夜期满。
