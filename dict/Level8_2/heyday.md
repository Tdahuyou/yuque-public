# 音标

- 英 `/'heɪdeɪ/`
- 美 `/'hede/`

# 词义

n. 全盛期
`the time when someone or something was most popular, successful, or powerful`

int. 嘿！（表喜悦或惊奇等）


# 同义

n. 全盛期
`noon`

int. 嘿！（表喜悦或惊奇等）
`here`

# 例句

In its heyday, the studio's boast was that it had more stars than there are in heaven.
该制片厂吹嘘，它在鼎盛时期拥有的明星比天上的星星还多。
