# 音标

- 英 `/'bleɪt(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'bleitənt/`

# 词义

adj. 喧嚣的；公然的；炫耀的；俗丽的
`something bad that is blatant is very clear and easy to see, but the person responsible for it does not seem embarrassed or ashamed`

# 同义

adj. 喧嚣的；公然的；炫耀的；俗丽的
`declared` `ambitious`

# 同根

- adv. `blatantly` 公然地；喧闹地；看穿了地

# 例句

blatant discrimination
公然的歧视
