# 音标

- 英 `/'stɔːlwət; 'stæl-/`
- 美 `/'stɔlwɚt/`

# 词义

adj. 坚定的；健壮的
`strong in appearance`

n. 坚定分子
`someone who is very loyal to a particular organization or set of ideas, and works hard for them`

# 同义

adj. 坚定的；健壮的
`committed` `firm` `confirmed` `stable` `decisive`
