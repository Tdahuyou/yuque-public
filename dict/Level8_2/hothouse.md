# 音标

- 英 `/'hɒthaʊs/`
- 美 `/'hɔthaus/`

# 词义

n. 温室；温床
`a heated building, usually made of glass, where delicate plants can grow`

adj. 温室的；过分保护的；娇弱的


# 同义

n. [园艺]温室；温床
`greenhouse` `soil` `forcing house`

adj. [园艺]温室的；过分保护的；娇弱的
`overprotective`

# 同根

- n. `hotbed` 滋长地，温床
