# 音标

- 英 `/'mɒn(ə)lɪθ/`
- 美 `/'mɑnə'lɪθ/`

# 词义

n. 整块石料；庞然大物
`A monolith is a very large, upright piece of stone, especially one that was put in place in ancient times`

# 同义

n. [建]整块石料；庞然大物
`leviathan` `dreadnaught`

# 例句

It is misleading to see the legal system as a monolith.
认为司法体制庞大而僵化是误导人的。
