# 音标

- 英 `/helm/`
- 美 `/hɛlm/`

# 词义

n. 舵；舵柄；领导地位；驾驶盘
`the wheel or control which guides a ship or boat`

vt. 指挥；给掌舵
`to direct or steer `

# 同义

n. 舵；舵柄；领导地位；驾驶盘
`steering wheel` `handwheel`

vt. 指挥；给...掌舵
`officer` `captain` `boss` `lead`

# 短语

- `at the helm` 掌舵；掌权

# 例句

After more than a decade at the helm of Colorado State University, Albert C Yates today announced his retirement.
在掌管科罗拉多州立大学10多年后，阿尔伯特·叶慈于今天宣布退休。
