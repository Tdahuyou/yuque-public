# 音标

- 英 `/'trænsɪtɪv; 'trɑːns-; -nz-/`
- 美 `/'trænsətɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 及物的；过渡的；可迁的
`a transitive verb must have an object, for example the verb ‘break’ in the sentence ‘I broke the cup’. Transitive verbs are marked [T] in this dictionary.`

n. 传递；及物动词


# 同义

n. 传递；及物动词
`XFER`

# 同根

- adj. `transitional` 变迁的；过渡期的
- adv. `transitively` 及物地
- n. `transition` 过渡；转变；[分子生物] 转换；变调
- n. `transit` 运输；经过
- n. `transitivity` 传递性；动词的及物性；转移性
- vi. `transit` 经过
- vt. `transit` 运送

# 短语

- `transitive verb` 及物动词；单宾动词；外动词
- `transitive closure` 传递闭包
