# 音标

- 英 `/rɪnʌnsɪ'eɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/rɪ,nʌnsɪ'eʃən/`

# 词义

n. 放弃；脱离关系；拒绝承认；抛弃；弃权
`when someone makes a formal decision to no longer believe in something, live in a particular way etc`

# 同义

n. [法]放弃；脱离关系；拒绝承认；抛弃；弃权
`quitting` `rejection`

# 同根

- adj. `renunciative` 放弃的；脱离关系的
- n. `renunciant` 断念者

# 例句

The talks were dependent on a renunciation of terrorism.
会谈以放弃恐怖主义为前提。
