# 词义

adj. 悲伤或失望到难忍程度的；令人悲伤的
`Something that is heartbreaking makes you feel extremely sad and upset`

v. 使…心碎（heartbreak的ing形式）


# 同根

- adj. `heartbroken` 悲伤的，伤心的
- n. `heartbreak` 心碎；伤心事

# 例句

This year we won't even be able to buy presents for our grandchildren. It's heartbreaking.
今年我们甚至不能给孙辈们买礼物，这令人极度伤心。
