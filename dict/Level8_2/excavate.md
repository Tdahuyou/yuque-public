# 音标

- 英 `/'ekskəveɪt/`
- 美 `/'ɛkskə'vet/`

# 词义

v. 挖掘；开凿
`if a scientist or archaeologist excavates an area of land, they dig carefully to find ancient objects, bones etc`

# 同义

vt. 挖掘；开凿
`to dig` `tap into`

# 同根

- n. `excavation` 挖掘，发掘
- n. `excavator` 挖掘机；开凿者；打洞机

# 例句

Schliemann excavated the ancient city of Troy.
谢里曼发掘出特洛伊古城。
