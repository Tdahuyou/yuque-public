# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'septɪv/`
- 美 `/dɪ'sɛptɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 欺诈的；迷惑的；虚伪的
`intended to make someone believe something that is not true`

# 同义

adj. 欺诈的；迷惑的；虚伪的
`artificial` `lost` `false`

# 同根

- adv. `deceptively` 迷惑地，骗人地；虚伪地
- n. `deception` 欺骗，欺诈；骗术
- n. `deceptiveness` 虚伪；迷惑骗人

# 例句

misleading and deceptive adverts
误导人且带有欺骗性的广告

deceptive practices
欺骗行为
