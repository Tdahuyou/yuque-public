# 音标

- 英 `/sneə/`
- 美 `/snɛr/`

# 词义

n. 陷阱；圈套；[外科]勒除器
`a trap for catching an animal, especially one that uses a wire or rope to catch the animal by its foot`

v. 捕捉；诱惑
`to catch an animal by using a snare`

# 同义

n. 陷阱；圈套；[外科]勒除器
`trap` `web` `mesh` `pit`

vt. 捕捉；诱惑
`tempt` `seduce`

# 例句

A rabbit was caught in the snare.
一只兔子被套索套住了。
