# 音标

- 英 `/'stʌtə/`
- 美 `/'stʌtɚ/`

# 词义

v. 结结巴巴地说话
`to speak with difficulty because you cannot stop yourself from repeating the first  consonant  of some words`

n. 口吃，结巴
`an inability to speak normally because you stutter`

# 同义

vi. 结结巴巴地说话
`splatter`

n. [内科]口吃，结巴
`impediment` `mogilalia`

# 同根

- n. `stutterer` 口吃；说话结巴者

# 例句

‘I’m D-d-david,’ he stuttered.
“我叫戴……戴……戴维。”他结结巴巴地说道。
