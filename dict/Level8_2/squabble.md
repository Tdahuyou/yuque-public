# 音标

- 英 `/'skwɒb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'skwɑbl/`

# 词义

v. 发生口角；大声争吵
`to argue about something unimportant`

n. 争吵；口角
`Squabble is also a noun`

# 同义

vi. 发生口角；大声争吵
`altercate`

vt. 弄乱（排好的铅字）
`mess` `disarrange`

n. 争吵；口角
`argument` `brush` `dispute`

# 例句

Mother is devoted to Dad although they squabble all the time.
妈妈深爱着爸爸，虽然他们总是为琐事争吵。

There have been minor squabbles about phone bills.
为电话账单已闹过口角。
