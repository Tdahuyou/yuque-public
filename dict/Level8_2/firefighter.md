# 音标

- 英 `/'faɪəfaɪtə/`
- 美 `/'faɪɚfaɪtɚ/`

# 词义

n. 消防队员
`someone whose job is to stop fires burning`

# 同义

n. 消防队员
`pompier`

# 同根

- n. `fireman` 消防队员；救火队员；锅炉工
- n. `firefight` 交火；炮战
- n. `firehouse` 消防站；消防队
- n. `fireplug` 消防栓，消防塞

# 例句

When every molecule in your body tells you to run away from the burning building, as a firefighter you have to resist the urge, override it and plunge in.
当你身体内每个细胞都告诉逃离失火的建筑物时，作为一名消防员，你必须克制这种冲动，超越它，投入火场进行战斗。

He mows lawns and gardens for half a dozen clients in Grafton, some of them family friends. And he is an active volunteer firefighter.
他为格拉夫顿六户人家修剪草坪和花园，他们中一些是父母的朋友，此外他还是个积极的义务消防员。

Locally and from the state standpoint, we had some firefighter jobs that were in jeopardy, up in the hundreds.
站在州政府的立场，我们有一些消防员在危险岗位工作，最多时有数百人。
