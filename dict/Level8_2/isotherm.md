# 音标

- 英 `/'aɪsə(ʊ)θɜːm/`
- 美 `/'aɪsə'θɝm/`

# 词义

n. [气象]等温线
`a line on a weather map joining places where the temperature is the same`

# 同义

n. [气象]等温线
`choroisotherm`

# 同根

- adj. `isothermal` 等温的；等温线的
- adj. `isothermic` 等温的；等温线的
- n. `isothermal` 等温线

# 短语

- `adsorption isotherm` 等温吸附线
- `sorption isotherm` 吸着等温线；等温吸附线

# 例句

That's the isotherm here.
等温线的方程了。

A thinner isotherm allows faster turnover of warmer waters from below, which speeds ice-melt.
更薄的等温线意味着下层温暖的海水能更快地交换上来，这将加速冰层溶化。

A thinner isotherm allows faster turnover of warmer waters from below, which speeds ice-melt. The process is self-perpetuating: as ice melts, the ocean absorbs more heat and melts more ice, and so on.
较薄的等温线使下面温暖的海水快速流转，从而加快冰融化。
