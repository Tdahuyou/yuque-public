# 音标

- 英 `/'sæŋ(k)təm/`
- 美 `/'sæŋktəm/`

# 词义

n. 圣所；密室；私室
`a private place or room that only a few important people are allowed to enter – often used humorously`

# 同义

n. 圣所；密室；私室
`holy place` `adytum`

# 同根

- n. `sanctuary` 避难所；至圣所；耶路撒冷的神殿

# 例句

Occasionally she would be allowed into the inner sanctum of his office.
偶尔她也获准进入他那私密的办公室。
