# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃɒtgʌn/`
- 美 `/'ʃɑtɡʌn/`

# 词义

n. 霰弹猎枪
`a long gun fired from the shoulder, that shoots many small round balls at one time, used especially for killing birds or animals`

adj. 猎枪的；被迫的


vt. 用猎枪射击；强迫


vi. 用猎枪射击


# 同义

adj. 猎枪的；被迫的
`forced`

vt. 用猎枪射击；强迫
`force` `enforce`

# 短语

- `ride shotgun` 运输途中担任护卫；坐在汽车前面的旅客座上

# 例句

The robbers were armed with sawn-off shotguns (= ones that have been made shorter, so the balls go in different directions ) .
抢劫者手持枪管被锯短的猎枪。
