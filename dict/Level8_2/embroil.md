# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'brɒɪl; em-/`
- 美 `/ɪm'brɔɪl/`

# 词义

v. 使卷入；使混乱
`to involve someone or something in a difficult situation`

# 同义

vt. 使卷入；使混乱
`cloud` `confuse`

# 同根

- n. `embroilment` 混乱，搅乱；纠纷，争执

# 例句

Any hostilities could result in retaliation and further embroil U.N. troops in fighting.
任何敌对活动都可能会招致报复，进而使联合国部队卷入战斗中。
