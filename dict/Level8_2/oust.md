# 音标

- 英 `/aʊst/`
- 美 `/aʊst/`

# 词义

v. 驱逐；剥夺；取代
`If someone is ousted from a position of power, job, or place, they are forced to leave it`

# 同义

vt. 驱逐；剥夺；取代
`shear` `expel`

# 同根

- n. `ouster` 驱逐；剥夺；罢黜

# 例句

The leaders have been ousted from power by nationalists.
那些领导人已被民族主义者赶下了台。

The Republicans may oust him in November.
共和党人可能在11月罢免他。
