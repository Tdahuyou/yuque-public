# 音标

- 英 `/'luːkwɔːm; luːk'wɔːm; lj-/`
- 美 `/,lʊk'wɔm/`

# 词义

adj. 冷淡的；微温的；不够热心的
`food, liquid etc that is lukewarm is slightly warm and often not as hot or cold as it should be`

# 同义

adj. 冷淡的；微温的；不够热心的
`cold` `nonchalant`

# 短语

- `lukewarm water` 温水

# 例句

She sipped some lukewarm coffee from her mug.
她抿了几口杯子里温热的咖啡。
