# 音标

- 英 `/'gra:s,ru:ts/`
- 美 `/'græs'rʊts/`

# 词义

adj. 基层的；草根的；乡村的


n. 草根；基础
`The grassroots of an organization or movement are the ordinary people who form the main part of it, rather than its leaders`

# 同义
