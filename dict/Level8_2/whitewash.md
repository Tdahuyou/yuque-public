# 音标

- 英 `/'waɪtwɒʃ/`
- 美 `/'waɪtwɑʃ/`

# 词义

n. 粉饰；白色涂料；石灰水
`a report or examination of events that hides the true facts about something so that the person who is responsible will not be punished`

v. 掩饰；把…刷白
`to hide the true facts about a serious accident or illegal action`

# 同义

n. 粉饰；[涂料]白色涂料；[建]石灰水
`embroidery` `window dressing`

vt. 掩饰；把…刷白
`mask` `belie`

# 同根

- adj. `whitish` 带白色的；发白的
- vi. `whiten` 变白
- vt. `whiten` 漂白；刷白

# 例句

The official report into the cause of the fire was labeled a whitewash.
官方的火灾原因报告被说成是对真相的隐瞒。
