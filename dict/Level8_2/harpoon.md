# 音标

- 英 `/hɑː'puːn/`
- 美 `/hɑr'pun/`

# 词义

v. 用鱼叉叉；用鱼叉捕获
`To harpoon a whale or large fish means to hit it with a harpoon`

n. 鱼叉
`a weapon used for hunting whales`

# 同义

vt. 用鱼叉叉；用鱼叉捕获
`gig`

n. 鱼叉
`gaff` `leister`

# 同根

- n. `harpooner` 鱼叉手

# 例句

Norwegian whalers said yesterday they had harpooned a female minke whale.
挪威的捕鲸人昨天说他们用捕鲸叉捕获了一头雌性小须鲸。
