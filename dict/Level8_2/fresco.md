# 音标

- 英 `/'freskəʊ/`
- 美 `/'frɛsko/`

# 词义

n. 壁画
`a painting made on a wall while the  plaster  is still wet`

# 同义

n. [建]壁画
`wall painting` `mural painting`

# 短语

- `al fresco` 在户外；在野外

# 例句

The medium of fresco makes great demands on a painter's techinical skill.
壁画这种绘画形式对画家的技巧有很高要求。

We show this in a scene in the Sistine Chapel [in Rome] where we create a big crack in the fresco of God and Adam.
我们将这个观点放到了发生在罗马西斯廷教堂的一幕里——一幅绘有上帝与亚当的壁画发生严重断裂。

Today, much of it is occupied by government offices and only vestiges of its beauty endure – admittedly including Gozzoli's fresco of the journey of the Magi.
如今政府办公室占据了它很多的建筑，而梅迪奇·里卡尔第宫也仅保留一丝当年的痕迹--这其中包括戈佐利所绘的壁画《三圣贤之旅》。
