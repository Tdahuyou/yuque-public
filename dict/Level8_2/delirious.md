# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'lɪrɪəs/`
- 美 `/dɪ'lɪrɪəs/`

# 词义

adj. 发狂的；神志昏迷的；精神错乱的
`Someone who is delirious is unable to think or speak in a sensible and reasonable way, usually because they are very ill and have a fever`

# 同义

adj. 发狂的；神志昏迷的；精神错乱的
`nuts` `distraught`

# 同根

- adv. `deliriously` 谵妄地；神志失常地；极其兴奋地
- n. `delirium` 精神错乱；发狂，狂热；说谵语状态

# 例句

I was delirious and blacked out several times.
我当时神志不清，几次昏过去。
