# 音标

- 英 `/plə'tuːn/`
- 美 `/plə'tun/`

# 词义

n. 排，团；一组
`a small group of soldiers which is part of a  company  and is led by a  lieutenant `

# 同义

n. 排，团；一组
`row` `rank` `range` `line` `mass`

# 短语

- `platoon leader` n. 排长

# 例句

I have stationed another platoon on the island.
我已向那个岛派出了又一个排的守军。

That’s hardly an army. It’s not even a platoon.
这根本算不上一个军队，甚至连一个排都算不上。
