# 音标

- 英 `/'sælɪveɪt/`
- 美 `/'sælɪvet/`

# 词义

v. 使流涎；使过量分泌唾液
`to look at or show interest in something or someone in a way that shows you like or want them very much – used to show disapproval`

# 同义

vi. 分泌唾液；流口水
`run at the mouth`

# 同根

- adj. `salivary` 唾液的；分泌唾液的
- n. `saliva` 唾液；涎
- n. `salivation` 分泌唾液；流涎症；汞中毒

# 例句

Any dog will salivate when presented with food.
任何狗见了食物都会流口水。
