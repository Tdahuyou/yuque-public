# 音标

- 英 `/sɪgə'ret/`
- 美 `/ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt, ˈsɪɡəˌrɛt/`

# 词义

n. 香烟；纸烟
`a thin tube of paper filled with finely cut tobacco that people smoke`

# 同义

n. 香烟；纸烟
`coffin nail`

# 短语

- `cigarette smoking` 吸烟
- `cigarette paper` 卷烟纸
- `cigarette lighter` 香烟打火机
- `cigarette case` 烟盒，香烟盒
- `cigarette ash` 烟灰
- `cigarette end` 烟头
- `cigarette holder` 烟斗
- `cigarette box` 香烟盒
- `cigarette butt` 烟蒂

# 例句

a packet of cigarettes
一包香烟
