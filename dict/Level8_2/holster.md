# 音标

- 英 `/'həʊlstə; 'hɒl-/`
- 美 `/'holstɚ/`

# 词义

n. 手枪皮套
`a leather object for carrying a small gun, that is worn on a belt`

Initially, Colt produced three "revolving" handguns--belt, holster and pocket pistols--and two rifles.
