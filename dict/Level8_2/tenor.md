# 音标

- 英 `/'tenə/`
- 美 `/'tɛnɚ/`

# 词义

n. 男高音；要旨，大意；票据的限期；稳定的进程
`a male singing voice that can reach the range of notes below the lowest woman’s voice, or a man with a voice like this`

adj. 男高音的
`a tenor voice or instrument has a range of notes that is lower than an  alto  voice or instrument`

# 同义

n. 男高音；要旨，大意；[金融]票据的限期；稳定的进程
`general idea` `main points`

# 同根

- n. `tenorist` 唱次中音者；男高音歌手

# 例句

Arthur Davies sings the tenor solo.
