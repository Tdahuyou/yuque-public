# 音标

- 英 `/'fʊtpɑːθ/`
- 美 `/'fʊtpæθ/`

# 词义

n. 人行道；小路；小径
`a narrow path for people to walk along, especially in the country`

# 同义

n. 人行道；小路；小径
`path` `pavement` `sidewalk`

# 例句

Fans blasted his music and danced energetically to the delight of tourists and passers-by on the crowded footpath.
在这条拥挤的人行道上，歌迷们向兴致盎然的游客和路人播放着杰克逊的音乐，并随之跳起劲舞。
