# 音标

- 英 `/'spɪəhed/`
- 美 `/'spɪr'hɛd/`

# 词义

n. 矛头；先锋；先锋部队
`a person or group of people who lead an attack or organized action`

v. 带头；做先锋
`to lead an attack or organized action`

# 同义

n. 矛头；先锋；先锋部队
`vanguard` `pioneer`

vt. 带头；做先锋
`take a lead` `tape head`

# 例句

The spearhead is made of hard flint and it's about the size of a small, slim mobile phone, but it's in the shape of a long thin leaf.
这矛头是由一颗坚硬的燧石制成的，大概一部超薄手机大小，细细长长的叶子状。 它的矛锋完好无损，仍然相当的尖锐。
