# 音标

- 英 `/,ɒsten'teɪʃəs/`
- 美 `/,ɑstɛn'teʃəs/`

# 词义

adj. 招摇的；卖弄的；夸耀的；铺张的；惹人注目的
`something that is ostentatious looks very expensive and is designed to make people think that its owner must be very rich`

# 同义

adj. 招摇的；卖弄的；夸耀的；铺张的；惹人注目的
`epideictic`

# 同根

- adv. `ostentatiously` 招摇地；铺张地；炫耀地
- n. `ostentation` 卖弄；虚饰；虚有其表

# 例句

She carried her car keys on an ostentatious gold key ring.
她把汽车钥匙挂在一个夸张的金钥匙圈上。

an ostentatious display of wealth
张扬地炫耀财富
