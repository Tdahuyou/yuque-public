# 音标

- 英 `/rə(ʊ)'zet/`
- 美 `/ro'zɛt/`

# 词义

n. 莲座丛；玫瑰形饰物；[建]圆花饰
`a circular  badge  made of coloured  ribbon  that is given to the winner of a competition, or that people in Britain wear to show support for a particular football team or political party`

# 同根

- adj. `rosy` 蔷薇色的，玫瑰红色的；美好的；乐观的；涨红脸的
- adj. `roseate` 玫瑰色的；容光焕发的；乐观的
- adj. `roselike` 似玫瑰的；玫瑰花似的
- n. `rosefish` 玫瑰鱼（产于北大西洋）

# 例句

Marjorie stood on the porch with a big yellow rosette tied around the post.
马乔里站在柱子上系着一个巨大的黄色玫瑰花形饰物的门廊里。
