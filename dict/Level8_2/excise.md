# 音标

- 英 `/'eksaɪz/`
- 美 `/ɛk'saɪz/`

# 词义

v. 切除；收税
`to remove or get rid of something, especially by cutting it out`

n. 消费税；货物税
`the government tax that is put on the goods that are produced and used inside a country`

# 同义

vt. 切除；收税
`resect` `abscise`

n. [税收]消费税；货物税
`consumption tax` `goods tax`

# 同根

- n. `excision` 删除，切除；切除之物
- n. `exciseman` 收税官

# 短语

- `excise tax` 消费税；特许权税
- `excise office` 税务署

# 例句

The tumour was excised.
肿瘤被切除了。
