# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'dʒɒɪmənt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'dʒɔɪmənt/`

# 词义

n. 享受；乐趣；享有
`the feeling of pleasure you get from having or doing something, or something you enjoy doing`

# 同义

n. 享受；乐趣；享有
`fun` `luxury` `joy` `sweet`

# 同根

- adj. `enjoyable` 快乐的；令人愉快的
- adv. `enjoyably` 愉快地；有趣地；可享乐地
- vt. `enjoy` 欣赏，享受；喜爱；使过得快活

# 例句

Acting has brought me enormous enjoyment.
演戏给我带来莫大的快乐。

Unfortunately, a small minority want to spoil everyone else’s enjoyment.
很遗憾，少数几个人想令其他所有人扫兴。
