# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪdʒɪt/`
- 美 `/'mɪdʒɪt/`

# 词义

n. 侏儒；极小者
`a very offensive word for someone who is very short because their body has not grown normally. Do not use this word.`

adj. 小型的
`very small`
