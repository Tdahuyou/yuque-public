# 音标

- 英 `/vɪk'tɔːrɪəs/`
- 美 `/vɪk'tɔrɪəs/`

# 词义

adj. 胜利的；凯旋的
`having won a victory, or ending in a victory`

# 同义

adj. 胜利的；凯旋的
`winning` `triumphal`

# 同根

- adv. `victoriously` 获胜地；凯旋归来地
- n. `victory` 胜利；成功；克服
- n. `victor` 胜利者

# 短语

- `the victorious team` 胜队

# 例句

the victorious team
得胜的球队

We were confident that the Allies would emerge victorious (= finally win ).
我们确信盟军最终一定能获胜。
