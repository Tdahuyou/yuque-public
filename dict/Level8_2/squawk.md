# 音标

- 英 `/skwɔːk/`
- 美 `/skwɔk/`

# 词义

v. 抗议；发出响而粗的叫声；大声抱怨
`if a bird squawks, it makes a loud sharp angry sound`

n. 抗议；叫声；诉苦；故障
`Squawk is also a noun`

# 同义

vi. 抗议；发出响而粗的叫声；大声抱怨
`protest against sth` `exclaim at`

n. 抗议；叫声；诉苦；故障
`fault` `trouble` `cry` `complaint`

# 例句

I threw pebbles at the hens, and that made them jump and squawk.
我朝母鸡扔卵石，吓得它们乱跳乱叫。

A mallard suddenly took wing, rising steeply into the air with an angry squawk.
一只野鸭突然展翅飞起，愤怒地尖鸣一声直冲天空。
