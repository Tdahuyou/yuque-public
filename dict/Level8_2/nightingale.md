# 音标

- 英 `/'naɪtɪŋ,geɪl/`
- 美 `/'naɪtn,gel/`

# 词义

n. 夜莺
`a small bird that sings very beautifully, especially at night`

# 同义

n. 夜莺
`Philomela` `bulbul`

# 短语

- `florence nightingale` 弗洛伦斯·南丁格尔（英国护士）

# 例句

I looked out for you as a nightingale looks out for the cherry garden, - sighed the sixth.
第六个女儿喊道：“我就像夜莺盼望着樱花园一样，整天张望着您。”
