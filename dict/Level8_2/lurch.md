# 音标

- 英 `/lɜːtʃ/`
- 美 `/lɝtʃ/`

# 词义

n. 突然倾斜；蹒跚；挫折
`Lurch is also a noun`

v. 倾斜；蹒跚
`to walk or move suddenly in an uncontrolled or unsteady way`

# 同义

n. 突然倾斜；蹒跚；挫折
`frustration` `setback`

vi. 倾斜；蹒跚
`pitch` `slope` `lean`

vt. 击败
`defeat` `down`

# 例句

The property sector was another casualty of the lurch toward higher interest rates.
房地产业是利率突然提高的又一受损行业。
