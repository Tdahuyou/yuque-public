# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃɔː'saɪtɪd/`
- 美 `/'ʃɔ:'saitid/`

# 词义

adj. 目光短浅的；近视的
`If someone is shortsighted about something, or if their ideas are shortsighted, they do not make proper or careful judgments about the future`

# 同义

adj. 目光短浅的；近视的
`myopic` `bleary-eyed`

# 同根

- n. `shortsightedness` 近视

# 例句

Environmentalists fear that this is a shortsighted approach to the problem of global warming.
环境保护主义者们担心这会是解决全球变暖问题的一个目光短浅的方法。
