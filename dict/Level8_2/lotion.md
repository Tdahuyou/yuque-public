# 音标

- 英 `/'ləʊʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'loʃən/`

# 词义

n. 洗液；洗涤剂
`a liquid mixture that you put on your skin or hair to clean,  soften  , or protect it`

# 同义

n. 洗液；洗涤剂
`washing liquor` `washer liquid`

# 短语

- `body lotion` 润肤露；身体乳液；润肤霜
- `moisturizing lotion` 保湿美颜乳；保湿营养乳液
- `hand lotion` 护手霜
- `calamine lotion` 炉甘石液
- `skin lotion` 化妆水；爽肤水；润肤霜

# 例句

suntan lotion
防晒露
