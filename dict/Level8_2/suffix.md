# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌfɪks/`
- 美 `/'sʌfɪks/`

# 词义

v. 添后缀


n. 后缀；下标
`a letter or letters added to the end of a word to form a new word, such as ‘ness’ in ‘kindness’ or ‘ly’ in ‘suddenly’`

# 同义

n. [语]后缀；下标
`sufix` `postfix`

# 同根

- n. `suffixation` 加后缀；加词尾
