# 音标

- 英 `/'hʌmbʌg/`
- 美 `/'hʌmbʌɡ/`

# 词义

n. 骗子；欺骗；谎话
`insincere or dishonest words or behaviour`

v. 欺骗；哄骗


# 同义

n. 骗子；欺骗；谎话
`fraud` `sharp`

vt. 欺骗；哄骗
`sell` `fox` `kid` `trick`

vi. 行骗
`mountebank`

# 例句

He dismissed the prime minister’s comments as ‘pure humbug’.
