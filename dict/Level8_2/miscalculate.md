# 音标

- 英 `/mɪs'kælkjʊleɪt/`
- 美 `/,mɪs'kælkjulet/`

# 词义

v. 算错；估计错误
`to make a mistake when deciding how long something will take to do, how much money you will need etc`

# 同义

vt. 算错；估计错误
`get it wrong`

vi. 算错；判断错误
`get it wrong`

# 同根

- n. `miscalculation` 算错；误算

# 例句

We miscalculated how long it would take to get there.
我们算错了要多久才能到那里。
