# 音标

- 英 `/ʃɪt/`
- 美 `/ʃɪt/`

# 词义

n. 屎；粪
`Some people use shit to refer to solid waste matter from the body of a human being or animal`

v. 拉屎
`to pass solid waste out of your body from your  bowel s `

int. 狗屁；呸
`used to express anger, annoyance, fear, or disappointment`

# 同义

n. 屎；粪
`turd` `ordure`

vi. 拉屎
`make a mess`

vt. 欺骗；在…拉屎
`sell` `fox` `kid`

int. 狗屁；呸
`bah` `foh`

# 短语

- `don't give a shit` 毫不在乎；毫无兴趣
- `a piece of shit` 一抛屎
- `shit happens` 不好的事情时常发生
- `eat shit` [俚语]卑躬屈膝
- `bull shit` 牛屎；废话；胡说八道
- `in the shit` [粗鄙语、俚语]
- `holy shit` [粗俗语]天啊（等于holy cats）
