# 音标

- 英 `/pɜːtʃ/`
- 美 `/pɝtʃ/`

# 词义

n. 鲈鱼;高位；栖木；杆
`a branch or stick where a bird sits`

v. 栖息；就位；位于；使坐落于
`To perch somewhere means to be on the top or edge of something`

# 同义

n. 高位；栖木；杆
`pole` `high-order`

vt. 栖息；就位；位于；使坐落于
`inhabit` `take post`

vi. 栖息；就位；位于
`sit` `take post`

# 同根

- n. `percher` 栖于树上的鸟；高坐者；织物检查工

# 例句

She watched the parade from her perch on her father’s shoulders.
她骑在父亲的肩上观看游行。

...the vast slums that perch precariously on top of the hills around which the city was built.
…摇摇欲坠地坐落于城市中心群山之顶的大片贫民窟。
