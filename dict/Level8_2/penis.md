# 音标

- 英 `/'piːnɪs/`
- 美 `/'pinɪs/`

# 词义

n. 阳物；[解剖]阴茎
`the outer sex organ of men and male animals, which is used for sex and through which waste water comes out of the body`

# 同义

n. 阳物；[解剖]阴茎
`dick` `willy`
