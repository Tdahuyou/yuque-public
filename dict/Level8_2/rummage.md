# 音标

- 英 `/'rʌmɪdʒ/`
- 美 `/'rʌmɪdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 检查；搜出；仔细搜查；翻找出
`to search for something by moving things around in a careless or hurried way`

n. 翻找；检查；查出的物件；零星杂物
`a careless or hurried search for something`

# 同义

vt. 检查；搜出；仔细搜查；翻找出
`examine` `inspect` `review`

n. 翻找；检查；查出的物件；零星杂物
`inspection` `examination` `checking` `monitoring`

vi. 翻找；仔细搜查
`hunt throw`

# 例句

They rummage through piles of secondhand clothes for something that fits.
他们在成堆的二手衣服中翻找合身的衣服。
