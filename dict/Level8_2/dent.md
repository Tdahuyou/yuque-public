# 音标

- 英 `/dent/`
- 美 `/dɛnt/`

# 词义

n. 凹痕；削弱；减少；齿
`a hollow area in the surface of something, usually made by something hitting it`

v. 产生凹陷；凹进去；削减
`if you dent something, or if it dents, you hit or press it so that its surface is bent inwards`

# 同义

n. 凹痕；削弱；减少；齿
`reduction` `loss` `decrease`

vi. 产生凹陷；凹进去；削减
`cut down on` `cut to the bone`

vt. 削弱；使产生凹痕
`break` `to weaken`

# 同根

- adj. `dental` 牙科的；牙齿的，牙的
- n. `dentist` 牙科医生
- n. `dental` 齿音
- n. `denture` 齿列，托牙；一副假牙
- n. `dentition` 齿列；生齿；[脊椎] 齿系

# 短语

- `make a dent` 产生印象；引起注意
- `dent resistance` 耐冲击性
- `make a dent in` v. 削弱；[口]取得有效进展

# 例句

Eight years of effort have hardly put a dent in drug trafficking.
八年的努力几乎没有减少贩毒活动。
