# 音标

- 英 `/'vɪdʒɪl/`
- 美 `/'vɪdʒɪl/`

# 词义

n. 守夜；监视；不眠；警戒
`a period of time, especially during the night, when you stay awake in order to pray, remain with someone who is ill, or watch for danger`

# 同义

n. 守夜；监视；不眠；警戒
`observation` `monitoring` `guard` `surveillance` `wake`

# 同根

- adj. `vigilant` 警惕的；警醒的；注意的；警戒的
- adv. `vigilantly` 警惕地；警觉地；留心地
- n. `vigilance` 警戒，警觉；警醒症
- n. `vigilante` 义务警员；治安维持会成员

# 例句

Eva and Paul kept a constant vigil by their daughter’s hospital bedside.
伊娃和保罗一直在医院里守在女儿的病床边。
