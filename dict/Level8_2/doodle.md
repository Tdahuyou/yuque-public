# 音标

- 英 `/'duːd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'dudl/`

# 词义

v. 涂鸦；闲混；随意弹奏
`When someone doodles, they draw doodles`

n. 涂鸦；蠢人
`A doodle is a pattern or picture that you draw when you are bored or thinking about something else`

# 同义

vi. 涂鸦；闲混；随意弹奏
`lounge away` `drone away`

n. 涂鸦；蠢人
`ass` `conehead`

vt. 乱涂出；闲混；随意弹奏
`lounge away` `drone away`

# 例句

He looked across at Jackson, doodling on his notebook.
他一边看着对面的杰克逊，一边在笔记本上信手涂鸦。

Dillworthy was staring into space, with a scrawl of doodles on the pad in front of him.
迪尔沃西凝望着天空，面前的纸上满是涂鸦。
