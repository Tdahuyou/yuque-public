# 音标

- 英 `/'frek(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'frɛkl/`

# 词义

n. 雀斑；斑点
`freckles are small brown spots on someone’s skin, especially on their face, which the sun can cause to increase in number and become darker`

v. 使生雀斑


# 同义

n. [皮肤]雀斑；斑点
`lentigo` `ephelis`

# 同根

- adj. `freckled` 有雀斑的；有斑点的
- v. `freckled` 生斑点；布满斑点（freckle的过去式和过去分词）

# 例句

He had short ginger-coloured hair and freckles.
他一头姜黄色的短发，脸上有些雀斑。
