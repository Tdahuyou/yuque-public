# 音标

- 英 `/'flɪn(t)ʃ/`
- 美 `/flɪntʃ/`

# 词义

v. 退缩；畏惧
`to move your face or body away from someone or something because you are in pain, frightened, or upset`

n. 退缩；畏惧
`the act or an instance of drawing back `

# 同义

vi. 退缩；畏惧
`cower` `shy away from`

n. 退缩；畏惧
`boggle`

# 短语

- `flinch from` 躲闪；在…面前退缩

# 例句

Leo stared back at him without flinching.
利奥毫不畏缩地回瞪着他。
