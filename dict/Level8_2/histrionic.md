# 音标

- 英 `/,hɪstrɪ'ɒnɪk/`
- 美 `/'hɪstrɪ'ɑnɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 戏剧的；演员的，表演的
`If you refer to someone's behaviour as histrionic, you are critical of it because it is very dramatic, exaggerated, and insincere`

n. 演员


# 同义

adj. 戏剧的；演员的，表演的
`dramatic` `performing` `scenic`

n. 演员
`player` `impersonator`

# 例句

Dorothea let out a histrionic groan.
多罗西亚发出了一声做作的呻吟。
