# 音标

- 英 `/ˈnɔ:ziə/`
- 美 `/'nɔzɪə/`

# 词义

n. 恶心，晕船；极端的憎恶
`the feeling that you have when you think you are going to vomit (= bring food up from your stomach through your mouth )`

# 同义

n. [临床]恶心，晕船；极端的憎恶
`queasiness` `naupathia`

# 同根

- adj. `nauseating` 令人恶心的；厌恶的
- v. `nauseating` 使恶心（nauseate的ing形式）
- vi. `nauseate` 作呕；厌恶；产生恶感
- vt. `nauseate` 使厌恶；使恶心；使作呕

# 例句

I was overcome with a feeling of nausea.
我感到一阵恶心。
