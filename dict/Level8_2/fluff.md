# 音标

- 英 `/flʌf/`
- 美 `/flʌf/`

# 词义

n. 绒毛；错误；无价值的东西
`soft light bits of thread that have come from wool, cotton, or other materials`

v. 念错；抖松；使…起毛
`to make something soft become larger by shaking it`

# 同义

n. 绒毛；错误；无价值的东西
`error` `mistake` `fault` `down`

vi. [纸]起毛；出错；变松
`go awry` `make mistakes`

# 同根

- adj. `fluffy` 蓬松的；松软的；毛茸茸的；无内容的
- n. `fluffiness` 起毛现象

# 例句

He was picking bits of fluff off his trousers.
他在揪裤子上的绒毛球。

a ball of carpet fluff
地毯的绒毛团
