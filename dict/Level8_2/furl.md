# 音标

- 英 `/fɜːl/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

v. 叠，卷收；收拢
`When you furl something made of fabric such as an umbrella, sail, or flag, you roll or fold it up because it is not going to be used`

n. 卷起，折叠；卷起之物


# 同义

vt. 叠，卷收；收拢
`tuck`

vi. 收拢，卷起
`curl up` `roll up`

n. 卷起，折叠；卷起之物
`rolled up` `foldover`

# 例句

An attempt was made to furl the headsail.
有人试着收起前桅帆。

...a furled umbrella.
...一把收拢的雨伞。
