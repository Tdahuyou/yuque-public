# 音标

- 英 `/,lærɪn'dʒaɪtɪs/`
- 美 `/'lærɪn'dʒaɪtɪs/`

# 词义

n. [耳鼻喉]喉炎
`an illness which makes talking difficult because your larynx and throat are swollen`

Acute laryngitis is caused by a viral infection, usually linked to the common cold or an upper respiratory infection.
