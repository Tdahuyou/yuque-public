# 音标

- 英 `/lʌl/`
- 美 `/lʌl/`

# 词义

v. 使平静；使安静；哄骗
`to make someone feel calm or as if they want to sleep`

n. 间歇；暂停；暂时平静
`a short period of time when there is less activity or less noise than usual`

# 同义

vt. 使平静；使安静；哄骗
`calm` `silence` `compose`

vi. 平息；减弱；停止
`fall` `lower` `cool` `subside`

n. 间歇；暂停；暂时平静
`hang` `pause`

# 例句

The hum of the tyres on the road lulled her to sleep.
一路上滚滚的车轮声使她昏昏欲睡。
