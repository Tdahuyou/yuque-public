# 音标

- 英 `/'kəʊkəʊ/`
- 美 `/'koko/`

# 词义

n. 可可粉；可可豆；可可饮料；深褐色
`a brown powder made from cocoa beans, used to make chocolate and to give a chocolate taste to foods`

# 同义

n. [作物]可可粉；可可豆；可可饮料；深褐色
`cacao powder` `cacao bean`

# 短语

- `cocoa butter` n. 可可油
- `cocoa powder` 可可粉
- `cocoa bean` 可可豆
- `cocoa mass` 可可浆；可可块

# 例句

a cup of cocoa
一杯可可
