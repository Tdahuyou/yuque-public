# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'plɑːnt/`
- 美 `/ɪm'plænt/`

# 词义

v. 种植；灌输；嵌入
`to strongly fix an idea, feeling, attitude etc in someone’s mind or character`

n. [医]植入物；植入管
`something artificial that is put into someone’s body in a medical operation`

# 同义

vt. 种植；灌输；嵌入
`crop` `plant` `grow` `bed`

# 同根

- n. `implantation` 移植；灌输；鼓吹

# 短语

- `dental implant` 牙移植
- `cochlear implant` 人工耳蜗；耳蜗植入；耳郭灌输治疗

# 例句

Surgeons successfully implanted an artificial hip.
外科医生成功地植入了一个人工髋关节。
