# 音标

- 英 `/fɔːn/`
- 美 `/fɔn/`

# 词义

v. 奉承
`to seek attention and admiration (from) by cringing and flattering `

n. 小鹿；浅黄褐色；小动物
`a young  deer `

adj. 浅黄褐色的
`having a pale yellow-brown colour`

# 同义

vi. 奉承
`honey` `lick one's boots`

n. 小鹿；浅黄褐色；小动物
`beastie`

adj. 浅黄褐色的
`cinnamon`

vt. 生（小鹿或小动物）
`bring into the world` `pup`

# 短语

- `fawn on` v. 奉承；讨好

# 例句

The fawn ran to the top of the ridge.
那只小鹿跑上山脊。
