# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'greɪʃɪeɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'ɡreʃɪet/`

# 词义

v. 使迎合；使讨好；使逢迎
`If someone tries to ingratiate themselves with you, they do things to try and make you like them`

# 同根

- adj. `ingratiating` 逢迎的，讨好的；迷人的，吸引人的
- adj. `ingratiatory` 逢迎的；讨好的
- adv. `ingratiatingly` 讨好地；奉承地
- n. `ingratiation` 逢迎；讨好

# 例句

Many politicians are trying to ingratiate themselves with her.
很多政客都在试图讨她的欢心。
