# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'feɪm/`
- 美 `/di'feim/`

# 词义

v. 诽谤；中伤
`to write or say bad or untrue things about someone or something, so that people will have a bad opinion of them`

# 同义

v. 诽谤；中伤
`speak evil of` `stab in the back`

# 同根

- adj. `defamatory` 诽谤的；中伤的；破坏名誉的
- n. `defamation` 诽谤；中伤

# 例句

Sgt. Norwood complained that the article defamed him.
诺维德中士抱怨这篇文章诽谤他。
