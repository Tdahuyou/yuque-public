# 音标

- 英 `/'glɑːshaʊs/`
- 美 `/'ɡlæshaʊs/`

# 词义

n. 温室；暖房
`a building made mainly of glass which is used for growing plants`

# 同义

n. [园艺]温室；暖房
`greenhouse` `hot-house`
