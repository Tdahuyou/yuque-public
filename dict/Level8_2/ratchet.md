# 音标

- 英 `/'rætʃɪt/`
- 美 `/'rætʃɪt/`

# 词义

n. 棘轮；棘齿
`a machine part consisting of a wheel or bar with teeth on it, which allows movement in only one direction`

v. 安装棘轮于；松脱


# 同义

n. [机]棘轮；棘齿
`rachet`

vt. 安装棘轮于；松脱
`get loose`

# 短语

- `ratchet wheel` n. [机]棘轮
- `ratchet effect` 齿轮效应
- `ratchet up` 逐渐升高；略微调高

# 例句

The chair has a ratchet below it to adjust the height.
这把椅子下面有一个棘轮来调整高度。
