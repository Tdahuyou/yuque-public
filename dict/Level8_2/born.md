# 音标

- 英 `/bɔːn/`
- 美 `/bɔrn/`

# 词义

v. 出世（bear的过去分词）
`when a person or animal is born, they come out of their mother’s body or out of an egg`

adj. 天生的
`someone who has a strong natural ability to lead, play music etc`

n. (Born)人名；(柬)邦；(英、西、俄、捷、德、瑞典、匈)博恩


# 同义

adj. 天生的
`natural` `inborn`

# 同根

- n. `bear` 熊
- n. `birth` 出生；血统，出身；起源
- vi. `bear` 承受；结果实
- vt. `bear` 忍受；具有；支撑

# 短语

- `was born` 出生
- `born on` 出生于（时间）
- `born of` 源于，出生于
- `born into` ..家庭
- `born again` 再生
- `be born with` 天生具有
- `born yesterday` 乳臭未干
- `first born` adj. 头生的；最长的
- `born approximation` 玻恩近似；玻饵似
- `born to win` 天生大赢家；强者的诞生；生而为赢
- `born days` [口语]有生之日
- `born in the purple` 出身显贵

# 例句

Forty lambs were born this spring.
今年春天有40头小羊羔出生。

I was born and raised (=  was born and grew up  ) in Alabama.
我是土生土长的亚拉巴马人。

a newly-born baby
新生儿，初生婴儿

the queen’s firstborn son
女王的长子
