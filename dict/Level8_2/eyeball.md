# 音标

- 英 `/'aɪbɔːl/`
- 美 `/'aɪbɔl/`

# 词义

n. 眼球；眼珠子
`the round ball that forms the whole of your eye, including the part inside your head`

v. 盯住看；仔细对…打量
`to look directly and closely at something or someone`

# 同义

n. [解剖]眼球；眼珠子
`augen`

vi. 仔细打量；盯着
`be glued to` `take aim at`

# 短语

- `eyeball to eyeball` 面对面；对峙

# 例句

They eyeballed each other suspiciously.
他们怀疑地互相打量。
