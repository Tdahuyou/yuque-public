# 音标

- 英 `/peɪv/`
- 美 `/pev/`

# 词义

v. 铺设；安排；作铺设之用
`If a road or an area of ground has been paved, it has been covered with asphalt or concrete, so that it is suitable for walking or driving on`

n. (Pave)人名；(西、塞)帕韦


# 同义

vt. 铺设；安排；作铺设之用
`schedule` `budget` `settle` `place in`

# 同根

- n. `paving` [建] 铺砌；铺路材料；块石面路
- n. `pavage` 铺路；铺设；铺路税
- v. `paving` 铺设；为…做准备（pave的ing形式）

# 短语

- `pave the way for` 为…做好准备；为…铺平道路
- `pave the way` 为...作准备；为...铺平道路
- `paved with good intentions` 充满着善意的

# 例句

The avenue had never been paved, and deep mud made it impassable in winter.
这条大街从来没有铺过，深泥使其在冬天无法通行。
