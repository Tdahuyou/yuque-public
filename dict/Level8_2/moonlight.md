# 音标

- 英 `/'muːnlaɪt/`
- 美 `/'munlaɪt/`

# 词义

adj. 月夜的；有月光的


n. 月光
`the light of the moon`

v. 兼职，夜袭
`to have a second job in addition to your main job, especially without the knowledge of the government tax department`

# 同根

- n. `moonflower` 月光花
- n. `moonlighter` 参与夜袭之人；非法酿酒或贩酒的人；兼差之人；月光团员

# 短语

- `by moonlight` 借月光
- `moonlight shadow` 月光下的影子；月影

# 例句

They walked along the road in the moonlight.
他们在月光下沿路散步。
