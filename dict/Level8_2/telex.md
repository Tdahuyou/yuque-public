# 音标

- 英 `/'teleks/`
- 美 `/'tɛlɛks/`

# 词义

n. 电传；电传机；用户直通电报
`a method of communica-tion, in which messages are written on a special machine and then sent using the telephone network`

v. 用电传拍发；用直通电报拍发
`If you telex a message to someone, you send it to them by telex`

# 同根

- n. `teletypewriter` 电传打字机
- vi. `televise` 电视播放
- vt. `televise` 用电视播放

# 短语

- `telex release` 电放；电放费；电报放货
- `tested telex` 加押电传
