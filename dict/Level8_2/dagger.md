# 音标

- 英 `/'dægə/`
- 美 `/'dæɡɚ/`

# 词义

n. 匕首，短剑
`a short pointed knife used as a weapon`

vt. 用剑刺


# 同义

n. 匕首，短剑
`knife` `stiletto`

# 例句

He was stabbed with a dagger.
他被刺了一匕首。

The murderer dubbed a dagger into his heart.
凶手将匕首刺进他的心脏。

He was heavily armed—an air rifle in one hand, a wooden dagger in the other.
他可是全副武装呢，一只手拿着气枪，另一只手握着一把木制匕首。
