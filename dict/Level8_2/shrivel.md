# 音标

- 英 `/ʃrɪv(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'ʃrɪvl/`

# 词义

v. 枯萎；皱缩
`if something shrivels, or if it is shrivelled, it becomes smaller and its surface becomes covered in lines because it is very dry or old`

# 同义

vi. 枯萎；皱缩
`flag` `wither away`

vt. 使枯萎；使皱缩；使束手无策
`blast` `wither up`

# 例句

The leaves change colour, then shrivel.
树叶变了颜色，然后干枯了。
