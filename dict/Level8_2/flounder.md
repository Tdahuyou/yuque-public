# 音标

- 英 `/'flaʊndə/`
- 美 `/'flaʊndɚ/`

# 词义

v. 挣扎，折腾；错乱地做事或说话
`to have a lot of problems and be likely to fail completely`

n. 挣扎，辗转；比目鱼
`a European flatfish, Platichthys flesus having a greyish-brown body covered with prickly scales: family Pleuronectidae: an important food fish `
