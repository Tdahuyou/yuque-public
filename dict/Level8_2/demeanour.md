# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'miːnə/`
- 美 `/di'mi:nə/`

# 词义

n. 行为
`Your demeanour is the way you behave, which gives people an impression of your character and feelings`

# 同义

n. 行为
`behavior` `dealing` `going` `act` `work`

# 同根

- n. `demeanor` 风度；举止；行为

# 短语

- `elegant demeanour` 风采

# 例句

...her calm and cheerful demeanour.
…她那冷静乐观的态度。
