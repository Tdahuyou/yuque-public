# 音标

- 英 `/mæl'fʌŋ(k)ʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,mæl'fʌŋkʃən/`

# 词义

v. 发生故障；不起作用
`If a machine or part of the body malfunctions, it fails to work properly`

n. 故障；失灵；疾病
`a fault in the way a machine or part of someone’s body works`

# 同义

vi. 发生故障；不起作用
`get out of order` `be out of order`

n. 故障；失灵；疾病
`fault` `trouble` `complaint` `sickness`

# 例句

The radiation can damage microprocessors and computer memories, causing them to malfunction.
该辐射会破坏微处理器和计算机存储器，使之出故障。
