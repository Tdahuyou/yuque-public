# 音标

- 英 `/dɪsɪ'luːʒ(ə)nd; -'ljuː-/`
- 美 `/,dɪsɪ'luʒnd/`

# 词义

adj. 醒悟的；幻想破灭的；不抱幻想的
`disappointed because you have lost your belief that someone is good, or that an idea is right`

v. 使幻想破灭（disillusion的过去分词）；唤醒


# 同义

v. 使幻想破灭（disillusion的过去分词）；唤醒
`woken` `awoken`

# 例句

I've become very disillusioned with politics.
我变得对政治非常失望。
