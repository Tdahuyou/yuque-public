# 音标

- 英 `/'frɒs(t)baɪt/`
- 美 `/'frɔst'baɪt/`

# 词义

vt. 使冻伤；使受霜害


vi. 参加冬季帆船比赛


n. 冻伤；冻疮；霜寒
`a condition caused by extreme cold, that makes your fingers and toes swell, become darker, and sometimes fall off`

# 同义

n. [外科]冻伤；冻疮；霜寒
`cold injury` `frozen injury`

# 同根

- adj. `frosted` 被霜覆盖的；冻伤的；冷淡的；无光泽的
- adj. `frostbitten` 冻伤的；受霜害的
- v. `frosted` 下霜
- v. `frostbitten` 使冻伤（frostbite的过去分词）

# 例句

I nearly got frostbite .
我险些冻伤。
