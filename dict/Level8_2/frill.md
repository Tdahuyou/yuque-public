# 音标

- 英 `/frɪl/`
- 美 `/frɪl/`

# 词义

n. 装饰；褶边
`a decoration that consists of narrow piece of cloth that has many small folds in it`

vt. 折成皱边


vi. 起边皱


# 同义

n. 装饰；褶边
`decoration` `adornment`

# 同根

- adj. `frilly` 镶褶边的；多饰边的
- adj. `frilled` 饰有褶边的

# 例句

She was wearing a white blouse with frills at the cuffs.
她穿了一件袖口有褶边的白衬衫。
