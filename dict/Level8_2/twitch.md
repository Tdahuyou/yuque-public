# 音标

- 英 `/twɪtʃ/`
- 美 `/twɪtʃ/`

# 词义

n. 抽搐；抽动；痉挛；阵痛
`a quick movement of a muscle, especially one that you cannot control`

v. 抽搐；抽动；阵痛
`if a part of someone’s body twitches, or if they twitch it, it makes a small sudden movement`

# 同义

n. 抽搐；抽动；痉挛；阵痛
`fit` `tic`

vi. 抽搐；抽动；阵痛
`labor pain`

vt. 使抽动；攫取；猛拉
`grab` `pounce at`

# 例句

a nervous twitch
神经性抽搐
