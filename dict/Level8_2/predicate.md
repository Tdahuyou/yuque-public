# 音标

- 英 `/'predɪkət/`
- 美 `/'prɛdɪkət/`

# 词义

v. 断定为…；使…基于


n. 谓语，述语
`the part of a sentence that makes a statement about the subject, such as ‘swim’ in ‘Fish swim’ and ‘is an artist’ in ‘She is an artist’`

adj. 谓语的；述语的


# 同义

vi. 断言，断定
`protest` `conclude`

n. 谓语，述语
`adnex`

# 同根

- adj. `predicative` 表语的；断定性的；谓语性的
- adv. `predicatively` 肯定地；断然地
- n. `predicative` 表语
- n. `predication` 断定；声明；谓项

# 短语

- `(grammatical) predicate` （语法）谓语
- `predicate logic` 谓词逻辑
- `predicate verb` 谓语动词
- `predicate calculus` 谓词演算

# 例句

The company’s expansion was predicated on the assumption that sales would rise.
公司的扩张是基于销售额会增长的假设。
