# 音标

- 英 `/bæʃ/`
- 美 `/bæʃ/`

# 词义

v. 猛击，痛击；怒殴
`to hit someone or something hard, in a way that causes pain or damage`

n. 猛烈的一击，痛击
`a hard strong hit`

# 同义

vt. 猛击，痛击；怒殴
`jab at` `lash out`

n. 猛烈的一击，痛击
`swashing blow` `oner`

# 短语

- `bash in` 打坏

# 例句

Someone bashed him on the back of his head.
有人猛击他的后脑勺。

Police bashed down the door to get in.
警察破门而入。
