# 音标

- 英 `/'θrɒt(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'θrɑtl/`

# 词义

n. 节流阀；[车辆]风门；喉咙
`a piece of equipment that controls the amount of  fuel  going into a vehicle’s engine`

v. 压制，扼杀；使……窒息；使……节流
`to make it difficult or impossible for something to succeed`

# 同义

n. [机]节流阀；[车辆]风门；喉咙
`throttling valve` `THORT`

vt. 压制，扼杀；使……窒息；使……节流
`kill` `silence`

vi. [机]节流，减速；窒息
`slow down` `speed reduction`

# 同根

- n. `throttling` 节气，节流；油门调节

# 短语

- `throttle valve` 节流阀
- `at full throttle` adv. 以最高速度；油门大开地
- `throttle control` 节流操纵；节流控制；推力调节
- `full throttle` 加足马力；全节流
- `hand throttle` 手油门；手节气门；手风门

# 例句

The team’s offense ran full throttle.
球队开始全力进攻。
