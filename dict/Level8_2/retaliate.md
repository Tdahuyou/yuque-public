# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'tælɪeɪt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'tælɪ'et/`

# 词义

v. 报复；回敬
`to do something bad to someone because they have done something bad to you`

# 同义

vi. 报复；回敬
`get back on` `get even with`

vt. 报复
`get back on` `get even with` `get back at`

# 同根

- adj. `retaliatory` 报复的
- n. `retaliation` 报复；反击；回敬

# 例句

I was sorely tempted to retaliate.
我非常想报复。
