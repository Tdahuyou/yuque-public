# 音标

- 英 `/θʌd/`
- 美 `/θʌd/`

# 词义

n. 砰的一声；重击
`the low sound made by a heavy object hitting something else`

v. 砰的一声掉下；发出砰声
`If something thuds somewhere, it makes a dull sound, usually when it falls onto or hits something else`

# 同义

n. 砰的一声；重击
`pound` `devel`

# 例句

His head hit the floor with a sickening thud .
他的头撞在地上，发出一声让人心惊的闷响。

She ran up the stairs, her bare feet thudding on the wood.
她跑上楼梯，光脚在木头上发出噔噔的声音。
