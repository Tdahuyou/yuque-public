# 音标

- 英 `/'plʌndə/`
- 美 `/'plʌndɚ/`

# 词义

n. 抢夺；战利品；掠夺品
`things that have been stolen during a violent attack, especially during a war`

v. 掠夺；抢劫；侵吞
`to steal large amounts of money or property from somewhere, especially while fighting in a war`

# 同义

n. 抢夺；战利品；掠夺品
`prize` `capture`

vt. 掠夺；抢劫；侵吞
`harry` `despoil`

vi. 掠夺；盗窃
`prey upon` `pillage`

# 同根

- n. `plunderage` 掠夺；盗用船货

# 例句

Henry’s army returned loaded down with plunder.
亨利的部队满载战利品而归。
