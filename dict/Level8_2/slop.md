# 音标

- 英 `/slɒp/`
- 美 `/slɑp/`

# 词义

v. 溢出，使溅出
`if liquid slops somewhere, it moves around or over the edge of a container in an uncontrolled way`

# 同义

vt. 溢出，使溅出
`flood` `spill over`

vi. 溅出；泼洒
`spill out`

# 同根

- adj. `sloping` 倾斜的；有坡度的；成斜坡的
- v. `sloping` 溢出（slop的ing形式）

# 例句

Coffee slopped over the rim of her cup.
咖啡从她的杯口溢了出来。

With each wave, more water slopped into the cabin.
随着一个个浪头打来，更多的水漫进了船舱。
