# 音标

- 英 `/'fɔːmən/`
- 美 `/'fɔrmən/`

# 词义

n. 领班；陪审团主席
`a worker who is in charge of a group of other workers, for example in a factory`

# 同义

n. [劳经]领班；陪审团主席
`gaffer` `ganger`

# 同根

- n. `forelady` 女工头；女领班；女工监督
- n. `foremanship` 基层管理能力

# 例句

There was applause as the foreman of the jury announced the verdict.
陪审团团长宣布裁定结果时响起了一片掌声。
