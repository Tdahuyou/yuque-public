# 词义

n. 狱卒，看守监狱的人
`someone who is in charge of guarding a prison or prisoners`

# 同根

- n. `jail` 监狱；监牢；拘留所
- n. `jailhouse` 监狱
- n. `jailor` 狱卒；看守监狱的人
- vt. `jail` 监禁；下狱

# 例句

He took with him several of his subordinates, and amongst them Dantès' jailer.
他带走了几个下属，看管唐太斯的狱卒也在其中。
