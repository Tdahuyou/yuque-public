# 音标

- 英 `/kriːs/`
- 美 `/kris/`

# 词义

n. 折痕；折缝
`Creases are lines that are made in cloth or paper when it is crushed or folded`

v. 起皱
`to become marked with a line or lines, or to make a line appear on cloth, paper etc by folding or crushing it`

# 同义

n. 折痕；折缝
`fell` `fold`

vi. 起皱
`wrinkle`

vt. 弄皱；使起折痕
`crush` `crumple`

# 短语

- `crease mark` 摺痕；折痕

# 例句

She stood up, frowning at the creases in her silk dress.
她站起来，看着丝裙上的折痕皱起了眉。

...cream-coloured trousers with sharp creases.
…裤缝笔挺的乳白色裤子。
