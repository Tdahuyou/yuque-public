# 音标

- 英 `/fluːt/`
- 美 `/flut/`

# 词义

n. 长笛；【工程设计】(刀具的)出屑槽
`a musical instrument like a thin pipe, that you play by holding it across your lips, blowing over a hole, and pressing down buttons with your fingers`

v. 用长笛吹奏


# 同根

- n. `fluting` 吹笛子；笛声；[机] 开槽
- n. `flutist` 笛手；横笛吹奏者（等于flautist）
- v. `fluting` 吹奏长笛（flute的ing形式）

# 短语

- `reed flute cave` 芦笛岩

# 例句

The boy trilled on a flute.
男孩在长笛上吹颤音。

He played a nice tune on the flute.
他吹奏了一首优美的笛子曲。
