# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌbtaɪt(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'sʌb'taɪtl/`

# 词义

n. 副标题；说明或对白的字幕
`the words printed over a film in a foreign language to translate what is being said by the actors`

v. 在…上印字幕；给…加副标题
`If you say how a book or play is subtitled, you say what its subtitle is`

# 同义

n. [计][图情]副标题；说明或对白的字幕
`subhead`

vt. 在…上印字幕；给…加副标题
`subgroup`

# 例句

a French film with English subtitles
附有英语字幕的法国电影

"Lorna Doone" is subtitled "a Romance of Exmoor."
《洛娜·杜恩》的副标题是《埃克斯穆尔的一个浪漫故事》。
