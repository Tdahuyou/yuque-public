# 音标

- 英 `/,sæbə'tɜː/`
- 美 `/,sæbə'tjʊr/`

# 词义

n. 怠工者，破坏者；从事破坏活动者
`someone who deliberately damages, destroys, or spoils someone else’s property or activities, in order to prevent them from doing something`

# 同义

n. 怠工者，破坏者；从事破坏活动者
`destructor` `disruptor`

# 例句

The lorries were wrecked by saboteurs.
这些大卡车被人蓄意损毁。
