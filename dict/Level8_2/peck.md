# 音标

- 英 `/pek/`
- 美 `/pɛk/`

# 词义

v. 啄食；扔石头；吹毛求疵
`if a bird pecks something or pecks at something, it makes quick repeated movements with its beak to try to eat part of it, make a hole in it etc`

n. 许多；配克（容量单位，等于2加仑）；啄痕；快速轻吻
`an action in which a bird pecks someone or something with its beak`

# 同义

vi. 啄食；扔石头；吹毛求疵
`cavil about` `find quarrel in a straw`

vt. 啄食；扔
`throw into`

n. 许多；配克（容量单位，等于2加仑）；啄痕
`lots of` `sea` `hundred` `much` `thousands of`

# 同根

- n. `pecker` 啄木鸟；鹤嘴锄；穿孔器；精神；阴茎

# 短语

- `a peck of` 许多，大量
- `peck at` 啄；少量地吃
- `gregory peck` n. 格里高利·派克（美国男影星）

# 例句

It was winter and the sparrows were pecking at whatever they could find.
已是冬天了，麻雀们能找到什么就啄食什么。
