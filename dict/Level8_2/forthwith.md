# 音标

- 英 `/fɔːθ'wɪθ; -ð/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

adv. 立刻，立即；不犹豫地
`immediately`

# 同义

adv. 立刻，立即；不犹豫地
`rapidly` `soon` `shortly` `directly` `now`

# 同根

- adj. `forthright` 直率的；直截了当的
- adv. `forthright` 直率地；马上；立即
- n. `forthright` 直路

# 例句

He was fined £40, with 28 days’ imprisonment if the money was not produced forthwith.
他被罚款40英镑，如不立即缴付则监禁28天。
