# 音标

- 英 `/'swɪv(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'swɪvl/`

# 词义

n. 转环；转椅座架


v. 使旋转
`to turn around quickly and face a different direction, or to make something do this`

# 同义

vt. [机]使旋转
`turn` `spin` `swing`

vi. [机]旋转
`wheel` `circle` `spin` `go round and round`

# 短语

- `swivel chair` n. 转椅
- `swivel joint` [机]回转接头；旋转管接头

# 例句

I spun around in my swivel chair and chatted with Jim.
我转动着我的旋转椅同一旁的吉姆聊起天来了。
