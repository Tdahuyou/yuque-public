# 音标

- 英 `/grʌnt/`
- 美 `/ɡrʌnt/`

# 词义

n. 咕哝；呼噜声
`a short low sound that a person or animal makes in their throat`

v. 作呼噜声；发哼声
`if a person or animal grunts, they make short low sounds in their throat`

# 同义

n. 咕哝；呼噜声
`oink`

vi. 作呼噜声；发哼声
`harrumph`

# 同根

- n. `grunter` 猪；作猪哼者

# 例句

Chris gave a grunt and went back to sleep.
克里斯哼了一声，又继续睡了。
