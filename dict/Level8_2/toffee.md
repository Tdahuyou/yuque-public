# 音标

- 英 `/'tɒfɪ/`
- 美 `/'tɔfi/`

# 词义

n. 乳脂糖，太妃糖
`a sticky sweet brown substance that you can eat, made by boiling sugar, water, and butter together, or a piece of this substance`

# 同义

n. 乳脂糖，太妃糖
`taffy`

# 同根

- n. `toffy` 乳脂糖；太妃糖（等于toffee）
