# 音标

- 英 `/'reprɪmɑːnd/`
- 美 `/'rɛprɪmænd/`

# 词义

n. 谴责；训斥；申诉
`Reprimand is also a noun`

v. 谴责；训斥；责难
`to tell someone officially that something they have done is very wrong`

# 同义

n. 谴责；训斥；申诉
`hell` `condemnation`

vt. 谴责；训斥；责难
`condemn` `denounce`

# 例句

He has been fined five thousand dollars and given a severe reprimand.
他被罚款5000美元，并且受到严厉的训斥。
