# 音标

- 英 `/fe'stuːn/`
- 美 `/fɛ'stʊn/`

# 词义

v. 结彩于；[建]以花彩装饰
`to cover something with flowers, long pieces of material etc, especially for decoration`

n. 花彩；[建]花彩装饰物
`a long thin piece of material, flowers etc, used especially for decoration`

# 同根

- n. `festoonery` 花彩装饰，彩饰

# 例句

The temples are festooned with lights.
该庙宇装有灯饰。
