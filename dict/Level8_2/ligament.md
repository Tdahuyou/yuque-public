# 音标

- 英 `/'lɪgəm(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'lɪɡəmənt/`

# 词义

n. 韧带；纽带，系带
`a band of strong material in your body, similar to muscle, that joins bones or holds an organ in its place`

# 同义

n. 韧带；纽带，系带
`tache` `retinaculum`

# 短语

- `anterior cruciate ligament` 前交叉韧带
- `periodontal ligament` n. 牙周韧带
- `collateral ligament` 侧韧带

# 例句

He tore a ligament in his left knee.
他拉伤了左膝韧带。

damaged ankle ligaments 
受伤的踝关节韧带
