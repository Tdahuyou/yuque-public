# 音标

- 英 `/heə'lɪp/`
- 美 `/,hɛə'lɪp/`

# 词义

n. 兔唇，[口腔]唇裂
`an offensive word for the condition of having your top lip divided into two parts because it did not develop correctly before birth`

# 同义

n. 兔唇，[口腔]唇裂
`cleft lip`

# 例句

Such as harelip and heart can check out first?
比如兔唇和先心能否检查出来？

Why does a baby suffer a harelip ? Is there any prevention?
兔唇宝宝是怎样造成的，能有效预防吗？

In walks this man -- short, ugly, balding, and speaking with a strange harelip.
那个人走进来——又矮，又丑，还秃顶，嘴上带着奇怪的唇裂。
