# 音标

- 英 `/daɪs/`
- 美 `/daɪs/`

# 词义

v. 切成方块
`to cut food into small square pieces`

n. 骰子
`a small block of wood, plastic etc that has six sides with a different number of spots on each side, used in games`

# 同义

n. 骰子
`cube`

vi. 掷骰子
`shake the elbows` `crap`

# 同根

- n. `dicer` 帽子，小礼帽；玩掷骰子游戏的人

# 短语

- `no dice` [俚语]不行；不成功
- `play with loaded dice` 使用欺诈手段,暗中搞鬼[亦作play with marked cards]

# 例句

diced carrots
切成丁的胡萝卜
