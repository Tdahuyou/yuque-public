# 音标

- 英 `/əʊvə'raɪd/`
- 美 `/,ovɚ'raɪd/`

# 词义

v. 推翻；不顾；践踏
`If someone in authority overrides a person or their decisions, they cancel their decisions`

n. 代理佣金


# 同义

vt. 推翻；不顾；践踏
`throw down` `topple down`

n. [会计]代理佣金
`agency commission`

# 同根

- adj. `overriding` 高于一切的，最重要的
- v. `overriding` 践踏；压垮；不顾（override的ing形式）

# 短语

- `manual override` 手动超越控制；并联手动阀

# 例句

The president vetoed the bill, and the Senate failed by a single vote to override his veto.
总统否决了议案，而参议院因一票之差未能推翻他的否决。
