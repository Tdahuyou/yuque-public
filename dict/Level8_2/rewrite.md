# 音标

- 英 `/riː'raɪt/`
- 美 `/'riraɪt/`

# 词义

v. 改写；重写
`to change something that has been written, especially in order to improve it, or because new information is available`

n. 重写或改写的文稿；改写的作品


# 同义

vt. 改写；[计]重写
`reword` `overtype`

vi. 改写；[计]重写
`write over`

# 同根

- n. `rewriting` 重写；改写
- v. `rewriting` 重写；改写（rewrite的ing形式）

# 例句

I’ll have to rewrite most of the essay.
这篇论文大部分我都得重写。
