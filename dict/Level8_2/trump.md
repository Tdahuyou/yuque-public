# 音标

- 英 `/trʌmp/`
- 美 `/trʌmp/`

# 词义

n. 王牌；法宝；喇叭
`the  suit (= one of the four types of cards in a set )  chosen to have a higher value than the other suits in a particular card game`

v. 胜过；打出王牌赢
`to play a trump that beats someone else’s card in a game`

# 同义

n. 王牌；法宝；喇叭
`horn` `matador`

vt. 胜过；打出王牌赢
`overcome` `exceed` `cap` `transcend`

vi. 出王牌；吹喇叭
`blow job`

# 短语
