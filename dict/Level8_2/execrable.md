# 音标

- 英 `/'eksɪkrəb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'ɛksɪkrəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 恶劣的；可憎恨的
`If you describe something as execrable, you mean that it is very bad or unpleasant`

# 同义

adj. 恶劣的；可憎恨的
`gross` `dirty` `wicked`

# 例句

Accusing us of being disloyal to cover his own sorry behaviour is truly execrable.
用指控我们的不忠来掩盖他个人的不当行为真是很恶劣。

...an execrable meal.
...糟透了的一顿饭。
