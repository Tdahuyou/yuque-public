# 音标

- 英 `/skraʊn(d)ʒ/`
- 美 `/skraʊndʒ/`

# 词义

v. 搜寻；乞讨
`to get money or something you want by asking other people for it rather than by paying for it yourself`

n. 讨要；索要
`to be trying to get money or things you want by asking other people for them`

# 同义

vi. 搜寻；乞讨
`search` `hunt for`

vt. 搜寻；乞讨；骗取
`search` `fish`

# 例句

We managed to scrounge every piece of gear you requested.
我们设法要来了你要求的每一件用具。
