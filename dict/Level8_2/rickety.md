# 音标

- 英 `/'rɪkɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/'rɪkəti/`

# 词义

adj. 摇晃的；虚弱的；患佝偻病的
`A rickety structure or piece of furniture is not very strong or well made, and seems likely to collapse or break`

# 同义

adj. 摇晃的；虚弱的；患佝偻病的
`weak` `shaky`

# 同根

- n. `rickets` [内科] 佝偻病
- n. `ricketiness` 摇摆；不牢靠

# 例句

Mona climbed the rickety wooden stairs.
莫娜爬上了摇摇晃晃的木楼梯。
