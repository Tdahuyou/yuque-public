# 音标

- 英 `/'skʌf(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'skʌfl/`

# 词义

v. 扭打；乱斗；拖着脚走路
`to have a short fight with someone, in a way that is not very serious or violent`

n. 混战；扭打
`a short fight that is not very violent`

# 同义

vi. 扭打；乱斗；拖着脚走路
`tussle`

n. 混战；扭打
`melee` `mix-up`

# 例句

Police scuffled with some of the protesters.
警察和一些抗议者打了起来。
