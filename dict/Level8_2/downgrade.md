# 音标

- 英 `/'daʊngreɪd; daʊn'greɪd/`
- 美 `/,daʊn'ɡred/`

# 词义

n. 退步；下坡


adj. 下坡的


adv. 下坡地


v. 使降级（过去式downgraded，过去分词downgraded，现在分词downgrading，第三人称单数downgrades）；小看
`to state that something is not as serious as it was`

# 同义

n. 退步；下坡
`setback` `declivity`

adj. 下坡的
`declivous`

# 短语

- `on the downgrade` 在走下坡路；衰落；每况愈下

# 例句

Hurricane Bob has been downgraded to a tropical storm.
飓风 “鲍勃” 已被降级为热带风暴。
