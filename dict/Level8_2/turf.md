# 音标

- 英 `/tɜːf/`
- 美 `/tɝf/`

# 词义

n. 草皮；泥炭；跑马场
`a surface that consists of soil with grass on top, or an artificial surface that looks like this`

v. 覆草皮于
`to cover an area of land with turf`

# 同义

n. [建]草皮；泥炭；跑马场
`greensward` `divot`

# 短语

- `on the turf` ◎以赛马为业；赛马场上的赌客
- `artificial turf` 人造草皮

# 例句

soft green turf
柔软的绿色草皮
