# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'mɔːsf(ʊ)l/`
- 美 `/rɪ'mɔrsfəl/`

# 词义

adj. 懊悔的；悔恨的
`If you are remorseful, you feel very guilty and sorry about something wrong that you have done`

# 同义

adj. 懊悔的；悔恨的
`rueful`

# 同根

- adv. `remorselessly` 无悔意地；冷酷地
- adv. `remorsefully` 极为懊悔地
- n. `remorse` 懊悔；同情

# 例句

He was genuinely remorseful.
他发自内心地感到懊悔。
