# 音标

- 英 `/'hʌs(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'hʌsl/`

# 词义

v. 催促；猛推；强夺
`If you hustle someone, you try to make them go somewhere or do something quickly, for example by pulling or pushing them along`

n. 推；奔忙；拥挤喧嚷
`busy and noisy activity`

# 同义

vt. 催促；猛推；强夺
`crowd` `urge`

vi. 赶紧；硬挤过去；拼命挣钱
`make haste` `jump to it`

n. 推；奔忙；拥挤喧嚷
`push` `jundy`

# 同根

- n. `hustler` 催促者；妓女；骗子

# 短语

- `hustle and bustle` 熙熙攘攘，忙碌

# 例句

The guards hustled Harry out of the car.
卫兵们把哈里从车子里拉了出来。
