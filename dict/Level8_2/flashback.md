# 音标

- 英 `/'flæʃbæk/`
- 美 `/'flæʃbæk/`

# 词义

n. 倒叙；闪回；迷幻药效幻觉重现
`a scene in a film, play, book etc that shows something that happened before that point in the story`

# 同义

n. 倒叙；闪回；迷幻药效幻觉重现
`backflash`

# 例句

The events of the hero’s childhood are shown as a series of flashbacks.
主人公童年的一些事情用一连串闪回镜头展现出来。
