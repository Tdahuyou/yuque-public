# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒkrəʊtʃ/`
- 美 `/'kɑk'rotʃ/`

# 词义

n. [昆]蟑螂
`a large black or brown insect that lives in dirty houses, especially if they are warm and there is food to eat`

# 同义

n. [昆]蟑螂
`black beetle` `roach`

# 例句

He stumbled to the door and found a six-foot cockroach standing there.
他跌跌撞撞地去打开门，见到一只六英尺高的蟑螂站在那儿！

He saw the cockroach scurry under it with the gecko far behind struggling to go under.
蟑螂急匆匆的从冰箱底下跑过去，壁虎在后面也努力的想要钻过去。

Competitions include Skating Around Poverty and Welfare Hurdles. Mascots include a cockroach and a rat.
比赛项目包括贫穷和福利绕障碍滑冰，吉祥物是蟑螂和老鼠。
