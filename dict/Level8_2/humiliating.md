# 音标

- 英 `/hjʊ'mɪlɪeɪtɪŋ/`
- 美 `/hju:'milieitiŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 丢脸的；羞辱性的
`making you feel ashamed, embarrassed, and angry because you have been made to look weak or stupid`

v. 使蒙耻（humiliate的ing形式）


# 同义

adj. 丢脸的；羞辱性的
`discreditable`

# 同根

- adj. `humiliated` 羞辱的
- n. `humiliation` 丢脸，耻辱；蒙羞；谦卑
- v. `humiliated` 屈辱（humiliate的过去式）；使…丢脸，使…蒙羞
- vt. `humiliate` 羞辱；使…丢脸；耻辱

# 例句

a humiliating defeat
丢脸的失败
