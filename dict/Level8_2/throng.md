# 音标

- 英 `/θrɒŋ/`
- 美 `/θrɔŋ/`

# 词义

n. 人群；众多
`a large group of people in one place`

v. 群集；挤满
`if people throng a place, they go there in large numbers`

adj. 拥挤的


# 同义

n. 人群；众多
`crowd` `mass`

vt. 群集；挤满
`burst at the seams` `be packed with`

vi. 蜂拥而至；群集
`host` `pack`

adj. 拥挤的
`crowded` `jammed`

# 例句

She got lost in the throng.
她在人群中迷路了。
