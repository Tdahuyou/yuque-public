# 音标

- 英 `/,gaɪnɪ'kɒlədʒɪ; dʒ-/`
- 美 `/,ɡaini'kɔlədʒi/`

# 词义

n. 妇科医学
`the study and treatment of medical conditions and illnesses that affect only women, and usually relating to a woman’s ability to have babies`

# 同义

n. 妇科医学
`gynecology`

# 同根

- adj. `gynecological` 妇科的；妇产科医学的
- adj. `gynaecological` 妇科的；妇科学的
- adj. `gynecologic` 妇产科医学的
- n. `gynecologist` 妇科医生
- n. `gynecology` 妇科学，妇科医学
- n. `gynaecologist` 妇科学家
