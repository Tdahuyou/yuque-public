# 音标

- 英 `/ə'tend(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/ə'tɛndənt/`

# 词义

adj. 伴随的；侍候的
`relating to or caused by something`

n. 服务员，侍者；随员，陪从
`someone whose job is to look after or help customers in a public place`

# 同义

adj. 伴随的；侍候的
`accompanied`

n. 服务员，侍者；随员，陪从
`waiter` `suite`

# 同根

- n. `attendance` 出席；到场；出席人数
- vi. `attend` 出席；致力于；照料；照顾
- vt. `attend` 出席；上（大学等）；照料；招待；陪伴

# 短语

- `flight attendant` 空中服务人员
- `room attendant` 客房服务员
- `train attendant` 列车服务员

# 例句

Such patients run an increased probability of hospitalization, with all the attendant costs to the patient and to the health care system that hospitalization entails.
此类患者趋于住院治疗的的几率增加，随之而来的患者和医疗保健系统住院治疗所承担的费用也增加。
