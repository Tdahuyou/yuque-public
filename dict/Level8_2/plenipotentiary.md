# 音标

- 英 `/,plenɪpə'tenʃ(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/'plɛnəpə'tɛnʃəri/`

# 词义

adj. 全权代表的；有全权的
`An ambassador plenipotentiary or minister plenipotentiary has full power or authority to represent their country`

n. 全权代表；全权大使
`someone who has full power to take action or make decisions, especially as a representative of their government in a foreign country`

# 同义

adj. 全权代表的；有全权的
`omnicompetent`

n. 全权代表；全权大使
`full-fledged member`

# 同根

- adv. `plenarily` 充分地；完全地；有全权地

# 例句

...the U.S. Plenipotentiary to the UN conference.
...美国在联合国大会的全权代表。
