# 音标

- 英 `/ə'drɪft/`
- 美 `/ə'drɪft/`

# 词义

adj. 漂泊的；漂浮着的
`a boat that is adrift is not fastened to anything or controlled by anyone`

adv. 随波逐流地；漂浮着


# 同义

adj. 漂泊的；漂浮着的
`vagrom` `anchorless`

# 例句

Several of the lifeboats were still afloat a month after being cast adrift .
放漂一个月后，有几艘救生艇还在漂流。
