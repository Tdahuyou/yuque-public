# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒɒs(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'dʒɑsl/`

# 词义

v. 推挤，撞；争夺
`to push or knock against someone in a crowd, especially so that you can get somewhere or do something before other people`

n. 推撞，挤拥


# 同义

vt. 推挤，撞；争夺
`bump against` `elbow`

vi. 竞争，争夺；推挤
`cope` `rival`

# 同根

- n. `jostling` 冲撞别人的马；推撞

# 例句

A thousand thoughts were jostling around inside my mind.
千头万绪在我脑海中翻涌。
