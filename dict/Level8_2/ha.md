# 音标

- 英 `/hɑː/`
- 美 `/hɑ/`

# 词义

int. 哈！（表示惊异，愉快，怀疑，胜利等）
`used when you are surprised or have discovered something interesting`

abbr. 一种元素(hahnium,原子序105)


n. (Ha)人名；(德)哈；(日)巴(姓)
`used when you are surprised or have discovered something interesting`
