# 音标

- 英 `/,riːdɪ'strɪbjuːt; riː'dɪs-/`
- 美 `/,ridɪ'strɪbjut/`

# 词义

v. 重新分配；再区分
`to give something to each member of a group so that it is divided up in a different way from before`

# 同义

vt. 重新分配；再区分
`reapportion` `agrarianize`

# 同根

- n. `redistribution` [经] 重新分配

# 例句

Wealth was redistributed more equitably among society.
财富在社会上被更公平地重新分配。
