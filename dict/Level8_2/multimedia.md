# 音标

- 英 `/'mʌltɪmiːdɪə/`
- 美 `/,mʌltɪ'midɪə/`

# 词义

n. 多媒体
`You use multimedia to refer to computer programs and products which involve sound, pictures, and film, as well as text`

adj. 多媒体的
`involving computers and computer programs that use a mixture of sound, pictures, video, and writing to give information`

# 短语

- `multimedia message` 彩信，多媒体消息

# 例句

...the case of an insurance company using multimedia to improve customer service in its branches.
…一家保险公司利用多媒体技术改善其分支机构的客户服务的实例。
