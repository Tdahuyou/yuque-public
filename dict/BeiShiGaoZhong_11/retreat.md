# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'triːt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'trit/`

# 词义

n. 隐遁
`A retreat is a quiet, isolated place that you go to in order to rest or to do things in private`



re(向后)+treat(=tract， 拉， 拽)→向后拉→撤退， 退却

# 同义

n. 撤退；休息寓所；撤退
`withdrawal` `pullback`

vi. 撤退；退避；向后倾
`withdraw` `bug out`

# 同根

- n. `retreatment` 再处理；再精制

# 短语

- `retreat into oneself` 退隐；离群索居
- `retreat from` 退出；放弃
- `in full retreat` 全线溃退
- `beat a retreat` [军]撤退；放弃

# 例句

He spent yesterday hidden away in his country retreat.
他躲在乡间的休养地度过了昨天。
