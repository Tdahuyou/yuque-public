# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'zaʊnd/`
- 美 `/rɪ'zaʊnd/`

# 词义

vi. 回响；回荡
`if a place resounds with a sound, it is full of that sound`

# 同义

vi. 回响；鸣响；被传颂
`ring` `sound`

vt. 回响；传颂；大声讲
`talk up`

# 同根

- adj. `resounding` 响亮的；轰动的；彻底的
- v. `resounding` 回响（resound的ing形式）

# 例句

A roar of approval resounded through the legislature.
一片赞成声在议会中回响。
