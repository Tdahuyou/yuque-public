# 词义

adv. 冷淡地
`if you do something coldly, you do it without any emotion or warm feeling, making you seem unfriendly`

# 同义

adv. 冷淡地；不友好地
`strange` `repulsively`

# 同根

- adj. `cold` 寒冷的；冷淡的，不热情的；失去知觉的
- adj. `coldhearted` 冷酷无情的；冷淡的
- adv. `cold` 完全地
- n. `cold` 寒冷；感冒
- n. `coldness` 寒冷；冷淡

# 例句

Janine looked at her coldly.
雅尼娜冷冷地看着她。
