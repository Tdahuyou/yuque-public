# 音标

- 英 `/ðə/`
- 美 `/ðə/`

# 词义

art. 这；那


adv. 更加（用于比较级，最高级前）
`used before two  comparative  adjectives or adverbs to show that the degree of one event or situation is related to the degree of another one`

# 例句

He described the scene to me.
他向我描述了那个情景。

He telegraphed me to go to the party.
他发电报要我去参加那个聚会。

The world exists, whether you like it or not.
不管你喜欢与否，这个世界照样存在。
