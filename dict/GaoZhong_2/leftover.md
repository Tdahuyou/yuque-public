# 音标

- 英 `/'leftəʊvə/`
- 美 `/'lɛftovɚ/`

# 词义

n. 残留物；吃剩的饭菜；剩余物
`food that has not been eaten at the end of a meal`

adj. 残余的；吃剩的
`remaining after all the rest has been used, taken, or eaten`

# 同义

n. 残留物；吃剩的饭菜；剩余物
`remain` `residuum`

adj. 残余的；吃剩的
`remanent` `vestigial`

# 短语

- `leftover material` 边角料；剩余材料

# 例句

Give the leftovers to the dog.
把吃剩的饭菜喂狗。
