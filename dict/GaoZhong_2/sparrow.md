# 音标

- 英 `/'spærəʊ/`
- 美 `/'spæro/`

# 词义

n. 麻雀；矮小的人
`a small brown bird, very common in many parts of the world`

# 同义

n. 麻雀；矮小的人
`small` `spadger`

# 短语

- `house sparrow` n. 麻雀（等于English sparrow）
- `sparrow hawk` 食雀鹰；鹞

# 例句

The myrtle, dove, sparrow, and swan are sacred to her.
桃金娘，鸽子，麻雀和天鹅是她的圣物。

Appearing in great danger, the tiny sparrow tumbled on to the lawn, but softy Flint appeared to have taken pity on him.
跌落在草地上的小麻雀看起来处于非常危险的境地，但是温柔的“弗林特”似乎非常同情他。
