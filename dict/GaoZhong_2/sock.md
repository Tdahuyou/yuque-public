# 音标

- 英 `/sɒk/`
- 美 `/sɑk/`

# 词义

n. 短袜；一击
`a piece of clothing made of soft material that you wear on your foot inside your shoe`

v. 重击；给……穿袜
`If you sock someone or something, you hit them hard`

adv. 正着地；不偏不倚地


adj. 非常成功的


# 同义

n. [服装]短袜；一击
`bobbysocks` `anklet`

vt. 重击；给……穿袜
`pound on` `gang bang`

adv. 正着地；不偏不倚地
`smack-dab`

# 短语

- `sock in` vt. 阻止…飞行；关闭…不许飞机起落

# 例句

a pair of socks
一双短袜

white ankle socks
白色的齐踝短袜

Once, after a boy made a comment, she socked him.
有一次，一个男孩子发表了一点意见，她就挥拳猛击他。
