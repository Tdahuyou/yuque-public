# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒp(ə)lɪ/`
- 美 `/'prɑpɚli/`

# 词义

adv. 适当地；正确地；恰当地
`correctly, or in a way that is considered right`

# 同义

adv. 适当地；正确地；恰当地
`correctly` `exactly` `well` `appropriately` `adequately`

# 同根

- adj. `proper` 适当的；本身的；特有的；正派的
- adv. `proper` 完全地

# 短语

- `behave properly` 行为正当

# 例句

The brakes don’t seem to be working properly.
刹车似乎不正常。

Then he’s not doing his job properly.
那么他没把分内事做好。

Parents should teach their children to behave properly in public.
家长应该教导子女在公共场合要有规有矩。

properly trained staff
训练有素的员工
