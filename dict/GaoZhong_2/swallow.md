# 音标

- 英 `/'swɒləʊ/`
- 美 `/'swɑlo/`

# 词义

v. 忍受；吞没
`If you swallow something, you cause it to go from your mouth down into your stomach`

n. 燕子；一次吞咽的量
`a small black and white bird that comes to northern countries in the summer`



s+wall(墙)+(l)ow → 燕子在墙角垒窝

# 同义

vt. 忍受；吞没
`stomach` `go` `stand` `abide` `tough`

vi. 吞下；咽下
`get sth down` `get down`

# 短语

- `swallow up` 淹没；吞下去；耗尽

# 例句

You are asked to swallow a capsule containing vitamin B.
你被要求吞下一颗含维生素B的胶囊。

Polly took a bite of the apple, chewed, and swallowed.
波莉咬了一口那个苹果，嚼了嚼吞了下去。
