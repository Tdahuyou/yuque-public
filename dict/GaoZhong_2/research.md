# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'sɜːtʃ; 'riːsɜːtʃ/`
- 美 `/'risɝtʃ/`

# 词义

n. 研究；调查
`serious study of a subject, in order to discover new facts or test new ideas`

v. 研究；调查
`to study a subject in detail, especially in order to discover new facts or test new ideas`



re+search (搜寻) →不断搜寻资料进行研究 →研究

# 同义
