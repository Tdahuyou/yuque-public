# 音标

- 英 `/pɜːsə'nel/`
- 美 `/,pɝsə'nɛl/`

# 词义

n. 人事部门；全体人员
`the department in a company that chooses people for jobs and deals with their complaints, problems etc`

adj. 人员的；有关人事的




来自person(人)

# 同义

n. 人事部门；全体人员
`crew` `Personnel Department`
