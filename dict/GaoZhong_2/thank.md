# 音标

- 英 `/θæŋk/`
- 美 `/θæŋk/`

# 词义

v. 感谢
`to tell someone that you are pleased and grateful for something they have done, or to be polite about it`

n. 感谢
`When you express your thanks to someone, you express your gratitude to them for something`

int. 谢谢
`When you express your thanks to someone, you express your gratitude to them for something`

# 同义

vt. 感谢
`be grateful to` `be obliged to`

n. 感谢
`appreciation` `acknowledgment`

int. 谢谢
`merci` `danke` `arigato`

# 同根

- adj. `thankful` 感谢的；欣慰的
- adj. `thankless` 不感谢的；忘恩的；不领情的
- adv. `thankfully` 感谢地；感激地
- n. `thankfulness` 感激

# 短语

- `thank you for` 为某事感谢
- `thank you very much` 非常感谢
- `thank you so much` 非常感谢你
- `thank god` 谢天谢地，幸亏
- `give thanks` 感恩；致谢；感谢
- `thank for` 感谢
- `thank goodness` 谢天谢地；幸亏
- `thank heaven` 谢天谢地

# 例句

I haven’t had a chance to thank him yet.
我还没有机会向他表示谢意。

They accepted their certificates with words of thanks.
他们接过证书，同时表示了谢意。
