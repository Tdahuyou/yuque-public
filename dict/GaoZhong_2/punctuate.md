# 音标

- 英 `/'pʌŋ(k)tʃʊeɪt/`
- 美 `/ˈpʌŋktʃuet/`

# 词义

v. 不时打断；强调；加标点于
`to divide written work into sentences, phrases etc using  comma s  ,  full stop s  etc`

# 同义

vt. 不时打断；强调；加标点于
`emphasize` `stress` `highlight`

# 同根

- n. `punctuation` 标点；标点符号

# 例句

The silence was occasionally punctuated by laughter.
寂静间或被笑声打破。
