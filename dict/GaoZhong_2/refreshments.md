# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'freʃm(ə)nts/`
- 美 `/rɪ'freʃm(ə)nts/`

# 词义

n. 点心；[食品]茶点；小吃
`Refreshments are drinks and small amounts of food that are provided, for example, during a meeting or a trip`

# 同义

n. 点心；[食品]茶点；小吃
`dimsum` `mug-up`

# 短语

- `light refreshments` 点心和饮料

# 例句

Lunch and refreshments will be provided.
将提供午餐和茶点。
