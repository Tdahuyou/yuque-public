# 音标

- 英 `/'məʊtəbaɪk/`
- 美 `/'motɚbaɪk/`

# 词义

n. 摩托车
`a small fast two-wheeled vehicle with an engine`

# 同义

n. 摩托车
`autocycle` `motor cycle`

# 同根

- n. `motorcycling` 摩托运动
- v. `motorcycling` 骑摩托车（motorcycle的ing形式）

# 例句

The motorbike is supposed to make annoying noise.
摩托车当然会发出令人感到烦恼的噪音。

Some earlier witness reports cited by police or security officials had explosives being thrown from a motorbike or the upper storey's of an adjacent hotel.
一些更早的证词报道引用警方或安全部门的话说，爆炸物从摩托车上扔下，或是从领进旅馆的楼上扔下来。
