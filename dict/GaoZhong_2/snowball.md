# 音标

- 英 `/'snəʊbɔːl/`
- 美 `/'snobɔl/`

# 词义

n. 雪球
`a ball of snow that children make and throw at each other`

v. 掷雪球；滚雪球般增大
`if a plan, problem, business etc snow-balls, it grows bigger at a faster and faster rate`

# 同义

n. [水文]雪球
`guelder-rose`

# 例句

We had a massive snowball fight .
我们打了一场大雪仗。
