# 音标

- 英 `/'pez(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'pɛznt/`

# 词义

n. 农民；乡下人
`a poor farmer who owns or rents a small amount of land, either in past times or in poor countries`

# 同义

n. 农民；乡下人
`farmer` `provincial`

# 同根

- n. `peasantry` 农民（总称）；农民身分

# 短语

- `peasant household` 庄户，农户

# 例句

Most villagers are peasant farmers.
大多数村民是农民。
