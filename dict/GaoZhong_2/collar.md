# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒlə/`
- 美 `/'kɑlɚ/`

# 词义

n. 衣领；颈圈
`the part of a shirt, coat etc that fits around your neck, and is usually folded over`

v. 抓住；给…上领子；给…套上颈圈
`to catch someone and hold them so that they cannot escape`



和dollar (n. 美元) 一起记

# 同义

n. [服装]衣领；颈圈
`neck`

vt. 抓住；给…上领子；给…套上颈圈
`grasp` `noble`

# 同根

- adj. `collarless` 无领的；无颈圈的

# 短语

- `white collar` 白领阶层；白领工人
- `blue collar` 蓝领
- `drill collar` 钻铤；钻环
- `in the collar` 行动不自由；受到约束
- `dog collar` 狗脖套
- `roll collar` 辊脊，辊环
- `against the collar` (马上坡时)轭具擦痛肩膀；费力地
- `stiff collar` 硬领

# 例句

He grabbed me by the collar.
他抓住我的衣领。

He loosened his collar and tie .
他松开了领子和领带。
