# 音标

- 英 `/sə'vaɪvə/`
- 美 `/sɚ'vaɪvɚ/`

# 词义

n. 幸存者；生还者；残存物
`If you describe a person or thing as a survivor from an earlier period, you mean that they are still present or available, but other people or things from that period have died or disappeared.`

# 同义

n. 幸存者；生还者；残存物
`leavings`

# 同根

- adj. `surviving` 继续存在的；未死的；依然健在的
- n. `survival` 幸存，残存；幸存者，残存物
- v. `surviving` 生存（survive的ing形式）
- vi. `survive` 幸存；活下来
- vt. `survive` 幸存；生还；幸免于；比...活得长

# 例句

Don’t worry about Kurt; he’s a survivor.
地震的幸存者需要紧急援助。
