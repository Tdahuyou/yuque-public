# 音标

- 英 `/'eseɪ/`
- 美 `/'ɛse/`

# 词义

n. 散文
`a short piece of writing about a particular subject by a student as part of a course of study`



光说(say)不写非文章(essay)

# 同义

n. 散文；试图；随笔
`attempt` `prose`

vt. 尝试；对…做试验
`sample` `have a try`

# 同根

- n. `essayist` 随笔作家，散文家；评论家

# 短语

- `essay test` n. 申论方式测验

# 例句

We asked Jason to write an essay about his hometown.
我们让杰森写一篇关于他家乡的短文。
