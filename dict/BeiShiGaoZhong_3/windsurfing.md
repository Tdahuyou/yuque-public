# 音标

- 英 `/'wɪn(d)sɜːfɪŋ/`
- 美 `/'wɪndsɝfɪŋ/`

# 词义

n. 风帆滑浪
`Windsurfing is a sport in which you move along the surface of the sea or a lake on a long narrow board with a sail on it`

# 同义

n. 帆板运动
`sailboarding`

# 例句

The island boasts two exceptional beaches: Tung Wan, a popular bathing area with an array of idyllic beach houses and Kwan-yin Wan, known as a windsurfing haven.
岛上自称有着两个特殊的游泳沙滩：东湾，它是一个受欢迎游泳区，具有田园诗般的很多海滨别墅；观音湾，它是是著名帆板运动的避风港。

I went windsurfing most afternoons.
下午我都去风帆冲浪。

He painted nuns driving racing cars and flying balloons, the pope windsurfing, Mona Lisa naked or smoking.
在他的画里，修女驾着赛车和热气球，主教在玩帆板冲浪，蒙娜丽莎（Mona Lisa）要么赤身裸体，要么吞云吐雾。
