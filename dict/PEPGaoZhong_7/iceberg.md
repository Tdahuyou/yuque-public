# 音标

- 英 `/'aɪsbɜːg/`
- 美 `/'aɪs'bɝg/`

# 词义

n. 冰山
`a very large mass of ice floating in the sea, most of which is under the surface of the water`



ice(冰) + berg(大冰块) → 冰山

# 同义

n. [地理]冰山；显露部分
`Berg` `floeberg`

# 短语

- `tip of the iceberg` 冰山一角；事物的表面部分
- `iceberg lettuce` 卷心莴苣；球叶莴苣

# 例句

The ship ran upon the iceberg.
轮船撞到冰山上。

Their ship circled the iceberg.
他们的船绕过了冰山。

The glacier calved a large iceberg.
冰川崩解而产生一个大冰山。
