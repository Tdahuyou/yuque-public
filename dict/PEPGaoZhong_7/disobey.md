# 音标

- 英 `/dɪsə'beɪ/`
- 美 `/'dɪsə'be/`

# 词义

v. 不服从；违抗
`to refuse to do what someone with authority tells you to do, or refuse to obey a rule or law`



dis(不) + obey(服从) → 不服从， 违抗

# 同义

v. 违反；不服从
`be in breach of` `run counter to`

# 短语

- `disobey the rules` 违抗规定

# 例句

You disobeyed my orders.
你违抗我的命令。
