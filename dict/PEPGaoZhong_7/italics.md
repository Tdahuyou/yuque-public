# 音标

- 英 `/i'tæliks/`
- 美 `/ɪ'tælɪks/`

# 词义

n. 斜体（字）
`a type of printed letters that lean to the right, often used to emphasize particular words`

# 同义

n. 斜体字，斜体
`italic type`

# 短语

- `italic type` 斜体活字；斜体

# 例句

Not enough purple italics. . . .
紫色斜体字不够......

Should bolding be applied first and then italics?
是否应先应用粗体后应用斜体？

This doesn't mean, however, that whenever you want to have something in bold or italics you should shoehorn it into these two tags.
但这并不意味着只要您想使某些内容变成粗体或斜体，就应该将它们硬塞进这两个标记中。
