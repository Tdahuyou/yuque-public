# 音标

- 英 `/dɪsə'bɪlɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/,dɪsə'bɪləti/`

# 词义

n. 伤残；无力；无能
`a physical or mental condition that makes it difficult for someone to use a part of their body properly, or to learn normally`



dis(不) + ability(能力) → 没有能力 → 无能

# 同义

n. 残疾；无能；无资格；不利条件
`physical disabilities` `drawback`

# 短语

- `learning disability` 学习障碍；智能障碍
- `physical disability` 残疾；肢体残疾；生理缺陷
- `disability insurance` 残疾保险

# 例句

Facilities for people with disabilities are still insufficient.
供残疾人使用的设施仍然不足。
