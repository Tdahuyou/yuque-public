# 音标

- 英 `/træns'fjuːʒ(ə)n; trɑːns-; -nz-/`
- 美 `/trænz'fjʊʒən/`

# 词义

n. 输血
`the process of putting blood from one person’s body into the body of someone else as a medical treatment`

# 同义

n. [临床]输血；输液；倾注；灌输
`blood filtering` `implantation`

# 同根

- vt. `transfuse` 输血；灌输；注入

# 短语

- `blood transfusion` 输血

# 例句

A blood transfusion saved his life.
输血挽救了他的生命。
