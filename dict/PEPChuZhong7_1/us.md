# 音标

- 英 `/əs; ʌs/`
- 美 `/əs; ʌs/`

# 词义

pron. (we的宾格)我们
`used by the person speaking or writing to refer to himself or herself and one or more other people`

# 短语

- `u turn` 掉头

# 例句

Please help us.
请帮助我们。

He arranged for us all to have a drink.
他安排我们大家一起喝一杯。

She’s invited us both.
我们俩她都请了。

My mother is coming to stay with us.
我妈要来和我们同住。

Send us a donation now.
现在就给我们捐款吧。
