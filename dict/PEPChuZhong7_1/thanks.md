# 音标

- 英 `/θæŋks/`
- 美 `/θæŋks/`

# 词义

interj. 感谢；谢谢
`used to tell someone that you are grateful for something they have given you or done for you`

n. 感谢；谢谢
`the things you say or do to show that you are grateful to someone`

# 同义

n. 谢谢（只用复数）
`Arigatou` `Grazie`

int. 谢谢
`merci` `danke` `arigato`

# 短语

- `thanks for` 多亏
- `thanks a lot` 多谢
- `many thanks` 非常感谢
- `heartfelt thanks` 由衷地感谢
- `thanks for your help` 感谢您的帮助
- `no thanks` 别管闲事
- `give thanks for` 感谢...
- `thanks a million` 非常感谢
- `thanks for helping` 感谢帮忙

# 例句

Joe got up and left without a word of thanks .
乔起身离开了，一句感谢的话也没有说。

‘Pass the salt, please…thanks.’
“请把盐递过来…谢谢。”
