# 音标

- 英 `/ðeə/`
- 美 `/ðɛr/`

# 词义

pron. 他（她、它）们的
`indicate that something belongs or relates to the group of people, animals, or things that you are talking about`

# 例句

They talked her round to their position.
他们说服她同意他们的看法。

He manoeuvred the enemy out of their position.
他用计谋使敌人离开他们的阵地。

He would not knuckle down under their pressure.
他不会在他们的压力下屈服的。
