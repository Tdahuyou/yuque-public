# 音标

- 英 `/jɔ:(r)/`
- 美 `/jʊər; jʊr/`

# 词义

det. 你的；你们的
`used when speaking or writing to one or more people to show that something belongs to them or is connected with them`

# 短语

- `on your back` 在你背上；仰卧

# 例句

You should keep in with your colleagues.
你应当与你的同事友好相处。

If you take this medicine,all your symptoms will fall away.
如果你使用这种药物，你的所有症状将会消失。
