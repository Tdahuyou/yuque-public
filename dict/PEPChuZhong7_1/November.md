# 音标

- 英 `/nə(ʊ)'vembə/`
- 美 `/no'vɛmbɚ/`

# 词义

n. 十一月
`the 11th month of the year, between October and December`

# 例句

And again in November.
在十一月又一次。

Too much snow in November.
太多的大雪在十一月。

By November, after all, it will be more than a year since the global crisis began in the US, and Obama and his team must show tangible prospects for recovery.
毕竟，到了十一月时，自全球性金融危机在美国爆发以来，已经过去一年多了。 奥巴马和他的团队必须展现出经济复苏的切实前景。
