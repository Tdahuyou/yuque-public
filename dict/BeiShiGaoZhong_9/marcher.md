# 音标

- 英 `/'mɑːtʃə/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 游行者，行进者
`a member of a group of people that are walking somewhere in order to express their ideas or protest about something`

# 同义

n. 行进者，游行者
`parader`

# 同根

- n. `march` 行军，进军；进行曲；示威游行
- vi. `march` 进军；走过
- vt. `march` 迫使……前进

# 例句

civil rights marchers
争取公民权的游行示威者
