# 音标

- 英 `/'breɪz(ə)n/`
- 美 `/ˈbreɪzən/`

# 词义

adj. 黄铜制的,厚颜无耻的
`used to describe a person, or the actions of a person, who is not embarrassed about behaving in a wrong or immoral way`



braz(=brass黄铜) + en → 像黄铜一样 → 厚脸皮的

# 同义

adj. 厚颜无耻的；黄铜制的
`as bold as brass` `bold-faced`

# 同根

- adv. `brazenly` 厚脸皮地，厚颜无耻地
- n. `braze` 铜焊
- n. `brazenness` 厚颜无耻
- vt. `braze` 用黄铜制；使成黄铜色

# 例句

her brazen admission that she was cheating on him
她恬不知耻地承认自己对他不忠
