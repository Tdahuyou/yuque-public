# 音标

- 英 `/'fʊtluːs/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

adj. 自由自在的,到处走动的
`free to do exactly what you want because you have no responsibilities, for example when you are not married or do not have children`

# 同义

adj. 自由自在的，不受束缚的
`untrammeled` `unrestricted`

# 例句

footloose students traveling around Europe
在欧洲旅游、自由自在的学生

When the kids leave home, you’ll be footloose and fancy-free (= free and without worries ) .
孩子离家之后，你就会自由自在、没有牵挂了。
