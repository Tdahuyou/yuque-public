# 音标

- 英 `/rɪt/`
- 美 `/rɪt/`

# 词义

n. 文书,正式文件,书面命令
`a document from a court that orders someone to do or not to do something`



write去掉e就变成了writ

# 同义

n. [法]令状；文书；法院命令
`clerical assistant` `diploma`

# 例句

He issued a writ against the newspaper.
他签发了传讯这家报纸的令状。

The company has been served with a writ for damages.
这家公司收到了给予损害赔偿的令状。
