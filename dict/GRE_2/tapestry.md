# 音标

- 英 `/'tæpɪstrɪ/`
- 美 `/'tæpəstri/`

# 词义

n. 织锦,挂毯
`a large piece of heavy cloth on which coloured threads are woven to produce a picture, pattern etc`

# 同义

n. 织锦；[纺]挂毯；绣帷
`brocade` `arras`

# 同根

- adj. `tapestried` 织在绣帷上的；饰挂绣帷的
- n. `tapis` 毛毡，织锦
- v. `tapestried` 饰以织锦画（tapestry的过去分词）

# 例句

a colourful tapestry depicting a hunting scene
描绘狩猎场面的彩色壁毯
