# 音标

- 英 `/kən'sɪlɪeɪt/`
- 美 `/kən'sɪlɪet/`

# 词义

v. 安慰,赢得,调和
`to do something to make people more likely to stop arguing, especially by giving them something they want`



concil(=council协商) + iate → 协商(解决) → 调和

# 同义

vt. 安抚，安慰；调和；驯服；怀柔；赢得
`comfort` `win` `purchase` `quiet`

# 同根

- adj. `conciliatory` 安抚的；调和的；调停的
- n. `conciliation` 调解；安抚；说服

# 例句

efforts to conciliate the unions
对工会进行调停的努力
