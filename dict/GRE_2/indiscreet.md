# 音标

- 英 `/ɪndɪ'skriːt/`
- 美 `/'ɪndɪ'skrit/`

# 词义

adj. 不慎重的,轻率的
`careless about what you say or do, especially by talking about things which should be kept secret`

# 同义

adj. 轻率的；不慎重的
`hasty` `thoughtless`

# 同根

- adv. `indiscreetly` 轻率地

# 例句

It was very indiscreet of Colin to tell them about our plan.
科林把我们的计划告诉了他们，太不谨慎了。
