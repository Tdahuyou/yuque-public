# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'prɪnt/`
- 美 `/'ɪmprɪnt/`

# 词义

v. 留下烙印
`If a surface is imprinted with a mark or design, that mark or design is printed on the surface or pressed into it`



im(不) + print(印记) → 留下印记 → 盖印

# 同义

n. 印记；[法]痕迹；特征；版本说明
`mark` `print` `characteristic` `trace`

# 同根

- n. `imprinting` 铭记（动物生命早期即起作用的一种学习机能）；胚教

# 例句

The company carries a variety of binders that can be imprinted with your message or logo.
该公司经营各种可以印上客户所需信息或标识的活页夹。
