# 音标

- 英 `/haɪpə'krɪtɪk(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'haɪpɚ'krɪtɪkl/`

# 词义

adj. 苛评的,吹毛求疵的
`too eager to criticize other people and things, especially about small details`

# 同义

adj. 吹毛求疵的，苛评的
`supercritical` `captiously`

# 例句

He's as big a geek as Zuckerberg, but of a different type: the hypercritical, even sometimes nasty type.
他与扎克伯格一样，是个超级极客，只不过是另一种类型的：他吹毛求疵，有时甚至令人讨厌。

I know it's time to grow up, stop being hypercritical and just enter the dating pool, with all its clichés and absolute disappointments.
我知道，是时候要长大了，我该停止吹毛求疵，去约会，不管它有多老套，有多让人失望。
