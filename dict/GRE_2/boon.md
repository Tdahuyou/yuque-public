# 音标

- 英 `/buːn/`
- 美 `/bʊn/`

# 词义

n. 恩惠,实惠,福利
`You can describe something as a boon when it makes life better or easier for someone`



从月亮(moon)得到恩惠(boon) → 天赐福利

# 同义

n. 恩惠；福利；利益
`behalf` `welfare` `sake` `benefit` `grace`

adj. 愉快的；慷慨的
`good` `bright` `generous` `handsome` `fragrant`

# 例句

It is for this reason that television proves such a boon to so many people.
正是这个原因电视机成为这么多人的一大福音。
