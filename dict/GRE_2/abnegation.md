# 音标

- 英 `/æbnɪ'geɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,æbnɪ'ɡeʃən/`

# 词义

n. 放弃


# 同义

n. 拒绝；放弃；克制
`rejection` `refusal` `restraint`

# 同根

- n. `abnegator` 放弃者；克制者
- vt. `abnegate` 放弃；舍弃；禁忌

# 例句

Only spontaneous love flowing with sincere generosity and self-abnegation can fertilize the soul of others.
唯有充满诚挚慷慨与自我放弃、油然而生的爱才能丰盛他者的灵魂。

It’s hard to ignore the fact that all these tools are at root about imposing limits and narrowing options—in other words, about a voluntary abnegation of freedom.
我们很难忽视这样一个事实：所有这些手段从根本上都是关于强加限制和减少选择，关于自愿地放弃自由。
