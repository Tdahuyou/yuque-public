# 音标

- 英 `/'tʃeɪs(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'tʃesn/`

# 词义

v. 磨炼
`to make someone realize that their behaviour was wrong or mistaken`



chaste(纯洁的) + n → 变纯洁 → 改正

# 同义

vt. 惩罚；磨炼；抑制
`control` `stay` `restrain`

# 同根

- n. `chastisement` 惩罚
- vt. `chastise` 惩罚；严惩；责骂

# 例句

Party workers have returned to their home towns, chastened by their overwhelming defeat.
党务工作者遭受惨败后打道回府。
