# 音标

- 英 `/'dæŋg(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'dæŋɡl/`

# 词义

v. 摇摆
`to offer something good to someone, in order to persuade them to do something`



“荡够” → 悬荡， 悬摆

# 同根

- adj. `dangling` 悬挂的；摇摆的
- v. `dangling` 摇晃（dangle的ing形式）

# 例句

A good pay package and a company car were dangled in front of her.
优厚的薪酬和公司配车摆在她面前。
