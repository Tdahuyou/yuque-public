# 音标

- 英 `/'efətlɪs/`
- 美 `/'ɛfɚtləs/`

# 词义

adj. 容易的,不费力气的
`something that is effortless is done in a very skilful way that makes it seem easy`

# 同义

adj. 容易的；不费力气的
`easy` `light` `easiest`

# 同根

- adj. `effortful` 需要努力的；显示努力的；充满努力的
- adv. `effortlessly` 轻松地；毫不费劲地
- n. `effort` 努力；成就

# 例句

Alexei rose to his feet with a single effortless movement.
阿列克谢轻巧地一跃而起。
