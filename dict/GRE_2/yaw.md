# 音标

- 英 `/jɔː/`
- 美 `/jɔ/`

# 词义

v. 偏航
`if a ship, aircraft etc yaws, it turns away from the direction it should be travelling in`

# 同义

n. （火箭、飞机、宇宙飞船等）[航]偏航
`leeway` `sheer`

vi. [航]偏航
`sheer`

# 短语

- `yaw rate` 角速度；偏航率
- `yaw angle` [航天]偏航角

# 例句

As the plane climbed to 370 feet, it started yawing.
飞机攀升到370英尺高时开始偏航。
