# 词义

adj. 说奉承话的,阿谀的
`praising important or powerful people too much because you want to get something from them – used in order to show disapproval`

# 同义

adj. 说奉承话的；阿谀的
`adulatory` `gnathonic`

# 同根

- adj. `sycophant` 奉承的；拍马的
- n. `sycophant` 谄媚者；奉承者
- n. `sycophancy` 谄媚；奉承；追随；拍马屁

# 例句

sycophantic journalists
好溜须拍马的新闻记者

a sycophantic letter
一封阿谀奉承的信
