# 音标

- 英 `/ɔː'θentɪkeɪt/`
- 美 `/ɔ'θɛntɪket/`

# 词义

v. 鉴别
`to prove that something is true or real`

# 同义

vt. 鉴定；证明…是真实的
`appraise` `to identify`

# 同根

- adj. `authentic` 真正的，真实的；可信的
- n. `authentication` 证明；鉴定；证实
- n. `authenticity` 真实性，确实性；可靠性
- n. `authenticator` 认证者；认证器

# 例句

The painting has been authenticated by experts.
这幅画专家鉴定为真迹。

passwords which can authenticate electronic documents
能够验证电子文档的密码
