# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'neɪg; rɪ'niːg/`
- 美 `/rɪ'niɡ/`

# 词义

v. 食言,违例出牌
`If someone reneges on a promise or an agreement, they do not do what they have promised or agreed to do`



re(反) + neg(否认) + e → 反过来不承认 → 食言， 违约

# 同义

vi. 食言；违例出牌
`eat his words` `break one's promise`

vt. 否认
`deny` `disown`

# 例句

He reneged on a promise to leave his wife.
他背弃了诺言，离开了他的妻子。
