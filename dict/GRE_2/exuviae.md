# 音标

- 英 `/ɪg'zjuːvɪiː; eg-/`
- 美 `/ɪg'zʊvji/`

# 词义

n. [动]脱落的皮,空壳
`layers of skin or cuticle shed by animals during ecdysis `

# 同义

n. 蜕皮；残骸；空壳
`remain` `debris`
