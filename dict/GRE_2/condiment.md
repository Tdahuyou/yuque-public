# 音标

- 英 `/'kɒndɪm(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'kɑndɪmənt/`

# 词义

n. 调味品
`a powder or liquid, such as salt or  ketchup  , that you use to give a special taste to food`

# 同义

n. [食品]调味品；佐料
`seasoning` `dressing`

# 例句

Fact: Apparently this condiment can reinstate shine to your small change.
事实：这显然是调味品的小改变让事物恢复光芒。
