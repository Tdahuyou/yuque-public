# 音标

- 英 `/,pæpɪ'reɪʃəs/`
- 美 `/,pæpə'reʃəs/`

# 词义

adj. 纸草的,纸状的
`of, relating to, made of, or resembling paper `

# 同义

adj. 纸状的；薄如纸的（等于papery）
`papery`

# 例句

Two types of mummification are known, the "hematic" type common to cattle and the " papyraceous " type of swine.
木乃伊有两种类型，常见于牛的“出血”型和猪的“纸状”型。
