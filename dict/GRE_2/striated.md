# 词义

adj. 有条纹的,有细条的,有线纹的
`having narrow lines or bands of colour`



来自striate(v. 加条纹)

# 同义

adj. 有条纹的；有线纹的
`streaky` `lineate`

# 短语

- `striated muscle` 横纹肌

# 例句

All striated wires are minus (–) in polarity, and should be connected to the minus (–) speaker terminals.
所有具有条纹线皮的电线都是负（-）极，须连接到负（-）的扬声器端子。
