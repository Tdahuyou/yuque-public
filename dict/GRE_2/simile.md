# 音标

- 英 `/'sɪmɪlɪ/`
- 美 `/'sɪməli/`

# 词义

n. [修辞]明喻,明喻的说法,明喻的词语用法
`an expression that describes something by comparing it with something else, using the words ‘as’ or ‘like’, for example ‘as white as snow’`



simil(相类似的)+e → 把相类似的事物进行比较 → 明喻

# 例句

Lister said people should follow the 1946 advice of writer George Orwell: "Never use a metaphor, simile, or other figure of speech which you are used to seeing in print.
李斯特说人们最好遵循作家乔治·奥威尔1946年提出的建议：“不要使用那些在各种出版物上频繁出现的暗喻、明喻或其他修辞格。”

The simile is a common device and a useful one, but similes coming in rapid fire, one right on top of another, are more distracting than illuminating.
明喻是常用的修辞术，也很好用，但是像连珠炮式的比喻与其说是富有启发性，不如说是在转移注意力。
