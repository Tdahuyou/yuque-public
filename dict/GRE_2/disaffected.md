# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/,dɪsə'fɛktɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 抱不平的,有叛意的,不服的
`not satisfied with your government, leader etc, and therefore no longer loyal to them or no longer believing they can help you`



dis(不) + affect(感动) → 不再感动 → 使不满

# 同义

adj. 不满的；有叛意的；愤愤不平的
`disgruntled` `malcontented`

v. 使疏远；使不友爱（disaffect的过去分词）
`estranged`

# 同根

- n. `disaffection` 不满；背叛
- vt. `disaffect` 使疏远；使不满意；使成为敌人

# 例句

the disaffected youth from poor neighborhoods
贫民区那些心存不满的年轻人
