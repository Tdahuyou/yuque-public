# 音标

- 英 `/'rʌdə/`
- 美 `/'rʌdɚ/`

# 词义

n. 舵,方向舵
`a flat part at the back of a ship or aircraft that can be turned in order to control the direction in which it moves`



最前面的奔跑者(runner)是领导者(rudder)

# 同根

- adj. `rudderless` 无舵的；无指导者的

# 短语

- `rudder angle` 方向舵偏角

# 例句

They are the rudder, keeping the ship going in the right direction.
它们也犹如船舵，保证采访之船行驶在正确的方向上。
