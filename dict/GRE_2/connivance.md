# 音标

- 英 `/kə'naɪv(ə)ns/`
- 美 `/kə'naivəns/`

# 词义

n. 假装不见,纵容,默许
`Connivance is a willingness to allow or assist something to happen even though you know it is wrong`

# 同义

n. 纵容；默许；假装不见
`acquiescence` `tacit consent`

# 同根

- adj. `conniving` 纵容的；默许的
- v. `conniving` 纵容；默许；假装不见；共谋（connive的ing形式）
- vi. `connive` 共谋；纵容；默许

# 例句

The deficit had grown with the connivance of the banks.
在银行纵容下，赤字增加。

The goods were exported with official connivance.
这些商品出口有官方的默认。
