# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'gɜːdʒɪteɪt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'ɡɝdʒɪtet/`

# 词义

v. (使)涌回,(使)流回,(使)反刍
`to bring food that you have already swallowed, back into your mouth`

# 同义

vi. 回流；回涌；反胃；反刍
`back flow` `inverse flow`

# 同根

- n. `regurgitation` 回流；反刍；流回

# 例句

Some birds and animals regurgitate food to feed their young.
有些鸟兽会回吐食物以喂哺幼崽。
