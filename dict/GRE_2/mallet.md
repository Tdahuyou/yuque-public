# 音标

- 英 `/'mælɪt/`
- 美 `/'mælɪt/`

# 词义

n. 槌棒
`a wooden hammer with a large end`

# 同义

n. 棒，槌棒；木槌；球棍
`rod` `bar` `staff`

# 例句

I even have a wooden mallet that was used by a judge over 300 years ago during condemnation proceedings in Salem.
我甚至还有一把300多年前的木槌。

The water-filled bowls, when rubbed with a leather-wrapped mallet, exhibit a lively dance of water droplets as they emit a haunting sound.
在用皮革包裹的木槌摩擦倒满水的碗时，碗里的水滴会跳起生动的舞蹈，同时它们发出萦绕不绝的声音。
