# 音标

- 英 `/'diːmɑːkeɪt/`
- 美 `/'dimɑr'ket/`

# 词义

v. 划分界线
`to decide or mark the limits of an area, system etc`



de + marc(=mark标记) + ate → 做标记 → 划分， 划界

# 同义

vt. 划分界线；区别
`to make a distinction` `tell from`

# 同根

- n. `demarcation` 划分；划界；限界

# 例句

A special UN commission was formed to demarcate the border.
一个特别联合国委员会被设立以期划定边界。
