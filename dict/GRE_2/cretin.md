# 音标

- 英 `/'kretɪn/`
- 美 `/'kritn/`

# 词义

n. [医]白痴病患者
`an offensive word for someone who is extremely stupid`

# 同根

- adj. `cretinous` 白痴病的
- n. `cretinism` 白痴病；呆小症

# 例句

No new young cretin has been found during or since the initiation of the Emergency Response Plan in 2007.
自从2007年紧急反应计划实施以来，还没有发现新的年轻白痴病患者。
