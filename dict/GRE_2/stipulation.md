# 音标

- 英 `/,stɪpjʊ'leɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,stɪpjə'leʃən/`

# 词义

n. 约定,约束,契约
`something that must be done, and which is stated as part of an agreement, law, or rule`



来自stipulate(v. 规定， 明确要求)

# 同义

n. 规定；约定；[法]条款；契约
`provision` `item` `convention` `do` `appointment`

# 同根

- adj. `stipulate` 有托叶的
- adj. `stipulatory` 合同的；规定的
- vi. `stipulate` 规定；保证
- vt. `stipulate` 规定；保证
