# 音标

- 英 `/,paɪrə(ʊ)'meɪnɪə/`
- 美 `/,pɪəro'menɪr/`

# 词义

n. 放火癖,放火狂
`the uncontrollable impulse and practice of setting things on fire `

# 同义

n. 放火狂；纵火癖
`firebug`

# 同根

- n. `pyromancer` 纵火者；火占师

# 例句

The person arrested for setting the fire had been suspected of pyromania on two previous occasions.
此人在先前的两个场合中阻止扑灭火灾涉嫌有纵火癖。
