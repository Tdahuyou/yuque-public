# 音标

- 英 `/,kɒnvə'les/`
- 美 `/,kɑnvə'lɛs/`

# 词义

v. 渐渐康复,渐愈
`If you are convalescing, you are resting and getting your health back after an illness or operation`

# 同根

- adj. `convalescent` 康复的；恢复期的
- n. `convalescence` 恢复期，康复期；逐渐康复
- n. `convalescent` 恢复中的病人；康复的人

# 例句

After two weeks, I was allowed home, where I convalesced for three months.
2星期后，我经允许回家静养，在家康复了3个月。
