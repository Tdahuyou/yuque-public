# 音标

- 英 `/stɪ'nɒgrəfɪ/`
- 美 `/stə'nɑɡrəfi/`

# 词义

n. 速记,速记法
`a fast way of writing in which you use special signs or short forms of words, used especially to record what someone is saying`

# 同义

n. [语]速记法，速记
`shorthand`

# 同根

- adj. `stenographic` 速记的；利用速记的；简洁的
- n. `stenographer` 速记员
- n. `stenograph` 速记机；速记文字
- vt. `stenograph` 速记

# 例句

She knew no more shorthand than if she had been a graduate in stenography just let slip upon the world by a business college.
她并不太精通速记，就像是刚刚走出商学院速记专业的毕业学生，来闯荡世界。

Coursework included: secretarial principles, office administration, management, business English, English word processing, stenography, bookkeeping.
课程包括：秘书原理，办公室行政，管理学，商用英语，英语文字处理，速记学，簿记。
