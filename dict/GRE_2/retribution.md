# 音标

- 英 `/retrɪ'bjuːʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,rɛtrɪ'bjuʃən/`

# 词义

n. 报偿
`Retribution is punishment for a crime, especially punishment that is carried out by someone other than the official authorities`



re(反) + tribut(给予) + ion → 反过来给予 → 报应

# 同义

n. 报应；惩罚；报答；报偿
`punishment` `rod` `payment` `discipline` `reward`

# 同根

- adj. `retributive` 报应的；惩罚的
- adj. `retributory` 报应的

# 例句

He didn't want any further involvement for fear of retribution.
由于害怕遭到惩罚，他不想进一步卷进去。
