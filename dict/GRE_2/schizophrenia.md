# 音标

- 英 `/,skɪtsə(ʊ)'friːnɪə/`
- 美 `/,skɪtsə'frinɪə/`

# 词义

n. [心]精神分裂症
`a serious mental illness in which someone’s thoughts and feelings are not based on what is really happening around them`

# 同根

- adj. `schizophrenic` 精神分裂症的
- n. `schizophrenic` 精神分裂症的患者
- n. `schizogony` 分裂生殖
