# 音标

- 英 `/pəʊ'pʊərɪ; -'riː; pɒt'pʊərɪ/`
- 美 `/,popʊ'ri/`

# 词义

n. 花香,肉菜杂烩
`a mixture of pieces of dried flowers and leaves kept in a bowl to make a room smell pleasant`



pot(锅) + pour(倾倒)+ri → 倒在一个锅里 → 混杂物

# 同义

n. 百花香；混合物；杂曲；大杂烩
`mixture` `hybrid` `mix` `compound`

# 例句

...a potpourri of architectural styles from all over the world.
...一种世界各地建筑风格的荟萃。
