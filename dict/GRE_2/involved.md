# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'vɒlvd/`
- 美 `/ɪn'vɑlvd/`

# 词义

adj. 棘手的,有关的
`If one person is involved with another, especially someone they are not married to, they are having a sexual or romantic relationship`



in(使…) + volv(卷) + e → 使卷入 → 牵涉， 牵连

# 同义

adj. 有关的；卷入的；复杂的
`relevant` `concerned` `complicated` `detailed` `sophisticated`

# 同根

- adj. `involute` [生物] 内卷的；纷乱的；错综复杂的
- n. `involvement` 牵连；包含；混乱；财政困难
- n. `involute` [数] 渐伸线
- vi. `involute` 恢复原状；消失
- vt. `involve` 包含；牵涉；使陷于；潜心于

# 短语

- `involved in` 涉及；包含；牵涉进…
- `involved with` 涉及；与…有关连
- `get involved in` 涉及，卷入
- `parties involved` 交换中的各方
- `become involved in` 使卷入，使陷入
- `got involved in` 卷入；涉及

# 例句

During a visit to Kenya in 1928 he became romantically involved with a married woman.
在1928年到肯尼亚访问期间，他和一位已婚妇女产生了暧昧关系。
