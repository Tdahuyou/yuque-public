# 音标

- 英 `/,ɪmpɔː'tjuːn/`
- 美 `/,ɪmpɔr'tun/`

# 词义

v. 强求
`to ask someone for something continuously in an annoying or unreasonable way`



im(进入) + port(搬运) + une → 向内搬 → 强求

# 同义

vt. 强求；一再向某人要求
`exact` `extort`

# 同根

- n. `importunity` 强求；硬要

# 例句

One can no longer walk the streets without seeing beggars importuning passersby.
走在这些街道上总能看到乞丐纠缠行人乞讨。
