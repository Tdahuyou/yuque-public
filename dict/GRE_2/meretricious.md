# 音标

- 英 `/,merɪ'trɪʃəs/`
- 美 `/'mɛrə'trɪʃəs/`

# 词义

adj. 华丽而庸俗的,俗气的,娼妓的,适合娼妓的
`If you describe something as meretricious, you disapprove of it because although it looks attractive it is actually of little value`

# 同义

adj. 俗气的；娼妓的；华丽而庸俗的
`tacky` `Philistine`

# 例句

...vulgar, meretricious, and shabby souvenirs.
...各种俗气花哨和劣质的纪念品。
