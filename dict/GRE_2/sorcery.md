# 音标

- 英 `/'sɔːs(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/'sɔrsəri/`

# 词义

n. 巫术,魔术
`magic that uses the power of evil forces`



sorc(巫术) ＋ ery → 巫术， 魔术

# 同义

n. 魔术，巫术
`medicine` `conjuring`

# 同根

- n. `sorcerer` 魔术师；男巫士

# 例句

Since a name was considered an integral part of a person, it could be an effective target for sorcery.
因为名字被认为是一个人的重要组成部分，就有可能成为巫术的有效攻击靶子。

Taking drugs that alter our state of mind is a form of sorcery, as are some forms of New Age healing/energy work.
吃药改变思想状态是巫术的一种形式，和一些新时期的康复治疗∕ 恢复能力是同样的形式。

In Papua, education about HIV is not widespread and many people still believe that sorcery is to blame for the growing number of cases each year.
对于艾滋病相关知识并没有得到广泛的普及。 人们依然认为巫术才是导致艾滋病数量逐年增多的元凶。
