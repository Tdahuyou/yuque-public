# 音标

- 英 `/'wɪt(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'wɪtl/`

# 词义

v. 切,削,削减,损害
`to cut a piece of wood into a particular shape by cutting off small pieces with a knife`



wh(看作whet， 磨刀) + ittle(看作little， 小) → 磨刀把木头削小 → 削(木头)

# 同义

vt. 削；削减；切；削弱
`break` `log`

n. 屠刀
`butcher knife`

# 短语

- `whittle down` v. 削减，削弱

# 例句

We need to whittle down the list of guests for the party.
我们需要缩减聚会的客人名单。
