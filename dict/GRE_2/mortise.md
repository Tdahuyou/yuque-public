# 音标

- 英 `/'mɔːtɪs/`
- 美 `/'mɔrtɪs/`

# 词义

n. 榫眼,印刷版穴
`a hole cut in a piece of wood or stone so that the shaped end of another piece will fit there firmly`

# 同义

n. 榫眼
`dapt`

# 同根

- n. `mortice` [木] 榫眼
- vt. `mortice` 榫接（等于mortise）

# 短语

- `mortise lock` 插锁
- `mortise and tenon` 阴阳榫
- `tenon and mortise` 榫臼；雌雄准
- `mortise joint` 镶榫接头
