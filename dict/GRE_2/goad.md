# 音标

- 英 `/gəʊd/`
- 美 `/ɡod/`

# 词义

n. (赶牲口用的)刺棒,激励物,刺激物
`something that forces someone to do something`

# 同义

n. 刺激；（赶牲口用的）刺棒；激励
`stimulus` `incentive` `animation` `excitement` `needle`

vt. 刺激；激励；用刺棒驱赶；煽动
`stimulate` `fan` `power` `egg` `motivate`

# 例句

The offer of economic aid was a goad to political change.
提供经济援助是政治改革的推动力。
