# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/fɔr'bɛrɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 忍耐的,宽容的
`patient and willing to forgive`

# 同义

adj. 宽容的；忍耐的
`kind` `tolerant`

v. 克制（forbear的现在分词）
`containing` `withholding`

# 同根

- n. `forbearance` 自制，忍耐；宽容
- n. `forbear` 祖先
- vi. `forbear` 忍耐；克制
- vt. `forbear` 忍耐；克制

# 短语

- `bear and forbear` [古语]一忍再忍

# 例句

By long forbearing is a prince persuaded, and a soft tongue breaketh the bone.
恒常忍耐，可以劝动君王。 柔和的舌头，能折断骨头。

The New Testament community was forbearing and patient with its members, embracing a love that covered a multitude of sins.
New Testament 团体对其成员是宽容而且耐心的，拥抱着爱来遮掩人们的罪。
