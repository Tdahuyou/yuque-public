# 音标

- 英 `/,nɒn'vaɪələnt/`
- 美 `/,nɑn'vaɪələnt/`

# 词义

adj. 非暴力的
`Nonviolent methods of bringing about change do not involve hurting people or causing damage`

# 同根

- adv. `nonviolently` 非暴力地
- n. `nonviolence` 非暴力；非暴力事件

# 例句

King was a worldwide symbol of nonviolent protest against racial injustice.
金是全世界以非暴力抗议种族不平等的象征。

I would only belong to an environmental movement if it was explicitly nonviolent.
我只会投身一个明确宣称只用非暴力手段的环护运动。
