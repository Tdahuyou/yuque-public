# 音标

- 英 `/prə(ʊ)'dʒenɪtə/`
- 美 `/pro'dʒɛnɪtɚ/`

# 词义

n. 祖先,起源
`a person or animal that lived in the past, to whom someone or something living now is related`



pro(前) + gen(产生) + itor → 生在前面的人 → 祖先

# 同义

n. 祖先；原著；起源
`birth` `father` `root`

# 例句

He was also a progenitor of seven presidents of Nicaragua.
他也是尼加拉瓜七位总统的祖先。
