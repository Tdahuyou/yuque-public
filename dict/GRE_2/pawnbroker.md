# 音标

- 英 `/'pɔːnbrəʊkə/`
- 美 `/'pɔnbrokɚ/`

# 词义

n. 典当商
`someone whose business is to lend people money in exchange for valuable objects. If the money is not paid back, the pawnbroker can sell the object.`

# 同根

- n. `pawn` 典当；抵押物；兵，卒；人质
- n. `pawnshop` 当铺
- vt. `pawn` 当掉；以……担保
