# 音标

- 英 `/kəm'pʌŋkʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/kəm'pʌŋkʃən/`

# 词义

n. 良心的谴责,后悔,悔恨
`a feeling that you should not do something because it is bad or wrong`



com＋punct(刺， 点)＋ion → (心)不断被刺 → 良心不安

# 同义

n. 悔恨，后悔；内疚
`the worm of conscience` `repentance`

# 例句

They used their tanks against the leftists without compunction .
他们肆无忌惮地使用坦克来对付左派人士。
