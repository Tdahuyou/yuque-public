# 音标

- 英 `/'rʌksæk; 'rʊk-/`
- 美 `/'rʊksæk/`

# 词义

n. (旅行用)帆布背包
`a bag used for carrying things on your back, especially by people on long walks`

# 例句

You have to run five kilometers every morning, carrying a heavy rucksack and rifle.
每天早晨，你必须背着重重的帆布背包和步枪跑完5公里。

He is wearing a crisp new T-shirt that reads "Revolución Bolivarana". She has a large rucksack.
男的穿着崭新的T恤，上面印着：“玻利瓦尔革命”；女的有个大大的帆布背包。

She went this way and that over what to take, and packed and repacked the saddlebag and rucksack I had got hold of for her.
是走这条路线还是走那条路线，她忙得不可开交，打好包后，又把我为她找到的挎包和帆布背包装上东西。
