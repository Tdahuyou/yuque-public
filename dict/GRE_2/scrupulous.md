# 音标

- 英 `/'skruːpjʊləs/`
- 美 `/'skrupjələs/`

# 词义

adj. 小心谨慎的,细心的
`Someone who is scrupulous takes great care to do what is fair, honest, or morally right`

# 同义

adj. 细心的；小心谨慎的；一丝不苟的
`meticulous` `circumspect`

# 同根

- adv. `scrupulously` 严谨地；小心翼翼地；多顾虑地
- n. `scrupulousness` 小心翼翼

# 例句

You're being very scrupulous, but to what end?
你表现得很讲良心，但目的是什么呢？

I have been scrupulous about telling them the dangers.
我告诉他们这些危险是很讲良心的。
