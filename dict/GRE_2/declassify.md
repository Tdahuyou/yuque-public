# 音标

- 英 `/diː'klæsɪfaɪ/`
- 美 `/,di'klæsɪfaɪ/`

# 词义

v. 不再当机密文件处理,从机密表删除
`If secret documents or records are declassified, it is officially stated that they are no longer secret`

# 同根

- n. `declassification` 解密；从机密表删除；不再当机密文件处理

# 例句

These reports were only declassified last year.
这些报告去年才被消密。
