# 音标

- 英 `/'bʌtrɪs/`
- 美 `/'bʌtrəs/`

# 词义

n. (建筑)扶壁,支持物
`Buttresses are supports, usually made of stone or brick, that support a wall`

# 同义

n. [建]扶壁；拱壁；支撑物
`support` `upholder`

vt. 支持；以扶壁支撑
`encourage` `second` `back` `carry` `hand`

# 例句

...the neo-Gothic buttresses of Riverside Church in Manhattan.
…曼哈顿的里弗赛德教堂中的新哥特式扶璧。
