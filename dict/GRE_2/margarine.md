# 音标

- 英 `/,mɑːdʒə'riːn; 'mɑːgəriːn/`
- 美 `/'mɑrdʒərən/`

# 词义

n. 人造黄油
`a yellow substance similar to butter but made from vegetable or animal fats, which you eat with bread or use for cooking`

# 同义

n. [食品]人造黄油；人造奶油
`oleomargarine` `butterine`
