# 音标

- 英 `/'prəʊleɪt/`
- 美 `/'prolet/`

# 词义

adj. 扁长的
`having a polar diameter of greater length than the equatorial diameter `

# 同义

adj. [数]扁长的；扩展的
`expanding` `patulous`

# 例句

Passive energy dissipation existing in the spinner of prolate dual-spin satellite will lead to nutation divergence.
细长体双自旋卫星自旋部分的能量耗散会导致卫星章动运动的发散。

In chapter 3, a novel optimal UWB shaping pulse design algorithm based on prolate spheroidal wave functions is described.
第3章主要介绍了一种基于扁长椭球波函数的超宽带成形脉冲优化设计算法。
