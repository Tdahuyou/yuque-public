# 音标

- 英 `/blen(t)ʃ/`
- 美 `/blɛntʃ/`

# 词义

v. 畏缩,回避
`to shy away, as in fear; quail `

# 同义

vi. 退缩；回避
`shy away from` `cower`

vt. 畏缩，退缩
`shrink back from` `recoil from`

# 例句

The conflict among the interest groups is a political puzzle unable to blench for all the societies of all ages.
利益集团之间的冲突是古往今来所有社会都无法回避的政治难题。
