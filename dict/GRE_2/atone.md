# 音标

- 英 `/ə'təʊn/`
- 美 `/ə'təun/`

# 词义

v. 弥补
`to do something to show that you are sorry for having done something wrong`



a + tone(看作stone， 石头) → 女娲用石头补天 → 补偿

# 同义

vt. 赎罪；弥补；偿还
`replace` `recover` `offset` `tender`

vi. 弥补；赎回
`make up for it` `make up for sth.`

# 同根

- n. `atonement` 赎罪；补偿，弥补

# 短语

- `atone for` 赎回；抵偿

# 例句

He felt he had atoned for what he had done to his son.
他觉得他已弥补曾对儿子所做的错事。
