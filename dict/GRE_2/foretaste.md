# 音标

- 英 `/'fɔːteɪst/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 预示
`If you describe an event as a foretaste of a future situation, you mean that it suggests to you what that future situation will be like`

# 同义

n. 先试；预示；先尝；试吃
`prefiguration` `portent`

# 同根

- n. `foretoken` 预兆；预示
- vt. `foretoken` 成为…之预兆

# 例句

It was a foretaste of things to come.
这是未来之事的预演。
