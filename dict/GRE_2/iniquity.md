# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'nɪkwɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/ɪ'nɪkwəti/`

# 词义

n. 不公正
`the quality of being very unfair or evil, or something that is very unfair`



in(不) + iqu(相同的) + ity → 不相同 → 不公正

# 同义

n. 邪恶；不公正
`evil` `wrong` `wickedness`

# 同根

- adj. `iniquitous` 邪恶的；不公正的
- adv. `iniquitously` 非法地；极不公正地

# 例句

They were trying to protect their son from iniquity.
他们竭力保护儿子免受不公正待遇。
