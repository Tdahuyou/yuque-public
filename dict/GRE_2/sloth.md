# 音标

- 英 `/sləʊθ/`
- 美 `/sloθ/`

# 词义

n. 怠惰,懒惰
`an animal in Central and South America that moves very slowly, has grey fur, and lives in trees`

# 同义

n. 怠惰，懒惰；[脊椎]树懒
`laziness` `idleness`

# 同根

- adj. `slothful` 怠惰的，懒惰的；迟钝的
- n. `slothfulness` 怠惰；懒惰

# 例句

He admitted a lack of motivation and a feeling of sloth.
他承认自己缺乏动力，感觉懒散。
