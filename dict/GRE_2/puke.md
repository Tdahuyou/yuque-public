# 音标

- 英 `/pjuːk/`
- 美 `/pjuk/`

# 词义

n. 呕吐,呕吐物,吐剂
`food brought back up from your stomach through your mouth`

# 同义

n. 呕吐；呕吐物（等于vomit）；吐剂
`sickness` `emesis`

vt. 呕吐
`shoot the cat` `throw up`

vi. 呕吐
`shoot the cat` `hork`

# 同根

- v. `puking` 呕吐（puke的ing形式）
