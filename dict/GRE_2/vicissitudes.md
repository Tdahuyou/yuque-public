# 音标

- 英 `/vi'sisitju:dz/`
- 美 `/vi'sisitju:dz/`

# 词义

n. 兴衰；枯荣；变迁
`the continuous changes and problems that affect a situation or someone’s life`

# 短语

- `vicissitudes of life` 悲欢离合

# 例句

Whatever the vicissitudes of her past life, Jill now seems to have come through.
不管过去经历了什么世事变迁，吉尔现在似乎已经度过难关了。
