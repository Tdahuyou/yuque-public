# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒsələtaɪz/`
- 美 `/'prɑsələtaɪz/`

# 词义

v. 使改宗
`If you proselytize, you try to persuade someone to share your beliefs, especially religious or political beliefs`



pros(靠近) + elyt(来到) + ize → 走到(佛祖)面前 → (使)皈依

# 同根

- n. `proselyte` 改信仰者
- n. `proselytism` 改变信仰；改信宗教；劝诱
- vi. `proselyte` 改变宗教信仰
- vt. `proselyte` 使改宗教信仰

# 例句

I assured him we didn't come here to proselytize.
我向他保证我们并不是来改变他的信仰的。

Christians were arrested for trying to convert people, to proselytise them.
基督徒因为试图改变他人的信仰而被捕。
