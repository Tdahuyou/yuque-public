# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'deɪn; -z-/`
- 美 `/dɪs'den/`

# 词义

n. 轻蔑,以高傲的态度对待
`If you feel disdain for someone or something, you dislike them because you think that they are inferior or unimportant`



dis(不) + dain(=dign高贵) → 把人弄得不高贵 → 轻视

# 同义

n. 蔑视
`defiance` `snotnose`

# 同根

- adj. `disdainful` 轻蔑的；倨傲的；鄙视的
- adv. `disdainfully` 轻蔑地；倨傲地
- n. `disdainfulness` 轻蔑；藐视

# 例句

Janet looked at him with disdain.
珍妮特轻蔑地看着他。
