# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'bʌf/`
- 美 `/rɪ'bʌf/`

# 词义

n. 回绝
`an unkind or unfriendly answer to a friendly suggestion or offer of help`



re(反) ＋ buff(=puff喷， 吹) → 反过喷气 → 断然拒绝

# 同义

n. 断然拒绝；回绝；漠不关心
`indifference` `apathy`

vt. 断然拒绝
`slap on`

# 例句

He received a humiliating rebuff from his manager.
他遭到经理的断然拒绝，十分丢脸。
