# 音标

- 英 `/'tæs(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'tæsl/`

# 词义

n. 穗,缨
`threads tied together at one end and hung as a decoration on clothes, curtains etc`

# 同义

n. [服装]流苏；缨；穗
`ear`

vi. 抽穗；长出穗状雄花
`ear`

# 例句

She crafted a version of the black-and-white tassel dress Oscar de la Renta sent down the runway last month by hand-sewing store-bought tassels onto a tank top.
她缝制了奥斯卡•德拉伦塔（Oscar de la Renta）上个月在服装展示中推出的黑白两色的穗裙，把商店里买来的穗子缝到吊带背心上。
