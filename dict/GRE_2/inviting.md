# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'vaɪtɪŋ/`
- 美 `/ɪn'vaɪtɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 引人动心的,有魅力的
`something that is inviting is very attractive and makes you want to be near it, try it, taste it etc`

# 同义

adj. 诱人的；有魅力的
`attractive` `catching`

# 同根

- adj. `invitational` 邀请的
- adj. `invitatory` 邀请的
- adv. `invitingly` 动人地
- n. `invitation` 邀请；引诱
- n. `invite` 邀请
- n. `invitee` 被邀请者
- n. `invitational` 四人两球赛
- n. `invitatory` 请祈祷的赞美诗；宣召
- vt. `invite` 邀请，招待；招致

# 短语

- `bid inviting` 招标

# 例句

The log fire looked warm and inviting.
篝火看上去温暖诱人。
