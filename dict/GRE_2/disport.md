# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spɔːt/`
- 美 `/dis'pɔ:t/`

# 词义

v. 玩耍,娱乐
`to amuse yourself by doing things that are active and enjoyable – used humorously`



dis(加强) + port(带) → 带走(时间) → 玩耍

# 同义

vt. 使欢娱；使快乐
`joy`

vi. 娱乐；玩耍
`to entertain` `recreate`

n. 娱乐
`entertainment` `pleasure` `sport` `recreation` `amusement`

# 例句

...the rich and famous disporting themselves in glamorous places.
...明星富豪们在豪宅中自娱自乐。
