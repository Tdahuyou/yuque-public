# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒvɪd(ə)ns/`
- 美 `/'prɑvɪdəns/`

# 词义

n. 深谋远虑,天意,上帝,神的眷顾
`Providence is God, or a force that is believed by some people to arrange the things that happen to us`

# 同义

n. 天意；深谋远虑
`forethoughtfulness`

# 同根

- adj. `provident` 节俭的；有先见之明的；顾及未来的
- adv. `providentially` 神助地；幸运地；照天意
- adv. `providently` 有远虑地

# 例句

These women regard his death as an act of providence.
这些妇女将他的死看成是天意的展现。
