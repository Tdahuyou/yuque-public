# 音标

- 英 `/ɪndɪ'skraɪbəb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/,ɪndɪ'skraɪbəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 难以形容的
`something that is indescribable is so terrible, so good, so strange etc that you cannot describe it, or it is too difficult to describe`

# 同义

adj. 难以形容的；莫名其妙的；不能用语言表达的
`unnameable` `nameless`

# 同根

- adv. `indescribably` 难以形容地；不明确地

# 例句

a feeling of indescribable joy
难以言表的喜悦
