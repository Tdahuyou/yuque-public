# 音标

- 英 `/sɪ'kweɪʃəs/`
- 美 `/sɪ'kweʃəs/`

# 词义

adj. 盲从的




sequ(跟随)+acious(多…) → 跟随大多数的 → 盲从的

# 同义

adj. 盲从的；合于逻辑的；缺乏独创性的
`implicit` `bigoted`

# 例句

Teenagers are very sequacious and they often emulate the behavior of their idols who are sometimes negative role models.
青少年非常盲从，经常模仿他们偶像的行为，而有些偶像却是负面榜样。

Respecting the audiences doesn't mean being sequacious to the audiences. In fact, the relationship between the interior designers and the popular is dialectical and unitive.
对受众的尊重并不意味着对受众的盲从，室内设计师与大众的关系实际上是一种辩证统一的关系。
