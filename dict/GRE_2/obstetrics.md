# 音标

- 英 `/əb'stetrɪks/`
- 美 `/əb'stɛtrɪks/`

# 词义

n. 产科学
`the part of medical science that deals with the birth of children`

# 同义

n. [妇产]产科学，助产术
`midwifery` `tocology`

# 同根

- adj. `obstetrical` 产科的；助产的
- n. `obstetrician` 产科医师

# 例句

...the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology.
...美国产科和妇科学院。
