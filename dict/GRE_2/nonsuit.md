# 音标

- 英 `/nɒn's(j)uːt/`
- 美 `/,nɔn'sju:t, 'nɔn'sju:t/`

# 词义

n. 诉讼驳回
`an order of a judge dismissing a suit when the plaintiff fails to show he has a good cause of action or fails to produce any evidence `

# 同义

vt. 驳回
`wave sth. away` `disallow`
