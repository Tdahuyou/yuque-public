# 音标

- 英 `/'senɪʃ(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'sɛnɪfəl/`

# 词义

n. (中世纪贵族城堡中)管家
`a steward of the household of a medieval prince or nobleman who took charge of domestic arrangements, etc `

# 同义

n. （中世纪贵族的）管家，总管
`chamberlain` `majordomo`

# 例句

Only he, as seneschal, was granted that privilege, particularly while the master lay ill.
只有他，作为总管，赋有这特权，特别是当总领病卧在床。
