# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪgnɪəs/`
- 美 `/'ɪgnɪəs/`

# 词义

adj. 火的,似火的,[地]火成的
`igneous rocks are formed from lava(= hot liquid rock )`



ign(点燃) + eous → 火的

# 同义

adj. [岩]火的；火成的；似火的
`pyrogenic`

# 短语

- `igneous rock` 火成岩
- `igneous petrology` 火成岩岩石学

# 例句

The Great Dyke of Zimbabwe is a layered mafic intrusion of igneous, metal-bearing rock that has been dated to approximately 2.5 billion years old.
津巴布韦大堤是一个分层的火成岩，金属镁铁质侵入岩轴承已追溯到大约2.5亿年的历史。

Dr. Matter is involved in another similar study of peridotite, an igneous rock, where it occurs in a large formation in the Middle Eastern country of Oman.
马特博士还参与了另一项在中东国家阿曼进行的、与此相似的火成岩橄榄岩研究。
