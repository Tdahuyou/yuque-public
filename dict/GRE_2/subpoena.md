# 音标

- 英 `/sə'piːnə/`
- 美 `/sə'pinə/`

# 词义

n. 传票
`a written order to come to a court of law and be a  witness `



sub(下面) + poena(＝penalty惩罚) → 接下来可能受到惩罚 → 传讯

# 同义

vt. 传唤，传审；传讯
`cite` `arraign`

n. [法]传票
`citation` `summons`

# 例句

He has been served with a subpoena to answer the charges in court.
他被送达了一张出庭应诉的传票。
