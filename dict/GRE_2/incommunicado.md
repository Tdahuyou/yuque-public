# 音标

- 英 `/,ɪnkəmjuːnɪ'kɑːdəʊ/`
- 美 `/,ɪnkə,mjunɪ'kɑdo/`

# 词义

adj. 禁止通信的,被单独囚禁的
`If someone is being kept incommunicado, they are not allowed to talk to anyone outside the place where they are`

# 例句

He was held incommunicado in prison for ten days before being released without charge.
在被不予指控释放前，他在监狱里被单独监禁了10天。
