# 音标

- 英 `/'eksɔːsɪzəm/`
- 美 `/'ɛksɔrsɪzəm/`

# 词义

n. 驱魔,驱邪所用的咒语
`a process during which someone tries to make an evil spirit leave a place by saying special words, or a ceremony when this is done`

# 同根

- n. `exorcist` 驱魔的人；召魂者
- vt. `exorcise` 驱邪；除怪

# 例句

The exorcism was broadcast on television.
电视上播出了驱魔。
