# 音标

- 英 `/trɪ'pæn/`
- 美 `/trɪ'pæn/`

# 词义

n. 环锯,钻孔机,陷阱
`an instrument resembling a carpenter's brace and bit formerly used to remove circular sections of bone (esp from the skull) `

# 同义

n. 环锯；钻孔机
`drilling machine` `driller`

vt. 欺骗；诱入圈套；用环锯行手术
`sell` `fox` `kid`
