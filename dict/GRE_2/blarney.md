# 音标

- 英 `/'blɑːnɪ/`
- 美 `/'blɑrni/`

# 词义

n. 谄媚,奉承话
`pleasant but untrue things that you say to someone in order to trick or persuade them`

# 同义

n. 奉承话；谄媚；胡扯
`obsequiousness` `rose water`

vt. 奉承；哄骗
`flatter` `lick one's boots`

vi. 拍马屁；用好话劝诱
`suck up to somebody` `kiss ass`

# 短语

- `blarney stone` 巧言石（爱尔兰布拉尼城堡的石头， 相传吻此石头后即善于花言巧语）
