# 音标

- 英 `/əs'fɪksɪeɪt/`
- 美 `/əs'fɪksɪet/`

# 词义

v. 窒息
`to prevent someone from breathing normally, usually so that they die, or stop breathing`

# 同义

vt. 使…窒息
`strangle`

vi. 窒息
`choke` `strangulate`

# 同根

- n. `asphyxiation` 窒息
- n. `asphyxia` [医] 窒息
- n. `asphyxiator` 动物窒息器；碳酸气灭火器

# 例句

Three people were asphyxiated in the crush for last week's train.
三个人为了赶上周的火车窒息在人群中。
