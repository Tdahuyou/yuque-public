# 音标

- 英 `/'hɔːzə/`
- 美 `/'hɔzɚ/`

# 词义

n. 大缆,曳船索,系船索
`A hawser is a large heavy rope, especially one used on a ship`

# 同义

n. 粗缆；系船索
`mooring line` `mooring rope`

# 例句

It is clearly to sec that sway motion is increased with the increasing of the length of hawser.
计算结果表明，随着系缆索的长度的增加，油船的摇艏运动明显增大。
