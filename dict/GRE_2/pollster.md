# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊlstə/`
- 美 `/'polstɚ/`

# 词义

n. 民意测验专家,整理民意测验结果的人
`someone who works for a company that prepares and asks questions to find out what people think about a particular subject`



poll(民意调查) + st + er(人) → 民意调查员

# 例句

Celinda Lake, an opinion pollster in Washington DC who carries out work for the Democrats, has a thumbnail sketch of that swing female voter.
华盛顿的民意测验专家塞琳达·莱克为民主党工作。 她为立场摇摆不定的美国女性选民们勾勒了一幅速写图
