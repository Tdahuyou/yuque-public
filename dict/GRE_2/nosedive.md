# 词义

v. -dived,-diving（飞机）俯冲，急降
`if a price, value, or condition of something nosedives, it suddenly goes down or gets much worse`

# 同义

vi. 俯冲；暴跌；突然变坏
`collapse` `sharp drop`

n. （价格等的）急降，暴跌；（飞机的）俯冲；（名声）低落；（情况）突然变坏
`bepelt` `meltdown`

# 例句

Sales have nosedived since January.
自1月份以来销售额骤降。
