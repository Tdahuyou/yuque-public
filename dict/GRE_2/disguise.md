# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'gaɪz/`
- 美 `/dɪs'ɡaɪz/`

# 词义

v. 假装,伪装,掩饰
`to change someone’s appearance so that people cannot recognize them`



dis + guise(姿态， 伪装) → 假扮； 掩饰

# 同义

vt. 掩饰；假装；隐瞒
`simulate` `come`

n. 伪装；假装；用作伪装的东西
`simulation` `acting`

# 短语

- `in disguise` 伪装；乔装
- `a blessing in disguise` 塞翁失马
- `in the disguise of` 伪装，假扮成；托辞；以……为借口

# 例句

He escaped across the border disguised as a priest.
他装扮成神父越境逃走了。
