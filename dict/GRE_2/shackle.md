# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃæk(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'ʃækl/`

# 词义

n. 手铐,脚镣,桎梏,束缚物
`one of a pair of metal rings joined by a chain that are used for fastening together a prisoner’s hands or feet, so that they cannot move easily or escape`

# 同义

n. 束缚；桎梏；[法]脚镣
`chain` `restriction` `cord`

vt. 束缚；加枷锁
`chain` `bound`

# 例句

They finally managed to throw off the shackles of colonialism.
他们终于挣脱了殖民主义的枷锁。
