# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'fɜːl/`
- 美 `/'ʌn'fɝl/`

# 词义

v. 展开,展示,公开,使旗招展
`if a flag, sail etc unfurls, or if someone unfurls it, it unrolls and opens`

# 同义

vt. 展开；使…临风招展
`spread out` `decoil`

vi. 展开；公开
`expand` `spread out`

# 例句

Once outside the inner breakwater, we began to unfurl all the sails.
一出内防波堤，我们就开始展开所有的风帆。
