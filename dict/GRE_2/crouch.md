# 音标

- 英 `/kraʊtʃ/`
- 美 `/kraʊtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 蜷缩,蹲伏
`to lower your body close to the ground by bending your knees completely`



注意不要和couch(n. 长沙发)相混

# 同义

vi. 蹲伏，蜷伏；卑躬屈膝
`stay down` `grovel`

vt. 低头；屈膝
`head drop` `drop on one's knees`

n. 蹲伏
`couching`

# 例句

He crouched in the shadows near the doorway.
他蹲在门道近旁的阴暗处。

Paula crouched down and held her hands out to the fire.
葆拉蹲下来，把手伸向火堆。
