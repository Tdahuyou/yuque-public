# 音标

- 英 `/'stɪp(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'stɪpl/`

# 词义

n. 点刻,点画
`the technique of stippling or a picture produced by or using stippling `



stip(点)+ple → 用点画 → 点画

# 同根

- adj. `stippled` 上面有点画的；点刻过的
- v. `stippled` 点画；点描（stipple的过去式）

# 例句

I use a relatively heavy foot and stipple deeply at around 4200 strokes per minute.
我使用的是相对沉重的脚，点画在每分钟约4200中风深刻。

This morning as I write, the stark gnarl and tangle of winter is sugared with snow, deer tracks stipple the backyard and the pale yolk of the sun is oozing through the thick clouds.
在我写下这些文字的早晨，我望着窗外突兀的树干；如白砂糖般满天飞舞的冬雪，看着自家后院点点雄鹿留下的足迹，天空中淡黄色的太阳光勉力地从厚实的云层里渗出。
