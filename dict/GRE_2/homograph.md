# 音标

- 英 `/'hɒməgrɑːf; 'həʊm-/`
- 美 `/'hɑməɡræf/`

# 词义

n. 同形异义字
`a word that is spelled the same as another, but is different in meaning, origin, grammar, or pronunciation. For example, the noun ‘record’ is a homograph of the verb ‘record’.`

# 同根

- adj. `homonymous` 同名的；双关的；同音异义的
- adj. `homonymic` 同名的；同音异义字的
- n. `homology` 同源；相同；异体同形；同族关系
- n. `homomorphism` [数] 同态，同形；异质同形

# 例句

In the third chapter, identical words have been divided into synonymy and homograph from semanteme.
第三章从词义的角度出发，将同形词分为同形同义词和同形异义词。
