# 音标

- 英 `/saɪ'dɪərɪəl/`
- 美 `/saɪ'dɪrɪəl/`

# 词义

adj. 恒星的
`of, relating to, or involving the stars `

# 例句

Tropical year , and sidereal year are two different concepts .
在即回归年与恒星年是不同的两个概念。

And this was the way in which the sidero-solar problem was resolved, for the sidereal position of the full moon is in opposition to the invisible position of the sun.
这个方法之所以得以解决恒星-太阳问题，正是因为由恒星测定的满月位置与我们无法看到的太阳位置相反。
