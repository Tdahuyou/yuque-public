# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'sʌf(ə)rəb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/ɪn'sʌfrəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 难以忍受的
`If you say that someone or something is insufferable, you are emphasizing that they are very unpleasant or annoying`

# 同义

adj. 难以忍受的；不可忍受的；令人厌恶的
`impossible` `ugly` `unbearable`

# 例句

He was an insufferable bore.
他是一个非常令人讨厌的家伙。
