# 音标

- 英 `/'skæbəd/`
- 美 `/'skæbɚd/`

# 词义

n. 鞘
`a metal or leather cover for the blade of a sword`

# 同义

n. 鞘；[军]枪套
`sheath` `vaginae`

# 例句

David ran and stood over him. He took hold of the Philistine's sword and drew it from the scabbard.
大卫跑去，站在非利士人身旁，将他的刀从鞘中拔出来，杀死他，割了他的头。

The archaeologists also found bows, arrows, saddles, leather chest-protectors, boots, woodenwares, knives, an iron aw, a leather scabbard, and a sweater in the graves.
考古学家在坟墓中还发现了金属杯、箭、鞍具、皮革护胸、靴子、木制餐具、刀具、一副刀鞘和一件汗衫。
