# 音标

- 英 `/'iːθɒs/`
- 美 `/'iθɑs/`

# 词义

n. 气质,道义,民族精神,社会思潮,风气
`the set of ideas and moral attitudes that are typical of a particular group`



eth(=ethn民族， 种族) + os → 风貌

# 同义

n. 民族精神；气质；社会思潮
`temperament` `national spirit`

# 例句

a community in which people lived according to an ethos of sharing and caring
人们本着分享与关爱精神来生活的社区
