# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'sɒlv(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'sɑlvənt/`

# 词义

adj. 破产的
`not having enough money to pay what you owe`

# 同义

n. [经]破产者；无力偿还者
`bankrupt`

adj. [经]破产的；无力偿还的
`broke` `bankrupt`

# 同根

- n. `insolvency` 破产，无力偿还；倒闭

# 短语

- `become insolvent` 破产

# 例句

The company was later declared insolvent (= officially said to be insolvent ).
公司随后被宣布破产。
