# 音标

- 英 `/serə'neɪd/`
- 美 `/,sɛrə'ned/`

# 词义

n. 小夜曲
`a song sung to someone, especially one that a man performs for the woman he loves while standing below her window at night`



seren(安静) ＋ ade → 夜曲

# 同义

n. 小夜曲
`A Little Night Music` `notturno`

# 例句

Placido Domingo sang his serenade of love.
普拉西多·多明戈演唱他的爱情小夜曲。
