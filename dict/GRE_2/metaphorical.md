# 音标

- 英 `/,metə'fɒrɪkl/`
- 美 `/'mɛtə'fɔrɪkl/`

# 词义

adj. 隐喻性的,比喻性的
`a metaphorical use of a word is not concerned with real objects or physical events, but with ideas or events of a non-physical kind`

# 同根

- adv. `metaphorically` 隐喻地；用比喻
- n. `metaphor` 暗喻，隐喻；比喻说法

# 例句

The word has a metaphorical as well as a literal meaning.
这个单词除了字面意思还有一层比喻意思。
