# 音标

- 英 `/'fʌrəʊ/`
- 美 `/'fɝo/`

# 词义

n. 犁沟,皱纹
`a deep line or fold in the skin of someone’s face, especially on the forehead`

# 同义

n. 皱纹；[农学]犁沟；车辙
`rugosity` `wrinkle`

vt. 犁；耕；弄绉
`till` `plough`

vi. 犁田；开沟；犁出浪迹
`break ground`

# 短语

- `furrow irrigation` 沟灌，畦灌；畦沟灌溉

# 例句

A deep furrow appeared between his brows.
他眉间出现了一道深深的皱纹。
