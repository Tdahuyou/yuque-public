# 音标

- 英 `/'kwɪzlɪŋ/`
- 美 `/'kwɪzlɪŋ/`

# 词义

n. 卖国贼
`someone who helps an enemy country that has taken control of their own country`

# 同义

n. 卖国贼；内奸
`traitor` `agent provocateur`

# 例句

He is a quisling.
他是一个卖国贼。

Some netizens are relentless in their resentment of her. Even her micro blog followers received warnings to stay away from the "quisling".
一些网民无情地对她发泄心中的怒火，她微博的一些粉丝甚至收到了警告，叫她的粉丝远离张铭依这个“卖国贼”。
