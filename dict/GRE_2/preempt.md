# 音标

- 英 `/prɪ'empt/`
- 美 `/,primpt/`

# 词义

v. 先占
`If you preempt an action, you prevent it from happening by doing something that makes it unnecessary or impossible`



pre(预先) + empt(拿到) → 预先拿到 → 以优先权取得

# 同根

- adj. `preemptive` 优先购买的；先发制人的；有先买权的
- n. `preemption` 优先购买；强制收购；抢先占有；取代
- n. `preemptor` 获优先购买权者；先取者；先发制人者

# 例句

The law would preempt stronger local rules.
该法律将预先制止更强硬的地方性法规。

"the survival of the fittest," a slogan that virtually preempted all debate.
“适者生存”，一个几乎无可辩驳的口号。
