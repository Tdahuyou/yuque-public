# 音标

- 英 `/'hɜːd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'hɝdl/`

# 词义

n. 篱笆,栏,障碍,跨栏,活动篱笆
`a problem or difficulty that you must deal with before you can achieve something`

# 同义

n. 障碍；栏；跳栏
`obstacle` `let` `bar` `dam` `stop`

vt. 克服
`overcome` `surmount`

vi. 克服；跳过
`overcome` `rise above`

# 同根

- n. `hurdler` 制造竹篱笆者；跳栏比赛选手
- n. `hurdling` 跳栏赛跑
- v. `hurdling` 跨栏（hurdle的现在分词）

# 短语

- `hurdle race` n. 障碍竞赛；跳栏赛跑

# 例句

Finding enough money for the project was the first hurdle.
第一道难关是为该项目筹措足够的资金。
