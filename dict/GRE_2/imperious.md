# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'pɪərɪəs/`
- 美 `/ɪm'pɪrɪəs/`

# 词义

adj. 专横的
`giving orders and expecting to be obeyed, in a way that seems too proud`



imper(命令) + ious → 命令的 → 专横的

# 同义

adj. 专横的；迫切的；傲慢的
`pressing` `stringent`

# 同根

- adv. `imperiously` 专制地；妄自尊大地

# 例句

She raised her hand in an imperious gesture.
她傲慢地扬起手。
