# 音标

- 英 `/'əʊdɪəm/`
- 美 `/'odɪəm/`

# 词义

n. 憎恶,讨厌
`Odium is the extreme disapproval or hatred that people feel for a particular person, usually because of something that the person has done`

# 同义

n. 憎恶；讨厌
`animosity` `phobia`

# 例句

The complainant has been exposed to public odium, scandal, and contempt.
原告遭到了公众的憎恶、流言和蔑视。
