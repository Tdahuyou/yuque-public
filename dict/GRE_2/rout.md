# 音标

- 英 `/raʊt/`
- 美 `/raʊt/`

# 词义

n. 溃退
`a complete defeat in a battle, competition, or election`



route(道路)去掉e → 成功的道路上一失误就会溃败 → 溃败

# 同义

vt. 击溃
`wallop`

vi. 搜寻
`search` `hunt for`

n. 溃败；暴动
`insurrection` `sedition`

# 短语

- `put to rout` 击溃；打垮

# 例句

The battle turned into a rout.
战斗彻底失败。
