# 音标

- 英 `/'pʌm(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'pʌml/`

# 词义

v. 击,打,用拳头连续揍
`to hit someone or something many times quickly, especially using your  fist s (= closed hands ) `

# 同义

vt. 击；打；用拳头连续揍
`strike` `beat upon`

n. （马鞍的）前鞍桥；球壮末端；（刀把或剑柄的）圆头（等于pommel）
`saddlebow` `rounded end`

# 例句

Diane leaned over and pummeled the pillows.
黛安娜俯下身不断捶打枕头。
