# 音标

- 英 `/kʌltʃ/`
- 美 `/kʌltʃ/`

# 词义

n. 贝壳屑
`a mass of broken stones, shells, and gravel that forms the basis of an oyster bed `

# 同义

n. 贝壳屑；牡蛎卵块；垃圾
`refuse` `garbage`

# 例句

raft and cultch culture of oysters and mussels.
牡蛎和贻贝筏式养殖和碎壳床养殖

A male hermit crab had found a very big cultch, was then clean the shell cleaning up, has made own house.
一只公寄居蟹找到了一只很大的空贝壳，便把贝壳清理干净，做成了自己的房子。
