# 音标

- 英 `/'tɑːnɪʃ/`
- 美 `/'tɑrnɪʃ/`

# 词义

v. 失去光泽
`if metals such as silver,  copper  , or  brass  tarnish, or if something tarnishes them, they become dull and lose their colour`



tarn(隐藏) + ish → 隐藏光泽 → 失去光泽， 晦暗

# 同义

n. 晦暗，无光泽；污点
`dulness` `discoloration`

vt. 玷污；使……失去光泽；使……变灰暗
`cloud` `pollute`

# 例句

Gold does not tarnish easily.
黄金色泽不容易变暗。

tarnished silver spoons
发乌的银勺
