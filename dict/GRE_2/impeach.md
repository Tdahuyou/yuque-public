# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'piːtʃ/`
- 美 `/ɪm'pitʃ/`

# 词义

v. [律]控告,检举,<主美>弹劾,怀疑
`if a government official is impeached, they are formally charged with a serious crime in a special government court`



im(进入) + peach(告发) → 控告； 弹劾

# 同义

vt. 弹劾；归咎；怀疑
`dispute` `question` `wonder`

n. 控告，检举；弹劾；怀疑
`doubt` `challenge` `charge` `prosecution` `accusation`

# 同根

- n. `impeachment` 弹劾；控告；怀疑；指摘

# 例句

The governor was impeached for using state funds improperly.
州长因违规使用州政府资金而受到弹劾。
