# 音标

- 英 `/prɒg'nɒstɪkeɪt/`
- 美 `/prəɡ'nɔstikeit/`

# 词义

v. 预言
`to foretell (future events) according to present signs or indications; prophesy `



pro(提前) + gno(知道) + stic + ate → 预示

# 同义

vt. 预言，预测
`predict` `forecast` `calculate`

# 同根

- adj. `prognostic` 预后的；预兆的
- n. `prognostic` 预兆；预言；预后症状
- n. `prognostication` 预测；预言；预兆
- n. `prognosticator` 预言者

# 例句

Unfortunately, I am not able to prognosticate how everything will eventually shake out.
不幸的是，我无法预言每件事的最终结果将会怎样。

It has long been fashionable for Silicon Valley visionaries to prognosticate about the direction of technology.
硅谷的想象者预测了技术的发展方向流行了好长时间。
