# 音标

- 英 `/ʃuːt/`
- 美 `/ʃut/`

# 词义

n. 瀑布,斜道
`a long narrow structure that slopes down, so that things or people can slide down it from one place to another`

# 同义

n. 瀑布；斜槽；降落伞；陡坡道
`fall` `waterfall`

# 短语

- `spiral chute` 蜗旋滑槽；螺旋斜槽

# 例句

a rubbish chute
垃圾滑槽

a laundry chute
洗衣槽

The pool had several water chutes.
这个游泳池有好几条滑水道。
