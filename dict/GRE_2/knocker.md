# 音标

- 英 `/'nɒkə/`
- 美 `/'nɑkɚ/`

# 词义

n. 门环
`a piece of metal on an outside door of a house that you hit against the door to attract the attention of the people inside`

# 同义

n. 门环；敲击者；吹毛求疵的人
`nitpicker` `quibbler`

# 同根

- n. `knock` 敲；敲打；爆震声
- vi. `knock` 敲；打；敲击
- vt. `knock` 敲；打；敲击；批评

# 短语

- `door knocker` 门环
