# 音标

- 英 `/,entə'mɒlədʒɪ/`
- 美 `/,ɛntə'mɑlədʒi/`

# 词义

n. 昆虫学
`the scientific study of insects`

# 同义

n. [昆]昆虫学
`insectology`

# 同根

- adj. `entomologic` 昆虫学的（等于entomological）
- adj. `entomological` 昆虫学的
- n. `entomologist` 昆虫学者

# 例句

“We’ve learned quite a lot, ” said K.A. Monney, a professor in the university’s entomology and wildlife department.
“我们学到了很多，”该大学的昆虫学和野生动物系教授K·A·曼尼说。

But the bees are not as happy as they sound, which is why Mr Wardell, who has a PhD in entomology and is a de facto bee doctor, is here.
然而蜂群并不像听上去那么快乐，这也是沃代尔先生——这位昆虫学博士和货真价实的蜜蜂大夫——来到此地的原因。
