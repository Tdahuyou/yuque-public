# 音标

- 英 `/trə'dʒekt(ə)rɪ; 'trædʒɪkt(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/trə'dʒɛktəri/`

# 词义

n. [物](射线的)轨道,弹道,轨线
`the curved path of an object that has been fired or thrown through the air`

# 同义

n. [物]轨道，[力]轨线；[航][军]弹道
`path` `track` `railway` `orbit`

# 短语

- `ballistic trajectory` 射击弹导；弹道轨迹

# 例句

The decision was certain to affect the trajectory of French politics for some time to come.
这一决定在未来的一段时间里必将影响法国的政治轨迹。
