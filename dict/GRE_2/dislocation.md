# 音标

- 英 `/,dɪslə(ʊ)'keɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,dɪslo'keʃən/`

# 词义

n. 混乱,断层,脱臼
`Dislocation is a situation in which something such as a system, process, or way of life is greatly disturbed or prevented from continuing as normal`

# 同义

n. 转位；混乱；[医]脱臼
`chaos` `disorder` `confusion` `involvement` `mix`

# 同根

- adj. `dislocated` 脱位的；离位的，移位的
- v. `dislocated` 脱臼；弄乱（dislocate的过去分词）
- vt. `dislocate` 使脱臼；使混乱

# 短语

- `dislocation density` 位错密度；差排密度
- `screw dislocation` 螺旋位错；螺旋变位
- `edge dislocation` 刃型位错；边缘位错；刃差排
- `dislocation motion` 位错运动

# 例句

Millions of refugees have suffered a total dislocation of their lives.
几百万难民饱受生活颠沛流离之苦。
