# 音标

- 英 `/'kɔːd(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'kɔrdn/`

# 词义

n. 哨兵线,警戒线,饰带
`a line of police officers, soldiers, or vehicles that is put around an area to stop people going there`

# 同义

n. 警戒线；绶带；束带层
`warning line` `warning limit`

# 短语

- `cordon off` 布置警戒线戒严；隔离

# 例句

Police formed a cordon between the two crowds.
警察在两群人之间设置了一条警戒线。
