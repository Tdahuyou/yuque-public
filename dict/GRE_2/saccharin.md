# 音标

- 英 `/'sækərɪn/`
- 美 `/'sækərɪn/`

# 词义

n. 糖精
`a chemical substance that tastes sweet and is used instead of sugar in drinks`

# 同义

n. [有化]糖精；邻磺酰苯甲酰亚胺
`gluside` `saxin`

# 同根

- adj. `saccharine` 含糖的；极甜的；和颜悦色的
- n. `saccharine` （美）糖精
- n. `saccharinity` 含糖量；甜蜜；甜度

# 短语

- `saccharin sodium` 糖精钠
- `sodium saccharin` [化]糖精钠

# 例句

The saccharin-fed animals, on the other hand, showed no such rise in temperature.
另一方面，食用糖精的小白鼠并没有出现体温升高的现象。

The use of saccharin is strongly discouraged during pregnancy because it can cross the placenta and may remain in fetal tissues.
怀孕期间要强烈阻止糖精的食用，因为它可以穿过胎盘并可以积聚在胎儿的体内。

Though not fully researched and proven, your metabolic rates could be being altered by diet foods, including the low-calorie sweetener saccharin, which increases the chance of weight gain.
尽管这还没有确切的研究证明，但是你的新陈代谢速度可能被减肥食物所改变，包括低热量的甜味剂糖精，它增大了你获得体重的机率。
