# 音标

- 英 `/mə'lest/`
- 美 `/mə'lɛst/`

# 词义

v. 骚乱,困扰,调戏
`to attack or harm someone, especially a child, by touching them in a sexual way or by trying to have sex with them`



mol(磨) + est → 摩擦 → 骚扰

# 同义

vt. 骚扰；调戏；干扰
`harry` `ply with`

# 同根

- n. `molestation` 干扰，妨害；折磨；调戏
- n. `molester` 猥亵者；性骚扰者

# 例句

men who molest young boys
