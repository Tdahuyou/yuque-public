# 音标

- 英 `/deprɪ'veɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,dɛprɪ'veʃən/`

# 词义

n. 剥夺
`If you suffer deprivation, you do not have or are prevented from having something that you want or need`



来自deprive(v. 剥夺)

# 同义

n. 剥夺；损失；免职
`removal` `losing` `expense` `disadvantage` `cost`

# 同根

- vt. `deprive` 使丧失，剥夺

# 短语

- `sleep deprivation` 睡眠剥夺，剥夺睡眠
- `relative deprivation` [社会]相对剥夺；相对性匮乏

# 例句

Millions more suffer from serious sleep deprivation caused by long work hours.
还有数百万人由于工作时数长导致他们严重睡眠不足。
