# 音标

- 英 `/'daʊntrɒd(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'daʊntrɑdn/`

# 词义

adj. 被践踏的,被蹂躏的,被压制的
`downtrodden people are treated badly and without respect by people who have power over them`

# 同义

adj. 被践踏的，被蹂躏的；受压迫的
`oppressed`

# 例句

The owner is making huge profits at the expense of downtrodden peasants.
物主靠压榨农民而赢得暴利。
