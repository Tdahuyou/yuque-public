# 音标

- 英 `/'skɪnflɪnt/`
- 美 `/'skɪnflɪnt/`

# 词义

n. 吝啬鬼
`someone who hates spending money or giving it away – used to show disapproval`



skin a flint(爱财如命)

# 同义

n. 吝啬鬼；一毛不拔的人
`screw` `tightwad`

# 例句

A skinflint worships money.
吝啬鬼崇拜金钱。

I find myself feeling like either a deep-pocketed patsy or a skinflint.
我觉得自己好像是一个财大气粗的帕齐或者是吝啬鬼。
