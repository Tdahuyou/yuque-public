# 音标

- 英 `/kən'teɪdʒ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/kən'tedʒən/`

# 词义

n. 传染,传染病,蔓延,歪风,腐化
`a situation in which a disease is spread by people touching each other`

# 同义

n. 传染病；蔓延；触染
`infection` `epidemic`

# 同根

- adj. `contagious` 感染性的；会蔓延的
- adv. `contagiously` 传染性地；蔓延地

# 短语

- `emotional contagion` 情绪感染

# 例句

There is some danger of contagion.
有接触传染的危险。
