# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'naʊns/`
- 美 `/rɪ'naʊns/`

# 词义

v. 断绝关系
`to publicly say or show that you no longer believe in something, or will no longer behave in a particular way`



re(重新) + nounc(讲话， 通告) + e → 重新宣布 → 声明放弃

# 同义

vt. 宣布放弃；与…断绝关系；垫牌
`break up with` `finish with`

# 同根

- n. `renouncement` 否认；拒绝；放弃

# 例句

These groups must renounce violence if there is to be progress towards peace.
若要迈向和平，这些组织就必须放弃使用暴力。

Young people renounced capitalism in favour of peace and love.
年轻人提倡和平和友爱，摒弃资本主义。
