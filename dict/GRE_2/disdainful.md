# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'deɪnfʊl; -f(ə)l; -z-/`
- 美 `/dɪs'denfl/`

# 词义

adj. 轻蔑的,倨傲的
`showing that you do not respect someone or something, because you think that they are not important or good enough`

# 同义

adj. 轻蔑的；倨傲的；鄙视的
`scornful` `sniffish`

# 同根

- adv. `disdainfully` 轻蔑地；倨傲地
- n. `disdain` 蔑视
- n. `disdainfulness` 轻蔑；藐视
- vt. `disdain` 鄙弃

# 例句

a disdainful look
藐视的眼神
