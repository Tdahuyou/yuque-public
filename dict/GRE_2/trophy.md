# 音标

- 英 `/'trəʊfɪ/`
- 美 `/trofɪ/`

# 词义

n. 战利品,奖品
`something that you keep to prove your success in something, especially in war or hunting`

# 同义

n. 奖品；战利品；纪念品
`prize` `award` `capture`

# 同根

- adj. `trophic` 营养的；有关营养的

# 例句

A lion’s head was among the trophies of his African trip.
他非洲之行的战利品之一是个狮头。
