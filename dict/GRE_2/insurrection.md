# 音标

- 英 `/,ɪnsə'rekʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,insə'rekʃən/`

# 词义

n. 起义
`an attempt by a large group of people within a country to take control using force and violence`



in(反) + surrect(升起) + ion → 反对活动出现 → 造反

# 同义

n. 暴动；叛乱
`rebellion` `sedition`

# 同根

- adj. `insurgent` 叛乱的；起义的
- adj. `insurrectional` 叛乱的；起义的；暴动的
- adj. `insurrectionary` 暴动的；起义的，造反的
- n. `insurgent` 叛乱者；起义者
- n. `insurgency` 叛乱；暴动；叛乱状态
- n. `insurgence` 暴动；起义；造反；叛乱
- n. `insurrectionary` 暴动者，起义者
- n. `insurrectionist` 起义者；造反者；暴动者

# 例句

I had to do it, because insurrection must have its discipline.
我应当这样做，因为起义应当有它的纪律。

The necessary tactics of insurrection are to drown small numbers in a vast obscurity, to multiply every combatant by the possibilities, which that obscurity contains.
把一小部分淹没在广大的黑暗中，用这黑暗所创造的条件来加强每个战士的战斗力，这是起义必要的战略。
