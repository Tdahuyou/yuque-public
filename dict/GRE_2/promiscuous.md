# 音标

- 英 `/prə'mɪskjʊəs/`
- 美 `/prə'mɪskjuəs/`

# 词义

adj. 混杂的
`involving a wide range of different things`

# 同义

adj. 混杂的；杂乱的
`miscellaneous` `jumbly`

adv. 偶然地；胡乱地
`random` `wild` `accidentally`

# 同根

- adv. `promiscuously` 混杂地，杂乱地

# 例句

When such a tool is running in promiscuous mode, it can "sniff" everything going around on that segment such as logins and data transfers.
当以 混杂方式运行这种工具时，它可以“嗅探出”该网段上发生的每件事，如登录和数据传输。
