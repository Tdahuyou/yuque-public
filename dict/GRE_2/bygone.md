# 音标

- 英 `/'baɪgɒn/`
- 美 `/'baɪɡɔn/`

# 词义

n. 过去的事


# 同义

adj. 过去的
`past` `ago` `back` `bypast`

# 例句

These pillars were known as the Straits of Gibraltar in bygone times.
这些石柱在过去被称为是直布罗陀海峡。

One historical example is "Slave Trader", by Fatheya Zouhny, conveying a much darker side of life during a bygone era in Egypt.
一幅名为“奴隶贩子”历史绘画，出自范缇亚·佐尼之手，展现了过去的时代中埃及更为黑暗的一面。

He seated himself close to her, he gazed at her in silence, or he talked to her of years gone by, of her childhood, of the convent, of her little friends of those bygone days.
他的生活都集中在这一刻里。 他坐在她身旁，静静地望着她，或者和她谈谈过去的那些年，她的童年时期，她在修女院的情景和她那时的小朋友。
