# 词义

n. 锉屑,锉末
`Filings are very small pieces of a substance, especially a metal, that are produced when it is filed or cut`

# 同义

n. [机]锉屑；锉末
`rasping`

# 短语

- `iron filings` 铁粉，铁屑；锉铁屑（iron filing的复数形式）

# 例句

...iron filings.
...铁锉屑。

...metal filings.
...金属屑。
