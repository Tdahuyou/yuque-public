# 音标

- 英 `/,resɪ'prɒsɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/,rɛsɪ'prɑsəti/`

# 词义

n. 互惠
`a situation in which two people, groups, or countries give each other similar kinds of help or special rights`

# 同义

n. 相互作用（复数reciprocities）；[数]相互性；互惠主义
`interaction` `mutual effect`

# 短语

- `reciprocity and mutual benefit` 互惠互利
- `reciprocity theorem` [物]互易定理；[电磁]倒易理论

# 例句

They said they would press for reciprocity with Greece in the issuing of visas.
他们说，他们将会对希腊施压，以达成签证发放的互惠。
