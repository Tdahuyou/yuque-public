# 音标

- 英 `/'sɒfɪstrɪ/`
- 美 `/'sɑfɪstri/`

# 词义

n. 诡辩
`the clever use of reasons or explanations that seem correct but are really false, in order to deceive people`

# 同义

n. 诡辩；诡辩法
`casuistry` `quiddity`

# 同根

- adj. `sophistic` 强词夺理的；诡辩的
- n. `sophisticate` 久经世故的人；精通者
- n. `sophistication` 复杂；诡辩；老于世故；有教养
- n. `sophist` 诡辩家；学者，哲学家
- n. `sophism` 诡辩
- vi. `sophisticate` 诡辩
- vt. `sophisticate` 弄复杂；使变得世故；曲解

# 例句

Political selection is more dependent on sophistry and less on economic literacy.
政治竞选更多取决于诡辩之术而非对经济是否懂行。
