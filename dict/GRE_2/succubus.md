# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌkjʊbəs/`
- 美 `/'sʌkjʊbəs/`

# 词义

n. 女妖,妓女,魔鬼
`a female devil that in the past was believed to have sex with a sleeping man`

# 同义

n. 女妖；妓女；魔鬼；男魇魔
`devil` `demon`

# 例句

The succubus is the female counterpart of the incubus. She is a demon seductress who preys on sleeping men, draining them of their “vitality.”
女淫妖与男淫妖相对应，指的是一种以入睡男子为猎物，吸收他们精元的妖魔。
