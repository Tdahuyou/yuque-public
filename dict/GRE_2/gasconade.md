# 音标

- 英 `/,gæskə'neɪd/`
- 美 `/ˌgæskən'ed/`

# 词义

n. 吹牛
`boastful talk, bragging, or bluster `

# 同义

vi. 夸口，吹牛
`hot air` `talk horse`

n. 夸口，吹牛
`fanfaronade` `blague`

# 例句

Honesty and frankness do more for the public's confidence … than extravagant boasting or supercilious gasconade.
诚实和率直比言过其实的吹嘘或者高傲的自夸更能增加公众的信心。
