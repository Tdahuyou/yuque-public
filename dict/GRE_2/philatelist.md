# 词义

n. 集邮家
`A philatelist is a person who collects and studies postage stamps`

# 同义

n. 集邮家
`stamp-collector`

# 同根

- adj. `philatelic` 集邮的；集邮癖的
- n. `philately` 集邮；集邮者

# 例句

He is a philatelist; part of his collection is rare sets of postage stamps of the Qing Dynasty.
他是一位集邮家。他藏品的一部分是罕见的套头的大清国邮票。

The history of the post office is rarely of interest to anyone but the uniquely eager philatelist.
一提到邮局的历史，除了那些特别痴迷的集邮家外，恐怕很难再有谁会感兴趣。
