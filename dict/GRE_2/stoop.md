# 音标

- 英 `/stuːp/`
- 美 `/stʊp/`

# 词义

n. 弯腰,屈背,屈服
`if you have a stoop, your shoulders are bent forward`



站(stood)直了别弯腰(stoop)

# 同义

vi. 弯腰；屈服；堕落
`succumb` `to surrender`

n. 弯腰，屈背；屈服
`waist bends` `yieldance`

vt. 辱没，堕落；俯曲
`go to the bowwows` `go to the deuce`

# 同根

- adj. `stooping` 弯曲的
- v. `stooping` 屈服，弯腰（stoop分词形式）

# 短语

- `stoop to something` ◎委曲以求，曲意迎合

# 例句

Mr Hamilton was an odd, quiet man who walked with a stoop.
汉密尔顿先生是个怪人，不爱说话，走路时弓着背。
