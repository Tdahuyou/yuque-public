# 音标

- 英 `/'tɪtɪveɪt/`
- 美 `/'tɪtɪvet/`

# 词义

v. 打扮,装饰,化妆
`to smarten up (oneself or another), as by making up, doing the hair, etc `

# 同义

vi. 化妆；装饰；打扮自己
`make up` `put on make-up`

vt. 化妆；打扮；装饰
`dress oneself` `paint` `hang` `array`

# 同根

- vi. `tittivate` 打扮；装饰（等于titivate）
- vt. `tittivate` 装饰；打扮
