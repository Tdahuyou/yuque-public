# 音标

- 英 `/bɪ'hest/`
- 美 `/bɪ'hɛst/`

# 词义

n. [诗]命令,吩咐,要求
`because someone has asked for something or ordered something to happen`

# 同义

n. 命令；邀请；请求
`instruction` `order` `bidding` `asking` `request`

# 短语

- `at the behest of` 在…命令下

# 例句

The committee was set up at the behest of the president.
这个委员会是奉总统之命成立的。
