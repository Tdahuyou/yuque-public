# 音标

- 英 `/'prɪsɪ/`
- 美 `/'prɪsi/`

# 词义

adj. 谨小慎微的
`behaving very correctly and easily shocked by anything rude – used to show disapproval`

# 同义

adj. 为小事挂虑的；谨小慎微的；神经质的
`jumpy` `screwed-up`

# 例句

I grew to dislike the people from my background – they were uptight and prissy.
我开始讨厌和我有相同背景的人－他们很保守，总是一本正经的。
