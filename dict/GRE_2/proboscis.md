# 音标

- 英 `/prə'bɒsɪs/`
- 美 `/pro'bɑsɪs/`

# 词义

n. 鼻子,长鼻
`the long thin nose of some animals such as an  elephant `

# 同义

n. 鼻子；[昆]喙；象鼻；[无脊椎]吻
`nose` `boko`

# 短语

- `proboscis monkey` n. [动]长鼻猴

# 例句

At least they didn't give me a butterfly's proboscis, or crab mandibles.
至少他们没有给我一只蝴蝶的鼻子，或者一只螃蟹的下颌。
