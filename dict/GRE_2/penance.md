# 音标

- 英 `/'penəns/`
- 美 `/'pɛnəns/`

# 词义

n. [天主教]忏悔,苦修
`something that you must do to show that you are sorry for something you have done wrong, especially in some religions`



pen(惩罚) ＋ ance → 惩罚 → 自我惩罚

# 同义

n. 苦修；忏悔
`confession` `shrift`

# 例句

...a time of fasting, penance, and pilgrimage.
...斋戒、赎罪和朝圣的一个时代。
