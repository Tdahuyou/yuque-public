# 词义

adv. 概要地,概略地,立刻,草率地
`immediately, and without following the normal process`

# 同义

adv. 概要地；立刻；概略地
`soon` `immediately` `now` `shortly` `roughly`

# 同根

- adj. `summary` 简易的；扼要的
- n. `summary` 概要，摘要
- n. `summarization` [图情] 摘要；概要

# 例句

He was summarily dismissed.
他被立即开除。
