# 音标

- 英 `/'siːkwəstreɪt; 'siːkwes-/`
- 美 `/'sikwəstret/`

# 词义

v. 扣押,没收
`to take property away from the person it belongs to because they have not paid their debts`

# 同义

vt. 没收；扣押
`expropriate` `impound`

# 同根

- adj. `sequestered` 隐退的；偏僻的；幽静的
- n. `sequestration` 隔离；扣押；退隐
- v. `sequestered` 扣押（sequester的过去分词）；使…隐退；使…分开
- vt. `sequester` 使隔绝；使隐退；没收，扣押

# 例句

He tried to prevent union money from being sequestrated by the courts.
他试图阻止法院扣押工会的钱。
