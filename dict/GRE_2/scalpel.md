# 音标

- 英 `/'skælp(ə)l/`
- 美 `/skæl'pɛl/`

# 词义

n. 解剖刀
`a small, very sharp knife that is used by doctors in operations`

# 同义

n. [医]解剖刀；外科手术刀
`dissecting knife`

# 例句

I pointed with my scalpel to the chest cavity, where nothing but a black lump of coal squatted in the lipstick-red center.
我用解剖刀指着她的胸腔，那里只有一团黑乎乎的煤块，伏在唇膏般红色的胸腔中部。
