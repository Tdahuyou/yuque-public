# 音标

- 英 `/'vɜːdɪkt/`
- 美 `/'vɝdɪkt/`

# 词义

n. [律](陪审团的)裁决,判决,判断,定论,结论
`an official decision made in a court of law, especially about whether someone is guilty of a crime or how a death happened`



ver(真实的)+dict(说) → 说出真话 → 裁定， 裁决

# 同义

n. 结论；裁定
`conclusion` `ruling`

# 例句

The verdict was ‘not guilty’.
裁决为“无罪”。
