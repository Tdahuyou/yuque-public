# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'viːg(ə)l; ɪn'veɪg(ə)l/`
- 美 `/ɪn'viɡl/`

# 词义

v. 诱骗
`If you inveigle someone into doing something, you cleverly persuade them to do it when they do not really want to`



in + veigle(看作veil， 面纱) → 盖上面纱 → 诱骗

# 同义

vt. [法]诱骗；诱使
`entice` `prevail upon`

# 例句

She inveigles Paco into a plot to swindle Tania out of her savings.
她把帕科诱骗进了一个骗取塔尼娅积蓄的阴谋里。
