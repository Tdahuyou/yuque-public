# 音标

- 英 `/pɜːspɪ'reɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,pɝspə'reʃən/`

# 词义

n. 排汗
`liquid that appears on your skin when you are hot or nervous`

# 同义

n. 汗水；流汗；努力
`effort` `trial` `pain` `struggle` `try`

# 同根

- vi. `perspire` 流汗；分泌；渗出
- vt. `perspire` 使流汗；分泌出

# 例句

He wiped the beads of perspiration (= drops ) from his brow.
他擦去了额头的汗珠。
