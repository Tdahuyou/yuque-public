# 音标

- 英 `/'seʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'sɛʃən/`

# 词义

n. 割让,转让,[律]让与(他人)债权
`the act of giving up land, property, or rights, especially to another country after a war, or something that is given up in this way`



来自cede(v. 割让)

# 同义

n. （权利的）转让，出让；（领土的）割让
`negotiation` `attornment`

# 例句

Spanish cession of the territory in 1818
1818年西班牙割让的领土
