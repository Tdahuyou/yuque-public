# 音标

- 英 `/flɜː'teɪʃəs/`
- 美 `/flɝ'teʃəs/`

# 词义

adj. 爱调戏的,轻浮的
`Someone who is flirtatious behaves toward someone else as if they are sexually attracted to them, usually not in a very serious way`

# 同义

adj. 轻浮的；调情的；爱调戏的
`featherbrained` `light-minded`

# 同根

- n. `flirt` 急扔；调情的人；卖弄风骚的人
- n. `flirtation` 调情；调戏；挑逗；不认真的考虑
- vi. `flirt` 调情；玩弄；轻率地对待；摆动
- vt. `flirt` 挥动；忽然弹出

# 例句

He was dashing, self-confident and flirtatious.
他风度翩翩、自信满满且善于调情。
