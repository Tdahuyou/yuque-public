# 音标

- 英 `/sɪ'rɪn(d)ʒ; 'sɪ-/`
- 美 `/sɪ'rɪndʒ/`

# 词义

n. 注射器,注油器,洗涤器
`an instrument for taking blood from someone’s body or putting liquid, drugs etc into it, consisting of a hollow plastic tube and a needle`

# 同义

vt. 注射，[医]冲洗
`to inject`

n. 注射器；[医]洗涤器
`injector` `washer`

# 短语

- `syringe pump` 注射泵；注射器泵
- `syringe needle` 注射器针头

# 例句

As he reached over, Azrak slid a hypodermic syringe into his left arm.
他把手臂伸过来时，阿兹拉克便将皮下注射器刺进他的左臂。
