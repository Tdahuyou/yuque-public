# 音标

- 英 `/aʊt'fɒks/`
- 美 `/,aʊt'fɑks/`

# 词义

v. 以计取胜
`If you outfox someone, you defeat them in some way because you are more clever than they are`



out(出) + fox(狐狸) → 胜过狐狸 → 以机智胜过

# 例句

There is no greater thrill than to bluff a man, trap him, and outfox him.
没有什么比虚张声势吓唬别人、让他钻入圈套并用计将他击败更加刺激了。
