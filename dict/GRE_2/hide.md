# 音标

- 英 `/haɪd/`
- 美 `/haɪd/`

# 词义

n. 兽皮,皮革
`an animal’s skin, especially when it has been removed to be used for leather`

# 同义

vt. 隐藏；隐瞒；鞭打
`lock` `pocket` `conceal`

vi. 隐藏
`bottle up` `keep in`

n. 躲藏；兽皮；躲藏处
`fell` `jook`

# 同根

- adj. `hidden` 隐藏的
- n. `hiding` 隐匿；躲藏处；殴打
- v. `hidden` 隐藏，躲藏（hide的过去分词）

# 短语

- `hide from` 隐瞒
- `hide and seek` 捉迷藏
- `cattle hide` 牛皮
- `hide all` 全部隐藏
- `hide away` 把……隐藏
- `raw hide` [化]生皮；血光皮
- `hide out` 躲藏

# 例句

ox hide gloves
牛皮手套
