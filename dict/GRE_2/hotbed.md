# 音标

- 英 `/'hɒtbed/`
- 美 `/'hɑtbɛd/`

# 词义

n. 温床
`a place where a lot of a particular type of activity, especially bad or violent activity, happens`

# 同义

n. 滋长地，温床
`soil` `nest`

# 同根

- adj. `hothouse` 温室的；过分保护的；娇弱的
- n. `hothouse` 温室；温床

# 例句

The university was a hotbed of radical protest.
那所大学是激进抗议活动的温床。
