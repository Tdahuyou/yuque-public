# 音标

- 英 `/'ɒf(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'ɔfl/`

# 词义

n. 碎屑,残渣,废弃物,垃圾,(猪、牛、羊等屠宰后的)头,尾,下水
`the inside parts of an animal, for example the heart, liver, and kidneys used as food`

# 同义

n. 内脏；[环境]垃圾；碎屑；工业下脚
`inside` `refuse` `garbage` `junk`

# 例句

...all the blood and offal the butchers shove down in the gutters.
…屠夫们倒入下水道的所有血和杂碎。
