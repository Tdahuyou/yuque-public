# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'freɪ/`
- 美 `/dɪ'fre/`

# 词义

v. 支付,支出
`to give someone back the money that they have spent on something`



def(看作deaf， 聋) + ray(光线) → 聋人得到光线 → 有人帮助付款 → 支付

# 同义

vt. 支出；支付
`foot` `disburse`

# 同根

- n. `defrayal` 支付；支出
- n. `defrayment` 支出；支付

# 例句

The proceeds from the competition help to defray the expenses of the evening.
比赛的收入帮助支付晚会的开销。
