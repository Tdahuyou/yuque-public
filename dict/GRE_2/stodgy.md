# 音标

- 英 `/'stɒdʒɪ/`
- 美 `/'stɑdʒi/`

# 词义

adj. 油腻的,不易消化的,乏味的,暗淡的,塞满的,平凡的,庸俗的,笨重的
`If someone or something is stodgy, they are dull, unimaginative, and commonplace`

# 同义

adj. 平凡的；塞满的；庸俗的
`ordinary` `stock` `crowded`

# 例句

The company hasn't been able to shake off its image as stodgy and old-fashioned.
该公司一直未能摆脱其古板守旧的形象。
