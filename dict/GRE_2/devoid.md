# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'vɒɪd/`
- 美 `/dɪ'vɔɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 全无的,缺乏的
`If you say that someone or something is devoid of a quality or thing, you are emphasizing that they have none of it`



de + void(空的) → 空的

# 同义

adj. 缺乏的；全无的
`lacking` `scarce`

# 短语

- `devoid of` 没有；缺乏的

# 例句

I have never looked on a face that was so devoid of feeling.
我从未见过这样一张完全没有感情的脸。
