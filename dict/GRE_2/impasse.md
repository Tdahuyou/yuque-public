# 音标

- 英 `/æm'pɑːs; 'æmpɑːs/`
- 美 `/'ɪmpæs/`

# 词义

n. 僵局
`a situation in which it is impossible to continue with a discussion or plan because the people involved cannot agree`



im(不) + pass(通过) + e → 通不过 → 僵局； 死路

# 同义

n. 僵局；死路
`logjam` `deadlock`

# 例句

Negotiations seemed to have reached an impasse.
谈判似乎陷入了僵局。
