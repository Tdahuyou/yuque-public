# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'testeɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'tɛstet/`

# 词义

adj. 没有立下遗嘱的,不能根据遗嘱处分的
`to die without having made a will(= a statement about who you want to have your property after you die )`



in(无) + test(看到) + ate → 没有留下可以看的东西 → 未留遗嘱的

# 同根

- n. `intestacy` 无遗嘱死亡

# 例句

There are strict guidelines that must be followed if someone dies intestate – in other words without a will.
未立遗嘱死亡的情况有严格的指导方针，换句话说，如果未立遗嘱。

The second deals with intestate succession i. e. the mode of distribution prescribed by law in default of any provision made by the deceased.
即在死者没有对财产分配作出任何决定的情况下，根据法律规定的来分配遗产的方式。
