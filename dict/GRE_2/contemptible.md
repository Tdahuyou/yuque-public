# 音标

- 英 `/kən'tem(p)tɪb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/kən'tɛmptəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 可鄙的
`not deserving any respect at all`



来自contempt(n. 蔑视， 轻视)

# 同义

adj. 可鄙的；卑劣的；可轻视的
`despicable` `underhanded`

# 同根

- adv. `contemptibly` 卑鄙地；下贱地
- n. `contempt` 轻视，蔑视；耻辱
- n. `contemptibility` 可鄙；卑鄙；下贱

# 例句

They were portrayed as contemptible cowards.
他们被刻画成可鄙的胆小鬼。
