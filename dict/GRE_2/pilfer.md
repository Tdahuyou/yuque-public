# 音标

- 英 `/'pɪlfə/`
- 美 `/'pilfə/`

# 词义

v. 盗,偷,窃
`to steal things that are not worth much, especially from the place where you work`

# 同义

vi. [法]偷窃；小偷小摸
`boost` `pick pocket`

vt. 窃；[法]偷窃；小偷小摸
`steal` `scrump`

# 同根

- n. `pilferage` 偷盗，行窃；脏物
- n. `pilferer` 小偷

# 例句

The staff were pilfering behind the bar.
工作人员在吧台后面偷东西。
