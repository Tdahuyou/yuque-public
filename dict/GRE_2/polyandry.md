# 音标

- 英 `/'pɒlɪændrɪ/`
- 美 `/,pɑlɪ'ændri/`

# 词义

n. 一妻多夫,[植]多雄蕊
`the practice or condition of being married to more than one husband at the same time `

# 同根

- adj. `polyandrous` 多雄蕊的；一妻多夫的
- n. `polyandrist` 多夫妇女；赞成一妻多夫制的妇女

# 例句

They practice what is known as fraternal polyandry -- where the brothers of one family marry the same woman.
他们是事实上所谓的兄弟一妻多夫制（fraternal polyandry ）---一家的兄弟娶同一个老婆。
