# 音标

- 英 `/'fɔːnɪkeɪt/`
- 美 `/'fɔrnɪket/`

# 词义

v. 通奸
`a word meaning to have sex with someone who you are not married to - used to show strong disapproval`

# 同义

adj. 弓形的；拱状的
`arcuate` `arciform`

# 同根

- n. `fornication` 通奸；乱伦
- n. `fornicator` 私通者
