# 音标

- 英 `/'siːskeɪp/`
- 美 `/'si,skep/`

# 词义

n. 海景,海景画
`a picture of the sea`

# 同义

n. [海洋]海景；海景画
`seapiece` `waterscape`

# 例句

Opponents say a unique landscape or seascape is being overshadowed, to the detriment of tourists and residents alike.
反对者认为，风能技术会遮挡独特的风景或海景，对当地居民及游客都不公平。

The show also includes an exquisite Seurat seascape, some notable Cézannes and considerable painting by Courbet, Gauguin, Van Gogh and others.
这次展出的作品，包括一幅修拉的出色海景画，数幅塞尚名画，及库尔贝、高更、梵高等人的不少画作。
