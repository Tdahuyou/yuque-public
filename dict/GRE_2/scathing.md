# 音标

- 英 `/'skeɪðɪŋ/`
- 美 `/'skeðɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 严厉的
`a scathing remark criticizes someone or something very severely`

# 同义

adj. 严厉的；损伤的
`severe` `tight` `hard` `harsh` `rigorous`

v. 损伤；伤害（scathe的ing形式）
`hurting`

# 同根

- adv. `scathingly` 尖刻地；严厉地；伤害地

# 例句

If someone excessively flatters you for a minor success, you need to internalize it the same way you would internalize a scathing insult.
如果有人对你的一点点成功就过分奉承，你需要自己内部消化这种阿谀奉承就像你内部消化别人的严厉侮辱一样。
