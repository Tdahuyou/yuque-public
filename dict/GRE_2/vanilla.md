# 音标

- 英 `/və'nɪlə/`
- 美 `/və'nɪlə/`

# 词义

n. [植]香草,香子兰
`a substance used to give a special taste to ice cream, cakes etc, made from the beans of a tropical plant`

# 同义

n. 香子兰，[植]香草
`herb` `bouquet garni`

# 短语

- `vanilla ice cream` 香草冰淇淋
- `vanilla bean` 香草豆荚
- `vanilla extract` 香草精，香草萃
- `plain vanilla` 单纯功能

# 例句

I added a dollop of vanilla ice cream to the pie.
我在馅饼中加了一块香草冰淇淋。
