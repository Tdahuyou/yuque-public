# 音标

- 英 `/'senʃə/`
- 美 `/'sɛnʃɚ/`

# 词义

v. 责难
`to officially criticize someone for something they have done wrong`

# 同义

vt. 责难，责备
`impugn` `decry`

n. 责难
`decrial` `dyslogy`

vi. 谴责，责备
`accuse of` `blame for`

# 同根

- adj. `censurable` 该责备的；可非难的

# 例句

He was officially censured for his handling of the situation.
他因对这个情况处理不当而受到严厉批评。
