# 音标

- 英 `/sɪ'lerɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/sə'lɛrəti/`

# 词义

n. 敏捷,快速
`rapidity; swiftness; speed `



celer(速度) + ity → 快速， 迅速

# 同义

n. 快速；敏捷
`speed` `fleetness`

# 例句

It is an effective liquid separation tool with advantages such as simpleness, celerity and veracity.
该量取器使用方法简单、快速，是一种准确、有效的液体分离工具。

It improves the celerity and accuracy of speed tracking.
提高速度跟踪的快速性和精确性。

The results showed that series-determine method with merits of nicety and celerity has notable effects.
研究结果表明，一次性连续测定法具有快速、准确等优点，效果显著。
