# 词义

n. 新手
`A tenderfoot is a newcomer to a place or activity, especially a newcomer to the mines or ranches of the western United States`

# 同义

n. 新手，新来者；无经验者
`rookie` `freshman`

# 例句

I may look like a tenderfoot, but I think I can follow a trail or carry a pack if necessary.
我也许看起来像个新手，不过有必要的话我认为我也能跟着上路或扛包。
