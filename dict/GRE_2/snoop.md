# 音标

- 英 `/snuːp/`
- 美 `/snu:p/`

# 词义

v. 探听,调查,偷窃
`If someone snoops around a place, they secretly look around it in order to find out things`

# 同义

vi. 调查，窥探
`probe` `examine` `mouse`

n. 私家侦探，到处窥视；爱管闲事的人
`sherlock` `private detective`

vt. 窥察，窥探
`pry about` `pry into`

# 同根

- adj. `snoopy` 爱窥探的；爱管闲事的
- n. `snooper` 刺探者；爱管闲事者

# 例句

Ricardo was the one she'd seen snooping around Kim's hotel room.
里卡多就是她曾见过的在金的旅馆房间里到处窥探的那个人。
