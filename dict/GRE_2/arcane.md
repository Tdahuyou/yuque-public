# 音标

- 英 `/ɑː'keɪn/`
- 美 `/ɑr'ken/`

# 词义

adj. 神秘的,不可思议的
`Something that is arcane is secret or mysterious`



arcan(秘密) + e → 神秘的

# 同义

adj. 神秘的；晦涩难解的；秘传的
`mysterious` `uncanny`

# 例句

Until a few months ago few people outside the arcane world of contemporary music had heard of Gorecki.
直至几个月前，现代音乐神秘世界之外只有少数人听说过戈莱斯基。
