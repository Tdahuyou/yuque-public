# 音标

- 英 `/kəm'pleɪs(ə)nsɪ/`
- 美 `/kəm'plesnsi/`

# 词义

n. 满足,安心
`Complacency is being complacent about a situation`



com + plac(平静， 满足) + ency → 满足， 安心

# 同义

n. 自满；满足；自鸣得意
`satisfaction` `content` `fill`

# 同根

- adj. `complacent` 自满的；得意的；满足的
- adv. `complacently` 满足地；自满地；沾沾自喜地

# 例句

...a worrying level of complacency about the risks of infection from AIDS.
…对艾滋病感染危险的令人担忧的自满程度。
