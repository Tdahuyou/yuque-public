# 音标

- 英 `/sə'vænə/`
- 美 `/sə'vænə/`

# 词义

n. 热带(或亚热带)稀树大草原
`a large flat area of grassy land, especially in Africa`

# 同义

n. [生态]热带草原；热带的稀树大草原
`campos`

# 例句

As they travel inland atspeeds in excess of 60 feet a year, they merge with the rich grass andscattered trees of the savanna.
它们以每年超过60英尺的速度向内地迁移，融入了热带稀树大草原的丰富草地和零星的树木。

A mature male lion rests in the grasses of the Kenyan savanna.
