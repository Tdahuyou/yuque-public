# 音标

- 英 `/'eɪpeks/`
- 美 `/'epɛks/`

# 词义

n. 顶点
`the top or highest part of something pointed or curved`

# 同义

n. [数]顶点；尖端
`height` `summit` `sky` `peak` `point`

# 短语

- `leaf apex` 叶端
- `root apex` 根锥顶；根头；牙根尖
- `shoot apex` 枝条尖；苗端

# 例句

the apex of the roof
房顶的尖端

the apex of a pyramid
金字塔塔尖
