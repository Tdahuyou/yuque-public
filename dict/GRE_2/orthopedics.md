# 音标

- 英 `/,ɔ:θə'pi:dɪks/`
- 美 `/,ɔrθə'pidɪks/`

# 词义

n. [医]整形外科,整形术
`the part of medicine that treats illnesses or injuries that affect people’s bones or muscles`
