# 音标

- 英 `/ɔːdɪ'neɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,ɔrdn'eʃən/`

# 词义

n. 排成等级,分类,任命,[宗]神职授任,颁布法令
`When someone's ordination takes place, they are made a minister, priest, or rabbi`

# 同义

n. [生物]分类；任命；神职授任；排成等级
`classification` `category` `appointment` `breakdown`

# 同根

- n. `ordinate` 纵座标；纵线

# 例句

...supporters of the ordination of women.
…女性担任圣职的支持者们。
