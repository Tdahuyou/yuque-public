# 音标

- 英 `/pə'lɪgəmɪ/`
- 美 `/pə'lɪɡəmi/`

# 词义

n. 一夫多妻,一妻多夫
`the practice of having more than one husband or wife at the same time`

# 同根

- adj. `polygamous` 一夫多妻的；一妻多夫的
- adj. `polygynous` 一夫多妻的；[植] 多花柱的；多雌蕊的
- n. `polygamist` 多配偶的人；多配偶论者
- n. `polygynist` 主张一夫多妻制者；多妻的男子
- n. `polygyny` 一夫多妻

In endorsing polygamy, these women, particularly those in remote rural areas without running water, may be less concerned with loneliness than with something more pragmatic: help with the chores.

However, Woodruff's pronouncement, theManifesto, forbade polygamy only in the United States, and for a decade or so it continued in Mexico and other places outside the U.S. government's jurisdiction.
