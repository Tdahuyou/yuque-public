# 音标

- 英 `/ækwɪ'es(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/,ækwɪ'ɛsənt/`

# 词义

adj. 默许的,默从的
`too ready to agree with someone or do what they want, without complaining or saying what you want to do`

# 同义

adj. 默认的；默许的；甘心接受的
`conniving`

# 同根

- n. `acquiescence` 默许；默从
- vi. `acquiesce` 默许；勉强同意

# 例句

Perhaps you are too acquiescent.
或许你太过顺从了。
