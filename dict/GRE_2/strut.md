# 音标

- 英 `/strʌt/`
- 美 `/strʌt/`

# 词义

n. 高视阔步,支柱,压杆
`a long thin piece of metal or wood used to support a part of a building, the wing of an aircraft etc`

# 同义

vt. 炫耀；在…上趾高气扬地走；给…加支撑
`show off` `brag about`

n. [建][航]支柱；高视阔步
`leg` `backbone` `stay` `mainstay`

# 例句

...the struts of a suspension bridge.
…一座悬索桥的支柱。
