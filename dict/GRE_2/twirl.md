# 音标

- 英 `/twɜːl/`
- 美 `/twɝl/`

# 词义

v. (使)快速转动,捻弄
`If you twirl something or if it twirls, it turns around and around with a smooth, fast movement`

# 同义

n. 转动，旋转；旋转的东西；万能钥匙
`revolution` `rotation` `rolling` `turning` `wheel`

vi. 旋转，转动
`wheel` `circle` `screw` `turn` `roll`

# 例句

Bonnie twirled her empty glass in her fingers.
邦妮用手指转动着她的空杯子。
