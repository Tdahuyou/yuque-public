# 音标

- 英 `/bɪ'siːtʃ/`
- 美 `/bɪ'sitʃ/`

# 词义

v. 恳求,哀求
`to eagerly and anxiously ask someone for something`



be + seech(=seek寻求) → 寻求 → 恳求

# 同义

vt. 恳求，哀求；乞求，急切地要求得到
`pray for` `obsecrate`

# 同根

- adj. `beseeching` 恳求似的
- adv. `beseechingly` 恳求地
- v. `beseeching` 哀求；请求（beseech的ing形式）

# 例句

She beseeched him to cut out his drinking and his smoking.
她央求他戒酒戒烟。
