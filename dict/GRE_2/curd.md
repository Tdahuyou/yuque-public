# 音标

- 英 `/kɜːd/`
- 美 `/kɝd/`

# 词义

n. (常用复数)凝块,凝乳,凝结物
`the thick substance that forms in milk when it becomes sour`

# 同义

n. [食品]凝乳；凝乳状物
`renneting`

vi. 凝结
`concrete` `to freeze`

# 同根

- n. `curdling` 乳凝；凝化
- v. `curdling` （使）成凝乳；（使）凝结；（使）变质（curdle的ing形式）

# 短语

- `bean curd` 豆腐
- `fermented bean curd` 腐乳，豆腐乳
- `soybean curd` 豆腐
- `preserved bean curd` 腐乳；酱豆腐
- `milk curd` 凝乳
