# 音标

- 英 `/,ɪntə'siːd/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɚ'sid/`

# 词义

v. 调解
`If you intercede with someone, you try to persuade them to forgive someone or end their disagreement with them`



inter(在…中间) + ced(走) + e → 走到中间 → 代为求情

# 同义

vi. 调解，调停；求情，说项
`make peace` `go between`

# 同根

- n. `intercession` 调解；说情；仲裁
- n. `intercessor` 调解人；仲裁者

# 例句

They asked my father to intercede with the king on their behalf.
他们请我父亲代他们向国王求情。

It has also asked Britain and the United States to intercede.
它也请了英国和美国出面调停。
