# 音标

- 英 `/'fɪk(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'fɪkl/`

# 词义

adj. (在感情等方面)变幻无常的,浮躁的,薄情的
`can be used to describe someone who is always changing their mind about people or things that they like, so that you cannot depend on them – used to show disapproval`



和tickle(v. 呵痒)一起记

# 同义

adj. 浮躁的；易变的；变幻无常的
`mobile` `variable` `liquid` `unstable`

# 同根

- n. `fickleness` 浮躁；变化无常

# 例句

an unpredictable and fickle lover
一个捉摸不透、三心二意的情人
