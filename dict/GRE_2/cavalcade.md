# 音标

- 英 `/,kæv(ə)l'keɪd; 'kæv(ə)lkeɪd/`
- 美 `/'kævl'ked/`

# 词义

n. 队伍
`a line of people on horses or in cars or carriages moving along as part of a ceremony`

# 同义

n. 行列，队伍；骑兵队
`train` `cortege`

# 同根

- n. `cavalry` 骑兵；装甲兵；装甲部队

# 例句

...a cavalcade of limousines and police motorcycles.
...一支豪车和警用摩托的车队。
