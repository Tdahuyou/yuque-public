# 音标

- 英 `/'juːlədʒɪ/`
- 美 `/'jʊlədʒi/`

# 词义

n. 赞词,颂词,歌功颂德的话
`a speech or piece of writing in which you praise someone or something very much, especially at a funeral`

# 同义

n. 悼词；颂词；颂扬；赞词
`celebration` `tribute`

# 同根

- adj. `eulogistic` 颂扬的；歌功颂德的
- n. `eulogist` 赞颂者；作颂词者
- vt. `eulogize` 颂扬；称赞
- vt. `eulogise` （英）颂扬；赞扬（等于eulogize）

# 例句

The minister delivered a long eulogy.
部长致长篇悼词。
