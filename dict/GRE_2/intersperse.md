# 音标

- 英 `/ɪntə'spɜːs/`
- 美 `/,ɪntɚ'spɝs/`

# 词义

v. 散布,点缀
`if something is interspersed with a particular kind of thing, it has a lot of them in it`



inter + spers(撒播) + e → 在中间撒播 → 点缀

# 同义

vt. 点缀；散布
`distribute` `spread`

vi. 点缀；散布
`shed` `lie around`

# 同根

- n. `interspersion` 散布；点缀；布置

# 例句

sunny periods interspersed with showers
晴天，间或有阵雨
