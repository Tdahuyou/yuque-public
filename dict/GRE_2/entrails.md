# 音标

- 英 `/'entreɪlz/`
- 美 `/'ɛntrelz/`

# 词义

n. 内脏,肠,(物体的)内部
`the inside parts of an animal’s or person’s body, especially their intestines`

# 同义

n. 内脏；肠；（物体的）内部
`interior` `inside` `inner`

# 例句

All the alien innards in the film were actually sheep brains, raspberry jam and chicken entrails.
照片中所有的外星人内脏，其实是羊脑，木莓酱和鸡肠。
