# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'meɪnz/`
- 美 `/rɪ'menz/`

# 词义

n. 残余,遗迹,遗体
`the parts of something that are left after the rest has been destroyed or has disappeared`



来自remain(v. 保持)

# 同义

n. 残余；遗骸
`leaving` `vestige`

# 同根

- adj. `remainder` 剩余的；吃剩的
- n. `remainder` [数] 余数，残余；剩余物；其余的人
- vi. `remainder` 廉价出售；削价出售
- vt. `remainder` 廉价出售；削价出售

# 短语

- `still remains` 仍然是

# 例句

extensive Roman remains (= of ancient buildings ) at Arles
阿尔勒的一大片古罗马遗迹
