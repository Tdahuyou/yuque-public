# 音标

- 英 `/'təʊtəm/`
- 美 `/'totəm/`

# 词义

n. 图腾,标识,徽章
`an animal, plant etc that is thought to have a special  spiritual  connection with a particular tribe, especially in North America, or a figure made to look like the animal etc`

# 同义

n. 图腾；崇拜物
`idol`

# 同根

- adj. `totemic` 图腾的
- n. `totemism` 图腾崇拜；图腾制度
- n. `totemist` 崇拜图腾种族的人

# 短语

- `totem pole` 图腾柱；阶级，等级

# 例句

This opera is one of the cultural totems of Western civilization.
这部歌剧是西方文明的文化标志之一。
