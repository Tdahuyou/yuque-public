# 音标

- 英 `/pelf/`
- 美 `/pelf/`

# 词义

n. 钱财
`money or wealth, esp if dishonestly acquired; lucre `

# 同义

n. 钱财；不义之财，赃物
`theft` `picking`

# 例句

After plundering enough pelf, he took a train bound for the border.
在抢得足够的钱财后，他乘上开往边境的火车。

After plundering enough pelf, he took a train bound for the border.
在抢了足够的财物之后，他乘上了去往边境的火车。
