# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'beɪs/`
- 美 `/dɪ'bes/`

# 词义

v. 贬低,降低
`to make someone or something lose its value or people’s respect`



de + base(低) → 使低下去 → 贬低

# 同义

vt. 降低；使…贬值；掺杂
`lower down` `bring down`

# 同根

- adj. `debased` 低质量的
- n. `debasement` 降低；贬质
- v. `debased` 贬低（debase的过去分词）

# 例句

The medical profession has been debased by these revelations.
这些事被披露后，医学界的声誉受到了损害。
