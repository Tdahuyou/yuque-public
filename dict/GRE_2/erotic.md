# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'rɒtɪk/`
- 美 `/ɪ'rɑtɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 性爱的,性欲的,色情的
`an erotic book, picture, or film shows people having sex, and is intended to make people reading or looking at it have feelings of sexual pleasure`

# 同义

adj. 色情的；[生物]性爱的；性欲的
`pornographic` `sexy`

n. 好色之徒
`satyr` `lewdster`

# 同根

- adv. `erotically` 性爱地；色情地
- n. `eroticism` 色情；性的兴奋，性欲亢进；性爱倾向
- n. `erotica` 色情作品；色情书籍
- n. `erotism` 性欲；性冲动；色情过旺
- vt. `eroticize` 使充满色情，使更加色情
