# 音标

- 英 `/'nɜːvlɪs/`
- 美 `/'nɝvləs/`

# 词义

adj. 无力的,松懈的
`used to describe someone’s fingers when they cannot hold something firmly, especially because they have had a shock`

# 同义

adj. [临床]无力的；松懈的
`weak` `relaxed` `powerless`

# 例句

The key fell from her suddenly nerveless fingers.
钥匙从她突然麻木的手上滑落。
