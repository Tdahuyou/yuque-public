# 音标

- 英 `/riːə'ʃʊərəns/`
- 美 `/,riə'ʃʊrəns/`

# 词义

n. 放心
`something that is said or done which makes someone feel calmer and less worried or frightened about a problem`

# 同根

- adj. `reassuring` 安心的；可靠的；鼓气的
- adj. `reassured` 使消除疑虑的；使放心的
- adv. `reassuringly` 安慰地；鼓励地
- v. `reassuring` 使放心（reassure的ing形式）
- v. `reassured` 使安心；再次保证（reassure的过去式）
- vt. `reassure` 使…安心，使消除疑虑

# 例句

Parents are looking for reassurance about their children’s safety.
家长们在寻找方法保证他们孩子的安全。
