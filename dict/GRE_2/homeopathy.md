# 音标

- 英 `/,həʊmɪ'ɒpəθɪ; hɒm-/`
- 美 `/,homɪ'ɑpəθi/`

# 词义

n. 同种疗法
`a system of medicine in which a disease is treated by giving extremely small amounts of a substance that causes the disease`

# 同义

n. [临床]顺势疗法；同种疗法
`homoeopathy`

# 同根

- adj. `homeopathic` 顺势疗法的；同种疗法的
- n. `homeopath` 同种疗法医师；顺势医疗论者
