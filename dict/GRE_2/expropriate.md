# 音标

- 英 `/ɪks'prəʊprɪeɪt; eks-/`
- 美 `/ɛks'proprɪet/`

# 词义

v. 没收,征用,剥夺
`if a government or someone in authority expropriates your private property, they take it away for public use`



ex + propr(拥有) + iate → 不再拥有 → 没收

# 同义

vt. 没收，征用；剥夺
`shear` `sequestrate`

# 同根

- n. `expropriation` 征收，征用；没收

# 例句

The Bolsheviks expropriated the property of the landowners.
布尔什维克征收了地主的土地。
