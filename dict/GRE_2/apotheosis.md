# 音标

- 英 `/ə,pɒθɪ'əʊsɪs/`
- 美 `/ə,pɑθɪ'osɪs/`

# 词义

n. 尊为神,神化,颂扬,崇拜,典范
`If something is the apotheosis of something else, it is an ideal or typical example of it`

# 同义

n. 神化；崇拜，颂扬；尊奉为神
`praise` `worship` `celebration`

# 同根

- vt. `apotheosize` 神化；尊崇为神

# 例句

The Oriental in Bangkok is the apotheosis of the grand hotel.
曼谷的东方酒店是大饭店的典范。
