# 音标

- 英 `/'eksaɪz/`
- 美 `/ɛk'saɪz/`

# 词义

n. 消费税,货物税,国产税
`the government tax that is put on the goods that are produced and used inside a country`



ex + cis(切) + e → 切出去 → 切除

# 同义

vt. 切除；收税
`resect` `abscise`

n. [税收]消费税；货物税
`consumption tax` `goods tax`

# 同根

- n. `excision` 删除，切除；切除之物
- n. `exciseman` 收税官

# 短语

- `excise tax` 消费税；特许权税
- `excise office` 税务署

# 例句

...this year's rise in excise duties.
…今年消费税的增长。
