# 音标

- 英 `/'kɑːdɪg(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'kɑrdɪɡən/`

# 词义

n. 开襟羊毛衫(=cardigansweater)
`a  sweater  similar to a short coat, fastened at the front with buttons or a zip`

# 同义

n. 羊毛衫，开襟羊毛衫（等于cardigan sweater）
`knitted sweater` `woolen sweater`
