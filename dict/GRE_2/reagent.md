# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'eɪdʒ(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'edʒənt/`

# 词义

n. 反应力,反应物,试剂
`a substance that shows that another substance in a  compound  exists, by causing a chemical  reaction `

# 同义

n. [试剂]试剂；[化学]反应物
`reactant` `agentia`

# 短语

- `chemical reagent` 化学试剂
- `grignard reagent` 格氏试剂
- `analytical reagent` [化]分析纯试剂
- `nessler's reagent` 奈斯勒试剂
- `chiral reagent` 手性试剂
- `karl fischer reagent` 费歇尔试剂
- `reagent feeder` 给药机；给药剂机
