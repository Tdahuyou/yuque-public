# 音标

- 英 `/ævə'rɪʃəs/`
- 美 `/,ævə'rɪʃəs/`

# 词义

adj. 贪财的,贪婪的
`An avaricious person is very greedy for money or possessions`



来自avarice(n. 贪婪， 贪心)

# 同义

adj. 贪婪的；贪得无厌的
`greedy` `grasping`

# 同根

- adv. `avariciously` 贪婪地
- n. `avarice` 贪婪，贪财

# 例句

He sacrificed his own career so that his avaricious brother could succeed.
他牺牲了自己的事业，其目的是让他贪婪的弟弟成功。
