# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌmpaɪə/`
- 美 `/'ʌmpaɪɚ/`

# 词义

n. 仲裁人,裁判员
`the person who makes sure that the players obey the rules in sports such as tennis, baseball, and cricket`

# 同义

vi. 当裁判，任裁判
`ump`

vt. 仲裁，裁判
`arbitrate`

n. 裁判员，仲裁人
`judge` `referee`

# 同根

- n. `umpirage` 仲裁；公断；仲裁人之地位

# 例句

The umpire's decision is final.
裁判的判决是不可更改的。
