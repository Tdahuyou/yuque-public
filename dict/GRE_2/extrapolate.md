# 音标

- 英 `/ɪk'stræpəleɪt; ek-/`
- 美 `/ɪk'stræpə'let/`

# 词义

v. 推断,[数]外推
`to use facts about the present or about one thing or group to make a guess about the future or about other things or groups`



extra(外面) + pol(放) + ate → 放出想法 → 预测， 推测

# 同义

vt. [数]外推；推断
`understand` `conclude`

# 同根

- adj. `extrapolated` 推测的
- n. `extrapolation` [数] 外推法；推断
- v. `extrapolated` 推测（extrapolate的过去分词）

# 例句

Extrapolating from his latest findings, he reckons about 80% of these deaths might be attributed to smoking.
从他最近的发现推断，他估计大约80%的这类死亡可能归因于吸烟。
