# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'sɒmnɪə/`
- 美 `/ɪn'sɑmnɪə/`

# 词义

n. 失眠,失眠症
`if you suffer from insomnia, you are not able to sleep`

# 同义

n. [内科]失眠症，失眠
`sleeplessness` `agrypnia`

# 例句

If you have insomnia, try my simple cure.
如果你有失眠症，试试我的简单疗法。

I have great friends whom I love but never want to hang out with because insomnia has stolen my desire to be social.
我有很多我喜爱的朋友，但是我再也不想和他们去逛街，因为失眠症使我失去了社交的欲望。
