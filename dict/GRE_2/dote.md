# 音标

- 英 `/dəʊt/`
- 美 `/dot/`

# 词义

v. 昏聩,溺爱
`If you say that someone dotes on a person or a thing, you mean that they love or care about them very much and ignore any faults they may have`

# 同义

vi. 溺爱；昏聩
`overindulge`

vt. 溺爱
`overprotect` `spoil`

# 同根

- adj. `doting` 溺爱的，偏爱的
- n. `dotage` 溺爱；老耄；老迷糊
- v. `doting` 宠爱（dote的ing形式）

# 短语

- `dote on` 溺爱，宠爱；过分喜爱

# 例句

He dotes on his nine-year-old son.
他对自己9岁的儿子十分溺爱。
