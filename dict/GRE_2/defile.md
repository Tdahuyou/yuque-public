# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'faɪl/`
- 美 `/dɪ'faɪl/`

# 词义

v. 染污
`to make something less pure and good, especially by showing no respect`



de + file(=vile卑鄙的) → 使…卑下 → 弄污

# 同义

vt. 污损，弄脏；染污
`soil` `mud` `spot` `dirty`

n. 狭谷；隘路
`narrow` `cajon`

# 同根

- n. `defilement` 污秽；弄脏；污辱
- n. `defiler` 亵渎者；玷污者；蝎子

# 例句

Hallam’s tomb had been defiled and looted.
哈勒姆的坟墓遭到了亵渎和洗劫。
