# 音标

- 英 `/,sɪmbɪ'əʊsɪs; -baɪ-/`
- 美 `/,sɪmbaɪ'osɪs/`

# 词义

n. [生]共生(现象),合作(或互利,互依)关系
`a relationship between people or organizations that depend on each other equally`



sym(共同) + bio(生命) + sis → 共生(关系)

# 同义

n. [生态]共生；合作关系；共栖
`partnership` `intergrowth`

# 同根

- adj. `symbiotic` [生态] 共生的；共栖的

# 例句

...the link between bacteria, symbiosis, and the evolution of plants and animals.
...细菌、共生和动植物进化间的联系。
