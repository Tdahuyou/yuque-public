# 音标

- 英 `/jʊ'zɜːp; jʊ'sɜːp/`
- 美 `/ju'zɝp/`

# 词义

v. 篡夺,篡位,侵占
`to take someone else’s power, position, job etc when you do not have the right to`

# 同义

vt. 篡夺；夺取；侵占
`seize` `snatch up`

# 同根

- n. `usurpation` 篡夺；夺取
- n. `usurper` 篡位者；篡夺者；僭取者

# 例句

There were a couple of attempts to usurp the young king.
屡次有人企图篡夺那位年轻国王的王位。
