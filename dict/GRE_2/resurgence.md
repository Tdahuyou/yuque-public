# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'sɜːdʒ(ə)ns/`
- 美 `/rɪ'sɝdʒəns/`

# 词义

n. 苏醒
`the reappearance and growth of something that was common in the past`



re(重新) + surg(升起) + ence → 再起

# 同义

n. 复活；再现；再起
`revival` `regenesis`

# 同根

- adj. `resurgent` 复活的；复苏的
- n. `resurrection` 复活；恢复；复兴
- n. `resurgent` 复活者
- vi. `resurrect` 复活
- vi. `resurge` 复活；再起
- vt. `resurrect` 使复活；复兴；挖出

# 短语

- `economic resurgence` 经济复生

# 例句

Police say drugs traffickers are behind the resurgence of violence.
警方说毒贩是暴力事件增加的原因。
