# 音标

- 英 `/dɑːt/`
- 美 `/dɑrt/`

# 词义

n. 标枪,镖,(昆虫的)刺,飞快的动作
`a small pointed object that is thrown or shot as a weapon, or one that is thrown in the game of darts`

# 同义

n. 镖；飞镖；标枪；猛冲
`javelin` `onrush`

vi. 突进；投掷；猛冲
`pitch` `throw`

vt. 投掷；投射
`cast` `toss`

# 同根

- n. `darter` 飞奔者；投掷者

# 例句

a poisoned dart
毒镖
