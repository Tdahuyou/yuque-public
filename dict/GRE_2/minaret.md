# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪnəret; ,mɪnə'ret/`
- 美 `/,mɪnə'rɛt/`

# 词义

n. 尖塔
`a tall thin tower on a  mosque  , from which Muslims are called to prayer`

# 同义

n. 尖塔
`steeple`

# 例句

They blasted a minaret too.
他们还朝一个尖塔开了炮。

The mosque of umar,located next to the church of the holy sepulcher,features a syrian-type minaret.
位于圣墓教堂附近的欧麦尔清真寺，有一个叙利亚式尖塔。
