# 音标

- 英 `/aɪ'detɪk/`
- 美 `/aɪ'dɛtɪk/`

# 词义

adj. (头脑中的印象)异常清晰的
`(of visual, or sometimes auditory, images) exceptionally vivid and allowing detailed recall of something previously perceived: thought to be common in children`

# 例句

Your eidetic memory must be a blessing.
你异常清晰的记忆，是一种祝福。

When he was twelve he’d learned that he’d been born with an eidetic memory.
当他还是十二岁的时候，他知道他天生有幅异常清晰的记忆。
