# 音标

- 英 `/,diːɪfɪ'keɪʃən/`
- 美 `/,di:ifi'keiʃən/`

# 词义

n. 祀为神,神格化,奉若神明
`If you talk about the deification of someone or something, you mean that they are regarded with very great respect and are not criticized at all`

# 同义

n. 神化；奉若神明；祀为神
`apotheosis`

# 同根

- adj. `deific` 予以神化的；神圣的；奉为神的
- vt. `deify` 把…奉若神明；把…神化；崇拜

# 例句

...the deification of science in the 1940s.
...二十世纪四十年代科学神圣化。
