# 音标

- 英 `/,prɒmə'nɑːd; -'neɪd; 'prɒm-/`
- 美 `/,prɑmə'ned/`

# 词义

n. 散步
`a wide road next to the beach, where people can walk for pleasure`



pro(向前) + men(to lead) + ade → 引着自己向前 → 散步， 开车兜风

# 同义

vi. 散步
`have a walk` `pasear` `to take a walk`

vt. 散步，漫步；骑马
`have a walk` `walk along`

n. 散步；舞会；骑马
`ball` `walk` `dance`

# 例句

an evening promenade
