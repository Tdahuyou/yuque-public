# 音标

- 英 `/'suːtʃə/`
- 美 `/'sutʃɚ/`

# 词义

n. [医]缝合,缝合处,缝合用的线
`A suture is a stitch made to join together the open parts of a wound, especially one made after a patient has been operated on`

# 同义

n. [外科]缝合；缝合处；缝合用的线
`stitching` `seaming`

vt. [外科]缝合
`seam` `oversew`

# 短语

- `suture line` 缝合线；缝线
- `suture needle` 缝（合）针

# 例句

It's suture.
那是缝合线。
