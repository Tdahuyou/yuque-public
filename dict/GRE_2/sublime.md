# 音标

- 英 `/səˈblaɪm/`
- 美 `/sə'blaɪm/`

# 词义

adj. 庄严的,崇高的,壮观的,卓越的,极端的,异常的
`used to describe feelings or behaviour that are very great or extreme, especially when someone seems not to notice what is happening around them`

# 同义

adj. 庄严的；令人崇敬的；极端的；超群的
`ultra` `extreme` `solemn` `sacred` `majestic`

n. 崇高；顶点
`height` `summit` `sky` `peak` `apex`

vi. 升华；纯化；变高尚
`rarefy`

# 同根

- adj. `sublimate` 纯净化的；理想化的；高尚的
- adv. `sublimely` 高尚地，卓越地
- n. `sublimation` [物] 升华，[心理] 升华作用；高尚化；升华物
- n. `sublimate` 升华物
- n. `sublimity` 崇高；庄严；气质高尚
- vi. `sublimate` 升华；纯化
- vt. `sublimate` 使升华；使高尚

# 例句

an air of sublime contentment
极为满足的神态
