# 音标

- 英 `/spɜːn/`
- 美 `/spə:n/`

# 词义

v. 弃绝
`to refuse to accept something or someone, especially because you are too proud`



spur(刺激) + n(看作no) → 不再刺激， 不再鼓励 → 拒绝， 摈弃

# 同义

vt. 唾弃；冷落；一脚踢开
`conspue` `absent treatment`

vi. 摒弃；藐视
`do without` `think scorn of`

n. 藐视，摒弃；踢开
`disdainfulness`

# 例句

She spurned all offers of help.
她骄傲地拒绝一切帮助。

a spurned lover
被轻蔑拒绝的爱慕者
