# 音标

- 英 `/fjuːm/`
- 美 `/fjum/`

# 词义

n. (浓烈或难闻的)烟,气体,一阵愤怒(或不安)
`Fumes are the unpleasant and often unhealthy smoke and gases that are produced by fires or by things such as chemicals, fuel, or cooking`



有声望(fame)的人不会因为小事发怒(fume)

# 同义

vi. 冒烟；发怒
`smoke` `anger`

n. 烟；愤怒，烦恼
`smoke` `anger` `worry` `trouble` `wrath`

# 同根

- adj. `fumed` 熏过的
- vt. `fumed` 熏（fume的过去分词）

# 短语

- `fume hood` 通风橱；烟橱
- `exhaust fume` 排烟；废气

# 例句

...car exhaust fumes.
…汽车尾气。
