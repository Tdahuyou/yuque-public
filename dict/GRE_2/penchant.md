# 音标

- 英 `/ˈpɒŋʃɒŋ/`
- 美 `/'pentʃənt/`

# 词义

n. (强烈的)倾向,趣味




pench(=pend挂) + ant → 对…挂着一颗心 → 爱好

# 同义

n. 嗜好；倾向
`tendency` `liability` `weakness` `habit` `trend`

# 例句

There is also reason to think that our penchant for makingunhealthy choices might be enhanced by the abundance of particularfoods.
我们也有理由认为，我们做出不健康选择的倾向，也许被大量特定食物加强了。
