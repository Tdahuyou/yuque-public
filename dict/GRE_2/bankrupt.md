# 音标

- 英 `/'bæŋkrʌpt/`
- 美 `/'bæŋkrʌpt/`

# 词义

n. 破产者
`someone who has officially said that they cannot pay their debts`

# 同义

adj. [经]破产的
`broke` `belly-up`

vt. [经]使破产
`put out of business` `bust`

n. [经]破产者
`insolvent`

# 同根

- n. `bankruptcy` 破产

# 短语

- `go bankrupt` 破产
- `bankrupt of` 完全缺乏

# 例句

In total, 80% of bankrupts are men.
总体来看，有80%的破产者为男性。
