# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'dem(p)ʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/rɪ'dɛmpʃən/`

# 词义

n. 赎回,偿还,拯救,履行
`the state of being freed from the power of evil, believed by Christians to be made possible by Jesus Christ`

# 同义

n. 赎回；拯救；[金融]偿还；实践
`payment` `practice` `retrieval` `salvation` `repayment`

# 同根

- adj. `redemptive` 赎回的；赎身的；挽回的

# 短语

- `shawshank redemption` 肖申克的救赎（电影名称）
- `beyond redemption` 不可救药；不可挽回

# 例句

He craves redemption for his sins.
他渴望对其罪孽的救赎。
