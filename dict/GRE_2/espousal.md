# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'spaʊz(ə)l; e-/`
- 美 `/i'spauzəl/`

# 词义

n. 订婚,婚礼,拥护
`A government's or person's espousal of a particular policy, cause, or belief is their strong support of it`

# 同义

n. 拥护；信奉；订婚
`up with` `advocacy`

# 同根

- vt. `espouse` 支持；嫁娶；赞成；信奉

# 例句

...the Slovene leadership's espousal of reform and nationalism.
...斯洛文尼亚领导层对改革和民族主义的拥护。
