# 音标

- 英 `/'tɪtjʊlə/`
- 美 `/'tɪtʃəlɚ/`

# 词义

adj. 有名无实的,有资格的,有头衔的
`A titular job or position has a name that makes it seem important, although the person who has it is not really powerful`



来自title(n. 头衔)

# 同义

adj. 有名无实的；有头衔的；标题的
`title` `nominal`

# 例句

He is titular head, and merely signs laws occasionally.
他是名义上的首长，仅是偶尔签署法律而已。
