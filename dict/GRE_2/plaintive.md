# 音标

- 英 `/'pleɪntɪv/`
- 美 `/'plentɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 悲哀的,哀伤的
`a plaintive sound is high, like someone crying, and sounds sad`



来自plaint(n. 哀诉)

# 同义

adj. 哀伤的；悲哀的
`sad` `sorrow-stricken`

# 同根

- adv. `plaintively` 哀怨地；伤心地
- n. `plaint` 感叹；诉苦；悲叹

# 例句

They lay on the firm sands, listening to the plaintive cry of the seagulls.
他们躺在坚实的沙滩上，听着海鸥的哀鸣。
