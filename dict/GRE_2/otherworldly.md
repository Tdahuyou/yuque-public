# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌðə'wɜːldlɪ/`
- 美 `/'ʌðɚ'wɝldli/`

# 词义

adj. 来世的,修来世的,专注于精神方面的
`Otherworldly people, things, and places seem strange or spiritual, and not much connected with ordinary things`

# 同根

- n. `otherworld` 来世；冥界
- n. `otherworldliness` 来世；冥间；理想世界；空想

# 例句

They encourage an image of the region as an otherworldly sort of place.
他们想为这个地区营造出一个世外桃源的形象。

...a strange, otherworldly smile.
...古怪而超然的微笑。
