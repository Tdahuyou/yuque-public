# 音标

- 英 `/spruːs/`
- 美 `/sprʊs/`

# 词义

n. 云杉
`a tree that grows in nor-thern countries and has short leaves shaped like needles`

# 同义

adj. 整洁的
`neat` `kempt`

n. [木][植]云杉
`Picea asperata` `picea`

# 短语

- `spruce up` 打扮整齐漂亮
- `sitka spruce` [植物]西加云杉；[植物]阿拉斯加云杉

# 例句

Trees such as spruce, pine, and oak have been planted.
像云杉、松树和橡树之类的树已经种了。
