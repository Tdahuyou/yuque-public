# 音标

- 英 `/priː'rekwɪzɪt/`
- 美 `/,pri'rɛkwəzɪt/`

# 词义

n. 先决条件
`something that is necessary before something else can happen or be done`



pre(预先) + re(一再) + quis(要求) + ite → 预先一再要求 → 先决条件

# 同义

n. 先决条件
`prior condition` `pre-condition`

# 例句

Good self-esteem is a prerequisite for a happy life.
良好的自尊心是幸福生活的先决条件。
