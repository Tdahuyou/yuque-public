# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'fæmət(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/dɪ'fæmətɔri/`

# 词义

adj. 破坏名誉的,诽谤的
`Speech or writing that is defamatory is likely to damage someone's good reputation by saying something bad and untrue about them`

# 同义

adj. 诽谤的；中伤的；破坏名誉的
`calumniatory` `infamatory`

# 同根

- n. `defamation` 诽谤；中伤
- v. `defame` 诽谤；中伤

# 例句

The article was highly defamatory.
这篇文章充满诽谤。
