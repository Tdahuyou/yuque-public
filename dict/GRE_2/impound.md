# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'pəʊnd/`
- 美 `/ɪm'paʊnd/`

# 词义

v. 关在栏中,拘留,扣押,没收
`if the police or law courts impound something you have or own, they keep it until it has been decided that you can have it back`

# 同义

vt. 没收；拘留；将…关在圈中
`hold` `sequestrate`

# 同根

- n. `impoundment` 蓄水；扣留

# 例句

He sued the police after they impounded his car.
警察扣押他的车后他提起诉讼。
