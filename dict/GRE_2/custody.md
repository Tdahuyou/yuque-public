# 音标

- 英 `/'kʌstədɪ/`
- 美 `/'kʌstədi/`

# 词义

n. 保管
`when someone is responsible for keeping and looking after something`

# 同义

n. [经管]保管；监护；拘留；抚养权
`keeping` `detention`

# 同根

- adj. `custodial` 保管的；保管人的
- n. `custodian` 管理人；监护人；保管人
- n. `custodial` 圣物保管容器

# 短语

- `in custody` 被拘留
- `custody transfer` 运输监护

# 例句

Managers are responsible for the safe custody and retention of records.
经理有责任安全保管并保留记录。

The collection of art books is now in the custody of the university.
这批艺术类藏书现在由这所大学保管。
