# 音标

- 英 `/mə'ræzməs/`
- 美 `/mə'ræzməs/`

# 词义

n. 消瘦,衰弱
`general emaciation and wasting, esp of infants, thought to be associated with severe malnutrition or impaired utilization of nutrients `

# 同义

n. 消瘦；衰弱
`breakdown` `tabefaction`

# 例句

An inability to do that is the price paid for protection from famine by those predisposed to marasmus.
由那些预先安排给消瘦症患者免于饥馑措施付出的代价就是，要做到这些相当无能为力。
