# 音标

- 英 `/prə'rəʊg/`
- 美 `/prəu'rəuɡ/`

# 词义

n. 休会




pro(前面) + rog(问) + ue → 在前面通知下次开会(的日期) → 休会

# 同根

- n. `prorogation` 休会；闭会

# 例句

It was the third time the prime minister had employed the prorogue in as many years.
这是多年以来首相第三次实施休会。

That's exactly what Canadian Prime Minister Stephen Harper did in early 2010, employing a time-honored Canadian political tactic known as a "prorogue" to shut down parliamentary debate.
这正是加拿大首相史蒂芬·哈珀在2010年初所做的，使用久负盛名、被称为“休会”的加拿大政治策略停止议会辩论。

The legislature moved to prorogue the present session.
立法机关提议休止目前的会议。
