# 音标

- 英 `/'hɑːd'hedɪd/`
- 美 `/'hɑ:d'hedid/`

# 词义

adj. 冷静的,脚踏实地的,无懈可击的,顽固的
`You use hardheaded to describe someone who is practical and determined to get what they want or need, and who does not allow emotions to affect their actions`



hard(硬的) + head(头) + ed → 头脑坚硬的 → 冷静的

# 同义

adj. 脚踏实地的；冷静的；无懈可击的；顽固的
`resistant` `cool` `philosophical`

# 例句

...a hardheaded and shrewd businesswoman.
...一位务实、精明的女商人。
