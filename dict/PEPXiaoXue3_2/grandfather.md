# 音标

- 英 `/'græn(d)fɑːðə/`
- 美 `/'græn'fɑðɚ/`

# 词义

`the father of your father or mother`

# 同义

n. 祖父；始祖
`ancestor` `primogenitor`

# 同根

- n. `granddaddy` 祖父；外公；爷爷

# 短语

- `grandfather clock` 落地式大摆钟
- `maternal grandfather` 外公，外祖父

# 例句

His grandfather lies in the churchyard.
他祖父葬于教堂的墓地。

His grandfather is always carping  about the young.
他的祖父总是指摘青年人不懂事。

His grandfather roamed over the earthquake in Tangshan for a long time.
他的祖父东拉西扯地谈了半天唐山的地震。
