# 音标

- 英 `/'strɔːb(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/'strɔbɛri/`

# 词义

`a soft red juicy fruit with small seeds on its surface, or the plant that grows this fruit`

# 短语

- `strawberry juice` 草莓汁，草莓果茶
- `strawberry jam` 草莓酱，草莓果酱
- `strawberry ice` 草莓冰
- `strawberry ice cream` 草莓冰激凌
- `wild strawberry` 野草莓；泡草莓

# 例句

Do you have strawberry?
你们有卖草莓吗?

Then he drank strawberry juice through a straw.
然后他用吸管喝了一些草莓果汁。

We have three flavors of ice-cream,vanilla chocolate and strawberry.
我们有三种口味的冰欺凌，香草，巧克力和草莓。
