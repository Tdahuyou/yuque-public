# 音标

- 英 `/'wɔːd(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'wɔrdn/`

# 词义

n. 管理员，监护人
`a person who is responsible for a particular place and whose job is to make sure its rules are obeyed`

# 同义

n. 区长；看守人；典狱官；学监
`keeper` `watchman`

# 同根

- n. `warder` 看守；守卫；典狱官
- n. `wardenship` 看守人的职权
- n. `wardress` 女典狱官

# 短语

- `traffic warden` n. 交通管理员
