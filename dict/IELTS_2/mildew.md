# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪldjuː/`
- 美 `/'mɪldu/`

# 词义

n&v. 霉，发霉
`a white or grey substance that grows on walls or other surfaces in wet, slightly warm conditions`

# 同义

n. 霉；[植保]霉病
`mould`

vt. 使发霉
`mold`

vi. 发霉；生霉
`mould` `go moldy`

# 短语

- `powdery mildew` 白粉病；白粉菌
- `downy mildew` 霜霉病；霜霉菌；大豆霜霉病；露菌病

# 例句

The room smelled of mildew.
该房间有股霉味。
