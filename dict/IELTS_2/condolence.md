# 音标

- 英 `/kən'dəʊl(ə)ns/`
- 美 `/kən'doləns/`

# 词义

n. 吊唁，慰问；吊慰
`sympathy for someone who has had something bad happen to them, especially when someone has died`

# 同义

n. 哀悼；慰问
`sympathy` `consolation`

# 同根

- adj. `condolent` 哀悼的；吊慰的
- vi. `condole` 慰问；吊唁；哀悼

# 例句

a letter of condolence
吊唁信
