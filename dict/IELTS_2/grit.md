# 音标

- 英 `/grɪt/`
- 美 `/ɡrɪt/`

# 词义

n. 粗砂；刚毅，勇气
`determination and courage`

v. 铺沙砾于
`to scatter grit on a frozen road to make it less slippery`

# 同义

vt. 研磨；在…上铺砂砾
`pulverize` `mull`

n. [岩][地质]粗砂，砂砾
`coarse sand` `arenes`

# 同根

- adj. `gritty` 坚韧不拔的；有砂砾的；多沙的；像砂的

# 短语

- `grit chamber` 沉砂池
- `steel grit` 钢砂；钢丸；钢粒

# 例句

If they gave gold medals for grit, Karen would be right up there on the winners' podium.
如果他们颁发毅力金牌，卡伦应该站在领奖台上。
