# 音标

- 英 `/'lɪtə/`
- 美 `/'lɪtɚ/`

# 词义

n. 杂乱物；一胎生下的小动物
`a group of baby animals that a mother gives birth to at the same time`

v. 使杂乱,乱丢
`if things litter an area, there are a lot of them in that place, scattered in an untidy way`

n. "




把little的“l”乱丢， 错拿成“r” → 乱扔

# 同义

n. 垃圾；轿，担架；一窝（动物的幼崽）；凌乱
`refuse` `garbage` `junk` `rubbish` `trash`

vi. 产仔；乱扔废弃物
`give birth`

# 同根

- n. `litterbug` 在街道或公共场所乱丢垃圾的人
