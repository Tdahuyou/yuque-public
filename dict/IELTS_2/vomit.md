# 音标

- 英 `/'vɒmɪt/`
- 美 `/'vɑmɪt/`

# 词义

vt&vi. 呕吐；大量喷出
`to bring food or drink up from your stomach out through your mouth, because you are ill`



v + o(形似嘴) + mit(词根mit指“发送”) → 张嘴发送 → 呕吐

# 同义

vt. 吐出；使……呕吐
`spit out` `regorge`

vi. [医]呕吐；吐出
`shoot the cat` `hork`

n. [医]呕吐；呕吐物；催吐剂
`sickness` `emesis`

# 同根

- adj. `vomitive` 呕吐的，使呕吐的
- adj. `vomitory` 呕吐的；使呕吐的
- n. `vomiter` 呕吐的人
- n. `vomitive` 催吐剂
- n. `vomitory` 催吐剂；出入口

# 例句

He had swallowed so much sea water he wanted to vomit.
他呛了这么多海水，所以想吐。

I knew I was really in trouble when I began vomiting blood.
我开始吐血的时候才知道我的麻烦大了。
