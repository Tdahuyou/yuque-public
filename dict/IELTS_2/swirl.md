# 音标

- 英 `/swɜːl/`
- 美 `/swɝl/`

# 词义

vt&vi. 打旋，旋动
`to move around quickly in a twisting circular movement, or to make something do this`

# 同义

n. 漩涡；打旋；涡状形
`vortex` `gulf`

vi. 盘绕；打旋；眩晕；大口喝酒
`swim`

# 短语

- `swirl chamber` 涡流室，涡流式燃烧室；涡良烧室

# 例句

The river had become a swirling torrent.
河水变成了翻滚的洪流。
