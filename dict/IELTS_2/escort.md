# 音标

- 英 `/'eskɔːt/`
- 美 `/ˈeskɔːrt/`

# 词义

n. 护卫者；护航舰
`a person or a group of people or vehicles that go with someone in order to protect or guard them`

v. 护送，护卫
`to take someone somewhere, especially when you are protecting or guarding them`

# 同义

n. 陪同；护航舰；护卫队；护送者
`guard`

vt. 护送；陪同；为…护航
`accompany vt.`

# 例句

a police escort 
警察护卫队
