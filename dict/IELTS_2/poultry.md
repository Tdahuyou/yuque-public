# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊltrɪ/`
- 美 `/'poltri/`

# 词义

n. 家禽；家禽肉
`birds such as chickens and ducks that are kept on farms in order to produce eggs and meat`



poul(小鸡) + try → 家禽； 禽肉

# 同义

n. [禽]家禽
`fowl`

# 同根

- n. `poulterer` 鸟贩；家禽贩

# 短语

- `poultry farm` n. 家禽饲养所
- `poultry feed` 家禽饲料
- `poultry and eggs` 禽蛋类
- `poultry farming` 家禽饲养

# 例句

...a poultry farm.
…一个家禽饲养场。
