# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'poʃən/`

# 词义

n. (药等的)一服；一剂
`A potion is a drink that contains medicine, poison, or something that is supposed to have magic powers`

# 同义

n. 一剂；一服；饮剂
`dose` `potus`

# 例句

...a magic potion that will make Siegfried forget Brunnhilde and fall in love with Gutrune.
…一种会使齐格弗里德忘掉布伦希德尔德而爱上古特鲁内娜的魔剂。
