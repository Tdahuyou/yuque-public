# 音标

- 英 `/ɒ'stensɪb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/ɑ'stɛnsəbl/`

# 词义

adj. (指理由)表面的；假装的
`seeming to be the reason for or the purpose of something, but usually hiding the real reason or purpose`

# 同义

adj. 表面的；假装的
`external` `surface` `affected` `assumed` `facial`

# 同根

- adv. `ostensibly` 表面上；外表

# 例句

The ostensible purpose of these meetings was to gather information on financial strategies.
这些会议声称旨在收集金融策略方面的信息。
