# 音标

- 英 `/geɪp/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

v. 张口，张开，裂开
`If you say that something such as a hole or a wound gapes, you are emphasizing that it is big or wide`

n. 目瞪口呆


# 同义

vi. 裂开，张开；[生理]打呵欠
`gap` `expand`

n. 裂口，张嘴；[生理]呵欠
`breakage` `chasm`

# 同根

- adj. `gapy` 患张口病的

# 例句

The front door was missing. A hole gaped in the roof.
前门没有了，屋顶裂开一个洞。
