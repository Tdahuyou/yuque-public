# 音标

- 英 `/'fræntɪk/`
- 美 `/'fræntɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 激动得发狂似的；狂暴的
`If you are frantic, you are behaving in a wild and uncontrolled way because you are frightened or worried`

# 同义

adj. 狂乱的，疯狂的
`crazy` `possessed` `mad`

# 同根

- adv. `frantically` 疯狂似地；狂暴地

# 例句

A bird had been locked in and was by now quite frantic.
一只鸟被关了起来，到现在它都非常狂躁。
