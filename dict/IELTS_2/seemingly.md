# 音标

- 英 `/'siːmɪŋlɪ/`
- 美 `/'simɪŋli/`

# 词义

adv. 好像；似乎
`If something is seemingly the case, you mean that it appears to be the case, even though it may not really be so`

# 同根

- adj. `seeming` 表面上的
- n. `seeming` 外观
- vi. `seem` 似乎；像是；装作

# 例句

A seemingly endless line of trucks waits in vain to load up.
看上去没有尽头的一排卡车徒劳地等着装货。
