# 音标

- 英 `/'trɪk(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'trɪkl/`

# 词义

v&n. (使)滴流，(使)细流
`if liquid trickles somewhere, it flows slowly in drops or in a thin stream`

# 同义

vi. 滴；细细地流；慢慢地移动
`creep`

n. 滴，淌；细流
`drop` `gutta`

# 短语

- `trickle down` 向下滴流
- `trickle irrigation` 滴灌；滴灌系统

# 例句

The water in the stream had been reduced to a trickle.
小溪里的水已只剩下一股细流。
