# 音标

- 英 `/frʌ'streɪt; 'frʌs-/`
- 美 `/'frʌstret/`

# 词义

v. 挫败；阻挠
`to prevent someone’s plans, efforts, or attempts from succeeding`

# 同义

vt. 挫败；阻挠；使感到灰心
`defeat` `discomfit`

vi. 失败；受挫
`founder` `come to naught`

adj. 挫败的；无益的
`thwarted` `unprofitable`

# 同根

- adj. `frustrated` 失意的，挫败的；泄气的
- adj. `frustrating` 令人沮丧的
- n. `frustration` 挫折
- v. `frustrated` 挫败；阻挠（frustrate的过去式和过去分词形式）
- v. `frustrating` 使沮丧（frustrate的ing形式）

# 例句

Their attempts to speak to him were frustrated by the guards.
他们想跟他说话，但被警卫拦住了。
