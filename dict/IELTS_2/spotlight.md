# 音标

- 英 `/'spɒtlaɪt/`
- 美 `/'spɑtlaɪt/`

# 词义

n. 聚光灯
`a light with a very bright beam which can be directed at someone or something. Spotlights are often used to light a stage when actors or singers are performing`

v. 聚光照明，使显著
`to shine a strong beam of light on something`

# 同义

n. [电]聚光灯；反光灯；公众注意的中心
`photospot` `revelation light`

# 短语

- `in the spotlight` 处于公众注意的中心；出风头

# 例句

The yard was lit by three huge spotlights.
院子里亮着三盏巨大的聚光灯。
