# 音标

- 英 `/'weɪdʒə/`
- 美 `/'weidʒə/`

# 词义

n. 赌注
`an agreement in which you win or lose money according to the result of something such as a race`

v. 押注，打赌
`to agree to win or lose an amount of money on the result of something such as a race`

# 同义

n. 赌注；赌博；赌物
`stake` `bet`

vi. 打赌
`lay` `stake` `make a bet`

vt. 下赌注
`back` `ante`

# 例句

There have been various wagers on certain candidates since the senator announced his retirement.
自从那位参议员宣布引退之后，对某些候选人的打赌猜测纷纷出现。
