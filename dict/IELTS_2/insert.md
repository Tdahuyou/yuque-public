# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'sɜːt/`
- 美 `/'ɪnsɝt/`

# 词义

v. 插入
`to put something inside or into something else`

n. 插入物，插页
`printed pages that are put inside a newspaper or magazine in order to advertise something`



in(进入) + sert(放置) → 放进去 → 插入， 嵌入

# 同义

vt. 插入；[建]嵌入
`bed` `build in`

n. [建]插入物
`inlet` `impaler`

# 同根

- n. `insertion` 插入；嵌入；插入物

# 短语

- `insert into` 插入；写入
- `insert mode` 插入模式
- `metal insert` 金属嵌入物
- `valve seat insert` 阀座嵌环；气门座镶圈

# 例句

He took a small key from his pocket and slowly inserted it into the lock.
他从衣袋里掏出一把小钥匙，然后缓缓地将其插入锁孔里。
