# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'stend/`
- 美 `/dɪ'stɛnd/`

# 词义

v. (使)扩张；膨胀
`to swell or make something swell because of pressure from inside`

# 同义

vi. 扩张；膨胀
`fill` `swell with`

# 同根

- adj. `distensible` 扩大的；可扩张的；胀的
- n. `distension` 膨胀，扩张
- n. `distention` 膨胀，扩张

# 例句

Through this incision, the abdominal cavity is distended with carbon dioxide gas.
通过此切口，二氧化碳气体把胸腔充得肿胀起来。
