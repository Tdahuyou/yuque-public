# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'tɪmɪdeɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪn'tɪmɪdet/`

# 词义

v. 恫吓，威胁
`to frighten or threaten someone into making them do what you want`

# 同义

vt. 恐吓，威胁；胁迫
`cow` `to menace`

# 同根

- adj. `intimidating` 吓人的
- adj. `intimidated` 害怕的；受到恐吓的
- n. `intimidation` 恐吓，威胁
- v. `intimidated` 威胁，恐吓（intimidate的过去式）

# 例句

Attempts to intimidate her failed.
恐吓她的企图失败了。
