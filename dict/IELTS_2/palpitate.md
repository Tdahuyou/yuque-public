# 音标

- 英 `/'pælpɪteɪt/`
- 美 `/'pælpə'tet/`

# 词义

v. 心脏急速跳动；人擅抖
`If someone's heart palpitates, it beats very fast in an irregular way, because they are frightened or anxious`

# 同义

vi. 发抖；（心脏）悸动；跳动
`pulse` `shake`

# 同根

- adj. `palpitant` 颤抖的；悸动的
- n. `palpitation` [内科] 心悸；跳动；颤动

# 例句

He felt suddenly faint, and his heart began to palpitate.
他突然感到晕厥，心脏也开始悸动。
