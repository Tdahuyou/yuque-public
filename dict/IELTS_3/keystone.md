# 音标

- 英 `/'kiːstəun/`
- 美 `/'ki'ston/`

# 词义

n. 拱顶石；基础，主旨
`the large central stone in an arch that keeps the other stones in position`

# 同义

n. 主旨，基本原则；[建]楔石
`substance` `ultimate` `gist`

# 例句

The government's determination to beat inflation has so far been the keystone of its economic policy.
政府对通货膨胀的坚决抑制迄今为止一直是其经济政策的根本。
