# 音标

- 英 `/ɪg'zɒtɪk/`
- 美 `/ɪɡ'zɑtɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 外来的；奇异的；醒目的，吸引人的
`Something that is exotic is unusual and interesting, usually because it comes from or is related to a distant country`

# 同义

adj. [物]异国的；[语]外来的；异国情调的
`outside` `extraneous`

# 同根

- n. `exoticism` 异国情调；异国风味；洋派

# 短语

- `exotic plant` 外来植物；引入的植物
- `exotic dancer` 脱衣舞女

# 例句

...brilliantly coloured, exotic flowers.
…色彩绚丽的奇异花卉。
