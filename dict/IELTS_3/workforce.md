# 音标

- 英 `/'wɜːkfɔːs/`
- 美 `/'wɝkfɔrs/`

# 词义

n. 受雇的或现有的工作人员总数；劳动人口
`The workforce is the total number of people in a country or region who are physically able to do a job and are available for work`



work(工作) + force(队伍) → 劳动人口

# 同义

n. 劳动力；工人总数，职工总数
`manpower` `labour force`

# 例句

...a country where half the workforce is unemployed.
…一个半数劳动力无业的国家。
