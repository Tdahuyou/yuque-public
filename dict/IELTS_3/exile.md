# 音标

- 英 `/'eksaɪl;'egzaɪl/`
- 美 `/'ɛksaɪl/`

# 词义

vt. 放逐，流放，
`to force someone to leave their country, especially for political reasons`



ex(出) + il(=sil跳跃) + e → 跳出去 → 放逐， 流放

# 同义

n. 流放，充军；放逐，被放逐者；流犯
`transportation` `banishment`

vt. 放逐，流放；使背井离乡
`transport` `banish`

# 短语

- `exile from` 使流亡；把…从…流放出去
- `go into exile` 流亡

# 例句

the exiled former president
流亡国外的前总统
