# 音标

- 英 `/'hɜːdzmən/`
- 美 `/'hɝdzmən/`

# 词义

n. 牧人
`a man who looks after a herd of animals`



herds(畜群) + man(人) → 牧人

# 同义

n. 牧人
`wrangler` `vaquero`

# 同根

- n. `herder` 牧人；（美俚）监狱看守

# 例句

You don't need a humanistic education in order to become a herdsman .
要成为一个牧人，你不需要接受人文主义的教育。

The herdsman drove afield battening his flocks with the fresh dews of night.
牧人把羊群赶到野外，用夜晚的新鲜露水催肥。

The west side of every wood and rising ground gleamed like a boundary of Elysium, the sun on our backs seemed like a gentle herdsman driving us home at evening.
每处林木的西边和凸起的地面流光溢彩，宛如乐园的边际，夕阳在背好似和蔼的牧人晚间赶车送我们回家。
