# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒɜːmɪneɪt/`
- 美 `/'dʒɝmɪnet/`

# 词义

v. 发芽，生长；发展
`if a seed germinates, or if it is germinated, it begins to grow`



germ(芽) + in + ate(使…)→  (使)发芽

# 同义

vt. 使发芽；使生长
`grow` `bud`

vi. 发芽；生长
`develop` `put`

# 同根

- n. `germination` 发芽；发生；伟晶作用

# 例句

The idea of setting up his own company began to germinate in his mind.
他萌生了自己开公司的想法。
