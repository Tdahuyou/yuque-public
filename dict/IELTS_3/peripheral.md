# 音标

- 英 `/pə'rɪfərəl/`
- 美 `/pə'rɪfərəl/`

# 词义

adj. 外围的；次要的，附带的
`not as important as other things or people in a particular activity, idea, or situation`

n. 外围设备
`a piece of equipment that is connected to a computer and used with it, for example a  printer `



peri(周围) + pher(带来) + al → 外围的

# 同义

adj. [昆][计]外围的；次要的
`secondary` `supporting` `minor`

# 同根

- adv. `peripherally` 周边地；外围地；次要地
- n. `periphery` 外围，边缘；圆周；圆柱体表面

# 短语

- `peripheral blood` 周边血液
- `peripheral nerve` 末梢神经，外周神经
- `peripheral equipment` 外部设备，外围设备
- `peripheral device` 外围设备
- `peripheral interface` [计]外围接口
- `peripheral nervous system` [解]周围神经系统
- `peripheral circuit` 外围电路；周边集成电路
- `peripheral vascular disease` 周围性血管疾病
- `peripheral vision` 周边视觉；间接视力
- `peripheral region` 周围区域
- `peripheral circulation` 外周环流；末梢循环
- `peripheral resistance` 外周阻力；末梢阻力
- `peripheral milling` 圆周铣削；周缘铣

# 例句

a diplomat who had a peripheral role in the negotiations
在谈判中发挥辅助作用的外交官

Her involvement in the case was peripheral.
她在这宗案件中是从犯。
