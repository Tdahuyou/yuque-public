# 音标

- 英 `/'gɒglz/`
- 美 `/'gɑglz/`

# 词义

n. 护目镜；风镜；游泳镜
`a pair of  glasses  made of glass or plastic with a rubber or plastic edge that fit against your skin and protect your eyes`

# 同义

n. [安全]护目镜；防护眼镜；瞪视（goggle的复数）
`blinkers`

# 短语

- `safety goggles` 防护眼镜

# 例句

And protect your eyes from snow and ice glare with dark glasses or goggles.
使用深色眼镜或者护目镜来保护你的眼镜不受冰雪眩光的影响。
