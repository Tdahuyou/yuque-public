# 音标

- 英 `/gə'rɪlə/`
- 美 `/ɡə'rɪlə/`

# 词义

n. 大猩猩
`a very large African monkey that is the largest of the  apes `

# 例句

It is this "humanness" of the gorilla which is so beguiling.
就是大猩猩的这种“人的特性”使人感到如此有趣。

He offers the mime a job to dress up as the gorilla until they can get another one.
他主动提供一个工作给这个哑剧演员，装扮成一只大猩猩直到他们能找到另一只真的。

I knew something about the gorilla sanctuary among the volcanoes, but had never planned to go there.
我知道一些在火山一带的大猩猩保护区的情况，但从未打算到那儿去。
