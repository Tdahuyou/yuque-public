# 音标

- 英 `/ˌmɪskən'sepʃn/`
- 美 `/'mɪskən'sɛpʃən/`

# 词义

n. 误解，错误想法
`an idea which is wrong or untrue, but which people believe because they do not understand the subject properly`

# 同义

n. 误解；错觉；错误想法
`misunderstanding` `misinterpretation`

# 例句

It is a misconception that Peggy was fabulously wealthy.
说佩吉以前极其富有，这是一个错误的看法。
