# 音标

- 英 `/ə'pærəntli/`
- 美 `/ə'pærəntli/`

# 词义

adv. 显然；看来，似乎
`according to the way someone looks or a situation appears, although you cannot be sure`

# 同义

adv. 显然地；似乎，表面上
`transparently` `manifestly`

# 同根

- adj. `apparent` 显然的；表面上的

# 例句

She turned to face him, her anger apparently gone.
她转过身面对着他，好像怒气已经消退了。
