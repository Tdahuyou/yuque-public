# 音标

- 英 `/bruːm/`
- 美 `/brum/`

# 词义

n. 扫帚
`a large brush with a long handle, used for sweeping floors`

# 同义

n. 扫帚；[纺]金雀花
`genista` `gorse`

vt. 扫除
`clean off` `sweep out`

# 短语

- `new broom` 新上任者；新晋升者
- `broom corn millet` 黍， 稷

# 例句

Deer brought the broom.
小鹿带来了扫帚。

The lobby is empty but for the postman and his broom.
大厅里除了邮差和他的扫帚以外别无他人。
