# 音标

- 英 `/'kæml/`
- 美 `/'kæml/`

# 词义

n. 骆驼
`a large desert animal with a long neck and either one or two  hump s (= large raised parts )  on its back`



came(来) + l → 把骆驼牵过来 → 骆驼

# 同义

n. [畜牧][脊椎]骆驼；打捞浮筒；工作作风官僚
`oont`

# 短语

- `camel hair` 驼绒；骆驼毛
- `bactrian camel` 双峰驼
