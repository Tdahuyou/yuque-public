# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'frɪndʒ/`
- 美 `/ɪn'frɪndʒ/`

# 词义

v. 侵犯，侵害；违背，触犯；干涉，干扰
`If someone infringes a law or a rule, they break it or do something that disobeys it`

# 同义

vt. 侵犯；违反；破坏
`destroy` `encroach on`

vi. 侵犯；侵害
`encroach on` `to violate`

# 同根

- n. `infringement` 侵犯；违反

# 短语

- `infringe upon` 侵犯；侵害
- `infringe on` 侵犯；侵害；破坏

# 例句

The film exploited his image and infringed his copyright.
该影片利用了他的肖像，侵犯了他的肖像权。
