# 音标

- 英 `/'miːgə(r)/`
- 美 `/'mi:gə/`

# 词义

adj. 少量的，粗劣的
`If you describe an amount or quantity of something as meagre, you are critical of it because it is very small or not enough`

# 同义

adj. 瘦的；贫弱的；贫乏的
`poor` `thin` `spare` `lean`

# 例句

The rations that they gave us were meagre and inadequate.
他们给我们的配给量是很少的，是不够的。
