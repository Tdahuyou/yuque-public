# 音标

- 英 `/'stɒkpaɪl/`
- 美 `/'stɑkpaɪl/`

# 词义

n. 〔物资的大量〕储备
`a large supply of things that is kept ready for use in the future`

v. 贮存，积聚〔物资、武器等〕
`to keep adding to a supply of goods, weapons etc that you are keeping ready to use if you need them in the future`



stock(储备) + pile(堆) → 成堆地储备 → 大量贮备

# 同义

n. 库存；积蓄
`amassment` `lay away`

vt. 贮存；储蓄
`squirrel away` `save up`

vi. 积累；储备物资
`pile up` `get together`

# 同根

- n. `stockpiling` 囤积；贮存
- v. `stockpiling` 储备（stockpile的现在分词）

# 例句

The two leaders also approved treaties to cut stockpiles of chemical weapons.
两位领导人也都赞同削减化学武器储备的条约。
