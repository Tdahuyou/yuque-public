# 音标

- 英 `/stɪf/`
- 美 `/stɪf/`

# 词义

adv. 极其，非常
`extremely bored, frightened, or worried`

adj. 硬的，僵硬的
`if someone or a part of their body is stiff, their muscles hurt and it is difficult for them to move`



可与still(a. 静止的)一起记

# 同义

adj. 呆板的；坚硬的；严厉的；拘谨的；拘谨的
`mechanical` `severe` `wooden` `rigid` `dead`

adv. 僵硬地；彻底地
`thoroughly` `entirely` `completely` `fully` `tight`

n. 死尸；令人讨厌者；[金融]流通票据；劳动者
`worker` `dead body`

# 同根

- n. `stiffness` 僵硬；坚硬；不自然；顽固
- vi. `stiffen` 变硬；变猛烈；变粘
- vt. `stiffen` 使变硬；使粘稠

# 短语

- `stiff with` [俚语]…供应充足的
- `stiff neck` 脖子发僵，斜颈；顽固之人
- `stiff upper lip` 坚定沉着；不露感情
- `stiff resistance` 顽强抵抗
- `stiff collar` 硬领

# 例句

As a child, I was scared stiff of going down to the cellar.
小时候，我非常害怕到地下室去。
