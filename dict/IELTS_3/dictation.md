# 音标

- 英 `/dɪk'teɪʃn/`
- 美 `/dɪk'teʃən/`

# 词义

n. 听写
`a piece of writing that a teacher reads out to test your ability to hear and write the words correctly`

# 同义

n. 听写；口述；命令
`instruction` `bidding` `order` `word` `charge`

# 同根

- n. `dictate` 命令；指示
- n. `dictator` 独裁者；命令者
- vi. `dictate` 口述；听写
- vt. `dictate` 命令；口述；使听写

# 例句

I hate doing French dictations .
我讨厌做法语听写。
