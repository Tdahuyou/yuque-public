# 音标

- 英 `/grʌdʒ/`
- 美 `/ɡrʌdʒ/`

# 词义

n. 不满，积怨，
`If you have or bear a grudge against someone, you have unfriendly feelings toward them because of something they did in the past`

# 同义

vt. 怀恨；吝惜；妒忌；不情愿做
`spare` `begrudge`

n. 怨恨；恶意；妒忌
`spite` `hatred`

# 同根

- adj. `grudging` 勉强的；吝啬的

# 例句

He appears to have a grudge against certain players.
他好像对某些选手有积怨。
