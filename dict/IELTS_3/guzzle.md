# 音标

- 英 `/'gʌzl/`
- 美 `/'ɡʌzl/`

# 词义

v. 狂饮，暴饮；大量消耗
`to eat or drink a lot of something, eagerly and quickly – usually showing disapproval`

# 同义

vt. 狂饮；暴食
`get a bag on`

vi. 狂饮；暴食；狼吞虎咽
`get a bag on` `gobble up`

n. 狂饮；豪饮作乐
`bowse`

# 例句

They’ve been guzzling beer all evening.
他们整个晚上都在狂饮啤酒。
