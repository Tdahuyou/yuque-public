# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdiːpɑːt'mentl/`
- 美 `/,dipɑrt'mɛntl/`

# 词义

adj. 部门的
`Departmental is used to describe the activities, responsibilities, or possessions of a department in a government, company, or other organization`

# 同义

adj. 部门的；各县的；分科的
`sectoral`

# 同根

- n. `department` 部；部门；系；科；局

# 短语

- `departmental budget` 分部预算；分部门预算

# 例句

The Secretary of Education is right to seek a bigger departmental budget.
教育部长寻求更多的部门预算是对的。
