# 音标

- 英 `/ˌrɪː'sɪt/`
- 美 `/ˈriːsɪt/`

# 词义

v&n. 重修，补考
`to take an examination again, because you failed it or did not do well enough`

# 同义

vt. （英）重考；补考
`reexamine`

n. 补考；重考
`make-up examination` `supplementary examination`

# 例句

Those who fail can resit the exam in 60 days.
考试不及格的人可以在60天后重考。
