# 音标

- 英 `/'hæmstə(r)/`
- 美 `/'hæmstɚ/`

# 词义

n. 仓鼠
`a small animal that looks like a mouse with no tail`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]仓鼠；仓鼠毛皮
`cricetid`

# 例句

Without Leaders, we would be spinning in a hamster wheel with no real vision.
没有领导型的人，我们就会像仓鼠在轮子里旋转那样，没有远见。

In laboratory experiments, for instance, cancer cells have been transferred by mosquitoes from one hamster to another.
比如，在实验室里，癌细胞通过蚊子从一只仓鼠转移到另一只身上。
