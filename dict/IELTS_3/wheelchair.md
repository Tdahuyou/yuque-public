# 音标

- 英 `/'wiːltʃeə(r)/`
- 美 `/'wiltʃɛr/`

# 词义

n. 轮椅
`a chair with wheels, used by people who cannot walk`



wheel(车轮) + chair(椅子) → 带轮子的椅子 → 轮椅

# 例句

Lynn has been confined to a wheelchair (= has had to use a wheelchair ) for the last year.
过去一年中林恩都被困在轮椅上。

special parking for wheelchair users
坐轮椅人士的专用停车位
