# 音标

- 英 `/'swɪvl/`
- 美 `/'swɪvl/`

# 词义

v. 旋转
`to turn around quickly and face a different direction, or to make something do this`

n. 转环，转节


# 同义

vt. [机]使旋转
`turn` `spin` `swing`

vi. [机]旋转
`wheel` `circle` `spin` `go round and round`

# 短语

- `swivel chair` n. 转椅
- `swivel joint` [机]回转接头；旋转管接头

# 例句

Anna swivelled round to face him.
安娜转过身来面对着他。

She swivelled her head round to watch what was happening.
她转过头来察看发生了什么事。

Danny swiveled his chair away from me.
丹尼把椅子从我身边转开了。
