# 音标

- 英 `/vet/`
- 美 `/vɛt/`

# 词义

vt. 审查，仔细检查
`to check someone’s past activities, relationships etc in order to make sure that person is suitable for a particular job, especially an important one`

n. 兽医
`someone who is trained to give medical care and treatment to sick animals`

# 同义

vt. 审查；诊疗
`oversee` `censor`

n. 兽医
`veterinarian` `farrier`

# 同根

- adj. `veterinary` 兽医的
- n. `veterinarian` [兽医] 兽医
- n. `veterinary` 兽医（等于veterinarian）

# 例句

All candidates are carefully vetted by Central Office.
所有候选人都由总部仔细审查。
