# 音标

- 英 `/ə'laɪt/`
- 美 `/ə'laɪt/`

# 词义

adj. 燃烧的
`burning`

v. 〔鸟或虫等〕飞落
`if a bird or insect alights on something, it stops flying and stands on it`



a + light(点着) → 点着的

# 同义

vi. 下来；飞落
`come down` `get down`

# 短语

- `set alight` 使燃烧，使着火

# 例句

The car was set alight and pushed over a hill.
那辆汽车被点燃后就被推下山去。
