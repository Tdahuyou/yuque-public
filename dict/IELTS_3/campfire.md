# 音标

- 英 `/'kæmpfaɪə(r)/`
- 美 `/'kæmpfaɪɚ/`

# 词义

n. 营火
`a fire made outdoors by people who are camping`



camp(露营， 宿营) + fire(火) → 营火

# 同义

n. 营火；营火会
`bonfire`

# 例句

We are sitting by the campfire.
我们正坐在营火旁。

The black soot deposited on a kettle by a smoky campfire is nearly pure carbon.
带烟的营火在水壶上熏积的黑煤烟几乎是纯碳。

A group of elderly men in uniforms, eyes bright with reminiscence, are gathered at a Grand Army of the Republic campfire.
一群上年纪穿着制服的退伍老兵，围在伟大共和国军的营火旁边，眼神明亮地回忆着过去的光辉岁月。
