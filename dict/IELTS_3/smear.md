# 音标

- 英 `/smɪə(r)/`
- 美 `/smɪr/`

# 词义

v. 胡乱涂抹；弄脏，弄污；诽谤，诋毁；变得模糊不清，
`to tell an untrue story about someone important in order to make people lose respect for them – used especially in newspapers`



遭到诽谤(smear)， 发誓(swear)报复

# 同义

vt. 诽谤；弄脏；涂上；把…擦模糊
`soil` `mud` `spot` `wrong` `dirty`

n. [生物]涂片；诽谤；污点
`scandal` `personalities`

vi. 被弄脏
`smudge`

# 短语

- `pap smear` 子宫颈抹片检查
- `blood smear` 血涂片；血液涂片

# 例句

an attempt to smear the party leadership
污蔑党领导的 企图
