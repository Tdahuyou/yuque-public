# 音标

- 英 `/'pɑːθweɪ/`
- 美 `/'pæθwe/`

# 词义

n. 路径，途径
`A pathway is a path that you can walk along or a route that you can take`



path(小路) + way(道路) → 路径， 途径

# 同义

n. 路，道；途径，路径
`mechanism` `tao` `approach` `pass` `routes`

# 短语

- `metabolic pathway` 代谢途径

# 例句

Richard was coming up the pathway.
理查德正沿路走来。
