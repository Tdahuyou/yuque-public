# 音标

- 英 `/frɔːt/`
- 美 `/frɔt/`

# 词义

adj. 充满…的；担心的，烦恼的
`If a situation or action is fraught with problems or risks, it is filled with them`



可能来自freight(n. 货物)

# 同义

adj. 担心的，忧虑的；充满…的
`worried` `afraid` `packed`

# 例句

The earliest operations employing this technique were fraught with dangers.
最早采用这一技术的手术充满了危险。
