# 音标

- 英 `/tæb/`
- 美 `/tæb/`

# 词义

n. 标签
`a small piece of paper, cloth, plastic etc that sticks out from the edge of something, so that you can find it more easily`

# 同义

n. 标签；制表；帐单
`ticket` `label` `tag` `prepared by`

# 同根

- adj. `table` 桌子的
- n. `table` 桌子；表格；平地层
- n. `tabbing` 跨栏
- v. `tabbing` 列表；指定（tab的ing形式）
- vt. `table` 制表；搁置；嵌合

# 短语

- `pick up the tab` 承担费用
- `tab key` 跳格键；制表键

# 例句

an index tab labeled ‘Expenses’
标着“支出”字样的检索标签
