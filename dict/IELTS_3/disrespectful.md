# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdɪsrɪ'spektfl/`
- 美 `/disri,spektful/`

# 词义

adj. 失礼的，无礼的
`If you are disrespectful, you show no respect in the way that you speak or behave to someone`

# 同义

adj. 无礼的；失礼的；不尊敬的
`rude` `offensive`

# 同根

- adv. `disrespectfully` 无礼地；不恭地
- n. `disrespect` 无礼，失礼，不敬

# 例句

...accusations that he had been disrespectful to a police officer.
...关于他曾对一名警官无礼的控告。
