# 音标

- 英 `/'hɪkʌp/`
- 美 `/'hɪkəp/`

# 词义

vi. 打嗝
`to have hiccups`

n. 嗝，呃逆；暂时困难
`a sudden repeated stopping of the breath, usually caused by eating or drinking too fast`

# 同义

n. [医]打嗝
`gurk` `eructation`

vi. [医]打嗝
`eruct` `belch`

# 例句

She was still hiccuping from the egg she had swallowed whole.
她还在因为先前整个吞下鸡蛋而打嗝儿。
