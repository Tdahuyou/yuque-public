# 音标

- 英 `/ɪk'skɜːʃn/`
- 美 `/ɪk'skɝʒn/`

# 词义

n. 远足，短途旅游；【物】偏移，漂移
`a short journey arranged so that a group of people can visit a place, especially while they are on holiday`

# 同义

n. 偏移；远足；短程旅行；离题；游览圆
`skewing` `short trip`

# 短语

- `temperature excursion` 温度剧增，超温

# 例句

a shopping excursion
购物游
