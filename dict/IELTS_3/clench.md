# 音标

- 英 `/klentʃ/`
- 美 `/klɛntʃ/`

# 词义

v. 握紧；咬紧；牢牢抓住
`to hold your hands, teeth etc together tightly, usually because you feel angry or determined`

# 同义

vt. 紧握；确定；把…敲弯
`confirm` `identify`

vi. 握紧；钉牢
`hammer on` `nail up`

# 例句

Jody was pacing the sidelines, her fists clenched.
乔迪在球场的边线上紧握着拳头来回踱步。
