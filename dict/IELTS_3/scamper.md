# 音标

- 英 `/'skæmpə(r)/`
- 美 `/'skæmpɚ/`

# 词义

vi. 奔跑，快跑
`When people or small animals scamper somewhere, they move there quickly with small, light steps`



s(音似： 嘶) + camper(露营者) → 露营者嘶喊着快跑 → 快跑

# 同义

vi. 蹦蹦跳跳；奔跑，惊惶奔跑
`run` `bounce about`

n. 蹦跳；奔跑
`run` `crowhop`

# 例句

Children scampered off the yellow school bus and into the playground.
孩子们跑下黄颜色的校车，奔跑进了操场。
