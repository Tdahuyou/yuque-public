# 音标

- 英 `/ˌfɔːθ'kʌmɪŋ/`
- 美 `/,fɔrθ'kʌmɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 即将到来的
`a forthcoming event, meeting etc is one that has been planned to happen soon`



forth(往前) + coming(就要来的) → 即将到来的

# 同义

adj. 即将来临的
`upcoming` `imminent`

n. 来临
`oncoming`

# 例句

the forthcoming elections
即将到来的选举

Keep an eye on the noticeboards for forthcoming events .
请注意布告牌上的活动预告。
