# 音标

- 英 `/ˌriːkrɪ'eɪt/`
- 美 `/,rikrɪ'et/`

# 词义

vt. 再现，再创造
`to make something from the past exist again in a new form or be experienced again`

# 同义

vi. 娱乐；消遣
`to entertain` `wile away`

# 同根

- adj. `recreational` 娱乐的，消遣的；休养的
- n. `recreation` 娱乐；消遣；休养

# 例句

You can never recreate the feeling of winning for the first time.
你永远无法再次体会第一次获胜时的心情。
