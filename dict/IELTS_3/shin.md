# 音标

- 英 `/ʃɪn/`
- 美 `/ʃɪn/`

# 词义

v. 爬
`to climb quickly up or down a tree, pole etc by using your hands and legs`

n. 胫，胫部，胫骨
`the front part of your leg between your knee and your foot`

# 同义

n. 胫骨
`shankbone` `tibia`

vt. 爬；攀
`climb`

vi. 攀；快步走
`double`

# 同根

- n. `shinny` 简易曲棍球棒
- vi. `shinny` 击球；攀爬

# 例句

He shinned up a tree.
他噌的一下爬到了一棵树上。
