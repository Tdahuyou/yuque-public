# 音标

- 英 `/bɑːdʒ/`
- 美 `/bɑrdʒ/`

# 词义

v. 猛撞；闯
`If you barge into a place or barge through it, you rush or push into it in a rough and rude way`

n. 驳船
`a large low boat with a flat bottom, used for carrying goods on a  canal  or river`



“八只” → 八只驳船 → 驳船

# 同义

vi. 蹒跚；闯入
`titubate` `burst into`

n. [船]驳船；游艇
`lighter` `catamaran`

# 短语

- `barge in` v. 闯入；干涉
- `barge into` 闯入；与…相撞
- `barge pole` n. 驳船撑竿

# 例句

Students tried to barge into the secretariat buildings.
学生们试图闯进秘书处大楼。
