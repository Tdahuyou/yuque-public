# 音标

- 英 `/ˌriːɪn'vɪgəreɪt/`
- 美 `/,riɪn'vɪɡəret/`

# 词义

vt. 使重新振作；使恢复活力
`to put vitality and vigour back into (someone or something) `

# 同义

vt. 使再振作；使复兴
`revive`

# 例句

The good news for committed shoppers is a so-called 'soft break' – a few minutes rest grabbing a coffee, for example - can reinvigorate our senses, although only temporarily.
对于那些热衷购物的人来说，好消息是，稍事休息一下——比如花几分钟喝杯咖啡——可以使我们的感官重新振作，虽然这仅是暂时的。
