# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'pel/`
- 美 `/rɪ'pɛl/`

# 词义

vt. 击退，驱逐；抵制，拒绝；使反感，使厌恶
`if something repels you, it is so unpleasant that you do not want to be near it, or it makes you feel ill`



re(=back向后) + pel(推) → 向后推 → 击退， 驱逐

# 同义

vt. 击退；抵制；使厌恶；使不愉快
`refuse` `roll back`

# 例句

The smell repelled him.
这气味让他反感。
