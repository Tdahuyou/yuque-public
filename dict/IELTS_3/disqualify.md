# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'kwɒlɪfaɪ/`
- 美 `/dɪs'kwɑlə'fai/`

# 词义

vt. 使丧失资格
`When someone is disqualified, they are officially stopped from taking part in a particular event, activity, or competition, usually because they have done something wrong`



dis(不) + qualify(具有资格) → 使不具有资格 → 使丧失资格

# 同根

- adj. `disqualified` 不合格的；被取消资格的
- n. `disqualification` 不合格；取消资格；被罚下场
- v. `disqualified` 使不合格；取消…的资格（disqualify的过去分词）

# 例句

Thomson was disqualified from the 400 metre freestyle.
汤姆森被取消了400米自由泳的参赛资格。
