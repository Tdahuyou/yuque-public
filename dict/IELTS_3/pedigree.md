# 音标

- 英 `/'pedɪgriː/`
- 美 `/'pɛdɪɡri/`

# 词义

n. 家谱；门第；血统
`the parents and other past family members of an animal or person, or an official written record of this`

# 同义

n. [遗]血统；家谱
`birth` `blood` `stem` `stock`

adj. 纯种的
`clean-bred` `pursang`

# 同根

- adj. `pedigreed` 血统明白的；有来历的
- v. `pedigreed` 建立纯种系谱而繁育（pedigree的过去分词）

# 例句

60 percent of dogs and ten percent of cats have pedigrees.
60%的狗和10%的猫都有血统记录。
