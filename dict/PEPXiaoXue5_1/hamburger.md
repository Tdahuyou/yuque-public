# 音标

- 英 `/ˈhæmbɜ:gə/`
- 美 `/hæmbɝ​ɡɚ/`

# 词义

`a flat round piece of finely cut beef(= meat from a cow ) which is cooked and eaten in a bread bun`



发音记忆: 音似“汉堡”

# 同义

n. 汉堡包，汉堡；牛肉饼，肉饼；碎牛肉
`burger` `beefburger`

# 例句

The whole stadium stink of the hamburger.
整个体育场内散发着汉堡包的香味。

So tell me. Why go back to hamburger after eating steak?
所以请告诉我，为什么有烤肉吃了还要回头啃汉堡呢？

'Why go out for hamburger when you have steak at home?' he once remarked.
纽曼曾经说道，“如果家里有了牛排，为什么要出去找汉堡？”
