# 音标

- 英 `/'ræbɪt/`
- 美 `/'ræbɪt/`

# 词义

`a small animal with long ears and soft fur, that lives in a hole in the ground`

# 同义

n. 兔子，野兔
`bunny` `hare`

vt. 让…见鬼去吧
`to hell with`

# 短语

- `rabbit hair` 兔毛
- `rabbit meat` 兔肉
- `rabbit on` 唠叨，喋喋不休
- `peter rabbit` 彼得兔（卡通人物）
- `rabbit food` 生吃的蔬菜
- `angora rabbit` 安哥拉兔
- `rabbit ears` 兔耳（好奇凡事都想知道）；室内天线

# 例句

The hound nips at a rabbit.
猎犬在野兔身后紧追不舍。

The rabbit disappeared in the bushes.
那只兔子消失在树丛中。

If your rabbit happens to like carrots, you have to carefully ration the stuff.
如果碰巧你的兔子喜欢吃胡萝卜，那你就要小心给它定好分量了。
