# 音标

- 英 `/'evrɪ/`
- 美 `/'ɛvri/`

# 词义

`used to refer to all the people or things in a particular group or all the parts of something`

# 同根

- adv. `everywhere` 到处
- n. `everywhere` 每个地方

# 短语

- `every time` 每次，每当
- `every one` 每个
- `every single` [口语][加强语气]每一个
- `every other` 所有其他的；每隔一个的
- `every bit` 完全；每一点；从头至尾
- `every inch` 彻底
- `every last` 每一个，全部
- `every bit as` 全部；完全一样
- `every so often` adv. 偶尔；常常；时常
- `every whit` 每一点；全部，完全
- `every which way` 四面八方；非常混乱地

# 例句

We looked carefully at every car that drove past.
我们仔细地看着驶过的每一辆汽车。

Every child will receive a certificate at the end of the course.
课程结束时，每一个孩子都可以获得证书。

I enjoyed every minute of the film.
那部电影我从头到尾都看得很过瘾。

I listened carefully to every word he said.
他说的每一个字我都听得很仔细。
