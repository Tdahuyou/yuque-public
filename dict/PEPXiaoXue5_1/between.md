# 音标

- 英 `/bɪ'twiːn/`
- 美 `/bəˈtwin/`

# 词义

`in or through the place that seprates two things, people, or places`

# 同义

prep. 在…之间
`entre`

adv. 在中间
`intermediately`

# 同根

- adv. `betwixt` 在其间；不走极端
- prep. `betwixt` 在……之间；在……中间

# 短语

- `in between` 在中间
- `between you and me` 只限于咱俩之间；我们之间的秘密
- `between ourselves` 只限于咱们之间；秘密的
- `go between` 调停；做中间人

# 例句

I sat down between Sue and Jane.
我在苏和简中间坐了下来。

a house and stables, with a yard in between
中间隔着院子的房子和马廐

The ball rolled between his feet.
球在他两脚之间滚来滚去。
