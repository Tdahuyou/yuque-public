# 音标

- 英 `/sə'l(j)uːt/`
- 美 `/sə'lut/`

# 词义

v. 行礼致敬，敬礼
`to move your right hand to your head, especially in order to show respect to an officer in the army, navy etc`



皇家礼炮(Royal Salute)——顶级苏格兰威士忌

# 同义

n. 致敬，欢迎；敬礼
`welcome` `Huanying`

vt. 行礼致敬，欢迎
`greet` `be welcome to`

vi. 致意，打招呼；行礼
`say hello` `touch one's hat`

# 同根

- adj. `salutatory` 致敬意的；表示欢迎的
- n. `salutation` 称呼；问候；招呼；寒喧
- n. `salutatory` 祝词；开幕词

# 例句

The two soldiers saluted Lieutenant Cecil.
那两名士兵向塞西尔中尉行了礼。

The men jumped to their feet and saluted.
这些人赶紧起立敬礼。
