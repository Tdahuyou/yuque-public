# 音标

- 英 `/pə'roul/`
- 美 `/pə'rol/`

# 词义

n. 假释
`permission for someone to leave prison, on the condition that they promise to behave well`

# 同义

n. 语言；誓言，诺言；释放宣言
`language` `word` `tongue` `engagement`

# 短语

- `on parole` 获得假释
- `out on parole` [美国俚语](女子)离了婚的

# 例句

She will become eligible for parole in 19 months.
19 个月以后她将符合假释条件。
