# 音标

- 英 `/lætʃ/`
- 美 `/lætʃ/`

# 词义

n. 门锁
`a type of lock for a door that you can open from the inside by turning a handle, but that you need a key to open from the outside`



和catch(v. 抓)一起记

# 同义

vi. 占有，抓住；闭锁
`take possession of` `seize on`

n. 门闩
`door release` `bolt`

# 短语

- `latch on` 占有；抓住；理解
- `on the latch` 关着但不上锁

# 例句

...a key clicked in the latch of the front door.
…钥匙在前门的弹簧锁里发出咔哒的声响。
