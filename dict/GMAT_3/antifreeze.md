# 音标

- 英 `/'æntifriːz/`
- 美 `/'æntɪfriz/`

# 词义

n. 抗冻剂
`a liquid that is put in the water in car engines to stop it from freezing`



anti(对抗的) + freeze(冷冻) → 抗冻剂

# 同义

n. [助剂]防冻剂
`deicing agent`

# 例句

DEG is used in antifreeze and as a solvent.
二甘醇用于防冻剂或作为一种溶剂使用。
