# 音标

- 英 `/'skɜːrələs/`
- 美 `/ˈskɜrələs/`

# 词义

adj. 粗俗的；诽谤的
`Scurrilous accusations or stories are untrue and unfair, and are likely to damage the reputation of the person that they relate to`



scurril(下流) + ous → 下流的； 和scurry(v. 急奔)一起记

# 同义

adj. 下流的；说话粗鄙恶劣的；无礼的
`dirty` `blue`

# 同根

- adj. `scurvy` 卑鄙的，下流的
- adv. `scurrilously` 粗野无礼地；下流地
- adv. `scurvily` 下流地；无礼地；卑鄙地
- n. `scurvy` [内科] 坏血病
- n. `scurrility` 下流；刻薄嘴；谈吐卑劣粗俗

# 例句

Scurrilous and untrue stories were being invented.
有人正在编造虚假诽谤的故事。
