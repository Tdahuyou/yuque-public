# 音标

- 英 `/'eθənɔːl/`
- 美 `/'ɛθənɔl/`

# 词义

n. 乙醇，酒精
`the type of alcohol in alcoholic drinks, which can also be used as a fuel for cars`

# 同义

n. 乙醇，[有化]酒精
`ethyl alcohol` `alki`

# 例句

But the significance of the quarrel goes well beyond ethanol.
但是这场争论的意义远远不止于乙醇问题。

They exposed live ones to either the smell of their dead brethren or an ethanol control.
他们在活的七鳃鳗前释放它们死去的同胞的气息或者一种乙醇制剂。

No cars in Brazil run on pure gasoline anymore. The government requires that all vehicles run on blended fuel of about one-quarter ethanol.
如今巴西不再使用单纯靠汽油驱动的汽车，政府要求所有机动车均采用含四分之一乙醇的混合燃料。
