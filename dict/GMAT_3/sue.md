# 音标

- 英 `/suː/`
- 美 `/su/`

# 词义

v. 向…起诉，提起诉讼
`If you sue someone, you start a legal case against them, usually in order to claim money from them because they have harmed you in some way`



“诉” → 起诉

# 同义

vt. 控告；请求
`law` `accuse sb. of sth.`

vi. 控告；提出请求
`law` `bring charge against`

# 短语

- `sue for` 控告
- `sue out` 向法院请求得到

# 例句

Mr. Warren sued for libel over the remarks.
沃伦先生对那些评论提起诽谤诉讼。

The company could be sued for damages.
该公司会因所造成的损失被起诉。
