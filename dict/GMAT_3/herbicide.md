# 音标

- 英 `/'ɜːrbɪsaɪd/`
- 美 `/'ɝbɪsaɪd/`

# 词义

n. 除草剂
`a substance used to kill unwanted plants`



herb(草) + i + cide(杀) → 除草剂

# 同义

n. [农药]除草剂
`weedicide`

# 例句

He attributed this to a dilution effect of the herbicide.
他把这归因于除草剂的稀释效应。

Weed identification technology is the key to the realization of selectively spraying herbicide for the environmental protection.
田间杂草识别技术是实现变量喷洒除草剂以保护环境的关键所在。
