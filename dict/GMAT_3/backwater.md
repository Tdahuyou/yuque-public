# 音标

- 英 `/'bækwɔːtər/`
- 美 `/'bækwɔtɚ/`

# 词义

n. 回水；死水
`a part of a river away from the main part, where the water does not move`

# 同义

n. [机]回水；死水；停滞不进的状态或地方
`standing water` `dead water`

# 例句

It is a commonplace to say that Northern Ireland is a backwater in modern Europe.
有关北爱尔兰是现代欧洲的一潭死水的说法已经是陈词滥调了。
