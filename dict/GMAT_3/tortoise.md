# 音标

- 英 `/'tɔːrtəs/`
- 美 `/ˈtɔrtəs/`

# 词义

n. 乌龟，龟
`a slow-moving land animal that can pull its head and legs into the hard round shell that covers its body`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]龟，乌龟（等于testudo）；迟缓的人
`turtle` `chelonian`

# 短语

- `tortoise shell` 龟甲；一种类似龟甲的蝴蝶

# 例句

Run like a tortoise.
像乌龟一样慢慢跑。

Either way, both the hare and tortoise get what they deserve based on how they behave.
无论如何，野兔和乌龟得到什么都是基于它们的行为举止的。
