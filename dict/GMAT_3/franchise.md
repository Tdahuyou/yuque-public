# 音标

- 英 `/'fræntʃaɪz/`
- 美 `/'fræntʃaɪz/`

# 词义

n. 特权；经销权
`permission given by a company to someone who wants to sell its goods or services`

# 同义

n. [贸易]特权；公民权；经销权；管辖权
`jurisdiction` `special privilege`

vt. 给…以特许（或特权）；赋予公民权
`citizenize`

# 短语

- `franchise chain` 特许连锁
- `franchise rights` 特许经营权

# 例句

a franchise holder
特许经营权持有人

a franchise agreement
特许经营协议
