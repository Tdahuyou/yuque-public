# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'stiːm/`
- 美 `/ɪ'stim/`

# 词义

v&n. 尊重，尊敬
`a feeling of respect for someone, or a good opinion of someone`

# 同义

vt. 尊敬；认为；考虑；估价
`assess` `honor` `expect` `evaluate` `guess`

n. 尊重；尊敬
`estimation` `respect` `regard` `worship`

# 短语

- `self esteem` 自尊

# 例句

Peden was greatly esteemed by the people of Ayrshire.
佩登深受艾尔郡人的尊敬。
