# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɑːbsə'lesns/`
- 美 `/'ɑbsə'lɛsns/`

# 词义

n. 荒废；过时
`when something becomes old-fashioned and no longer useful, because something newer and better has been invented`



来自obsolete(a. 废弃的)

# 同义

n. [生物]退化；荒废
`regression` `degradation` `deterioration` `degeneration`

# 同根

- adj. `obsolescent` 荒废的；即将过时的；逐渐被废弃的

# 例句

The aircraft was nearing obsolescence by early 1942.
这种飞机在1942年年初就濒临淘汰了。
