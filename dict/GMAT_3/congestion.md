# 音标

- 英 `/kən'dʒestʃən/`
- 美 `/kən'dʒɛstʃən/`

# 词义

n. 拥挤，堵塞；充血
`If there is congestion in a place, the place is extremely crowded and blocked with traffic or people`



来自congest(v. 充塞， 拥挤)

# 同义

n. 拥挤；拥塞；[病理]充血
`injection` `crowding`

# 同根

- adj. `congestive` 充血的，充血性的
- vi. `congest` 充血；拥挤
- vt. `congest` 使充血；充塞

# 短语

- `traffic congestion` 通信量拥挤；信号拥挤
- `congestion control` 拥塞控制；拥挤控制
- `congestion pricing` 拥挤收费；堵车费；高峰期行车收费
- `nasal congestion` 鼻塞；鼻壅塞；鼻充血
- `congestion avoidance` n. 拥塞避免

# 例句

The problems of traffic congestion will not disappear in a hurry.
交通堵塞问题不会很快消失。
