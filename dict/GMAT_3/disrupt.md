# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'rʌpt/`
- 美 `/dɪs'rʌpt/`

# 词义

v. 扰乱；中断，瓦解
`If someone or something disrupts an event, system, or process, they cause difficulties that prevent it from continuing or operating in a normal way`



dis(分开) + rupt(断) → 断开 → 中断

# 同义

vt. 破坏；使瓦解；使分裂；使中断；使陷于混乱
`destroy` `tear` `undermine` `founder`

adj. 分裂的，中断的；分散的
`divided` `scattered`

# 同根

- adj. `disruptive` 破坏的；分裂性的；制造混乱的
- n. `disruption` 破坏，毁坏；分裂，瓦解

# 例句

Anti-war protesters disrupted the debate.
反战示威者们扰乱了辩论。
