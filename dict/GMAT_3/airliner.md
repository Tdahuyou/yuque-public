# 音标

- 英 `/'erlaɪnər/`
- 美 `/'ɛrlaɪnɚ/`

# 词义

n. 班机；大型客机
`a large plane for passengers`



来自airline(n. 航线)

# 同义

n. [航]班机；大型客机
`liner` `scheduled flight`

# 同根

- adj. `airline` 航线的
- n. `airline` 航空公司；航线

# 短语

- `jet airliner` 喷气客机；喷气式飞机

# 例句

Seven years ago at this hour, a doomed airliner plunged from the sky, split the rock and steel of this building, and changed our world forever.
七年前的这一时刻，一架被当作毁灭性武器的航空班机从天而降，撞裂了这座大楼的钢筋水泥，永远改变了这个世界。

Australian officials had indicated that if nothing was found by that stage, then the search operation for the airliner would not be extended.
澳大利亚官员曾表示，如果届时仍一无所获，搜索这架客机的行动将不会被延长。
