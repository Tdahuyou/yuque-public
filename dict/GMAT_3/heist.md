# 音标

- 英 `/haɪst/`
- 美 `/haist/`

# 词义

v. 抢劫，强夺


n. 抢劫；
`A heist is a robbery, especially one in which money, jewellery, or art is stolen`



电影《偷天盗影》的英文是Heist

# 同义

vt. 抢劫，拦劫；强夺
`rob of` `spoliate`

n. 抢劫；强夺
`robbery` `despoliation`

vi. 抢劫
`rob of`

# 例句

This was a 21st century bank heist that reached through the Internet to span the globe.
这是一种21世纪通过互联网遍布全球的银行抢劫。

As each minute of the heist ticks by, true motives are revealed and unexpected twists ratchet up the stakes to a thrilling crescendo.
作为抢劫的每分钟运转的，真正的动机是揭示和意想不到的曲折加大赌注一个激动人心的高潮。

After a botched heist, Eddie (Bruce Willis), a murderous crime boss, hunts down the seductive thief Karen (Claire Forlani) who failed him.
一个拙劣的抢劫后，埃迪（布鲁斯·威利斯），一个凶残的黑帮老大，查获诱人的小偷卡伦（克莱尔·弗兰妮）谁辜负了他。
