# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spel/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spɛl/`

# 词义

v. 驱散；消除
`to make something go away, especially a belief, idea, or feeling`



dis(分开) + pel(推) → 推开 → 驱散

# 同义

vt. 驱散，驱逐；消除（烦恼等）
`eliminate` `avoid`

# 例句

We want to dispel the myth that you cannot eat well in Britain.
我们要消除在英国吃不好的误解。

Light poured into the hall, dispelling the shadows.
阳光洒进大厅，驱散了阴影。
