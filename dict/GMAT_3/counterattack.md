# 音标

- 英 `/'kauntərəˌtæk/`
- 美 `/'kaʊntərə,tæk/`

# 词义

n. 反攻，反击
`an attack you make against someone who has attacked you, in a war, sport, or argument`



counter(相反的) + attack(攻击) → 反击

# 同义

vt. 反击；反攻
`Break back` `knock back`

vi. 反击；反攻
`Break back` `knock back`

n. 反击；反攻
`retaliation` `fightback`

# 同根

- adj. `counter` 相反的
- adv. `counter` 相反地
- n. `counter` 计数器，计算器；柜台；计算者
- v. `counter` 反击；反对

# 例句

The army began its counterattack this morning.
今天早晨部队开始了反攻。
