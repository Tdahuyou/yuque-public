# 音标

- 英 `/pə'tɜːrnəlɪzəm/`
- 美 `/pə'tɝnəlɪzəm/`

# 词义

n. 家长式统治；家长主义
`when people in charge of an organization or society protect the members and give them what they need but do not allow them any freedom or responsibility`



来自paternal(a. 父亲的)

# 同根

- adj. `paternal` 父亲的；得自父亲的；父亲般的
- n. `paterfamilias` 家长

# 例句

...the company's reputation for paternalism.
...该公司家长作风的名声。
