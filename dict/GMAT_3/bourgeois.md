# 音标

- 英 `/ˌbur'ʒwɑː/`
- 美 `/,bʊr'ʒwɑ/`

# 词义

n. 资本家；中产阶级的人
`a member of the middle class, esp one regarded as being conservative and materialistic or (in Marxist thought) a capitalist exploiting the working class `

# 同义

n. 资本家；九点活字；中产阶级的人；追求物质享受和社会地位者
`capital`

adj. 资产阶级的；中产阶级的；贪图享受的
`middle-class`

# 短语

- `bourgeois liberalization` [法]资产阶级自由化
- `petty bourgeois` 小资产阶级分子

# 例句

That was the way bourgeois society valued a man.
这就是资产阶级社会评价人的方法。

He thought they might infect others with their bourgeois ideas.
他认为他们可以用他们的资产阶级思想去影响别人。
