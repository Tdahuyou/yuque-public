# 音标

- 英 `/'pɜːrvjuː/`
- 美 `/'pɝvju/`

# 词义

n. 范围
`within or outside the limits of someone’s job, activity, or knowledge`

# 同义

n. 范围，权限；视界；条款
`extent` `boundary` `region` `scope` `rights` `spectrum` `territory`

# 例句

This matter comes within the purview of the Department of Health.
这件事属于卫生部的管辖范围。
