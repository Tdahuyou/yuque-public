# 音标

- 英 `/'lɪbrəlaɪz/`
- 美 `/'lɪbrəlaɪz/`

# 词义

v. 使自由化；宽大；自由化
`When a country or government liberalizes, or liberalizes its laws or its attitudes, it becomes less strict and allows people more freedom in their actions`



liberal(自由的) + ize(动词后缀， 使…) → 使自由化
