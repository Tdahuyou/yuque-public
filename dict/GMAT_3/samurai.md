# 音标

- 英 `/'sæmuraɪ/`
- 美 `/'sæmuraɪ/`

# 词义

n. 武士；
`a member of a powerful military class in Japan in the past`



来自日语， 指推崇武士道的人

# 同义

n. （日）武士；武士阶级
`knight` `swordsman`

# 例句

Istara: It would suit a famous samurai like you.
这绝对适合像你这么著名的武士。

In Japan, only a talented samurai can break through the ninjas.
在日本，只有一个有才华的武士可以通过忍者突破。

But by 1638 thousands of the peasants and their samurai mercenaries had been slaughtered.
但是在1638年之前，数千农民和他们的佣兵武士都被屠戮。
