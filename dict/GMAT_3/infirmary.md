# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'fɜːrməri/`
- 美 `/ɪn'fɝməri/`

# 词义

n. 医院，医务室
`a hospital – often used in the names of hospitals in Britain`



in(不) + firm(结实的) + ary → 柔弱的人常去的地方 → 医院

# 同义

n. 医务室；医院；养老院
`hospital` `old people's home`

# 例句

Dallas:Something has attached itself to him. We have to get him to the infirmary right away.
达拉斯：有东西寄生在他体内。我们必须马上把他送进医务室。
