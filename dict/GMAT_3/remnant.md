# 音标

- 英 `/'remnənt/`
- 美 `/'rɛmnənt/`

# 词义

n. 残余物，残迹
`The remnants of something are small parts of it that are left over when the main part has disappeared or has been used or destroyed`

# 同义

n. 剩余
`surplus` `residual` `spare`

adj. 剩余的
`remaining` `surplus` `residual` `odd`

# 短语

- `supernova remnant` 超新星遗迹，超新星残骸

# 例句

Beneath the present church were remnants of Roman flooring.
在目前的这座教堂下面还有残余的古罗马地板。
