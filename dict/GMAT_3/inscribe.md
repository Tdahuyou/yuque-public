# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'skraɪb/`
- 美 `/ɪn'skraɪb/`

# 词义

v. 刻；【数】使内接
`If you inscribe words on an object, you write or carve the words on the object`



in + scribe(写， 刻) → 刻

# 同义

vt. 题写；题献；铭记；雕
`brand` `heart`

# 同根

- adj. `inscribed` 内切的；记名的；有铭刻的
- v. `inscribed` 题写；雕刻；内切（inscribe的过去分词）

# 例句

Some galleries commemorate donors by inscribing their names on the walls.
一些展览馆把捐赠者的名字刻在墙上以纪念他们。
