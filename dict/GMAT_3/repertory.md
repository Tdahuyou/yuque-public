# 音标

- 英 `/'repərtɔːri/`
- 美 `/'rɛpɚtɔri/`

# 词义

n. 剧目，作品
`a type of theatre work in which actors perform different plays on different days, instead of doing the same play for a long time`

# 同义

n. 储备；仓库；全部剧目
`reserve` `warehouse`

# 同根

- n. `repertoire` 全部节目；计算机指令系统；（美）某人或机器的全部技能

# 例句

a repertory company
轮演剧目剧团
