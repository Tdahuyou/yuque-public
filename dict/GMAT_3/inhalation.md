# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪnhə'leɪʃn/`
- 美 `/,ɪnhə'leʃən/`

# 词义

n. 吸入
`Inhalation is the process or act of breathing in, taking air and sometimes other substances into your lungs`



来自inhale(v. 吸入)

# 同义

n. [生理]吸入；吸入药剂
`imbibition` `indraft`

# 同根

- adj. `inhalant` 供吸入的
- n. `inhaler` [临床] 吸入器；空气过滤器；吸入者
- n. `inhalant` 吸入剂；吸入器
- n. `inhalator` [临床] 吸入器；人工呼吸补助器
- vi. `inhale` 吸气
- vt. `inhale` 吸入；猛吃猛喝

# 短语

- `inhalation anesthesia` 吸入麻醉

# 例句

They were taken to the hospital suffering from smoke inhalation.
他们因吸入浓烟被送进医院。

Take several deep inhalations.
做几次深的吸气。
