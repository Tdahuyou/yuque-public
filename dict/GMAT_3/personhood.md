# 词义

`the condition of being a person who is an individual with inalienable rights, esp under the 14th Amendment of the Constitution of the United States `



person(人) + hood → 人格

- adj. `personal` 个人的；身体的；亲自的

- adv. `personally` 亲自地；当面；个别地；就自己而言
- n. `personality` 个性；品格；名人
- n. `personal` 人事消息栏；人称代名词

- n. `personification` 人格化；化身；拟人法（一种修辞手法）；象征

- vt. `personalize` 使个性化；把…拟人化
- vt. `personify` 使人格化；赋与…以人性
