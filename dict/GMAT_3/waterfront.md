# 音标

- 英 `/'wɔːtərfrʌnt/`
- 美 `/'wɔtɚfrʌnt/`

# 词义

n. 海滨；滩




water(水) + front(前沿) → 水的前沿 → 水边 → 海滨

# 同义

n. 滩，海滨；水边
`shore` `oceanfront`

It is the centrepiece of Doha's outstanding waterfront landmark, the West Bay business and residential complex.
