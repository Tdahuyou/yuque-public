# 音标

- 英 `/ɪg'zempt/`
- 美 `/ɪg'zɛmpt/`

# 词义

v. 免除，豁免；
`to give someone permission not to do or pay something`

adj. 被免除的
`not affected by something, or not having to do it or pay it`



ex(出) + empt(拿) → 拿出去 → 免除

# 同义

vt. 免除；豁免
`discharge` `forgive`

# 同根

- n. `exemption` 免除，豁免；免税

# 短语

- `exempt from` 豁免，免除
- `exempt from tax` 免税
- `tax exempt` 免税的

# 例句

South Carolina claimed the power to exempt its citizens from the obligation to obey federal law.
南卡罗来纳州宣称有权使其公民免除遵守联邦法律的义务。
