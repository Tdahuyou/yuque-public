# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spaɪz/`
- 美 `/dɪ'spaɪz/`

# 词义

v. 轻视，轻蔑
`If you despise something or someone, you dislike them and have a very low opinion of them`



de(不) + spise(=spic看) → 不值得看 → 轻视

# 同义

vt. 轻视，鄙视
`slight` `set light by`

# 同根

- adj. `despised` 受轻视的
- v. `despised` 轻视（despise的过去分词）；看不起

# 例句

I can never, ever forgive him. I despise him.
我永远，永远都不会原谅他。我鄙视他。
