# 音标

- 英 `/kən'sɜːrvətɪvli/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

adv. 保守地


# 同义

adv. 谨慎地；保存地；适当地
`properly` `well` `adequately` `deliberately` `appropriately`

# 同根

- adj. `conservative` 保守的
- adj. `conservatory` 有保存力的；保存性的
- adj. `conserved` 保守的
- adj. `conserving` 保存的
- n. `conservative` 保守派，守旧者
- n. `conservation` 保存，保持；保护
- n. `conserve` 果酱；蜜饯
- n. `conservatory` 温室；音乐学校
- n. `conservancy` 管理；保护；保存
- n. `conservationist` 自然资源保护论者
- v. `conserved` 保存；保全（conserve的过去式）
- v. `conserving` 保护；保藏；糖渍（conserve的ing形式）
- vt. `conserve` 保存；将…做成蜜饯；使守恒

Out of the 200 roles that were applied for, the submissions which were accompanied by a low-cut dress received 62 more interview offers than their more conservatively dressed counterparts.
