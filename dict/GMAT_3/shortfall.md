# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃɔːrtfɔːl/`
- 美 `/'ʃɔrtfɔl/`

# 词义

n. 亏空；不足
`the difference between the amount you have and the amount you need or expect`



short(短) + fall(下落) → 因收入下降而短少 → 亏空

# 同义

n. 差额；缺少
`poverty` `apart from`

# 例句

The government has refused to make up a $30,000 shortfall in funding.
政府已经拒绝补足3万美元的资金短缺。
