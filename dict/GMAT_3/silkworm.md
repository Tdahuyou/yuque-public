# 音标

- 英 `/'sɪlkwɜːrm/`
- 美 `/'sɪlkwɝm/`

# 词义

n. 蚕，桑蚕
`a type of  caterpillar  which produces silk thread`



silk(蚕丝) + worm(虫) → 吐蚕丝的虫 → 蚕

# 短语

- `silkworm chrysalis` 蚕蛹
- `silkworm moth` 蚕蛾
- `silkworm pupa` 蚕蛹

# 例句

The first silkworm diseases began to appear in 1845, creating an epidemic.
1845年，首批桑蚕疾病开始出现，并引起了一次流行。

An epidemic of several silkworm diseases caused production to fall, especially in France, where the industry never recovered.
而几种桑蚕疾病的流行导致了丝绸产品下滑，特别在法国，丝绸工业再也没有恢复。
