# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'ʃraɪn/`
- 美 `/ɪn'ʃraɪn/`

# 词义

v. 奉为神圣
`If something such as an idea or a right is enshrined in something such as a constitution or law, it is protected by it`



en(进入) + shrine(圣地) → 进入圣地 → 奉为神圣

# 同义

vt. 铭记，珍藏；把…置于神龛内；把…奉为神圣
`brand` `heart`

# 例句

This system is enshrined in our Constitution.
我们的宪法使该制度神圣而不可侵犯。

The apartheid system which enshrined racism in law still existed.
以法律形式保护种族主义的种族隔离制度依然存在着。
