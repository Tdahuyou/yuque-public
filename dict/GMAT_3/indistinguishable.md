# 音标

- 英 `/ˌɪndɪ'stɪŋgwɪʃəbl/`
- 美 `/'ɪndɪ'stɪŋgwɪʃəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 难区分的，不能分辨的
`things that are indistinguishable are so similar that you cannot see any difference between them`



in(不) + distinguish(区分) + able(能) → 不能分辨的

# 同根

- n. `indistinguishability` 不可分辨性

# 例句

Replica weapons are indistinguishable from the real thing.
仿真武器与真武器难以辨别。
