# 音标

- 英 `/mæg'niːziəm/`
- 美 `/mæg'nizɪrm/`

# 词义

n. 【化】镁
`Magnesium is a light, silvery white metal which burns with a bright white flame`



magn(大) + esium → 镁光灯亮度很大 → 镁
