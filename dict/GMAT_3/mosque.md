# 音标

- 英 `/mɑːsk/`
- 美 `/mɑsk/`

# 词义

n. 清真寺
`a building in which Muslims worship`



和“莫斯科”发音颇像

# 例句

A red sandstone mosque stands on one side.
一座红色砂岩清真寺耸立在一旁。

Would you go to church or the synagogue or the mosque?
你会去教堂，犹太教会，或者清真寺吗？

Before we got the tents, some children were studying in the mosque.
在我们有这些帐篷之前，一些孩子在清真寺学习。
