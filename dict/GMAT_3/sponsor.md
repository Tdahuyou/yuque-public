# 音标

- 英 `/'spɑːnsər/`
- 美 `/'spɑnsɚ/`

# 词义

v. 赞助；
`to agree to help someone or be responsible for what they do`

n. 赞助人
`a person or company that pays for a show, broadcast, sports event etc, especially in exchange for the right to advertise at that event`



spons(允诺) + or → 答应给钱 → 赞助

# 同义

n. 赞助者；主办者；保证人
`friend` `guarantee` `entrepreneur`

vt. 赞助；发起
`launch` `support of`

# 同根

- n. `sponsorship` 赞助；发起；保证人的地位；教父母身份

# 例句

Dozens of companies, including Hewlett-Packard, are sponsoring the event.
包括惠普在内的许多公司都在赞助这项赛事。
