# 音标

- 英 `/kən'strɪkt/`
- 美 `/kən'strɪkt/`

# 词义

v. 收缩；使狭窄
`If a part of your body, especially your throat, is constricted or if it constricts, something causes it to become narrower`

# 同义

vt. 压缩；束紧
`to compress` `telescope`

# 同根

- adj. `constrictive` 紧缩的；束紧的；压缩性的
- n. `constriction` 收缩；压缩；紧窄感；压缩物
- n. `constrictor` 大蟒；括约肌；使压缩之物

# 例句

Severe migraines can be treated with a drug that constricts the blood vessels.
严重偏头痛可以用一种收缩血管的药物来治疗。
