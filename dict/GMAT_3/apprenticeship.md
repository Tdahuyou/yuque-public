# 音标

- 英 `/ə'prentɪʃɪp/`
- 美 `/ə'prɛntɪʃɪp/`

# 词义

n. 学徒期；学徒身份
`the job of being an apprentice, or the period of time in which you are an apprentice`



ap + prentice(徒弟) + ship → 学徒身份

# 同根

- n. `apprentice` 学徒；生手
- vi. `apprentice` 当学徒
- vt. `apprentice` 使…当学徒

# 例句

He’s serving an apprenticeship as a printer.
他现在是一名印刷工学徒。

a five-year apprenticeship
五年学徒期
