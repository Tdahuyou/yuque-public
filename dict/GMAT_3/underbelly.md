# 音标

- 英 `/'ʌndərbeli/`
- 美 `/'ʌndɚ'bɛli/`

# 词义

n. 下腹部；薄弱部分；易受攻击的部位、区域等
`the weakest part of an organization or a person’s character, that is most easily attacked or criticized`



under(下面的) + belly(腹部) → 下腹部

# 例句

I know where his soft underbelly is, and that's his material possessions.
我知道他的弱点是什么，是他的财产。
