# 音标

- 英 `/kræs/`
- 美 `/kræs/`

# 词义

adj. 粗劣的；愚钝的
`behaving in a stupid and offensive way which shows that you do not understand or care about other people’s feelings`

# 同义

adj. 愚钝的；粗鲁的；非常的
`intense` `robust` `crude` `dense` `rude`

# 同根

- n. `crassitude` 愚钝；粗糙；粗大
- n. `crassness` 愚钝；粗俗；极端

# 例句

a crass remark
粗鲁的言语

an act of crass stupidity
愚蠢的行为
