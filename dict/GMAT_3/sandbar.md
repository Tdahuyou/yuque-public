# 音标

- 英 `/'sændbɑːr/`
- 美 `/'sændbɑr/`

# 词义

n. 沙洲
`A sandbar is a sandbank which is found especially at the mouth of a river or harbour`

# 同义

n. 沙洲；沙堤
`hurst` `alluvion`

# 例句

Without the fungi, this would be just another sandbar.
如果没有真菌，这里将成为另一个沙洲。

The group also reported the dusky and sandbar sharks as being threatened.
该组织还报告了灰鲨和沙洲鲨也正受到威胁。

Low water bares gray strips of sandbar on which the natives build tiny palm-thatch shelters for overnight fishing trips.
水浅的地方露出灰茫茫的狭长沙洲，土著人在沙洲上为过夜的渔夫搭建了小小的棕榈茅舍。
