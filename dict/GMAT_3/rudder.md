# 音标

- 英 `/'rʌdər/`
- 美 `/'rʌdɚ/`

# 词义

n. 船舵
`a flat part at the back of a ship or aircraft that can be turned in order to control the direction in which it moves`

# 同根

- adj. `rudderless` 无舵的；无指导者的

# 短语

- `rudder angle` 方向舵偏角

# 例句

If, say, ideal is to successful a boat, so, I'll take good rudder.
如果说，理想是驶向成功的一艘小船，那么，我一定会把好船舵。

If unfurling your sails before the storm you snap the rudder in two.
如果你在风暴前扯起船帆，你把船舵折成两半。

If the ideal is to succeed in a small boat, then, I will take good rudder.
