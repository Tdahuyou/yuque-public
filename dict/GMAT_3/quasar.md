# 音标

- 英 `/'kweɪzɑːr/`
- 美 `/'kwezɑr/`

# 词义

n. 【天】类星体
`an object in space that is similar to a star and that shines very brightly`

# 例句

This quasar was the only one without a known host galaxy.
这个类星体似乎无依无傍，不在任何一个星系当中。

But as matter spirals toward a black hole, some of it is blown away by the heat and light of the quasar.
但是随着物质盘旋飞向一个黑洞，它的一部分会被这个类星体的热量和光吹走。
