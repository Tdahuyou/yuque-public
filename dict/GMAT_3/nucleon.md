# 音标

- 英 `/'nuːklɪɑn/`
- 美 `/'nʊklɪɑn/`

# 词义

n. 核子
`a proton or neutron, esp one present in an atomic nucleus `



nucle(核的) + on(物) → 核子

# 同根

- n. `nucleonics` 核子学；原子核物理学

# 例句

The greatest amount of nuclear binding energy per nucleon occurs for nuclei in the vicinity of iron.
按每个核子计，最大的核结合能量出现在铁附近的核中。

To clarify the nuclear effects of deuteron will contribute to a better understanding of parton distributions in bound nucleon.
澄清氘核结构函数的核效应，有助于我们更好的了解束缚核子的部分子分布函数。

The structure, property and working principle of nucleon turning tray steelyard were introduced, and its working feature as well as main factors of disturbing measuring accuracy were analyzed.
介绍了核子转盘秤的结构、性能及工作原理，分析了它的工作特征及干扰计量准确度的主要因素。
