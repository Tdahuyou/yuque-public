# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'spærɪdʒɪŋ/`
- 美 `/dɪs'pærɪdʒɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 贬低的，轻蔑的
`criticizing someone or something, and showing that you do not think they are very good or important`



来自disparage(v. 蔑视， 贬低)， dis(不) + par(平等) + age → 不平等看人 → 贬低的

# 同义

adj. 毁谤的；轻蔑的
`scornful` `contemptuous`

# 同根

- adv. `disparagingly` 以轻视的态度，蔑视地；以贬抑的口吻
- n. `disparagement` 轻蔑；轻视
- n. `disparager` 毁谤者
- vt. `disparage` 蔑视；毁谤

# 例句

He was critical of the people, disparaging of their crude manners.
他批评那些人，贬低他们粗鲁的举止。
