# 音标

- 英 `/ˌaut'strɪp/`
- 美 `/,aʊt'strɪp/`

# 词义

v. 超过，胜出
`to do something better than someone else or be more successful`



out(超过) + strip(条， 带) → 最早超出终点线的人就胜出

# 同义

vt. 超过；胜过；比…跑得快
`better than` `overcome` `top` `cap` `outdo`

# 例句

We outstripped all our competitors in sales last year.
去年我们的销售额超过了所有竞争对手。
