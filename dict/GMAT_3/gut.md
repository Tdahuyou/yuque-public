# 音标

- 英 `/gʌt/`
- 美 `/ɡʌt/`

# 词义

v. 毁坏〔建筑物〕的内部〔尤指烧毁〕
`to completely destroy the inside of a building, especially by fire`

# 同义

n. 内脏；肠子；剧情；胆量；海峡
`courage` `inside` `neck` `channel` `narrow`

vt. 取出内脏；摧毁内部装置
`eviscerate`

adj. 简单的；本质的，根本的
`simple` `organic` `plain` `fundamental` `essential`

# 同根

- adj. `gutless` 无胆量的；没勇气的；没有生气的

# 短语

- `gut feeling` 直觉

# 例句

The building was completely gutted by fire.
大楼被火烧空了。
