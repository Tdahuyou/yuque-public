# 词义

n. 灵丹妙药
`something that people think will make everything better and solve all their problems`



pan(全部) + acea(治疗) → 全都能治疗的 → 灵丹妙药

# 同义

n. 灵丹妙药；万能药
`catholicon` `elixir`

# 例句

Trade is not a panacea for the world's economic or social ills.
贸易不是世界经济或社会弊端的万灵丹。
