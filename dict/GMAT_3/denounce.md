# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'nauns/`
- 美 `/dɪ'naʊns/`

# 词义

v. 公然抨击；告发
`If you denounce someone who has broken a rule or law, you report them to the authorities`



de + nounce(讲， 说) → 打小报告 → 告发

# 同义

vt. 谴责；告发；公然抨击；通告废除
`condemn` `denunciate`

# 同根

- n. `denouncement` 告发；痛骂；公开抨击

# 例句

They were at the mercy of informers who might at any moment denounce them.
他们完全受那些随时可能告发他们的告密者的支配。
