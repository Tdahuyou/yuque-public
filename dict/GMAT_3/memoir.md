# 音标

- 英 `/'memwɑːr/`
- 美 `/'mɛmwɑr/`

# 词义

n. 传略，实录
`a short piece of writing about a person or place that you knew well, or an event that you experienced`



memo(记忆) + ir → 回忆录

# 同义

n. 回忆录；研究报告；自传；实录
`autobiography`

# 例句

George W. Bush—soon, like so many American workers, to have lots of time on his hands—may soon be shopping a memoir, and for good reason.
布什不久就会像许多美国工人那样拥有相当多的闲暇时光，他可能会用一个极好的理由购买一本实录。
