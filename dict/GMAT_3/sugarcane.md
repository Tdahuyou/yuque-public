# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃugərˌken/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 甘蔗
`a tall tropical plant from whose stems sugar is obtained`



sugar(糖) + cane(藤条， 茎) → 糖条 → 甘蔗

# 短语

- `sugarcane juice` 蔗汁

# 例句

The sugared vinegar is refined from the sugarcane and pine.
加糖的醋是从甘蔗和松木中精炼出来的。

There are vast fields of sugarcane in Queensland, Australia.
在澳大利亚昆士兰州有大片甘蔗田。

Plant waxes include carnauba wax candelilla wax and sugarcane wax used in polishes. About90% of the waxes in commerce are recovered by dewaxing petroleum.
植物蜡主要是巴西棕榈蜡，小烛树蜡和甘蔗蜡都用作擦亮剂。工业用蜡中约有90%是由石油中回收的。
