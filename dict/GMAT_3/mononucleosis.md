# 音标

- 英 `/ˌmɑːnouˌnuːkli'ousɪs/`
- 美 `/,mɑno,nuklɪ'osɪs/`

# 词义

n. 单核细胞增多症
`Mononucleosis is a disease which causes swollen glands, fever, and a sore throat`

# 同根

- adj. `mononuclear` [细胞] 单核的
- adj. `mononucleate` 细胞等单核的
- n. `mononuclear` 单核细胞

# 短语

- `infectious mononucleosis` 传染性单核细胞增多

# 例句

For viral infections, including stomach flu (gastroenteritis) and mononucleosis, the best treatment is often rest and plenty of fluids.
对于肠胃型流感（肠胃炎）和单核细胞增多症这些病毒感染来说，最好的治疗方法通常是休息以及大量饮水。

Bilateral cervical adenopathy is also prominent in tuberculosis coccidioidomycosis infectious mononucleosis toxoplasmosis sarcoid lymphomas and leukemias.
双侧颈部腺病在结核、球孢子菌病、传染性单核细胞增多症、弓形虫病、肉样瘤、淋巴瘤和白血病。
