# 音标

- 英 `/'rʌgbi/`
- 美 `/'rʌɡbi/`

# 词义

n. 橄榄球
`an outdoor game played by two teams with an  oval (= egg-shaped )  ball that you kick or carry`



rug(地毯) + by → 在地毯一样的草坪上玩橄榄球

# 短语

- `rugby union` 英式橄榄球联合会
- `rugby football` 橄榄球
- `rugby league` n. 英式橄榄球联盟

# 例句

I ghosted his weekly rugby column for the Telegraph.
我为他代写《电讯报》每周的橄榄球专栏。

U8 is the least of all group, but they all worked hard, and never be late, it is obvious to see their deep love to rugby.
U8的人数是所有组里面最少的，但是他们都十分努力从来不迟到。他们对橄榄球深深地热爱是显而易见的。
