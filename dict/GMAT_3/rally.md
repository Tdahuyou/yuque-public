# 音标

- 英 `/'ræli/`
- 美 `/'ræli/`

# 词义

n. 跌后复升，反弹
`an occasion when something, especially the value of shares, becomes stronger again after a period of weakness or defeat`

# 同义

vi. 团结；重整；恢复；（网球等）连续对打
`league` `restore`

vt. 团结；集合；恢复健康、力量等
`assemble` `fall in`

n. 集会；回复；公路赛车会
`assembly` `feedback` `meet` `gathering` `congregation`

# 短语

- `(hold a) rally` 团结；（举行）集会
- `pep rally` 赛前动员会，鼓舞士气的集会
- `rally point` 集结点

# 例句

a late rally in the Tokyo stock market
最近东京股市的回暖
