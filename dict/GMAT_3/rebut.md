# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'bʌt/`
- 美 `/ri'bʌt/`

# 词义

v. 反驳
`to prove that a statement or a charge made against you is false`



re(反) + but(=butt顶撞) → 反顶撞 → 反驳

# 同义

vt. 反驳；揭露；拒绝
`reveal` `negative` `disclose` `refuse` `expose`

vi. 反驳；揭露
`open up` `bring to light`

n. 驳回；提出反证
`disallowance`

# 同根

- n. `rebuttal` 反驳；辩驳；反证
- n. `rebutter` 反驳者；第三次答辩

# 例句

He spent most of his speech rebutting criticisms of his foreign policy.
他大部分的演讲都用来反驳他在外交政策上受到的各种批评。
