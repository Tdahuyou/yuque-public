# 音标

- 英 `/snʌf/`
- 美 `/snʌf/`

# 词义

v. 吸；
`to inhale through the nose `

n. 鼻烟
`a type of tobacco in powder form, which people breathe in through their noses`

# 同义

vt. 剪烛花；掐灭；消灭；嗅出
`destroy` `eradicate`

n. 鼻烟；烛花；灯花
`snus`

vi. 扑灭；断气；嗅
`smell` `stamp out`

# 同根

- n. `snuffer` 海豚；吸鼻烟者；以鼻吸表示轻视者

# 短语

- `snuff bottle` 鼻烟壶
- `snuff out` 扼杀；消灭；死掉
- `up to snuff` 精明的；状况良好的；正常的

# 例句

He took a pinch of snuff .
他吸了一撮鼻烟。

a snuff box
鼻烟盒
