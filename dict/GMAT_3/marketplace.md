# 音标

- 英 `/'mɑːrkɪtpleɪs/`
- 美 `/'mɑrkɪtples/`

# 词义

n. 市场；市集
`the part of business activity that is concerned with buying and selling goods in competition with other companies`



market(市场) + place(地方) → 市场存在的地方 → 市集

# 同义

n. 市场；商场；市集
`exchanges` `plaza` `fair`

# 同根

- adj. `marketable` 市场的；可销售的；有销路的
- n. `marketing` 行销，销售
- n. `marketer` [贸易] 市场商人；市场营销人员
- v. `marketing` 出售；在市场上进行交易；使…上市（market的ing形式）

# 例句

Some retailers worry that new regulations will hurt their ability to compete in the marketplace.
有些零售商担心新规定会削弱他们的市场竞争力。
