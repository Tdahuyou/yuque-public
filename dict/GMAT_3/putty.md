# 音标

- 英 `/'pʌti/`
- 美 `/'pʌti/`

# 词义

n. 油灰；易被摆布的人
`a soft whitish substance that becomes hard when it dries and that is used to fix glass into window frames`

# 同义

n. [建]油灰；氧化锡
`badigeon` `stannic oxide`

# 短语

- `polyester putty` 聚酯油灰
- `putty powder` n. [物]氧化锡粉（等于油灰粉）
- `putty knife` 油灰刀；腻子刀
