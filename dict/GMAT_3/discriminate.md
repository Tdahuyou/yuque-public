# 音标

- 英 `/dɪ'skrɪmɪneɪt/`
- 美 `/dɪ'skrɪmɪnet/`

# 词义

v. 歧视；辨别
`to treat a person or group differently from another in an unfair way`



dis + crimin(罪行) + ate → 一种罪行 → 歧视

# 同义

vt. 歧视；区别；辨别
`tell from` `distinguish from`

vi. 歧视；区别；辨别
`tell from` `distinguish from`

# 同根

- adj. `discriminatory` 有辨识力的；差别对待的
- adj. `discriminating` 识别的；有差别的；有识别力的
- adj. `discriminative` 区别的，歧视的；有识别力的
- adj. `discriminable` 可辨别的；可分别的
- n. `discrimination` 歧视；区别，辨别；识别力
- n. `discriminator` [电子] 鉴别器；辨别者
- v. `discriminating` 识别（discriminate的ing形式）；区别对待

# 短语

- `discriminate against` 歧视；排斥

# 例句

The impressions are wrong, people now no longer discriminate each other, the crimination only exists in few people.
这些印象都是错误的，人们现在不再彼此歧视，歧视只在少数人身上存在。
