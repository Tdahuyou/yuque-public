# 音标

- 英 `/ˌdɪsɪn'tʃæntɪd/`
- 美 `/,dɪsɪn'tʃæntɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 不再着迷的，不抱有幻想的
`disappointed with someone or something, and no longer believing that they are good`



来自disenchant(v. 使清醒)

# 同根

- adj. `disenchanting` 使不再着迷的
- n. `disenchantment` 醒悟，清醒；不抱幻想
- v. `disenchanting` 使不再着迷（disenchant的ing形式）
- vt. `disenchant` 使清醒；使不抱幻想

# 例句

The electorate had grown disenchanted with politics.
选民已变得对政治不再抱幻想。
