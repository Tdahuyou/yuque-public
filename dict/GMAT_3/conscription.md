# 音标

- 英 `/kən'skrɪpʃn/`
- 美 `/kən'skrɪpʃən/`

# 词义

n. 征兵，征募
`when people are made to join the army, navy etc`



来自conscript(v. 征兵， 征募)

# 同义

n. 征兵；征兵制度；征用
`expropriation` `levy`

# 同根

- adj. `conscript` 被征召的
- n. `conscript` 应征士兵
- vt. `conscript` 征召

# 例句

Your body full compliance with the requirements of conscription.
你的身体完全符合征兵要求。

They were rather earnest about the Tommies, and the threat of conscription, and the shortage of sugar and toffee for the children.
他们对于军队，对于征兵的恐吓，对于儿童们的糖与糖果的缺乏，是颇郑重其事的。

Other sources of extra-budgetary income include funds from the central government and local governments for defense mobilization preparations, conscription, and demobilization.
其他预算外进项来源包括来自中央和地方用于防卫动员的准备工作、征兵以及复员安排的资金。
