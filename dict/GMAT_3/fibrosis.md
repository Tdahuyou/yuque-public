# 音标

- 英 `/faɪ'brousɪs/`
- 美 `/faɪ'brosɪs/`

# 词义

n. 纤维化
`the formation of an abnormal amount of fibrous tissue in an organ or part as the result of inflammation, irritation, or healing `



来自fibre(n. 纤维)

# 同义

n. [医][病理]纤维化，纤维变性
`fibering`

# 同根

- adj. `fibrous` 纤维的，纤维性的；纤维状的
- adj. `fibroid` 纤维性的；纤维状的
- adj. `fibrinous` 纤维蛋白的
- adj. `fibrocartilaginous` 纤维软骨的
- n. `fibroblast` [基医] 纤维原细胞；纤维组织母细胞
- n. `fibroid` 子宫肌瘤；[肿瘤] 纤维瘤
- n. `fibrinolysin` 溶纤维蛋白酵素
- n. `fibrinolysis` [生化] 纤维蛋白溶解
- n. `fibrinopeptide` [生化] 血纤维蛋白肽
- n. `fibroadenoma` 纤维性瘤
- n. `fibrocartilage` [解剖] 纤维软骨
- n. `fibroma` [肿瘤] 纤维瘤
- n. `fibrositis` [内科] 纤维组织炎

# 短语

- `pulmonary fibrosis` 肺纤维化
- `cystic fibrosis` 囊胞性纤维症

# 例句

The fibrosis around a breast implant may produce deformity and pain in some women.
乳腺植入物周围的纤维化可能在某些妇女中引起变形与疼痛。

HSCs are the major cell type involved in liver fibrosis, which is the formation of scar tissue in response to liver damage.
肝星状细胞是一种与肝纤维化密切相关的主要的细胞种类，它在肝受到损伤时形成瘢痕组织。

In workplace settings sulfuric acid mists lead to pulmonary fibrosis, and some have suggested lung cancer as another outcome.
在工厂设施中，硫酸尘雾导致肺纤维化。 一些人已经表示肺癌是它的另一后果。
