# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'dʒest/`
- 美 `/ɪn'dʒɛst/`

# 词义

v. 咽下，吸收
`When animals or plants ingest a substance, they take it into themselves, by eating or absorbing it`



in(进入) + gest(运， 载) → 运入 → 吸收

# 同义

vt. 摄取；咽下；吸收；接待
`absorb` `receive`

# 同根

- n. `ingestion` 摄取；吸收；咽下

# 例句

...side effects occurring in fish that ingest this substance.
...在吃了此物质的鱼身上产生的副作用。
