# 音标

- 英 `/ˌsegrɪ'geɪʃn/`
- 美 `/'sɛgrɪ'geʃən/`

# 词义

n. 隔离，分离
`when people of different races, sexes, or religions are kept apart so that they live, work, or study separately`



来自segregate(v. 使隔离)

# 同义

n. 隔离，[遗]分离；种族隔离
`separation` `insulation` `isolation`

# 同根

- adj. `segregated` 被隔离的
- adj. `segregationist` 种族隔离主义的；种族隔离主义者的
- n. `segregationist` 种族隔离主义者
- n. `segregator` [机] 分离器；分离者
- v. `segregated` 隔离（segregate的过去式）；分离
- vt. `segregate` 使隔离；使分离；在…实行种族隔离

# 短语

- `racial segregation` 种族隔离
- `gravity segregation` 重力偏析；密度偏析

# 例句

The Supreme Court unanimously ruled that racial segregation in schools was unconstitutional.
最高法院一致裁定学校实施的种族隔离措施违反宪法。
