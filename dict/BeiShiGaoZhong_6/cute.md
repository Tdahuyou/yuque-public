# 音标

- 英 `/kjuːt/`
- 美 `/kjʊt/`

# 词义

adj. 娇小可爱的
`Something or someone that is cute is very pretty or attractive, or is intended to appear pretty or attractive`

# 同义

adj. 可爱的；漂亮的；聪明的，伶俐的
`lovely` `pretty` `intelligent` `taking` `wise`

# 同根

- adv. `cutely` 精明地，伶俐地
- n. `cuteness` 娇小可爱

# 短语

- `cute girl` 可爱女孩

# 例句

Oh, look at that dog! He's so cute.
哦，看那条狗！它真可爱。
