# 音标

- 英 `/'vɪdɪə(ʊ)fəʊn/`
- 美 `/'vɪdɪofon/`

# 词义

n. 可视电话
`a type of telephone that allows you to see the person you are talking to on a screen`



video (视频) +phone (电话) →可视电话

# 同义

n. 电视电话
`picturephone`

# 例句

Your wristwatch videophone suddenly rings.
突然，你手表上的可视电话响了。
