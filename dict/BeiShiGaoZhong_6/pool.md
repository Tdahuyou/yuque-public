# 音标

- 英 `/puːl/`
- 美 `/pul/`

# 词义

n. 台球
`a game in which you use a stick to hit numbered balls into holes around a table, which is often played in bars`

# 同义

n. 联营；撞球；水塘；共同资金
`billiard ball`
