# 音标

- 英 `/'wʌrɪd/`
- 美 `/'wɝrɪd/`

# 词义

`unhappy because you keep thinking about a problem, or about something bad that might happen`

# 同义

adj. 担心的
`afraid` `fraught`

# 同根

- adj. `worrying` 令人担心的；烦恼的
- adv. `worriedly` 担心地；焦虑地
- n. `worry` 担心；烦恼；撕咬
- v. `worrying` 担心（worry的ing形式）
- vi. `worry` 担心；烦恼；撕咬
- vt. `worry` 担心；发愁；折磨

# 短语

- `worried about` 为…担心
- `feel worried` 感到担忧；感到忧虑

# 例句

She gave me a worried look.
她担心地看了我一眼。

His parents must be worried sick (= extremely worried ) .
他的父母肯定担心死了。
