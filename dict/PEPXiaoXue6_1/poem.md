# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊɪm/`
- 美 `/'poəm/`

# 词义

`a piece of writing that expresses emotions, experiences, and ideas, especially in short lines using words that  rhyme (= end with the same sound ) `

# 同义

n. 诗
`poetry` `verse`

# 短语

- `love poem` 爱情诗
- `narrative poem` 叙事诗；叙述诗
- `prose poem` n. 散文诗
- `symphonic poem` 交响诗

# 例句

You need to touch up the poem.
你需要给这首诗润色一下。

The poem was all the rage then.
这首诗在当时十分流行。

His poem compares poorly with yours.
他的诗做得不如你的好。
