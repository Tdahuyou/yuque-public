# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒæzmɪn; 'dʒæs-/`
- 美 `/'dʒæzmɪn/`

# 词义

`a plant that grows up a wall, frame etc and has small sweet-smelling white or yellow flowers`

# 同义

n. 茉莉；淡黄色
`pikake` `straw yellow`

# 短语

- `jasmine tea` 茉莉花茶；香片
- `jasmine flower` 茉莉花茶，茉莉花
- `winter jasmine` 迎春花；冬茉莉
- `cape jasmine fruit` 栀子；栀子粉末
