# 音标

- 英 `/lʌst/`
- 美 `/lʌst/`

# 词义

n. 欲望;
`very strong sexual desire, especially when it does not include love`

# 同义

n. 性欲；强烈的欲望
`sexual desire` `libido`

vi. 贪求，渴望
`rare` `aspire`

# 同根

- adj. `lustful` 好色的；贪欲的；渴望的

# 短语

- `lust for` 贪求

# 例句

My feelings for Lauren were pure lust.
我对劳伦的感觉纯粹是肉欲。
