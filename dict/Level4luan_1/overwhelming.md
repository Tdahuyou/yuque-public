# 音标

- 英 `/ˌəʊvəˈwelmɪŋ/`
- 美 `/,ovɚ'wɛlmɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 难以抗拒的
`having such a great effect on you that you feel confused and do not know how to react`

# 同义

adj. 压倒性的；势不可挡的
`inundatory` `preponderant`

v. 压倒；淹没（overwhelm的ing形式）；制服
`drowning`

# 同根

- adv. `overwhelmingly` 压倒性地；不可抵抗地
- vt. `overwhelm` 压倒；淹没；受打击

# 短语

- `overwhelming superiority` 绝对优势

# 例句

an overwhelming sense of guilt
一阵不能自制的内疚感

She felt an overwhelming desire to hit him.
她恨不得揍他一顿。

She found the city quite overwhelming when she first arrived.
她初来时觉得这座城市令她不知所措。
