# 音标

- 英 `/ɔ:’θentik/`
- 美 `/ɔ'θɛntɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 真实的
`a painting, document, book etc that is authentic has been proved to be by a particular person`

# 同义

adj. 真正的，真实的；可信的
`actual` `sincere` `genuine` `true` `real`

# 同根

- adv. `authentically` 真正地，确实地；可靠地
- n. `authentication` 证明；鉴定；证实
- n. `authenticity` 真实性，确实性；可靠性
- vt. `authenticate` 鉴定；证明…是真实的

# 例句

an authentic work by Picasso
毕加索的真迹
