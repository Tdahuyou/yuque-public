# 音标

- 英 `/ˈmɜ:sɪfəli/`
- 美 `/'mɝsɪfəli/`

# 词义

adv. 幸运地；仁慈地
`fortunately or luckily, because a situation could have been much worse`

# 同义

adv. 仁慈地
`benevolently` `kindly`

# 同根

- adj. `merciful` 仁慈的；慈悲的；宽容的
- adj. `merciless` 残忍的；无慈悲心的
- adv. `mercilessly` 残忍地；毫无慈悲地
- n. `mercy` 仁慈，宽容；怜悯；幸运；善行

# 例句

Mercifully, I managed to stop the car just in time.
幸亏我及时刹住了车。

The journey was mercifully brief.
幸而旅途很短。
