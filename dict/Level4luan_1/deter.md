# 音标

- 英 `/dɪˈtɜ:(r)/`
- 美 `/dɪ'tɝ/`

# 词义

v. 阻止，制止
`to stop someone from doing something, by making them realize it will be difficult or have bad results`

# 同义

vt. 制止，阻止；使打消念头
`dispute` `block` `restrain`

# 同根

- n. `determent` 制止；威慑

# 例句

The company’s financial difficulties have deterred potential investors.
该公司的财务困境令潜在的投资者望而却步。
