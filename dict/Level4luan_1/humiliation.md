# 音标

- 英 `/hju:ˌmɪlɪ'eɪʃn/`
- 美 `/hjʊ,mɪlɪ'eʃən/`

# 词义

n. 丢脸
`a feeling of shame and great embarrassment, because you have been made to look stupid or weak`

# 同义

n. 丢脸，耻辱；蒙羞；谦卑
`lost face` `stigma`

# 同根

- adj. `humiliated` 羞辱的
- adj. `humiliating` 丢脸的；羞辱性的
- n. `humility` 谦卑，谦逊
- v. `humiliated` 屈辱（humiliate的过去式）；使…丢脸，使…蒙羞
- v. `humiliating` 使蒙耻（humiliate的ing形式）
- vt. `humiliate` 羞辱；使…丢脸；耻辱

# 例句

the humiliation of having to ask her parents for money
不得不开口伸手向她父母要钱的难堪
