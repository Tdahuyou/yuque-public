# 音标

- 英 `/ˌhaɪərəˈglɪfɪks/`
- 美 `/,haɪərə'ɡlɪfɪks/`

# 词义

n. 象形字
`pictures and symbols used to represent words or parts of words, especially in the ancient Egyptian writing system`

# 同义

n. 象形文字；难以辨认或理解的文字（hieroglyphic的复数形式）
`pictographs`
