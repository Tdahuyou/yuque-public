# 音标

- 英 `/kənˈteɪdʒəs/`
- 美 `/kən'tedʒəs/`

# 词义

adj. 有感染力的；会蔓延的
`if a feeling, attitude, or action is contagious, other people are quickly affected by it and begin to have it or do it`

# 同根

- adv. `contagiously` 传染性地；蔓延地
- n. `contagion` 传染病；蔓延；触染

# 短语

- `contagious disease` 接触传染病

# 例句

her contagious enthusiasm
她那富有感染力的热情
