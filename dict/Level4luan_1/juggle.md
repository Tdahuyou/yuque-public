# 音标

- 英 `/ˈdʒʌgl/`
- 美 `/'dʒʌɡl/`

# 词义

v. 同时应付
`to try to fit two or more jobs, activities etc into your life, especially with difficulty`

# 同义

vi. 玩杂耍；欺骗；歪曲
`kid` `pull a fast one`

vt. 歪曲；欺骗
`colour` `sell` `fox`

n. 玩戏法；欺骗
`fraud` `deception`

# 同根

- adj. `juggling` 欺诈的；变戏法的；欺骗的
- n. `juggling` 杂耍；欺骗（等于jugglery）
- n. `juggler` 变戏法的人；骗子
- n. `jugglery` 戏法；杂耍；欺骗
- v. `juggling` 玩杂耍（juggle的ing形式）

# 短语

- `juggle with` 欺骗；歪曲；玩弄
