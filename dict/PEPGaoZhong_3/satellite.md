# 音标

- 英 `/'sætəlaɪt/`
- 美 `/'sætəlaɪt/`

# 词义

n. 人造卫星；卫星
`a machine that has been sent into space and goes around the Earth, moon etc, used for radio, television, and other electronic communication`

# 同义

n. [天]卫星；[航]人造卫星；随从；卫星国家
`moon` `suite`
