# 例句

Obon has a family-reunion element, as the Japanese will return to their ancestral homes to be reunited with relatives living and deceased.
因为在节日期间，日本人会回到祖籍和那些活着及死去的亲属团聚，盂兰盆节也有了家庭团聚的因素。
