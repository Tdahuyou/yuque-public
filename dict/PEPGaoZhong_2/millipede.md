# 音标

- 英 `/'mɪlɪpiːd/`
- 美 `/'mɪlɪpid/`

# 词义

n. (等同于millepede)千足虫
`a long thin creature with a very large number of legs`



milli(千) + ped(脚) + e → 千足虫

# 例句

Because of their ability to create cyanide, the millipede smells like almonds (the common odor of cyanide).
因为它们可以产生氰化物，所以这种千足虫带有杏仁味（氰化物的普遍特征）。
