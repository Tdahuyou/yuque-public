# 音标

- 英 `/'wɪləʊ/`
- 美 `/'wɪlo/`

# 词义

n. [林]柳树；[木]柳木制品
`a type of tree that has long thin branches and grows near water, or the wood from this tree`

adj. 柳木制的


# 同义

n. [林]柳树；[木]柳木制品
`Salix`

# 短语

- `willow tree` 柳树
- `weeping willow` [植]垂柳；[俚]枕头

# 例句

He worships that big willow south of the house.
他很崇拜房子南面的那颗大柳树。
