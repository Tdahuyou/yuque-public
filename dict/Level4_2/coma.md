# 音标

- 英 `/'kəʊmə/`
- 美 `/'komə/`

# 词义

n. [医]昏迷；[天]彗形像差
`someone who is in a coma has been unconscious for a long time, usually because of a serious illness or injury`

# 同义

n. [医]昏迷；[天]彗形像差
`stupor` `exanimation`

# 短语

- `in a coma` 陷于昏迷
- `diabetic coma` 糖尿病性昏睡；糖尿病昏迷症

# 例句

She was in a coma for seven weeks.
她昏迷了七周。
