# 音标

- 英 `/riː'prɪnt/`
- 美 `/,ri'prɪnt/`

# 词义

v. 再版；重印
`to print a book, story, newspaper article etc again`

n. 重印；翻版
`an occasion when more copies of a book are printed because all the copies of it have been sold`

# 同义

vt. 再版；[图情][印刷]重印
`republish`

n. [图情][印刷]重印；翻版
`reimpression`

# 例句

It remained an exceptionally rare book until it was reprinted in 1918.
在1918年再版之前，这本书一直非常罕见。
