# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'dʒʌstɪfaɪd/`
- 美 `/ʌn'dʒʌstɪfaɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 不正当的；未被证明其正确的
`If you describe a belief or action as unjustified, you think that there is no good reason for having it or doing it`

# 同义

adj. 不正当的；未被证明其正确的
`wrongful` `illicit`

# 例句

Your report last week was unfair. It was based upon wholly unfounded and totally unjustified allegations.
你上周的报告是不公正的，它基于毫无事实根据和完全无正当理由的指控。
