# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'maɪndə/`
- 美 `/rɪ'maɪndɚ/`

# 词义

n. 暗示；提醒的人/物；催单
`something, for example a letter, that reminds you to do something which you might have forgotten`

# 同义

n. 暗示；提醒的人/物；催单
`connotation` `implication` `allusion`

# 同根

- adj. `remindful` 提醒的；留意的
- vt. `remind` 提醒；使想起

# 例句

a reminder from the dentist for your check-up
牙医寄来的检查提醒函
