# 音标

- 英 `/skrəʊl/`
- 美 `/skrol/`

# 词义

n. 卷轴，画卷；名册；卷形物
`a long piece of paper that can be rolled up, and is used as an official document`

v. 成卷形


# 同义

n. 卷轴，画卷；名册；卷形物
`book` `roll`

# 短语

- `scroll bar` [计]滚动条
- `scroll down` 向下滚动；向下卷动
- `silk scroll` 绢本
- `scroll painting` 画轴，卷轴画
- `horizontal scroll bar` [计]水平滚动条
- `scroll case` 螺旋形箱
- `scroll up` 向上滚动

# 例句

Ancient scrolls were found in caves by the Dead Sea.
在死海边的洞穴里发现了古代的卷轴。
