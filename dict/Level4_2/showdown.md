# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃəʊdaʊn/`
- 美 `/'ʃodaʊn/`

# 词义

n. 摊牌；紧要关头；最后一决胜负
`a meeting, argument, fight etc that will settle a disagreement or competition that has continued for a long time`

# 同义

n. 摊牌；紧要关头；最后一决胜负
`tight spot` `tight corner`

# 例句

They may be pushing the president toward a final showdown with his party.
他们可能会逼迫总统与其政党做最后的较量。
