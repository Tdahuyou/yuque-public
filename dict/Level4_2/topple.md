# 音标

- 英 `/'tɒp(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'tɑpl/`

# 词义

v. 倾倒；倒塌；摇摇欲坠
`to become unsteady and then fall over, or to make something do this`

n. (Topple)人名；(英)托佩尔


# 同义

vi. 倾倒；倒塌；摇摇欲坠
`collapse` `founder`

vt. 推翻；颠覆；使倒塌
`collapse` `upset`

# 例句

He just released his hold and toppled slowly backwards.
他就松手，慢慢地向后倒下了。
