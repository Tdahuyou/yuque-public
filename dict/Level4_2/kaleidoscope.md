# 音标

- 英 `/kə'laɪdəskəʊp/`
- 美 `/kə'laɪdəskop/`

# 词义

n. 万花筒；千变万化
`a pattern, situation, or scene that is always changing and has many details or bright colours`

# 同根

- adj. `kaleidoscopic` 千变万化的；万花筒似的

# 例句

...the vivid kaleidoscope of colours displayed in the plumage of the peacock.
...孔雀羽毛的五彩缤纷。
