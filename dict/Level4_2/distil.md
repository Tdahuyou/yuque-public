# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/dis'til/`

# 词义

v. 蒸馏；提炼；渗出
`If a liquid such as whisky or water is distilled, it is heated until it changes into steam or vapour and then cooled until it becomes liquid again. This is usually done in order to make it pure. `

# 同义

vi. 蒸馏；提炼；渗出
`still` `try out`

vt. 蒸馏；提取…的精华；使渗出
`still` `fine-still`

# 同根

- n. `distillation` 蒸馏，净化；蒸馏法；精华，蒸馏物
- n. `distillate` 精华；蒸溜物
- n. `distiller` 蒸馏器；蒸馏酒者
- n. `distillery` 酿酒厂；蒸馏室
- n. `distillment` 蒸馏；蒸馏物；蒸馏法；精华（等于distilment或者distillation）
- vi. `distill` 蒸馏；滴下；作为精华产生（等于distil）
- vt. `distill` 提取；蒸馏；使滴下

# 例句

The whisky had been distilled in 1926 and sat quietly maturing until 1987.
这瓶威士忌是1926年被蒸馏的，存放至1987年酿造成熟。
