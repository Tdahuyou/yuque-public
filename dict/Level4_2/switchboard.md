# 音标

- 英 `/'swɪtʃbɔːd/`
- 美 `/'swɪtʃbɔrd/`

# 词义

n. 配电盘；接线总机
`a system used to connect telephone calls in an office building, hotel etc, or the people who operate the system`

# 同义

n. [电]配电盘；接线总机
`distribution panel` `cabinet panel`

# 同根

- n. `switching` [电] 开关；转换；整流；配电
- v. `switching` 转换（switch的ing形式）

# 短语

- `telephone switchboard` 电话交换台，电话交换机
- `switchboard operator` 接线员

# 例句

switchboard operators
电话总机接线员

Hundreds of callers jammed the switchboard trying to win the tickets (= there were too many calls for the switchboard to deal with ) .
几百个电话打进来想赢取门票，总机应接不暇。
