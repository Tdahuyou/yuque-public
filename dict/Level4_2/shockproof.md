# 词义

adj. 防震的；防电击的
`a watch, machine etc that is shockproof is designed so that it is not easily damaged if it is dropped or hit`

# 同义

adj. [建]防震的；[电]防电击的
`vibration-proof`

# 例句

Although you can go swimming with this shockproof and water-resistant watch, be sure to dry the USB connector before plugging it into your PC.
这款腕表能够防震、防水，你可以戴着它去游泳，但是，回来之后你必须把USB的接口弄干才可以插到你的个人电脑上。
