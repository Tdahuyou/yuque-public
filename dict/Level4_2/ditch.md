# 音标

- 英 `/dɪtʃ/`
- 美 `/dɪtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 在…上掘沟；把…开入沟里；丢弃
`to stop having something because you no longer want it`

n. 沟渠；壕沟
`a long narrow hole dug at the side of a field, road etc to hold or remove unwanted water`

# 同义

vt. 在…上掘沟；把…开入沟里；丢弃
`reject` `slough off`

vi. 开沟；掘沟
`furrow`

n. 沟渠；壕沟
`conduit` `sike`

# 短语

- `oxidation ditch` 氧化沟
- `foundation ditch` 基坑；基础沟
- `last ditch` 最后防线，最后手段
- `drainage ditch` n. 排水沟
- `to the last ditch` ◎直到最后，直至最终
- `irrigation ditch` 灌溉水渠；农渠

# 例句

The government has ditched plans to privatise the prison.
政府放弃了监狱私有化的计划。
