# 音标

- 英 `/'θʌmtæk/`
- 美 `/'θʌmtæk/`

# 词义

n. 图钉
`a short pin with a flat top that is used for fixing notices on walls`

v. 用图钉钉住


# 同义

n. 图钉
`pushpin` `drawing pin` `thumb pin`
