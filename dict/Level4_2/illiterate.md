# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'lɪt(ə)rət/`
- 美 `/ɪ'lɪtərət/`

# 词义

adj. 文盲的；不识字的；没受教育的
`someone who is illiterate has not learned to read or write`

n. 文盲
`someone who has not learned to read or write`

# 同义

adj. 文盲的；不识字的；没受教育的
`letterless` `unlettered`

n. 文盲
`unschooled` `analphabetic`

# 同根

- n. `illiteracy` 文盲；无知

# 例句

It was an illiterate letter, full of mistakes.
这封信文笔拙劣，错误连篇。
