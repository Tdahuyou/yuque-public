# 音标

- 英 `/'trʌmpɪt/`
- 美 `/'trʌmpɪt/`

# 词义

n. 喇叭；喇叭声


v. 吹喇叭；吹嘘
`to tell everyone about something that you are proud of, especially in an annoying way`

# 同义

n. 喇叭；喇叭声
`horn` `loudspeaker`

vt. 吹喇叭；吹嘘
`crow about` `brag about`

vi. 吹喇叭；发出喇叭般的声音
`blow job`

# 同根

- n. `trumpeter` 吹鼓手；号兵

# 例句

They are proudly trumpeting the fact that they are creating more jobs.
他们得意地宣扬他们是在创造更多的工作岗位。
