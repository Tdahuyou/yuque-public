# 音标

- 英 `/'tekstaɪl/`
- 美 `/'tɛkstaɪl/`

# 词义

n. 纺织品，织物
`any type of woven cloth that is made in large quantities, used especially by people in the business of making clothes etc`

adj. 纺织的


# 同义

n. [纺]纺织品，织物
`cloth` `web`

adj. [纺]纺织的
`spinning`
