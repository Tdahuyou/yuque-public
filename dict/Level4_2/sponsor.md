# 音标

- 英 `/'spɒnsə/`
- 美 `/'spɑnsɚ/`

# 词义

n. 赞助者；主办者；保证人
`someone who officially agrees to help someone else, or to be responsible for what they do`

v. 赞助；发起
`to give money to a sports event, theatre, institution etc, especially in exchange for the right to advertise`

# 同义

n. 赞助者；主办者；保证人
`friend` `guarantee` `entrepreneur`

vt. 赞助；发起
`launch` `support of`

# 同根

- n. `sponsorship` 赞助；发起；保证人的地位；教父母身份

# 例句

You cannot get a work visa without an American sponsor.
如果没有美国担保人就拿不到工作签证。
