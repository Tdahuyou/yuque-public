# 音标

- 英 `/'gærɪs(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'gærəsn/`

# 词义

n. 要塞；卫戍部队
`a group of soldiers living in a town or fort and defending it`

v. 驻防；守卫
`to send a group of soldiers to defend or guard a place`

# 同义

n. 要塞；卫戍部队
`stronghold` `presidio`

vt. 驻防；守卫
`sentinel`

# 短语

- `in garrison` 驻防的，执行卫戍任务的

# 例句

The garrison was called out when news of the enemy’s advance was received.
收到敌人推进的情报后，卫戍部队奉命出动。

a garrison town
有部队驻防的城镇
