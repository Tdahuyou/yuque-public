# 音标

- 英 `/'laɪvstɒk/`
- 美 `/'laɪvstɑk/`

# 词义

n. 牲畜；家畜
`animals such as cows and sheep that are kept on a farm`

# 同义

n. 牲畜；[畜牧]家畜
`cattle` `dumb things`

# 短语

- `livestock breeding` 畜牧业，畜牧饲养；家畜繁育
- `livestock farming` 畜牧业；养畜业
- `livestock farm` 畜牧场；禽畜饲养场；牧场

# 例句

The heavy rains and flooding killed scores of livestock.
大雨和洪水淹死了许多家畜。
