# 音标

- 英 `/'priːfɪks/`
- 美 `/'prifɪks/`

# 词义

n. 前缀
`a group of letters that is added to the beginning of a word to change its meaning and make a new word, such as ‘un’ in ‘untie’ or ‘mis’ in ‘misunderstand’`

v. 加前缀；将某事物加在前面
`to add a prefix to a word, name, or set of numbers`

# 例句

To telephone from the U.S. use the prefix 011 33 before the numbers given here.
从美国拨出电话，请在所给号码前加拨前置代码011 33。
