# 音标

- 英 `/'dæz(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'dæzl/`

# 词义

n. 耀眼的光；灿烂


v. 使……目眩；使……眼花
`if a very bright light dazzles you, it stops you from seeing properly for a short time`

# 同义

n. 耀眼的光；灿烂
`effulgence` `flashiness`

vi. 眼花缭乱；炫耀
`show off` `brag about`

# 同根

- adj. `dazzling` 耀眼的；眼花缭乱的
- adj. `dazzled` 目眩的；眼花撩乱的
- adv. `dazzlingly` 耀眼地；灿烂地
- adv. `dazedly` 头昏眼花地；恍惚地；眼花缭乱地
- v. `dazzling` 使…眼花（dazzle的ing形式）
- v. `dazzled` （使）眼花（dazzle的过去式）

# 例句

And, though the gleams blind and dazzle, yet do they convey a hint of beauty and serenity greater than we have known or imagined.
虽然这光芒令人眼花缭乱，但它确实给予我们一种不曾经历和无法想象的美感和静谧的启示。

But strong flashes do more than just dazzle one's friends—they distort the quality of the picture.
不过强闪光不仅仅是闪得被拍的朋友眼花缭乱——强闪光也会使照片失真变形。
