# 音标

- 英 `/riː'pleɪ/`
- 美 `/'riple/`

# 词义

v. 重放；重演；重新比赛
`to play a game again because neither team won the first time`

n. 重赛；重播；重演
`a game that is played again because neither team won the first time`

# 同义

vt. [电视][广播]重放；重演；重新比赛
`re-act`

n. 重赛；[电视]重播；重演
`re-run`

# 例句

The match will be replayed on Wednesday.
比赛将在星期三再进行一次。
