# 音标

- 英 `/gʊd'wɪl/`
- 美 `/'gʊd'wɪl/`

# 词义

n. [贸易]商誉；友好；好意
`kind feelings towards or between people and a willingness to be helpful`

# 同义

n. [贸易]商誉；友好；好意
`kindness` `amity`

# 短语

- `goodwill ambassador` 亲善大使，友好大使

# 例句

A fund was set up as a goodwill gesture to survivors and their families.
成立了基金会，以向幸存者及其家属作出善意的表示。
