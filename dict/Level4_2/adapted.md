# 音标

- 英 `/ə'dæptid/`
- 美 `/ə'dæptɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 适合的
`If something is adapted to a particular situation or purpose, it is especially suitable for it`

v. 使适应，改编（adapt的过去式）


# 同义

adj. 适合的
`calculated` `propitious`

v. 使适应，改编（adapt的过去式）
`accommodated`

# 短语

- `adapted from` 改编自
- `get adapted to` 习惯于

# 例句

The camel's feet, well adapted for dry sand, are useless on mud.
骆驼的脚十分适合干旱沙地，但在泥地上毫无用处。
