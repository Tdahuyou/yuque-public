# 音标

- 英 `/'suːtɪd/`
- 美 `/'sju:tid/`

# 词义

adj. 合适的
`If something is well suited to a particular purpose, it is right or appropriate for that purpose. If someone is well suited to a particular job, they are right or appropriate for that job. `

v. 适合（suit的过去分词）


# 同义

adj. 合适的
`becoming` `shaped` `applicable` `likely` `possible`

# 同根

- adj. `suitable` 适当的；相配的
- adv. `suitably` 适当地；相配地；适宜地
- n. `suit` 诉讼；组；套装；恳求
- n. `suitability` 适合；适当；相配
- n. `suiting` 西装料；一身西服
- v. `suiting` 适宜；相配；供给…衣服（suit的ing形式）
- vi. `suit` 合适；相称
- vt. `suit` 适合；使适应

# 短语

- `be suited for` 适合做…；适合于…

# 例句

The area is well suited to road cycling as well as off-road riding.
这个地区很适合公路自行车竞赛和越野骑马。
