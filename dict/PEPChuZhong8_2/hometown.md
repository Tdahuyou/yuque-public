# 音标

- 英 `/'həʊm'taʊn/`
- 美 `/'homtaʊn/`

# 词义

n. 家乡，故乡
`Someone's hometown is the town where they live or the town that they come from`



home(家)+town(乡镇)→家乡

# 同义

n. 家乡；故乡
`country` `native land`

# 例句

There's a subway in my hometown, but there was none two years ago.
我的家乡现在有了地铁，可两年之前还没有呢。

She expressed the wish to be interred in the graveyard of hometown.
她表达了死后葬在故乡的墓地上的愿望。
