# 音标

- 英 `/'sɪzəz/`
- 美 `/'sɪzɚz/`

# 词义

n. 剪刀，剪子
`a tool for cutting paper, cloth etc, made of two sharp blades fastened together in the middle, with holes for your finger and thumb`



sciss(分开， 分裂) ＋ ors → 能把物品分开的东西 → 剪刀

# 同义

n. [五金]剪刀；剪式跳法
`forfex` `cutting tools`

# 同根

- n. `scissor` 剪刀
- vt. `scissor` 用剪刀剪；删去

# 例句

a pair of scissors
一把剪刀
