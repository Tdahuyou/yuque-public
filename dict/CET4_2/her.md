# 音标

- 英 `/hɜː/`
- 美 `/hɚ/`

# 词义

pron. (宾格)她；她的
`used to refer to a woman, girl, or female animal that has already been mentioned or is already known about`

# 例句

Jane? I don’t really know her.
简？我不太认识她。

Margaret wants me to go with her.
玛格丽特想让我和她一起去。

Give her the keys.
把钥匙给她。

I think it was her, but I’m not sure.
我觉得是她，但不肯定。
