# 音标

- 英 `/gaʊn/`
- 美 `/ɡaʊn/`

# 词义

n. 长袍，长外衣
`a long loose piece of clothing worn for special ceremonies by judges, teachers, lawyers, and members of universities`

# 同义

n. 长袍，长外衣；礼服；睡袍；法衣
`cope` `robe`

# 短语

- `wedding gown` 结婚礼服；新娘礼服
- `evening gown` n. 女子夜礼服
- `dressing gown` 晨衣，便袍
- `bridal gown` 新娘服装，新娘礼服
- `cap and gown` n. 方帽长袍
- `ball gown` 舞会袍
- `silk gown` [英国英语]
