# 音标

- 英 `/kən'dens/`
- 美 `/kən'dɛns/`

# 词义

v. 压缩，使缩短
`to make something that is spoken or written shorter, by not giving as much detail or using fewer words to give the same information`



con ＋ dense(密集的) → 变得密集的 → 压缩

# 同义

vi. 浓缩；凝结
`concentrate` `to freeze`

vt. 使浓缩；使压缩
`dewater` `inspissate`

# 同根

- adj. `condensate` 浓缩的
- n. `condensation` 冷凝；凝结；压缩
- n. `condenser` 冷凝器；[电] 电容器；[光] 聚光器
- n. `condensate` 冷凝物；浓缩物
- n. `condensing` 冷凝
- v. `condensing` 冷凝；压缩（condense的ing形式）

# 短语

- `condense into` 把…缩短；把…压缩

# 例句

the mist which condensed on every cold surface
每个冰冷的表面上凝结的水汽
