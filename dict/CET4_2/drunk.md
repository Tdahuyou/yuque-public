# 音标

- 英 `/drʌŋk/`
- 美 `/drʌŋk/`

# 词义

adj. 醉的；陶醉的
`unable to control your behaviour, speech etc because you have drunk too much alcohol`



喝(drink)多了自然会醉(drunk)

# 同义

adj. 喝醉了的
`loaded` `buffy`

# 同根

- adj. `drunken` 喝醉的；酒醉的；常醉的
- adj. `drinkable` 可饮用的；可以喝的
- n. `drink` 酒，饮料；喝酒
- n. `drinking` 喝，饮；喝酒
- n. `drinkability` 酒劲
- n. `drinkable` 饮料（常用复数）
- n. `drinker` 酒徒；喝的人；酒豪
- v. `drinking` 饮（drink的ing形式）；喝
- vi. `drink` 喝酒；饮水；干杯
- vt. `drink` 喝，饮；吸收；举杯庆贺

# 短语

- `get drunk` 喝醉
- `drunk driving` 酒后开车

# 例句

You’re drunk.
你喝醉了。

David would get drunk and I would have to take him home and put him to bed.
戴维常会喝醉，我就得把他弄回家放到床上。
