# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'nʌf/`
- 美 `/ɪ'nʌf/`

# 词义

adj. 足够的
`Enough is also an adjective`

adv. 足够地
`to the degree that is necessary or wanted`

# 同义

adv. 足够地，充足地
`adequately` `sufficiently`

n. 很多；充足
`amplitude` `ton` `plenty of`

adj. 充足的
`adequate` `plenitudinous`

# 短语

- `enough for` 足够地，充分的；对……足够的
- `good enough` 足够好的
- `old enough` 年龄足够大
- `more than enough` 绰绰有余；太， 过于
- `sure enough` 果然，果真
- `enough to do` 足以…
- `soon enough` 很快
- `enough is enough` 够了；适可而止
- `fair enough` 有道理；说得对；敢情好
- `strangely enough` 说来也奇怪（用作插入语）
- `enough of it` 够了；停止吧
- `oddly enough` 说来也奇怪
- `have enough of` 受够了
- `enough said` 无须多说，毋庸赘言
- `have enough to do` 有很大的困难，需要全力以赴
- `enough and to spare` 绰绰有余，足够而且有余

# 例句

Her disappearance and death would give proof enough of Charles' guilt.
她的失踪和死亡将提供足够的证据证明查尔斯有罪。
