# 音标

- 英 `/sweə/`
- 美 `/swɛr/`

# 词义

v. 宣(誓)
`to make a public official promise, especially in a court of law`

v. 诅咒
`to use rude and offensive language`

# 同义

vt. 发誓；咒骂
`plight one's troth` `cross my heart`

vi. 发誓，宣誓；诅咒
`plight one's troth` `take an oath`

n. 宣誓；诅咒
`juration` `oath`

# 同根

- adj. `sworn` 发过誓的；宣誓过的
- n. `swearing` 发誓；咒骂
- n. `swearer` 宣誓者；咒骂者
- v. `sworn` 发誓（swear的过去分词）
- v. `swearing` 发誓；咒骂（swear的ing形式）

# 短语

- `swear by` 对…发誓；[口]极其信赖
- `swear an oath` 宣誓
- `swear on` v. 凭…发誓
- `swear in` 使宣誓就职
- `swear at` 诅咒；咒骂；与…不协调
- `swear off` 发誓戒除；放弃
- `swear for` 保证；担保
- `swear black is white` 硬把黑的说成是白的，固执谬见，强词夺理
- `swear fealty` 发誓效忠

# 例句

Remember that you have sworn an oath and so must tell the truth.
记住你已经宣过誓了，所以必须讲真话。

Presidents must swear allegiance to the US constitution.
总统必须宣誓效忠美国宪法。
