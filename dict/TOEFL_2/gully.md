# 音标

- 英 `/'gʌlɪ/`
- 美 `/'ɡʌli/`

# 词义

n. 小峡谷,排水沟
`a small narrow valley, usually formed by a lot of rain flowing down the side of a hill`

# 同义

n. [地理]冲沟；水沟
`slot` `gulch`

vi. 形成沟
`gutter`

# 同根

- n. `gulch` 峡谷；冲沟；干谷峡谷

# 短语

- `gully erosion` 冲沟侵蚀；挖深浸蚀；雏谷侵蚀

# 例句

The bodies of the three climbers were located at the bottom of a steep gully.
3名登山者的尸体在一个陡峭的隘谷谷底找到了。
