# 音标

- 英 `/,pri:di'tə:mind/`
- 美 `/,pridɪ'tɝmɪnd/`

# 词义

adj. 预订的
`decided or arranged before something happens, so that it does not happen by chance`

# 同根

- n. `predetermination` 预先决定；原有意图
- vt. `predetermine` 预先确定；预先决定；预先查明

# 例句

The prince's destiny was predetermined from the moment of his birth.
该王子的命运从出生那一刻起就已经被决定了。
