# 音标

- 英 `/'festɪv/`
- 美 `/'fɛstɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 庆祝的,喜庆的
`looking or feeling bright and cheerful in a way that seems suitable for celebrating something`

# 同义

adj. 节日的；喜庆的；欢乐的
`convivial`

# 同根

- adj. `festival` 节日的，喜庆的；快乐的
- adj. `festal` 节日的；喜庆的，欢乐的
- n. `festival` 节日；庆祝，纪念活动；欢乐
- n. `festivity` 欢庆，欢宴；庆典；欢乐

# 短语

- `festive season` 欢乐季节
- `festive time` 节日期间

# 例句

The atmosphere was festive and jolly.
气氛喜庆欢乐。

John was obviously in a festive mood .
约翰显然喜气洋洋的。
