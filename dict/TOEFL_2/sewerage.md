# 音标

- 英 `/'suːərɪdʒ; 'sjuː-/`
- 美 `/'suərɪdʒ/`

# 词义

n. 排水设备,污水
`the system by which waste material and used water are carried away in sewers and then treated to stop it being harmful`

# 同义

n. 污水；排水设备
`wastewater` `polluted water`

# 同根

- n. `sewer` 下水道；阴沟；裁缝师
- vi. `sewer` 清洗污水管
- vt. `sewer` 为…铺设污水管道；用下水道排除…的污水

# 短语

- `sewerage system` 排水系统；污水工程系统

# 例句

...without access to any services such as water or sewerage.
...不能享受任何如供水或污水处理的服务。
