# 音标

- 英 `/ɪnte'staɪn(ə)l; ɪn'testɪn(ə)l/`
- 美 `/ɪn'tɛstɪnl/`

# 词义

adj. 肠的
`Intestinal means relating to the intestines`

# 同义

adj. 肠的
`enteral`

# 同根

- adj. `intestine` 内部的；国内的
- n. `intestine` 肠

# 短语

- `intestinal obstruction` 肠梗阻
- `intestinal tract` [医]肠道
- `intestinal flora` 肠菌类
- `intestinal metaplasia` 肠上皮化生
- `intestinal bacteria` 肠道细菌
- `intestinal wall` 肠壁，小肠壁
- `intestinal cancer` 肠癌
- `intestinal canal` 肠道；肠管

# 例句

Cholera is an acute intestinal infection caused by ingestion of food or water contaminated with the bacterium Vibrio cholerae.
霍乱是由摄入受霍乱弧菌这种杆菌污染的食物或水引起的一种急性肠道感染。

At these clinics, the health workers treat people for diseases such as diarrhoea, respiratory infections, anaemia and intestinal worms.
在这些诊所里，卫生工作者为人们治疗腹泻、呼吸道感染、贫血和肠道蠕虫一类疾病。

They required silver in return for tea, without which, some believed, western barbarians would go blind and develop intestinal tumours.
他们用茶叶换取白银，一些人相信，没有茶，西方的野蛮人就会失明，并患肠道肿瘤。
