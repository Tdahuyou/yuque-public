# 词义

adv. 不隐瞒地,公然地
`in a way that does not hide your feelings, opinions, or the facts`

# 同义

adv. 公开地；公然地；坦率地
`directly` `in public` `publicly` `straight` `barely`

# 例句

Sarah talked openly about her problems.
萨拉毫不掩饰地说起自己的问题。

He was openly critical of his colleagues.
他公开批评自己的同事。
