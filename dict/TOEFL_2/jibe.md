# 音标

- 英 `/dʒaɪb/`
- 美 `/dʒaɪb/`

# 词义

v. 嘲笑,与一致,使转向
`To jibe means to say something rude or insulting that is intended to make another person look foolish`

# 同义

vi. 嘲笑
`laugh at` `make a mockery of`

vt. 嘲笑
`laugh at` `deride`

n. 嘲笑
`derision` `sarcasm`

# 短语

- `jibe with` 与…一致；符合…

# 例句

"No doubt he'll give me the chance to fight him again," he jibed.
“毫无疑问，他会给我机会与他再打一次。”他嘲讽道。
