# 音标

- 英 `/rɪft/`
- 美 `/rɪft/`

# 词义

n. 裂口
`a situation in which two people or groups have had a serious disagreement and begun to dislike and not trust each other`

v. 裂开


# 同义

n. [木]裂缝；不和；裂口
`crack` `split`

vt. [地质]使断裂；使分开
`separate` `unhinge`

vi. 裂开
`gap` `seam` `spring off`

# 短语

- `rift valley` 裂谷；地堑
- `rift zone` 裂谷带；断裂带
- `great rift` 大裂缝

# 例句

He set out to heal the rifts in the party.
他着手弥合党内的裂痕。
