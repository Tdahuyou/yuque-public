# 音标

- 英 `/'kruːsɪb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'krʊsəbl/`

# 词义

n. 坩锅,严酷的考验
`Crucible is used to refer to a situation in which something is tested or a conflict takes place, often one which produces something new`

# 同义

n. [冶]坩埚；严酷的考验
`copple` `xble`

# 短语

- `graphite crucible` 石墨坩埚
- `porcelain crucible` 瓷坩埚

# 例句

...a system in which ideas are tested in the crucible of party contention.
...一个在严峻党派纷争中检验不同观点的体系。
