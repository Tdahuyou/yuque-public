# 音标

- 英 `/'si:,kəust/`
- 美 `/'si,kost/`

# 词义

n. 海滨
`land bordering on the sea; a coast `

# 同义

n. [海洋]海岸，沿岸；海滨
`coast` `rivage`

# 例句

Armenia lacks a seacoast or a port.
亚美尼亚没有海岸和港口。

The group's boat capsized in rough seas as the pirates raced away from patrolling warships off the coast of Somalia. 3 other pirates aboard the boat were rescued on the nearby seacoast.
据悉，他们在躲避索马里海域巡逻的军舰时遇到风浪，船被打翻，船上的另外3名海盗在附近海岸获救。
