# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'krɪtɪk(ə)l/`
- 美 `/,ʌn'krɪtɪkl/`

# 词义

adj. 无批判力的,不加批判的
`unable or unwilling to see faults in something or someone – used to show disapproval`

# 同根

- adv. `uncritically` 不加鉴别地；不加批评地

# 例句

...the conventional notion of women as uncritical purchasers of heavily advertised products.
...妇女是对广告轰炸宣传的商品不批判的购买者这一传统观点。
