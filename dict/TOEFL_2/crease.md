# 音标

- 英 `/kriːs/`
- 美 `/kris/`

# 词义

n. 折缝，皱褶v(使)起皱
`a line on a piece of cloth, paper etc where it has been folded, crushed, or pressed`

# 同义

n. 折痕；折缝
`fell` `fold`

vi. 起皱
`wrinkle`

vt. 弄皱；使起折痕
`crush` `crumple`

# 短语

- `crease mark` 摺痕；折痕

# 例句

She smoothed the creases from her skirt.
她捋平裙子上的皱褶。

I’ll have to iron out the creases.
我得把皱褶熨平。
