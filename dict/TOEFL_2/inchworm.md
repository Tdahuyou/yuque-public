# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪn(t)ʃwɜːm/`
- 美 `/'ɪntʃwɝm/`

# 词义

n. [昆]尺蠖


# 同义

n. [植保]尺蠖
`cankerworm` `spanworm` `loopworm`

# 例句

The adorable inchworm is really the caterpillar of the geometer moth.
可爱的尺蠖就是尺蠖毛虫。

If you have ever experienced the childlike delight of discovering an inchworm, you know how fun they are to watch.
如果你有过看到尺蠖露出孩子般灿烂笑脸的经历，你就知道观察尺蠖是多么有趣。

And with the inchworm, he flew to where other birds needed to be measured.
知更鸟带著尺蠖飞到其他鸟需要被测量的地方。
