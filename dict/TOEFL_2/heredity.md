# 音标

- 英 `/hɪ'redɪtɪ/`
- 美 `/hə'rɛdəti/`

# 词义

n. 遗传,形质遗传
`the process by which mental and physical qualities are passed from a parent to a child before the child is born`

# 同义

n. [遗]遗传，遗传性
`inheritance` `descendiblity`

# 同根

- adj. `hereditary` 遗传的；世袭的；世代相传的
- n. `hereditary` 遗传类
- n. `hereditarianism` 遗传主义；遗传论

# 例句

Heredity is not a factor in causing the cancer.
遗传并非导致这种癌症的一个因素。
