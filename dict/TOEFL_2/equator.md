# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'kweɪtə/`
- 美 `/ɪ'kwetɚ/`

# 词义

n. 赤道
`an imaginary line drawn around the middle of the Earth that is exactly the same distance from the North Pole and the South Pole`



equ(相等) + at + or → 将地球进行等分 → 赤道

# 同义

n. [天][地理]赤道
`equinoctial`

# 短语

- `celestial equator` [天]天球赤道（等于equator，equinoctial，equinoctial circle，equinoctial line）

# 例句

The United States is north of the equator.
美国位于赤道以北。

In the hot zone near the equator the sun heats the air continually; hence the air there ascends.
在靠近赤道的热带地区，太阳不断地加热那里的空气，于是那里的热空气就上升。
