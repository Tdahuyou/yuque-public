# 音标

- 英 `/'kɔːtʃɪp/`
- 美 `/'kɔ:tʃip/`

# 词义

n. 求爱,求爱时期
`the period of time during which a man and woman have a romantic relationship before marrying`

# 同义

n. 求爱；求婚；求爱期
`Passionate Proposing` `marriage proposal`

# 同根

- n. `court` 法院；球场；朝廷；奉承
- vi. `court` 求爱
- vt. `court` 招致（失败、危险等）；向…献殷勤；设法获得
