# 音标

- 英 `/helθ/`
- 美 `/hɛlθ/`

# 词义

n. 健康
`A person's health is the condition of their body and the extent to which it is free from illness or is able to resist illness`

# 同义

n. [医]健康；卫生；保健；兴旺
`fitness` `hygiene` `wellness`

# 同根

- [n. `healthfulness` 于健康有益；卫生
- adj. `healthy` 健康的，健全的；有益于健康的
- adj. `healthful` 健康的；有益健康的；卫生的
- adv. `healthily` 健康地
- n. `healthcare` 医疗保健；健康护理，健康服务；卫生保健
- n. `healthiness` 健康；健全
