# 音标

- 英 `/'miːnwaɪl/`
- 美 `/'minwaɪl/`

# 词义

adv. 其间
`in the period of time between two events`

# 同义

adv. 同时，其间
`together` `simul`

# 短语

- `in the meanwhile` 同时；在此期间

# 例句

The flight will be announced soon. Meanwhile, please remain seated.
很快就要广播通知登机，在此期间请继续留在座位上。

I knew I wouldn’t get my exam results for several weeks, and I wasn’t sure what to do in the meanwhile.
我知道我要几星期后才能拿到考试成绩，我不知道这期间该做什么。
