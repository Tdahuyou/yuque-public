# 音标

- 英 `/nə'reɪt/`
- 美 `/næret/`

# 词义

v. 叙述,作解说
`to explain what is happening in a film or television programme as part of the film or programme`

# 同义

vt. 叙述；给…作旁白
`relate` `recount`

vi. 叙述；讲述
`tell about` `tell of`

# 同根

- adj. `narrative` 叙事的，叙述的；叙事体的
- n. `narrative` 叙述；故事；讲述
- n. `narrator` 叙述者；解说员
- n. `narration` 叙述，讲述；故事

# 例句

a wildlife film narrated by David Attenborough
一部由大卫•艾登堡解说的野生动物影片
