# 音标

- 英 `/'steɪdʒkəʊtʃ/`
- 美 `/'stedʒkotʃ/`

# 词义

n. 公共马车
`a vehicle pulled by horses that was used in past times for carrying passengers and letters`



stage(驿站) + coach(马车) → 驿站马车， 公共马车

# 同义

n. 公共马车；驿站马车
`dawk`

# 例句

The Pony Express was much faster than the stagecoach.
驿马快信要比当时的公共马车快多了。
