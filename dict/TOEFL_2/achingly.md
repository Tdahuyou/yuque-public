# 词义

adv. 极其,非常痛地
`You can use achingly for emphasis when you are referring to things that create feelings of wanting something very much, but of not being able to have it`

# 同义

adv. 极其；痛惜地；非常痛地
`extremely` `most` `rare`

# 例句

...three achingly beautiful ballads.
...三首美得令人心痛的民谣。
