# 音标

- 英 `/,kæti'gɔrikli/`
- 美 `/,kæti'gɔrikli/`

# 词义

adv. 断然地,明确地
`in such a sure and certain way that there is no doubt`

# 同义

adv. 绝对地；直截了当地
`absolutely` `utterly`

# 同根

- adj. `categorical` 绝对的（名词categoricalness，副词categorically，异体字categoric）；直接了当的；无条件的；属于某一范畴的

# 例句

Can you state categorically that her death was caused by lack of food?
你能明确地说她是饿死的吗？
