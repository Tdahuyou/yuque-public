# 音标

- 英 `/elk/`
- 美 `/ɛlk/`

# 词义

n. 麋鹿
`a very large brown North American, European, and Asian animal with wide flat horns`

# 同义

n. 麋鹿；软鞣粗皮；驼鹿皮革
`milu` `wapiti`

# 例句

I covered the elk with a tarp and sat down near it, wrapping myself in blankets.
我用一块防水布盖住了麋鹿，然后用毯子把自己裹起来，坐在它旁边。

When the elk got up, the tarp slid off.
麋鹿站起来的时候，防水布从它身上滑落。
