# 音标

- 英 `/skɑː/`
- 美 `/skɑr/`

# 词义

n. 伤痕,疤痕
`a permanent mark that is left on your skin after you have had a cut or wound`

v. 结疤,使留下伤痕
`if a wound or cut scars you, it leaves a permanent mark on your body`



s + car(汽车) → 被汽车撞了一下， 留下伤痕 → 疤痕

# 同义

vt. 伤害；给留下伤痕
`knife` `injure`

vi. 结疤；痊愈
`cure` `heal up`

n. 创伤；伤痕
`wound` `trauma`

# 同根

- adj. `scared` 害怕的
- v. `scared` 使害怕（scare的过去分词）
- vt. `scarify` 在…上划痕；乱划；严厉批评

# 短语

- `scar tissue` [医]瘢痕组织

# 例句

He had a long, curved scar on his right cheek.
他的右脸上有一道弯弯的长疤。

a deep cut that could leave a permanent scar
可能会留下永久性疤痕的一道很深的伤口
