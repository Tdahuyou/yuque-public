# 音标

- 英 `/'saɪən/`
- 美 `/'saɪən/`

# 词义

n. 蓝绿色,青色
`a highly saturated green-blue that is the complementary colour of red and forms, with magenta and yellow, a set of primary colours `

# 同义

n. 蓝绿色
`aquamarine blue` `blue-green`

adj. 蓝绿色的
`glaucous` `turquoise`
