# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/rɪ'lɪdʒəsli/`

# 词义

adv. 虔诚地,笃信地
`If you do something religiously, you do it very regularly because you feel you have to`

# 同义

adv. 虔诚地，笃信地
`piously` `prayerfully`

# 同根

- adj. `religious` 宗教的；虔诚的；严谨的；修道的
- n. `religious` 修道士；尼姑
- n. `religiosity` 虔诚；宗教狂；虚伪信仰
- n. `religionism` 宗教狂，笃信宗教
- n. `religionist` 笃信宗教者；宗教家
- n. `religiousness` 虔敬，笃信

# 例句

Do these exercises religiously every day.
每天要认真地做这些练习。
