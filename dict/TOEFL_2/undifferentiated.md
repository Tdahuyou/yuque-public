# 音标

- 英 `/,ʌndɪfə'renʃɪeɪtɪd/`
- 美 `/'ʌndɪfə'rɛnʃɪ'etɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 无差别的,一致的
`something which is undifferentiated is not split into parts, or has different parts but you cannot tell the difference between them`

# 同义

adj. 无差别的；一致的
`united` `consistent` `corresponding` `matching` `uniform`

# 例句

shows aimed at large undifferentiated groups of people
针对不加以细分的大众的节目

Colonial officials tended to see Indian society as an undifferentiated whole.
殖民地官员往往把印度社会看成一个统一整体。
