# 音标

- 英 `/'skaʊə/`
- 美 `/'skaʊɚ/`

# 词义

v. 四处搜索；洗涤
`If you scour something such as a place or a book, you make a thorough search of it to try to find what you are looking for`

# 同义

vi. 冲刷；擦；腹泻
`mop up` `wipe`

vt. 擦亮，洗涤；冲洗，清除
`wash` `rub up`

n. 擦，冲刷；洗涤剂；（畜类等的）腹泻
`swabbing` `brushup`

# 同根

- n. `scouring` 洗擦
- n. `scourer` 擦洗者，洗刷器；泻药；打光机

# 例句

Rescue crews had scoured an area of 30 square miles.
救援人员已经搜遍了30平方英里的范围。
