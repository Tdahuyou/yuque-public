# 音标

- 英 `/'geɪtweɪ/`
- 美 `/'ɡetwe/`

# 词义

n. 入口；通道
`the opening in a fence, wall etc that can be closed by a gate`

# 同义

n. [建]门；[计]网关；方法；通道；途径
`door` `system` `mechanism` `method` `approach`

# 短语

- `default gateway` 默认网关；缺省网关
- `border gateway` 边界网关（位于自治域之间的路由器）
- `border gateway protocol` 边界网关协议

# 例句

He walked across the park and through a gateway.
他走过那个公园，并穿过一个大门口。
