# 音标

- 英 `/lɑːk/`
- 美 `/lɑrk/`

# 词义

n. 云雀,百灵鸟
`a small brown singing bird with long pointed wings`

# 同义

n. 云雀；百灵鸟；欢乐
`joy` `festival`

vt. 愚弄
`befool` `assify`

# 短语

- `for a lark` 当作玩笑；为了取乐
- `rise with the lark` 早起

# 例句

It sounds like the singing of lark birds.
这歌声就像百灵鸟一样清脆。

Only the little lark never sang.
不过这只百灵鸟从来不歌唱。
