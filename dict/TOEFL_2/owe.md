# 音标

- 英 `/əʊ/`
- 美 `/o/`

# 词义

v. 欠(债等),感激
`to know that someone’s help has been important to you in achieving something`

# 同义

vt. 欠；感激；应给予；应该把……归功于
`appreciate` `be grateful to`

# 同根

- adj. `owing` 未付的；欠着的
- v. `owing` 欠；把…归功于（owe的ing形式）

# 例句

How much do I owe you (= often used to show that you want to pay for something )?
我该付你多少钱？
