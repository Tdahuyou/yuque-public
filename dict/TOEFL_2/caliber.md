# 音标

- 英 `/'kælibə/`
- 美 `/'kæləbɚ/`

# 词义

n. 口径,才干


# 同义

n. [军]口径；才干；水准（等于calibre）；器量
`standard` `savoir-faire`

# 同根

- n. `calibre` [军] 口径；才干；水准
- vt. `calibrate` 校正；调整；测定口径

Perhaps the first thing that students notice about their college is the caliber of their fellow students.
可能学生们对这所大学关注的第一件事就是同学的才干。

Also, the further an executive rises, the more he or she must deal with high-caliber people who know how to get what they want, are difficult, strong-willed and have a sharp appetite for power.
