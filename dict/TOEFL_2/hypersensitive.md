# 音标

- 英 `/haɪpə'sensɪtɪv/`
- 美 `/,haɪpɚ'sɛnsətɪv/`

# 词义

adj. 过份敏感的
`if someone is hypersensitive to a drug, substance etc, their body reacts very badly to it`

# 同义

adj. [医]过敏的；高灵敏度的；超感光度的
`irritable` `touchy`

# 同根

- n. `hypersensitivity` 过敏症；高灵敏度

# 例句

Student teachers were hypersensitive to any criticism of their performance.
实习教师对任何针对他们表现的批评意见都异常敏感。
