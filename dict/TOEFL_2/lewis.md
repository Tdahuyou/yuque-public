# 音标

- 英 `/'luːɪs/`
- 美 `/'lʊɪs/`

# 词义

n. [机]吊楔,起重爪
`a lifting device for heavy stone or concrete blocks consisting of a number of curved pieces of metal or wedges fitting into a dovetailed recess cut into the block `
