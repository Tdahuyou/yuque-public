# 音标

- 英 `/sai'ljuəriən; si-/`
- 美 `/sai'ljuəriən; si-/`

# 词义

adj. 〈地〉志留纪的
`of, denoting, or formed in the third period of the Palaeozoic era, between the Ordovician and Devonian periods, which lasted for 25 million years, during which fishes first appeared `

# 短语

- `silurian system` 志留系
- `silurian period` [地]志留纪
