# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/'kɔləlis/`

# 词义

adj. 无领的,无马轭(或颈圈)的
`a collarless jacket, shirt etc is one that does not have a collar`

# 同根

- n. `collar` 衣领；颈圈
- vt. `collar` 抓住；给…上领子；给…套上颈圈

# 例句

Originally the collarless shirt and trousers were nondescript, of flannel or wool.
起初无领的衬衫和长裤是没用固定特征的，或者是法兰绒的或者是羊毛的。

A slender forty-nine-year-old with salt-and-pepper hair and a fixed expression, he wore a dark suit with a collarless striped shirt.
马沙49岁，身材修长，头发斑白，脸上的表情一成不变。 他穿一套深色西装，里面是一件无领子的条纹T恤。
