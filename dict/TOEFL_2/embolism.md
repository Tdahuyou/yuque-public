# 音标

- 英 `/'embəlɪz(ə)m/`
- 美 `/'ɛmbə,lɪzəm/`

# 词义

n. 加闰日,栓塞
`something such as a hard mass of blood or a small amount of air that blocks a tube carrying blood through the body`

# 同根

- n. `embolus` [病理] 栓子；栓塞物

# 短语

- `pulmonary embolism` 肺栓塞；肺血管阻塞症
- `cerebral embolism` [医]脑栓塞
- `air embolism` [医]气泡栓塞
- `amniotic fluid embolism` 羊水栓塞
