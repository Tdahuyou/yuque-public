# 音标

- 英 `/'læntən/`
- 美 `/'læntɚn/`

# 词义

n. 灯笼，提灯
`a lamp that you can carry, consisting of a metal container with glass sides that surrounds a flame or light`

# 同义

n. 灯笼；提灯；灯笼式天窗
`scaldfish`

# 同根

- n. `lanternfish` 发光的鱼，灯笼鱼

# 短语

- `lantern festival` 元宵节，灯节
- `chinese lantern` 可以折叠起来的灯笼
- `palace lantern` 宫灯
- `lantern show` 灯展

# 例句

In the course of time, this kind of lantern has become an exclusive of the Shens.
后来， 这种灯笼成了沈家的绝活。
