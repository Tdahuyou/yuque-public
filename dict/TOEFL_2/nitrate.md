# 音标

- 英 `/'naɪtreɪt/`
- 美 `/'naɪtret/`

# 词义

n. [化]硝酸盐,硝酸钾
`used in the name of substances containing nitrogen and oxygen. Nitrates are often used to improve soil.`

# 同根

- n. `nitrite` 亚硝酸盐
- n. `niter` [矿物] 硝石；硝酸钠
- n. `nitre` 硝酸钾；硝石

- `silver nitrate` [化]硝酸银
- `nitrate nitrogen` 硝态氮；硝酸盐氮
- `sodium nitrate` 硝酸钠

- `calcium nitrate` 硝酸钙

- `lanthanum nitrate` 硝酸镧
- `zinc nitrate` 硝酸锌

- `nickel nitrate` 硝酸镍

- `nitrate ion` 硝酸根离子
