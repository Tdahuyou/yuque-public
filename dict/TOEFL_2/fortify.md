# 音标

- 英 `/'fɔːtɪfaɪ/`
- 美 `/'fɔrtɪfaɪ/`

# 词义

v. 增强(体力,结构等)
`to encourage an attitude or feeling and make it stronger`

v. 筑防御工事
`to build towers, walls etc around an area or city in order to defend it`



fort(强大) + ify(使…) → 使强大 → 加强

# 同义

vt. 加强；增强；（酒）的酒精含量；设防于
`enhance` `cement` `muscle` `build up`

# 同根

- n. `fort` 堡垒；要塞；（美）边界贸易站
- vi. `fort` 构筑要塞
- vt. `fort` 设要塞保卫

# 例句

Her position was fortified by election successes and economic recovery.
她的地位因为选举获胜及经济复苏而得到巩固。
