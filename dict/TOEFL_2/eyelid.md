# 音标

- 英 `/'aɪlɪd/`
- 美 `/'aɪ,lɪd/`

# 词义

n. 眼皮,眼睑
`a piece of skin that covers your eye when it is closed`

# 同义

n. [解剖]眼睑；眼皮
`palpebrae` `lid`

# 例句

His eyelids began to droop (= close, because he was sleepy ).
他的眼皮开始往下垂。

The room spun. Her eyelids fluttered (= moved up and down quickly ) and she fainted.
房间快速旋转起来。她的眼皮上下翻动，然后便昏了过去。
