# 音标

- 英 `/'wɪləʊ/`
- 美 `/'wɪlo/`

# 词义

n. 柳(树)
`a type of tree that has long thin branches and grows near water, or the wood from this tree`



把枕头(pillow)晒在柳树(willow)上

# 同义

n. [林]柳树；[木]柳木制品
`Salix`

# 短语

- `willow tree` 柳树
- `weeping willow` [植]垂柳；[俚]枕头

# 例句

The willow trees cast their shadows all around.
柳荫匝地。

He worships that big willow south of the house.
他很崇拜房子南面的那颗大柳树。

He has also captured pollen from alder, pine and willow.
他还从桤树、松树和柳树上捕捉到了花粉。
