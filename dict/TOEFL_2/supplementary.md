# 音标

- 英 `/,sʌplɪ'ment(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/,sʌplɪ'mɛntri/`

# 词义

adj. 增补的，补充的
`provided in addition to what already exists`

# 同义

adj. 补充的；追加的
`complementary` `adscititious`

n. 补充者；增补物
`enlargement`

# 同根

- adj. `supplemental` 补充的（等于supplementary）；追加的
- adj. `supplying` 供应的
- n. `supply` 供给，补给；供应品
- n. `supplement` 补充，补遗；补充物；附录
- n. `supplying` 供应；补给
- n. `supplementation` 补充；增补
- v. `supplying` 供应；补充；代理（supply的ing形式）
- vi. `supply` 供给；替代
- vt. `supply` 供给，提供；补充
- vt. `supplement` 补充，增补

# 短语

- `supplementary agreement` 补充协议；增补协议
- `supplementary food` 补料
- `supplementary civil action` 附带民事诉讼
- `supplementary pension` 企业年金；辅助退休金
- `supplementary contract` 补充契约
- `supplementary provision` 补充规定；追加拨款，增补拨款；追加备付款项

# 例句

supplementary information
补充信息
