# 音标

- 英 `/'dændɪlaɪən/`
- 美 `/'dændɪlaɪən/`

# 词义

n. 蒲公英
`a wild plant with a bright yellow flower which later becomes a white ball of seeds that are blown away in the wind`

# 同义

n. 蒲公英
`taraxacum`

# 例句

The wind puffed away the seeds of the dandelion.
风吹散了蒲公英的种子。

It looks like the head of a dandelion seed, you know, with all the lollipops coming out of it, and with a center in it, that we started out as the tweet.
它看起来像蒲公英的花朵，你知道的，从花朵中飞出的所有棒棒糖一样的种子都有一个中心，正如我们首发的那条微博。
