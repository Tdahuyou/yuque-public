# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'truːdə/`
- 美 `/ɪn'trudɚ/`

# 词义

n. 入侵者
`someone who illegally enters a building or area, usually in order to steal something`

# 同义

n. 侵入者；干扰者；妨碍者
`penetrator` `raider`

# 同根

- adj. `intrusive` 侵入的；打扰的
- n. `intrusion` 侵入；闯入
- n. `intrusiveness` 干涉性；侵扰性；侵入性
- vi. `intrude` 闯入；侵入；侵扰
- vt. `intrude` 把…强加；把…硬挤

# 例句

The police think the intruder got in through an unlocked window.
警察认为闯入者是从一扇没上锁的窗子进入的。
