# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪndɪəm/`
- 美 `/'ɪndiəm/`

# 词义

n. [化]铟
`a rare soft silvery metallic element associated with zinc ores: used in alloys, electronics, and electroplating`

# 短语

- `indium oxide` 氧化铟
