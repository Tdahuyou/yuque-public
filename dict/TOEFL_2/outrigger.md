# 音标

- 英 `/'aʊtrɪgə/`
- 美 `/'aʊt'rɪgɚ/`

# 词义

n. (承力外伸)支架
`a framework for supporting a pontoon outside and parallel to the hull of a boat to provide stability `

# 同义

n. 舷外支架；悬臂梁
`cantilever beam` `cantilever girder`

# 同根

- adj. `outrigged` 装有外伸支架的；[船] 带有舷外装置的

# 例句

The outrigger tripod is available in both a high and low profile version, to fulfill a diverse array of environmental requirements.
外伸的三脚架有高型和低型两种配置，以满足不同的环境要求。
