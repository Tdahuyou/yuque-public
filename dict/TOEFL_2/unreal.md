# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'rɪəl/`
- 美 `/,ʌn'riəl/`

# 词义

adj. 不真实的,虚幻的
`an experience, situation etc that is unreal seems so strange that you think you must be imagining it`

# 同义

adj. 不真实的；假的；幻想的；虚构的
`impossible` `untrue`

# 同根

- n. `unreality` 不真实；非现实

# 例句

It seemed unreal to be sitting and talking to someone so famous.
和这么一位大名鼎鼎的人坐在一起谈话似乎很不真实。
