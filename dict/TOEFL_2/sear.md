# 音标

- 英 `/sɪə/`
- 美 `/sɪr/`

# 词义

adj. 烤焦的,枯萎的


v. 烤焦,变干枯
`To sear something means to burn its surface with a sudden intense heat`

# 同义

vt. 烤焦；使…枯萎
`singe`

vi. 干枯；烧焦；凋谢
`sap rot` `air burn`

adj. 枯萎的；烤焦的
`blasted` `emarcid`

n. 烙印；烧焦痕迹
`brand` `burn`

# 例句

Grass fires have seared the land near the farming village of Basekhai.
草地野火烧焦了巴斯克亥农庄附近的土地。
