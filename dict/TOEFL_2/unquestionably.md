# 词义

adv. 无可非议地,确凿地
`used to emphasize that something is certainly true`

# 同义

adv. 无可非议地；确凿地
`justifiably`

# 同根

- adj. `unquestionable` 毫无疑问的；确实的；无可挑剔的

# 例句

The Eiffel Tower is unquestionably one of Paris’s most familiar landmarks.
埃菲尔铁塔无疑是巴黎最为人熟知的地标之一。
