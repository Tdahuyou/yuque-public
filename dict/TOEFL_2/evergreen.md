# 音标

- 英 `/'evəgriːn/`
- 美 `/'ɛvɚɡrin/`

# 词义

n. 常绿树
`a tree or bush that does not lose its leaves in winter`

adj. 常绿的
`an evergreen tree or bush does not lose its leaves in winter`

# 同义

adj. [林][植]常绿的；永葆青春的
`indeciduous`

# 短语

- `evergreen tree` 常青树；冬青树
- `evergreen forest` 常绿林
- `evergreen plant` 常绿植物

# 例句

Holly, like ivy and mistletoe, is an evergreen.
冬青与常春藤和槲寄生一样，也是一种常绿植物。
