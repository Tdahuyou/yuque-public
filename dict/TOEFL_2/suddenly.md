# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌd(ə)nlɪ/`
- 美 `/'sʌdənli/`

# 词义

adv. 突然地
`quickly and unexpectedly`

# 同义

adv. 突然地；忽然
`sharp` `abruptly`

# 同根

- adj. `sudden` 突然的，意外的；快速的
- n. `sudden` 突然发生的事
- n. `suddenness` 意外；突然

# 例句

I suddenly realized that there was someone following me.
我突然意识到有人跟着我。

George died very suddenly.
乔治死得非常突然。

Suddenly, the eagle opened its wings.
突然，鹰展开翅膀。
