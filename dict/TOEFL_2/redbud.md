# 音标

- 英 `/'redbʌd/`
- 美 `/'rɛd,bʌd/`

# 词义

n. 紫荆属植物
`an American leguminous tree, Cercis canadensis, that has heart-shaped leaves and small budlike pink flowers `

# 例句

The production of flowers and fruits on the older branches or trunks of woody plants, such as the redbud, and many mostly tropical plants, including cacao.
木本植物，如紫荆以及包括可可在内的许多热带植物，其老枝或树干上生出的花朵或果实。
