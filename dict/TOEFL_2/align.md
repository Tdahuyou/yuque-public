# 音标

- 英 `/ə'laɪn/`
- 美 `/ə'laɪn/`

# 词义

v. 排列，使结盟
`If you align yourself with a particular group, you support them because you have the same political aim`



a + lign(看做line， 线) → 排成一条线 → (使)结盟

# 同义

vt. 使结盟；使成一行；匹配
`to match`

vi. 排列；排成一行
`arrange` `form`

# 同根

- n. `alignment` 队列，成直线；校准；结盟

# 短语

- `align with` v. 与……结盟

# 例句

When war broke out, they aligned themselves with the rebel forces.
战争爆发以后，他们与叛军结了盟。
