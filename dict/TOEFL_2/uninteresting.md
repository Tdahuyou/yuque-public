# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'ɪnt(ə)rɪstɪŋ/`
- 美 `/ʌn'ɪntrəstɪŋ/`

# 词义

adj. 无趣味的，乏味的
`If you describe something or someone as uninteresting, you mean they have no special or exciting qualities`

# 同义

adj. 无趣味的，乏味的；令人厌倦的
`stupid` `flavourless`

# 例句

Their media have earned the reputation for being rather dull and uninteresting.
他们的媒体得到的名声是乏味无聊。
