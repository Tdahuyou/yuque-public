# 音标

- 英 `/wɪm/`
- 美 `/wɪm/`

# 词义

n. 一时的兴致,奇想
`a sudden feeling that you would like to do or have something, especially when there is no important or good reason`

# 同义

n. 奇想；一时的兴致；怪念头；幻想
`vagary` `fancy`

# 同根

- n. `whimsy` 怪念头；反复无常
- n. `whimsey` 奇想；怪念头

An idle whim, I fear, sir,' was my answer; `or else an idle whim is going to spirit me away.
