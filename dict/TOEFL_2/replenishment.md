# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'plenɪʃmənt/`
- 美 `/rɪ'plɛnɪʃmənt/`

# 词义

n. 补给,补充
`Replenishment is the process by which something is made full or complete again`

# 同义

n. [摄]补充，补给
`recruitment` `supply` `renewal`

# 同根

- vt. `replenish` 补充，再装满；把…装满；给…添加燃料

# 例句

There is a concern about replenishment of the population.
有人担心人口的补充问题。
