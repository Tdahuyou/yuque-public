# 音标

- 英 `/kəʊ'ɜːs/`
- 美 `/ko'ɝs/`

# 词义

v. 强制
`to force someone to do something they do not want to do by threatening them`

# 同义

vt. 强制，迫使
`pressure` `enforce`

# 同根

- adj. `coercive` 强制的；胁迫的；高压的
- n. `coercion` 强制；强迫；高压政治；威压

# 例句

The real magic in MochiKit.DOM is its willingness to flexibly coerce various types of objects into the right types during method calls, including doing so recursively.
DOM 真正的魔法在于它在方法调用过程中积极而灵活地把各种对象类型强制转换成正确的类型，包括在递归过程中。
