# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'daʊm(ə)nt; en-/`
- 美 `/ɪn'daʊmənt/`

# 词义

n. 捐赠,天资,捐款
`a sum of money given to a college, hospital etc to provide it with an income, or the act of giving this money`

# 同义

n. [经]捐赠；捐助；捐款；天资
`talent` `donation`

# 同根

- vt. `endow` 赋予；捐赠；天生具有

# 短语

- `endowment insurance` 养老保险
- `natural endowment` 先天禀赋；天然条件
- `factor endowment` 要素禀赋；生产要素禀赋；具备的生产要素，天赋要素
- `endowment fund` 留本基金

# 例句

...the National Endowment for the Arts.
…美国艺术基金会。
