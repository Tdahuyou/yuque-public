# 音标

- 英 `/gleɪz/`
- 美 `/ɡlez/`

# 词义

v. 变呆滞
`if your eyes glaze over, they show no expression, usually because you are very bored or tired`

v. 上釉
`to cover plates, cups etc made of clay with a thin liquid that gives them a shiny surface`

n. 釉(料)
`a liquid that is used to cover plates, cups etc made of clay to give them a shiny surface`



师傅给陶器上釉(glaze)的时候， 双眼盯着(gaze)陶器， 生怕出错

# 同义

n. [陶瓷]釉；光滑面
`glost` `glair`

# 同根

- adj. `glazed` 光滑的；像玻璃的；上过釉的；呆滞无神的
- n. `glazer` 上釉工人；加光工人；上光机；抛光轮
- n. `glazier` 装玻璃的工人；上釉工人
- v. `glazed` 上釉（glaze的过去分词）；装以玻璃

# 短语

- `colored glaze` 色釉，彩釉；色彩釉
- `ceramic glaze` n. 釉
- `coloured glaze` 琉璃；色釉，彩釉
- `glaze over` （指眼睛）呆滞

# 例句

Sometimes his eyes would glaze over for a second or two.
有时候他会两眼发直地愣一两秒钟。
