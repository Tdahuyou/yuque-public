# 音标

- 英 `/'gɑːlənd/`
- 美 `/'gɑrlənd/`

# 词义

n. 花环vt戴花环
`a ring of flowers or leaves, worn on your head or around your neck for decoration or for a special ceremony`

# 同义

n. 花环
`lei` `anadem`

# 短语

- `judy garland` 朱迪·加兰

# 例句

They wore blue silk dresses with cream sashes and garlands of summer flowers in their hair.
她们穿着配有米色腰带的蓝色丝质连衣裙，头上戴着夏季的花儿编成的花环。
