# 音标

- 英 `/prɪ'kɜːsə/`
- 美 `/pri'kɝsɚ/`

# 词义

n. 先驱
`something that happened or existed before something else and influenced its development`



pre(在…前面) + curs(跑) + or → 跑在前面的 → 先驱

# 同义

n. 先驱，前导
`forerunner` `vaunt-courier`

# 同根

- adj. `precursory` 前兆的（等于precursive）；先驱的

# 短语

- `precursor chemicals` 易制毒化学品

# 例句

Error is often the precursor of what is correct.
错误常常是正确的先导。

Viewed as a precursor to a wider open data movement, it could be as important as any we have seen in the web era.
它被视为更宽泛的数据开放运动的先导，它将像我们在互联网时代看到的所有重要的事物一样重要。
