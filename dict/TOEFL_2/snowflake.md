# 音标

- 英 `/'snəʊfleɪk/`
- 美 `/'snoflek/`

# 词义

n. 雪花
`a small soft flat piece of frozen water that falls as snow`



snow(雪) + flake(薄片) → 雪花， 雪片

# 同义

n. [植][气象]雪花；雪片莲；雪巫
`galanthus nivalis`

# 例句

Star join supports snowflake schema as well.
星型连接也支持雪花模式。

Trying to create a "template" for social media is like building a snowflake factory - at best, you´re boring, and at worst, you fall apart before you even hit the ground.
试着建立一个社区媒体模板，因为它就像建立一个雪花工厂一样，至多是你觉得厌烦了；最坏也就是你在落地前就融化解体了。
