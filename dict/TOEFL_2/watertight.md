# 音标

- 英 `/'wɔːtətaɪt/`
- 美 `/'wɔtɚtaɪt/`

# 词义

adj. 不漏水的,无懈可击的
`an argument, plan etc that is watertight is made very carefully so that people cannot find any mistakes in it`

# 同义

adj. 水密的；[建]不漏水的；无懈可击的
`invulnerable` `unexceptionable`

# 例句

Lucky for him, his alibi is watertight.
算他走运，不在犯罪现场的证据无懈可击。

Unless the ban is watertight, EU laws will overturn it.
这条禁令除非是十分严密，否则会被欧盟法律推翻。
