# 音标

- 英 `/səb'saɪd/`
- 美 `/səb'saɪd/`

# 词义

v. 下沉,平静
`if a feeling, pain, sound etc subsides, it gradually becomes less and then stops`

# 同义

vi. 平息；减弱；沉淀；坐下
`fall` `settle` `lower` `cool` `deposit`

# 同根

- n. `subsidence` 下沉；沉淀；陷没
- n. `subsiding` 下沉
- v. `subsiding` 下沉；沉淀（subside的ing形式）
- vi. `subsidise` 消退；下沉；平息
- vt. `subsidise` 资助（等于subsidize）；给...补助金

# 例句

Simon waited until the laughter subsided.
西蒙一直等到笑声平息下来。

The pains in his head had subsided, but he still felt dizzy and sick.
他的头痛已经减轻，但仍感到眩晕和恶心。
