# 音标

- 英 `/'meɪnstriːm/`
- 美 `/'menstrim/`

# 词义

n. 主流
`the most usual ideas or methods, or the people who have these ideas or methods`



main(主要的) + stream(溪， 流) → 主流

# 同义

n. 主流
`artery` `main river`

# 例句

Genet started as a rebel, but soon became part of the literary mainstream.
热内起初很叛逆，但不久就回归了文学主流。
