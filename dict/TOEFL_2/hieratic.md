# 音标

- 英 `/,haɪə'rætɪk/`
- 美 `/'haɪə'rætɪk/`

# 词义

adj. 僧侣的,僧侣用的
`of or relating to priests `

# 同义

adj. 僧侣用的；僧侣的
`monkish` `sacerdotal`

# 同根

- adj. `hieratical` 僧侣的；僧侣用的

# 例句

As for composers, modernists like Béla Bartók were hieratic and formalist, and postmodernists, like John Adams, were playful and interested in deconstructing.
