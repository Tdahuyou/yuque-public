# 音标

- 英 `/'tenən/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. [木工]榫
`an end of a piece of wood, that has been cut to fit exactly into a  mortise  in order to form a strong joint`

vt. 接榫,造榫
`to form a tenon on (a piece of wood) `

# 短语

- `mortise and tenon` 阴阳榫
- `tenon and mortise` 榫臼；雌雄准

# 例句

This tenon won't fit.
这个榫头对不上。
