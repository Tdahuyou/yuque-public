# 音标

- 英 `/dʒɜː'meɪnɪəm/`
- 美 `/dʒɝ'menɪəm/`

# 词义

n. 锗
`a brittle crystalline grey element that is a semiconducting metalloid, occurring principally in zinc ores and argyrodite: used in transistors, as a catalyst, and to strengthen and harden alloys`
