# 音标

- 英 `/heɪl/`
- 美 `/hel/`

# 词义

n. 雹
`frozen raindrops which fall as hard balls of ice`

v. 下雹,高呼
`if it hails, small balls of ice fall like rain`



冰雹(hail)让航行(sail)计划流产(fail)了

# 同义

n. [气象]冰雹；致敬；招呼；一阵
`precipitation` `blast`

int. 万岁；欢迎
`welcome` `rah`

# 短语

- `hail from` （车、船）来自；（人）出生于
- `hail storm` 冰雹风暴

# 例句

heavy showers of rain and hail
夹着冰雹的阵阵大雨
