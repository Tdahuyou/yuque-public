# 音标

- 英 `/ɪntə'neɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/,ɪntə'neʃən/`

# 词义

n. 语调,声调
`the way in which the level of your voice changes in order to add meaning to what you are saying, for example by going up at the end of a question`

# 同义

n. [声]声调，[语]语调；语音的抑扬
`parlance`

# 同根

- vt. `intonate` 吟咏；给…一种特殊声调

# 例句

intonation patterns
语调类型
