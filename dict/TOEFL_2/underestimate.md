# 音标

- 英 `/ʌndər'estɪmeɪt/`
- 美 `//ˌʌndərˈestɪmeɪt//`

# 词义

vt& n. 估计不足，低估
`to think or guess that something is smaller, cheaper, easier etc than it really is`

# 同义

vt. 低估；看轻
`disappreciate` `sell short`

n. 低估
`disappreciation`

# 同根

- n. `underestimation` 低估；过低之估价

# 例句

Fourteen percent may be an underestimate.
14%可能是低估的。
