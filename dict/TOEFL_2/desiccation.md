# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/,dɛsɪ'keʃən/`

# 词义

n. 干燥
`the process of becoming completely dry`

# 同义

n. 干燥
`drying` `dryness` `aridness`

# 同根

- adj. `desiccant` 去湿的，使干燥的
- adj. `desiccated` 干的（等于desiccate）；粉状的
- n. `desiccant` [助剂] 干燥剂
- v. `desiccated` 使干燥（desiccate的过去式）；变干
- vi. `desiccate` 变干
- vt. `desiccate` 使干；干贮

# 例句

...the disastrous consequences of the desiccation of the wetland.
...该湿地干燥的灾难性后果。
