# 音标

- 英 `/'flɪpə/`
- 美 `/'flɪpɚ/`

# 词义

n. 鳍状肢
`a flat part on the body of some large sea animals such as  seals  ,that they use for swimming`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]鳍状肢；鳍；烤饼；胖听罐头
`fin` `fim`

adj. 无礼的
`rude` `impolite`

# 同根

- adj. `flip` 无礼的；轻率的
- n. `flip` 弹；筋斗
- n. `flippancy` 轻率；无礼；言语尖刻
- vi. `flip` 用指轻弹；蹦跳
- vt. `flip` 掷；轻击

# 例句

Not only that, but when a wave threatened to return the seal to danger, the humpback used its massive flipper (at five metres, the longest in the animal kingdom) to nudge it back on.
不仅如此，当一个浪头即将把海豹扫回险境之时，座头鲸还利用它的巨大的鳍状肢（五米长，动物王国中最长的）把它轻轻地推了回来。
