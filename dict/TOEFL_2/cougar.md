# 音标

- 英 `/'kuːgə/`
- 美 `/'kuɡɚ/`

# 词义

n. [动]美洲狮(尤指Felisconcolor)
`a large brown wild cat from the mountains of western North America and South America`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]美洲狮，美洲豹（特指Felis concolor）
`mountain lion` `leopard`

# 例句

Then Mrs. McAlister grabbed a shovel in the yard and swung it at the cougar.
于是阿里斯特太太抓起后院的一把铁锹对着美洲狮挥来挥去。

Finally, the cougar ran off and Mrs. McAlister held her children and was thankful they were safe.
最后美洲狮跑开了，阿里斯特太太抱着她的孩子，很欣慰他们都平安无事。

Now they ought to be listed with other endangered species like the Eastern cougar and Kemp's Ridley turtle.
如今，它们应该同濒临灭绝东部美洲狮和肯氏龟一起列入濒危物种的名录。
