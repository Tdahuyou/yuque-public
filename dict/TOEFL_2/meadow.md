# 音标

- 英 `/'medəʊ/`
- 美 `/'mɛdo/`

# 词义

n. 草地,牧场
`a field with wild grass and flowers`

# 同义

n. [畜牧]草地；牧场
`grass` `field` `lawn`

# 短语

- `alpine meadow` 高山草甸
- `meadow soil` 草甸土；湿草原土

# 例句

The meadow has been ploughed up.
那片草地已用犁翻过。

The colt bounded through the meadow.
那匹马驹跳着跑过牧场。

There are many lambs capering in the meadow.
很多小羊在草地上蹦蹦跳跳。
