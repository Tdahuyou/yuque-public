# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌt(ə)ltɪ/`
- 美 `/'sʌtlti/`

# 词义

n. 微妙,精明
`the quality that something has when it has been done in a clever or skilful way, with careful attention to small details`

# 同义

n. 微妙；敏锐；精明
`delicacy` `acuity`

# 同根

- adj. `subtle` 微妙的；精细的；敏感的；狡猾的；稀薄的
- adv. `subtly` 精细地；巧妙地；敏锐地
- vi. `subtilize` 过分精细；微妙化
- vt. `subtilize` 使稀薄；使精细

# 例句

The play lacks subtlety.
这部戏不够精细。

She argued her case with considerable subtlety.
她非常巧妙地为自己的论点辩护。
