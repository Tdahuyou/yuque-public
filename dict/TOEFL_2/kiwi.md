# 音标

- 英 `/'kiːwiː; -wɪ/`
- 美 `/'kiwi/`

# 词义

n. [鸟]几维(一种新西兰产的无翼鸟)
`a New Zealand bird that cannot fly`

# 同义

n. 猕猴桃；几维（一种新西兰产的无翼鸟）；新西兰人
`actinidia` `Chinese gooseberry`

# 短语

- `kiwi fruit` 猕猴桃；奇异果

# 例句

Other sources of vitamin E include wheat germ oil, dry almond, safflower oil, hazelnut, peanut butter, spinach, broccoli, kiwi, and mango.
