# 音标

- 英 `/flaʊt/`
- 美 `/flaʊt/`

# 词义

vt&n. 轻视,嘲笑
`If you flout something such as a law, an order, or an accepted way of behaving, you deliberately do not obey it or follow it`

# 同义

n. 嘲笑；轻视；愚弄
`despite` `derision`

vt. 嘲笑；藐视；愚弄
`scoff at` `laugh at`

vi. 嘲笑；表示轻蔑
`laugh at` `make a mockery of`

# 例句

...illegal campers who persist in flouting the law.
…一向无视法律的非法露营者。
