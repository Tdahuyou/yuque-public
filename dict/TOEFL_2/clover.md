# 音标

- 英 `/'kləʊvə/`
- 美 `/'klovɚ/`

# 词义

n. [植]三叶草
`a small plant, usually with three leaves on each stem. If you find one with four leaves, it is thought to bring you luck.`

# 同义

n. [植]三叶草；苜蓿；红花草
`trifolium` `shamrock`

# 同根

- n. `cloverleaf` 四叶苜蓿形的立体交叉公路

# 短语

- `in clover` 生活优裕
- `red clover` 红三叶草
- `white clover` 白三叶；白车轴草
- `four-leaf clover` 四叶苜蓿（等于four-leaved clover）；幸运草
- `sweet clover` 草木樨；草木犀属

# 例句

a four-leaf clover
四叶（车轴）草
