# 音标

- 英 `/æl'biːdəʊ/`
- 美 `/æl'bido/`

# 词义

n. [天文](星体)反照率
`the ratio of the intensity of light reflected from an object, such as a planet, to that of the light it receives from the sun `

# 同义

n. （行星等的）[核][天]反射率；星体反照率
`reflectivity` `reflection power`
