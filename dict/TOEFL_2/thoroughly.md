# 音标

- 英 `/ˈθʌrəli/`
- 美 `/ˈθʌrəʊli; θʌroli/`

# 词义

adv. 十分地,彻底地
`carefully, so that nothing is forgotten`

# 同义

adv. 彻底地，完全地
`entirely` `completely` `totally` `fully` `absolutely` `strictly`

# 同根

- adj. `thorough` 彻底的；十分的；周密的
- adj. `thoroughgoing` 完全的，彻底的
- n. `thoroughness` 十分，完全；彻底

# 短语

- `rinse thoroughly` 彻底清洗
- `thoroughly remould oneself` 脱胎换骨

# 例句

The room had been thoroughly cleaned.
房间彻底打扫过了。
