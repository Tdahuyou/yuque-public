# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'selfɪʃ/`
- 美 `/ʌn'sɛlfɪʃ/`

# 词义

adj. 无私的,慷慨的
`caring about other people and thinking about their needs and wishes before your own`

# 同义

adj. 无私的，慷慨的
`generous` `handsome` `selfless` `liberal`

# 同根

- adv. `unselfishly` 无私地；不自私地
- n. `unselfishness` 不自私；慷慨

# 例句

She started to get a reputation as an unselfish girl with a heart of gold.
她开始得到有着金子般心灵的无私女孩的名声。
