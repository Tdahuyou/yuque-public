# 音标

- 英 `/'plætɪpəs/`
- 美 `/'plætɪpəs/`

# 词义

n. [动]鸭嘴兽
`a small furry Australian animal that has a beak and feet like a duck, lays eggs, and produces milk for its young`

# 同义

n. [脊椎]鸭嘴兽
`ornithorhynchus` `duckbill`

# 例句

The female platypus lays eggs like a bird.
像雌鸟一样，雌鸭嘴兽也下蛋。

Even so, the platypus genome reveals the animal held onto genes for odor-detection.
尽管如此，鸭嘴兽的基因组还显示其带有气味感觉的基因。
