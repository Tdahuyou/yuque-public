# 音标

- 英 `/stiːl; 'stiːlɪ/`
- 美 `/stil/`

# 词义

n. 石碑,匾额
`an upright stone slab or column decorated with figures or inscriptions, common in prehistoric times `

# 同义

n. [建]石碑；[植]中柱；石柱
`kingbolt`
