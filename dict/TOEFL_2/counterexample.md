# 音标

- 英 `/'kaʊntərɪɡ'zɑːmpl/`
- 美 `/'kaʊntɚɪg'zæmpl/`

# 词义

n. 反例
`an example or fact that is inconsistent with a hypothesis and may be used in argument against it `
