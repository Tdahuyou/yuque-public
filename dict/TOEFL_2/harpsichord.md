# 音标

- 英 `/'hɑːpsɪkɔːd/`
- 美 `/'hɑ:psikɔ:d/`

# 词义

n. 大键琴
`a musical instrument like a piano, used especially in the past`

# 同义

n. 羽管键琴，大键琴
`clavecin` `cembalo`

# 例句

One piece was the harpsichord part for the Concerto in G (molto allegro) and the other was the Prelude in G major.
一件是G大调协奏曲的大提琴部分，另外一件是G大调协奏曲的序曲。

There are also 19 harpsichord suites and several variation sets.
还有19间套房和大键琴套几个变化。
