# 音标

- 英 `/'flæʃbʌlb/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. [摄]闪光灯泡
`A flashbulb is a small bulb that can be attached to a camera. It makes a bright flash of light so that you can take photographs indoors. `

# 同根

- adj. `flashing` 闪烁的；闪光的
- adj. `flashy` 闪光的；瞬间的；俗丽的；暴躁的
- n. `flashing` 闪光；防水板；遮雨板
- n. `flasher` 发出闪光之物；闪光装置

# 例句

Actually even so-called 'flashbulb memories'—like where you were when Kennedy was shot—can be quite inaccurate or easily change over time.
事实上，即使是闪光灯记忆，就像你在肯尼迪被射杀的现场，也是非常不精确的，非常容易改变的。
