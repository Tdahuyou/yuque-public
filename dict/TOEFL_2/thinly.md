# 词义

adv. 稀疏地,瘦
`scattered or spread over a large area, with a lot of space in between`

# 同义

adv. 薄；稀疏地；细；瘦
`sparsely`

# 同根

- adj. `thin` 薄的；瘦的；稀薄的；微弱的
- adj. `thinner` 薄的；瘦的；细的
- adv. `thin` 稀疏地；微弱地
- n. `thin` 细小部分
- n. `thinner` 稀释剂，冲淡剂
- n. `thinned` 揭薄
- n. `thinness` 薄；瘦；细
- v. `thinned` 变薄（thin的过去式）
- vi. `thin` 变薄；变瘦；变淡
- vt. `thin` 使瘦；使淡；使稀疏

# 例句

Sow the radish seeds thinly.
把萝卜籽撒播得稀一些。

The mountain regions are more thinly populated than the lowlands.
山区比低地人烟稀少。
