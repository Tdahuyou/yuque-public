# 音标

- 英 `/'petɪkəʊt/`
- 美 `/'pɛtɪkot/`

# 词义

n. 衬裙,裙子
`a piece of women’s underwear like a thin skirt or dress that is worn under a skirt or dress`

# 同义

n. 衬裙；裙子
`skirt` `underskirt`

adj. 女性的
`female` `feminine`
