# 音标

- 英 `/sen'seɪʃ(ə)n(ə)lɪz(ə)m/`
- 美 `/sɛn'seʃənəlɪzəm/`

# 词义

n. 追求轰动效应,感觉论
`a way of reporting events or stories that makes them seem as strange, exciting, or shocking as possible – used in order to show disapproval`

# 同义

n. 追求轰动效应；哗众取宠
`grandstanding`

# 同根

- adj. `sensational` 轰动的；耸人听闻的；非常好的；使人感动的
- n. `sensation` 感觉；轰动；感动
