# 音标

- 英 `/'rægtaɪm/`
- 美 `/'ræɡtaɪm/`

# 词义

n. 拉格泰姆音乐
`a type of music and dancing that has a strong beat and was popular in the US in the early part of the 20th century`

adj. 使人发笑的


# 同义

adj. 滑稽的；不严肃的
`funny` `humouristic`

# 例句

Joplin is considered the foremost composer of ragtime music.
吉普林被认为是拉格泰姆（Ragtime）音乐最重要的作曲家。

He has recorded every major work for the violin, and has also recorded jazz, ragtime and Jewish folk music.
他录制了每一首主要的小提琴曲，爵士乐，拉格泰姆以及犹太人民间音乐。
