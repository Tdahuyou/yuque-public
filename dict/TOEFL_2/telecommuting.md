# 音标

- 英 `/'telɪkə,mjuːtɪŋ/`
- 美 `/'tɛlɪkə,mjʊtɪŋ/`

# 词义

n. 远程联机,远程办公
`Telecommuting is working from home using equipment such as telephones, fax machines, and modems to contact people`

# 同根

- 电话会议；[通信]. `teleconference` 电话会议；[通信] 远程会议

# 例句

There is also the potential to develop telecommuting and other more flexible working practices.
也有发展家庭远程办公及其他更灵活的工作方式的可能性。
