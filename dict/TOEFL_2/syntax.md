# 音标

- 英 `/'sɪntæks/`
- 美 `/'sɪntæks/`

# 词义

n. 语法,句法
`the way words are arranged to form sentences or phrases, or the rules of grammar which control this`

# 同义

n. 语法；[计][语]句法；有秩序的排列
`grammar` `phraseology`

# 同根

- adj. `syntactic` 句法的；语法的；依据造句法的
- adv. `syntactically` 依照句法地；在语句构成上

# 短语

- `syntax analysis` 语法分析
- `syntax error` 语法错误；句法误差
- `syntax tree` 语法树；句法树

# 例句

His grammar and syntax, both in oral and written expression, were much better than the average.
无论在口头还是笔头上，他的语法和句法都非常出众。
