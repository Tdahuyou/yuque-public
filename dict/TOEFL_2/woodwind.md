# 音标

- 英 `/'wʊdwɪnd/`
- 美 `/'wudwind/`

# 词义

n. 木管乐器
`musical instruments made of wood or metal that you play by blowing and that usually have finger holes or  key s `

# 同根

- n. `woodshed` 贮放柴薪的木棚；柴间
- vi. `woodshed` 练习乐器

# 短语

- `woodwind instrument` 木管乐器

# 例句

woodwind instruments such as the flute or saxophone
木管乐器，如长笛和萨克斯管
