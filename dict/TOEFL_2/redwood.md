# 音标

- 英 `/'redwʊd/`
- 美 `/'rɛdwʊd/`

# 词义

n. [植]红杉
`a very tall tree that grows in California and Oregon, or the wood from this tree`

# 同义

n. [木]红木树，[林]红杉；红树木材
`sequoia`

# 短语

- `redwood furniture` 红木家具

# 例句

Members of the group stand atop the stump of a giant redwood tree.
研究项目组的成员长在一块巨大的红杉树桩之上。

So far her team has mapped out how climate change might alter the habitats of a dozen species, from the Canadian lynx to the California redwood.
到目前为止她的队伍已经绘制出天气怎样的变化会改变一些物种的习性，从加拿大的山猫到加利福尼亚的红杉。
