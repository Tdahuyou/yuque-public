# 音标

- 英 `/'brəʊmiːn/`
- 美 `/'bromin/`

# 词义

n. [化]溴
`a pungent dark red volatile liquid element of the halogen series that occurs in natural brine and is used in the production of chemicals, esp ethylene dibromide`

# 短语

- `bromine water` [化]溴水
