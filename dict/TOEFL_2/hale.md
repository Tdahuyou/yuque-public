# 音标

- 英 `/heɪl/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

adj. 强壮的
`someone, especially an old person, who is hale and hearty is very healthy and active`

v. 硬拖


# 同义

adj. 矍铄的（尤指老人）；强壮的
`strong` `tough`

adv. 完全地
`totally` `entirely` `completely` `absolutely` `thoroughly` `strictly` `fully`

vt. 迫使；猛拉
`pressure` `reduce to`

# 同根

- n. `haleness` 健壮

# 短语

- `hale and hearty` 精神充沛的，矍铄的；老当益壮的，健壮的

# 例句

She’s still hale and hearty at 74.
她74岁仍然精神矍铄。
