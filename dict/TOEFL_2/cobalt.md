# 音标

- 英 `/'kəʊbɔːlt; -ɒlt/`
- 美 `/'kobɔlt/`

# 词义

n. [化]钴
`a shiny silver-white metal that is often combined with other metals or used to give a blue colour to substances such as glass. It is a chemical element: symbol Co`

# 短语

- `cobalt oxide` 氧化钴
- `cobalt blue` n. 艳蓝色；钴蓝色
- `cobalt chloride` 氯化钴；二氯化钴
- `cobalt acetate` 醋酸钴
- `cobalt naphthenate` 环烷酸钴
- `cobalt alloy` 钴合金

# 例句

...a country rich in copper, cobalt, and diamonds.
...一个铜、钴和钻石资源丰富的国家。
