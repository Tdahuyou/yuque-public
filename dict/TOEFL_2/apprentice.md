# 音标

- 英 `/ə'prentɪs/`
- 美 `/ə'prɛntɪs/`

# 词义

n. 学徒


v. 当学徒


# 同义

n. 学徒；[劳经]生手
`freshman` `fresh hand`

# 同根

- n. `apprenticeship` 学徒期；学徒身分

# 例句

The apprentice lingered his work.
这个徒弟磨洋工。

His apprentice always leaves the tools about.
他的徒弟老是乱放工具。

That would be good for my apprentice to have, complementary.
补充说一下，那会是成为我徒弟的优势。
