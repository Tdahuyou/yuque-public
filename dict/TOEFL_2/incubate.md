# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪŋkjʊbeɪt/`
- 美 `/'ɪŋkjubet/`

# 词义

v. 孵卵
`if a bird incubates its eggs, or if the eggs incubate, they are kept warm until they hatch(= the birds inside are born )`



in(在…里面) + cub(躺) + ate → 躺在里面 → 孵化

# 同义

vt. 孵化；培养；温育；逐渐发展
`cultivate` `rear` `plant` `foster` `nurse`

vi. 孵化；酝酿
`hatch`

# 同根

- n. `incubation` 孵化；[病毒][医] 潜伏；抱蛋
- n. `incubator` [禽] 孵卵器；[儿科] 保温箱；早产儿保育器；细菌培养器

# 例句

The birds returned to their nests and continued to incubate the eggs.
那些鸟儿返巢继续孵卵。
