# 音标

- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 豆科灌木
`an American tree or bush, or the wood from it that is used to give food a special taste when it is being cooked on a  barbecue `

# 例句

The mesquite common on North American deserts in both tree and shrub forms, does not begin to grow above ground until its root system is completely developed, ensuring the plant a supply of moisture.
牧豆树属植物不论是乔木和灌木，在北美沙漠中常常可见，在它根部系统完全生长发达到能保证提供充足的水分时才长出地面。
