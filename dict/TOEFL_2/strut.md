# 音标

- 英 `/strʌt/`
- 美 `/strʌt/`

# 词义

n. 高视阔步
`a proud way of walking, with your head high and your chest pushed forwards`

v. 昂首阔步地走


# 同义

vt. 炫耀；在…上趾高气扬地走；给…加支撑
`show off` `brag about`

n. [建][航]支柱；高视阔步
`leg` `backbone` `stay` `mainstay`

# 例句

Peacocks strut in the surrounding meadow.
孔雀在周围的草地上昂首阔步。
