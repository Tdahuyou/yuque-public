# 音标

- 英 `/'ræbɪt/`
- 美 `/'ræbɪt/`

# 词义

n. 兔
`a small animal with long ears and soft fur, that lives in a hole in the ground`

# 同义

n. 兔子，野兔
`bunny` `hare`

vt. 让…见鬼去吧
`to hell with`

# 短语

- `rabbit hair` 兔毛
- `rabbit meat` 兔肉
- `rabbit on` 唠叨，喋喋不休
- `peter rabbit` 彼得兔（卡通人物）
- `rabbit food` 生吃的蔬菜
- `angora rabbit` 安哥拉兔
- `rabbit ears` 兔耳（好奇凡事都想知道）；室内天线

# 例句

The rabbit disappeared in the bushes.
那只兔子消失在树丛中。

At this time, a robust rabbit said: "No, or eat me !
这时，一只健壮的兔子又说：“不，还是先吃我吧！

Mother rabbit told him that he was old enough to eat other food, but not keep pestering her to suckle.
兔妈妈让他不要老缠着妈妈吃奶，他已经能够自己去吃些别的东西啦。
