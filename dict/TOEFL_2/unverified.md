# 音标

- 英 `/ʌn'verɪfaɪd/`
- 美 `/ʌn'vɛrɪ,faɪd/`

# 词义

adj. 未经证实的,未经核对的
`not having been confirmed, substantiated, or proven to be true `

# 同义

adj. 未经核对的，未经证实的
`unchecked` `uncorroborated`

# 例句

The general goal was to close all "unverified defects" before a specific date.
主要的目标是在一个具体日期前消除所有“未经核对的缺陷”。

One potential hazard is the temptation to report unverified claims by scientists that would be rejected by a peer-reviewed journal.
一个潜在危险是你会被诱惑报道科学家未经证实的主张，而这些主张可能会被同行评议的杂志拒绝。
