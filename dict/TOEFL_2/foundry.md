# 音标

- 英 `/'faʊndrɪ/`
- 美 `/'faʊndri/`

# 词义

n. 铸造,铸造场
`a place where metals are melted and poured into  mould s (= hollow shapes )  to make parts for machines, tools etc`

# 同义

n. [机]铸造，铸造类；铸造厂
`casting` `molding`

# 短语

- `foundry coke` 铸用焦炭
- `foundry equipment` 全套铸造设备
- `steel foundry` 铸钢厂；铸钢车间

# 例句

an iron foundry
铸铁厂
