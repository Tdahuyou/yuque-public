# 音标

- 英 `/'sʌnlaɪt/`
- 美 `/'sʌnlaɪt/`

# 词义

n. 日光,阳光
`natural light that comes from the sun`

# 同义

n. 日光
`Nikko` `day light`

# 短语

- `direct sunlight` 直射阳光

# 例句

The water sparkled in the bright sunlight.
水面在灿烂的阳光下闪闪发亮。

(= came in large amounts ) through the windows.
阳光从窗户流泻进来。

a shaft of sunlight
一束阳光
