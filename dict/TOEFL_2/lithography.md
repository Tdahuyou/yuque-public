# 音标

- 英 `/lɪ'θɒgrəfɪ/`
- 美 `/lɪ'θɑɡrəfi/`

# 词义

n. 平版印刷术
`a method of printing in which a pattern is cut into stone or metal so that ink sticks to some parts of it and not others`

# 同根

- adj. `lithographic` 平版的；平版印刷的
- n. `lithograph` 平版印刷
- n. `lithographer` 石版家；石印工
- vt. `lithograph` 用平版印刷术印刷

# 短语

- `electron beam lithography` 电子束曝光；电子束蚀刻
- `optical lithography` 光学平板刻法，光学制版
