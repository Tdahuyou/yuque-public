# 音标

- 英 `/'bæt(ə)lʃɪp/`
- 美 `/'bætlʃɪp/`

# 词义

n. 战舰
`the largest type of ship used in war, with very big guns and heavy  armour `

# 同根

- n. `battle` 战役；斗争
- n. `battler` 战斗者；勇士
- vi. `battle` 斗争；作战
- vt. `battle` 与…作战

# 例句

A new battleship was launched from a shipyard.
一艘新战舰从船坞下水。

The new battleship shall be armed with 16-inch guns.
新战舰将以16英寸口径的大炮武装起来。
