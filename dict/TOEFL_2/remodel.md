# 音标

- 英 `/riː'mɒd(ə)l/`
- 美 `/,ri'mɑdl/`

# 词义

v. 重新塑造,改造
`To remodel something such as a building or a room means to give it a different form or shape`

# 同义

vt. 改造；改变；改型
`influence` `fashion` `shift` `vary`

# 例句

Workmen were hired to remodel and enlarge the farm buildings.
雇用了工人来改造和扩建农场的房子里。
