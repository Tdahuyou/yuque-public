# 音标

- 英 `/'rɪtʃlɪ/`
- 美 `/'rɪtʃli/`

# 词义

adv. 富裕地,丰富地
`If you are richly rewarded for doing something, you get something very valuable or pleasant in return for doing it`

# 同义

adv. 丰富地；富裕地；完全地
`totally` `entirely` `completely` `absolutely` `thoroughly` `strictly` `fully`

# 同根

- adj. `rich` 富有的；肥沃的；昂贵的
- n. `richness` 丰富；富裕；肥沃；华美

# 例句

It is a difficult book to read, but it richly rewards the effort.
这是本难读的书，不过它会丰厚回报为之所付出的努力。
