# 音标

- 英 `/ɪ'lɪmɪneɪt/`
- 美 `/ɪ'lɪmɪnet/`

# 词义

v. 排除,除去
`to completely get rid of something that is unnecessary or unwanted`



e(出) + limin(界限) + ate → 划在界限之外 → 消除； 淘汰

# 同义

vt. 消除；排除
`avoid` `exclude`

# 同根

- n. `elimination` 消除；淘汰；除去
- n. `eliminator` 消除器；消除者

# 短语

- `eliminate poverty` 消除贫困
- `eliminate noise` 消除噪声
- `eliminate illiteracy` 扫盲，扫除文盲

# 例句

Recent measures have not eliminated discrimination in employment.
最近的举措还未能根除就业歧视。
