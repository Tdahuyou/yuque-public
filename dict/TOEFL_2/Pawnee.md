# 音标

- 英 `/pɔ:'ni:/`
- 美 `/pɔn'i/`

# 词义

n. 波尼族人(语)
`a member of a confederacy of related North American Indian peoples, formerly living in Nebraska and Kansas, now chiefly in Oklahoma `

# 例句

The American Indian exhibit intrigues visitors with its collection of totem poles and its Pawnee Earth Lodge, created to explain the traditional ways of the Pawnee Indians, a Nebraska tribe.
美国印第安人展厅的图腾柱和波尼土房也吸引了不少观众，那里展示了内布拉斯加州印第安人部落波尼人的传统生活方式。
