# 音标

- 英 `/gɑːb/`
- 美 `/ɡɑrb/`

# 词义

n. 服装
`a particular style of clothing, especially clothes that show your type of work or look unusual`

v. 装扮




演出时间快到了， 那个演员抓起(grab)演出服装(garb)匆匆赶到后台

`clothing` `wear` `garment` `apparel` `costume`

vt. 装扮；穿衣
`rig out` `to dress`
