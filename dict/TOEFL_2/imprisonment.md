# 音标

- 英 `/ɪm'prɪz(ə)nm(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/ɪm'prɪznmənt/`

# 词义

n. 关押,监禁
`Imprisonment is the state of being imprisoned`

# 同义

n. [法]监禁，关押；坐牢；下狱
`prison` `durance`

# 同根

- vt. `imprison` 监禁；关押；使…下狱

# 短语

- `life imprisonment` 无期徒刑；终身监禁
- `false imprisonment` n. 非法监禁

# 例句

She was sentenced to seven years' imprisonment.
她被判处7年监禁。
