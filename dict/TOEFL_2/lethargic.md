# 音标

- 英 `/lɪ'θɑːdʒɪk/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

adj. 昏睡的


# 同义

adj. [动]昏睡的；无生气的；泠漠的
`soporose` `pulseless`

# 同根

- adv. `lethargically` 昏睡地；迟钝地
- n. `lethargy` 昏睡；死气沉沉；嗜眠（症）

# 例句

The cat became lethargic and had trouble breathing.
这只猫整天昏睡而且呼吸困难。

The koala seemed to be asleep, and I wondered for the first time just how one went about startling such a lethargic creature.
那只树熊似乎睡着了。 第一次干这活儿，我真不知怎么去惊吓一只昏睡的动物。

The organisers say they are also considering giving a prize to the slowest cell, since lethargic cells would be a boon for cancer therapy.
主办方表示他们也在考虑颁一个奖给速度最慢的细胞，因为这种昏睡细胞也是癌症治疗的福音。
