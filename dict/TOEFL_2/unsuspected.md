# 音标

- 英 `/ʌnsə'spektɪd/`
- 美 `/,ʌnsə'spektid/`

# 词义

adj. 未知的,未被怀疑的
`If you describe something as unsuspected, you mean that people do not realize it or are not aware of it`

# 同义

adj. 不受怀疑的；未知的
`unknown` `sealed`

# 例句

A surprising number of ailments are caused by unsuspected environmental factors.
惊人数量的慢性病是由未知的环境因素引起的。
