# 音标

- 英 `/'bʌk(ə)l/`
- 美 `/undefined/`

# 词义

n. 带扣v扣住,变弯曲
`A buckle is a piece of metal or plastic attached to one end of a belt or strap, which is used to fasten it`

# 同义

vi. 扣住；变弯曲
`button up`

vt. 扣住；使弯曲
`wind` `curve`

# 短语

- `belt buckle` 皮带搭扣；束衣扣
- `buckle down` 倾全力；开始认真从事
- `buckle up` 系好安全带；把…扣紧

# 例句

He wore a belt with a large brass buckle.
他系了一根有很大黄铜扣的皮带。
