# 音标

- 英 `/kəʊks/`
- 美 `/koks/`

# 词义

v. 巧妙地（耐心地）处理,哄
`to persuade someone to do something that they do not want to do by talking to them in a kind, gentle, and patient way`

# 同义

vi. 哄骗；劝诱
`trick` `rope into`

# 同根

- adv. `coaxingly` 以巧言诱哄，以甜言哄骗

# 例句

‘Please, Vic, come with us,’ Nancy coaxed.
“求你了，维克，跟我们一起去吧。”南希哄劝道。
