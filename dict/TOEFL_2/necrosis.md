# 音标

- 英 `/ne'krəʊsɪs/`
- 美 `/nɛ'krosɪs/`

# 词义

n. 坏死
`Necrosis is the death of part of someone's body, for example because it is not getting enough blood`

# 同义

n. [病理]坏死；坏疽；骨疽
`thanatosis` `mortification`

# 同根

- adj. `necrotic` [病理] 坏死的；骨疽的，坏疽的
- n. `necrobiosis` 渐进性坏死
- vi. `necrose` 陷于坏死
- vt. `necrose` 使…陷于坏死

# 短语

- `ischemic necrosis` 缺血性坏死
- `coagulation necrosis` [医]凝固性坏死
- `necrosis virus` 坏死病毒

# 例句

...liver necrosis.
...肝坏死。
