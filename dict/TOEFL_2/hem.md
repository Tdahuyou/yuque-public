# 音标

- 英 `/hem/`
- 美 `/hɛm/`

# 词义

n. 边,缘v给缝边
`the edge of a piece of cloth that is turned under and stitched down, especially the lower edge of a skirt, trousers etc`

# 同义

n. 边，边缘；摺边
`margin` `rim`

vt. 包围；给...缝边
`case` `invest`

# 短语

- `hem and haw` 支支吾吾；哼哼哈哈；犹豫
- `bottom hem` 下摆卷边；底卷边

# 例句

The seamstress bound the hem of the dress with a ruffle.
女裁缝用褶裥饰边给女上衣镶边。
