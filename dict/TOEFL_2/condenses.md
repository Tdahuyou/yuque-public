# 词义

v. 浓缩,压缩
`If you condense something, especially a piece of writing or a speech, you make it shorter, usually by including only the most important parts`



con + dense(密集的) → 变得密集 → (使)压缩

# 短语

- `condense into` 把…缩短；把…压缩

# 例句

When you summarize, you condense an extended idea or argument into a sentence or more in your own words.
总结的时候，用自己的话把拓展了的观念或论据简缩成一句或几句话。
