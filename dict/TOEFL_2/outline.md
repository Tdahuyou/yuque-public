# 音标

- 英 `/'aʊtlaɪn/`
- 美 `/'aʊtlaɪn/`

# 词义

n. 大纲
`the main ideas or facts about something, without the details`

v. 描画轮廓,略述
`to show the edge of something, or draw around its edge, so that its shape is clear`



out(外面的) + line(线条) → 轮廓； 概要

# 同义

n. 轮廓；大纲；概要；略图
`profile` `program` `brief` `contour`

vt. 概述；略述；描画…轮廓
`summarize` `sum up`

# 短语

- `in outline` ◎画…的草图(或轮廓)
- `general outline` 大纲，概要
- `outline form` 外形；洞缘形
- `outline view` n. 大纲视图
- `outline map` 略图
- `outline plan` 纲要计划
- `outline drawing` 外形图；轮廓图
- `outline dimension` 外形尺寸

# 例句

a research proposal outline
研究计划梗概
