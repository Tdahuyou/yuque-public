# 音标

- 英 `/'peɪpəbæk/`
- 美 `/'pepɚbæk/`

# 词义

n. 平装本,纸面本
`A paperback is a book with a thin cardboard or paper cover. Compare . `

# 同根

- adj. `paperbacked` 平装的；纸面的
- v. `paperbacked` 以平装本出版（paperback的过去式及过去分词）

# 短语

- `paperback edition` 平装本；平装版本

# 例句

She said she would buy the book when it comes out in paperback.
她说等那本书的平装本面市后她会买一本。
