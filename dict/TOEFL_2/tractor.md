# 音标

- 英 `/'træktə/`
- 美 `/'træktɚ/`

# 词义

n. 拖拉机
`a strong vehicle with large wheels, used for pulling farm machinery`

# 同义

n. [农机]拖拉机；[车辆]牵引机
`traction engine` `hauling machine`

# 同根

- adj. `tractive` 牵引的；拖的；曳引的
- n. `traction` 牵引；[机][车辆] 牵引力

# 短语

- `walking tractor` 手扶拖拉机
- `wheeled tractor` 轮式拖拉机
- `crawler tractor` 覆带式拖拉机
- `tractor plant` 拖拉机厂

# 例句

Iron-ox is another name for tractor.
铁牛是拖拉机的别名。

The tractor hauled the fertilizer away.
拖拉机把肥料拉走了。

In addition to the tractor, we have acquired a new rice transplanter.
除了拖拉机， 我们又添了一台插秧机。
