# 音标

- 英 `/'mɑːʃlənd/`
- 美 `/'mɑrʃlænd/`

# 词义

n. 沼泽地
`an area of low wet ground that is always soft`

# 同义

n. 沼泽地，沼泽地区
`wetland` `swampland`

# 同根

- adj. `marshy` 沼泽的；湿地的

# 例句

At Honghu Lake, fish spawning has been affected, said Zeng Xiaodong, leader of the Honghu marshland management office.
另据洪湖沼泽管理办公室的曾晓东介绍，洪湖的鱼类的产卵量也受到了很大影响。

Designed by architect Lord Norman Foster –who created the new Wembley Stadium –the airport would be a modern-day feat of British engineering built on reclaimed marshland.
该机场由建筑大师诺曼·福斯特爵士设计建造——他曾设计了新温布利球场——机场将建造在开垦过的沼泽地上，这也是现代英国建筑工程的一大壮举。
