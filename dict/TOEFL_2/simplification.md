# 音标

- 英 `/,sɪmplɪfɪ'keɪʃən/`
- 美 `/,sɪmplɪfɪ'keʃən/`

# 词义

n. 简化
`You can use simplification to refer to the thing that is produced when you make something simpler or when you reduce it to its basic elements`

# 同根

- adj. `simple` 简单的；单纯的；天真的
- adj. `simplex` 单纯的；单一的
- adv. `simply` 简单地；仅仅；简直；朴素地；坦白地
- n. `simple` 笨蛋；愚蠢的行为；出身低微者
- n. `simplex` 单形；单层公寓
- n. `simpleness` 简单；单纯
- n. `simplism` 简化主义；单纯化主义
- vt. `simplify` 简化；使单纯；使简易

# 例句

Like any such diagram, it is a simplification.
正如任何此类图表，这是个简图。
