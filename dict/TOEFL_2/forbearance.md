# 音标

- 英 `/fɔː'beər(ə)ns/`
- 美 `/fɔr'bɛrəns/`

# 词义

n. 自制，忍耐
`the quality of being patient, able to control your emotions, and willing to forgive someone who has upset you`

# 同义

n. 自制，忍耐；宽容
`tolerance` `possession` `charity` `mercy`

# 同根

- adj. `forbearing` 宽容的；忍耐的
- n. `forbear` 祖先
- v. `forbearing` 克制（forbear的现在分词）
- vi. `forbear` 忍耐；克制
- vt. `forbear` 忍耐；克制

# 例句

There is too much torment, renunciation, and anxiety in this love for it to be anything but infinite forbearance.
这爱中有太多的折磨、太多的自制、与太多的焦虑，除了无穷无尽的忍耐，这些就成了它的一切。

But forbearance alone cannot be a long-term answer to the problem of Pakistan.
但是仅仅是忍耐不能做为巴基斯坦问题的一个长期答案。

The second principle is forbearance.
第二个原则是忍耐。
