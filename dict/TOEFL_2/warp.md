# 音标

- 英 `/wɔːp/`
- 美 `/wɔrp/`

# 词义

v. 弯曲,歪曲
`If something warps or is warped, it becomes damaged by bending or curving, often because of the effect of heat or water`



战争(war)扭曲(warp)了人们的心灵

# 同义

n. [物]弯曲，歪曲；偏见；乖戾
`violence` `curve` `give` `winding`

vt. 使变形；使有偏见；曲解
`transfigure` `deform`

vi. 变歪，变弯；曲解
`read into` `put a force upon`

# 同根

- adj. `warping` 翘曲的
- n. `warping` 翘曲
- v. `warping` 弯曲；曲解（warp的ing形式）

# 短语

- `warp knitting` 织经纱，经编
- `warp knitting machine` 经编机
- `warp yarn` 经纱；经线；帆布经线
- `warp beam` 经轴
- `warp and woof` ◎基础
- `warp knitted fabric` 经向针织布/物
- `double warp` 双经线
- `time warp` 时间隧道
- `warp direction` 经向，经方向

# 例句

Left out in the heat of the sun, tapes easily warp or get stuck in their cases.
放在太阳底下暴晒后，磁带容易翘曲变形或卡带。
