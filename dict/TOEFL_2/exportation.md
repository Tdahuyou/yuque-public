# 音标

- 英 `/,ekspɔː'teɪʃən/`
- 美 `/,ɛkspɔr'teʃən/`

# 词义

n. 出口
`the act, business, or process of exporting goods or services `

# 同义

n. [贸易]出口
`exit` `way out` `debouche`

# 同根

- adj. `exportable` 可输出的；可出口的
- n. `export` 输出，出口；出口商品
- n. `exporter` 出口商；输出国
- vi. `export` 输出物资
- vt. `export` 输出，出口

# 例句

"The laws prohibiting such exportation must be reinstated as soon as possible, " he said.
“关于禁止出口珍稀林木的法案必须尽快得到恢复，”他说。

The princess, refusing to go without the fabric she loved, would finally break the imperial ban on silk worm exportation.
这位公主在没有她所爱的织物而拒绝出嫁，最终将打破了皇帝桑蚕出口的禁令。

India, with its concentration of talented scientists, is a true hot spot for pharmaceutical innovation and a leader in the production and exportation of low-cost medical products.
印度人才荟萃，有许多才华出众的科学家，堪称为真正的医药创新热土，在生产和出口低成本医疗产品领域具有领先地位。
