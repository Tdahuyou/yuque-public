# 音标

- 英 `/'klævɪkɔːd/`
- 美 `/'klævɪkɔrd/`

# 词义

n. 翼琴(钢琴的前身)
`a musical instrument like a piano, that was played especially in the past`

# 同义

n. 古钢琴，翼琴
`fortepiano`

# 例句

It is called a clavichord.
它被称作古钢琴。 这是一个被动语态的句子。

Our clavichord is kept in the living room.
我们的这架古钢琴存放在起居室里。

That's why she wanted to play the clavichord.
这就是她想弹古钢琴的原因。
