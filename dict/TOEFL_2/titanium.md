# 音标

- 英 `/taɪ'teɪnɪəm; tɪ-/`
- 美 `/taɪ'tenɪəm; tɪ-/`

# 词义

n. [化]钛
`a strong light silver-white metal that is used to make aircraft and spacecraft, and is often combined with other metals. It is a chemical  element  : symbol Ti`
