# 音标

- 英 `/'pəʊst'wɔː/`
- 美 `/'post'wɔr/`

# 词义

adj. 战后的
`Postwar is used to describe things that happened, existed, or were made in the period immediately after a war, especially World War II, 1939-45`

# 同义

adj. 战后的
`afterwar`

# 例句

Anaesthetics and bottle feeding were popular in the early postwar years.
麻醉药和奶瓶喂养在战后早些年代非常流行。
