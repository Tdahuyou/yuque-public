# 音标

- 英 `/skɔːtʃ/`
- 美 `/skɔrtʃ/`

# 词义

v. 烧焦,枯萎
`if you scorch something, or if it scorches, its surface burns slightly and changes colour`

n. 烧焦,枯黄
`a mark made on something where its surface has been burnt`



用火把(torch)烤焦(scorch)

# 同义

vt. 烧焦；使枯萎；挖苦
`blast` `wither up`

vi. 烧焦；枯萎
`flag` `wither away`

n. 烧焦；焦痕
`singe`

# 同根

- adj. `scorching` 灼热的；激烈的；讽刺的；过早硫化的
- adj. `scorched` 烧焦的
- n. `scorched` 烧焦，烤焦；焦头烂额
- v. `scorching` 把…烧焦；严厉批评；切割；过早硫化（scorch的ing形式）
- v. `scorched` 使烧焦；使枯萎（scorch的过去分词）

# 例句

The walls had been blackened and scorched by fire.
这些墙被大火熏黑了。
