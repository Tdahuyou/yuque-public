# 音标

- 英 `/'baɪəʊm/`
- 美 `/'baɪom/`

# 词义

n. (生态)生物群系
`a type of environment that is described according to the typical weather conditions and plants that exist there`

# 同根

- adj. `biomedical` 生物医学的
- n. `biometrics` [生物] 生物统计学；[生物] 生物测定学；寿命统计
- n. `biometry` 寿命测定（等于biometrics）；生物统计学
