# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒesəʊ/`
- 美 `/'dʒesəu/`

# 词义

n. (雕刻、绘画用的)石膏,石膏粉
`a white ground of plaster and size, used esp in the Middle Ages and Renaissance to prepare panels or canvas for painting or gilding `

# 同义

n. [材]石膏，石膏粉；石膏底
`gypse` `plaister`
