# 词义

n. 富饶；富裕
`if something has richness, it contains a lot of interesting things`

# 同义

n. 丰富；富裕；肥沃；华美
`abundance` `plenty` `pregnancy` `amplitude` `enrichment`

# 同根

- adj. `rich` 富有的；肥沃的；昂贵的
- adv. `richly` 丰富地；富裕地；完全地

# 短语

- `species richness` 物种丰富度；种繁富度

# 例句

But I would like to suggest enough of them to impress upon you the richness and complexity of my background.
但我仍会给出足够多的建议让你对我的背景留下丰富而富有综合性的印象。
