# 音标

- 英 `/əʊvə'hiːt/`
- 美 `/,ovɚ'hit/`

# 词义

v. 使过热，变得过热
`to become too hot, or to make something too hot`

# 同义

vi. [热]过热；愤怒起来
`hot shot`

vt. [热]使过热；使愤怒
`parboil`

n. [热]过热；激烈
`violence` `excessive heating`

# 例句

I think the engine’s overheating again.
我想引擎又过热了。

Try not to overheat the sauce.
注意别把调味汁热过头了。
