# 音标

- 英 `/ə'genst; ə'geɪnst/`
- 美 `/ə'ɡɛnst/`

# 词义

prep. 反对,逆,防御
`in the opposite direction to the movement or flow of something`

# 同义

prep. 反对，违反；靠；倚；防备
`in opposition to` `in contravention of`

adj. 不利的；对立的
`adverse` `contrary` `opposite` `disadvantageous`

# 短语

- `up against` 面临
- `go against` 不利于；违反；反对
- `over against` 在…对面；正对着；与…相反；与…形成对照
- `as against` 与…相对照；比…
- `back against` 背朝着

# 例句

sailing against the wind
逆风航行

She dived down and swam out strongly against the current.
她跳下水，逆流奋力游出去。
