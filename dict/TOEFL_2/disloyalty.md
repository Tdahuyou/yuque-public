# 音标

- 英 `/dɪs'lɒɪ(ə)ltɪ/`
- 美 `/dis'lɔiəlti/`

# 词义

n. 不忠实
`Disloyalty is disloyal behaviour`

# 同义

n. 不忠实；不义，不贞；背信弃义
`unfaithfulness` `falseness`

# 同根

- adj. `disloyal` 不忠的；不忠诚的；背叛的
- adv. `disloyally` 不义地；不忠地

# 例句

Charges had already been made against certain officials suspected of disloyalty.
已对某些被怀疑有不忠行为的官员提出指控。
