# 音标

- 英 `/'mɔːtɪs/`
- 美 `/'mɔrtɪs/`

# 词义

n. 榫眼v用榫接合
`a hole cut in a piece of wood or stone so that the shaped end of another piece will fit there firmly`

# 同义

n. 榫眼
`dapt`

# 同根

- n. `mortice` [木] 榫眼
- vt. `mortice` 榫接（等于mortise）

# 短语

- `mortise lock` 插锁
- `mortise and tenon` 阴阳榫
- `tenon and mortise` 榫臼；雌雄准
- `mortise joint` 镶榫接头

# 例句

And there are any metallic connectives on this bed, joggle is between bed leg and bedplate, craftsman certains ably good mortise, this bed combination is together.
并且这个床上没有任何金属连接物，床腿和床板之间都是榫接的，工匠巧妙地凿好榫眼，将这个床组合在一起。
