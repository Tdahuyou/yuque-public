# 音标

- 英 `/ʌp'greɪd/`
- 美 `/,ʌp'ɡred/`

# 词义

n. 升级,上升
`Upgrade is also a noun`

v. 使升级,提升
`to make a computer, machine, or piece of software better and able to do more things`

adv. 往上


# 同义

vt. [计]使升级；提升；改良品种
`promote` `prefer`

n. [计]升级；上升；上坡
`rise` `raise` `updates` `ascension`

adj. 向上的
`upward` `up`

# 短语

- `upgrade oneself` 提升自我
- `on the upgrade` 上升的；进步的
- `upgrade kit` 扩充成套

# 例句

...equipment which needs expensive upgrades.
…需要进行昂贵升级的设备。
