# 音标

- 英 `/,ʌnrekəg'naizəbl/`
- 美 `/,ʌnrɛkəɡ'naɪzəbl/`

# 词义

adj. 未被承认的,无法识别的
`someone or something that is unrecognizable has changed or been damaged so much that you do not recognize them`

# 同义

adj. 未被承认的；无法认出的
`unadmitted` `unheralded`

# 例句

I hadn’t been to the city for 20 years and it was almost unrecognizable.
我已经 20 年没有到过这座城市，几乎认不出来了。
