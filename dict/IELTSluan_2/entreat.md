# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'triːt; en-/`
- 美 `/ɪn'trit/`

# 词义

v. 恳求，央求
`to ask someone, in a very emotional way, to do something for you`

# 同义

vt. 恳求；请求
`pray for` `appeal for`

vi. 恳求；乞求
`pray for` `cry on`

# 同根

- adv. `entreatingly` 乞求地；哀求地
- n. `entreaty` 恳求；乞求

# 例句

He entreated them to delay their departure.
他恳求他们晚些再走。
