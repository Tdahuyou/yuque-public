# 音标

- 英 `/kæ'ʃɪə; kə-/`
- 美 `/kæ'ʃɪr/`

# 词义

n. 出纳员
`someone whose job is to receive or pay out money in a shop`

# 同义

n. [会计]出纳员；司库
`teller` `treasurer`

vt. 解雇；抛弃
`get the boot` `to sack`

# 例句

The cashier absconded with the money.
出纳员携款潜逃。

The cashier had run out with eighty thousand pounds in cash.
这个出纳员带着八万镑现钞消失得无影无踪。
