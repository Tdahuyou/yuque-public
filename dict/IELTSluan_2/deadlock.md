# 音标

- 英 `/'dedlɒk/`
- 美 `/'dɛdlɑk/`

# 词义

n. 僵局，僵局
`a situation in which a disagreement cannot be settled`

# 同义

n. 僵局，停顿
`impasse` `logjam`

vi. [计]死锁；停顿
`bog down`

vt. 停顿；相持不下
`bog down`

# 短语

- `break the deadlock` 打破僵局

# 例句

The talks have reached a complete deadlock .
会谈完全陷入僵局。

a last-ditch effort to break the deadlock
打破僵局的最后努力

Negotiations ended in deadlock.
谈判最后陷入了僵局。
