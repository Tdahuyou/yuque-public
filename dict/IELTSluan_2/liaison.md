# 音标

- 英 `/lɪ'eɪz(ə)n; -zɒn/`
- 美 `/lɪ'ezɑn/`

# 词义

n. 联络
`the regular exchange of information between groups of people, especially at work, so that each group knows what the other is doing`

# 同根

- vi. `liaise` 保持联络；担任联络官

# 短语

- `liaison office` 联络处
- `liaison officer` n. 联络官
- `liaison committee` 联络委员会

# 例句

Liaison between police forces and the art world is vital to combat art crime.
警方和艺术界之间的联络对于打击艺术犯罪是至关重要的。
