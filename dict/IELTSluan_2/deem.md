# 音标

- 英 `/diːm/`
- 美 `/dim/`

# 词义

v. 认为，视为
`to think of something in a particular way or as having a particular quality`



和seem(v. 似乎)一起记

# 同义

vt. 认为，视作；相信
`count` `feel` `find` `guess` `rate`

vi. 认为，持某种看法；作某种评价
`consider` `take for`

# 例句

French and German were deemed essential.
法语和德语被认为是必需的。
