# 音标

- 英 `/'tʊənəm(ə)nt/`
- 美 `/'tɔnəmənt/`

# 词义

n. 比赛，联赛


# 同义

n. 锦标赛，联赛；比赛
`competition` `championship` `game` `match` `play`

# 短语

- `invitational tournament` 邀请赛
- `tournament committee` 竞赛委员会
- `open tournament` 公开赛
- `tournament director` 竞赛主任；比赛指导

# 例句

She is aching to join in the tournament.
她渴望参加比赛。

They dropped two games in a row and were eliminated from the tournament.
他们在划船比赛中输了两场比赛，并被淘汰出局。

It's no use egging him on; he's made up his mind not to join in the tournament.
他已拿定主意不参加比赛了，你再将他也没有用。
