# 音标

- 英 `/ɪn'fɜːm(ə)rɪ/`
- 美 `/ɪn'fɝməri/`

# 词义

n. 医务，医务室
`a hospital – often used in the names of hospitals in Britain`

# 同义

n. 医务室；医院；养老院
`hospital` `old people's home`

# 例句

All they need now are the keys to the infirmary.
现在所需要的就是医务室的门钥匙。

A smile creeps onto his face as he realizes that he’s in the infirmary.
一丝笑容爬上他的脸上，他意识到自己身处于医务室中。
