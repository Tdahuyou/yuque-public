# 音标

- 英 `/'ʃæmb(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'ʃæmbl/`

# 词义

v. 蹒跚，拖沓
`to walk slowly and awkwardly, not lifting your feet much, for example because you are tired, weak, or lazy`

# 同义

vi. 蹒跚地走；摇晃不稳；摇摇晃晃地走
`mouch`

n. 蹒跚；摇晃的脚步
`titubation` `hitchiness`

# 同根

- adj. `shambling` 拖沓的；呆滞的
- v. `shambling` 拖着脚步走；蹒跚的走（shamble的ing形式）

# 例句

Zombies shamble up the rows of the grid toward your house, and if they get past your defenses, well, you know.
僵尸们在网格的每一行向你的房子蹒跚而行，如果他们越过了你的防线，那么，你知道的。
