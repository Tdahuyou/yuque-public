# 音标

- 英 `/haʊnd/`
- 美 `/haʊnd/`

# 词义

n. 猎狗；卑鄙的人
`a dog that is fast and has a good sense of smell, used for hunting`

v. 用猎狗追，追逐


# 同义

vt. 追猎；烦扰；激励
`stimulate` `eat` `burden` `power`

n. 猎犬；卑劣的人
`hunter` `gundog`

# 短语

- `hound dog` 猎狗，猎犬；猎犬乐队
- `basset hound` 矮腿猎犬
- `afghan hound` 阿富汗猎狗
- `follow the hounds` 带着一群猎犬去猎狐

# 例句

A troubled hound was baying the moon.
一条受惊不安的猎狗正在对月狂吠。

Our fox and hound find their long friendship thoroughly obliterated and end up trying to kill each other.
我们的狐狸和猎狗发现他们的老交情彻底被毁了，最后居然要杀死对方。
