# 音标

- 英 `/pəust'mɔ:təm/`
- 美 `/,post'mɔrtəm/`

# 词义

n&a. ，验尸，尸体解剖；事后的，死后的
`A postmortem is a medical examination of a dead person's body in order to find out how they died`

# 同义

n. 验尸；检视；尸体检查
`autopsy` `necroscopy`

# 短语

- `postmortem examination` 尸检；尸体剖检

# 例句

A postmortem was carried out to establish the cause of death.
进行了尸检以确定死因。
