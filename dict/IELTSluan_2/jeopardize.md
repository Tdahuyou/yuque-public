# 音标

- 英 `/'dʒepədaɪz/`
- 美 `/'dʒɛpɚdaɪz/`

# 词义

v. 使受危害，使陷险境
`To jeopardize a situation or activity means to do something that may destroy it or cause it to fail`

# 同义

vt. 危害；使陷危地；使受危困
`harm` `compromise`

# 同根

- vt. `jeopardise` 危及（等于jeopardize）；使…受危险

# 例句

He has jeopardized his future career.
他损害了他的前程。
