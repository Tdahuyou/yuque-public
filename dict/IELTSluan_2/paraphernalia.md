# 音标

- 英 `/,pærəfə'neɪlɪə/`
- 美 `/'pærəfə'nelɪə/`

# 词义

n. 随身用品；贴身用品
`You can refer to a large number of objects that someone has with them or that are connected with a particular activity as paraphernalia`

# 同义

n. 随身用具；全部有关的事物；设备
`equipment` `furniture` `facility` `plant`

# 例句

...a large courtyard full of builders' paraphernalia.
…一个充满建筑装备的大院子
