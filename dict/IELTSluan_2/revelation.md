# 音标

- 英 `/revə'leɪʃ(ə)n/`
- 美 `/'rɛvə'leʃən/`

# 词义

n. 揭示，揭露
`a surprising fact about someone or something that was previously secret and is now made known`



revel(=reveal揭露) + ation → 揭示， 显示

# 同义

n. 启示；揭露；出乎意料的事；被揭露的真相
`exposure` `message`

# 同根

- adj. `revelatory` 泄露的，启示的

# 例句

...following the revelation of his affair with a former secretary.
…在他和前任秘书的风流韵事被揭露之后。
