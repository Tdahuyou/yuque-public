# 音标

- 英 `/kən'kɒkt/`
- 美 `/kən'kɑkt/`

# 词义

v. 调合；编造(故事等)
`to invent a clever story, excuse, or plan, especially in order to deceive someone`

# 同义

vt. 捏造；混合而制；调合；图谋
`manufacture` `trump up`

# 同根

- n. `concoction` 混合；调合；调合物

# 例句

John concocted an elaborate excuse for being late.
约翰为迟到编了一个巧妙的借口。
