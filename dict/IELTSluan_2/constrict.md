# 音标

- 英 `/kən'strɪkt/`
- 美 `/kən'strɪkt/`

# 词义

v. 压缩，缩小
`to make something narrower or tighter, or to become narrower or tighter`

# 同义

vt. 压缩；束紧
`to compress` `telescope`

# 同根

- adj. `constrictive` 紧缩的；束紧的；压缩性的
- n. `constriction` 收缩；压缩；紧窄感；压缩物
- n. `constrictor` 大蟒；括约肌；使压缩之物

# 例句

Caffeine constricts the blood vessels in your body.
咖啡因会使体内的血管收缩。

Linda’s throat constricted and she started to cry.
琳达喉咙发紧，哭了起来。
