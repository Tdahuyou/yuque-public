# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'pent/`
- 美 `/rɪ'pɛnt/`

# 词义

vi&vt. 悔悟，后悔
`to be sorry for something and wish you had not done it – used especially when considering your actions in a religious way`

# 同义

vi. 后悔；忏悔
`rue`

vt. 后悔；对…感到后悔
`regret`

adj. [植]匍匐生根的；[动]爬行的
`acrawl` `creepy`

# 同根

- adj. `repentant` 悔改的；后悔的
- adv. `repentantly` 悔悟地；后悔地
- n. `repentance` 悔改；后悔

# 短语

- `repent of` v. 后悔

# 例句

Those who refuse to repent, he said, will be punished.
他说，那些不肯悔悟的人将受到惩罚。
