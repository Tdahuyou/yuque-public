# 音标

- 英 `/'træmp(ə)l/`
- 美 `/'træmpl/`

# 词义

v. 踩，践踏；蔑视，粗暴对待
`to step heavily on something, so that you crush it with your feet`

# 同义

vt. 践踏；蔑视，伤害
`knife` `ride down`

vi. 践踏；蔑视，伤害；脚步沉重地走
`ride down` `wound`

n. 蹂躏；践踏声
`havoc`

# 同根

- n. `trampling` 践踏；踩踏
- v. `trampling` 蹂躏（trample的ing形式）

# 短语

- `trample on` v. 践踏；蹂躏
- `trample down` 践踏

# 例句

Their in-terests and rights had been trampled underfoot .
他们的利益和权利都被踩在了脚下。
