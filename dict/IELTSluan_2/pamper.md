# 音标

- 英 `/'pæmpə/`
- 美 `/'pæmpɚ/`

# 词义

v. 纵容，娇惯
`to look after someone very kindly, for example by giving them the things that they want and making them feel warm and comfortable`

# 同义

vt. 纵容；使…过量；给…吃得过多
`baby` `indulge`

# 例句

She spent her childhood as the pampered daughter of a wealthy family.
她出身于一个富裕家庭，从小娇生惯养。
