# 音标

- 英 `/'ɪdɪəm/`
- 美 `/'ɪdɪəm/`

# 词义

n. 习语，成语
`a group of words that has a special meaning that is different from the ordinary meaning of each separate word. For example, ‘under the weather’ is an idiom meaning ‘ill’.`

# 同义

n. 成语，习语；土话
`phrase` `dialect`

# 例句

the new musical idiom
新的音乐风格
