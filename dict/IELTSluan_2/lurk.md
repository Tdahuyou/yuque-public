# 音标

- 英 `/lɜːk/`
- 美 `/lɝk/`

# 词义

v. 埋伏，潜伏
`to wait somewhere quietly and secretly, usually because you are going to do something wrong`

# 同义

vi. 潜伏；潜藏；埋伏
`to conceal` `lie low`

n. 潜伏；埋伏
`latency` `incubation`

# 同根

- n. `lurker` 潜伏者

# 例句

a dark formless danger, lurking in the shadows
一种潜藏在阴影里的无形危险
