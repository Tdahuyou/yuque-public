# 音标

- 英 `/refjʊ'dʒiː/`
- 美 `/'rɛfjʊdʒi/`

# 词义

n. 避难者，难民
`someone who has been forced to leave their country, especially during a war, or for political or religious reasons`

# 同义

n. 难民，避难者；流亡者，逃亡者
`asylee` `displaced persons`

# 短语

- `refugee camp` 难民营；难民收容所

# 例句

Refugees were streaming across the border.
难民正涌过边境。
