# 音标

- 英 `/flɜːt/`
- 美 `/flɝt/`

# 词义

v. 挥动，调情
`to behave towards someone in a way that shows that you are sexually attracted to them, although you do not really want a relationship with them`

n. 调情者；急动
`someone who flirts with people`

# 同义

vi. 调情；玩弄；轻率地对待；摆动
`play fast and loose` `toy with`

vt. 挥动；忽然弹出
`whisk`

n. 急扔；调情的人；卖弄风骚的人
`scammer`

# 同根

- adj. `flirtatious` 轻浮的；调情的；爱调戏的
- n. `flirtation` 调情；调戏；挑逗；不认真的考虑

# 短语

- `flirt with` 同…调情；不认真地考虑

# 例句

Dad's flirting with all the ladies, or they're all flirting with him, as usual.
爸爸在和所有的女士打情骂俏，或者说她们都在和他调情，跟平常一样。
