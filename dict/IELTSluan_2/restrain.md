# 音标

- 英 `/rɪ'streɪn/`
- 美 `/rɪ'stren/`

# 词义

v. 限制，约束
`If you restrain someone, you stop them from doing what they intended or wanted to do, usually by using your physical strength`



re(回) + strain(拉紧) → 阻止； 抑制

# 同义

vt. 抑制，控制；约束；制止
`possess` `regulate` `contain` `manage` `bottle`

# 同根

- adj. `restrained` 克制的，受限制的；拘谨的
- n. `restraint` 抑制，克制；约束
- n. `restrainer` [摄] 抑制剂（尤指摄影中用的溴化钾等）；限制器；控制者
- v. `restrained` 抑制；约束（restrain的过去分词）

# 短语

- `restrain oneself` 自制，约束自己
- `restrain from` v. 抑制；制止；阻止…去…

# 例句

Wally gripped my arm, partly to restrain me and partly to reassure me.
沃利抓住了我的胳膊，部分是想制止我，部分是想安慰我。
