# 音标

- 英 `/ʃed/`
- 美 `/ʃɛd/`

# 词义

n. 棚；车库
`a small building, often made of wood, used especially for storing things`

v. 发出；脱落
`if a plant sheds its leaves or if an animal sheds skin or hair, they fall off as part of a natural process`

# 同义

vt. 流出；摆脱；散发；倾吐
`stream` `steam` `slip`

vi. 流出；脱落；散布
`discharge` `issue`

n. 小屋，棚；分水岭
`cottage` `divide` `cabin`

# 同根

- n. `shedding` 脱落；蜕落
- n. `shedder` 脱壳期的虾和蟹；使流出的人；倒注者
- v. `shedding` 脱落（shed的ing形式）

# 短语

- `shed light on` 阐明；使…清楚地显出
- `shed crocodile tears` [谚]猫哭老鼠；假慈悲
- `shed skin` 蜕皮

# 例句

a tool shed
工具棚

a cattle shed
牛棚
