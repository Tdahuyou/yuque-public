# 音标

- 英 `/snætʃ/`
- 美 `/snætʃ/`

# 词义

vt&vi. 抢夺，迅速获得
`If you snatch something or snatch at something, you take it or pull it away quickly`

# 同义

n. 抢夺；抓举；小量
`reif` `despoilment`

vt. 夺得；抽空做；及时救助
`capture`

vi. 抢走；很快接受
`reave`

# 同根

- n. `snatcher` 绑架者；抢夺贼

# 短语

- `snatch at` 抓取

# 例句

Mick snatched the cards from Archie's hand.
米克一把夺过阿彻手里的牌。

He snatched up the telephone.
他一把抓起了电话。
