# 音标

- 英 `/'kaʊntəfɪt; -fiːt/`
- 美 `/'kaʊntɚ'fɪt/`

# 词义

adj. 伪造的
`made to look exactly like something else, in order to deceive people`

n. 伪造
`Counterfeit is also a noun`

v. 伪造，仿造
`to copy something exactly in order to deceive people`

# 同义

vt. [法]伪造，仿造；假装，伪装
`simulate` `doctor` `come`

vi. 仿造；假装
`act` `put on`

n. 赝品；冒牌货；[法]伪造品
`stumour` `shoddy`

adj. 假冒的，[法]伪造的；虚伪的
`artificial` `false`

# 同根

- n. `counterfeiter` [法] 伪造者；货币伪造者

# 短语

- `counterfeit money` 假币；伪钞
- `counterfeit products` 假货；仿造产品
- `counterfeit software` 盗版软件

# 例句

He admitted possessing and delivering counterfeit currency.
他承认持有和运送了假币。

Levi Strauss says counterfeits of the company's jeans are flooding Europe.
利瓦伊·斯特劳斯说该公司牛仔裤的仿冒品充斥着欧洲市场。
