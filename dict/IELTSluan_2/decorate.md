# 音标

- 英 `/'dekəreɪt/`
- 美 `/'dɛkəret/`

# 词义

v. 装饰；油漆；授勋
`to make something look more attractive by putting something pretty on it`



decor(装饰) + ate(动词后缀) → 装饰， 装潢

# 同义

vt. 装饰；布置；授勋给
`paint` `hang` `make` `post`

vi. 装饰；布置
`set sth out` `trick out`

# 同根

- adj. `decorative` 装饰性的；装潢用的
- adj. `decorated` 装饰的，修饰的
- adv. `decoratively` 装饰地
- n. `decoration` 装饰，装潢；装饰品；奖章
- n. `decor` 装饰，布置
- n. `decorator` 装饰者；室内装潢师
- v. `decorated` 装饰，修饰（decorate的过去式和过去分词形式）

# 短语

- `decorate with` 以…来装饰
- `decorate the house` 装饰房间

# 例句

Children’s pictures decorated the walls of the classroom.
教室的墙上装点着儿童画。
