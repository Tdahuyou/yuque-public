# 音标

- 英 `/'letɪs/`
- 美 `/'lɛtɪs/`

# 词义

n. 莴苣，生菜
`a round vegetable with thin green leaves eaten raw in salads`

# 同义

n. [园艺]生菜；莴苣；（美）纸币
`William` `banknote`

# 短语

- `iceberg lettuce` 卷心莴苣；球叶莴苣
- `romaine lettuce` 直立莴苣；长叶莴苣
- `leaf lettuce` 散叶莴苣；油麦菜
- `asparagus lettuce` 莴苣，莴笋

# 例句

One potential test of public acceptance could come with the marketing of irradiated spinach and lettuce.
一个民众接受程度的可能性测试将与辐照菠菜和莴苣的上市销售同步进行。

If you've suffered anxiety, headaches, or muscle or joint pain, you might already be familiar with wild lettuce.
如果你曾经遭受过焦虑、头痛、肌肉或关节疼痛的症状，你也许已经熟悉了野莴苣。

So why does it feel like even the lettuce is out to get us?
那么为什么感觉甚至连莴苣都要加害我们呢？
