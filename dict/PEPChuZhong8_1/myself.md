# 音标

- 英 `/maɪ'self/`
- 美 `/maɪ'sɛlf/`

# 词义

pron. 我自己
`used by the person speaking or writing to show that they are affected by their own action`

# 短语

- `by myself` 独立完成

# 例句

I blame myself for what has happened.
发生这一切都怪我自己。

I can look after myself.
我可以照顾好自己。

I’m making myself a sandwich.
我在给自己做三明治。
