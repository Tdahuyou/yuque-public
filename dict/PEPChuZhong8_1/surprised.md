# 音标

- 英 `/undefined/`
- 美 `/sɚ'praɪzd/`

# 词义

adj. 感到惊讶的
`If you are surprised at something, you have a feeling of surprise, because it is unexpected or unusual`

v. 使惊奇（surprise的过去分词形式）


# 同根

- adj. `surprise` 意外的
- adj. `surprising` 令人惊讶的；意外的
- adv. `surprisingly` 惊人地；出人意外地
- adv. `surprisedly` 吃惊地；诧异地
- n. `surprise` 惊奇，诧异；突然袭击
- n. `surprisal` 惊异
- v. `surprising` 使惊奇；意外发现（surprise的ing形式）
- vt. `surprise` 使惊奇；奇袭

# 短语

- `pleasantly surprised` 惊喜
- `be surprised at` 对…感到惊奇；使…感到意外；对……很吃惊

# 例句

This lady was genuinely surprised at what happened to her pet.
这位女士确实对发生在她宠物身上的事感到吃惊。
