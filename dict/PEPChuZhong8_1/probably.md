# 音标

- 英 `/'prɒbəblɪ/`
- 美 `/'prɑbəbli/`

# 词义

adv. 大概；或许；很可能
`If you say that something is probably the case, you think that it is likely to be the case, although you are not sure`

# 同义

adv. 大概；或许；很可能
`possibly` `maybe` `presumably`

# 同根

- adj. `probable` 很可能的；可信的
- n. `probability` 可能性；机率；[数] 或然率
- n. `probable` 很可能的事；大有希望的候选者

# 例句

The White House probably won't make this plan public until July.
白宫可能要到7月份才会公布这项计划。

Van Gogh is probably the best-known painter in the world.
梵高可能是世界上最知名的画家。
