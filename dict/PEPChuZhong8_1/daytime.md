# 音标

- 英 `/'deɪtaɪm/`
- 美 `/'detaɪm/`

# 词义

n. 日间，白天
`the time during the day between the time when it gets light and the time when it gets dark`

# 同义

n. 日间，白天
`daylight`

# 短语

- `in the daytime` 在白天；在白昼

# 例句

Can I take your daytime telephone number (= the number of the telephone you use during the day ) ?
你能把你的日间电话号码给我吗？

daytime television
日间电视节目
