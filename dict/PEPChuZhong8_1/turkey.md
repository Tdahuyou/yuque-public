# 音标

- 英 `/'tɜːkɪ/`
- 美 `/'tɝki/`

# 词义

n. 火鸡
`a bird that looks like a large chicken and is often eaten at Christmas and at Thanksgiving`



土耳其(Turkey)火鸡(turkey)

# 同义

n. 火鸡；笨蛋；失败之作
`simple` `put`

# 短语

- `roast turkey` 烤火鸡；烧火鸡
- `cold turkey` 突然完全停止使用毒品
- `wild turkey` 野火鸡（威士忌品牌）
- `go cold turkey` 快速戒掉坏习惯

# 例句

They simply demolished the turkey.
他们全吃光了那只火鸡。

I knew then that we would have no turkey or ham for Christmas and I felt sad.
我知道在我们的圣诞节餐桌上肯定不会有火鸡或火腿，我感到难过。
